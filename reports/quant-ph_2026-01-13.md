## 量子理论中的局部尺度不变性：非厄米主义导波公式

**Original Title:** Local Scale Invariance in Quantum Theory: A Non-Hermitian Pilot-Wave Formulation

**Authors:** Indrajit Sen, Matthew Leifer

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.03567v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.03567v2

**Submitted:** 2026-01-07


### 摘要

我们表明，Weyl放弃的局部尺度不变性思想在导波（德布罗格里-博姆）理论中在量子水平上得到了自然的实现。我们通过对电磁规范耦合参数进行复化来获得Weyl协变求导。由此产生的非厄米性有一个自然的解释，在局部尺度不变性的导频波理论。保守电流密度由$修改|ψ| ' 2 $与本地规模不变量、依赖于零件的比率$|ψ| #2/ \mathbel {1}#2 [\mathCal{C}]$，其中$\mathbel 1[\mathCal C]$是取决于配置空间中导波轨迹$\mathCal C$的比例因子。我们的方法是通用的，我们将其应用于与外部电磁场耦合的薛定格、泡利和狄拉克方程。我们还在量子场论中实现了量子轴子场与量子电磁场相互作用的情况。我们讨论了平衡概率密度，并表明相应的轨迹是唯一的。


### Abstract

We show that Weyl's abandoned idea of local scale invariance has a natural realization at the quantum level in pilot-wave (deBroglie-Bohm) theory. We obtain the Weyl covariant derivative by complexifying the electromagnetic gauge coupling parameter. The resultant non-hermiticity has a natural interpretation in terms of local scale invariance in pilot-wave theory. The conserved current density is modified from $|ψ|^2$ to the local scale invariant, trajectory-dependent ratio $|ψ|^2/ \mathbf{1}^2[\mathcal{C}]$, where $\mathbf 1[\mathcal C]$ is a scale factor that depends on the pilot-wave trajectory $\mathcal C$ in configuration space. Our approach is general, and we implement it for the Schrödinger, Pauli, and Dirac equations coupled to an external electromagnetic field. We also implement it in quantum field theory for the case of a quantized axion field interacting with a quantized electromagnetic field. We discuss the equilibrium probability density and show that the corresponding trajectories are unique.

## 利用边界电导率进行超导电路的精确多模式量子化

**Original Title:** Exact Multimode Quantization of Superconducting Circuits via Boundary Admittance

**Authors:** Mustafa Bakr, Robin Wopalenski

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.04407v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.04407v2

**Submitted:** 2026-01-07


### 摘要

我们表明，节点电导率矩阵的舒尔补将多端口电磁环境简化为约瑟夫森结处的驱动点电导率$Y_{mathrm{in}（s）$，自然会导致决定修饰模式谱的特征值相关边界条件。该识别提供了四步量化过程：（i）计算或测量$Y_{\mathrm{in}}（s）$，（ii）针对修饰频率求解边界条件$sY_{\mathrm{in}}（s）+ 1/L_J = 0$，（iii）合成等效的无源网络，（iv）在保留全Cosine非线性的情况下进行迭代。在无源集总元件电路理论中，我们证明了当结端口具有有限的旁路电容时，结参与在高频下衰减为$O（ω_n^{-1}）$，从而确保微扰和的紫外收敛而无需施加截止。标准电路QED参数，耦合强度$g$、反调和性$a $和频散位移$x $，作为受控极限出现，具有明确的有效性条件。


### Abstract

We show that the Schur complement of the nodal admittance matrix, which reduces a multiport electromagnetic environment to the driving-point admittance $Y_{\mathrm{in}}(s)$ at the Josephson junction, naturally leads to an eigenvalue-dependent boundary condition determining the dressed mode spectrum. This identification provides a four-step quantization procedure: (i) compute or measure $Y_{\mathrm{in}}(s)$, (ii) solve the boundary condition $sY_{\mathrm{in}}(s) + 1/L_J = 0$ for dressed frequencies, (iii) synthesize an equivalent passive network, (iv) quantize with the full cosine nonlinearity retained. Within passive lumped-element circuit theory, we prove that junction participation decays as $O(ω_n^{-1})$ at high frequencies when the junction port has finite shunt capacitance, ensuring ultraviolet convergence of perturbative sums without imposed cutoffs. The standard circuit QED parameters, coupling strength $g$, anharmonicity $α$, and dispersive shift $χ$, emerge as controlled limits with explicit validity conditions.

## 混合时间的普遍预测因素超过了刘维利安差距

**Original Title:** Universal Predictors for Mixing Time more than Liouvillian Gap

**Authors:** Yi-Neng Zhou

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.06256v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.06256v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-09


### 摘要

我们分析了由Lindblad主方程控制的开放量子系统的混合时间，表明它不仅由刘维能隙决定，还由刘维超操作子最低激发态的踪迹规范决定。通过利用这些混合时间的通用预测因子，我们分别建立快速和快速混合的一般条件。具体来说，我们推导出强和弱消散区的快速混合条件，并将其表述为Hamilton和局部Lindblad操作员的稀疏性约束。我们的研究结果提供了计算混合时间的通用框架，并为设计消散以实现所需的混合速度提供了指南，这对高效的实验状态准备具有重要影响。


### Abstract

We analyze the mixing time of open quantum systems governed by the Lindblad master equation, showing it is not only determined by the Liouvillian gap, but also the trace norm of the lowest excited state of Liouvillian superoperator. By utilizing these universal predictors of mixing time, we establish general conditions for the fast and rapid mixing respectively. Specifically, we derive rapid mixing conditions for both the strong and weak dissipation regimes, formulated as sparsity constraints on the Hamiltonian and the local Lindblad operators. Our findings provide a general framework for calculating mixing time and offer a guide for designing dissipation to achieve desired mixing speeds, which has significant implications for efficient experimental state preparation.

## 潜在分裂作为因果调查

**Authors:** Santiago Zamora, Pedro Lauand, Isadora Veeren, Davide Poderini, Rafael Chaves

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.06265v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.06265v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-09


### 摘要

贝尔框架对因果网络的推广产生了新的基础见解和应用，包括使用干预来增强对沟通场景中非经典性的检测。然而，当所有可观察变量都像太空一样分离时，此类干预就变得没有信息。为了解决这一局限性，我们引入了潜在分裂过程，这是对量子网络干预的概括，其中受控操纵被应用于潜在量子系统。我们表明，即使传统干预措施失败，潜在分裂也可以通过结合观察和干预数据来检测非经典性。我们专注于三角网络，推导出新的分析证据，这些证据强有力地证明了非经典性，包括最小二进制变量场景的非线性不等式以及之前提出的实验的非经典区域的扩展。


### Abstract

Generalizations of Bell's framework to causal networks have yielded new foundational insights and applications, including the use of interventions to enhance the detection of nonclassicality in scenarios with communication. Such interventions, however, become uninformative when all observable variables are space-like separated. To address this limitation, we introduce the latent splitting procedure, a generalization of interventions to quantum networks in which controlled manipulations are applied to latent quantum systems. We show that latent splitting enables the detection of nonclassicality by combining observational and interventional data even when conventional interventions fail. Focusing on the triangle network, we derive new analytical witnesses that robustly certify nonclassicality, including nonlinear inequalities for minimal binary-variable scenarios and extensions of the nonclassical region of previously proposed experiments.

## 在开源项目中挖掘量子软件模式

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.06281v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.06281v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-09


### 摘要

近年来，量子计算已成为一个活跃的研究领域，其在密码学、优化和材料科学等领域的应用前景广阔。伴随着这些发展，量子软件工程领域也存在挑战和机遇，因为框架和更高级抽象的开发吸引了来自不同背景的从业者。与基于电路模型的初始量子框架不同，最近的框架和库利用更高级的抽象来创建量子程序。本文对来自80个开源项目的985本Inbox笔记本进行了实证研究，以研究量子模式如何应用于实践。我们的工作涉及两个主要阶段。首先，我们从三个量子计算框架（QisKit、PennyLane和Classiq）构建了知识库。这个过程使我们识别并记录了9种新模式，这些模式完善和扩展了现有的量子计算模式目录。其次，我们开发了一个可重复使用的语义搜索工具，以在我们的大规模数据集中自动检测这些模式，提供以搜索者为中心的分析。我们的研究结果表明，开发人员在三个层次上使用模式：从基础电路实用程序，到常见的算法原语（例如，振幅放大），直到特定领域的金融和优化应用。这表明一个成熟的领域，开发人员越来越多地使用高级构建块来解决实际问题。


### Abstract

Quantum computing has become an active research field in recent years, as its applications in fields such as cryptography, optimization, and materials science are promising. Along with these developments, challenges and opportunities exist in the field of Quantum Software Engineering, as the development of frameworks and higher-level abstractions has attracted practitioners from diverse backgrounds. Unlike initial quantum frameworks based on the circuit model, recent frameworks and libraries leverage higher-level abstractions for creating quantum programs. This paper presents an empirical study of 985 Jupyter Notebooks from 80 open-source projects to investigate how quantum patterns are applied in practice. Our work involved two main stages. First, we built a knowledge base from three quantum computing frameworks (Qiskit, PennyLane, and Classiq). This process led us to identify and document 9 new patterns that refine and extend the existing quantum computing pattern catalog. Second, we developed a reusable semantic search tool to automatically detect these patterns across our large-scale dataset, providing a practitioner-focused analysis. Our results show that developers use patterns in three levels: from foundational circuit utilities, to common algorithmic primitives (e.g., Amplitude Amplification), up to domain-specific applications for finance and optimization. This indicates a maturing field where developers are increasingly using high-level building blocks to solve real-world problems.

## 耦合系统去相的量子算法：去耦合和IQP二元性

**Original Title:** Quantum algorithm for dephasing of coupled systems: decoupling and IQP duality

**Authors:** Sabrina Yue Wang, Raul A. Santos

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.06298v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.06298v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-09


### 摘要

在与外部环境相耦合的量子系统的动力学中，噪音和去相干无处不在。在环境相关性迅速衰减的情况下，林布拉德量子主方程很好地描述了子系统的演变。在这项工作中，我们引入了一种量子算法，通过在没有额外辅助的情况下对正量子通道进行采样来模拟单位林德布拉德动力学。使用辅助量子位，我们表明该算法也允许逼近一般的林德布拉德人。对于耦合两个子系统的交互去相Lindbladians，我们开发了一种脱钩方案，可以降低模拟的电路复杂性。这是通过从时间相关概率分布中进行采样来实现的，该分布由一个子系统的演变确定，该分布指定了在补充子系统上实现的随机电路。我们通过研究玻色子通过去相与费米子耦合的模型来展示我们的方法，该模型自然产生于与浴相耦合的电声子系统中的非调和效应。我们的方法能够追踪玻色子自由度，将部分动态简化为对瞬时量子多项（IQP）电路进行采样。然后，采样的位串定义了相应的费米子问题，在非相互作用的情况下，该问题可以经典有效地解决。我们利用从IQP电路中采样被认为是经典困难的已知事实来评论这类消耗性问题的计算复杂性。


### Abstract

Noise and decoherence are ubiquitous in the dynamics of quantum systems coupled to an external environment. In the regime where environmental correlations decay rapidly, the evolution of a subsytem is well described by a Lindblad quantum master equation. In this work, we introduce a quantum algorithm for simulating unital Lindbladian dynamics by sampling unitary quantum channels without extra ancillas. Using ancillary qubits we show that this algorithm allows approximating general Lindbladians as well. For interacting dephasing Lindbladians coupling two subsystems, we develop a decoupling scheme that reduces the circuit complexity of the simulation. This is achieved by sampling from a time-correlated probability distribution - determined by the evolution of one subsystem, which specifies the stochastic circuit implemented on the complementary subsystem. We demonstrate our approach by studying a model of bosons coupled to fermions via dephasing, which naturally arises from anharmonic effects in an electron-phonon system coupled to a bath. Our method enables tracing out the bosonic degrees of freedom, reducing part of the dynamics to sampling an instantaneous quantum polynomial (IQP) circuit. The sampled bitstrings then define a corresponding fermionic problem, which in the non-interacting case can be solved efficiently classically. We comment on the computational complexity of this class of dissipative problems, using the known fact that sampling from IQP circuits is believed to be difficult classically.

## 使用深度强化学习或遗传算法设计量子位链上量子状态转移控制方案的利弊

**Original Title:** The pros and cons of using deep reinforcement learning or genetic algorithms to design control schemes for quantum state transfer on qubit chains

**Authors:** Sofía Perón Santana, Ariel Fiuri, Martín Domínguez, Omar Osenda

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.06303v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.06303v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-09


### 摘要

近年来，基于不同优化技术的控制方法揭示了许多量子系统中处理信息的可能性。当探索量子状态的传输时，更快的传输时间是强制性的，以避免破坏传输过程的多个去相关来源的有害影响。特别是，使用强化学习来设计分步外部控制序列，可以在短传输时间内提供良好的传输策略。我们提出了两种方法来控制量子位链中量子状态的传输，使用外部控制来迫使链状态的动态进化。第一种方法依赖于著名的遗传算法来生成外部控制序列，而第二种方法使用强化学习的变体。遗传算法以与强化学习一样短的传输时间实现了出色的传输保真度，超过了后一种方法所实现的传输保真度。然而，当控制脉冲由于脉冲计时不完美、控制设备缺陷或其他相散相关来源而足够有噪音时，强化学习方法提供了鲁棒的控制策略。我们给出了每种方法最适合控制任意量子位状态传输的制度。


### Abstract

In recent years, control methods based on different optimization techniques have shed light on the possibilities of processing information in many quantum systems. When exploring the transmission of quantum states, faster transmission times are mandatory to avoid the deleterious effects of multiple sources of decoherence that spoil the transmission process. In particular, using Reinforcement Learning to devise sequences of step-wise external controls provides good transfer policies at short transmission times. We present two approaches to control the transmission of quantum states in qubit chains using external controls to force the dynamical evolution of the chain state. The first approach relies on the well-known Genetic Algorithm to generate a sequence of external controls, while the second approach uses a variant of Reinforcement Learning. The Genetic algorithm achieves excellent transmission fidelity at as short transmission times as Reinforcement Learning, surpassing the fidelities achieved by the latter method. Nevertheless, the Reinforcement Learning method offers robust control policies when the control pulses are noisy enough, owing to an imperfect timing of the pulses, deficient control devices, or other sources of phase decoherence. We present the regime where each method is best suited to control the transmission of arbitrary qubit states.

## 寻找量子热力学工作

**Original Title:** Looking for Work in Quantum Thermodynamics

**Authors:** Eugene Y. S. Chua

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.06312v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.06312v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-09


### 摘要

本文诊断了量子热力学中一个备受讨论的问题，即将经典工作推广到量子领域。我从Perarnau-Llobet等人（2017年）的不去定理开始：量子功的通用测量方案无法满足两个直观的、经典一致的需求。我评估这种不兼容性源于测量问题。去相干恢复了所有实际目的的兼容性，但引发了“普遍性”的含义以及任何测量方案是否可以是“普遍性”的问题。我通过使用量子势定义基于量子论的量子功概念，考虑了一种不同的普遍性标准--在存在论方面。虽然这保留了功作为力在距离上的积分的经典角色，并避免了不去定理的张力，但一致性在其他地方失败了;似乎没有一个量子功概念能够保留所有经典特征，这引发了人们对成功将功概念推广到量子热力学需要什么的问题。


### Abstract

This paper diagnoses a much-discussed problem in quantum thermodynamics, that of generalizing classical work into the quantum domain. I begin with the no-go theorem of Perarnau-Llobet et al (2017): no universal measurement scheme for quantum work satisfies two intuitive, classically consilient desiderata. I assess this incompatibility as stemming from the measurement problem. Decoherence restores compatibility for all practical purposes, but raises questions about what 'universality' should mean and whether any measurement scheme can be 'universal'. I consider a different standard of universality -- in terms of ontology -- by defining a trajectory-based notion of quantum work using the quantum potential. While this preserves the classical role of work as the integral of forces over distances, and evades the tension of the no-go theorem, consilience fails elsewhere; no single quantum work concept seems capable of preserving all classical features, raising questions for what it takes for successful generalization of the work concept to quantum thermodynamics.

### 摘要

我们报告的创建$^{86}$Sr$_2$分子的晕态和相邻的弱束缚态。通过单光子光缔合的有效分子生产依赖于电子激发态和基态势中的目标振动态之间的足够的波函数重叠。使用Autler-Townes光谱，测量过渡强度以确定产生弱结合分子的最佳途径。测量了三个最小束缚振动态的振动分裂，并评估了主导系统的不确定性，其不确定性低于100 Hz。从这些分裂中，确定了这些弱束缚振动状态的绝对结合能。这些结果为使用Sr$_2$测量分子同位素位移铺平了道路。


### Abstract

From these splittings, absolute binding energies for these weakly bound vibrational states are determined. The results pave the way to a molecular isotope shift measurement with Sr$_2$.

## 基于分布式测量的量子计算图状态的双划分

**Original Title:** Bipartitioning of Graph States for Distributed Measurement-Based Quantum Computing

**Authors:** Kjell Fredrik Pettersen, Matthias Heller, Giorgio Sartor, Raoul Heese

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.06332v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.06332v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-09


### 摘要

基于测量的量子计算（MBQC）本质上非常适合分布式量子计算（DQC）：一旦资源状态准备好并分布在量子节点网络上，计算就会通过经典通信协调的本地测量进行。然而，由于作用于不同量子处理单元（QPU）的非本地门是一个瓶颈，因此优化量子位分配以最大限度地减少共享资源的节点间纠缠至关重要。对于在两个QPU之间共享的图状态资源，此任务简化为查找具有最小切割排序的双分区。我们引入了一种基于模拟退火炉的算法，当两个顶点在双分区上交换边时，该算法可以有效地更新切割等级，这样就没有必要从头开始计算新的切割等级（这会昂贵得多）。我们通过在网格图和基于测量的量子逼近优化算法（QAOA）上进行测试，表明该方法对于确定分布式MBQC中的量子位分配非常有效。


### Abstract

Measurement-Based Quantum Computing (MBQC) is inherently well-suited for Distributed Quantum Computing (DQC): once a resource state is prepared and distributed across a network of quantum nodes, computation proceeds through local measurements coordinated by classical communication. However, since non-local gates acting on different Quantum Processing Units (QPUs) are a bottleneck, it is crucial to optimize the qubit assignment to minimize inter-node entanglement of the shared resource. For graph state resources shared across two QPUs, this task reduces to finding bipartitions with minimal cut rank. We introduce a simulated annealing-based algorithm that efficiently updates the cut rank when two vertices swap sides across a bipartition, such that computing the new cut rank from scratch, which would be much more expensive, is not necessary. We show that the approach is highly effective for determining qubit assignments in distributed MBQC by testing it on grid graphs and the measurement-based Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm (QAOA).
