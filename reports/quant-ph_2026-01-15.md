## 通过定向简并实现量子码的偏感知BP解码

**Original Title:** Bias-Aware BP Decoding of Quantum Codes via Directional Degeneracy

**Authors:** Mohammad Rowshan

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.07240v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.07240v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-12


### 摘要

我们研究了量子CSS代码的定向知情信念传播（BP）解码，其中各向异性Tanner图结构和偏置噪音沿着首选方向集中简并。我们通过在Tanner图边缘放置方向权重，将它们聚合为每个量子位的方向权重，并定义\ð {定向简并列举出简并如何沿着这些方向集中的\ð {定向简并计数器}来形式化这一点。单个偏差参数~$β$将这些权重映射到与站点相关的log似然比（TLR）中，从而产生各向异性先验，这些先验直接插入标准BP$\rightarrow$OSC解码器，而无需改变代码结构。我们推导出与方向距离和海明距离相关的界限，上界是每个综合征的退化错误类别的数量，作为距离、速率和方向偏差的函数，并给出方向计数器的麦克威廉姆斯型表达。代码容量噪音下的字节长度模拟显示逻辑错误率显着降低--通常在中等物理错误率下降低一个数量级--这证实了适度的各向异性是获得硬件感知解码收益的简单有效途径。


### Abstract

We study directionally informed belief propagation (BP) decoding for quantum CSS codes, where anisotropic Tanner-graph structure and biased noise concentrate degeneracy along preferred directions. We formalize this by placing orientation weights on Tanner-graph edges, aggregating them into per-qubit directional weights, and defining a \emph{directional degeneracy enumerator} that summarizes how degeneracy concentrates along those directions. A single bias parameter~$β$ maps these weights into site-dependent log-likelihood ratios (LLRs), yielding anisotropic priors that plug directly into standard BP$\rightarrow$OSD decoders without changing the code construction. We derive bounds relating directional and Hamming distances, upper bound the number of degenerate error classes per syndrome as a function of distance, rate, and directional bias, and give a MacWilliams-type expression for the directional enumerator. Finite-length simulations under code-capacity noise show significant logical error-rate reductions -- often an order of magnitude at moderate physical error rates -- confirming that modest anisotropy is a simple and effective route to hardware-aware decoding gains.

## 多量子添加猫状态的非经典性

**Original Title:** Nonclassicality of multi-photon-added cat states

**Authors:** Jhordan Santiago, Petr Steindl

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.08894v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.08894v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-13


### 摘要

通过重复将创建操作符应用于猫状态来构建添加多量子的猫状态。我们详细研究了它们的光数分布、$Q$参数、压缩性质和维格纳函数。我们表明，每当添加奇数个量子时，量子的添加就会在原始宇称配置中引起$pi $相移，反映为交换的消失概率和原点处的相空间位移。值得注意的是，同样的过程会将这些状态驱动到亚波森状态，而无论它们的相干状态成分之间的相对相如何，使它们成为量子成像的宝贵资源，代价是失去了四角压缩，但获得了幅度平方。我们还讨论如何使用现有硬件生成这些状态。


### Abstract

Multi-photon-added cat states are constructed by repeatedly applying the creation operator to a cat state. We study in detail their photon-number distribution, $Q$ parameter, squeezing properties, and Wigner function. We show that photon addition induces a $π$ phase shift in the original parity configuration whenever an odd number of photons is added, reflected as swapped vanishing probabilities and phase space displacements at the origin. Remarkably, the same process drives these states into a sub-Poissonian regime regardless of the relative phase between their coherent state components, making them valuable resources for quantum imaging, at the cost of losing quadrature squeezing, but gaining amplitude-squared one. We also discuss how these states can be generated using existing hardware.

## 打破量子低脂码中的同向性障碍

**Original Title:** Breaking the Orthogonality Barrier in Quantum LDPC Codes

**Authors:** Kenta Kasai

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.08824v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.08824v2

**Submitted:** 2026-01-13


### 摘要

经典的低密度Parity-check（Low密度Parity-check）码是一种广泛部署且成熟的技术，构成了现代通信和存储系统的支柱。众所周知，在这种经典设置中，增加坦纳图的周长同时保持规则度分布，同时会带来良好的信念传播（BP）解码性能和较大的最小距离。然而，在量子环境中，这一原则并不直接适用，因为量子低脂码必须满足其宇称检查矩阵之间的额外垂直性约束。当以直接的方式强制执行垂直性和规律性时，周长通常会减少，并且最小距离在结构上成为上界。   在这项工作中，我们通过使用具有受控交换性的置换矩阵并将垂直性约束仅限制在结构的必要部分，同时保留规则的检查矩阵结构来克服这一限制。该设计打破了垂直性、规律性、周长和最小距离之间的传统权衡，使我们能够构造具有大周长且没有通常的距离上限的量子低脂码。作为具体演示，我们构造了一个周长-8，（3，12）-常规$[9216，4612，\leq 48]$量子低脂码，并表明，在BP解码结合低复杂度后处理算法下，它在去极化通道上实现了低至$10 ' s的帧错误率为$4 '%$。


### Abstract

Classical low-density parity-check (LDPC) codes are a widely deployed and well-established technology, forming the backbone of modern communication and storage systems. It is well known that, in this classical setting, increasing the girth of the Tanner graph while maintaining regular degree distributions leads simultaneously to good belief-propagation (BP) decoding performance and large minimum distance. In the quantum setting, however, this principle does not directly apply because quantum LDPC codes must satisfy additional orthogonality constraints between their parity-check matrices. When one enforces both orthogonality and regularity in a straightforward manner, the girth is typically reduced and the minimum distance becomes structurally upper bounded.
  In this work, we overcome this limitation by using permutation matrices with controlled commutativity and by restricting the orthogonality constraints to only the necessary parts of the construction, while preserving regular check-matrix structures. This design breaks the conventional trade-off between orthogonality, regularity, girth, and minimum distance, allowing us to construct quantum LDPC codes with large girth and without the usual distance upper bounds. As a concrete demonstration, we construct a girth-8, (3,12)-regular $[[9216,4612, \leq 48]]$ quantum LDPC code and show that, under BP decoding combined with a low-complexity post-processing algorithm, it achieves a frame error rate as low as $10^{-8}$ on the depolarizing channel with error probability $4 \%$.

## 10兆赫空间光调制器

**Original Title:** A 10 Megahertz Spatial Light Modulator

**Authors:** Xin Wei, Zeyang Li, Abhishek V. Karve, Adam L. Shaw, David I. Schuster, Jonathan Simon

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.08906v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.08906v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-13


### 摘要

光场的快速和可编程成形是现代显微镜、显示技术、光通信和传感、量子工程和量子信息处理的核心。当前的波阵面整形技术面临着基本的二分法：空间光调制器（STM）提供高像素数，但刷新率较低，而声光偏转器（AOD）提供中等速度，且受限的束几何形状。尽管光集成电路的最新进展实现了快速切换，但目前还没有工具可以提供兆赫速率、连续运动和对一组受限制的光点的任意重新配置控制。在这里，我们介绍了一类新型空间光调制器，它既提供2D像素几何形状又提供高速。该设备的工作原理是通过宽带光相调制器将空间信息编码在频率范围中，并通过首创的高分辨率2D光谱仪对其进行解码。该光谱仪基于我们称之为重成像相控阵列（RIPA）的架构，通过光谱仪内重成像镜头导杆实现长路径长度实现其灵敏度。我们展示了上升时间为44（1）~ns的站点分辨光脉冲，对应于超过每秒1000万帧的帧率，以及任意、可重新配置的2D编址和多站点操作，包括同步、独立的束运动、分裂和重组。利用这些工具为整个科学领域的物质快速光学操纵开辟了新的视野，从接近量子处理器中原子惯性和辐射极限的快速、可扩展的控制，到显微镜和神经生物学成像中的动态可编程、微秒分辨率的照明。


### Abstract

Rapid and programmable shaping of light fields is central to modern microscopy, display technologies, optical communications and sensing, quantum engineering, and quantum information processing. Current wavefront shaping technologies face a fundamental dichotomy: spatial light modulators (SLMs) offer high pixel count but suffer from low refresh rates, while acousto-optic deflectors (AODs) provide moderate speed with restricted optical beam geometries. Though recent advances in photonic integrated circuits achieve fast switching, there is currently no tool that provides MHz-rate, continuous motion, and arbitrarily reconfigurable control over a set of diffraction-limited spots. Here we introduce a new class of spatial light modulator that provides both 2D pixel geometry and high speed. The device operates by encoding spatial information in frequency bins via a broadband optical phase modulator, and decoding them via a first-of-its-kind, high-resolution 2D spectrometer. The spectrometer, based on the architecture which we call the Re-Imaging Phased Array (RIPA), achieves its sensitivity through long path-lengths, enabled by intra-spectrometer re-imaging lens-guides. We demonstrate site-resolved optical pulsing with a 44(1)~ns rise time, corresponding to frame rates exceeding 10 million frames per second, as well as arbitrary, reconfigurable 2D addressing and multi-site operations, including asynchronous, independent beam motion, splitting, and recombination. Leveraging these tools opens new horizons in rapid optical manipulation of matter across science, from fast, scalable control that approaches the inertial and radiation limits of atoms in quantum processors, to dynamically programmable, microsecond-resolved illumination in microscopy and neuro-biological imaging.

## 有噪经典和完美量子通道对中零误差容量的超加性

**Original Title:** Superadditivity of Zero-Error Capacity in Noisy Classical and Perfect Quantum Channel Pairs

**Authors:** Ambuj, Anushko Chattopadhyay, Kunika Agarwal, Rakesh Das, Amit Mukherjee

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.08913v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.08913v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-13


### 摘要

我们在不对称通信环境中证明了单次零错误经典容量的超加性，其中有噪经典通道与完美量子通道并行使用。每个通道单独仅支持固定数量的完全可区分的消息。它们的联合使用使得传输的信息数量严格多于个人能力产品所允许的信息数量。我们提出了实现这种增强的显式结构，并确定用完美经典通道取代完美量子通道可以消除这种影响。最后，我们确定了控制这种效应的有噪通道的结构标准，并表明量子优势植根于Kochen-Specker上下文。


### Abstract

We demonstrate superadditivity of one-shot zero-error classical capacity in an asymmetric communication setting where a noisy classical channel is used in parallel with a perfect quantum channel. Each channel individually supports only a fixed number of perfectly distinguishable messages. Their joint use enables transmission of strictly more messages than permitted by the product of the individual capacities. We present explicit constructions achieving this enhancement and establish that replacing the perfect quantum channel with a perfect classical channel eliminates the effect. Finally, we identify a structural criterion on the noisy channel governing this effect and show that the quantum advantage is rooted in Kochen-Specker contextuality.

## 驱动光格子中单向s-p轨道布局边态的观察

**Original Title:** Observation of Unidirectional s-p Orbital Topological Edge States in Driven Photonic Lattices

**Authors:** Gayathry Rajeevan, Sebabrata Mukherjee

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.08914v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.08914v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-13


### 摘要

静态系统的时间周期调制是实现鲁棒单向拓扑状态的有力方法。到目前为止，所有这些实现都是基于$s $轨道之间的相互作用，没有考虑轨道间的耦合。在这里，我们通过在正方形网格中的光学$s $和$p $轨道之间引入周期性调制耦合来演示更高轨道Floquet拓扑绝缘体。$s $-$p $耦合的交错相导致网格的每个格子产生合成均匀的$pi $磁通量，并且耦合的周期性驱动打开了一个以Floquet绕线数为特征的拓扑带隙。我们描绘了绕拐角单向传播的$s $-$p $轨道的拓扑边模式。这里，通过周期性驱动和合成磁通量的组合效应来实现拓扑相。因此，当合成通量关闭时，系统在一定范围内变得微不足道。我们的结果通过引入轨道自由度为探索拓扑现象开辟了一条有希望的途径。


### Abstract

Time-periodic modulation of a static system is a powerful method for realizing robust unidirectional topological states. So far, all such realizations have been based on interactions among $s$ orbitals, without incorporating inter-orbital couplings. Here, we demonstrate higher-orbital Floquet topological insulators by introducing periodically modulated couplings between the optical $s$ and $p$ orbitals in a square lattice. The staggered phase of the $s$-$p$ couplings gives rise to a synthetic uniform $π$ magnetic flux per plaquette of the lattice, and periodic driving of the couplings opens a topological bandgap, characterized by the Floquet winding number. We image topological edge modes of $s$-$p$ orbitals traveling unidirectionally around a corner. Here, the topological phases are realized by a combined effect of the periodic driving and synthetic magnetic flux. Consequently, when the synthetic flux is turned off, the system becomes trivial over a range of driving parameters. Our results open a promising pathway for exploring topological phenomena by introducing the orbital degree of freedom.

## 用极端非信号盒探索贝尔非局部性

**Original Title:** Exploring Bell Nonlocality with Extremal Non-Signaling Boxes

**Authors:** Emmanuel Zambrini Cruzeiro, Junior R. Gonzales-Ureta, Raman Choudhary, Hugo Abreu, Adán Cabello, Sébastien Designolle

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.08924v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.08924v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-13


### 摘要

极端非信号（ANS）盒是与贝尔场景的非信号多胞格的顶点相对应的相关性。量子理论和任何理想测量理论都不允许ANS盒。也就是说，根据量子理论，ENS盒子是非物理的。尽管如此，ENS盒对于解决贝尔非本地性的许多问题至关重要。在这里，我们获得任意二方Bell场景中的ENS盒，并为几个未探索的场景呈现完整的ENS盒列表。配备这些盒子后，我们重新审视了几个基本问题。我们发现任何ENS盒的两个副本已经违反了排他性（或局部垂直性）和Specker原则。我们根据ENS框提供了幻方相关性（自然界中已知的最简单完美相关性）的最小分解。我们确定了通信dit（d < 6）不足以模拟ENS盒的最低情况。我们的结果表明，ENS盒方法可以产生新的结果，并为研究开辟了新的途径。


### Abstract

Extremal non-signaling (ENS) boxes are correlations that correspond to vertices of the non-signaling polytope of a Bell scenario. Neither quantum theory nor any theory for ideal measurements allows for ENS boxes. That is, according to quantum theory, ENS boxes are nonphysical. Still, ENS boxes are crucial for addressing a number of problems in Bell nonlocality. Here, we obtain ENS boxes in arbitrary bipartite Bell scenarios and present the complete list of ENS boxes for several unexplored scenarios. Equipped with the boxes, we revisit several foundational questions. We find that already two copies of any ENS box violate the exclusivity (or local orthogonality) and Specker's principles. We provide the minimal decomposition of the magic square correlation - the simplest known perfect correlation in nature - in terms of ENS boxes. We identify the minimal scenario in which a dit of communication (with d < 6) is insufficient to simulate ENS boxes. Our results show that the ENS boxes approach leads to new results and opens new avenues for research.

## $pi $-通量受挫格子中的出现手征希格斯模

**Original Title:** Emergent chiral Higgs mode in $π$-flux frustrated lattices

**Authors:** Maria Lanaro, Lorenzo Maffi, Marco Di Liberto

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.08925v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.08925v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-13


### 摘要

中性原子量子模拟器提供了一个强大的平台，用于实现强相关相，从而能够访问准粒子和对称性破缺过程的动态特征。受最近对具有不为零的稳态电流的通量受阻梯中量子相的观察的启发，我们研究了二维二聚化BBH格子上的相互作用玻色子-最初是在更高级的布局的背景下引入的。绘制出包括涡旋超流（V-SF）、涡旋莫特绝缘体（V-MI）和无特征莫特绝缘体（MI）相的相图后，我们将重点关注整填充情况。在那里，MI/V-SF转变同时打破了$\mathbb Z_2^{T}$和U（1）对称性，其中$\mathbb Z_2^{T}$对应于时间反转对称性（TRS）。使用奴隶玻色子描述，我们解析了整个转变的激发光谱，并揭示了一种手征希格斯模，其质量在临界点软化，提供了我们通过激灭动力学进行数值探索的出现手征性的动力学标志。我们的结果为探测强相互作用量子物质中非常规的TRS破缺相和具有本征手征性的准粒子建立了一个实验现实的设置。


### Abstract

Neutral-atom quantum simulators provide a powerful platform for realizing strongly correlated phases, enabling access to dynamical signatures of quasiparticles and symmetry breaking processes. Motivated by recent observations of quantum phases in flux-frustrated ladders with non-vanishing ground state currents, we investigate interacting bosons on the dimerized BBH lattice in two dimensions-originally introduced in the context of higher-order topology. After mapping out the phase diagram, which includes vortex superfluid (V-SF), vortex Mott insulator (V-MI), and featureless Mott insulator (MI) phases, we focus on the integer filling case. There, the MI/V-SF transition simultaneously breaks the $\mathbb Z_2^{T}$ and U(1) symmetries, where $\mathbb Z_2^{T}$ corresponds to time-reversal symmetry (TRS). Using a slave-boson description, we resolve the excitation spectrum across the transition and uncover a chiral Higgs mode whose mass softens at criticality, providing a dynamical hallmark of emergent chirality that we numerically probe via quench dynamics. Our results establish an experimentally realistic setting for probing unconventional TRS-broken phases and quasiparticles with intrinsic chirality in strongly interacting quantum matter.

## 自由空间光脉冲中大相干态叠加的产生

**Original Title:** Generation of Large Coherent-State Superpositions in Free-Space Optical Pulses

**Authors:** Lucas Caron, Hector Simon, Hugo Basset, Romaric Journet, Rosa Tualle-Brouri

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.09672v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.09672v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-14


### 摘要

非高斯量子状态的产生是普适连续变量量子信息处理的关键要求。我们报告了自由空间光脉冲上实验产生的大幅度压缩相干态叠加（压缩猫态），达到幅度$a = 2.47$，据我们所知，这超过了之前所有报道的值。我们的协议依赖于Fock状态$的受控混合|1\rangle $和$|2通过可调谐分束器，然后通过零差检测进行预告。生成的状态在其Wigner函数中显示三个分辨率良好的负区域，并实现了0.53美元的保真度，目标状态$\propto \hat{S}（z）（|阿尔法角-|- a\rangle）$，其中$a = 2.47$，压缩参数$z = 0.56$。这些结果构成了时间育种协议和光学GKP状态迭代生成的重要里程碑，为可扩展和耐故障的量子架构开辟了新的前景。


### Abstract

The generation of non-Gaussian quantum states is a key requirement for universal continuous-variable quantum information processing. We report the experimental generation of large-amplitude squeezed coherent-state superpositions (squeezed cat states) on free-space optical pulses, reaching an amplitude of $α= 2.47$, which, to our knowledge, exceeds all previously reported values. Our protocol relies on the controlled mixing of the Fock states $|1\rangle$ and $|2\rangle$ through a tunable beam splitter, followed by heralding via homodyne detection. The resulting state displays three well-resolved negative regions in its Wigner function and achieves a fidelity of $0.53$ with the target state $\propto \hat{S}(z)(|α\rangle - |-α\rangle)$, with $α= 2.47$ and squeezing parameter $z = 0.56$. These results constitute a significant milestone for temporal breeding protocols and for the iterative generation of optical GKP states, opening new perspectives for scalable and fault-tolerant photonic quantum architectures.

## 同形的量子图

**Original Title:** Quantum graphs of homomorphisms

**Authors:** Andre Kornell, Bert Lindenhovius

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.09685v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.09685v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-14


### 摘要

我们引入了量子图的一个类别$\mathsf{qGph}$，其定义完全源自非交换几何。对于$\mathsf{qGph}$中的所有量子图$G$和$H$，然后我们构造一个从$G$到$H$的同质的量子图$[G，H]$，使$\mathsf{qGph}$成为一个封闭对称的一元论范畴。我们证明，对于所有有限图$G$和$H$，量子图$[G，H]$是非空的当条件是$（G，H）$-homomorphism博弈有获胜的量子策略，直接推广了经典情况。   $\mathsf{qGph}$中的有限量子图是踪迹的、实的和自伴随的，它们之间的态射是与单位$*$-homomorphism相伴的CP态射。我们表明，韦弗的两个概念的CP态射在这种情况下相吻合。我们还证明了每个有限自反量子图都是量子信道的可混淆量子图，回答了Daws的一个问题。


### Abstract

We introduce a category $\mathsf{qGph}$ of quantum graphs, whose definition is motivated entirely from noncommutative geometry. For all quantum graphs $G$ and $H$ in $\mathsf{qGph}$, we then construct a quantum graph $[G,H]$ of homomorphisms from $G$ to $H$, making $\mathsf{qGph}$ a closed symmetric monoidal category. We prove that for all finite graphs $G$ and $H$, the quantum graph $[G,H]$ is nonempty iff the $(G,H)$-homomorphism game has a winning quantum strategy, directly generalizing the classical case.
  The finite quantum graphs in $\mathsf{qGph}$ are tracial, real, and self-adjoint, and the morphisms between them are CP morphisms that are adjoint to a unital $*$-homomorphism. We show that Weaver's two notions of a CP morphism coincide in this context. We also show that every finite reflexive quantum graph is the confusability quantum graph of a quantum channel, answering a question of Daws.
