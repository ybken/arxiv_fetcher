## 混合状态管形上等级变化点附近的几何学：布雷斯度规、圆锥奇异性和Lindblad动力学

**Original Title:** Geometry near rank-changing points on the mixed-state manifold: Bures metric, conical singularities, and Lindblad dynamics

**Authors:** Yu-Huan Huang, Xu-Yang Hou, Hao Guo, Chih-Chun Chien

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27907v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.27907v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-27


### 摘要

我们阐明了密度矩阵变阶点附近的量子状态空间中的布雷斯度规，并展示了两级（$N=2$）系统与更高级别系统的对比行为。由于$N=2$的光滑纯状态边界，我们证明了表观度量偏离仅仅是坐标伪影，并提出了三个Lindblad过程，在等级变化点附近表现出质上不同的演变，展示了测地方法、乘势律缩放和纯状态逃逸定律。对于更高维度（$N\ge 3$）系统，等级变化点附近的几何形状有根本不同。在密度矩阵及其接近纯状态的适当限制下，布雷斯度规简化为纯状态在锥尖的圆锥度规。这样的圆锥几何形状导致了真正的弯曲奇异性：二维圆锥在尖端附近表现出狄拉克三角函数弯曲，而更高维的圆锥在朝向圆锥尖端表现出乘势定律的弯曲。提出了具有圆锥奇异性的$N=3$系统的Lindblad进化的构建，以及对未来实验和理论研究的可能影响。


### Abstract

We elucidate the Bures metric in quantum state space near a rank-changing point of the density matrix and show contrasting behavior for two-level ($N=2$) systems versus higher-level systems. Due to the smooth pure-state boundary for $N=2$, we prove the apparent metric divergences to be merely coordinate artifacts and present three Lindblad processes exhibiting qualitatively different evolution near rank-changing points, showing geodesic approach, power-law scaling, and pure-state escape law. For higher-dimensional ($N\ge 3$) systems, the geometry near a rank-changing point differs fundamentally. Under suitable restrictions of the density matrix and its approach towards a pure state, the Bures metric reduces to a conical metric with the pure state at the cone tip. Such a conic geometry leads to genuine curvature singularities: A two-dimensional cone exhibits a Dirac delta-function curvature near the tip while a higher-dimensional cone shows a power-law divergence of the curvature towards the cone tip. A construction of Lindblad evolution for $N=3$ systems with conic singularities is presented, along with possible implications for future experimental and theoretical research.

## 学习任意量子错误纠正码的逻辑运算

**Original Title:** Learning Logical Operations for Arbitrary Quantum Error Correction Codes

**Authors:** Nico Meyer, Christopher Mutschler, Dominik Seuß, Andreas Maier, Daniel D. Scherer

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28162v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.28162v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-27


### 摘要

逻辑运算对于量子误差纠正码中的量子计算至关重要。然而，发现它们的物理实现具有挑战性，尤其是对于缺乏稳定器描述的非添加性代码。我们提出了一个基于学习的通用框架，仅给定编码电路，该框架就可以构建逻辑操作的物理实现，同时强制执行横向性或浅深度等结构属性。通过重新发现标准稳定器代码的已知逻辑操作来验证我们的方法。然后，我们将其扩展到一个共同设计过程，称为变分早期故障容忍量子计算（VarEFTQC），该过程将非添加性编码定制到给定的噪音模型，并强制执行所需的逻辑门集，例如横向IoP型家族或低深度通用集。软件库实现完整的学习管道，包括损失函数变体、模拟器家族和优化例程。总而言之，这些结果使VarEFTQC成为发现早期容错量子计算的硬件适应逻辑小工具的实用工具。


### Abstract

Logical operations are essential for quantum computation within quantum error-correcting codes. However, discovering their physical realizations is challenging, especially for non-additive codes that lack a stabilizer description. We present a general learning-based framework that, given only an encoding circuit, constructs physical implementations of logical operations while enforcing structural properties such as transversality or shallow depth. Our approach is validated by rediscovering known logical operations of standard stabilizer codes. We then extend it to a co-design procedure, dubbed variational early fault-tolerant quantum computing (VarEFTQC), which tailors non-additive encodings to a given noise model and enforces desired logical gate sets, such as transversal IQP-type families or low-depth universal sets. A software library implements the complete learning pipeline, including loss-function variants, ansatz families, and optimization routines. Together, these results position VarEFTQC as a practical tool for discovering hardware-adapted logical gadgets for early fault-tolerant quantum computing.

## 会面体散射形式的边界--剩余关联余代数

**Original Title:** A Boundary--Residue Incidence Coalgebra for Associahedral Scattering Forms

**Authors:** Ioannis P. Zois

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30390v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30390v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-28


### 摘要

我们引入了与正几何的面偏序集相关的边界-剩余关联余代数，并将其应用于关联面体散射形式。该构造的动机是费曼图上的Connes-Kreimer余积与典范形式的递回剩余结构之间的类比。对于Stasheff关联面体\（K_n\），其面由\（（n+1）\）-边形的非交叉解剖来索引，我们证明了关联余积记录了相应树级纯量幅度的所有中间嵌套平面因式分解通道。由解剖因子标记的面上的规范形式的剩余部分被分解为与所得子多边形相关的下关联面体上规范形式的外积。我们明确说明了五角关联面体\（K_4\）的构造，对应于五点平面纯量幅度。然后我们制定一个循环级扩展：每当平面循环被积函数由正几何表示时，关联面体面偏序集就会被相应循环几何的边界偏序集取代。单圈面体给出了一个具体的纯量例子，而在非平面的情况下，我们在log奇异层水平上定义了相关的关联余代数。最后，我们比较了边界剩余余代数与三角形或规则CW时空的细胞关联余代数。有限规则CW复合体的面偏序集重建了其重心细分，从而重建了其底层的多边体，而在正几何中，相同的关联机制组织了规范形式的残基。这在细胞时空布局和正几何幅度因子分解之间产生了一个事件优先的桥梁，而不假设度量或因果数据是由布局确定的。


### Abstract

We introduce a boundary--residue incidence coalgebra associated with the face poset of a positive geometry and apply it to associahedral scattering forms. The construction is motivated by the analogy between the Connes--Kreimer coproduct on Feynman graphs and the recursive residue structure of canonical forms. For the Stasheff associahedron \(K_n\), whose faces are indexed by non-crossing dissections of an \((n+1)\)-gon, we prove that the incidence coproduct records all intermediate nested planar factorisation channels of the corresponding tree-level scalar amplitude. The residue of the canonical form on a face labelled by a dissection factorises as the exterior product of canonical forms on the lower associahedra associated with the resulting subpolygons. We illustrate the construction explicitly for the pentagon associahedron \(K_4\), corresponding to the five-point planar scalar amplitude. We then formulate a loop-level extension: whenever a planar loop integrand is represented by a positive geometry, the associahedral face poset is replaced by the boundary poset of the corresponding loop geometry. The one-loop halohedron gives a concrete scalar example, while in the non-planar case we define the associated incidence coalgebra at the level of logarithmic singularity strata. Finally, we compare the boundary--residue coalgebra with the cellular incidence coalgebra of a triangulated or regular CW spacetime. The face poset of a finite regular CW complex reconstructs its barycentric subdivision, and hence its underlying polyhedron, while in positive geometry the same incidence mechanism organises canonical-form residues. This yields an incidence-first bridge between cellular spacetime topology and positive-geometric amplitude factorisation, without assuming that metric or causal data are determined by topology.

## 通过动态镜补偿恢复大面积双原子干涉陀螺仪速度抗扰度

**Authors:** Jie Gu, Yin-fei Mao, Zhan-Wei Yao, An-qing Zhang, Si-Bin Lu, Shao-kang Li, Min Jiang, Xiao-Li Chen, Min Ke, Xi Chen, Run-Bing Li, Jin Wang, Ming-Sheng Zhan

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.29929v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.29929v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-28


### 摘要

我们提出并演示了一种动态镜补偿方案，以恢复大面积双原子干涉仪陀螺的速度免疫性。在理想的马赫-曾德配置中，移相本质上不受原子速度的影响，但地球自转通过科里奥利效应打破了这一特性。我们通过在脉冲序列期间主动旋转拉曼镜来消除与时间相关的角度偏差来克服这个问题。该实现依赖于可重新装配的校准补偿链来消除旋转引起的时间相关项。该方案在干涉面积为21.1厘米2的双原子干涉仪陀螺仪上得到了验证。补偿后，相对原子速度的依赖性降低了40倍，速度对比例因子稳定性的贡献被评估为0.13 ppm。该传感器在4500 s积分时实现了1.3x10 - 8} rad/s/Hz ' x10 - 10} rad/s的旋转灵敏度和1.9x10 - 10} rad/s的稳定性，以及高达459的公共模式噪音抑制比，已在地震事件中得到证实。这项工作消除了原子干涉仪陀螺仪比例因子稳定的关键障碍，并为其在惯性导航和地球物理学中的应用铺平了道路。


### Abstract

We propose and demonstrate a dynamical mirror compensation scheme to restore velocity immunity in a large-area dual-atom-interferometer gyroscope. In an ideal Mach-Zehnder configuration, the phase shift is inherently immune to atomic velocity, but this property is broken by the Earth's rotation via the Coriolis effect. We overcome this by actively rotating the Raman mirrors during the pulse sequence to cancel the time-dependent angular offset. The scheme is validated on a dual-atom-interferometer gyroscope with an interference area of 21.1 cm^2. After compensation, the phase's dependence on atomic velocity is reduced 40-fold, and the velocity contribution to scale-factor stability is evaluated to be 0.13 ppm. The sensor achieves a rotation sensitivity of 1.3\times10^{-8} rad/s/Hz^{1/2} and a stability of 1.9\times10^{-10} rad/s at 4500 s integration, together with a common-mode noise rejection ratio of up to 459, demonstrated in a seismic event. This work removes a key obstacle to scale-factor stabilization in atom-interferometer gyroscopes and paves the way for their applications in inertial navigation and geophysics.

## 根据NO$v $A和T2 K中Fisher信息确定轻子CP相

**Original Title:** Leptonic CP Phase Determination from Fisher Information in NO$ν$A and T2K

**Authors:** Neetu Raj Singh Chundawat, Luis A. Delgadillo, Yu-Feng Li

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30404v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30404v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-28


### 摘要

轻子CP相$δ_CP $的精确确定仍然是当前和未来长基线（LBL）中微子振荡实验的中心目标之一。量子估计理论提供了一个自然的框架来量化估计以量子状态编码的物理参数的最终精度限制。在这项工作中，我们利用量子Fisher信息来研究有多少有关$δ_CP}的信息本质上编码在中微子状态中，以及在当前的LBL实验（例如T2 K和NO$v $A）中提取这些信息的效率如何。我们首先分析了中微子和反中微子状态的固有量子灵敏度，并演示了物质效应如何产生取决于中微子质量排序的信息结构。为了将量子状态的固有信息含量与通过味道测量实验可获得的信息进行比较，我们使用Poisson统计量从重建的事件谱中计算事件级Fisher信息。我们发现这两个实验都只提取了潜在量子状态中可用总信息的一小部分。这种提取效率在最大CP违反区域附近变得特别受到抑制，其中重建的事件谱对$δ_CP}$的微小变化表现出降低的敏感性。我们的分析为LBL中微子实验中振荡参数的精确估计提供了补充的信息论视角。


### Abstract

The precise determination of the leptonic CP phase $δ_{\rm CP}$ remains one of the central objectives of current and future long-baseline (LBL) neutrino oscillation experiments. Quantum estimation theory provides a natural framework to quantify the ultimate precision limits for estimating physical parameters encoded in quantum states. In this work, we employ the quantum Fisher information to investigate how much information about $δ_{\rm CP}$ is intrinsically encoded in neutrino states and how efficiently it is extracted in present LBL experiments such as T2K and NO$ν$A. We first analyze the intrinsic quantum sensitivity of neutrino and antineutrino states and demonstrate how matter effects generate a neutrino mass-ordering dependent information structure. To compare the intrinsic information content of the quantum state with the information experimentally accessible through flavor measurements, we compute the event-level Fisher information from reconstructed event spectra using Poisson statistics. We find that both experiments extract only a small fraction of the total information available in the underlying quantum state. This extraction efficiency becomes particularly suppressed near maximally CP-violating regions, where the reconstructed event spectra exhibit reduced sensitivity to small variations in $δ_{\rm CP}$. Our analysis provides a complementary information-theoretic perspective on precise estimation of oscillation parameters in LBL neutrino experiments.

## 迈向现象学上可接受的量子循环宇宙

**Original Title:** Toward a Phenomenologically Acceptable Quantum Cyclic Universe

**Authors:** Sean M. Carroll, Nadiia Diachenko, Saakshi Dulani

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30405v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30405v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-28


### 摘要

我们提出了一个量子宇宙学模型，它完全是周期性的，但避免了波尔兹曼脑问题。如果用有限维希尔伯特空间中单位进化的量子状态来描述宇宙，那么它的进化将是反复发生的：如果有足够的时间，该状态将任意地返回到接近其初始状态。人们担心，这种情况在现象学上是不可接受的，因为国家将大部分时间都在高熵平衡宏观状态中度过，其中很少有极小的熵向下波动，而绝大多数观察者将处于远离平衡的最小波动，或“波尔兹曼大脑”。“在这里我们表明这不一定是正确的。如果能量特征值的差异是可预测的，那么进化就不是简单的反复出现的，而是完全周期性的。此外，如果状态从最小热力学熵开始，那么它的演变可能会表现出显着的熵漂移，这比人们从传统公式$P（ΔS）\propto \BEP（-ΔS）$中预期的要明显得多。这种漂移可能代表我们的大爆炸，在大崩溃发生和循环再次开始之前，在随后的平衡阶段中发生的波尔兹曼波动相对较少。我们推测这种量子宇宙的时空解释。


### Abstract

We put forward a quantum model of cosmology that is exactly periodic but avoids the Boltzmann Brain problem. If the universe is described by a quantum state evolving unitarily in a finite-dimensional Hilbert space, its evolution will be recurrent: given enough time, the state will return arbitrarily close to its initial state. There is a worry that such a scenario cannot be phenomenologically acceptable, because the state will spend most of its time in a high-entropy equilibrium macrostate, with rare fluctuations downward in entropy, and the vast majority of observers will be minimal fluctuations away from equilibrium, or ``Boltzmann Brains." Here we show that this is not necessarily true. If the differences in energy eigenvalues are commensurable, the evolution is not simple recurrent, but exactly periodic. Moreover, if the state starts at minimum thermodynamic entropy, its evolution can feature a distinguished entropy excursion that is much more pronounced than one would expect from the conventional expression $P(ΔS) \propto \exp(-ΔS)$. This excursion could represent our Big Bang, with relatively few Boltzmann fluctuations occurring in the subsequent equilibrium phase before a Big Crunch occurs and the cycle begins again. We speculate on the spacetime interpretation of this kind of quantum universe.

## 优化网格上超模块性和次可加性的优化先兆

**Original Title:** Majorization precursors to supermodularity and subadditivity on the majorization lattice

**Authors:** Alexander Stévins, Michael G. Jabbour, Serge Deside, Nicolas J. Cerf

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30331v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30331v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-28


### 摘要

我们建立了两个结构优化关系，我们称之为前体，其基础是优化引起的格子上的超模块性和次可加性的性质。这些都是前身，因为它们直接暗示着所有凹函数的和（我们称之为和-凹函数）在优化网格上都是超模和次加性的。然后，使用这些优化关系，我们展示了Tsallis entric（对于所有$a $）和Rényi entric（对于所有$a> 1$）的超模性和次可加性（在格论意义上），并在此过程中恢复了香农信息的这些性质。我们进一步加强了这些不等式，表明：（i）所有这些熵函在优化格上都是严格次加性的;（ii）察利斯（以及因此香农信息）在优化格上是严格超模的。


### Abstract

We establish two structural majorization relations, which we call precursors, underlying the properties of supermodularity and subadditivity on the lattice induced by majorization. These are precursors in that they immediately imply that all sums of concave functions, which we dub sum-concave functions, are supermodular and subadditive on the majorization lattice. Using these majorization relations, we then show the supermodularity and subadditivity (in the lattice-theoretic sense) of Tsallis entropies (for all $α$) and Rényi entropies (for all $α> 1$), also recovering these properties for the Shannon entropy in the process. We further strengthen these inequalities, showing that: (i) all these entropic functionals are strictly subadditive on the majorization lattice; (ii) Tsallis entropies (and therefore the Shannon entropy as well) are strictly supermodular on the majorization lattice.

## 轴子中的新量子信息视角--量子和中微子系统

**Original Title:** New quantum information perspectives in the axion--photon and neutrino systems

**Authors:** Aaditya Datar, Arun M. Thalapillil, Palak Thareja

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30419v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30419v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-28


### 摘要

在这项工作中，我们提出了轴子--量子混合的量子信息论处理方法。受一类新兴的量子增强轴子搜索的启发，我们分析了轴子-量子振荡的两级单激发部分，展示了耦合动力学如何自然地产生二部轴子-量子模式纠缠。我们详细研究了纠缠性、并发性、负性、量子互信息和不和谐、纠缠容量等后续方面，以及相应的新的和以前未解决的中微子类似物。特别是，我们强调了将最大轴子--量子纠缠与共振或强混合转换联系起来的特征，以及量子信息测量获得的极端值的不同阈值。我们研究了曼德卢斯-塔姆和马戈勒斯-莱维丁的量子速度极限的各个方面。虽然正交化仅发生在轴子-量子共振或最大中微子混合时（两个边界重合），但远离这些极限，马戈勒斯-莱维廷束缚在最大转化时饱和，而曼德卢斯-塔姆束缚通常较弱。我们还研究了轴子-光转换的纠缠量子速度极限，该极限分为衰变主导的和磁混合主导的区域，并发现它在一段时间内饱和，然后束缚变得弱。这项工作的结果确定了轴子--量子转换固有的量子资源和限制时间尺度，并将轴子现象学、中微子振荡和量子信息理论联系起来。


### Abstract

In this work, we broach a quantum information-theoretic treatment of axion--photon mixing. Motivated by an emerging class of quantum-enhanced axion searches, we analyse the two-level single-excitation sector of axion--photon oscillations, demonstrating how the coupled dynamics naturally generate bipartite axion--photon mode entanglement. We study in detail the ensuing aspects of entanglement entropy, concurrence, negativity, quantum mutual information and discord, and capacity of entanglement, and the corresponding neutrino analogues wherever novel and previously unaddressed. In particular, we highlight the characteristic features that connect maximal axion--photon entanglement to resonant or strong-mixing conversion, and the distinct thresholds for the extremal values attained by the quantum information measures. We study aspects of the Mandelstam--Tamm and Margolus--Levitin quantum speed limits for both the axion--photon and neutrino systems. While orthogonalisation occurs only at axion--photon resonance, or at maximal neutrino mixing, where the two bounds coincide, away from these limits, the Margolus--Levitin bound is saturated at maximal conversion, while the Mandelstam--Tamm bound is generally weaker. We also study an entanglement quantum speed limit for axion--photon conversion, that separates into detuning-dominated and magnetic-mixing-dominated regimes, and find that it is saturated for a period and then the bound becomes weak. The results in this work identify the quantum resources and limiting timescales intrinsic to axion--photon conversion, and connect axion phenomenology, neutrino oscillations and quantum information theory.

## 基于氦离子约瑟夫森电感的双调谐YBCO共面波导谐振器
