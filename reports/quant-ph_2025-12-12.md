### 摘要

## 量子计算机上药物发现的实用蛋白质口袋水合位点预测

**Original Title:** Practical protein-pocket hydration-site prediction for drug discovery on a quantum computer

**Authors:** Daniele Loco, Kisa Barkemeyer, Andre R. R. Carvalho, Jean-Philip Piquemal

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.08390v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.08390v2

**Submitted:** 2025-12-09


### 摘要

演示噪音中等规模量子（NISQ）硬件在计算机辅助药物发现中重复性任务中的实际用途至关重要。我们通过在量子计算机上进行三维蛋白质口袋水合位点预测来应对这一挑战。我们将水布局问题表述为二次无约束二元优化（QUBO），使用混合方法将经典的三维参考相互作用点模型（3D-Rism）与高效的量子优化求解器相结合，运行各种硬件实验，最多可达123个量子位。与经典方法的精确度相匹配，我们的结果重现了对现实生活中蛋白质-配体复合物的实验预测。此外，通过详细的资源估计分析，我们表明准确性可以随着量子位数量的增加而系统性地提高，这表明完全的量子效用触手可及。最后，我们提供了证据，表明经典优化难以提供最优解决方案的系统可以找到有利的情况。该方法有潜力协助模拟蛋白质-配体复合物，以进行药物先导优化和建立对接计算。


### Abstract

Demonstrating the practical utility of Noisy Intermediate-Scale Quantum (NISQ) hardware for recurrent tasks in Computer-Aided Drug Discovery is of paramount importance. We tackle this challenge by performing three-dimensional protein pockets hydration-site prediction on a quantum computer. Formulating the water placement problem as a Quadratic Unconstrained Binary Optimization (QUBO), we use a hybrid approach coupling a classical three-dimensional reference-interaction site model (3D-RISM) to an efficient quantum optimization solver, to run various hardware experiments up to 123 qubits. Matching the precision of classical approaches, our results reproduced experimental predictions on real-life protein-ligand complexes. Furthermore, through a detailed resource estimation analysis, we show that accuracy can be systematically improved with increasing number of qubits, indicating that full quantum utility is in reach. Finally, we provide evidence that advantageous situations could be found for systems where classical optimization struggles to provide optimal solutions. The method has potential for assisting simulations of protein-ligand complexes for drug lead optimization and setup of docking calculations.

## 用于RF光注入器应用的高OSC深紫外扭曲光发生

**Original Title:** High-OAM Deep Ultraviolet Twisted Light Generation for RF-Photoinjector Applications

**Authors:** A. S. Dyatlov, D. M. Dolgintsev, V. V. Gerasimov, V. V. Kobets, V. P. Nazmov, M. A. Nozdrin, A. N. Sergeev, D. S. Shokin, K. E. Yunenko, D. V. Karlovets

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.08442v3

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.08442v3

**Submitted:** 2025-12-09


### 摘要

我们报告了使用三种类型的制造的折射光学元件（DOE）来产生和描述具有高轨道角动量（OSC）的紫外（波长266纳米）扭曲光：反射叉栅、高电荷螺旋相板（SPP）和二元轴棱镜。所有元件都集成到电子RF光注入器的驱动激光束线中，从而能够在加速器相关条件下进行直接评估。SPP产生了高纯度拉盖尔-高斯模，OAM l = 64，测得的转换效率约为80%。二元轴子产生具有最高m = 10的准贝塞尔扭曲光，表现出低分歧和稳定的多瓣环结构。叉状格栅可靠地产生了较低次模式，l = 2-8，模拟和圆柱形镜头诊断之间具有良好的一致性。据我们所知，这些结果构成了用制造DOE生成并通过模式转换测量验证的深紫外高AM束的首次全面实验演示。所演示的技术与RF光注入器中使用的高功率紫外激光系统兼容，并为在粒子加速器设施中实现结构化光阴极照明和产生相对论涡旋电子提供了一条实用途径。


### Abstract

We report on the generation and characterization of ultraviolet (wavelength 266 nm) twisted light with high orbital angular momentum (OAM) using three types of fabricated diffractive optical elements (DOEs): a reflective fork grating, a high-charge spiral phase plate (SPP), and binary axicons. All elements were integrated into a drive-laser beamline of an electron RF-photoinjector, enabling direct evaluation under accelerator-relevant conditions. The SPP produced a high-purity Laguerre-Gaussian mode with OAM l = 64 and a measured conversion efficiency of approximately 80%. Binary axicons generated quasi-Bessel twisted light with topological charges up to m = 10, exhibiting low divergence and stable multi-lobe ring structures. The fork grating reliably produced lower-order modes, l = 2-8, with good agreement between simulations and cylindrical-lens diagnostics. These results constitute, to our knowledge, the first comprehensive experimental demonstration of deep-UV high-OAM beams generated with fabricated DOEs and validated through mode-conversion measurements. The demonstrated techniques are compatible with high-power UV laser systems used in RF-photoinjectors and offer a practical route toward structured photocathode illumination and the generation of relativistic vortex electrons at a particle accelerator facility.

## 虚级谱变形的自发去相关

**Original Title:** Spontaneous Decoherence from Imaginary-Order Spectral Deformations

**Authors:** Sridhar Tayur

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.09236v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.09236v2

**Submitted:** 2025-12-10


### 摘要

研究了自发退相干的一种机制，其中量子动力学的生成元被辅助阶（与实阶分数微积分有根本不同）光谱变形$H &{1+iβ}$所取代，用于正自伴Hamilton $H$。变形通过因子$E^{iβ}=e^{iβ\log E}$修改动力学相，其快速振荡抑制了不同能量之间的干扰。非静止相分析产生定量估计：对幅度或去相关函函数的振荡贡献至少衰减为$\mathCal{O}（1/|β|)$.量子力学的运动学结构--Hilbert空间内积、投影操作符和Born规则--保持不变;这种修改完全是动态的，并且仅通过谱相起作用。   变形的物理动机源于时钟不完善、引入log谱项的重正化（群和有效）作用修正，以及半经典重力分析（其中复杂作用产生形式为$E &{iβ}$的谱因子）。该机制在与量子引力启发的量子力学相关的例子中得到了说明。   详细的相关工作分析将目前的机制与米尔伯恩型本征退相关、Diósi-Penrose引力坍缩、GRW/ESL模型、时钟引起的退相关和节能坍缩模型以及环境框架进行了对比，例如林德布拉德主方程、卡尔代拉-莱格特浴和非埃尔米特汉密尔顿变形。这将$H &{1+iβ}$动力学定位为量子引力激励的有效理论中产生的log谱修正的紧凑、可测试且真正新颖的现象学封装，同时保持与标准量子运动学完全兼容。


### Abstract

A mechanism of spontaneous decoherence is examined in which the generator of quantum dynamics is replaced by the imaginary-order (which is fundamentally different from real-order fractional calculus) spectral deformation $H^{1+iβ}$ for a positive self-adjoint Hamiltonian $H$. The deformation modifies dynamical phases through the factor $E^{iβ}=e^{iβ\log E}$, whose rapid oscillation suppresses interference between distinct energies. A non-stationary-phase analysis yields quantitative estimates: oscillatory contributions to amplitudes or decoherence functionals decay at least as $\mathcal{O}(1/|β|)$. The kinematical structure of quantum mechanics -- the Hilbert-space inner product, projection operators, and the Born rule -- remains unchanged; the modification is entirely dynamical and acts only through spectral phases.
  Physical motivations for the deformation arise from clock imperfections, renormalization--group and effective--action corrections that introduce logarithmic spectral terms, and semiclassical gravity analyses in which complex actions produce spectral factors of the form $E^{iβ}$. The mechanism is illustrated in examples relevant to quantum-gravity-inspired quantum mechanics.
  A detailed related-work analysis contrasts the present mechanism with Milburn-type intrinsic decoherence, Diósi-Penrose gravitational collapse, GRW/CSL models, clock-induced decoherence, and energy-conserving collapse models, as well as environmental frameworks such as Lindblad master equations, Caldeira-Leggett baths, and non-Hermitian Hamiltonian deformations. This positions $H^{1+iβ}$ dynamics as a compact, testable, and genuinely novel phenomenological encapsulation of logarithmic spectral corrections arising in quantum-gravity- motivated effective theories, while remaining fully compatible with standard quantum kinematics.

## 量子资源的深度热化和测量

**Authors:** Naga Dileep Varikuti, Soumik Bandyopadhyay, Philipp Hauke

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.09999v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.09999v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-10


### 摘要

量子资源理论（QRT）为描述受物理约束的有用量子现象提供了统一的框架，但众所周知，在实验系统中很难评估。在这封信中，我们引入了一个统一的协议，用于量化适用于多个QT的任意量子进化的资源生成能力（RGP）。它基于深度热化（DT），它最近因其在部分投影测量量子状态设计出现中的作用而受到关注。我们方法的核心是使用最近用于探测DT的投影集合，以及新的旋转身份，使我们能够直接推断潜在动态的RGP。这些身份进一步揭示了资源如何在通用量子电路中积累和热化。最后，我们表明量子资源本身在子系统层面经历了深度热化，为推断RGP提供了一种补充的、另一种实验上可访问的路径。我们的工作将深度热化与资源量化联系起来，为各种资源在生成状态设计中的重要作用提供了新的视角。


### Abstract

Quantum resource theories (QRTs) provide a unified framework for characterizing useful quantum phenomena subject to physical constraints, but are notoriously hard to assess in experimental systems. Finally, we show that quantum resources themselves undergo deep thermalization at the subsystem level, offering a complementary and another experimentally accessible route to infer the RGP. Our work connects deep thermalization to resource quantification, offering a new perspective on the essential role of various resources in generating state designs.

## 物理模型和广义上下文的统一线性代数框架

**Original Title:** A Unified Linear Algebraic Framework for Physical Models and Generalized Contextuality

**Authors:** Farid Shahandeh, Theodoros Yianni, Mina Doosti

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10000v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.10000v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-10


### 摘要

我们开发了一个自下而上，概率优先的框架中，一个操作理论的全部概率内容被编码在其矩阵的事件的条件结果概率（COPE）。在此设置中，五个模型类（preGPTs，GPTs，准概率，本体论，和noncontextual本体论）被统一为COPE矩阵的约束因子分解。我们确定equirank因子分解的结构核心的GPT和非上下文本体模型，并建立其关系，断层摄影的完整性。这产生了一个简单的，模型不可知的标准noncontextuality：一个操作理论承认一个noncontextual本体模型，当且仅当它的COPE矩阵承认一个equirank非负矩阵分解（ENMF）。因此，所有实体论模型中平分条件的失败建立了上下文性。我们通过线性代数框架提供的两种补充方法来操作等级分离。首先，我们使用ENMF将非上下文实体模型解释为嵌套的多表位。这使我们能够确定盒子世界运营理论是存在实体背景的。其次，我们应用离散数学的技术来推导出表现稀疏模式的COPE矩阵的实体维度的下限，并使用这个界限来建立一个新的证明，证明量子位理论的离散版本表现出实体语境。通过将语境重新定义为矩阵分析中的一个问题，我们的工作为其系统研究提供了统一的结构，并为探索非经典资源开辟了新的途径。


### Abstract

We develop a bottom-up, statistics-first framework in which the full probabilistic content of an operational theory is encoded in its matrix of conditional outcome probabilities of events (COPE). Within this setting, five model classes (preGPTs, GPTs, quasiprobabilistic, ontological, and noncontextual ontological) are unified as constrained factorizations of the COPE matrix. We identify equirank factorizations as the structural core of GPTs and noncontextual ontological models and establish their relation to tomographic completeness. This yields a simple, model-agnostic criterion for noncontextuality: an operational theory admits a noncontextual ontological model if and only if its COPE matrix admits an equirank nonnegative matrix factorization (ENMF). Failure of the equirank condition in all ontological models therefore establishes contextuality. We operationalize rank separation via two complementary methods provided by the linear-algebraic framework. First, we use ENMF to interpret noncontextual ontological models as nested polytopes. This allows us to establish that the boxworld operational theory is ontologically contextual. Second, we apply techniques from discrete mathematics to derive a lower bound on the ontological dimensionality of COPE matrices exhibiting sparsity patterns, and use this bound to establish a new proof that a discrete version of qubit theory exhibits ontological contextuality. By reframing contextuality as a problem in matrix analysis, our work provides a unified structure for its systematic study and opens new avenues for exploring nonclassical resources.

## 通过瞬时条件和无条件重置在开放量子系统中实现普遍松弛加速

**Original Title:** Universal relaxation speedup in open quantum systems through transient conditional and unconditional resetting

**Authors:** Parvinder Solanki, Igor Lesanovsky, Gabriele Perfetto

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10005v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.10005v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-10


### 摘要

加速多体量子系统的松弛动力学在多种情况下都很重要，包括量子计算和状态准备。我们证明，这种加速可以通过瞬时随机重置来普遍实现。这意味着，在有限持续时间的初始时间间隔内，动态被重置中断，重置将系统在随机选择的时间进入指定状态。我们针对少体开放系统以及具有挑战性的多体情况来说明这个想法，其中一级相转变会导致松弛时间的分歧。在所有情况下，都会观察到达到稳态的显着、有时甚至呈指数级加速，类似于所谓的姆彭巴效应。这种加速的普遍本质在于，重置协议的设计仅需要了解目标状态的一些宏观属性，例如相转变的序参数，而不需要对初始状态进行任何微调操纵。


### Abstract

Speeding up the relaxation dynamics of many-body quantum systems is important in a variety of contexts, including quantum computation and state preparation. We demonstrate that such acceleration can be universally achieved via transient stochastic resetting. This means that during an initial time interval of finite duration, the dynamics is interrupted by resets that take the system to a designated state at randomly selected times. We illustrate this idea for few-body open systems and also for a challenging many-body case, where a first-order phase transition leads to a divergence of relaxation time. In all scenarios, a significant and sometimes even exponential acceleration in reaching the stationary state is observed, similar to the so-called Mpemba effect. The universal nature of this speedup lies in the fact that the design of the resetting protocol only requires knowledge of a few macroscopic properties of the target state, such as the order parameter of the phase transition, while it does not necessitate any fine-tuned manipulation of the initial state.
