## 交战背景--可证明的古典与可证明的非古典

**Original Title:** Warring Contextualities -- Provably Classical vs Provably Nonclassical

**Authors:** Enrico Bozzetto, Jonte R. Hance

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14319v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.14319v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-15


### 摘要

在文献中，语境有两种不同的定义：科亨和斯佩克的定义，以及斯佩肯斯的定义（或“概括”）。然而，使用其中一个定义的研究人员很少考虑另一个定义，这意味着对这两个概念的比较分析很少见。在本文中，我们提出了这样的观点：科兴-斯佩克语境性提供了系统从根本上来说是非经典的思想的概括，而斯佩克肯斯的非语境性提供了系统是经典的思想的概括。这使我们能够调和这两种方法，将其视为古典性/非古典性层次结构中的不同阶段。


### Abstract

In the literature, there are two differing definitions of contextuality: Kochen and Specker's, and Spekkens' (or ``generalised''). However, researchers using one of these definitions rarely consider the other, meaning comparative analysis of these two notions is rare. In this paper, we advance the idea that Kochen-Specker contextuality provides a generalisation of the idea of system being fundamentally nonclassical, while Spekkens' noncontextuality provides a generalisation of the idea of a system being classical. This allows us to reconcile the two approaches, as different stages in a hierarchy of classicality/nonclassicality.

## 可调谐超辐射频率梳的观察

**Original Title:** Observation of Tunable Superradiant Frequency Combs

**Authors:** Tian Xie, Rikuto Fukumori, Wai-Keong Mok, Jiahui Li, Joonhee Choi, Andrei Faraon

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14601v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.14601v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-16


### 摘要

量子发射器与共振器耦合的腔量子电动力学（QED）为工程光物质相互作用和探索集体现象提供了一个强大的平台。特别是，由发射体之间集体量子干涉产生的超辐射已被探索为获得超稳定连续辐射的途径。然而，在时间域中工程超辐射以实现周期性脉冲源或频率梳在很大程度上仍然没有探索。在这里，我们研究了耦合到片上超导共振器的驱动旋转系综的非平衡多体动力学，并揭示了从连续波到周期性脉冲超辐射发射的动态相转变。为了定量捕获观察到的动力学相，我们引入了一个驱动-消散腔-QED模型，该模型解释了周期性脉冲超辐射相如何从集体的、周期性重复的旋转动力学中出现，该动力学由同性增长、无序和消散的相互作用稳定。我们还发现，同时表现出光学和微波跳变的稀土离子旋转系统能够在微波和光学域中实现相同步的双轨超辐射频率梳。我们的结果不仅为量子计量和信息处理中的双轨频率梳应用开辟了新的途径，而且还建立了周期性脉冲超辐射与连续时间晶体作为驱动开放系统中新型非平衡相的出现之间的基本联系。


### Abstract

Cavity quantum electrodynamics (QED) with quantum emitters coupled to resonators provides a powerful platform for engineering light-matter interactions and exploring collective phenomena. In particular, superradiance, arising from collective quantum interference among emitters, has been explored as a route to ultrastable continuous radiation. However, engineering superradiance in the time domain to realize periodic pulsed sources or frequency combs remains largely unexplored. Here, we investigate the non-equilibrium many-body dynamics of a driven spin ensemble coupled to an on-chip superconducting resonator and uncover a dynamical phase transition from continuous-wave to periodic pulsed superradiant emission. To quantitatively capture the observed dynamical phases, we introduce a driven-dissipative cavity-QED model that elucidates how the periodic pulsed superradiant phase emerges from collective, periodically repeating spin dynamics stabilized by the interplay of coherence growth, disorder, and dissipation. We also find that rare-earth ion spin systems exhibiting both optical and microwave transitions enable phase-synchronized, dual-rail superradiant frequency combs in both the microwave and optical domains. Our results not only open new avenues for dual-rail frequency-comb applications in quantum metrology and information processing, but also establish a fundamental connection between periodic pulsed superradiance and the emergence of a continuous time crystal as a novel nonequilibrium phase in driven open systems.

## LiDMaS+中混合连续变量和离散变量量子误差纠正的统一硬件到解码器架构

**Original Title:** A Unified Hardware-to-Decoder Architecture for Hybrid Continuous-Variable and Discrete-Variable Quantum Error Correction in LiDMaS+

**Authors:** Dennis Delali Kwesi Wayo, Chinonso Onah, Leonardo Goliatt, Sven Groppe

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15389v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.15389v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-16


### 摘要

我们提出了一个架构级硬件到逻辑到解码器的执行栈，用于LiDMaS+中的混合连续变量和离散变量量子错误纠正。提供商原生记录被标准化为单个解码器IO合同，并在MWPM、UF、BP和neural-MWPM之间的固定控制下重播。在使用固定装置输入和采样公共数据集的Xanadu案例研究中，重播完整性是完整的：108/108固定装置和4000/4000真实切片请求响应行，请求解析错误为零，响应解析错误为零，解码器名称不匹配为零。在匹配的输入下，解码器行为显然取决于制度。对于加权固定装置总结，平均翻转计数为1.296（MWPM）、1.296（UF）、0.667（BP）和1.296（神经-MWPM）。对于加权真实数据总结，平均翻转计数为0.641（MWPM）、0.741（UF）、0.318（BP）和0.641（神经-MWPM）;相应的非空翻转率为0.490、0.490、0.318和0.490。在固定装置数据中，BP相对于MWPM减少了48.6%;在真实切片中，BP相对于MWPM减少了50.4%，相对于UF减少了57.1%。质量控制显示了核心的可解释性权衡：BP的干预是保守的，但留下了更高的剩余负担，而MWPM系列解码器的干预更积极，并清除了更多的综合症。警告无综合症率保持解码器不变和水平网驱动（固定装置加权0.259;实加权0.510），确认了从硬件输入到逻辑纠正保留的稀疏性语义。重新运行的分析阶段再现了相同的SHA-256工件，从而实现了确定性研究迭代。这些结果为面向PhotoGKP的硬件程序建立了实用的基准测试基础，其中必须根据操作制度来选择解码器策略。


### Abstract

We present an architecture-level hardware-to-logical-to-decoder execution stack for hybrid continuous-variable and discrete-variable quantum error correction in LiDMaS+. Provider-native records are normalized into a single decoder IO contract and replayed under fixed controls across MWPM, UF, BP, and neural-MWPM. In a Xanadu case study using fixture inputs and sampled public datasets, replay integrity was complete: 108/108 fixture and 4000/4000 real-slice request-response lines, with zero request-parse errors, zero response-parse errors, and zero decoder-name mismatches. Under matched inputs, decoder behavior is clearly regime-dependent. For weighted fixture summaries, average flip count was 1.296 (MWPM), 1.296 (UF), 0.667 (BP), and 1.296 (neural-MWPM). For weighted real-data summaries, average flip count was 0.641 (MWPM), 0.741 (UF), 0.318 (BP), and 0.641 (neural-MWPM); corresponding nonempty-flip rates were 0.490, 0.490, 0.318, and 0.490. Across fixture data, BP reduced weighted correction volume by 48.6\% versus MWPM; across real slices, BP reduced weighted correction volume by 50.4\% versus MWPM and 57.1\% versus UF. Quality controls show the central interpretability tradeoff: BP is intervention-conservative but leaves higher residual burden, while MWPM-family decoders intervene more aggressively and clear more syndrome. Warning-no-syndrome rates remained decoder-invariant and dataset-driven (fixture weighted 0.259; real weighted 0.510), confirming preserved sparsity semantics from hardware input to logical correction. Re-running analysis stages reproduced identical SHA-256 artifacts, enabling deterministic study iteration. These results establish a practical benchmarking foundation for photonic GKP-oriented hardware programs where decoder policy must be selected as a function of operating regime.

## 序列量子状态识别的投影动态规划

**Original Title:** Projected Dynamic Programming for Sequential Quantum State Discrimination

**Authors:** Jaehun Jeong, Donghwa Ji, Hyunjun Jang, Kabgyun Jeong

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15393v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.15393v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-16


### 摘要

顺序量子状态辨别（SQSD）可以自然地被定义为顺序决策问题：在每个时间步，代理人必须决定是执行额外的测量来收集更多信息，还是根据当前信念得出最佳决策。在本文中，我们正式将SQSD转换为静态隐藏状态部分可观察马尔科夫决策过程（POMDP）框架。我们证明，这种公式准确地将传统的最小错误识别（MED）方案纳入了特殊的一步情况。此外，我们将常规的基于网格的离散化应用于连续信念单形，并使用有限库逼近可能连续的测量空间。然后，我们为由此产生的错误提供严格的数学界限，并分析离线规划和在线执行的计算复杂性。我们的分析证实，准确性和复杂性之间固有的权衡，以及有关假设数量的维度诅咒，在量子体系中也显着地观察到。最后，我们提供了二元状态辨别的工作示例，以推导出各种函数的显式形式，并对三重状态辨别进行了数值模拟，以可视化我们基于POMDP的SQSD的序列结构。


### Abstract

Sequential Quantum State Discrimination (SQSD) can be naturally framed as a sequential decision-making problem: at each time step, an agent must decide whether to perform an additional measurement to gather more information or to conclude with an optimal decision based on the current belief. In this paper, we formally cast SQSD into a static-hidden-state Partially Observable Markov Decision Process (POMDP) framework. We demonstrate that this formulation precisely subsumes the conventional minimum-error discrimination (MED) scheme as a special one-step case. Furthermore, we apply a regular grid-based discretization to the continuous belief simplex and approximate the possibly continuous measurement space using a finite library. Then we provide rigorous mathematical bounds on the resulting errors and analyze the computational complexity for both offline planning and online execution. Our analysis confirms that the inherent trade-off between accuracy and complexity, as well as the curse of dimensionality regarding the number of hypotheses, are also prominently observed in the quantum regime. Finally, we provide a working example of binary state discrimination to derive explicit forms of various functions and present numerical simulations for trine state discrimination to visualize the sequential structure of our POMDP-based SQSD.

## 研究hBN中机械隔离量子发射体的光谱动力学和旋转特征

**Original Title:** Investigating Spectral Dynamics and Spin Signatures of a Mechanically Isolated Quantum Emitter in hBN

**Authors:** Sajedeh Shahbazi, Alexander Pachl, Kathrin Schwer, Patrick Maier, Alexander Kubanek

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14998v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.14998v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-16


### 摘要

六方CBN中机械隔离的缺陷中心是有前途的相干量子发射体，但光谱不稳定性仍然存在，其与旋转相关的性质仍不清楚。在这里，我们研究了集成在共面导体上的hBN中的单个机械隔离量子发射器。发射器表现出异常明亮的共振荧光，饱和计数率超过$10\，\mathrm {Mc/s}$。高分辨率光谱揭示了源自同一缺陷复合体的两个紧密间隔的零音素线转变。时间分辨光谱表明，这些转变表现出明显不同的光谱扩散动力学，与不同的供体-受体-对样重组途径一致，对局部静电波动具有不同的敏感性。非共振蓝色照明在两个转变之间重新分配发射，并增加发射占空比，而不会显着改变低温下的主要光谱扩散率，这表明从长寿命搁置状态重新抽运。磁场相关的光致发光、光学检测的磁共振和泵探测测量揭示了毫秒级的驰豫动力学和磁场相关的荧光对比度，证明了亚稳搁置状态下的旋转相关粒子群动力学。这些结果阐明了电荷驱动的光谱波动和依赖于旋转的搁置如何共同塑造光循环动力学。


### Abstract

Mechanically isolated defect centers in hexagonal boron nitride are promising coherent quantum emitters, yet spectral instabilities persist, and their spin-related nature remains unclear. Here we investigate a single mechanically isolated quantum emitter in hBN integrated onto a coplanar waveguide. The emitter exhibits exceptionally bright resonant fluorescence with saturation count rates exceeding $10\,\mathrm{Mc/s}$. High-resolution spectroscopy reveals two closely spaced zero-phonon-line transitions originating from the same defect complex. Time-resolved spectroscopy shows that these transitions exhibit markedly different spectral diffusion dynamics, consistent with distinct donor-acceptor-pair-like recombination pathways with different sensitivities to local electrostatic fluctuations. Off-resonant blue illumination redistributes emission between the two transitions and increases the emission duty cycle without significantly modifying the dominant spectral diffusion rates at low temperature, indicating repumping from long-lived shelving states. Magnetic-field-dependent photoluminescence, optically detected magnetic resonance, and pump-probe measurements reveal millisecond-scale relaxation dynamics and magnetic-field-dependent fluorescence contrast, demonstrating spin-dependent population dynamics in the metastable shelving state. These results clarify how charge-driven spectral fluctuations and spin-dependent shelving jointly shape the optical cycling dynamics.

## 从紧凑描述具体化稳定器状态和Clifford门的最佳算法

**Original Title:** Optimal algorithms for materializing stabilizer states and Clifford gates from compact descriptions

**Authors:** Hyunho Cha, Jungwoo Lee

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15405v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.15405v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-16


### 摘要

稳定器状态允许紧凑的经典描述，但许多下游任务仍然需要它们的全幅度载体。由于输出本身的大小为$2 ' n $，主要的算法问题是是否可以在最佳$O（2 ' n）$时间内实现$n$-量子位稳定器状态载体，而不是支付额外的多项费用。我们对这个问题的回答是肯定的。从稳定器状态的标准二次形式表示开始，我们给出了一个在$O（2 ' n）$时间和$O（2#n）$空间中运行的算法。其想法是维护一个缓存的宇称字，该字同时记录所有未来的非对角线二次阶段增量。因此，我们获得了从标准检验矩阵描述具体化稳定器状态载体的最佳过程，以及将Clifford画面扩展到其全稠密矩阵的最佳算法。这些结果缩小了稠密稳定剂和Clifford物化的渐进差距。


### Abstract

Stabilizer states admit compact classical descriptions, but many downstream tasks still require their full amplitude vectors. Since the output itself has size $2^n$, the main algorithmic question is whether one can materialize an $n$-qubit stabilizer state vector in optimal $O(2^n)$ time, rather than paying an additional polynomial overhead. We answer this question in the affirmative. Starting from the standard quadratic-form representation of stabilizer states, we give an algorithm that runs in $O(2^n)$ time and $O(2^n)$ space. The idea is to maintain a cached parity word that records all future off-diagonal quadratic phase increments simultaneously. As consequences, we obtain an optimal procedure for materializing a stabilizer state vector from a standard check-matrix description, and an optimal algorithm for expanding a Clifford tableau into its full dense matrix. These results close the asymptotic gap for dense stabilizer and Clifford materialization.

## 量子核方法中推理的最佳算法复杂度

**Original Title:** Optimal algorithmic complexity of inference in quantum kernel methods

**Authors:** Elies Gil-Fuster, Seongwook Shin, Sofiene Jerbi, Jens Eisert, Maximilian J. Kramer

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15214v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.15214v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-16


### 摘要

量子核方法是在监督学习中实现量子优势的主要候选方法之一。一个关键瓶颈是推理成本：在新数据上评估训练模型需要估计$N$个核值的加权和$\sum_{i=1}' N a_i k（x，x_i）$到加性精度$\varepð $，其中$a $是训练系数的载体。标准方法通过抽样独立估计每个项，产生的查询复杂性为$O（N\lVert a\rVert_2#2/\varepsilon#2）$。在这项工作中，我们确定了两个独立的改进轴：（1）如何估计单个核值（抽样与量子幅度估计），以及（2）如何估计总和（逐项与通过单个观察值），并系统地分析其所有组合。查询最优组合将完整推断和编码为单个可观察值的期望值并应用量子幅度估计，实现了$O（\lVert a\rVert_1/\varepðs）$的查询复杂性，消除了对$N$的依赖性，并在$\lVert a\rVert_1 $和$\varepðs $中产生二次改进。我们证明了$Q（\lVert a\rVert_1/\varepð）$的匹配下界，从而建立了我们方法的查询最优性，直至对数因子。除了查询复杂性之外，我们还分析了这些改进如何转化为入口成本，并表明从入口复杂性的角度来看，查询最优策略在实践中并不总是最优的。我们的结果根据硬件能力提供了查询最优算法和实际最优的策略选择，以及指导从业者的完整中间方法。所有算法仅需要幅度估计作为子程序，因此是早期故障容忍实现的自然候选者。


### Abstract

Quantum kernel methods are among the leading candidates for achieving quantum advantage in supervised learning. A key bottleneck is the cost of inference: evaluating a trained model on new data requires estimating a weighted sum $\sum_{i=1}^N α_i k(x,x_i)$ of $N$ kernel values to additive precision $\varepsilon$, where $α$ is the vector of trained coefficients. The standard approach estimates each term independently via sampling, yielding a query complexity of $O(N\lVertα\rVert_2^2/\varepsilon^2)$. In this work, we identify two independent axes for improvement: (1) How individual kernel values are estimated (sampling versus quantum amplitude estimation), and (2) how the sum is approximated (term-by-term versus via a single observable), and systematically analyze all combinations thereof. The query-optimal combination, encoding the full inference sum as the expectation value of a single observable and applying quantum amplitude estimation, achieves a query complexity of $O(\lVertα\rVert_1/\varepsilon)$, removing the dependence on $N$ from the query count and yielding a quadratic improvement in both $\lVertα\rVert_1$ and $\varepsilon$. We prove a matching lower bound of $Ω(\lVertα\rVert_1/\varepsilon)$, establishing query-optimality of our approach up to logarithmic factors. Beyond query complexity, we also analyze how these improvements translate into gate costs and show that the query-optimal strategy is not always optimal in practice from the perspective of gate complexity. Our results provide both a query-optimal algorithm and a practically optimal choice of strategy depending on hardware capabilities, along with a complete landscape of intermediate methods to guide practitioners. All algorithms require only amplitude estimation as a subroutine and are thus natural candidates for early-fault-tolerant implementations.

## 共形量子力学中的重正化和非微扰动力学

## 太赫兹希格斯-极化子的量子统计中的超强耦合特征

**Original Title:** Ultrastrong Coupling Signatures in Photon Statistics from Terahertz Higgs-Polaritons

**Authors:** Spenser Talkington, Benjamin Kass, Martin Claassen

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15417v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.15417v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-16


### 摘要

腔量子和量子材料的超强耦合机制已成为修改材料性质的一种途径，但超强耦合的明确特征仍然难以捉摸。我们重点关注通过嵌入腔体的超导体传输的光的量子统计，表明强耦合的双量子希格斯极化子在单太赫量子水平上实现了量子非线性。我们发现，随着光-物质耦合的增加，由于形成具有有限光占有率的混合光-物质暗腔状态，因此量子统计数据显示出显着的变化，产生了超强耦合的可测试特征。我们推导出一个非马尔科夫输入输出关系，并研究腔嵌入式超导体2 H-NBSe 2，因为它接近超强光物质耦合。我们的结果揭示了双量子符合统计中的超强耦合的诊断，而总计数中不存在。


### Abstract

Our results reveal a diagnostic for ultrastrong coupling in the two-photon coincidence statistics that is absent in total counts.

## 仅限单态始终开启无间隙交换（SAGE）旋转量子位：电荷噪音效应和双量子位门

**Original Title:** Singlet-only always-on gapless exchange (SAGE) spin qubits: Charge noise effects and two-qubit gates

**Authors:** Nathan L. Foulk, Katharina Laubscher, Silas Hoffman, Sankar Das Sarma

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15419v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.15419v1
