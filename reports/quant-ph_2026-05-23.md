## 基于诱饵状态的时间同步

**Original Title:** Decoy State based Time Synchronization

**Authors:** Lukas Tiefenthaler, Hannah Thiel, Davide Rusca, Antia Lamas Linares

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20857v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.20857v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-20


### 摘要

时间同步是量子密钥分发（QKD）8协议中的关键要求，通过正确分配原始密钥的位来确保准确的密钥生成，9通过精确记录量子统计数据来实现窃听检测。最先进的10实验通常使用额外的通道通过经典信号同步发射机和接收机11的时钟。在这项工作中，我们研究了通过用于密钥生成的信号执行时钟同步12的可能性，这些信号已经存在于基于诱饵状态的BB 8413协议中。14在不以任何方式改变协议的情况下，我们使用15个信号和诱饵状态的不同平均量子数进行时间同步，而无需能够实现16个时钟同步的专用物理通道。所提出的方法仅依赖于17密钥生成中发送和接收的量子，并且不需要对QKD协议进行任何更改。18实验中唯一的变化是在软件层面，因此实现起来非常简单。19我们在特定基于光纤的QKD 20实验的模拟中演示了时钟同步方法。与其他基于诱饵状态的BB 84协议一样，它基于弱相关脉冲。21在此模拟中，我们研究参数空间以找到我们22提出的方法的限制和最佳选择。23除了非协议改变时钟同步方法之外，我们还讨论了一种24方法，该方法通过引入具有非常高平均值的额外25诱饵状态来显着提高有损通道中的性能量子数。26通过消除对能够进行时钟同步的额外通道的需要，提出的两种方法27都有可能降低QKD系统的复杂性和成本，并提高其敏捷性


### Abstract

Time synchronization is a crucial requirement in quantum key distribution (QKD)8 protocols, ensuring accurate key generation via the correct assignment of bits of raw key and9 enabling eavesdropping detection via the precise recording of photon statistics. State-of-the-art10 experiments typically use an extra channel to synchronize the clocks of the transmitter and receiver11 via classical signals. In this work, we study the possibility of performing clock synchronization12 via the signals used for the key generation, which are already present in decoy-state-based BB8413 protocols.14 Without altering the protocol in any way, we use the different mean photon numbers of the15 signal and decoy states for time synchronization without a dedicated physical channel capable of16 clock synchronization. The proposed method relies only on the photons sent and received for17 key generation and does not require any change to the QKD protocol. The only change in the18 experiment is on the software level, thus making it very simple to implement.19 We demonstrate clock synchronization method in a simulation of a specific fiber-based QKD20 experiment. Like other decoy-state-based BB84 protocols, it is based on weak coherent pulses.21 In this simulation, we investigate the parameter space to find limits and optimal choices of our22 proposed method.23 In addition to the non-protocol-altering clock synchronization method, we also discuss an24 approach that significantly improves performance in lossy channels by introducing an additional25 decoy state with a very high mean photon number.26 By eliminating the need for an extra channel capable of clock synchronization, both methods27 proposed potentially reduce the complexity and cost of QKD systems and improve their agility

## 里德伯离子晶体定制分子势中的非平衡激子动力学

**Original Title:** Non-equilibrium exciton dynamics in tailored molecular potentials of Rydberg ion crystals

**Authors:** Simon Euchner, Mathias B. M. Svendsen, Igor Lesanovsky

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21250v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21250v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-20


### 摘要

被激发到高电子状态的俘获离子将强耦合的集体振动和电子自由度与远程粒子间相互作用结合起来。这些成分使得能够量子模拟与非微扰参数区域中激子的动力学相关的生化过程。这种量子模拟器的关键特征是可以强耦合的电子状态相关分子势面。这使得我们能够揭示激子传输背后的各种机制。我们在由三个俘获离子组成的系统中说明了这一点，该系统适合从头算处理。鉴于可以用数百个离子常规制备离子陷阱，这些量子模拟器可以立即实现当前数值方法无法实现的场景。


### Abstract

Trapped ions excited to high-lying electronic states combine strongly coupled collective vibrational and electronic degrees of freedom with long-ranged interparticle interactions. These ingredients enable the quantum simulation of biochemical processes, associated with the dynamics of excitons in non-perturbative parameter regimes. The key feature of such a quantum simulator are electronic-state-dependent molecular potential surfaces which can be strongly coupled. This allows to shed light on a variety of mechanisms underlying exciton transport. We illustrate this in a system of three trapped ions, which is amenable to an ab initio treatment. Given that ion traps can be routinely prepared with hundreds of ions, these quantum simulators can immediately realise scenarios which are inaccessible by current numerical methods.

## 最佳Quantum Feshbach发动机

**Original Title:** Optimal Quantum Feshbach Engines

**Authors:** Aaron Wandhammer, Vincent Hardel, Paul-Antoine Hervieux, Giovanni Manfredi

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21562v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21562v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-20


### 摘要

我们开发了一个用俘获玻色-爱因斯坦凝聚体实现的高效量子循环的优化框架，其控制参数是陷阱硬度和通过费什巴赫共振调谐的相互作用强度。循环中每个行程的最佳驱动协议是从凝结动力学的变分描述结合尼尔森随机量子化来获得的，该量子量子化将量子进化映射到有效的奥恩斯坦-乌伦贝克过程。最佳协议是通过最小化用户定义的成本函数来获得的，该函数选择了协议持续时间和任意物理约束（例如消耗的工作或接近隔热进化）之间的最佳权衡，并在重复循环中表现出显着的稳定性。该方法还为通用非线性薛定格方程提供了最优控制的系统途径，为非线性光学、量子流体和量子等离子体等各个领域的最优控制策略铺平了道路。


### Abstract

We develop an optimization framework for high-efficiency quantum cycles implemented with a trapped Bose-Einstein condensate, whose control parameters are the trap stiffness and the interaction strength tuned via a Feshbach resonance. Optimal driving protocols for each stroke of the cycle are obtained from a variational description of the condensate dynamics combined with Nelson's stochastic quantization, which maps the quantum evolution onto an effective Ornstein-Uhlenbeck process. The optimal protocol is obtained by minimizing a user-defined cost functional that selects the best trade-off between protocol duration and arbitrary physical constraints (such as the expended work or the proximity to an adiabatic evolution), and exhibits remarkable stability over repeated cycles. The method also provides a systematic route to optimal control for generic nonlinear Schrödinger equations, paving the way to optimal control strategies in fields as diverse as nonlinear optics, quantum fluids, and quantum plasmas.

## 任意本征态和多输出的量子纯度放大

**Original Title:** Quantum Purity Amplification for Arbitrary Eigenstates and Multiple Outputs

**Authors:** Zhaoyi Li, Elias Theil, Aram W. Harrow, Isaac Chuang

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21570v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21570v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-20


### 摘要

量子纯度放大（QPA）是将混合态的$n$副本连贯地转换为所选本征态的高保真副本的任务。我们在$n$输入副本、$m$输出副本、任意目标本征态、任意局部维度$d$和通用输入谱的一般设置下求解QPA。我们描述了最佳渠道的特征，并推导出其跨输出机制的全站点和单站点性能定律。对于渐进分析，我们使用路径图参数化来表明，当目标特征值具有恒定的谱间隙$D_{k，\mathrm{min}}$时，实现全站点错误$\varepð $需要独立于$d$的输入副本数量并缩放为$O（m/（\varepðD_{k，\mathrm{min}}）$。当$m/n$接近一个常数时，性能表现出类相机制，我们明确描述了这一机制。对于非渐进分析，我们开发了一种广义杨图理论，该理论产生了紧的样本复杂性界限，并为最佳QPA提供了第一个维度一致保证。我们还提供了最佳协议的渐进高效实现。总而言之，这些结果将QPA确立为具有维度均匀样本复杂性的连贯量子信息处理的严格示例，为后续工作中开发的连贯-非连贯分离提供了技术基础。


### Abstract

Quantum purity amplification (QPA) is the task of coherently transforming $n$ copies of a mixed state into high-fidelity copies of a chosen eigenstate. We solve QPA in the general setting of $n$ input copies, $m$ output copies, arbitrary target eigenstates, arbitrary local dimension $d$, and generic input spectra. We characterize the optimal channel and derive its all-site and one-site performance laws across output regimes. For the asymptotic analysis, we use a path-graph parametrization to show that, when the target eigenvalue has a constant spectral gap $D_{k,\mathrm{min}}$, achieving all-site error $\varepsilon$ requires a number of input copies independent of $d$ and scaling as $O(m/(\varepsilon D_{k,\mathrm{min}}^2))$. When $m/n$ approaches a constant, the performance exhibits phase-like regimes, which we characterize explicitly. For the nonasymptotic analysis, we develop a theory of generalized Young diagrams that yields tight sample complexity bounds and provides the first dimension-uniform guarantee for optimal QPA. We also provide asymptotically efficient implementations of the optimal protocol. Together, these results establish QPA as a rigorous example of coherent quantum information processing with dimension-uniform sample complexity, supplying the technical foundation for the coherent-incoherent separation developed in the companion work.

## 代数局部性和不可逆高斯定律

**Original Title:** Algebraic locality and non-invertible Gauss laws

**Authors:** Nicholas Holfester, Jonathan Sorce

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21591v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21591v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-20


### 摘要

我们研究了2+1D闭格子上存在不可逆对称高斯定律的代数定域性原理。arXiv：2509.03589中的先前工作表明，当执行可逆对称的高斯定律时，“哈格二元性”原理被准确保留，并且在适当处理离散性伪影后，“不相交可加性”被保留。在这里，我们表明，对于一大类不可逆的现场对称性，哈格二元性仅在足够好的、“无尖点”的区域中才被完全保留。对于尖角区域，我们有一种弱形式的哈格二元性，需要添加项圈。我们的结果适用于具有纯磁性约束的双模型，以及由霍普夫代数的现场作用引起的更一般的约束框架。特别是，我们显式地处理了一类扩展的串网模型。我们还证明了基于群的双模型的不相交可加性，以及在一般霍普夫代数的背景下不相交可加性的一种减弱形式。


### Abstract

We study algebraic locality principles on a 2+1D closed lattice in the presence of a Gauss law for a non-invertible symmetry. Prior work in arXiv:2509.03589 showed that when enforcing the Gauss law of an invertible symmetry, the principle of "Haag duality" is preserved exactly, and "disjoint additivity" is preserved after appropriate treatment of discreteness artifacts. Here we show that for a large class of non-invertible on-site symmetries, Haag duality is preserved exactly only for sufficiently nice, "cuspless" regions. For cusped regions, we instead have a weak form of Haag duality that requires adding a collar. Our results apply to double models with a purely magnetic constraint, and to the more general framework of constraints induced by the on-site action of a Hopf algebra. In particular, we treat a class of extended string-net models explicitly. We also demonstrate disjoint additivity for double models based on a group, and a weakened form of disjoint additivity in the setting of a general Hopf algebra.

## 测试探测器轨迹的叠加

**Original Title:** Testing Superpositions of Detector Trajectories

**Authors:** Cisco Gooding, Taylor Cey, Robert Mann

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21595v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21595v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-20


### 摘要

我们提出了一个可实现的实验来测试在与相对论量子场相互作用的位置叠加中制备的粒子探测器的响应。使用分束器准备调制激光探针的两个重叠分支，这些分支被引导在两个不同的位置与煎饼形玻色-爱因斯坦凝聚体相交。然后，这些分支与另一个分束器重新组合。通过对其中一个输出进行异频，对应于与（2+1）维无质量纯量场相互作用的位置叠加中的Unruh-deWitt探测器的响应函数被证明出现在差异光电流功率谱中。使用压缩光在超过标准量子极限的情况下工作，我们估计了用于提取一组广泛基带频率上的响应函数的信噪比$SNR\gtrsim 10$。


### Abstract

We propose a realizable experiment to test the response of a particle detector prepared in a superposition of locations interacting with a relativistic quantum field. Using a beamsplitter to prepare two superposed branches of a modulated laser probe, these branches are directed to intersect a pancake-shaped Bose-Einstein condensate at two separate locations. The branches are then recombined with another beamsplitter. Heterodyning one of the outputs, the response function corresponding to an Unruh-deWitt detector in a superposition of locations interacting with a (2+1)-dimensional massless scalar field is shown to appear in the difference photocurrent power spectrum. Operating beyond the standard quantum limit using squeezed light, we estimate the signal-to-noise ratio $SNR\gtrsim 10$ for extracting the response function over a broad set of baseband frequencies.

## 矩阵产品运营商Magnus扩展和Dyson系列的编码

**Original Title:** Matrix Product Operator Encodings of the Magnus Expansion and Dyson Series

**Authors:** Victor Vanthilt, Maarten Van Damme, Jutho Haegeman, Ian P. McCulloch, Laurens Vanderstraeten

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21597v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21597v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-20


### 摘要

我们为具有时变Hamilton的一维量子格子模型引入了马格努斯展开式和戴森系列的矩阵积运算符（MPO）编码。MPO结构可以精确到时间步中的任意阶数，它可以应用于有限和无限系统，并且可以处理远程相互作用。由此产生的MPO可以与基于矩阵积状态的最新时间进化算法相结合，从而大幅改进在时间相关Hamilton下模拟进化的过程。我们的MPO结构还可用于在依赖于时间的Hamilton量子模拟的背景下优化量子电路。


### Abstract

We introduce a matrix product operator (MPO) encoding of the Magnus expansion and the Dyson series for one-dimensional quantum lattice models with time-dependent Hamiltonians. The resulting MPO can be combined with state-of-the-art time evolution algorithms based on matrix product states, allowing for drastic improvements in simulating evolution under time-dependent Hamiltonians. Our MPO construction can also be used for the optimization of quantum circuits in the context of quantum simulation of time-dependent Hamiltonians.

## 当我们谈论消散量子混乱时我们谈论什么

**Original Title:** What We Talk About When We Talk About Dissipative Quantum Chaos

**Authors:** Lucas Sá, Pedro Ribeiro, Sergey Denisov

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21628v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21628v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-20


### 摘要

消散量子混乱是一种新兴理论，预计将传统汉密尔顿量子混乱的思想、概念和方法论从连贯进化扩展到开放量子动力学。新理论应该提供一套工具来区分混乱的开放量子系统与可积的系统，以及其混乱性（或相反，可积性）的定量测量。这个理论的基础是在20世纪80年代末奠定的，从一开始就很明显，就像它的汉密尔顿前身一样，它必须基于管理开放量子进化的操作员的光谱性质。在迈出这些第一步之后，该领域多年来一直保持相对安静，直到过去十年，消散量子混乱的发展才得到了强劲的推动，这一点已得到有关该主题的大量出版物以及最近进行的第一次实验的证实。测试其理论预测。在本章中，我们回顾了这些最新进展，并概述了消散量子混乱的基本基础。


### Abstract

Dissipative quantum chaos is an emerging theory that is expected to extend the ideas, concepts, and methodology of conventional Hamiltonian quantum chaos from coherent evolution to open quantum dynamics. The new theory should provide a set of tools to distinguish chaotic open quantum systems from integrable ones, as well as quantitative measures of their chaoticity (or, conversely, integrability). The foundations of this theory were laid in the late 1980s, and from the very start it was clear that, like its Hamiltonian predecessor, it had to be based on the spectral properties of the operators governing open quantum evolution. After these first steps, the field remained relatively quiet for many years and it is only over the last decade that the development of dissipative quantum chaos has received a strong boost, as confirmed by a large number of publications on this topic and, very recently, the first experiments performed to test its theoretical predictions. In this chapter, we review these recent developments and outline the basic foundations of dissipative quantum chaos.

## 量子增强原子干涉术中准布拉格区和速度选择性的平衡

**Original Title:** Balancing Quasi-Bragg Regime and Velocity Selectivity in Quantum-Enhanced Atom Interferometry

**Authors:** Christian Miguel Karres, Daniel Derr, Enno Giese

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21643v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21643v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-20


### 摘要

原子系综中的自旋压缩使得原子干涉测量的灵敏度低于散粒噪声极限，但相关的纠缠非常容易丢失，使得原子光学中的缺陷成为一个核心限制。布拉格衍射是一种用于驱动原子动量态之间的跃迁的成熟技术，并且在保持内部状态的同时通过高阶衍射实现大动量转移。然而，它本质上受到两种竞争机制的限制：短光脉冲将寄生衍射诱导到超出有效两级描述的非共振阶，而长脉冲则面临速度选择性。我们推导出解析表达式的原子光学和马赫-曾德尔干涉仪的相位不确定性，包括这些影响的第二量子化的框架。我们证明，子散粒噪声缩放仅在中间脉冲持续时间的制度中实现。此外，我们表明，高阶衍射的有害影响部分减轻通过优化输入量子态。


### Abstract

Spin squeezing in atomic ensembles enables atom interferometry with sensitivities below the shot-noise limit, but the associated entanglement is highly susceptible to loss, making imperfections in atom optics a central limitation. Bragg diffraction is an established technique for driving transitions between atomic momentum states and enables large-momentum transfer through higher-order diffraction while preserving the internal state. However, it is intrinsically limited by two competing mechanisms: short light pulses induce parasitic diffraction into off-resonant orders beyond an effective two-level description, while long pulses face velocity selectivity. We derive analytical expressions in a second-quantized framework for the atom optics and phase uncertainty of a Mach-Zehnder interferometer including these effects. We demonstrate that sub-shot-noise scaling is achieved only in a regime of intermediate pulse duration. Furthermore, we show that deleterious effects of higher-order diffraction are partially mitigated by optimizing the input quantum state.

## 可调谐耦合器量子系统的保真度频率分配和跨射协同设计

**Original Title:** Fidelity-Aware Frequency Allocation and Transpilation Co-Design for Tunable Coupler Quantum Systems

**Authors:** Dylan VanAllen, Evan McKinney, Israa G. Yusuf, Girgis Falstin, Gaurav Agarwal, Jason Pollack, Michael Hatridge, Alex K. Jones

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21662v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21662v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-20


### 摘要

频率拥挤是高温量子架构中的一个基本限制，尤其是在可调耦合器系统中。我们提出了一个框架，该框架通过错误预算方法显式地建模了一致的观众引起的错误和不一致的寿命影响。使用该模型，我们分析了频率拥挤如何随着模块大小和连接性规模而影响门保真度，并制定了一个受约束的优化问题，以在现实的分离和硬件限制下分配量子位和耦合器频率。我们展示了可扩展的频率分配策略，可以最大限度地减少观众引起的错误。我们进一步表明，增加模块内的量子位计数和耦合密度会导致通用性与连接性的权衡。为了探索系统规模的好处，我们开发了一种名为FINESSE的噪音感知蒸发方法，该方法通过选择满足SWAP插入的连接性的高保真路径，同时联合优化下游门执行来最大限度地减少错误。我们为基于SNAIL的三阶耦合器展示了这种基于物理知识的架构-蒸发协同设计方法，该方法本地实现了具有频率感知门极灵敏度的$\SQRT{iSWAP}$基础。在SNAIL架构上，与SABRE相比，FINESSE实现了登录不保真度成本平均降低8.9%和电路深度降低6.8%。我们还比较了IBM布里斯班的架构上的结果。


### Abstract

Frequency crowding is a fundamental limitation in superconducting quantum architectures, particularly in tunable-coupler systems. We present a framework that explicitly models both coherent spectator-induced errors and incoherent lifetime effects through an error budgeting approach. Using this model, we analyze how frequency crowding impacts gate fidelity as module size and connectivity scale, and formulate a constrained optimization problem to assign qubit and coupler frequencies under realistic separation and hardware constraints. We demonstrate scalable frequency allocation strategies that minimize spectator-induced errors. We further show that increasing qubit count and coupling density within a module leads to a fidelity-connectivity tradeoff. To explore the benefits at the system scale, we have developed a noise-aware transpilation approach called FINESSE, which minimizes error by selecting high-fidelity paths that satisfy connectivity via SWAP insertion while jointly optimizing downstream gate execution. We demonstrate this physics-informed architecture-transpilation co-design approach for a SNAIL-based third-order coupler that natively realizes the $\sqrt{iSWAP}$ basis with frequency aware gate fidelities. On SNAIL architectures, FINESSE achieves an average 8.9% reduction in log-infidelity cost and 6.8% reduction in circuit depth vs. SABRE. We also compare results on IBM Brisbane's architecture.

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.21664v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21664v1
