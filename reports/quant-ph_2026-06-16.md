## 针对非本地性且无纠缠的半设备独立认证

**Original Title:** Semi-Device-Independent Certification for Nonlocality without Entanglement

**Authors:** Hanwool Lee, Joonwoo Bae

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.13667v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.13667v2

**Submitted:** 2026-06-11


### 摘要

在这项工作中，我们提出了一个框架，用于演示和证明纠缠基础中的测量（称为全局测量）与局部操作和经典通信（LOCC）（称为无纠缠的非局部性（NLWE））之间的区别。准确地说，我们通过最大置信度测量以每个检测事件的猜测（称为置信度）来显示NLWE，这是一种细粒度的猜测概率，涵盖最小误差和明确的状态区分策略。我们表明，在给定测量结果率的情况下，可以通过使用LOCC限制置信度来认证未知测量的NLWE;该认证是半设备独立的，因为状态准备是可信的。我们举例说明了NLWE针对反平行量子位状态的演示和认证。我们的结果使得使用具有缺陷（例如非单位检测效率）的测量设备在实验上实现NLWE成为可能，因为最大置信度测量仅依赖于检测到的事件。


### Abstract

In this work, we present the framework for demonstrating and certifying the distinction between measurements in an entangled basis, called global measurements, and local operations and classical communication (LOCC), known as nonlocality without entanglement (NLWE). To be precise, we show NLWE via a maximum-confidence measurement in terms of a guess per detection event, called a confidence, a fine-grained guessing probability that encompasses both minimum-error and unambiguous state-discrimination strategies. We show that NLWE for unknown measurements can be certified, given the measurement-outcome rates, by bounding the confidence using LOCC; the certification is semi-device-independent in that state preparation is trusted. We illustrate the demonstration and certification of NLWE for antiparallel qubit states. Our results make it feasible to experimentally realize NLWE using measurement devices with imperfections, such as non-unit detection efficiency, since maximum-confidence measurements rely only on detected events.

## 面向工业应用的量子机器学习

**Original Title:** Quantum Machine Learning for Industrial Applications

**Authors:** Léo Monbroussou

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.14822v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.14822v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-12


### 摘要

机器学习的最新进展改变了许多工业部门，但经典范式面临着根本性的限制：数据量快速增长、计算成本上升、能源消耗巨大以及传统硬件架构的物理扩展限制。量子计算已成为解决这些挑战的一种有希望的计算范式，从而催生了量子机器学习（QML）领域。本文研究了QML的理论基础，重点关注近期和未来的实际应用。解决了三个核心挑战：变分量子电路的可训练性、其表现力以及对高效经典模拟的抵抗力。首先研究了保持Hamming权的变分量子电路的可训练性，并建立了理论保证，解决了关于该电路家族不存在贫瘠高原的公开猜测。然后引入了保留子空间的QML算法，包括光电路和量子卷积神经网络，旨在模拟经典ML子程序，同时提供多项量子优势。最后，将变分量子电路作为量子傅里叶模型进行分析，并推导出一个共同描述表达性和可训练性的框架，从中获得量子模型可证明与经典模型分离的条件。这些贡献旨在推进在现实世界应用中利用近期和未来量子技术的理论路线图。


### Abstract

Recent advances in Machine Learning have transformed numerous industrial sectors, yet classical paradigms face fundamental limitations: rapidly growing data volumes, rising computational costs, significant energy consumption, and the physical scaling limits of conventional hardware architectures. Quantum computing has emerged as a promising computational paradigm to address these challenges, giving rise to the field of Quantum Machine Learning (QML). In this thesis, the theoretical foundations of QML are investigated, with a focus on near-term and future practical applications. Three central challenges are addressed: the trainability of variational quantum circuits, their expressivity, and their resistance to efficient classical simulation. The trainability of Hamming-weight preserving variational quantum circuits is first studied, and theoretical guarantees are established that resolve an open conjecture on the absence of barren plateaus for this circuit family. Subspace-preserving QML algorithms are then introduced, including photonic circuits and quantum convolutional neural networks, and are designed to mimic classical ML subroutines while offering polynomial quantum advantage. Finally, variational quantum circuits are analyzed as quantum Fourier models, and a framework is derived to jointly characterize expressivity and trainability, from which conditions are obtained under which quantum models provably separate from their classical counterparts. These contributions are intended to advance the theoretical roadmap for harnessing near-term and future quantum technologies in real-world applications.

## 与QAOA中的地方打交道

**Original Title:** Dealing with locality in QAOA

**Authors:** Mithilesh Kumar, Yusuf Tahir

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.14447v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.14447v2

**Submitted:** 2026-06-12


### 摘要

稀疏、大直径MaxCut实例上的浅深度QAOA面临着局部瓶颈：在深度\（p\）处，局部可观测量只能依赖于电路交互图的有界邻居。我们提出了一种传输增强的QAOA，它保持MaxCut成本Hamilton不变，但通过优化的、未加权的捷径耦合（预定的\（XX+YY\））来丰富混合器，以缩小有效的交互图直径。使用精确的有限深度支持回归，我们将最佳捷径放置与有界直径图增强联系起来，并在基准测试中表明，一旦直径减小，性能就会变得有效大小不变（与ma-QAOA不同）。对于双方家庭（基础直径4），将相互作用路径减少到\（d=1\）将整体平均近似比从0.7378（ma-QAOA）提高到\（p=1\）的0.9767。（\（σ=0.0251\），9个系统大小）;在随机树（基直径10）上，在\（p=2\）时，它从0.9226提高到0.9997（\（σ=0.0001\））。


### Abstract

Shallow-depth QAOA on sparse, high-diameter MaxCut instances faces a locality bottleneck: at depth \(p\), local observables can depend only on a bounded neighborhood of the circuit interaction graph. We propose a transport-augmented QAOA that keeps the MaxCut cost Hamiltonian unchanged but enriches the mixer with optimized, unweighted shortcut couplings (scheduled \(XX+YY\)) to collapse the effective interaction-graph diameter. Using exact finite-depth support recursions, we relate optimal shortcut placement to bounded-diameter graph augmentation, and show in benchmarks that (unlike ma-QAOA) performance becomes effectively size-invariant once the diameter is reduced. For bipartite families (base diameter 4), reducing the interaction path to \(d=1\) raises the ensemble-averaged approximation ratio from 0.7378 (ma-QAOA) to 0.9767 at \(p=1\) (\(σ=0.0251\), nine system sizes); on random trees (base diameter 10), at \(p=2\) it improves from 0.9226 to 0.9997 (\(σ=0.0001\)).

## 完整七阶段安德森定位景观的实验实现

**Original Title:** Experimental realization of the complete seven-phase Anderson-localization landscape

**Authors:** Yao Qin, Chao Yang, Yuzhe Zhang, Yucheng Wang, Jingyun Fan

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.14825v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.14825v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-12


### 摘要

安德森局部化已经远远超出了扩展状态和局部状态之间的传统二分法。现代本地化理论预测了一个完整的运输层次结构，包括扩展阶段、关键阶段和本地阶段以及其中所有共存阶段，形成七阶段安德森本地化景观。尽管它具有根本性的重要性，但这种层次结构从未在单一系统中通过实验实现过。在这里，我们实现了一维Floquet量子格子中完整的七相安德森局部化景观。通过设计包含非均匀分布的跳频零点的准周期跳频轮廓，我们生成临界状态并使其与扩展和局部化的扇形共存。由此产生的输运状态通过其独特的时空动力学直接解决，包括弹道膨胀、受限临界振荡和持续局部化。我们观察了所有七个相，包括难以捉摸的三重共存的扩展-临界-局部相，并通过实验跟踪连接它们的相转变。我们的结果建立了安德森定位景观的第一个完整实验地图，并为研究移动性边缘、多分数性和可编程一致传输提供了一个统一的平台。


### Abstract

Anderson localization has evolved far beyond the conventional dichotomy between extended and localized states. Modern localization theory predicts a complete transport hierarchy comprising extended, critical, and localized phases together with all coexistence phases among them, forming a seven-phase Anderson-localization landscape. Despite its fundamental importance, this hierarchy has never been experimentally realized within a single system. Here we realize the complete seven-phase Anderson-localization landscape in a one-dimensional Floquet photonic lattice. By engineering quasiperiodic hopping profiles containing inhomogeneously distributed hopping zeros, we generate critical states and enable their coexistence with extended and localized sectors. The resulting transport regimes are directly resolved through their distinct spatiotemporal dynamics, including ballistic expansion, confined critical oscillations, and persistent localization. We observe all seven phases, including the elusive triply coexisting extended-critical-localized phase, and experimentally track the phase transitions connecting them. Our results establish the first complete experimental map of the Anderson-localization landscape and provide a unified platform for investigating mobility edges, multifractality, and programmable coherent transport.

## 测量SU（2）格点规范理论中的非稳定性

**Original Title:** Measuring Non-Stabilizerness in an SU(2) Lattice Gauge Theory

**Authors:** Henry Froland, Dorota M. Grabowska

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.14842v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.14842v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-12


### 摘要

量子模拟的目标之一是为量子系统提供新的见解，例如与高能物理学和核物理学相关的规范理论。近年来，这些模拟所需的硬件和软件都得到了迅速的改进。设计此类模拟的核心考虑因素是给定量子状态的量子复杂性。这项工作朝着研究一个简单但非平凡的系统中的特定复杂性（即非稳定性）迈出了一步：两个模板的SU（2）格点规范理论。研究了低能量本征态的非稳定性，并讨论了其对量子模拟的影响。在ibm_marrakesh上模拟了该系统的实时演变，并使用随机测量协议测量了非稳定性。开发了提高该协议效率的新技术，包括计算非稳定性估计器的新方法和称为比特串去相关重组的灵活错误缓解技术。这种缓解方法对于准确解决非稳定性的实验时间依赖性至关重要，预计将在数字量子模拟中具有广泛的适用性。


### Abstract

One of the goals of quantum simulation is to provide novel insights into quantum systems, such as the gauge theories that are relevant for high-energy and nuclear physics. Recent years have seen rapid improvements in both the hardware and software necessary for these simulations. A central consideration in the design of such simulations is the quantum complexity of a given quantum state. This work takes a step towards studying a specific kind of complexity, namely the non-stabilizerness, in a simple yet non-trivial system: SU(2) lattice gauge theory of two plaquettes. The non-stabilizerness of low-energy eigenstates is studied and the implications for quantum simulations are discussed. The real-time evolution of this system is simulated on ibm_marrakesh and the non-stabilizerness is measured using a random measurement protocol. New techniques enhancing the efficiency of this protocol are developed, including both a new way to calculate the estimator for non-stabilizerness and a flexible error mitigation technique called Bit String Decoherence Renormalization. This mitigation method is central to accurately resolving the experimental time dependence of non-stabilizerness, and is anticipated to have broad applicability in digital quantum simulations.

## 量子网络7量子位寄存器中的高保真两量子位门

**Original Title:** High-fidelity two-qubit gates in a 7-qubit register for quantum networks

**Authors:** Margriet van Riggelen, Jiwon Yun, H. Benjamin van Ommen, Tim H. Taminiau

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.14847v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.14847v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-12


### 摘要

基于光学活性固态旋转的量子网络可能会实现包括远程量子通信和分布式量子计算在内的量子技术。包含多个高保真量子位的网络节点可以促进大规模的容差操作。然而，对于多量子位寄存器来说，严格的错误阈值仍然遥不可及。在这项工作中，我们基于与钻石中氮空位（NV）中心耦合的核旋转，在7量子位寄存器中展示了高保真度的两量子位门。我们分析了高度连接的旋转系统中的串话，开发了一种有效的优化程序，并使用门集断层扫描来描述门。两量子位门可信度（最佳：99.61（5）%，平均：99.18（2）%）表明多量子位寄存器处于分布式量子计算的阈值。最后，作为示例应用，我们对H2和LiH分子的接地能量进行了变分量子本征解算器（VQE）模拟。这些结果证明了基于固态旋转的可扩展量子网络的关键先决条件之一。


### Abstract

Quantum networks based on optically active solid-state spins may enable quantum technologies including long-range quantum communication and distributed quantum computing. Network nodes containing multiple high-fidelity qubits can facilitate large-scale fault-tolerant operation. However, the stringent error thresholds remain out of reach for multi-qubit registers. In this work, we demonstrate high-fidelity two-qubit gates in a 7-qubit register, based on nuclear spins coupled to a nitrogen-vacancy (NV) center in diamond. We analyze crosstalk in highly connected spin systems, develop an efficient optimization procedure, and characterize the gates using gate set tomography. The two-qubit gate fidelities (best: 99.61(5)%, average: 99.18(2)%) demonstrate a multi-qubit register at the threshold for distributed quantum computation. Finally, as an example application, we perform a variational quantum eigensolver (VQE) simulation of the ground-state energy of H2 and LiH molecules. These results demonstrate one of the key prerequisites for scalable quantum networks based on solid-state spins.

## 量子旋转链中的魔法转移

**Original Title:** Magic transfer in quantum spin chains

**Authors:** Antonio Palamara, Chad Nelmes, Enrico C. Domanti, Francesco Perciavalle

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.14855v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.14855v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-12


### 摘要

基于自旋链的量子通信协议已经得到了广泛的研究，但它们传输非稳定器资源的能力还没有得到系统的解决。我们通过非稳定态初始化系统的自然动力学研究了量子魔法在自旋链中的输运，并通过稳定态范数对输运资源进行了量化。我们分析了三个实验可行的状态转移协议，从嘈杂的（准）完美的转移，包括一个可实现的捕获离子平台。我们发现，注入状态的几何形状强烈影响运输：在较低的布洛赫半球的状态实现更高的传输质量，而在上半球的状态产生一个有效的魔术运输只有超过一个阈值的参数控制走向完美的转移的趋势。这些功能在所有协议中都很强大，并识别有利于高质量传输的Hamilton和状态属性。此外，我们确定了一个与初始状态准备相关的参数区域，其中传输的魔力超过了初始编码，这表明此类旋转系统可以充当魔力放大通道。我们的结果建立了非稳定剂资源有效传输的条件，并证明量子魔法是超越种群动力学的量子传输的敏感探测器。


### Abstract

Quantum communication protocols based on spin chains have been extensively studied, yet their ability to transmit nonstabilizer resources has not been systematically addressed. We investigate the transport of quantum magic in spin chains through the natural dynamics of systems initialized in nonstabilizer states, and quantify the transported resource via the stabilizer norm. We analyze three experimentally feasible state-transfer protocols, ranging from noisy to (quasi-)perfect transfer, including one realizable in trapped-ion platforms. We find that the geometry of the injected state strongly influences transport: states in the lower Bloch hemisphere achieve higher transfer quality, whereas states in the upper hemisphere give rise to an efficient magic transport only beyond a threshold value of the parameter controlling the tendency towards perfect transfer. These features are robust across all protocols and identify the Hamiltonian and state properties that favor high-quality transfer. Moreover, we identify a parameter region, relevant to the initial state preparation, in which the transported magic exceeds the initial encoding, indicating that such spin systems can act as magic-amplification channels. Our results establish the conditions for efficient transport of nonstabilizer resources and demonstrate quantum magic as a sensitive probe of quantum transport beyond population dynamics.

## 一维连续体模型的伪元矩阵积状态

**Original Title:** Finite-Element Matrix Product States for Continuum Models in One Dimension

**Authors:** Akshay Shankar, Karel Van Acoleyen, Jutho Haegeman

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.14873v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.14873v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-12


### 摘要

我们提出了一个矩阵积状态框架，用于使用非垂直单粒子基集模拟连续体中的一维量子多体系统。通过将物理问题映射到辅助计算空间，我们表明所得的多体重叠运算符可以有效地编码为充分局部化轨道的矩阵积运算符，从而推广了首次出现在[arXiv：2405.10285]中的结构。这种结构将变分底状态搜索改写为广义特征值问题，该问题可以使用广义密度矩阵重整化群算法来解决。作为主要应用，我们采用一阶有限单元展开来研究存在非均匀性时利布-利尼格气体的稳态性质。这种方法还为精确细化网格提供了自然设置，从而实现矩阵产品状态的多网格优化策略。


### Abstract

We present a matrix product state framework for simulating one-dimensional quantum many-body systems in the continuum using non-orthogonal single-particle basis sets. By mapping the physical problem to an auxiliary computational space, we show that the resulting many-body overlap operator can be efficiently encoded as a matrix product operator for sufficiently localized orbitals, thereby generalizing a construction that first appeared in [arXiv:2405.10285]. This construction recasts the variational ground-state search into a generalized eigenvalue problem, which can be solved using a generalized density matrix renormalization group algorithm. As a primary application, we employ a first-order finite-element expansion to study the ground state properties of the Lieb-Liniger gas in the presence of inhomogeneities. This approach also provides a natural setting for exactly refining the lattice, thereby enabling multigrid optimization strategies for matrix product states.

## 广义Kerr-Cat量子位码

**Original Title:** Generalized Kerr-Cat Qubit Codes

**Authors:** Alonso Viladomat, Shahram Dehdashti, Amin Kargarian, Janis Nötzel, Peter van Loock

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.14901v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.14901v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-12


### 摘要

我们对从克尔型相干态构建的薛定格猫码进行了系统研究，包括置换克尔相干态和Barut-Girardello Kerr相干态，每种都包含由克尔非线性符号确定的两个不同的家族。通过调整克尔参数和相关状态幅度，这些状态在$\mathfrak{su}（2）$、$\mathfrak{su}（1，1）$相关状态之间进行插值，为这种类型的玻色子量子误差修正提供统一且通用的基础。与标准的双分量薛定格猫码（其中单个光损失事件会引发不可纠正的位翻转）不同，克尔猫状态的非线性相空间结构能够在统一的恢复框架内同时检测和纠正光损失和去相误差，并通过凸优化确定最佳恢复操作。我们证明，在组合丢失和去相噪音下，Kerr cat编码的性能显着优于传统cat码，并且明智的参数优化可以将两个错误通道抑制到一定程度，从而减少额外错误纠正层的负担。我们进一步表明，在工程双量子驱动下，克尔变形的相干状态管形出现为驱动消散动力学的有效稳态，单量子退相干被强烈抑制，受保护的管形外部的泄漏仅作为变形强度的更高级修正出现。我们的扩展形式主义将广义Kerr Schrödinger猫码确定为非线性光电子学等实验平台中耐故障玻色子量子计算的有希望的候选者。


### Abstract

We present a systematic study of Schrödinger cat codes constructed from Kerr-type coherent states, including displaced Kerr coherent states and Barut--Girardello Kerr coherent states, each admitting two distinct families determined by the sign of the Kerr nonlinearity. By tuning the Kerr parameter and coherent-state amplitude, these states interpolate between $\mathfrak{su}(2)$, $\mathfrak{su}(1,1)$ coherent states, providing a unified and versatile foundation for this type of bosonic quantum error correction. Unlike standard two-component Schrödinger cat codes, where a single photon-loss event induces an uncorrectable bit-flip, the nonlinear phase-space structure of Kerr cat states enables simultaneous detection and correction of both photon-loss and dephasing errors within a unified recovery framework, with optimal recovery operations determined via convex optimization. We demonstrate that Kerr cat encodings significantly outperform conventional cat codes under combined loss and dephasing noise, and that judicious parameter optimization can suppress both error channels to a level that reduces the overhead of additional error correction layers. We further show that Kerr-deformed coherent-state manifolds under engineered two-photon driving emerge as effective steady states of driven-dissipative dynamics, with single-photon decoherence strongly suppressed and leakage outside the protected manifold appearing only as higher-order corrections in the deformation strength. Our extended formalism identifies generalized Kerr Schrödinger cat codes as promising candidates for fault-tolerant bosonic quantum computation in experimental platforms such as nonlinear photonics.

## 优化纠缠蒸馏政策

**Original Title:** Optimising Entanglement Distillation Policies

**Authors:** Jigyen Bhavsar, Rajni Bala, Siddhartha Santra

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.14908v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.14908v1
