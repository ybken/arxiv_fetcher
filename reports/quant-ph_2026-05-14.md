## 用Polfed.jl计算量子多体系统的本征对

**Original Title:** Computing eigenpairs of quantum many-body systems with Polfed.jl

**Authors:** Rok Pintar, Konrad Pawlik, Rafał Świętek, Miroslav Hopjan, Jan Šuntajs, Jakub Zakrzewski, Piotr Sierant, Lev Vidmar

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.10191v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.10191v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-11


### 摘要

我们呈现Polfed$.$ jl，一个开源Julia包，实现了Polynomially Filtered Exact Diagonalification（POLPED）算法，用于计算量子多体Hamilton的中谱本征值和本征载体（简称为本征对）。获取此类特征对对于研究非平衡多体物理学至关重要，但受到Hilbert空间维度指数增长的阻碍。POLPED通过在Lanczos迭代中动态评估的多项谱变换来解决这一挑战，与其他对角化方法相比，保留了汉密尔顿稀疏性并大幅降低了存储成本。该软件包支持灵活的能量目标、结构化Hamilton谱映射的自动优化以及图形处理器加速，这特别有效，因为主要的计算成本降低到重复的稀疏矩阵-载体相乘。无序旋转链和费米子模型的基准表明，与替代方法相比，可以获得更大的系统尺寸，而且中央处理器与图形处理器的比较证实了显着的加速。特别是，我们还提供了用于构建量子太阳模型Hamilton的代码，Hamilton是多体历经性打破转变的玩具模型。虽然我们的重点是多身体汉密尔顿主义者，但Polfed$.$ J1可以应用于任何大的稀疏矩阵。


### Abstract

We present Polfed$.$jl, an open-source Julia package implementing the Polynomially Filtered Exact Diagonalization (POLFED) algorithm for computing mid-spectrum eigenvalues and eigenvectors (shortly, eigenpairs) of quantum many-body Hamiltonians. Access to such eigenpairs is essential for studying non-equilibrium many-body physics, but is hindered by the exponential growth of Hilbert-space dimension. POLFED addresses this challenge through a polynomial spectral transformation evaluated on the fly within a Lanczos iteration, preserving Hamiltonian sparsity and substantially reducing memory costs compared to other diagonalization methods. The package supports flexible energy targeting, automatic optimization of the spectral mapping for structured Hamiltonians, and GPU acceleration, which is particularly effective since the dominant computational cost reduces to repeated sparse matrix-vector multiplications. Benchmarks on disordered spin-chain and fermionic models demonstrate access to larger system sizes than alternative approaches, and CPU--GPU comparisons confirm significant speedups. In particular, we also provide code for constructing the quantum sun model Hamiltonian, a toy model of a many-body ergodicity-breaking transition. While our focus is on many-body Hamiltonians, Polfed$.$jl may be applied to any large sparse matrix.

## 量化阿达玛弹性定律：发现NISQ时代分类器中的一致性差距

**Original Title:** Quantifying the Hadamard Resilience Law: Discovery of the Coherence Gap in NISQ-Era Classifiers

**Authors:** Wladimir Silva

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.10638v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.10638v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-11


### 摘要

我们报告了NISQ时代分类器中随机噪音模型和算法性能之间的根本差异。利用ibm_kingston处理器，我们描述了“Kingston Constant”（$k\大约0.07$），代表93%的信号幅度崩溃。尽管存在这种衰退，我们证明阿达玛测试感知器保持了93.9%的MNIST准确性，验证了我们提出的阿达玛弹性定律。然而，系统性分歧--“一致性差距”（$N = 256 $）--出现在高特征深度（$N=256$），其中物理硬件崩溃，而随机模拟保持弹性。这一差距将连贯的相差识别为缩放量子线性层的主要障碍，而不是去极化噪音。此外，ibm_kingston处理器上的实验结果揭示了$N=256$处的“一致性墙”，其中电路深度（$D \大约10 k $）超过了硬件的弹性深度限制（$D_{max} \大约3.5k$）。我们提供了一个精确的硬件感知模型，该模型考虑了这种相干性引起的信号衰减，为当前NISQ设备上的稳健量子线性层建立了预测边界。


### Abstract

We report on a fundamental disparity between stochastic noise models and algorithmic performance in NISQ-era classifiers. Utilizing the ibm_kingston processor, we characterize the "Kingston Constant" ($κ\approx 0.07$), representing a 93% signal magnitude collapse. Despite this decay, we demonstrate that the Hadamard Test Perceptron maintains a 93.9% MNIST accuracy, validating our proposed Hadamard Resilience Law. However, a systemic divergence -- the "Coherence Gap" ($Δρ\approx 0.91$) -- emerges at high feature depths ($N=256$), where physical hardware collapses while stochastic simulations remain resilient. This gap identifies coherent phase errors, rather than depolarizing noise, as the primary barrier to scaling quantum linear layers. Furthermore, experimental results on the ibm_kingston processor reveal a "Coherence Wall" at $N=256$, where circuit depth ($D \approx 10k$) exceeds the hardware's resilient depth limit ($D_{max} \approx 3.5k$). We provide a refined hardware-aware model that accounts for this coherence-induced signal decay, establishing a predictive boundary for robust quantum linear layers on current NISQ devices.

## MCB生物负载来自真空接口

**Original Title:** CMB Birefringence from Vacuum Interfaces

**Authors:** Nemanja Kaloper

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.11065v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.11065v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-11


### 摘要

宇宙微波背景极化旋转的暗示（$Δ\vartheta \sim 10^{-3}$ rad）通常归因于超轻轴子的后期动力学。我们表明，这种超轻自由度是不需要的。极化旋转自然产生，作为当量子穿过地形不同的暗扇形真空之间的界面时获得的几何界面相。该效应是一个离散的移相，由壁支撑的电磁陈-西蒙斯相互作用的标准化固定，并受到低能量有效理论的新兴1美元形式对称性的保护。这种机制重现了由轻轴子域壁引起的熟悉的隔热旋转，但对于轴子较重或不存在的任意薄壁来说仍然存在。在这个区域中，旋转表现为局部位于真空界面的潘查勒南相，与自然紫外截止以下的红移和量子频率无关。因此，宇宙双折射作为暗区真空结构的探测器出现，而不是光场动力学的探测器。


### Abstract

Hints of cosmic microwave background polarization rotation ($Δ\vartheta \sim 10^{-3}$ rad) are commonly attributed to late-time dynamics of ultralight axions. We show that such ultralight degrees of freedom are not required. Polarization rotation naturally arises as a geometric interface phase acquired when photons cross interfaces between topologically distinct dark sector vacua. The effect is a discrete phase shift fixed by the normalization of a wall-supported electromagnetic Chern--Simons interaction and protected by an emergent $1$-form symmetry of the low energy effective theory. This mechanism reproduces the familiar adiabatic rotation induced by light axion domain walls, but persists for arbitrarily thin walls where the axion is heavy or absent. In this regime the rotation manifests as a Pancharatnam phase localized at vacuum interfaces, independent of redshift and photon frequency below a natural ultraviolet cutoff. Cosmic birefringence thus emerges as a probe of vacuum structure in the dark sector, rather than of light-field dynamics.

## 了解致密Si-MOS量子点阵列中氧化物厚度相关的变化性

**Original Title:** Understanding oxide-thickness-dependent variability in dense Si-MOS quantum dot arrays

**Authors:** Arne Loenders, Jacques Van Damme, Clement Godfrin, Paola Favia, Jacopo Franco, Thomas Van Caekenberghe, Bart Raes, Gulzat Jaliel, Sylvain Baudot, Luis Francisco Pinotti, Alexander Grill, George Simion, Kristof Moors, Vukan Levajac, Sofie Beyne, Sugandha Sharma, Stefan Kubicek, Yosuke Shimura, Roger Loo, Massimo Mongillo, Danny Wan, Kristiaan De Greve

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12143v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.12143v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-12


### 摘要

实现对半导体旋转量子位的统一和可扩展控制仍然是大规模量子计算的一个关键挑战。在这项工作中，我们研究了栅氧化层厚度如何影响致密二维硅量子点阵列的均匀性。我们使用在300 mm CMOS工艺中制造的7 x 7阵列，通过极紫外光蚀刻图案化，对四种不同氧化物厚度的392个量子点进行统计学特征。阈值电压、电容、杠杆臂和充电能量是使用基于平行行的测量来提取的，并且我们确定了17纳米的最佳SiO2厚度，可以将阈值电压变异性最小化到63 mA标准差以下。我们的观察说明了多种疾病来源如何引入竞争性的氧化物厚度依赖性，从而导致非单调趋势。这些结果为密集、可扩展的硅旋转量子位架构提供了关键设计指南。


### Abstract

Achieving uniform and scalable control of semiconductor spin qubits remains a key challenge for large scale quantum computing. In this work, we investigate how gate oxide thickness influences uniformity in dense two dimensional silicon quantum dot arrays. Using a 7 x 7 array fabricated in a 300 mm CMOS-process patterned by EUV lithography, we statistically characterize 392 quantum dots across four different oxide thicknesses. The threshold voltages, capacitances, lever arms, and charging energies are extracted using parallel row based measurements and we identify an optimal SiO2 thickness of 17 nm that minimizes threshold voltage variability below 63 mV standard deviation. Our observations illustrate how multiple sources of disorder can introduce competing oxide-thickness dependencies, resulting in non-monotonic trends. These results provide key design guidelines for dense, scalable silicon spin qubit architectures.

## QuPort：模块化多QPU量子系统的布局、端口和拥挤感知编译

**Original Title:** QuPort: Topology-, Port-, and Congestion-Aware Compilation for Modular Multi-QPU Quantum Systems

**Authors:** Soumyadip Sarkar, Subhasree Bhattacharjee

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12583v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.12583v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-12


### 摘要

模块化量子处理器需要编译器同时推理两种资源：本地设备连接性和QPU之间的通信。如果在单个耦合图上可接受的映射创建过多的跨QPU流量、将流量集中在少量互连链路上或将许多边界量子位分配给具有很少通信端口的QPU，那么在单个耦合图上可接受的映射可能不适合模块化机器。本文介绍了QuPort，这是一个基于Python和Qiskit的编译框架，它通过显式的三层模型来研究这种设置：加权逻辑交互图、有向物理耦合图和无向QPU级互连图。主要的分区方法TPCCAP优化了由加权切割距离、通信端口溢出和路由链路负载拥挤构成的实施目标。该框架还包括重边缘集群、平衡贪婪划分、模拟退变细化、通信端口感知布局、远程两量子位操作的提取、每个QPU电路的仅本地路由以及基于布局感知的调度估计。该模型是一个编译器级抽象。它没有要求经过校准的硬件运行时间或物理远程门协议的实现。


### Abstract

Modular quantum processors require a compiler to reason about two resources at the same time: local device connectivity and communication across QPUs. A mapping that is acceptable on a single coupling graph may be unsuitable for a modular machine if it creates excessive cross-QPU traffic, concentrates that traffic on a small number of interconnect links, or assigns many boundary qubits to a QPU with few communication ports. This paper presents QuPort, a Python and Qiskit-based compilation framework that studies this setting through an explicit three-level model: a weighted logical interaction graph, a directed physical coupling map, and an undirected QPU-level interconnect graph. The main partitioning method, TPCCAP, optimizes the implemented objective formed by weighted cut distance, communication-port overflow, and routed link-load congestion. The framework also includes heavy-edge clustering, balanced greedy partitioning, simulated-annealing refinement, communication-port-aware layout, extraction of remote two-qubit operations, local-only routing of per-QPU circuits, and topology-aware schedule estimation. The model is a compiler-level abstraction. It does not claim a calibrated hardware runtime or an implementation of a physical remote-gate protocol.

## 对旋转子的连贯控制

**Original Title:** Coherent control of spinmons

**Authors:** Johanne Bratland Tjernshaugen, Florinda Viñas Boström, Jeroen Danon, Jacob Linder, Karsten Flensberg, Antonio L. R. Manesco

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12588v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.12588v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-12


### 摘要

保护超导量子位免受某些噪音源的影响通常是以提高对其他去相干通道的敏感性为代价的。在这里，我们探索了一种避免这种权衡的途径，方法是将量子信息编码在与被俘获的安德烈耶夫准粒子的旋转纠缠的转子的量子状态中。我们将此类设备称为“纺锤”。我们通过引入塞曼场来提升旋转子Kramers简并度，并开发了两种通过静电门和AC通量驱动进行全量子位控制的路线，为实验实现提供了多个方向。最后，我们计算相关时间并验证量子位对通量和电荷噪音源的鲁棒性。


### Abstract

The protection of superconducting qubits from certain noise sources often comes at the cost of increased sensitivity to other decoherence channels. Here, we explore a route to avoid this tradeoff by encoding quantum information in quantum states of a transmon entangled with the spin of a trapped Andreev quasiparticle. We term such devices spinmons. We lift the spinmon Kramers degeneracy by introducing a Zeeman field and develop two routes for full qubit control via electrostatic gates and an AC flux drive, providing multiple directions for experimental implementations. Finally, we compute coherence times and verify the qubit robustness against flux and charge noise sources.

## 费米子格子可以通过相同大小的量子位格子来模拟，具有$\mathCal{O}（1）$交互作用负载

**Original Title:** Fermion lattices can be simulated by same-size qubit lattices with $\mathcal{O}(1)$ interaction overhead

**Authors:** Gregor Aigner, Berend Klaver, Martin Lanthaler, Wolfgang Lechner

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12600v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.12600v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-12


### 摘要

电子之间的局部相互作用是相关材料许多复杂性质的基础。虽然乔丹-维格纳变换可以沿着一个空间维度保留这个局部性，但沿着其余维度的交互通常会产生大量的额外费用。我们展示了如何模拟$N$位二维费米子格子上的所有几何局部相互作用，而没有相互作用数量的渐进额外负担，也没有空间额外负担。我们方法的主要负担是电路深度，它在量子位网格上与费米交换网络的电路深度相匹配，扩展为$\mathcal{O}（\squtt {N}）$，但在可重新配置的量子位阵列上减少到$\mathcal {O}（\log N）$，在基于网格手术的表面代码架构中减少到$\mathcal{O}（1）$。这是通过动态重新定向Jordan-Wigner变换以切换保留局部性的格子维度来实现的。此外，我们还研究了费米子路由，这是模拟非局部相互作用所需的。当使用量子位网格时，我们可以实现渐进地匹配量子位路由的资源扩展，而在完全连接的量子位设备上，可以实现任意接近$\mathCal{O}（\log N）$的深度扩展。这使得费米子快速傅里叶变换能够在量子位格子上实现，并且在这些局部性约束下实现渐进最优的资源缩放。值得注意的是，我们所有的构造自然地扩展到$d$维格子。除了缩放改进之外，我们还展示了我们方法的明确示例，包括平方、Lieb和kagome格子的费米-哈伯德模型模拟以及费米快速傅里叶变换。


### Abstract

Local interactions among electrons underlie many complex properties of correlated materials. While the Jordan-Wigner transformation can preserve this locality along one spatial dimension, interactions along the remaining dimensions typically incur substantial overhead. We show how to simulate all geometrically local interactions on an $N$-site two-dimensional fermion lattice with no asymptotic overhead in the number of interactions and no space overhead. The primary overhead of our method is circuit depth, which on a qubit lattice matches that of fermionic swap networks, scaling as $\mathcal{O}(\sqrt{N})$, but reduces to $\mathcal{O}(\log N)$ on reconfigurable qubit arrays and to $\mathcal{O}(1)$ in lattice-surgery-based surface-code architectures. This is enabled by dynamically reorienting the Jordan-Wigner transformation to switch the lattice dimension along which locality is preserved. Furthermore, we study fermion routing, as required for the simulation of non-local interactions. When using qubit lattices, we reach resource scaling that asymptotically matches that of qubit routing, whilst on fully connected qubit devices, a depth scaling arbitrarily close to $\mathcal{O}(\log N)$ is reached. This allows the fermionic fast Fourier transform to be implemented on qubit lattices with asymptotically optimal resource scaling under these locality constraints. Notably, all of our constructions naturally extend to $d$-dimensional lattices. Beyond scaling improvements, we show explicit examples of our method, including Fermi-Hubbard-model simulations of the square-, Lieb- and kagome lattice and the fermionic fast Fourier transform.

## 群的量子状态同质化问题

**Original Title:** Quantum state isomorphism problems for groups

**Authors:** Alexandru Gheorghiu, Dale Jacobs, Saeed Mehraban, Arsalan Motamedi

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.12615v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.12615v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-12


### 摘要

我们研究群作用下量子状态同质化问题的计算复杂性：给定两个准备纯状态或混合状态的量子电路，决定这两个状态是否通过群作用相关。这可以被视为隐藏子集问题的量子状态版本，就像状态隐藏子集问题是普通隐藏子集问题的量子版本一样。   我们证明了这个计算问题的几个结果：   - 对于纯状态版本，我们表明该问题对于所有非平凡群来说都是BQP难的，并且包含在QCMA $\cap$ QCSZK中。我们进一步获得了特定感兴趣群的细化结果：对于阿贝尔群，我们表明该问题简化为广义二面体群上的状态隐藏子群问题;对于克利福德群，该问题至少与在多项时间约简下的图同质一样困难;对于泡利群，它是BQP-完全的。   - 对于混合状态版本，对于非平凡、有限且可有效表示的群，问题是QSZK完全的。   - 我们还研究了无限群上这个问题的一个变体，特别是玻色线性光学单位。我们表明，在量子状态的经典描述是在一个被称为恒星表示的合适波函数表示中给出的设置中，这个问题至少与图同质一样困难，并且包含在NP $\cap$ SZK中。   在我们的工作之前，仅针对对称群[LG 17]研究了状态同质化问题。作为我们的结果的结果，我们解决了[HEC 25]中提出的一个悬而未决的问题，即混合状态上阿贝尔状态隐藏子群问题的量子算法的存在性。我们表明，在最坏的情况下，这个问题是QSZK难的，从而排除了有效的量子算法，除非QSZK = BQP。


### Abstract

We study the computational complexity of quantum state isomorphism problems under group actions: given two quantum circuits that prepare pure or mixed states, decide whether the two states are related by a group action. This can be seen as a quantum state version of the Hidden Shift Problem, in much the same way that the State Hidden Subgroup Problem is a quantum version of the ordinary Hidden Subgroup Problem.
  We prove several results for this computational problem:
  - For the pure-state version, we show that the problem is BQP-hard for all nontrivial groups, and contained in QCMA $\cap$ QCSZK. We further obtain refined results for specific groups of interest: for abelian groups we show that the problem reduces to the state hidden subgroup problem over the generalized dihedral group; for the Clifford group, the problem is at least as hard as Graph Isomorphism under polynomial-time reductions; for the Pauli group it is BQP-complete.

Our approach eliminates the need for instantaneous, discontinuous state collapse and provides a unified description of the postmeasurement quantum state reduction as a form of quantum state evolution. Notably, it does not require invoking concepts such as the quantum state assigned to a classical apparatus. At the same time, the stochastic character of selective measurement and the Born rule remain unchanged. We present several numerical solutions of the evolution equation for quasilinear selective measurement in two-level quantum systems and compare them with the standard von Neumann projection. The results demonstrate agreement between the two measurement schemes in their fundamental properties. Furthermore, we investigate phenomena associated with the structural instability of the evolution equation and identify very narrow parameter regions in which the outcomes deviate from those predicted by the von Neumann projection. These regions may offer opportunities to test the proposed approach experimentally. Finally, using specific analytical solutions, we discuss the Stern-Gerlach experiment within the framework of quasilinear measurement.
