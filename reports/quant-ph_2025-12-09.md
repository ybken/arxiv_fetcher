## 热场中的加速度诱导透明保护了纠缠

**Original Title:** Entanglement is protected by acceleration-induced transparency in thermal field

**Authors:** Yongjie Pan, Baocheng Zhang, Qingyu Cai

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.06043v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.06043v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-05


### 摘要

最近有人提出加速诱导透明度（AIT）效应可以扩大两级检测器的转变概率，并为实验检测Unruh效应提供了一种潜在的途径。本文探讨了AIT效应对热场背景中加速的两个探测器之间量子纠缠的影响，因为热背景场在任何实验中都无法完全避免。有趣的是，我们发现，尽管背景热场通常会降低探测器之间的纠缠度，但AIT效应可以有效地保护它。


### Abstract

The acceleration-induced transparency (AIT) effect has been suggested recently to amply the transition probability of the two-level detctor and offers a potential avenue for the experimental detection of the Unruh effect. In this paper, we explore the influence of the AIT effect on quantum entanglement between two detectors accelerated in a thermal field background, since the thermal backgound field cannot be avoided completely in any experiments. Interestingly, we find that although the backgound thermal field generally degrade the entanglement between the detectors, the AIT effect can effectively protect it.

## 量子、外交和地缘政治

**Original Title:** Quantum, Diplomacy, and Geopolitics

**Authors:** Axel Ferrazzini

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.06052v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.06052v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-05


### 摘要

量子技术--跨越通信、传感、计算和密码学--正在迅速成为地缘政治竞争和战略国防创新的关键途径。与传统技术进步不同，量子引入了新颖的能力，从根本上颠覆了既定的安全、情报和外交接触规范。这项战略分析通过外交和地缘政治的双重视角探索了不断变化的量子格局，对国防领导人、政策制定者和行业利益相关者产生了具体影响。从外交和地缘政治的角度审视量子技术的好处和挑战，以帮助领导人做出明智的战略决策。主要大国现在认识到量子是一个技术领先地位直接转化为地缘政治影响力的领域，迫使一场激烈的主导地位争夺战，同时推出旨在管理风险和机遇的新形式多边外交。量子技术并非都具有相同的运营成熟度，但技术进步正在加速。后量子密码学需要立即采取行动--今天创建的每一个加密通信都可能在十年内被配备量子能力的对手收集和解密。


### Abstract

Quantum technologies -- spanning communication, sensing, computing, and cryptography -- are rapidly emerging as critical paths of geopolitical competition and strategic defence innovation. Unlike traditional technological advances, quantum introduces novel capabilities that fundamentally disrupt established norms of security, intelligence, and diplomatic engagement. This strategic analysis explores the evolving quantum landscape through the dual lenses of diplomacy and geopolitics, with specific implications for defence leaders, policymakers, and industry stakeholders. The benefits and challenges of quantum technologies are examined from a diplomatic and geopolitical perspective to help leaders make informed strategic decisions. Leading powers now recognise quantum as a domain where technological leadership directly translates to geopolitical influence, compelling an intense race for dominance alongside new forms of multilateral diplomacy aimed at managing both risks and opportunities. Quantum technologies do not all have the same operational maturity, but technological progress is accelerating. Post-quantum cryptography demands immediate action -- every encrypted communication created today may be harvested and decrypted within the decade by adversaries equipped with quantum capabilities.

## 魔角螺旋磁场诱导DPPH的铁磁相转变

**Original Title:** Ferromagnetic Phase Transition of DPPH Induced by a Magic Angle Helical Magnetic Field

**Authors:** Emmanouil Markoulakis, John Chatzakis, Antonios Konstantaras, Iraklis Rigakis, Emmanuel Antonidakis

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.06053v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.06053v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-05


### 摘要

我们报告的结果和独特的仪器配置的一个新的实验中，我们成功地过渡到DPPH样品从其自然顺磁状态，基本上是一个非磁性材料的铁磁状态在室温下。这是使用专门施加的螺旋磁通磁场实现的。DPPH样品（2，2-二苯基-1-苦基肼基）在实验后至少一小时内保持铁磁性，表明材料中的转变是由外场引起的，而不仅仅是仅在实验期间观察到的暂时磁性相变。所使用的外部磁场具有相对于+z轴的约54.7 °$的螺旋桨距角，在数学上称为魔角，其与法向S到N外部场的磁矩矢量对齐。根据实验的现象学，我们推断，这个特定的魔角对应于均匀直通量磁场下自由电子的已知量子化岁差旋转角，这可能会提高DPPH材料内未成对价电子的百分比，使它们能够平行排列与所施加的外场。通常，在顺磁性材料中，不成对电子的量子旋转相对于外场的分布几乎是随机的，大约有50%的机会平行或反平行排列。由于波尔兹曼热分布，在一个排列方向上仅存在轻微的多数偏好，这有助于这些材料的顺磁性。在我们的测量中，我们发现DPPH样本的感应铁磁性导致$μ 1.4$处的相对导磁率异常千倍小数增加，而该材料的典型顺磁值为$1.0001$。


### Abstract

We report the results and unique instrument configuration of a novel experiment in which we successfully transitioned a DPPH sample from its natural paramagnetic state and essentially a non-magnetic material to a ferromagnetic state at room temperature. This was achieved using a specifically applied helical flux magnetic field. The DPPH sample (2,2-diphenyl-1-picrylhydrazyl) remained ferromagnetic for at least one hour after the experiment, indicating that a transformation in the material was induced by the external field rather than being merely a temporary magnetic phase transition observed only during the experiment. The external magnetic field used had a helical pitch angle of approximately $54.7°$, known mathematically as the Magic Angle, relative to the +z-axis, which is aligned with the normal S to N external field's magnetic moment vector. Based on the phenomenology of the experiment, we infer that this specific magic angle corresponding to the known quantization precession spin angle of free electrons under a homogeneous straight flux magnetic field potentially enhances the percentage of unpaired valence electrons within the DPPH material, allowing them to align in parallel with the applied external field. Typically, in paramagnetic materials, the distribution of unpaired electrons' quantum spins relative to an external field is nearly random, showing roughly a 50% chance of either parallel or antiparallel alignment. Only a slight majority preference exists in one alignment direction due to the Boltzmann thermal distribution, which contributes to the paramagnetic nature of these materials. In our measurements, we found that the induced ferromagnetism of the DPPH sample resulted in an abnormal thousand-fold decimal value increase in relative magnetic permeability at $μ{\approx}1.4$, compared to its typical paramagnetic value of $1.0001$ for this material.

## RedCardD：基于Cartan分解的固定深度酉合成量子辅助算法

**Original Title:** RedCarD: A Quantum Assisted Algorithm for Fixed-Depth Unitary Synthesis via Cartan Decomposition

**Authors:** Omar Alsheikh, Efekan Kökcü, Bojko N. Bakalov, A. F. Kemper

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.06070v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.06070v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-05


### 摘要

开发量子模拟电路的一个关键步骤是使用电路构建块合成所需的么正操作符。从李代数的角度研究一元元及其生成元，产生了几种基于动态李代数的Cartan分解的合成算法。对于形式为$e^{-itH}$的个位数，例如与时间无关的汉密尔顿模拟，所得电路的深度不取决于模拟时间$t$。然而，寻找此类电路在成本函数评估和多维优化问题中存在大量的经典负担。在这项工作中，通过进一步划分动态李代数，我们将优化问题分解为更小的独立子问题。此外，由此产生的代数结构使我们能够轻松地将成本函数的评估转移到量子计算机，进一步减少算法的经典开销。作为新混合算法的应用，我们在几台IBM设备和Quantinuum的H1-1量子计算机上合成了4位横场伊辛模型的时间演化正性。


### Abstract

A critical step in developing circuits for quantum simulation is to synthesize a desired unitary operator using the circuit building blocks. Studying unitaries and their generators from the Lie algebraic perspective has given rise to several algorithms for synthesis based on a Cartan decomposition of the dynamical Lie algebra. For unitaries of the form $e^{-itH}$, such as time-independent Hamiltonian simulation, the resulting circuits have depth that does not depend on simulation time $t$. However, finding such circuits has a large classical overhead in the cost function evaluation and the high dimensional optimization problem. In this work, by further partitioning the dynamical Lie algebra, we break down the optimization problem into smaller independent subproblems. Moreover, the resulting algebraic structure allows us to easily shift the evaluation of the cost function to the quantum computer, further cutting the classical overhead of the algorithm. As an application of the new hybrid algorithm, we synthesize the time evolution unitary for the 4-site transverse field Ising model on several IBM devices and Quantinuum's H1-1 quantum computer.

## 量子场论中的孪生悖论

**Original Title:** The Twin Paradox in Quantum Field Theory

**Authors:** Matheus H. Zambianco, T. Rick Perche

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.06076v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.06076v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-05


### 摘要

量子场论中的真空涨落对我们短尺度测量时间的能力施加了根本限制。为了研究普适量子场论效应对依赖于操纵杆的时间测量的影响，我们引入了一个基于有限大小量子系统真空衰变概率的时钟模型。使用这个模型，我们研究了微观孪生悖论场景，发现在最小的尺度下，时间不仅取决于连接两个事件的轨迹，还取决于真空涨落如何与时钟的微观细节相互作用。


### Abstract

Vacuum fluctuations in quantum field theory impose fundamental limitations on our ability to measure time in short scales. To investigate the impact of universal quantum field theory effects on observer-dependent time measurements, we introduce a clock model based on the vacuum decay probability of a finite-sized quantum system. Using this model, we study a microscopic twin paradox scenario and find that, in the smallest scales, time is not only dependent on the trajectory connecting two events, but also on how vacuum fluctuations interact with the microscopic details of the clocks.

## 时空核的时间相关性和混乱

**Original Title:** Temporal correlations and chaos from spacetime kernel

**Authors:** Rathindra Nath Das, Arnab Kundu, Matheus H. Martins Costa, Nemai Chandra Sarkar

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.06078v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.06078v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-05


### 摘要

我们开发了最近引入的“类时纠缠”概念的有限维公式，该概念是根据不同柯西切片上支持的运算符之间的两点函数来定义的。使用局部正规正交运算符基础，我们根据广义响应张量重新铸造了这个结构。在此基础上，我们引入了一个与较高点相关函数相对应的广义时空密度核（GSDK），包括时间有序和时间外有序相关器。我们表明，哈尔平均$（2N）$点函数产生了在$N$增强的有效温度下评估的光谱形状因子（SFF）的$（2N）$阶矩。GSDK操作符的相关函数还产生SFF，物理时间尺度有效减少$（1/N）$-。GSDK将扰乱诊断和光谱统计置于类似的基础上，并澄清了更高点相关器和非平凡时间排序如何捕获量子系统的细粒度动态信息。


### Abstract

We develop a finite-dimensional formulation of the recently introduced notion of ``timelike entanglement'', defined in terms of two-point functions between operators supported on different Cauchy slices. Using a local orthonormal operator basis, we recast this construction in terms of a generalized response tensor. Building on this, we introduce a generalized spacetime density kernel (GSDK) corresponding to higher-point correlation functions, including time-ordered as well as out-of-time-ordered correlators. We show that the Haar-averaged $(2N)$-point function yields the $(2N)$-th moment of the spectral form factor (SFF), evaluated at an $N$-enhanced effective temperature. The correlation functions of the GSDK operators also yield the SFF, with an effective $(1/N)$-reduction of the physical time-scales. The GSDK places both scrambling diagnostics and spectral statistics on a similar footing and clarifies how higher-point correlators and non-trivial time ordering capture fine-grained dynamical information of a quantum system.

## 一元系统-巴斯动力学中的纠缠转变

**Original Title:** Entanglement transition in unitary system-bath dynamics

**Authors:** Bo Xing, Giuliano Chiriacò, Paola Cappellaro, Rosario Fazio, Dario Poletti

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.06081v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.06081v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-05


### 摘要

与浴相结合的系统的演变通常由主方程来描述，该方程在长时间限制下产生稳态密度矩阵。然而，当相同的进化被解开为量子轨迹时，随着系统与浴槽耦合的增加，可以观察到系统内纠缠的缩放发生转变--这种现象在系统的原子平均约化密度矩阵中是不可见的。在这里，我们超越了主方程的轨迹范式，探索在组合系统-沐浴设置的单一进化中是否出现了定性类似的纠缠-缩放转变。我们研究了由自由费米子的2D网格组成的正量子设置中纠缠的缩放，其中每个位点都耦合到费米子浴。改变系统-浴槽耦合揭示了从热力定律到面积定律缩放的转变，这在对数费米子负性、互信息以及相关性中可见。这种情况发生在系统的稳态特性微不足道的情况下，凸显了这些不同缩放的特征在浴缸相关性内。


### Abstract

The evolution of a system coupled to baths is commonly described by a master equation that, in the long-time limit, yields a steady-state density matrix. However, when the same evolution is unraveled into quantum trajectories, it is possible to observe a transition in the scaling of entanglement within the system as the system-bath coupling increases - a phenomenon that is invisible in the trajectory-averaged reduced density matrix of the system. Here, we go beyond the paradigm of trajectories from master equations and explore whether a qualitatively analogous entanglement-scaling transition emerges in the unitary evolution of the combined system-bath setup. We investigate the scaling of entanglement in a unitary quantum setup composed of a 2D lattice of free fermions, where each site is coupled to a fermionic bath. Varying the system-bath coupling reveals a transition from logarithmic-law to area-law scaling, visible in the logarithmic fermionic negativity, mutual information, and also in the correlations. This occurs while the system's steady-state properties are trivial, highlighting that the signatures of these different scalings are within the bath-bath correlations.

## 跨可见到电信微波的电荷和可调量子光源的高性能迷宫圆形布拉格栅设计

**Original Title:** High-Performance Labyrinth Circular Bragg Grating Design for Charge and Stark-Tunable Quantum Light Sources Spanning Visible to Telecom Wavelengths

**Authors:** Rohit Prasad, Quirin Buchinger, Fei Chi Kristy Yuen, Yorick Reum, Sven Höfling, Tobias Huber-Loyola

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.06117v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.06117v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-05


### 摘要

嵌入圆形布拉格栅（CBG）中的半导体量子点是最有效的集成单量子源之一。然而，传统CBG的完全蚀刻环限制了通过电接触实现充电和斯塔克调谐。为了克服这一限制，提出了具有四个桥的迷宫式CBG几何结构，但添加的桥显着降低了光学性能。在这项工作中，我们通过数值证明了周期性迷宫CBG设计可以保持高耦合效率和强Purcell增强，同时如果在引入桥后进行优化，则可以实现电气集成。我们在780纳米、930纳米和1550纳米发射波长下展示了三种优化设计，因为这些波长与量子点最相关，并且表明了我们方法的普遍适用性。在所有三个波长下，数字口径为0.7时的收集效率超过90%，珀塞尔因子大于25。此外，我们提出了一种器件布局，该器件布局结合了隔离p和n型掺杂半导体区域的阻挡层，引入该阻挡层是为了防止其中一个电荷载体发生隧道效应以进行选择性充电。此外，这种设计可以重新优化，以在没有隧道屏障的情况下保留设备的性能。这些结果将迷宫CBG确立为具有高效率和可扩展性的电可调量子点单量子源的平台。


### Abstract

Semiconductor quantum dots embedded in circular Bragg gratings (CBGs) are among the most efficient integrated single-photon sources. However, the fully etched rings of conventional CBGs restrict the implementation of charge and Stark tuning via electrical contacts. To overcome this limitation, a labyrinth CBG geometry with four bridges has been proposed, yet the added bridges significantly degraded optical performance. In this work, we numerically demonstrate that a periodic labyrinth CBG design preserves both high coupling efficiency and strong Purcell enhancement while enabling electrical integration if optimized after introducing the bridges. We show three optimized designs at emission wavelengths of 780 nm, 930 nm, and 1550 nm, because these wavelengths are among the most relevant for quantum dots and show the general applicability of our approach. At all three wavelengths collection efficiencies exceeding 90% into a numerical aperture of 0.7 and Purcell factors greater than 25 are achieved. Furthermore, we propose a device layout incorporating a barrier layer that separates p- and n-doped semiconductor regions, which is incorporated to prevent tunneling of one of the charge carriers for selective charging. Also this design can be reoptimized to retain the performance of a device without tunnel barrier. These results establish labyrinth CBGs as a platform for electrically tunable quantum dot single-photon sources with high efficiency and scalability.

## 加速药物发现的协同计算方法：整合量子力学、统计热力学和量子计算

**Original Title:** Synergistic Computational Approaches for Accelerated Drug Discovery: Integrating Quantum Mechanics, Statistical Thermodynamics, and Quantum Computing

**Authors:** Farzad Molani, Art E. Cho

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.06141v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.06141v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-05


### 摘要

准确预测蛋白质-配体结合自由能（BFE）仍然是药物发现的核心挑战，特别是因为最可靠的方法，例如自由能微扰（BEP），计算密集型且难以扩展。在这里，我们引入了一种混合量子-经典框架，该框架将挖掘极小采样与量子机械细化配体部分电荷、QM/MM相互作用评估和基于变分量子本征解算器（VQE）的电子能量修正相结合。这种设计能够明确处理在纯经典评分方案中经常被低估的极化、电荷重新分布和电子相关效应，同时保持计算效率。在23种蛋白质靶点和543种配体中，该方法实现了约1.10 kCal/摩尔的平均绝对误差，具有较强的等级顺序保真度（Pearson R = 0.75，Spearman rho = 0.76，Kendall tau = 0.57），与当代BEP协议的性能一致。值得注意的是，该工作流程在标准计算资源上只需要每个配体约25分钟，因此相对于炼金术自由能方法，计算成本降低了约20倍。这种水平的准确性和效率使该方法非常适合药物发现中的高通量潜在线索优化和迭代设计周期。该框架还为未来与机器学习模型的集成提供了自然的基础，以实现预测性、大规模和自适应的筛选策略。


### Abstract

Accurately predicting protein-ligand binding free energies (BFEs) remains a central challenge in drug discovery, particularly because the most reliable methods, such as free energy perturbation (FEP), are computationally intensive and difficult to scale. Here, we introduce a hybrid quantum-classical framework that combines Mining Minima sampling with quantum mechanically refined ligand partial charges, QM/MM interaction evaluation, and variational quantum eigensolver (VQE)-based electronic energy correction. This design enables explicit treatment of polarization, charge redistribution, and electronic correlation effects that are often underestimated in purely classical scoring schemes, while retaining computational efficiency. Across 23 protein targets and 543 ligands, the method achieves a mean absolute error of about 1.10 kcal/mol with strong rank-order fidelity (Pearson R = 0.75, Spearman rho = 0.76, Kendall tau = 0.57), consistent with the performance of contemporary FEP protocols. Notably, the workflow requires only about 25 minutes per ligand on standard compute resources, resulting in an approximate 20-fold reduction in computational cost relative to alchemical free energy approaches. This level of accuracy and efficiency makes the method well-suited for high-throughput lead optimization and iterative design cycles in pharmaceutical discovery. The framework also provides a natural foundation for future integration with machine learning models to enable predictive, large-scale, and adaptive screening strategies.

## 从单间隙到六间隙：钻石碳缺陷的计算见解

**Original Title:** From Mono- to Hexa-Interstitials: Computational Insights into Carbon Defects in Diamond

**Authors:** Nima Ghafari Cherati, Arsalan Hashemi, Ádám Gali

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.06167v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.06167v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-05


### 摘要

我们对金刚石中碳自间隙缺陷（从单间隙复合物到六间隙复合物）进行了全面的第一性原理研究。通过原子间势模型的支持下的量子力学密度函数理论，我们有效地对复杂的配置景观进行采样，并识别已知和之前未报告的缺陷几何形状。我们的结果揭示了聚集的明显能量驱动力：从孤立的分裂片状体到紧凑的多片状体，每个片状体的形成能系统性地降低，四片状体成为一个特别稳定的结构基序。此外，电荷分析表明，金刚石中主要的顺时针键在缺陷中心内变得更具电极性。缺陷能量级的分析表明，只有研究的单、二、五和六间隙配合物引入带隙内电子状态，而三和四间隙团簇是电子惰性的。振动光谱学进一步揭示了自光谱会产生特征签名。缺陷核内部的短碳-碳键在1375至1925厘米$之间产生高频振动模式，这些模式具有强IR活性，但拉曼活性较弱。总而言之，这些发现提供了碳自晶体的结构、电子和振动特征的连贯图片，并为其实验识别建立了一个强大的框架。


### Abstract

We present a comprehensive first-principles investigation of carbon self-interstitial defects in diamond, ranging from mono- to hexa-interstitial complexes. By quantum mechanical density functional theory, empowered by interatomic potential models, we efficiently sample the complex configurational landscape and identify both known and previously unreported defect geometries. Our results reveal a pronounced energetic driving force for aggregation: the formation energy per interstitial decreases systematically from isolated split interstitials to compact multi-interstitial clusters, with the tetra-interstitial platelet emerging as a particularly stable structural motif. Additionally, charge analysis indicates that the predominantly covalent bonding in diamond becomes more polar within the defect centers. Analysis of defect energy levels shows that only the investigated mono-, di-, penta-, and hexa-interstitial complexes introduce in-gap electronic states, whereas the tri- and tetra-interstitial clusters are electronically inert. Vibrational spectroscopies further reveal that self-interstitials generate characteristic signatures. Short carbon-carbon bonds inside the defect cores give rise to high-frequency vibrational modes between 1375 and 1925 cm$^{-1}$, which are strongly IR-active but exhibit weak Raman activity. **Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.06940v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.06940v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-07


### 摘要

本文用最优输运方法研究了自由薛定谔方程的一个解。这个解定义的概率测度曲线μ t在Wasserstein空间W_2（\mathbb{R}^3）中是绝对连续的。计算从$μ_t$到$μ_s$的最优传输映射、该传输的成本（即Wasserstein距离）和沿$μ_t$的Fisher信息的值。最后证明，自由薛定谔方程的解可以自然地解释为所谓的形状空间中的曲线，它忘记了空间中的任何位置，而只描述形状的性质。在Shape空间中，$μ_t$仍然是最短路径测地线。


### Abstract

A solution of the free Schrödinger equation is investigated by means of Optimal transport. The curve of probability measures $μ_t$ this solution defines is shown to be an absolutely continuous curve in the Wasserstein space $W_2(\mathbb{R}^3)$. The optimal transport map from $μ_t$ to $μ_s$, the cost for this transport (i.e. the Wasserstein distance) and the value of the Fisher information along $μ_t$ are being calculated. It is finally shown that this solution of the free Schrödinger equation can naturally be interpreted as a curve in so-called Shape space, which forgets any positioning in space but only describes properties of shapes. In Shape space, $μ_t$ continues to be a shortest path geodesic.

## 抑制固体旋转量子位中的快速偶极噪音

**Original Title:** Suppressing Fast Dipolar Noise in Solid-State Spin Qubits

**Authors:** Jaime García Oliván, Ainitze Biteri-Uribarren, Oliver T. Whaites, Jorge Casanova

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.06948v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.06948v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-07


### 摘要

旋转量子位一致性是实现量子技术的基本资源。对于固体平台来说，旋转退散由格子中的磁活性环境主导，限制了其适用性。虽然哈恩回声等标准的动态脱钩技术可以扩展中心旋转相干性，但它们无法抑制浴内强偶极相互作用产生的快噪音。在这里，我们提出了一种脱钩机制Hybrid-LG，它抑制了浴内偶极相互作用（因此，抑制了作用在旋转量子位上的快噪音），并通过高效的内部CCE模拟证明了其在扩展旋转相关性方面的有效性。具体来说，我们研究了最广泛利用的固态量子平台之一：金刚石中氮空位（NV）中心的集合，与大而密集的替代氮顺磁性杂质（P1中心）浴相结合。我们的结果显示，相对于包括P1中心驱动在内的标准技术，NV相关时间至少提高了两倍，而不需要额外的控制功率。


### Abstract

Spin qubit coherence is a fundamental resource for the realization of quantum technologies. For solid-state platforms, spin decoherence is dominated by the magneto-active environment in the lattice, limiting their applicability. While standard dynamical decoupling techniques, such as the Hahn echo, extend central spin coherence, they fail to suppress the fast noise arising from strong dipolar interactions within the bath. Here, we present a decoupling mechanism, Hybrid-LG, that suppresses intra-bath dipolar interactions -- thus, fast noise acting on spin qubits- and demonstrate its effectiveness in extending spin coherence through efficient in-house CCE simulations. Specifically, we investigate one of the most widely exploited solid-state quantum platforms: an ensemble of nitrogen-vacancy (NV) centers in diamond coupled to a large and dense bath of substitutional nitrogen paramagnetic impurities (P1 centers). Our results reveal at least a twofold enhancement in NV coherence time relative to standard techniques including P1 center driving, without requiring additional control power.

## 量子力学的可能扩展

**Original Title:** On possible extensions of quantum mechanics

**Authors:** Yiruo Lin

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.06964v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.06964v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-07


### 摘要

有人认为[1]，量子力学的扩展不可能对自由选择的测量结果具有更好的预测能力，独立于不在其未来光锥中的任何事件。测量选择的假设被批评为过于强烈，在物理上不必要，量子力学的扩展被证明[3]在更宽松的测量假设下是可能的。在这里，我指出批评中的一个错误，并观察到不去定理的实际错误在于[1]的证明中隐含地做出的毫无根据的假设。因此，量子力学只有在测量结果完全确定和完全不确定的情况下才保证具有最大的预测能力。然后我表明，测量假设可以进一步放宽，而不会影响量子力学与替代理论的预测能力的结论。我进一步研究了通过任何替代理论对一对纠缠量子位的局部旋转测量的量子力学的最佳预测改进，并猜测严格的上限。


### Abstract

It was argued [1] that there can be no extension of quantum mechanics with improved predictive power on a measurement freely chosen, independently of any event that is not in its future light cone. The assumption of measurement choice was criticized [2] to be too strong to be physically necessary and extensions of quantum mechanics were shown [3] to be possible under a more relaxed measurement assumption. Here I point out an error in the criticism and observe that the actual mistake of the no-go theorem lies in an unwarranted assumption implicitly made in the proof of [1]. Hence, quantum mechanics is guaranteed to have the maximal predictive power only in situations of complete certainty and complete uncertainty about measurement outcomes. I then show that the measurement assumption can be further relaxed without affecting the conclusion on the predictive power of quantum mechanics versus alternative theories. I further study the optimal predicative improvement over quantum mechanics of local spin measurements on a pair of entangled qubits by any alternative theory and conjecture a strict upper bound.
