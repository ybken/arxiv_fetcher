## 双电子约化密度矩阵的低秩压缩

**Original Title:** Low-rank compression of two-electron reduced density matrices

**Authors:** Kemal Atalar, Hugh G. A. Burton, Andreas Grüneis, George H. Booth

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.11253v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.11253v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-11


### 摘要

两体简化密度矩阵（2 RCM）编码了电子状态的基本两电子物理学，但它们的四次存储成本对实际工作流程构成了重大限制。我们研究了一种简单的协议，将过渡和非过渡2 RSM压缩为较低等级的表示，该表示在截断下保留了它们的楔形积结构和物理对称性。由此产生的分解通过一组公共的低阶因子将库伦和交换通道耦合起来，从而产生比单通道分解更紧凑的阶稀疏表示。对于相关状态，有效等级随系统大小呈线性缩放，从而实现辛烷的耦合簇2 RCM的$\sim 99 $\%压缩，同时保持化学准确性。我们将其应用于最近引入的{\em ab initio}本征向延拓工作流程，其中多体波函数在具有平均场成本的核几何图形上进行插值。在这里，训练状态之间的2 RSM充当子空间的投影仪，但它们的内存扩展限制了应用程序到更大的系统。该压缩方案将存储成本从四次降低到二次，每个电子的固定误差。研究了系统控制分解的条件，从而能够从光激发H$_{28}链的非隔热分子动力学模拟中统计解析的结构、动力学和光谱观测值，并从压缩的近精确DMRG训练数据中进行插值。这为实际的相关电子结构工作流程建立了这些结构保留压缩中间体。


### Abstract

Two-body reduced density matrices (2RDMs) encode the essential two-electron physics of electronic states, but their quartic storage cost poses a major limitation in practical workflows. We investigate a simple protocol to compress both transition and non-transition 2RDMs into a lower-rank representation that preserves their wedge-product structure and physical symmetries under truncation. The resulting decomposition couples Coulomb and exchange channels through a common set of low-rank factors, yielding a more compact rank-sparse representation than single-channel factorizations. For correlated states, the effective rank scales linearly with system size, achieving a $\sim99$\% compression for the coupled-cluster 2RDM of octane while retaining chemical accuracy. We apply this to the recently introduced {\em ab initio} eigenvector continuation workflows, where many-body wave functions are interpolated across nuclear geometries with mean-field cost. Here, 2RDMs between training states act as projectors into a subspace but their memory scaling limits applications to larger systems. The compression scheme reduces the memory cost from quartic to quadratic for a fixed error per electron. Metrics to systematically control the decomposition are investigated, enabling statistically resolved structural, dynamical and spectroscopic observables from nonadiabatic molecular dynamics simulations of photoexcited H$_{28}$ chains, interpolating from compressed near-exact DMRG training data. This establishes these structure-preserving compressed intermediates for practical correlated electronic structure workflows.

## 用于盖驱动腔问题的具有量子可训练嵌入的QPINN框架

**Original Title:** A QPINN Framework with Quantum Trainable Embeddings for the Lid-Driven Cavity Problem

**Authors:** Nahid Binandeh Dehaghani, Ban Q. Tran, Susan Mengel, Rafal Wisniewski, A. Pedro Aguiar

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13892v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.13892v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-12


### 摘要

稳态不可压缩Navier-Stokes方程由于其非线性对流项和压力-速度耦合而带来了巨大的计算挑战。物理信息神经网络（PINN）为逼近此类系统提供了一个无网格框架，但经典PINN在非线性流域中可能会遇到优化困难。在这项工作中，我们提出了一个基于量子物理的神经网络（QPINN）框架，该框架具有基于量子神经网络（QNN）的可训练嵌入，用于解决盖驱动腔问题。所提出的方法使用QNN来学习数据自适应量子特征地图，这些地图对空间坐标进行编码，然后由物理知识损失公式中的变分量子电路进行处理。数值实验表明，与经典PINN和使用经典嵌入的混合量子模型相比，所提出的QNN-TE-QPINN表现出稳定的训练行为和有竞争力的解决方案准确性，同时需要的可训练参数明显更少。这些结果并没有声称计算加速，而是强调了可训练量子嵌入在参数高效的物理信息学习方面的潜力。研究结果表明，嵌入设计在量子辅助的PED求解器中发挥着重要作用，并支持进一步研究基于QNN的可训练嵌入，用于非线性流体动力学基准。


### Abstract

The steady incompressible Navier--Stokes equations pose significant computational challenges due to their nonlinear convective terms and pressure--velocity coupling. Physics-informed neural networks (PINNs) provide a mesh-free framework for approximating such systems, but classical PINNs can experience optimization difficulties in nonlinear flow regimes. In this work, we propose a quantum physics-informed neural network (QPINN) framework with a quantum neural network (QNN)-based trainable embedding for the lid-driven cavity problem. The proposed approach uses a QNN to learn data-adaptive quantum feature maps that encode spatial coordinates before they are processed by a variational quantum circuit within a physics-informed loss formulation. Numerical experiments show that the proposed QNN-TE-QPINN exhibits stable training behavior and competitive solution accuracy compared with classical PINNs and hybrid quantum models using classical embeddings, while requiring significantly fewer trainable parameters. Rather than claiming computational speedup, these results highlight the potential of trainable quantum embeddings for parameter-efficient physics-informed learning. The findings suggest that embedding design plays an important role in quantum-assisted PDE solvers and support further investigation of QNN-based trainable embeddings for nonlinear fluid dynamics benchmarks.

## OAM诱导的格子旋转揭示了容错GKP量子传感中的部分最优

**Original Title:** OAM-Induced Lattice Rotation Reveals a Fractional Optimum in Fault-Tolerant GKP Quantum Sensing

**Authors:** Simanshu Kumar, Nandan S Bisht

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13271v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.13271v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-13


### 摘要

光损失和去相迅速降低了量子传感器的灵敏度，但还不存在设计几何形状同时适应传感任务和噪音通道的误差修正码的系统方法。在这里，我们建立了轨道角动量（OSC）编码和Gottesman-Kitaev-Preskill（GKP）网格几何结构在结构上是耦合的：拓扑荷$的OSC模式引发了相空间旋转$0_\ell=\ell pi/\ell_{\max}$，对应于一类扭曲的GKP稳定器网格。使用端到端可微Strawberry Fields--TensorFlow电路，我们联合优化$\ell$、格子长宽比$r$和有限能量包封$e $，以最大化受$P_{\rm err}影响的量子Fisher信息。最佳值发生在分数电荷$\ell=1.5$（$θ= 67.5\circ $）处，可用半整螺旋相板实现，这相对于方形网格基线将$P_{\rm err}$减少了23.9\x $，同时保留$\mathcal{F}_Q$不变至$0.2\%$内。这超过了最佳整值（$\ell=2$，$15.7\times $），并源于$P_{\rm err}（θ）$景观的精确$180\circ $周期性，并通过分析和数字证实。我们推导出最佳角度$*（n，γ，r）$的超越平衡方程，并证明它随$γ$和$一起减小。香农启发的收件箱容量$\mathCal{C}=\mathCal{F}_Q\csot（-\lnP_{\rm err}）$最大化为$\ell=1.5$，在正方形网格上获得了41美元$的收益，量化了联合灵敏度--故障容忍资源。这些结果为噪音自适应量子传感器建立了几何设计原则，以及可扩展到其他玻色子代码家族的完全开源可微模板。


### Abstract

Photon loss and dephasing rapidly degrade the sensitivity of quantum sensors, yet systematic methods for designing error-correcting codes whose geometry is simultaneously adapted to the sensing task and the noise channel do not exist. Here we establish that orbital-angular-momentum (OAM) encoding and Gottesman-Kitaev-Preskill (GKP) lattice geometry are structurally coupled: an OAM mode of topological charge $\ell$ induces a phase-space rotation $θ_\ell=\ellπ/\ell_{\max}$, corresponding to a family of twisted GKP stabilizer lattices. Using an end-to-end differentiable Strawberry Fields--TensorFlow circuit, we jointly optimise $\ell$, the lattice aspect ratio $r$, and the finite-energy envelope $ε$ to maximise quantum Fisher information subject to $P_{\rm err}\leq10^{-3}$. The optimum occurs at the fractional charge $\ell=1.5$ ($θ=67.5^\circ$), implementable with a half-integer spiral phase plate, which reduces $P_{\rm err}$ by $23.9\times$ relative to the square-lattice baseline while leaving $\mathcal{F}_Q$ unchanged to within $0.2\%$. This surpasses the best integer value ($\ell=2$, $15.7\times$) and arises from an exact $180^\circ$ periodicity of the $P_{\rm err}(θ)$ landscape, confirmed analytically and numerically. We derive a transcendental balance equation for the optimal angle $θ^*(η,γ,r)$ and prove that it decreases with both $γ$ and $η$. A Shannon-inspired metrological capacity $\mathcal{C}=\mathcal{F}_Q\cdot(-\ln P_{\rm err})$, maximised at $\ell=1.5$ with a $41\%$ gain over the square lattice, quantifies the joint sensitivity--fault-tolerance resource. These results establish a geometric design principle for noise-adaptive quantum sensors and a fully open-source differentiable template extensible to other bosonic code families.

## 通过随机抽象的线性时间T门优化

**Original Title:** Linear-Time T-Gate Optimization via Random Abstraction

**Authors:** Aws Albarghouthi

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13929v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.13929v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-13


### 摘要

量子计算机有望为密码学、化学和优化领域的问题带来指数级加速。实现这一承诺需要故障容忍：物理量子位是有噪的，因此逻辑量子位必须使用量子错误纠正码在许多物理量子位之间进行冗余编码。在大多数实际的故障容忍方案中，T门不能横向实现，而是需要涉及一组复杂操作的昂贵魔状态蒸馏协议。因此，T门计数可以主导大规模量子计算的资源预算，使T门计数最小化成为量子优势道路上的核心瓶颈。然而，现有的T计数优化工具无法扩展到量子优势所需的电路。   我们介绍了T门优化的理论和实践结果。在理论方面，我们基于一种新颖的随机静态分析，给出了一种线性时间随机化相折叠算法。我们的静态分析以任意高的概率完全逼近了可达量子状态集。我们的关键见解是一个静态分析，不跟踪符号表达式，但传播恒定宽度的位串的电路。在实践方面，我们的实现，TZAP，比最先进的工具（如PyZX，VOQC和Feynman）快几个数量级，与标准基准测试的T计数减少非常匹配，并且在笔记本电脑上几秒钟内就可以优化具有数百万门的电路。


### Abstract

Quantum computers promise exponential speedups for problems in cryptography, chemistry, and optimization. Realizing this promise requires fault tolerance: physical qubits are noisy, so logical qubits must be encoded redundantly across many physical ones using quantum error-correcting codes. In most practical fault-tolerance schemes, T gates cannot be implemented transversally and instead require costly magic-state distillation protocols involving a complex set of operations. As a result, T-gate count can dominate the resource budget of large-scale quantum computations, making T-count minimization a central bottleneck on the path to quantum advantage. Existing T-count optimization tools, however, do not scale to the circuits that quantum advantage demands.
  We present theoretical and practical results on T-gate optimization. On the theoretical side, we give a linear-time randomized algorithm for phase folding, based on a novel randomized static analysis. Our static analysis soundly approximates the set of reachable quantum states with an arbitrarily high probability. Our key insight is a static analysis that does not track symbolic expressions, but propagates constant-width bitstrings down the circuit. On the practical side, our implementation, TZAP, is multiple orders of magnitude faster than state-of-the-art tools -- such as PyZX, VOQC, and Feynman -- closely matches their T-count reductions on standard benchmarks, and within seconds on a laptop computer can optimize circuits with millions of gates.

## 来自三维金属电荷涨落的费米表面几何

**Original Title:** Fermi Surface Geometry from Charge Fluctuations in Three-Dimensional Metals

**Authors:** Pok Man Tam, Yarden Sheffer, Xiao-Chuan Wu, F. D. M. Haldane, Shinsei Ryu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13951v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.13951v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-13


### 摘要

对于三维非相互作用多带金属，我们表明有关费米表面形状和量子几何形状的重要信息被编码在二体电荷涨落的次领先对数项中。这个对数项与无因次$有关|\mathBF{q}|' 3 $-动量空间中结构因子的系数，这两个量都可以表示为费米表面弯曲张量和量子度量张量的费米表面积分。当真实空间分区表面是二次曲面时（即，球体或椭圆体）时，对数系数满足仅取决于费米表面的欧拉特性和陈数的拓学界限，说明了多带金属中拓学和量子拓学之间的非平凡相互作用。


### Abstract

For three-dimensional non-interacting multi-band metals, we show that important information about the shape and the quantum geometry of Fermi surfaces is encoded in the subleading logarithmic term of bipartite charge fluctuations. This logarithmic term is related to the dimensionless $|\mathbf{q}|^3$-coefficient of the structure factor in momentum space, and both quantities can be expressed as Fermi surface integrals of the Fermi surface curvature tensor and the quantum metric tensor. When the real-space partition surface is a quadric (i.e., sphere or ellipsoid), the logarithmic coefficient satisfies a topological bound depending only on the Euler characteristic and the Chern number of the Fermi surface, illustrating a non-trivial interplay between topology and quantum topology in multi-band metals.

## 双尺度SYK的q-Askey变形

**Original Title:** q-Askey Deformations of Double-Scaled SYK

**Authors:** Sergio E. Aguilar-Gutierrez, Trivko Kukolj, Josef Seitz

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13956v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.13956v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-13


### 摘要

我们构建了双尺度SYK（DSSYN）模型的变形族，并研究了它们的整体解释。我们引入了SYK模型的微观变形，在系综平均后并在双倍尺度限制下，该模型由q-Askey方案中对基本垂直方程的循环关系进行编码的转移矩阵来描述。对于有限温度下半经典极限中的某些变形族，弦数（编码Krylov复杂性）对应于连接世界末日膜和反德西特渐进边界的爱因斯坦-罗森桥的长度。通过增加其中一个变形参数，模型最终表现出离散的能量水平，标志着重力的辛曲线的新几何转变。通过SYK-Schur二元性，Krylov复杂性还接受了一种表示论解释，即SU（2）旋转在$\mathCal{N}=2$ SU（2）规范理论的指数中的传播。我们研究变形理论的运算子代数。这些代数可以是类型II $_1 $或类型I$_\infty$因子，具体取决于包含的运算符。纯状态的II $_1 $型代数之间的纠缠量通过Ryu-Takayanagi公式表现为极端表面。我们讨论了我们的结果与大量婴儿宇宙的出现之间的联系。


### Abstract

We construct families of deformations of the double-scaled SYK (DSSYK) model and investigate their bulk interpretation. We introduce microscopic deformations of the SYK model which, after ensemble averaging and in the double-scaling limit, are described by a transfer matrix encoding the recurrence relations of basic orthogonal polynomials in the q-Askey scheme. For certain families of deformations in the semiclassical limit at finite temperature, the chord number (encoding Krylov complexity) corresponds to the length of an Einstein-Rosen bridge connecting an End-Of-The-World brane to an anti-de Sitter asymptotic boundary. By increasing one of the deformation parameters, the models eventually exhibit discrete energy levels, signaling a new geometric transition in sine dilaton gravity. Via the SYK-Schur duality, Krylov complexity also admits a representation-theoretic interpretation as the spread of the SU(2) spin in the index of an $\mathcal{N}=2$ SU(2) gauge theory. We study the operator algebras of the deformed theories. The algebras can be type II$_1$ or type I$_\infty$ factors, depending on the operators that are included. The entanglement entropy between the type II$_1$ algebras for a pure state manifests as an extremal surface through the Ryu-Takayanagi formula. We discuss connections between our results and the emergence of baby universes in the bulk.

## 跨格子几何、维度和微观耦合的普适旋转压缩动态相转变

**Original Title:** Universal Spin Squeezing Dynamical Phase Transitions across Lattice Geometries, Dimensions, and Microscopic Couplings

**Authors:** Arman Duha, Thomas Bilitewski

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13969v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.13969v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-13


### 摘要

最近的工作确定了功率定律相互作用双层XXZ旋转模型中的动态压缩相转变，将具有海森堡限制压缩的完全集体相与具有普适临界标度的部分集体相分离。在这里，我们测试并建立了沿着两个性质不同的微观轴的这种转变的普遍性：通过研究方形、三角形和蜂窝状的$2\mathrm {D}$双层以及$1\mathrm {D}$梯子，以及相对于层内耦合的保密性重新缩放$。将Bogoliubov不稳定性分析与离散截断的维格纳模拟相结合，我们发现这种转变在所有四种网格几何结构中持续存在，并且临界指数在误差范围内一致，为真正的非平衡普适性类提供了强有力的证据。Bogoliubov理论恢复了之前确定的长期相互作用区$a < d+2$中的缩放$a_Z^* \propto L$，并产生了关键长宽比的分析缩放$a_Z^* \propto L^{2/（a-d）}$，系统大小为$a>d+2$，其中$a $是维度$d$中的乘势律指数。这揭示了以前未被认识到的短程相互作用的亚线性机制。通过调整$，我们改变固定层间距下的层间耦合强度，证明动力学转变可以纯粹通过相互作用工程来驱动，而无需修改基础几何形状。这些发现提供了一种通用途径来控制里德伯格阵列、极分子和俘获离子平台中的纠缠产生，并应用于量子传感和模拟。


### Abstract

Recent work has identified a dynamical squeezing phase transition in power-law interacting bilayer XXZ spin models, separating a fully collective phase with Heisenberg-limited squeezing from a partially-collective phase with universal critical scaling. Here we test and establish the universality of this transition along two qualitatively different microscopic axes: lattice geometry, by studying square, triangular, and honeycomb $2\mathrm{D}$ bilayers as well as $1\mathrm{D}$ ladders, and a symmetry-preserving rescaling $λ$ of the interlayer couplings relative to the intralayer ones. Combining a Bogoliubov instability analysis with discrete truncated Wigner simulations, we find that the transition persists across all four lattice geometries and over a wide range of $λ$ with critical exponents consistent within error, providing strong evidence for a genuine non-equilibrium universality class. The Bogoliubov theory recovers the previously identified scaling $a_Z^* \propto L$ in the long-range interacting regime $α< d+2$, and yields an analytical scaling $a_Z^* \propto L^{2/(α-d)}$ for the critical aspect ratio with system size for $α>d+2$, with $α$ the power-law exponent in dimension $d$. This uncovers a previously unrecognized sub-linear regime for short-range interactions. By tuning $λ$ we vary the interlayer coupling strength at fixed layer spacing, demonstrating that the dynamical transition can be driven purely through interaction engineering without modifying the underlying geometry. These findings provide a versatile route toward controlling entanglement generation in Rydberg-array, polar molecule, and trapped-ion platforms with applications in quantum sensing and simulation.

## 通过旋转轨道相匹配的全电量子状态转移

**Original Title:** All-Electric Quantum State Transfer via Spin-Orbit Phase Matching

**Authors:** Madhumita Sarkar, Roopayan Ghosh, Charles G. Smith, Maksym Myronov, Sougato Bose

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13976v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.13976v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-13


### 摘要

半导体空洞-旋转量子位由于其弱的超精细相互作用和强的内在旋转-轨道耦合使得量子位能够电控制，为量子计算提供了一条有希望的途径。然而，可扩展的架构需要连贯的长距离量子状态转移，而这在这些系统中会受到旋转轨道引起的各向异性交换的阻碍。在这里，我们表明可以通过使用全电动控制协议来克服这个限制。通过调节电场强度，我们确定了离散的旋转轨道相匹配条件，可以恢复近乎完美的状态转移，而与旋转轴无关。补充地，控制电场方向可以对齐旋转轨道轴，抑制激发非保守过程，并在无需微调的情况下实现稳健的传递。我们的结果确定，通过幅度调整或轴对齐对旋转轨道相进行电控制是在孔-旋转量子点阵列中实现稳健量子信息传输的实用途径。


### Abstract

Semiconductor hole-spin qubits offer a promising route to quantum computation due to their weak hyperfine interaction, and strong intrinsic spin-orbit coupling enabling electric control of qubits. Scalable architectures, however, require coherent long-distance quantum state transfer, which is hindered in these systems by spin-orbit induced anisotropic exchange. Here we show that this limitation can be overcome by using an all-electric control protocol. By tuning the electric field strength, we identify discrete spin-orbit phase-matching conditions that restore near-perfect state transfer, independent of the rotation axis. Complementarily, controlling the electric field direction aligns the spin-orbit axis, suppressing excitation non-conserving processes and enabling robust transfer without fine tuning. Our results establish that electrical control of spin-orbit phases through either magnitude tuning or axis alignment as a practical route for robust quantum information transport in hole-spin quantum dot arrays.

## 通过量子经典算法在神经网络中赢得彩票

**Original Title:** Winning Lottery Tickets in Neural Networks via a Quantum-Inspired Classical Algorithm

**Authors:** Natsuto Isogai, Hayata Yamasaki, Sho Sonoda, Mio Murao

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13979v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.13979v1
