## 一种用于颜色感知实验研究的量子信息操作框架

**Original Title:** An Operational Quantum Information Framework for Experimental Studies on Color Perception

**Authors:** Roberto Leporini, Edoardo Provenzi, Michel Berthier

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.00132v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.00132v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-28


### 摘要

Berthier、Provenzi及其合作者从1920年Schrödinger发起并最终在1974年由Resnikoff完善的感知色彩空间的基础公理化开始，最近提出了量子信息框架内感知色彩属性的重新表述。他们的工作基于量子理论的乔丹代数形式主义，更具体地说，基于用实数域上的旋转因子描述的量子系统。这个理论框架不是普通量子力学的理论框架，主要是因为它需要处理重比特，而后者使用量子比特。本文的目的是表明这种差异绝不会阻碍测试颜色感知模型预测有效性的实验协议的实施。特别是，我们展示了如何根据量子位密度矩阵计算感知颜色的基于量子信息的感知属性。


### Abstract

Starting from the foundational axiomatization of the perceptual color space initiated by Schrödinger in 1920 and eventually refined by Resnikoff in 1974, Berthier, Provenzi and their collaborators have recently proposed a reformulation of perceptual color attributes within the framework of quantum information. Their work is based on the Jordan algebra formalism of quantum theories and, more specifically, on a quantum system described by a spin factor over the field of real numbers. This theoretical framework is not that of ordinary quantum mechanics, mainly because it requires dealing with rebits, whereas the latter uses qubits. The aim of this paper is to show that this difference in no way hinders the implementation of experimental protocols for testing the validity of the predictions of the color perception model. In particular, we show how to compute the quantum information based perceptual attributes of perceived colors in terms of qubit density matrices.

## 高中生擅长牛津量子课程使用图形数学

**Original Title:** High schoolers excel at Oxford quantum course using pictorial mathematics

**Authors:** Bob Coecke, Aleks Kissinger, Stefano Gogioso, Selma Dündar-Coecke, Caterina Puca, Lia Yeh, Muhammad Hamza Waseem, Emmanuel M. Pothos, Sieglinde Pfaendler, Vincent Wang-Mascianica, Thomas Cervoni, Ferdi Tomassini, Vincent Anandraj, Peter Sigrist, Ilyas Khan

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.00141v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.00141v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-28


### 摘要

我们正处于第二次量子革命的曙光，我们创建和控制单个量子系统的能力有望推动基础科学、计算和日常生活的变革性进步。然而，量子理论长期以来一直被认为是出了名的难学，这对劳动力发展、利益相关者和政策制定者的知情决策以及更广泛的公众理解造成了重大障碍。   本文关注量子图像主义，这是量子物理学的一种新颖的视觉数学语言。这个严格的图表框架最初是二十多年前开发的，旨在探索量子理论的基础结构，此后已被学术界和工业界采用，成为量子计算研究和软件开发的强大工具。在这里，我们展示了其作为变革性教育方法论的潜力。   我们报告了一项试点研究的结果，涉及54名英国高中生，他们从英国各地的734名志愿者中随机选出。尽管缺乏先进的数学先决条件，但这些学生对关键量子原理和运算表现出了强大的概念掌握。在由大学研究生水平考试问题组成的评估中，参与者的通过率达到了82%，其中48%的人获得了独特级别的成绩。   这些结果为使量子更具包容性、降低量子学习的传统认知和人口障碍铺平了道路。这种方法有可能扩大该领域的参与，并为利益相关者、未来专家和公众提供一个有希望的新切入点。


### Abstract

We are at the dawn of the second quantum revolution, where our ability to create and control individual quantum systems is poised to drive transformative advancements in basic science, computation, and everyday life. However, quantum theory has long been conceived as notoriously hard to learn, creating a significant barrier to workforce development, informed decision-making by stakeholders and policymakers, and broader public understanding.
  This paper is concerned with Quantum Picturalism, a novel visual mathematical language for quantum physics. Originally developed over two decades ago to explore the foundational structure of quantum theory, this rigorous diagrammatic framework has since been adopted in both academia and industry as a powerful tool for quantum computing research and software development. Here, we demonstrate its potential as a transformative educational methodology.
  We report the findings from a pilot study involving 54 UK high school students, randomly selected from a pool of 734 volunteers across the UK. Despite the absence of advanced mathematical prerequisites, these students demonstrated a strong conceptual grasp of key quantum principles and operations. On an assessment comprising university graduate-level exam questions, participants achieved an 82% pass rate, with 48% obtaining a distinction-level grade.
  These results pave the way for making quantum more inclusive, lowering traditional cognitive and demographic barriers to quantum learning. This approach has the potential to broaden participation in the field and provide a promising new entry point for stakeholders, future experts, and the general public.

## 将贝尔不等式定制到qudit toric代码和自我测试

**Original Title:** Tailoring Bell inequalities to the qudit toric code and self testing

**Authors:** Eloïc Vallée, Owidiusz Makuta, Patrick Emonts, Rhine Samajdar, Jordi Tura

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.00146v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.00146v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-28


### 摘要

贝尔非定域性为量子状态的有效认证提供了一种稳健的可扩展途径。在这里，我们引入了一个通用框架，用于构造针对奇素局部维的$\mathbb{Z}_d$ toric码量身定制的贝尔不等式。选择稳定器操作符的合适子集并将其映射到广义测量可观测量，我们计算其量子极大值允许平方和分解的多方贝尔表达。我们表明，这些不等式最大限度地被$\mathbb{Z}_d$ toric码的基状态管中的所有状态违反，并通过组合瓦片参数和显式优化的组合来确定它们的经典（局部）界限。作为具体应用，我们分析了$d=3$的情况，并证明最大违反自测试完整的qutrit复码子空间，直到局部等距和复共聚。据我们所知，这是有史以来第一个自我测试quutrit子空间的例子。扩展这些构造，我们进一步提出了提高经典量子界比的方案，从而提高对实验缺陷的鲁棒性。我们的结果为高度纠缠的拓扑量子物质的设备独立认证建立了一条途径，并为在错误纠正代码和量子模拟平台中验证qudit状态提供了新工具。


### Abstract

Bell nonlocality provides a robust scalable route to the efficient certification of quantum states. Here, we introduce a general framework for constructing Bell inequalities tailored to the $\mathbb{Z}_d$ toric code for odd prime local dimensions. Selecting a suitable subset of stabilizer operators and mapping them to generalized measurement observables, we compute multipartite Bell expressions whose quantum maxima admit a sum-of-squares decomposition. We show that these inequalities are maximally violated by all states in the ground-state manifold of the $\mathbb{Z}_d$ toric code, and determine their classical (local) bounds through a combination of combinatorial tiling arguments and explicit optimization. As a concrete application, we analyze the case of $d=3$ and demonstrate that the maximal violation self-tests the full qutrit toric-code subspace, up to local isometries and complex conjugation. This constitutes, to our knowledge, the first-ever example of self-testing a qutrit subspace. Extending these constructions, we further present schemes to enhance the ratio of classical--quantum bounds and thus improve robustness to experimental imperfections. Our results establish a pathway toward device-independent certification of highly entangled topological quantum matter and provide new tools for validating qudit states in error-correcting codes and quantum simulation platforms.

## 当中心很重要时：二面体格子规范理论中的色彩屏蔽和胶团

**Original Title:** When the center matters: color screening and gluelumps in dihedral lattice gauge theories

**Authors:** Pavel P. Popov, Edoardo Ballini, Alberto Bottarelli, Michele Burrello, Pietro Silvi, Matteo M. Wauters, Philipp Hauke

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.00152v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.00152v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-28


### 摘要

限制是量子色动力学（QCD）的标志之一。然而，即使在更简单的模型中，它的第一原则描述仍然难以捉摸。通过群论论证和数值分析的结合，我们表明，一类离散非阿贝尔格点规范理论（LGT）（二面体群$D_N$）中束缚的物理后果与$\mathbb{Z}_2$中心亚群的存在密切相关。当中心很平凡时（对于奇数$N$），静电荷被胶子云屏蔽，形成SU$（N）$规范理论中称为胶子团的复合物体。这一发现意味着弦断裂只能通过电场的波动发生，而不需要从真空中使粒子-反粒子对成核。此外，数值分析暗示连续极限下胶团之间存在有限范围的相互作用。   我们的结果展示了通常与QCD相关的丰富而复杂的物理学如何在更简单的离散非阿贝尔LGT中出现，使它们成为在数值计算和近期量子设备中测试这种现象的理想环境。


### Abstract

Confinement is one of the hallmarks of quantum chromodynamics (QCD). Yet, its first-principle characterization, even in simpler models, remains elusive. Through a combination of group-theoretical arguments and numerical analysis, we show that the physical consequences of confinement in a class of discrete non-Abelian lattice gauge theories (LGTs), the dihedral groups $D_N$, are intimately connected with the presence of a $\mathbb{Z}_2$ central subgroup. When the center is trivial (for odd $N$), static charges are screened by a gluon cloud, forming composite objects known in SU$(N)$ gauge theories as gluelumps. This finding implies that string breaking can occur through fluctuations of the electric field only, without the need to nucleate particle--antiparticle pairs from the vacuum. Furthermore, numerical analysis hints at finite-range interactions between the gluelumps in the continuum limit.
  Our results showcase how the rich and intricate physics typically associated with QCD can emerge in much simpler discrete non-Abelian LGTs, making them ideal settings to test this phenomenology both in numerical calculations and in near-term quantum devices.

### Abstract

## 多粒子Carroll-Schrdinger量子系统的动力学

**Original Title:** On the Dynamics of Multiparticle Carroll-Schrdinger Quantum Systems

**Authors:** José Rojas, Melvin Arias

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.00247v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.00247v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-28


### 摘要

我们研究$1{+}1$维的多粒子卡罗尔-薛定汉（CS）量子系统的动力学，其中$x$作为进化变量，$t$作为位姿坐标。我们在等-$x$切片上推导出$N$-body理论，作为相对论多时间克莱因-戈登模型的卡洛利极限，通过与时间能量操作符的最小耦合引入时间相互作用。$x$相关规范变换将其映射到具有显式多体势的等效描述，由表现出同步的时间耦合振子模型来说明。采用具有静态势的补充空间观点$U_{\！tot}（\mathBF x）$，我们表明进化是由集体力量驱动的$\sum_j\partial_{x_j}U_{\！tot}$;对于任何平移不变的相互作用（例如正规化的库伦势），这些内部力量都会抵消，使集体动力学自由并凸显卡罗尔超局部性。我们还构造了一个通过施瓦茨求导可分离的薛定格汉密尔顿元到CS生成元的坐标二元映射。交换对称性是在时间域中公式化的，通过二阶相干函数$g^{（2）}（t，t '）$产生玻色子的时间聚束和费米子的反聚束。在第二次量子化中，接触极限产生了一个时间求导的三次五次非线性薛定格方程，其非线性系数理论上固定$β=-3/16$。最后，通过将典范对耦合到外部纯量场和规范场，我们建立了具有一维电流密度函数理论的同质性，概述了Carrollian Hohenberg-Kohn映射和Kohn-Sham方案。


### Abstract

We study the dynamics of multiparticle Carroll-Schrödinger (CS) quantum systems in $1{+}1$ dimensions, where $x$ acts as the evolution variable and $t$ as the configuration coordinate. We derive the $N$-body theory on equal-$x$ slices as the Carrollian limit of a relativistic multi-time Klein-Gordon model, introducing temporal interactions via minimal coupling to the temporal energy operators. An $x$-dependent gauge transformation maps this to an equivalent description with explicit many-body potentials, illustrated by a temporal coupled-oscillator model that exhibits synchronization. Adopting a complementary spatial viewpoint with a static potential $U_{\!tot}(\mathbf x)$, we show that the evolution is driven by the collective force $\sum_j\partial_{x_j}U_{\!tot}$; for any translation-invariant interaction (such as a regularized Coulomb potential), these internal forces cancel, rendering the collective dynamics free and highlighting Carrollian ultralocality. We also construct a coordinate duality mapping separable Schrödinger Hamiltonians to CS generators via Schwarzian derivatives. Exchange symmetry is formulated in the time domain, yielding temporal bunching for bosons and antibunching for fermions via the second-order coherence function $g^{(2)}(t,t')$. In second quantization, the contact limit yields a temporal derivative cubic--quintic nonlinear Schrödinger equation with a theoretically fixed nonlinearity coefficient $β=-3/16$. Finally, by coupling canonical pairs to external scalar and gauge fields, we establish an isomorphism with one-dimensional current-density functional theory, outlining a Carrollian Hohenberg-Kohn mapping and Kohn-Sham scheme.

## 用于大容量卫星纠缠分布的多模式量子存储器

**Original Title:** Multi-Mode Quantum Memories for High-Throughput Satellite Entanglement Distribution

**Authors:** Connor Casey, Albert Williams, Catherine McCaffrey, Eugene Rotherham, Nathan Darby

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.00282v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.00282v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-29


### 摘要

量子网络寻求通过陆地和自由空间通道实现全球纠缠分布;然而，这些通道的指数级损失需要配备高效、长寿命量子存储器（QM）的量子转发器。基于空间的架构，特别是卫星辅助链路，提供了一条真正全球连接的途径，但它们要求QM与红外辐射等轨道因素和在卫星上操作的独特挑战兼容。在这项工作中，我们提出了一种基于原子频梳（AFC）协议的低地球轨道（LEO）转发器的多模式量子存储器（MMQM）。我们的设计在光学腔中集成了混合碱性稀有气体系综，使用碱性原子进行强量子物质耦合，并使用稀有气体核旋转进行数分钟到数小时的连贯性，所有这些都不需要低温。该架构原生支持时间和频谱多路传输，能够存储100种模式，以并行化概率操作并克服轻度限制的往返时间。代表性的链路预算在$h = 500$ km（实际口径为$n_{\text{}\gtrsim 70%$和$t_{\text{buffer}}$几分钟预测，相对于无记忆双下行链路，每次通过成功概率和瞬时SKR将提高高达两个数量级，并且在$N$中有明显的扩展。我们的贡献是（i）非低温、空间就绪的多模式存储器，（ii）将模式计数、存储时间和轨道几何形状与可实现速率相结合的系统分析，以及（iii）近期实施路线图。总而言之，这些结果表明了当前到近期技术的可行性，并为实现高速率、空间使能量子互联网提供了一条实用的途径。

Quantum networking seeks to enable global entanglement distribution through terrestrial and free space channels; however, the exponential loss in these channels necessitates quantum repeaters with efficient, long lived quantum memories (QMs). Space based architectures, particularly satellite assisted links, offer a path to truly global connectivity, yet they demand QMs that are compatible with orbital factors such as infrared radiation and the unique challenges of operating aboard a satellite. In this work, we propose a multimode quantum memory (MMQM) for low Earth orbit (LEO) repeaters based on the atomic frequency comb (AFC) protocol. Our design integrates a hybrid alkali noble gas ensemble in an optical cavity, using alkali atoms for strong photon matter coupling and noble gas nuclear spins for minutes to hours coherence, all without the need for cryogenics. The architecture natively supports temporal and spectral multiplexing, enabling the storage of 100 modes to parallelize probabilistic operations and overcome light limited round trip times. Representative link budgets at $h = 500$ km with realistic apertures, $η_{\text{mem}}\gtrsim 70%$, and $t_{\text{buffer}}$ of several minutes predict improvements of up to two orders of magnitude in per pass success probability and instantaneous SKR relative to a memoryless dual downlink, with clear scaling in $N$. Our contributions are (i) a non cryogenic, space ready multimode memory, (ii) a systems analysis coupling mode count, storage time, and orbital geometry to achievable rate, and (iii) a near term implementation roadmap. Together, these results indicate feasibility with current to near term technology and provide a practical path toward a high rate, space enabled quantum internet.

### 摘要

利用微扰理论构造了沿[001]晶体学方向生长的圆柱形HgTE纳米线的低能量有效Hamilton。考虑了凯恩模型的各向异性项和体反转不对称项。尽管各向异性项已将$E_{1}$和$H_{1}$子带之间的交叉转换为$k_{z}R\！=\！0$，子带结构中的间隙关闭和重新打开转变仍然可以发生在波向$k_{z}R\！\大约\！\ pm0.24$对于临界纳米线半径$R\！\大约\！3.45$纳米。体反相不对称性不会影响低能量有效Hamilton，即，对于[001]取向的圆柱形纳米线，$E_{1}$、$H_{1}$和$H_{2}$子带中不存在旋转分裂。


### Abstract

The low-energy effective Hamiltonian of a cylindrical HgTe nanowire grown along the [001] crystallographic direction is constructed by using the perturbation theory. ## 2+1维普遍角纠缠量的精确计算：从伊辛到高斯量子临界点

**Original Title:** Precise computation of universal corner entanglement entropy at 2+1 dimension: From Ising to Gaussian quantum critical points

**Authors:** Ben Lee-Yeung Ngai, Justin Tim-Lok Chau, Junchen Rong, Yuan Da Liao, Zi Yang Meng

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.00382v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.00382v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-29


### 摘要

计算（2+1）d量子多体系统纠缠态（EE）中的次领先对数项仍然是一个重大挑战，尽管它在揭示有关量子状态和量子临界点（QCP）的普适信息方面发挥着关键作用。利用最新的算法进步，使EE能够稳定计算为指数观测值，我们开发了一种{\it bubble based}投影量子蒙特卡罗（QMC）算法，以精确有效地计算沿着连接伊辛和高斯QCP的参数轨迹的宇宙角EE;在后一种情况下，存在一个可以进行基准测试的分析值。我们的方法直接消除了QMC抽样中的主导面积定律项，从而将令人垂涎的次领先对数项提升为领先贡献。我们应用这种技术来计算增强了四体相互作用的（2+1）d方格子横场伊辛模型中的EE，追踪从伊辛到高斯QCP，然后到一级转变的路径。沿着路径获得的通用角EE的一致值验证了我们提取具有挑战性的纠缠属性的方法的可靠性和精确性。这些结果建立了精确可解极限的普适值与（2+1）d处强相关制度的普适值之间长期寻求的联系。


### Abstract

Computing the subleading logarithmic term in the entanglement entropy (EE) of (2+1)d quantum many-body systems remains a significant challenge, despite its critical role in revealing universal information about quantum states and quantum critical points (QCPs). Capitalizing on recent algorithmic advances that enable the stable calculation of EE as an exponential observable, we develop a {\it bubble basis} projector quantum Monte Carlo (QMC) algorithm to precisely and efficiently compute the universal corner EE along a parameter trajectory connecting the Ising and Gaussian QCPs; where in the latter case there exists an analytic value to benchmark with. Our approach eliminates the dominant area-law term directly within the QMC sampling, thereby promoting the coveted subleading logarithmic term to the leading contribution. We apply this technique to calculate the EE in a (2+1)d square-lattice transverse-field Ising model augmented with a four-body interaction, tracing a path from the Ising to the Gaussian QCPs and then to the first-order transition. The consistent values obtained for the universal corner EE along the path validate the reliability and precision of our approach for extracting challenging entanglement properties. These results establish the long-sought connection between the universal values of an exactly solvable limit and those of a strongly correlated regime at (2+1)d.

## UNIQ：通过统一非线性MIDI编程实现通信高效的分布式量子计算

We further develop analytical criterion for level crossing, which serves as a condition for identifying quantum critical points in both homonuclear and heteronuclear systems, and apply it to multiple settings to analyze their quantum critical points. We establish a direct link between the quantum entanglement quantifier and experimentally accessible NMR observables, enabling entanglement to be quantified through NMR signal processing. This provides a practical framework for characterizing quantum correlations using standard NMR experiments. These findings provide insights into the thermal control of quantum features, with implications for quantum-enhanced NMR, low-temperature spectroscopy, and emerging quantum technologies.

## 用于量子多体相转变实验检测的无监督机器学习

**Original Title:** Unsupervised Machine Learning for Experimental Detection of Quantum-Many-Body Phase Transitions

**Authors:** Ron Ziv, David Wei, Antonio Rubio-Abadal, Daniel Adler, Anna Keselman, Eran Lustig, Ronen Talmon, Johannes Zeiher, Immanuel Bloch, Mordechai Segev

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.01091v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.01091v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-30


### 摘要

量子多体（QMB）系统通常在计算上很困难：准确模拟它们所需的计算资源通常可以超过现有计算资源几个数量级。出于这个原因，理查德·费曼提出了量子模拟器的概念：量子系统被设计为遵守规定的进化方程并多次重复实验。然而，从实验上来说，正如我们在下面解释的那样，描述该系统的绝大多数可观测量都是不可访问的。因此，虽然费曼的想法解决了模拟量子动力学的问题，但它仍然没有解决同样基本的问题，即从实验中可用的有限可观测量推断基础物理学。事实上，许多与QMB系统相关的复杂现象仍然难以捉摸。也许，最重要的例子是在不存在简单顺序参数时识别QMB系统中的相转变，这对今天构成了重大挑战。使问题进一步复杂化的是，在大多数情况下，不可能从数值模拟中学习，因为基础系统通常太大而无法计算，而小型QMB可能表现出强烈的有限大小效应，掩盖了转变的存在。在这里，我们提出了一种研究QMB实验的无监督机器学习方法，专门旨在直接从原始实验测量中检测相转变和交叉。我们在经历多体定位交叉和莫特到超流相转变的系统上展示了我们的方法，表明它从非常部分的实验数据中揭示了集体现象，并且没有任何特定于模型的系统先验知识。这种方法为复杂量子多体系统中的新兴现象的数据驱动发现提供了一种通用且可扩展的途径。


### Abstract

Quantum many-body (QMB) systems are generally computationally hard: the computing resources necessary to simulate them exactly can often exceed the existing computation resources by orders of magnitude. For this reason, Richard Feynman proposed the concept of a quantum simulator: quantum systems engineered to obey a prescribed evolution equation and repeating the experiment multiple times. Experimentally, however, as we explain below, the vast majority of observables describing the system are inaccessible. Thus, while Feynman's idea addresses the problem of simulating quantum dynamics, it leaves unsolved the equally fundamental problem of inferring the underlying physics from the limited observables accessible in experiments. Indeed, many complex phenomena associated with QMB systems remain elusive. Perhaps, the most important example is identifying phase transitions in QMB systems when no simple order-parameter exists, which poses major challenges to this day. Complicating the problem further is the fact that, in most cases, it is impossible to learn from numerical simulations, as the underlying systems are often too large to be computable, and small QMB can show strong finite size effects, masking the presence of the transition. Here, we present an unsupervised machine learning approach to study QMB experiments, specifically aimed at detecting phase transitions and crossovers directly from raw experimental measurements. We demonstrate our methodology on systems undergoing Many-Body Localization cross-over and Mott-to-Superfluid phase-transition, showing that it reveals collective phenomena from the very partial experimental data and without any model-specific prior knowledge of the system. This approach offers a general and scalable route for data-driven discovery of emergent phenomena in complex quantum many-body systems.

## Spin-$1/2$核磁共振系统中的非经典性分析和纠缠见证

**Original Title:** Nonclassicality Analysis and Entanglement Witnessing in Spin-$1/2$ NMR Systems

**Authors:** Fatemeh Khashami

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.01100v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.01100v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-30


### 摘要

我们研究了外磁场下热平衡下双旋转$1/2$核磁共振系统的量子特征和非经典性质。更具体地说，使用合适的量化器，我们分析核磁共振系统中的量子相关性、混合性和纠缠性，并检查它们在系统中的特征。我们推导出量子元素的封闭式分析表达，并展示它们如何依赖于温度和磁场强度。我们证明，在零温度下，该系统表现出量子临界点，其特征是相干性测量中的非分析行为和混合性中的尖锐峰值。此外，我们使用合适的纠缠证人来分析系统的纠缠。这为测试核磁共振系统中的纠缠提供了一个实验友好的设置。换句话说，证人将系统中的纠缠与量子可观测量联系起来，使其可以在核磁共振实验中直接证明。我们在量子信息量化器和系统的实验可访问的核磁共振光谱之间建立了联系，从而能够通过核磁共振信号处理量化纠缠、相关性和混合性。


### Abstract

We investigate quantum features and non-classical nature of two-spin-$1/2$ NMR systems at thermal equilibrium under external magnetic fields. More specifically, using suitable quantifiers, we analyze quantum coherence, mixedness, and entanglement in NMR systems and examine their features within the system. We derive closed-form analytical expressions for the quantum elements and show how they depend on temperature and magnetic field strength. We demonstrate that at zero temperature, the system exhibits a quantum critical point, characterized by non-analytic behavior in the measures of coherence, and a sharp peak in mixedness. Moreover, we analyze the entanglement of the system using a suitable entanglement witness. This provides an experimentally friendly setting for testing entanglement in NMR systems. In other words, the witness links the entanglement in the system to quantum observables, making it directly provable in NMR experiments. We establish a connection between quantum information quantifiers and experimentally accessible NMR spectra of the system, enabling the quantification of entanglement, coherence, and mixedness through NMR signal processing.
