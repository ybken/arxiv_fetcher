## 用于超导器件中通用连续变量计算的构建模块

**Original Title:** Building Block For Universal Continuous Variables Computation In Superconducting Devices

**Authors:** Bruno A. Veloso, Ciro M. Diniz, Luiz O. R. Solak, Antonio S. M. de Castro, Daniel Z. Rossatto, Celso J. Villas-Bôas

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.00212v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.00212v2

**Submitted:** 2026-03-31


### 摘要

连续变量（CV）量子计算通过利用玻色子模的无限维Hilbert空间提供了基于量子位的计算的替代方案。尽管最近取得了进展，但超导体平台尚未展示出能够进行通用计算的可扩展架构。在这里，我们设计并数值模拟了一种双层超导架构，该架构在实验可访问的区域内实现了通用CV门组的所有五种相互作用（旋转、位移、压缩、克尔和分束器）。为此，我们使用DC-SQUID作为玻色子模，使用fluxium量子位来调解非线性相互作用，以及两个辅助量子位来实现高斯和多模式操作。通过调整通量和频率，我们在最先进的参数范围内的所有门上实现了高灵敏度（$\geq 98\%$）。该设计的模块化性质允许直接扩展，建立了一条可行的途径，实现基于超导电路的高保真、通用CV量子计算。


### Abstract

Continuous variable (CV) quantum computation offers an alternative to qubit-based computing by exploiting the infinite-dimensional Hilbert space of bosonic modes. Despite recent progress, superconducting platforms have yet to demonstrate a scalable architecture capable of universal computation. Here, we design and numerically simulate a two-layer superconducting architecture that implements all five interactions of the universal CV gate set (rotation, displacement, squeezing, Kerr, and beam splitter) within experimentally accessible regimes. To this end, we employ a DC-SQUID as the bosonic mode, a fluxonium qubit to mediate nonlinear interactions, and two ancillary qubits that enable Gaussian and multi-mode operations. By tuning fluxes and frequencies, we achieve high fidelities ($\geq 98\%$) across all gates within state-of-the-art parameter ranges. The modular nature of the design allows straightforward scaling, establishing a feasible pathway toward high-fidelity, universal CV quantum computation based on superconducting circuits.

## 直接可视化量子点分子的能量结构

**Original Title:** Directly visualizing the energy level structure of quantum dot molecules

**Authors:** Heun Mo Yoo, Tanner M. Janda, Connor Nasseraddin, Jason R. Petta

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.00232v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.00232v2

**Submitted:** 2026-03-31


### 摘要

硅量子点的轨道、旋转和谷自由度支持许多旋转量子位操作模式。然而，在大参数空间范围内获得有关能量级谱的信息通常具有挑战性。我们展示了一种光谱学形式，它能够将双量子点的能量结构映射为水平失调、点间隧道耦合和磁场的函数。在单电子区域中，随着点间隧道耦合的增加，我们直接观察到从孤立量子点的类原子能量级到类分子成键和反成键状态的转变。我们还解决了磁场中地磁场和激发谷态的塞曼分裂。在双电子状态中，我们可以获得与失调相关的单重三重态分裂。我们的工作可能会扩展到更广泛的系统类别，例如强旋转轨道材料或接近量子点，从而允许直接提取各种能间隙。


### Abstract

The orbital, spin and valley degrees of freedom in silicon quantum dots support many modes of spin qubit operation. However, it is generally challenging to obtain information about the energy level spectrum over large ranges of parameter space. We demonstrate a form of spectroscopy that is capable of mapping the energy level structure of a double quantum dot as a function of level detuning, interdot tunnel coupling, and magnetic field. In the one electron regime, we directly observe the transition from the atom like energy levels of isolated quantum dots to molecular like bonding and anti bonding states with increasing interdot tunnel coupling. We also resolve the Zeeman splitting of ground and excited valley states in a magnetic field. In the two electron regime, we gain access to the detuning dependent singlet triplet splitting. Our work may be extended to a broader class of systems, such as strong spin-orbit materials or proximitized quantum dots, allowing the direct extraction of various energy gaps.

## 利用张量网络前端进行量子增强处理，用于隐私意识的联邦医疗诊断

**Original Title:** Quantum-Enhanced Processing with Tensor-Network Frontends for Privacy-Aware Federated Medical Diagnosis

**Authors:** Hiroshi Yamauchi, Anders Peter Kragh Dalskov, Hideaki Kawaguchi, Rodney Van Meter

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01616v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.01616v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-02


### 摘要

我们提出了一个用于联邦医学图像分类的隐私感知混合框架，该框架结合了张量网络表示学习、MPC安全聚合和聚合后量子细化。该框架的动机是隐私感知联邦学习中的两个实际限制：MPC可能会引入大量的通信负担，并且用少量量子位对多维医学图像进行直接量子处理是不现实的。为了解决单一架构内的两个约束，客户端张量网络前端、矩阵积状态（MPS）、树张量网络（TTN）和多尺度纠缠重正化类比（MERA）将本地输入压缩为紧凑的潜在表示，之后量子增强处理器（QEP）通过量子状态嵌入和基于可观测的读出来细化聚集的潜在特征。喀麦隆MNIST上的实验表明，QEP的效果取决于前端，而不是跨架构一致的。在目前的环境下，TTN+QEP组合展现出最平衡的整体轮廓。结果还表明，当量子位计数与潜在维度充分匹配时，QEP的表现更加稳定，而相对于无噪音设置，有噪音的条件会降低性能。MPC基准进一步表明，通信成本主要由受保护的潜在代表的维度决定。这表明张量网络压缩发挥着双重作用：它能够对压缩的潜在特征进行小量子位量子处理，并减少与安全聚合相关的通信负担。总而言之，这些结果支持协同设计的角度，其中表示压缩、聚合后量子细化和隐私感知部署应该联合优化。


### Abstract

We propose a privacy-aware hybrid framework for federated medical image classification that combines tensor-network representation learning, MPC-secured aggregation, and post-aggregation quantum refinement. The framework is motivated by two practical constraints in privacy-aware federated learning: MPC can introduce substantial communication overhead, and direct quantum processing of high-dimensional medical images is unrealistic with a small number of qubits. To address both constraints within a single architecture, client-side tensor-network frontends, Matrix Product State (MPS), Tree Tensor Network (TTN), and Multi-scale Entanglement Renormalization Ansatz (MERA), compress local inputs into compact latent representations, after which a Quantum-Enhanced Processor (QEP) refines the aggregated latent feature through quantum-state embedding and observable-based readout. Experiments on PneumoniaMNIST show that the effect of the QEP is frontend-dependent rather than uniform across architectures. In the present setting, the TTN+QEP combination exhibits the most balanced overall profile. The results also suggest that the QEP behaves more stably when the qubit count is sufficiently matched to the latent dimension, while noisy conditions degrade performance relative to the noiseless setting. The MPC benchmark further shows that communication cost is governed primarily by the dimension of the protected latent representation. This indicates that tensor-network compression plays a dual role: it enables small-qubit quantum processing on compressed latent features and reduces the communication overhead associated with secure aggregation. Taken together, these results support a co-design perspective in which representation compression, post-aggregation quantum refinement, and privacy-aware deployment should be optimized jointly.

## 通过耦合光学机械腔的相增强非互惠光-音素转换

**Original Title:** Phase-enhanced nonreciprocal photon-phonon conversion via coupled optomechanical cavities

**Authors:** Divya Mishra, Parvendra Kumar

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.01879v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.01879v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-02


### 摘要

以方向相关的信号传播为特征的非互惠性是隔离器、信号路由和精确传感等技术的基础。这封信从理论上证明了非互惠音素输运以及耦合光机腔中通过相依赖驱动实现的光机械腔中的光与声音素信号之间的转换。研究表明，与非可逆的音素输运（需要消散和相诱导的时间反转对称性破坏）相反，光-音素转换中的非可逆性可以在不违反时间反转对称性的情况下发生。我们证明，这种非互惠性是由于光-光子转换中路径相关的不对称性而产生的。此外，我们证明，通过适当修改驱动激光器的相差，可以进一步增强光-光子转换的非可逆性，实现高达40分贝的隔离水平。


### Abstract

Nonreciprocity, characterized by direction-dependent signal propagation, is fundamental to technologies such as isolators, signal routing, and precision sensing. This letter theoretically demonstrates nonreciprocal phonon transport and the conversion between photon and acoustic phonon signals in coupled optomechanical cavities via phase-dependent driving. It is demonstrated that, in contrast to nonreciprocal phonon transport, which necessitates both dissipation and phase-induced violation of time reversal symmetry, the nonreciprocity in photon-phonon conversion can occur without violating time reversal symmetry. We demonstrate that such nonreciprocity arises due to the path-dependent asymmetry in photon-phonon conversion. Furthermore, we demonstrate that the nonreciprocity of photon-phonon conversion can be further enhanced, achieving isolation levels of up to 40 dB by suitably modifying the phase difference of the driving lasers.

## 逼近解算器上约束优化惩罚权重的可扩展确定

**Original Title:** Scalable Determination of Penalization Weights for Constrained Optimizations on Approximate Solvers

**Authors:** Edoardo Alessandroni, Sergi Ramos-Calderer, Michel Krispin, Fritz Schinkel, Stefan Walter, Martin Kliesch, Leandro Aolita, Ingo Roth

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02416v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.02416v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-02


### 摘要

二次无约束二元优化（QUBO）为各种计算问题提供了问题公式，这些问题可以使用专用的QUBO求解器来解决，这些求解器可以基于经典或量子计算。约束组合优化问题的一种常见方法是通过添加惩罚项来强制实施QUBO公式中的约束。惩罚引入了一个额外的超参数，会显着影响求解器的功效：客观项和惩罚项之间的相对权重。我们开发了一种预计算策略，用于确定惩罚权重，并为Gibbs求解器提供可证明保证，为广泛问题类提供多项复杂性。跨各种问题和求解器架构的实验（包括富士通Digital Annealer上的大规模实例）显示出比现有启发式方法强大的性能和数量级的加速。


### Abstract

Quadratic unconstrained binary optimization (QUBO) provides problem formulations for various computational problems that can be solved with dedicated QUBO solvers, which can be based on classical or quantum computation. A common approach to constrained combinatorial optimization problems is to enforce the constraints in the QUBO formulation by adding penalization terms. Penalization introduces an additional hyperparameter that significantly affects the solver's efficacy: the relative weight between the objective terms and the penalization terms. We develop a pre-computation strategy for determining penalization weights with provable guarantees for Gibbs solvers and polynomial complexity for broad problem classes. Experiments across diverse problems and solver architectures, including large-scale instances on Fujitsu's Digital Annealer, show robust performance and order-of-magnitude speedups over existing heuristics.

## 无需统计平均即可证明的量子热化

**Original Title:** Provable quantum thermalization without statistical averages

**Authors:** Amit Vikram

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02417v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.02417v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-02


### 摘要

我们开发了一种严格的系统不可知方法来预测多体系统中绝大多数可访问纯状态中的量子热化，完全根据少数体可观测量的某些超时有序的相关子。与之前关于热化的严格结果不同，我们的方法并不限于可观测量的统计平均值，例如各遍性的时间平均值或混合中的状态平均值。此外，与此类方法一致，我们保留了不需要对能量本征状态结构或热力学大时间的详细知识的优势，这对于具有少数粒子的系统来说可能变得棘手。我们的方法以一个几何结果为中心，该结果将热化与希尔伯特空间中的高维子空间的排列联系起来，这是由“可控非局部”的非时序相关子的饱和决定的。这种形式主义将在几乎所有复杂的多体状态中在有限时间建立纯状态量子热化的问题简化为对少体脉动的理论或实验上可行的研究，即使是在热力学大的系统中。


### Abstract

We develop a rigorous system-agnostic method to predict quantum thermalization in an overwhelming fraction of accessible pure states in a many-body system, entirely in terms of certain out-of-time-ordered correlators of few-body observables. In contrast to previous rigorous results on thermalization with semiclassical counterparts, our method is not limited to statistical averages of observables, such as time averages in ergodicity or state averages in mixing. Moreover, consistent with such approaches, we retain the advantage of not requiring a detailed knowledge of energy eigenstate structure or thermodynamically large times, which can become intractable for systems with more than a handful of particles. Our approach is centered on a geometric result that connects thermalization to the alignment of high dimensional subspaces in a Hilbert space, which is determined by the saturation of "controllably nonlocal" out-of-time-ordered correlators. This formalism reduces the problem of establishing pure state quantum thermalization at finite times in almost all complex many-body states to a theoretically or experimentally accessible study of few-body correlators, even in thermodynamically large systems.

## 将状态的纠缠与其光谱结合起来

**Original Title:** Bounding the entanglement of a state from its spectrum

**Authors:** Jofre Abellanet-Vidal, Guillem Müller-Rigat, Albert Rico, Anna Sanpera

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02420v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.02420v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-02


### 摘要

最近的努力重点是描述一组无法被任何全球单一化转型纠缠在一起的可分离国家。在这里，我们描述了纠缠内容在任何正正性下都不能增加的一组状态。通过使用线性映射（及其逆映射），我们从密度矩阵的谱中推导出对可实现纠缠度的约束。我们特别关注消极性和施密特数。我们的方法仅使用其特征值的一个子集就可以量化任意维度上满阶状态的纠缠含量的分析和实用标准。此外，一些推导出的条件可以用于限制施密特数见证者的光谱。


### Abstract

Recent efforts have focused on characterizing the set of separable states that cannot be made entangled by any global unitary transformation. Here we characterize the set of states whose entanglement content cannot be increased under any unitary. By employing linear maps (and their inverses), we derive constraints on the achievable degree of entanglement from the spectrum of the density matrix. In particular, we focus on the negativity and the Schmidt number. Our approach yields analytical and practical criteria for quantifying the entanglement content of full-rank states in arbitrary dimensions using only a subset of their eigenvalues. Moreover, some of the derived conditions can be used to bound the spectra of Schmidt number witnesses.

## 局部纠缠净化

We introduce a new type of multipartite entanglement purification, Localized Entanglement Purification (LEP), a family of protocols that purify entanglement at the level of network regions rather than globally. By exploiting spatial noise asymmetries, LEP reduces resource consumption and enables scalable purification strategies for larger quantum systems.

## 绝对施密特数：特征化、检测和资源理论量化

**Original Title:** Absolute Schmidt number: characterization, detection and resource-theoretic quantification

**Authors:** Bivas Mallick, Saheli Mukherjee, Nirman Ganguly, A. S. Majumdar

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02439v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.02439v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-02


### 摘要

由施密特数量化的纠缠维度是广泛量子信息处理任务的宝贵资源。在这项工作中，我们引入了绝对施密特数的概念，指的是施密特数无法通过任何全球正变换增加的状态。我们提供了任意维状态集的描述，其施密特数在所有全球正性下都是不变的。我们的方法使我们能够开发基于时间和基于时刻的技术来检测非绝对施密特数状态，这可以通过全球正元的施密特数增强来提供显着的操作优势。接下来，我们分别基于施密特数见证和鲁棒性，制定了非绝对施密特数状态的两个资源理论测量，并展示了后者在通道区分任务中的操作实用性。最后，我们通过引入一类具有绝对施密特数属性的新通道，将我们的分析扩展到量子通道。我们推导出识别通道何时具有绝对施密特数属性的充要条件，将我们的分析限制在协变通道类。


### Abstract

The dimensionality of entanglement, quantified by the Schmidt number, is a valuable resource for a wide range of quantum information processing tasks. In this work, we introduce the notion of the absolute Schmidt number, referring to states whose Schmidt number cannot be increased by any global unitary transformation. We provide a characterization of the set of arbitrary-dimensional states whose Schmidt number is invariant under all global unitaries. Our approach enables us to develop both witness-based and moment-based techniques to detect nonabsolute Schmidt number states which could provide significant operational advantages through Schmidt number enhancement by global unitaries. We next formulate two resource-theoretic measures of nonabsolute Schmidt number states, based respectively on Schmidt number witness and robustness, and demonstrate an operational utility of the latter in a channel discrimination task. Finally, we extend our analysis to quantum channels by introducing a new class of channels that possess the absolute Schmidt number property. We derive a necessary and sufficient condition for identifying when a channel has the absolute Schmidt number property, confining our analysis to the class of covariant channels.

量子和经典系统可以通过非正时间不可逆机制一致地耦合。本文描述了哪种相应的动力学在稳态区域收敛到热混合状态，即在典型系综的约束下使混合排列熵最大化的密度矩阵。引入详细的平衡条件，发现混合Lindblad方程的特定子集满足所需的要求。主要理论结果通过一组具体例子进行了示例，这些例子还减轻了每个隔离子系统的热状态如何受到相互耦合的影响。特别是，当增加经典子系统与量子二阶子系统的相互作用强度时，高斯热态可能会变成双峰分布。

## AQ-Stacker：一种通过并行Hadamard堆叠进行缩放的自适应量子矩阵相乘算法

**Original Title:** AQ-Stacker: An Adaptive Quantum Matrix Multiplication Algorithm with Scaling via Parallel Hadamard Stacking

**Authors:** Wladimir Silva

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02530v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.02530v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-02


### 摘要

矩阵相乘（MatMul）是现代机器学习的计算支柱，但其经典复杂性仍然是大规模数据处理的瓶颈。我们提出了一种基于Hadamard测试的自适应配置的矩阵相乘的混合量子经典算法。通过利用量子随机访问存储器（QRAM）进行状态准备，我们证明计算两个载体的内能积的复杂性可以降低到$O（\log N）$。我们引入了一个“自适应堆叠”框架，允许算法根据可用的量子位资源动态地重新配置其执行模式，从顺序水平堆叠到大规模垂直并行。这种灵活性实现了可调的时间复杂度范围，理论上在耐故障系统上达到$O（N#2）$，同时保持与近期硬件的兼容性。我们通过量子机器学习（QML）模拟验证了我们方法的数字稳定性，在MNIST手写数字数据集上实现了96%的准确率。我们的结果表明，自适应量子MatMul提供了一条在多维线性代数运算中实现超经典效率的可行途径。


### Abstract

Matrix multiplication (MatMul) is the computational backbone of modern machine learning, yet its classical complexity remains a bottleneck for large-scale data processing. We propose a hybrid quantum-classical algorithm for matrix multiplication based on an adaptive configuration of Hadamard tests. By leveraging Quantum Random Access Memory (QRAM) for state preparation, we demonstrate that the complexity of computing the inner product of two vectors can be reduced to $O(\log N)$. We introduce an "Adaptive Stacking" framework that allows the algorithm to dynamically reconfigure its execution pattern from sequential horizontal stacking to massive vertical parallelism based on available qubit resources. This flexibility enables a tunable time-complexity range, theoretically reaching $O(N^2)$ on fault-tolerant systems while maintaining compatibility with near-term hardware. We validate the numerical stability of our approach through a Quantum Machine Learning (QML) simulation, achieving 96% accuracy on the MNIST handwritten digit dataset. Our results suggest that adaptive quantum MatMul provides a viable path toward super-classical efficiency in high-dimensional linear algebra operations.

## 通过吉文斯变换设计完美的状态转移图

### 摘要

Finally, an error analysis of charge-parity detection is performed, and it is found that qubit readout is currently the largest source of error. We believe this work lays the foundation for future exploration of ultra-low energy particles.

### Abstract

In this extended setting, complementary CRF/TDS perspectives become unitarily related while preserving global past and future. We discuss how this frame-perspectival approach informs the broader question of empirical realizability of abstract process matrices.

将纠缠分发给网络中的远程方是量子信息处理和量子网络的核心任务。纠缠状态对损失的敏感性使得需要使用纠缠路由策略。最近，有人提出了一种使用Greenberger-Horne-Zeilinger（GZZ）测量而不是Bell状态测量（BSM）的路由策略。我们通过明确考虑GZZ测量的不同测量成功概率来进一步推进这一研究方向。此外，我们将分析从方形网格网络扩展到复杂的网络模型，例如Waxman网络和无标度网络，以及SURFnet（荷兰的一种现实世界的网络布局）。考虑到不同的成功概率，GZZ路由的天真应用获得的速率远低于传统BSM路由。相反，我们提出了一种混合GHZ-BSM路由策略。混合GHZ-BSM路由策略在方形网格网络中优于BSM路由。然而，在其他网络中，需要使用全球信息进行更复杂的调整。


### Abstract

Distributing entanglement to distant parties in a network is a central task in quantum information processing and quantum networking. The sensitivity of entangled states to loss necessitates the use of entanglement routing strategies. Recently, a routing strategy using Greenberger-Horne-Zeilinger (GHZ) measurements instead of Bell state measurements (BSM) has been proposed. We further this direction of research by explicitly considering the varying measurement success probabilities of GHZ measurements. Moreover, we extend the analysis beyond square grid networks to complex network models such as Waxman networks and scale-free networks, as well as SURFnet, a real-world network topology in the Netherlands. Taking into account the varying success probabilities, naive application of GHZ routing achieves rates much lower than the conventional BSM routing. Instead, we propose a hybrid GHZ-BSM routing strategy. The hybrid GHZ-BSM routing strategy outperforms BSM routing in square grid networks. In other networks, however, more sophisticated adaptations using global information are required.

## 拓扑格上的量子接触过程

**Original Title:** Quantum Contact Processes on a Topological Lattice

**Authors:** Julius Bohm, Richard Schmidt, Michael Fleischhauer, Daniel Brady

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.03184v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.03184v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-03


### 摘要

接触过程在经典非平衡动力学中发挥着重要作用，描述疾病的传播、地震和森林火灾的动态以及通过互联网的信息分布。在这里，我们表明，它们的量子对应物（通过相干耦合进行传播）显示出更丰富的动力学并提供了新的控制手段。一个非平凡的非平凡的格子上的量子接触过程可以被限制在一个受保护的子空间中，该子空间对应于一个单位点或一个完全激发的格子。此外，当使用拓扑泵时，可以控制激励扩展以量化步骤并按需发生。我们表明，激发域的多体动力学可以映射到有效的单粒子模型，该模型也决定了拓扑性质。在整个工作中，我们考虑了一种特定类型的接触过程，该过程对应于一维网格中被俘获原子的镊子阵列中的连贯里德伯促进。


### Abstract

Contact processes play an important role in classical non-equilibrium dynamics, describing the spreading of diseases, the dynamics of earthquakes and forest fires, and the distribution of information through the internet. Here we show that their quantum counterpart, where the spreading occurs through coherent couplings, displays even richer dynamics and offers new means of control. A quantum contact process on a topologically non-trivial lattice can be confined to a protected subspace corresponding to either a single site or a fully excited lattice. Furthermore, excitation spreading can be controlled to occur in quantized steps and on demand when employing topological pumps. We show that the many-body dynamics of excited domains can be mapped to an effective single-particle model, which also determines the topological properties. Throughout this work, we consider a specific type of contact process corresponding to coherent Rydberg facilitation in a tweezer array of trapped atoms in a one-dimensional lattice.

## 尼尔森的随机力学：测量、非局部性和经典极限

**Original Title:** Nelson's Stochastic Mechanics: Measurement, Nonlocality, and the Classical Limit

**Authors:** Partha Ghose

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.03214v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.03214v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-03


### 摘要

一旦扩散规模由$\hbar $固定并且允许状态受到波函数上通常的单值条件的限制，尼尔森的随机力学就可以被理解为非相对论量子力学的随机基础或重建。在这篇文章中，我简要说明了这条路线的实现以及为什么它在概念上仍然具有吸引力。强调了三个优势。首先，它提供了基本过程的清晰配置空间随机图片。第二，它提供了一个明显不同的视角来看待测量和非定域性：特别是，坍缩不需要被视为一个额外的公理，与纠缠态相关的非定域性相对于确定性的Bohmian指导图像被软化了。第三，通过将量子性与扩散尺度联系起来，它自然地暗示了从严格经典到严格量子力学范围内的物理描述的连续性。最后，我提出了一个自然的距离尺度随机力学和研究其影响测试可能的限制贝尔相关性。


### Abstract

Nelson's stochastic mechanics may be understood as a stochastic underpinning, or reconstruction, of nonrelativistic quantum mechanics, once the diffusion scale is fixed by $\hbar$ and the admissible states are restricted by the usual single-valuedness condition on the wavefunction. In this note I briefly indicate what this route achieves and why it remains conceptually attractive. Three advantages are emphasized. First, it supplies a clear configuration-space stochastic picture of the underlying processes. Second, it offers a markedly different perspective on measurement and nonlocality: in particular, collapse need not be treated as an extra axiom, and the nonlocality associated with entangled states is softened relative to the deterministic Bohmian guidance picture. Third, by tying quantumness to a diffusion scale, it naturally suggests a continuum of physical descriptions ranging from the strictly classical to the strictly quantum-mechanical regime. I conclude by proposing a natural distance scale in stochastic mechanics and examining its implications for testing possible limits of Bell correlations.

## 多体量子系统的信念传播和张量网络扩展：严格结果和基本极限

**Original Title:** Belief Propagation and Tensor Network Expansions for Many-Body Quantum Systems: Rigorous Results and Fundamental Limits

**Authors:** Siddhant Midha, Grace M. Sommers, Joseph Tindall, Dmitry A. Abanin

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.03228v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.03228v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-03


### 摘要

信念传播（BP）为循环图上的张量网络收缩提供了一种可扩展的启发式方法，但它在量子多体环境中的成功在很大程度上依赖于经验证据。在最近引入的张量网络簇扩展框架的基础上，我们严格研究了BP对多体量子系统的适用性。对于表示为满足“循环衰变”条件的PEPS的状态，我们证明了由集群修正补充的BP以指数级小的相对误差逼近局部可观测量，并给出了将局部期望值表示为BP预测的显式公式，该预测由与可观测区域相交的连接集群修饰。这种表示在集群修正和物理相关函数之间建立了直接联系。结果，我们表明，“循环衰变”\{必然意味着}相关相关性的指数衰变，从而为BP何时可以和何时不能成功给出尖锐、严格的标准，并排除其在临界点的有效性。零温度和有限温度下二维和三维横场伊辛模型的数值模拟证实了我们的分析预测，证明了在间隙相深处的定量准确性和接近临界状态的系统故障。


### Abstract

Belief propagation (BP) provides a scalable heuristic for contracting tensor networks on loopy graphs, but its success in quantum many-body settings has largely rested on empirical evidence. Developing upon a recently introduced cluster-expansion framework for tensor networks, we rigorously study the applicability of BP to many-body quantum systems. For a state represented as a PEPS satisfying a ``loop-decay" condition, we prove that BP supplemented by cluster corrections approximates local observables with exponentially small relative error, and we give explicit formulas expressing local expectation values as BP predictions dressed by connected clusters intersecting the observable region. This representation establishes a direct link between cluster corrections and physical correlation functions. As a result, we show that ``loop-decay" \emph{necessarily implies} exponential decay of connected correlations, yielding sharp, rigorous criteria for when BP can and cannot succeed, and ruling out its validity at critical points. Numerical simulations of the two- and three-dimensional transverse field Ising model at zero and finite temperature confirm our analytical predictions, demonstrating quantitative accuracy deep in gapped phases and systematic failure near criticality.
