## 来自黑匣子的真正可认证随机性

**Original Title:** Genuine certifiable randomness from a black-box

**Authors:** Liam P. McGuinness

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.00401v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.00401v2

**Submitted:** 2026-02-28


### 摘要

随机性是量子力学固有的;量子状态测量的结果是随机变量。该功能已应用于随机性认证，其中一方必须决定他们接收的数据是否真正随机。然而，现有的演示不是黑匣子，为了避免错误地认证确定性数据，必须对数据的生成方式做出假设。在这里，我们在黑匣子环境中展示了真正的随机性认证--在这种环境中，即使拥有无限的计算能力，也没有确定性对手能够成功地获得数据认证。我们使用它仅使用对单粒子状态的测量而无需随机种子即可证明生成随机数。


### Abstract

Randomness is intrinsic to quantum mechanics; the outcome of a measurement on a quantum state is a random variable. This feature has been applied to randomness certification, where one party must decide whether the data they receive is truly random. However, existing demonstrations are not black-box, to avoid falsely certifying deterministic data, assumptions must be made on how the data was generated. Here we demonstrate genuine randomness certification in the black-box setting -- one in which no deterministic adversary, even with unlimited computational power, will succeed in getting their data certified. We use it to provably generate random numbers using only measurements on single particle states and without a random seed.

## 典型玻色子抽样中干扰项和封闭1模边缘的解析抵消

**Authors:** Jiang Liu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02272v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.02272v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-01


### 摘要

尽管已知典型玻色子采样（CBS）的$k$模式边缘分布可以在多项时间内计算，但驱动这种计算效率的物理机制在数学上仍然不透明。在这项工作中，我们提供了CBS中精确的1模边际分布的直接、自下而上的物理推导，可在$\mathCal{O}（R#2）$时间内计算，其中$R$是量子总数。我们明确地将这种物理推导与1级矩阵永久性的数学理论联系起来，证明多量子干涉自然地简化为由因乘玻色子聚束因子缩放的对称性多项。至关重要的是，我们证明了我们的循环组合公式规避了特征函数方法的算法负担，完全绕过了对多元插值或傅里叶变换的需要。最后，我们应用这个公式来识别聚束的宏观特征，提供了一个严格的、高度可扩展的指标，用于使用标准阈值检测器将真正的量子干涉与经典的可混合粒子模型区分开来。


### Abstract

We explicitly bridge this physical derivation with the mathematical theory of rank-1 matrix permanents, proving that multiphoton interference natively reduces to a symmetric polynomial scaled by a factorial bosonic bunching factor. Crucially, we demonstrate that our recursive combinatorial formulation circumvents the algorithmic overhead of characteristic function methods, entirely bypassing the need for polynomial interpolation or Fourier transforms. Finally, we apply this formula to identify macroscopic signatures of bunching, providing a rigorous, highly scalable metric for distinguishing genuine quantum interference from classical distinguishable-particle models using standard threshold detectors.

## Unruh-DeWitt组合中的量子回归定理

**Original Title:** Quantum regression theorem in the Unruh-DeWitt battery

**Authors:** Manjari Dutta, Arnab Mukherjee, Sunandan Gangopadhyay

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.02287v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.02287v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-02


### 摘要

本文利用开放量子系统研究中的有力工具量子回归定理来分析研究Unruh-DeWitt探测器的相关函数，这是一个均匀加速的两级量子系统，吸收外部经典相干脉冲的电荷。因此，该系统可以被视为一个相对论量子电池，它与其感知粒子的环境（即无质量纯量场的量子）相互作用。通过考虑林德勒时空中的相对论电池运动，在博恩-马尔科夫逼近下，我们推导出了控制系统约化密度矩阵演变的Gorini-Kossakowski-Sudarshan-Lindblad主方程。此外，我们对Wightman函数进行傅里叶变换，并使用指数正规化来计算主方程中出现的函数形式。接下来，我们推导出系统的运营商的单次期望值的发展方程。我们不仅解出了这些方程的单次时间平均值，而且利用量子回归定理确定了一阶和二阶的两次相关函数。我们详细地分析了它们，以解释自发辐射现象，并分析性地展示了加速如何增强相关的耗散。此外，我们解决了一个特殊形式的二阶相关函数相关的背景下，光子聚束所产生的玻色-爱因斯坦统计。最后，我们通过分析推导出电池探测器的自发发射光谱，该光谱在长时间限制下在高频区域显示出明确的洛伦兹线性形状。


### Abstract

In this paper, we employ the quantum regression theorem, a powerful tool in the study of open quantum systems, to analytically study the correlation functions of an Unruh-DeWitt detector, which is an uniformly accelerated two-level quantum system, absorbing charges from an external classical coherent pulse. The system can thus be viewed as a relativistic quantum battery that interacts with the environment of its perceived particles, namely, the quanta of a massless scalar field. By considering the relativistic battery moving in Rindler spacetime, under Born-Markov approximation, we derive the Gorini-Kossakowski-Sudarshan-Lindblad master equation governing the evolution of the system's reduced density matrix. Moreover, we perform the Fourier transformation of the Wightman functions and use exponential regularisation to compute the functional forms appearing in the master equation. Next, we derive the evolution equations for the the single-time expectation values of the system's operators. We not only solve these equations to find out the single time averages, but also employ the quantum regression theorem to determine the two-time correlation functions of first and second order. We analyse them in details to explain the phenomenon of spontaneous emission and show analytically how the acceleration can enhance the associated dissipation. Furthermore, we address a special form of second order correlation function relevant to the context of photon bunching arising in Bose-Einstein statistics. Finally, we derive the spontaneous emission spectrum of the battery detector analytically, which in the long-time limit displays a well-defined Lorentzian line shape in the high frequency regime.
