## 消散里德伯格格中的量子捕食者-食物权循环

**Original Title:** Quantum predator-prey cycles in dissipative Rydberg lattices

**Authors:** Ya-Xin Xiang, Zhengyang Bai, Yu-Qiang Ma

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26295v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.26295v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-30


### 摘要

Lotka-Volterra模型是远离平衡的系统中自组织捕食者-猎物振荡的范式，但在现实世界生态系统中测试它受到不可控制的微观参数的阻碍。在这里，我们使用可调二维里德伯原子阵列提出了捕食者-猎物动力学的量子模拟。通过基于开放系统离散截断维格纳逼近的平均场分析和数值模拟，我们证明了Rydberg激励在微秒级时间尺度上表现出捕食者-猎物循环。我们表明，量子一致性驱动自发对称性破缺，而远程相互作用则稳定全球振荡，以对抗量子噪音引起的去对称化。我们进一步揭示了量子跳跃会引发准循环，其幅度与系统大小的平方根成正比。我们的工作将捕食者-猎物模型的研究扩展到量子领域，并推进了利用工程多体非平衡效应的量子模拟策略。


### Abstract

The Lotka-Volterra model is a paradigm for self-organized predator-prey
oscillations in far-from-equilibrium systems, yet testing it in real-world
ecosystems is hindered by uncontrollable microscopic parameters. Here, we
propose a quantum analogue of predator-prey dynamics using a tunable
two-dimensional Rydberg atom array. Through mean-field analysis and numerical
simulations based on the open-system discrete truncated Wigner approximation,
we demonstrate that Rydberg excitations exhibit predator-prey cycles on
microsecond timescales. We show that quantum coherence drives spontaneous
symmetry breaking, while long-range interactions stabilize global oscillations
against quantum-noise-induced desynchronization. We further reveal that quantum
jump induce quasicycles whose amplitude scales inversely with the square root
of the system size. Our work extends the study of predator-prey models to the
quantum realm and advances quantum simulation stratagies that leverage
engineered many-body nonequilibrium effects.

## 超越精确经典模拟的被俘获离子量子计算机上的费米子动力学

**Original Title:** Fermionic dynamics on a trapped-ion quantum computer beyond exact classical simulation

**Authors:** Faisal Alam, Jan Lukas Bosse, Ieva Čepaitė, Adrian Chapman, Laura Clinton, Marcos Crichigno, Elizabeth Crosson, Toby Cubitt, Charles Derby, Oliver Dowinton, Paul K. Faehrmann, Steve Flammia, Brian Flynn, Filippo Maria Gambetta, Raúl García-Patrón, Max Hunter-Gordon, Glenn Jones, Abhishek Khedkar, Joel Klassen, Michael Kreshchuk, Edward Harry McMullan, Lana Mineh, Ashley Montanaro, Caterina Mora, John J. L. Morton, Dhrumil Patel, Pete Rolph, Raul A. Santos, James R. Seddon, Evan Sheridan, Wilfrid Somogyi, Marika Svensson, Niam Vaishnav, Sabrina Yue Wang, Gethin Wright

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26300v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.26300v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-30


### 摘要

费米子多体系统时间动力学的模拟长期以来一直被预测是量子计算机的关键应用之一。此类模拟--经典方法往往不准确--对于提高我们对量子化学和材料的知识和理解至关重要，奠定了从生物化学到清洁能源技术和化学合成等广泛领域的基础。然而，所有之前数字量子模拟的性能都与经典方法相匹配，迄今为止尚不清楚近期、中规模量子硬件是否可以在这一领域提供任何计算优势。在这里，我们在Quantinuum的系统模型H2捕获离子量子计算机上实现了一种高效的量子模拟算法，用于56量子位系统的时间动态，该系统过于复杂，无法进行精确的经典模拟。我们重点关注周期性的有旋2D费米-哈伯德模型，并提供了旋转-电荷分离的证据，即基本电子的电荷和旋转脱钩。在通过精确的经典模拟获得地面真相的有限情况下，我们发现它与我们从量子设备获得的结果一致。采用远程威尔逊操作来研究旋子之间的有效规范场和电荷子之间的有效势的去封闭，我们发现了与经典张量网络方法所做的预测不同的行为。我们的结果预示着使用量子计算来模拟超出经典计算能力的强相关电子系统。


### Abstract

Simulation of the time-dynamics of fermionic many-body systems has long been
predicted to be one of the key applications of quantum computers. Such
simulations -- for which classical methods are often inaccurate -- are critical
to advancing our knowledge and understanding of quantum chemistry and
materials, underpinning a wide range of fields, from biochemistry to
clean-energy technologies and chemical synthesis. However, the performance of
all previous digital quantum simulations has been matched by classical methods,
and it has thus far remained unclear whether near-term, intermediate-scale
quantum hardware could offer any computational advantage in this area. Here, we
implement an efficient quantum simulation algorithm on Quantinuum's System
Model H2 trapped-ion quantum computer for the time dynamics of a 56-qubit
system that is too complex for exact classical simulation. We focus on the
periodic spinful 2D Fermi-Hubbard model and present evidence of spin-charge
separation, where the elementary electron's charge and spin decouple. In the
limited cases where ground truth is available through exact classical
simulation, we find that it agrees with the results we obtain from the quantum
device. Employing long-range Wilson operators to study deconfinement of the
effective gauge field between spinons and the effective potential between
charge carriers, we find behaviour that differs from predictions made by
classical tensor network methods. Our results herald the use of quantum
computing for simulating strongly correlated electronic systems beyond the
capacity of classical computing.

## 双场量子密钥分发：协议、安全性和开放问题

**Original Title:** Twin-Field Quantum Key Distribution: Protocols, Security, and Open Problems

**Authors:** Syed M. Arslan, Syed Shahmir, Noureldin Mohammad, Saif Al-Kuwari, Muataz Alhussein

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26320v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.26320v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-30


### 摘要

双场量子密钥分发（TF-QKD）已成为远距离安全通信的潜在协议，克服了传统量子密钥分发的速率距离限制，而不需要可信的转发器。通过让双方向不受信任的中心节点传输相编码弱相关脉冲（WCP），TF-QKD利用单量子干扰来实现秘密密钥速率按通道长度的平方根缩放，从而实现前所未有的距离上的量子安全通信。本调查提供了对TF-QKD的全面调查，涵盖原始协议、其基本原则和密钥速率推导。我们讨论了主要的TF-QKD变体，包括相匹配QKD和发送或不发送QKD，以及各种改进版本。我们比较了它们的性能、实现权衡、协议特定的漏洞和对策。该调查总结了安全证据，从渐进诱饵状态分析到有限密钥可组合框架、实验里程碑、技术使能因素和实际部署挑战。最后，我们概述了该领域的未决问题，并提出了将TF-QKD集成到可扩展量子网络中的路线图，强调了其在未来量子互联网中的核心作用。


### Abstract

Twin-Field Quantum Key Distribution (TF-QKD) has emerged as a potential
protocol for long distance secure communication, overcoming the rate-distance
limitations of conventional quantum key distribution without requiring trusted
repeaters. By having two parties transmit phase encoded weak coherent pulses
(WCP) to an untrusted central node, the TF-QKD exploits single-photon
interference to achieve secret key rates scaling as square-root of channel
length, enabling quantum-secured communication over unprecedented distances.
This survey provides a comprehensive survey of TF-QKD, covering the original
protocol, its fundamental principles, and key-rate derivation. We discuss major
TF-QKD variants, including Phase-Matching QKD and Sending-or-Not-Sending QKD,
with various improved versions. We compare their performance, implementation
trade-offs, protocol-specific vulnerabilities, and countermeasures. The survey
summarizes security proofs ranging from asymptotic decoy-state analyses to
finite-key composable frameworks, experimental milestones, technological
enablers, and practical deployment challenges. Finally, we outline open
problems in the field and present a roadmap for integrating TF-QKD into
scalable quantum networks, underscoring its central role in the future quantum
internet.

## 离散时间中的弱记忆动力学

**Original Title:** Weak-Memory Dynamics in Discrete Time

**Authors:** Hugues Meyer, Kay Brandner

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26325v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.26325v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-30


### 摘要

离散动力学在时间转换对称性被破坏的系统中自然产生，并且通常由代表经典或量子马尔科夫链的一阶回归关系来描述。然而，当隐藏自由度引起的记忆效应相关时，通常需要更高阶的离散进化方程。专注于线性动力学，我们确定了一个轮廓明确的弱记忆机制，其中此类方程可以在中间时间尺度上系统地简化为作用于同一状态空间的独特一阶对应方程。我们将我们的结果表达为数学定理，并给出两个例子，展示如何将它们应用于粗粒度和量子碰撞模型下的随机Floquet动力学。


### Abstract

Discrete dynamics arise naturally in systems with broken temporal translation
symmetry and are typically described by first-order recurrence relations
representing classical or quantum Markov chains. When memory effects induced by
hidden degrees of freedom are relevant, however, higher-order discrete
evolution equations are generally required. Focusing on linear dynamics, we
identify a well-delineated weak-memory regime where such equations can, on an
intermediate time scale, be systematically reduced to a unique first-order
counterpart acting on the same state space. We formulate our results as a
mathematical theorem and work out two examples showing how they can be applied
to stochastic Floquet dynamics under coarse-grained and quantum collisional
models.

## 具有一般成本和量子比特最优耦合的量子最优传输的强Kantorovich二元性

**Original Title:** Strong Kantorovich duality for quantum optimal transport with generic cost and optimal couplings on quantum bits

**Authors:** Gergely Bunth, József Pitrik, Tamás Titkos, Dániel Virosztek

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26326v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.26326v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-30


### 摘要

我们证明了最近提出的非二次量子最优输运问题的线性化版本的坎托罗维奇二元性，其中量子通道实现输运。作为一个应用，我们在量子比特和区分成本操作符的情况下使用这种二元性来确定原始问题和二元问题的最佳解决方案，并对所涉及的状态进行一定的限制。最后，我们使用最优解的信息来给出诱导量子沃瑟斯坦分歧的三角不等式的分析证明。


### Abstract

We prove Kantorovich duality for a linearized version of a recently proposed
non-quadratic quantum optimal transport problem, where quantum channels realize
the transport. As an application, we determine optimal solutions of both the
primal and the dual problem using this duality in the case of quantum bits and
distinguished cost operators, with certain restrictions on the states involved.
Finally, we use this information on optimal solutions to give an analytical
proof of the triangle inequality for the induced quantum Wasserstein
divergences.

## 通过混合单量子和激光来基准量子密钥分配

**Original Title:** Benchmarking quantum key distribution by mixing single photons and laser light

**Authors:** Yann Portella, Petr Steindl, Juan Rafael Álvarez, Tim Hebenstreit, Aristide Lemaître, Martina Morassi, Niccolo Somaschi, Loïc Lanco, Filip Rozpędek, Pascale Senellart, Dario A. Fioretto

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26337v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.26337v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-30


### 摘要

量子密钥分发是量子力学的一个关键应用，塑造了隐私和安全通信的未来。许多协议需要单量子，通常用强衰减的激光脉冲来逼近。在这里，我们利用嵌入微柱中的量子点的发射并探索一种混合方法，其中信息被编码在单量子和激光脉冲的混合物上。我们对两个光源不连贯地混合以执行BB 84协议的配置进行了现象学分析，显示理论和实验之间几乎完美匹配。这提供了一种灵活的技术来补偿单光子源的有限收集亮度，以及对单光子统计优于泊松分布统计的情况进行彻底调查。解释说，我们的模型突出了单光子相对于激光的无条件优势的效率阈值，以及对BB84性能中单光子纯度和收集亮度之间相互作用的见解。


### Abstract

Quantum key distribution is a key application of quantum mechanics, shaping
the future of privacy and secure communications. Many protocols require single
photons, often approximated by strongly attenuated laser pulses. Here, we
harness the emission of a quantum dot embedded in a micropillar and explore a
hybrid approach where the information is encoded on a mixture of single photons
and laser pulses. We derive a phenomenological analysis of the configuration
where both sources of light are mixed incoherently to perform the BB84
protocol, showing nearly perfect matching between theory and experiment. This
provides a flexible technology compensating limited collected brightnesses of
single-photon sources as well as a thorough investigation of single-photon
statistics advantage scenarios over Poisson-distributed statistics. Explicitly,
our model highlights an efficiency threshold for unconditional advantage of
single photons over laser along with insights on the interplay between
single-photon purity and collected brightness in the performances of BB84.

## 扩展一致状态

**Original Title:** Extended Coherent States

**Authors:** Z. M. McIntyre, A. Kasman, R. Milson

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26338v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.26338v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-30


### 摘要

利用玛雅图和阶梯运算符的形式主义，我们描述了谐振子有理扩张类的零化运算符的代数。这使得我们能够在巴鲁特和吉拉德洛意义上构建相应的连贯状态。由此产生的含时函数是含时薛定格方程的精确解和零化子代数的联合本征函数。使用基于舒尔函数的论点，我们还表明，新表现出的相干状态渐进地最小化了位置动量不确定性。


### Abstract

Using the formalism of Maya diagrams and ladder operators, we describe the
algebra of annihilating operators for the class of rational extensions of the
harmonic oscillator. This allows us to construct the corresponding coherent
state in the sense of Barut and Girardello. The resulting time-dependent
function is an exact solution of the time-dependent Schr\"odinger equation and
a joint eigenfunction of the algebra of annihilators. Using an argument based
on Schur functions, we also show that the newly exhibited coherent states
asymptotically minimize position-momentum uncertainty.

## 延迟约束下的量子非定域性

**Original Title:** Quantum Nonlocality under Latency Constraints

**Authors:** Dawei Ding, Zhengfeng Ji, Pierre Pocreau, Mingze Xu, Xinyu Xu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26349v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.26349v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-30


### 摘要

贝尔不等式违反是一种现象，即多个非通信方可以使用量子资源表现出相关性，而如果他们只能使用经典资源，这些关联性是不可能的。强制非通信的一种方法是应用延迟限制：各方在收到输入后必须在短于任何一对各方之间的光速延迟速度的时间窗口内产生输出。如果放松这种延迟限制，以便各方的子集可以进行通信，我们就可以获得一组新的相关性不等式，以非常自然的方式扩展贝尔不等式。此外，通过这种放宽的延迟限制，我们还可以在一个参与方子集之间进行量子通信，从而实现这些新不等式的可能量子违反。我们最终希望回答一个基本问题：“在不同的延迟限制下，多方之间物理上可实现的相关性是什么？“为了回答这个问题，我们引入了延迟约束游戏，这是一个数学框架，将非本地游戏扩展到一部分各方可以通信的环境。延迟约束游戏的概念可以在现实世界中应用，包括高频交易、分布式计算、计算机架构和分布式控制系统。


### Abstract

Bell inequality violation is the phenomenon where multiple non-communicating
parties can exhibit correlations using quantum resources that are impossible if
they can only use classical resources. One way to enforce non-communication is
to apply a latency constraint: the parties must all produce outputs after they
receive their inputs within a time window shorter than the speed of light delay
between any pair of parties. If this latency constraint is relaxed so that a
subset of the parties can communicate, we can obtain a new set of inequalities
on correlations that extends Bell inequalities in a very natural way. Moreover,
with this relaxed latency constraint, we can also have quantum communication
between a subset of parties and thereby achieve possible quantum violations of
these new inequalities. We ultimately wish to answer the fundamental question:
"What are the physically realizable correlations between multiple parties under
varying latency constraints?" To answer this question, we introduce
latency-constrained games, a mathematical framework that extends nonlocal games
to the setting where a subset of parties can communicate. The notion of
latency-constrained games can have real-world applications, including high
frequency trading, distributed computing, computer architecture, and
distributed control systems.

## 静态和旋转磁场中大旋转的量子动力学：纠缠共振和扭结

**Original Title:** Quantum dynamics of large spins in static and rotating magnetic fields: Entanglement resonances and kinks

**Authors:** Nargis Sultana, Siddharth Seetharaman, Rejish Nath

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26351v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.26351v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-30


### 摘要

我们研究了静态和旋转磁场存在下大旋转的量子动力学。通过将系统映射到非相互作用的1/2粒子的气体上，我们可以得到不同初始状态动力学的精确分析结果。无论旋转有多大，动力学在两个最大伸展状态之间都会表现出周期性振荡。此外，我们观察到磁量子数符号相反的子级之间的周期性转变。此外，动力学特征是从叠加状态开始，旋转周期性地转移到最大伸展状态。在每种情况下，我们还探讨了亚马子矩的演变，正如预期的那样，它正在围绕瞬时合成磁场进行旋进。此外，我们将我们的分析扩展到一对旋转，并考虑它们之间的偶极-偶极相互作用。我们分析了旋转之间的底薪纠缠如何依赖于外场。两个旋转的量子动力学揭示了纠缠共振和扭结，当考虑弱横向场强度时，可以从能谱中识别出这些纠缠共振和扭结。最后，我们讨论了偶极相互作用相对较弱的状态。


### Abstract

We examine the quantum dynamics of a large spin in the presence of static and
rotating magnetic fields. By mapping the system onto a gas of non-interacting
spin-1/2 particles, we derive exact analytical results for the dynamics with
different initial states. The dynamics exhibit periodic oscillations between
two maximally stretched states, irrespective of how large the spin is. Further,
we observe periodic transitions between sublevels with magnetic quantum numbers
of opposite signs. Additionally, the dynamics features the periodic transfer of
the spin to the maximally stretched state starting from a superposition state.
The evolution of the dipole moment is also explored in each case, and as
expected, it is precessing about the instantaneous, resultant magnetic field.
Furthermore, we extend our analysis to a pair of spins, taking into account the
dipole-dipole interactions between them. We analyze how the ground state
entanglement between the spins depends on the external fields. The quantum
dynamics of the two spins reveal entanglement resonances and kinks, which can
be identified from the energy spectrum when weak transverse field strengths are
considered. Finally, we discuss the regime in which the dipolar interactions
are relatively weak.

## 量子超吸收中的纠缠和热力学标度定律

**Original Title:** Entanglement and Thermodynamic Scaling Laws in Quantum Superabsorption

**Authors:** Juan David Álvarez-Cuartas, John H. Reina

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26373v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.26373v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-30


### 摘要
