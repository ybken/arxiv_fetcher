## 时空泡利过程：用于建模量子误差修正中相关噪音的量子梳

**Original Title:** Spatiotemporal Pauli processes: Quantum combs for modelling correlated noise in quantum error correction

**Authors:** John F Kam, Angus Southwell, Spiro Gicev, Muhammad Usman, Kavan Modi

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05474v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.05474v2

**Submitted:** 2026-03-05


### 摘要

相关噪音是量子错误纠正（QEC）中的一种关键故障模式，因为时间记忆和空间结构将错误集中为错误突发，从而破坏了标准阈值假设。然而，QEC中普遍存在的随机泡利模型与物理设备动态学的微观非马尔科夫描述之间仍然存在根本性差距。我们通过引入时空泡利过程（SPP）来缩小这一差距。通过将由保利框架随机化操作实现的多时间泡利旋转应用到一般过程张量，我们将任意多时间、非马尔科夫动态映射到多时间泡利过程。这个过程由过程可分离的梳来表示，或者等效地，时空中泡利轨迹上定义明确的联合概率分布。我们表明SPP继承了有效的张量网络表示，其键维受环境的刘维尔空间维限制。为了解释这些结构，我们开发了将光谱与相关性衰变联系起来的转移运算符诊断，以及适合类别的SPP的精确隐藏马尔科夫表示。我们通过表面代码存储器和稳定性模拟的框架高达19美元的距离（一）时间相关的“风暴”模型，在固定的边际误差率调谐相关长度，和（ii）一个真正的时空二维量子元胞自动机浴，映射到一个非线性概率元胞自动机下旋转。调整一致浴相互作用将系统推入伪临界状态，表现出临界减慢和宏观误差雪崩，导致表面代码距离缩放完全崩溃。总而言之，这些结果证明SPP是一个基于运营的、可扩展的工具包，用于对QEC中的相关噪音进行建模、诊断和基准测试。


### Abstract

Correlated noise is a critical failure mode in quantum error correction (QEC), as temporal memory and spatial structure concentrate faults into error bursts that undermine standard threshold assumptions. Yet, a fundamental gap persists between the stochastic Pauli models ubiquitous in QEC and the microscopic, non-Markovian descriptions of physical device dynamics. We close this gap by introducing Spatiotemporal Pauli Processes (SPPs). By applying a multi-time Pauli twirl, operationally realised by Pauli-frame randomisation, to a general process tensor, we map arbitrary multi-time, non-Markovian dynamics to a multi-time Pauli process. This process is represented by a process-separable comb, or equivalently, a well-defined joint probability distribution over Pauli trajectories in spacetime. We show that SPPs inherit efficient tensor network representations whose bond dimensions are bounded by the environment's Liouville-space dimension. To interpret these structures, we develop transfer operator diagnostics linking spectra to correlation decay, and exact hidden Markov representations for suitable classes of SPPs. We demonstrate the framework via surface code memory and stability simulations of up to distance $19$ for (i) a temporally correlated "storm'' model that tunes correlation length at fixed marginal error rates, and (ii) a genuinely spatiotemporal 2D quantum cellular automaton bath that maps exactly to a nonlinear probabilistic cellular automaton under twirling. Tuning coherent bath interactions drives the system into a pseudo-critical regime, exhibiting critical slowing down and macroscopic error avalanches that cause a complete breakdown of surface code distance scaling. Together, these results justify SPPs as an operationally grounded, scalable toolkit for modelling, diagnosing, and benchmarking correlated noise in QEC.

## 多模式电路-QED系统中光-物质耦合的现场特征

**Original Title:** In-situ Characterization of Light-Matter Coupling in Multimode Circuit-QED Systems

**Authors:** Kellen O'Brien, Won Chan Lee, Alexandra Behne, Ali Fahimniya, Yu-Xin Wang, Maya Amouzegar, Alexey V. Gorshkov, Alicia J. Kollár

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05583v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.05583v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-05


### 摘要

多模式腔QED系统可以用于探索广泛的物理现象;然而，复杂的多模式环境使得光物质相互作用的系统性描述具有挑战性。在这里，我们提出了一种通用的测量协议，适用于原子和合成腔QED系统，该协议能够确定与单个光模的耦合。该方法利用AC斯塔克效应和克尔效应的测量以及已知的失调依赖性，以消除对单量子分辨率、独立子数校准或插入损失校准的需要。我们使用耦合到一维微波共振器格子的超导转换子量子位来演示该方法。我们验证了在多个量子位失调下确定的提取的光-物质耦合$g$的一致性，并根据三个量子模式的自克尔和交叉克尔位移，这为三个模式中的每一个提供了$g$的单独测量。


### Abstract

Multimode cavity-QED systems can be leveraged to explore a wide range of physical phenomena; however, a complex multimode environment makes systematic characterization of light-matter interactions challenging. Here we present a general measurement protocol, applicable to both atomic and synthetic cavity-QED systems, that enables the determination of coupling to individual photonic modes. The method leverages measurements of the AC-Stark and Kerr effects, along with known detuning dependencies, to eliminate the need for single-photon resolution, independent photon-number calibration, or insertion-loss calibration. We demonstrate the method using a superconducting transmon qubit coupled to a one-dimensional microwave resonator lattice. We validate the consistency of the extracted light-matter couplings $g$ determined at multiple qubit detunings, and from the self-Kerr and cross-Kerr shifts for three photon modes, which provide separate measurements of $g$ for each of the three modes.

### Abstract

We investigate a two-dimensional matter-wave interferometer where both spatial and rotational dynamics of a nanoparticle are intertwined in closing the one-loop interferometer in the Stern-Gerlach type setup. We consider the spin-contrast of the nitrogen-vacancy (NV) centred nanodiamond in combination with a two-dimensional magnetic field setup to extend the one-dimensional Stern--Gerlach interferometry. We analyse the dynamical motion along with the rigid rotation under the influence of the external magnetic field. Regarding rotation, we incorporate Euler-angle dynamics to analyse the stability of rotational degrees of freedom and their influence on the spin contrast to address the Humpty-Dumpty problem. We show that by imparting external rotation provides the gyroscopic stability to the liberating mode of the NV-spin and hence helps to improve the contrast. ## 电可调量子点分子的超慢轨道和旋转动力学

**Original Title:** Ultra-slow orbital and spin dynamics in an electrically tunable quantum dot molecule

**Authors:** Christopher Thalacker, Michelle Lienhart, Markus Stöcker, Nadeem Akhlaq, Irina Ivanova, Nikolai Bart, Arne Ludwig, Johannes Schall, Stephan Reitzenstein, Dirk Reuter, Steffen Wilksen, Christopher Gies, Krzysztof Gawarecki, Paweł Machnikowski, Kai Müller, Jonathan Finley

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05610v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.05610v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-05


### 摘要

隧道耦合光学活性量子点分子（QDM）有潜力作为具有耦合旋转的旋转-量子界面运行，同时与两个不同的量子频率相互作用。先决条件是在QDM中确定性地准备两个（电子或黑洞）旋转，并能够电调谐轨道状态耦合。在这里，我们展示了具有两个电子旋转的单个QDM的顺序光充电，同时保持使用静电场广泛调节轨道耦合的能力，并光学驱动系统以产生量子光。我们光学地准备单和双旋转状态，通过光泵初始化，并探索单和双旋转状态的轨道和旋转松弛动力学作为轨道状态能量失调和杂交的函数。对于双旋转状态，观察到明显长的S-T驰豫时间，延伸到$\sim 100μs$以上，并且强烈依赖于基和激发双旋转状态的相对能量。通过$\mathBF{k \csot p}$计算观察到定性一致。我们的结果提供了对单旋转和双旋转状态动力学的新的定量理解，并证实了QDM通过利用零磁场下的可调旋转-旋转交换耦合结合光学驱动来创建多维量子团簇状态的适用性。


### Abstract

Tunnel-coupled optically active quantum dot molecules (QDMs), have the potential to operate as spin-photon-interfaces with coupled spins that interact with two different photon frequencies at the same time. A prerequisite is to deterministically prepare two (electron or hole) spins in the QDM and be able to electrically tune the orbital state couplings. Here, we demonstrate the sequential optical charging of a single QDM with two electron spins while simultaneously maintaining the ability to widely tune orbital couplings using static electric fields and optically drive the system for quantum light generation. We optically prepare one- and two-spin states, initialize via optical pumping and explore orbital and spin relaxation dynamics for one and two-spin states as a function of the energy detuning and hybridization of orbital states. For two-spin states, remarkably long S-T relaxation times are observed extending beyond $\sim 100μs$ with strong dependence on the relative energy of ground and excited two-spin states. Qualitative agreement is observed with $\mathbf{k \cdot p}$ calculations of phonon-mediated spin-relaxation. Our results provide new quantitative understanding of the dynamics of one and two-spin states and confirm their suitability of QDMs for creating multidimensional photonic cluster states by exploiting tunable spin-spin exchange couplings at zero magnetic fields combined with optical driving.

### Abstract

Donor impurities in wide direct band gap semiconductors provide a promising platform for spin--photon quantum technologies by combining a donor spin qubit with optically addressable transitions. In ZnO, the shallow donor with the largest reported binding energy has long been associated with the I$_{10}$ bound exciton line, but its microscopic origin has remained unresolved. Here we demonstrate the controlled formation and identification of this donor as a Sn--Li complex through a combination of ion implantation, annealing, optical spectroscopy, and first-principles calculations. Resonant two-laser coherent population trapping measurements reveal an electron--$^{119}$Sn hyperfine interaction of $392 \pm 15$\,MHz, establishing a coupled electron--spin--1/2, nuclear--spin--1/2 system with one of the largest hyperfine couplings reported for shallow donors in semiconductors. Density functional theory calculations show that a nearest-neighbor Sn$_{\mathrm{Zn}}$--Li$_{\mathrm{Zn}}$ complex has favorable formation energetics, donor character with the electron localized on Sn, and an extrapolated hyperfine interaction consistent with experiment. The large donor binding energy and excited-state structure indicate enhanced thermal robustness of the optical transition relative to conventional group--III donors, while the strong hyperfine interaction enables fast electron--nuclear spin control and prospects for direct nuclear--spin--photon interfaces. We further observe efficient optically induced nuclear spin polarization, highlighting a path toward nuclear spin initialization. More broadly, our results reveal how a donor--acceptor complex can access previously unexplored regimes of shallow donor physics, extending the design space of quantum defects beyond isolated substitutional dopants.

## 具有威尔逊费米子一种和两种风味的汉密尔顿格子QED$_3$：布局结构和响应

### 摘要

用威尔逊费米子对（2+1）D量子电动力学中的拓扑相进行量子模拟，为在近期格子实验中实现拓扑现象提供了一条有希望的途径。我们表明，汉密尔顿规范理论中常用的交错费米子离散化具有精确的时间逆转对称性，这禁止了非平凡的拓扑相的出现，并导致了现有文献中的混乱。在这项工作中，我们通过系统地分析与满足高斯定律的U（1）规范场耦合的费米子的（2+1）D格子Hamilton中的费米子离散化效应来解决这一障碍。我们表明，威尔逊费米子已经在最小一味理论中，自然地实现了具有非零陈数的拓扑机制，并且有限化学势下的两味扩展进一步丰富了可及的拓扑结构。我们开发了拓扑响应的规范不变诊断，包括多体陈数和电流脉动器，它们在弱耦合下仍然是稳健的探针。最后，通过在两种口味设置中进行广泛的精确对角化计算，我们描述了光谱、相关子和拓扑不变量，为格场理论中拓扑相的近期量子模拟提供了具体基础。联合提交文件[1]分析了这项工作对格场理论量子模拟的影响。


### Abstract

The quantum simulation of topological phases in (2+1)D quantum electrodynamics with Wilson fermions provides a promising route toward realizing topological phenomena in near-term lattice experiments. We show that the commonly used staggered-fermion discretizations in Hamiltonian gauge theories possesses an exact time-reversal symmetry, which forbids the emergence of nontrivial topological phases and has led to confusion in the existing literature. In this work, we resolve this obstacle by systematically analyzing fermion discretization effects in (2+1)D lattice Hamiltonians of fermions coupled to U(1) gauge fields that satisfy Gauss' law. We show that Wilson fermions, already in the minimal one-flavor theory, naturally enable topological regimes with nonzero Chern numbers, and that the two-flavor extension at finite chemical potential further enriches the accessible topological structure. We develop gauge-invariant diagnostics of topological response, including many-body Chern numbers and current correlators that remain robust probes at weak coupling. Finally, through extensive exact diagonalization calculations across both flavor settings, we characterize the spectrum, correlators, and topological invariants, providing a concrete foundation for near-term quantum simulations of topological phases in lattice field theories. The implications of this work for quantum simulations of lattice field theory are analyzed in a joint submission [1].

## 一般概率理论中的经典解释

**Original Title:** Classical Explanations in (and of) General Probabilistic Theories

**Authors:** John Harding, Alex Wilce

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05627v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.05627v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-05


### 摘要

我们引入了一个（广义）概率模型由另一个（广义）概率模型“解释”的概念，作为概率模型和态射类别$\Prob$中的特定类型的跨度。我们表明，解释是在标准回调结构下进行的（尽管$\Prob$不支持任意回调）。然后我们表明，每个局部有限概率模型都有一个典型的、尖锐的经典解释。该结构是函式的，因此每个局部有限概率理论都有一个规范的、尖锐的经典（当然，通常是非局部的）表示。


### Abstract

We introduce a notion of the ``explanation" of one (generalized) probabilistic model by another as particular kind of span in the category $\Prob$ of probabilistic models and morphisms. We show that explanations compose under a standard pullback construction (notwithstanding that $\Prob$ does not support arbitrary pullbacks). We then show that every locally-finite probabilistic model has a canonical, sharp classical explanation. The construction is functorial, so every locally-finite probabilistic theory has a canonical, sharp classical (though of course, usually non-local) representation.

提出了一种基于德布罗意相干波（CBW）的量子波调制方案，使用M耦合马赫曾德干涉仪（MZZ）架构来实现超分辨率传感和计量。尽管CBW没有达到海森堡极限，但它规避了基于N 00 N状态的量子传感的关键实际限制，包括有限的量子数N、降低的条纹可见性以及对量子损失的强烈敏感性。CBW方法能够以任意大的M实现损耗容忍操作，同时保持接近单位的条纹可见性。CBW方案与相干光学完全兼容，可以直接集成到传统的波阵系统中，提供超分辨率和增强的灵敏度。使用M=2的Sagnac集成往返MZZ结构实现了证明基于CBW的超分辨率的原理验证实验，验证了拟议量子波阵设计的可行性。

A proof of principle experiment demonstrating CBW based superresolution is implemented using a Sagnac integrated round trip MZI structure for M=2, validating the feasibility of the proposed quantum wavemetry design.

## 无无序的局部化：结构图上的量子行走

**Original Title:** Localization Without Disorder: Quantum Walks on Structured Graphs

**Authors:** Shyam Dhamapurkar, K. Venkata Subrahmanyam

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05643v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.05643v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-05


### 摘要

连续时间量子行走（CTQWs）表现出与经典量子行走有根本不同的局部化现象，但网络结构、光谱简并性和受限动力学之间的精确关系仍然不完全理解。在这项工作中，我们在两个高度对称的图族（哑铃图和派系星图）上对CTQW的局部化进行了完整的分析描述。这些网络将显着的谱简并性与模块化结构相结合，实现了本征状态和动态逆参与比（IPR）的精确对角化和显式计算。我们的分析表明，局部化是由退化子空间和不变子空间之间的相互作用决定的，退化子空间会产生限制模族，而不变子空间之间的杂交会重新分配谱权重。值得注意的是，动态IPR可以超出仅基于本征状态IPR的预期，这表明简并本征空间内的连贯叠加增强了限制。通过将IPR值与访问的有效顶点数联系起来，我们为预测模块化网络中的量子输运结果提供了结构诊断，从而建立了仅连接性就可以确定量子行走局部化的位置以及强度有多强。


### Abstract

Continuous-time quantum walks (CTQWs) exhibit localization phenomena that differ fundamentally from their classical counterparts, yet the precise relationship between network structure, spectral degeneracy, and confined dynamics remains incompletely understood. In this work, we present a complete analytical characterization of localization in CTQWs on two highly symmetric graph families: barbell graphs and star-of-cliques graphs. These networks combine pronounced spectral degeneracy with modular structure, enabling exact diagonalization and explicit computation of both eigenstate and dynamical inverse participation ratios (IPRs). Our analysis reveals that localization is governed by the interplay between degenerate subspaces, which generate families of confined modes, and hybridization between invariant subspaces, which redistributes spectral weight. Notably, the dynamical IPR can exceed expectations based solely on eigenstate IPRs, demonstrating that coherent superposition within degenerate eigenspaces enhances confinement. By connecting IPR values to the effective number of vertices visited, we provide a structural diagnostic for predicting quantum transport outcomes in modular networks, establishing that connectivity alone can determine where and how strongly a quantum walk localizes.

我们提出了一种利用里德伯格原子处理器和基于样本的量子对角化（SQD）计算费米-哈伯德模型的底态能量的方法。通过利用费米-哈伯德模型和海森堡模型之间的微扰关系，该程序从里德伯原子处理器上准备的海森堡模型中采样，并使用这些样本对角化大U的费米-哈伯德模型。我们包括各向异性和次近邻相互作用，并讨论了一维费米-哈伯德模型中准超导电性的相关状态。文中给出了Aquila量子处理器上的数值和实验结果，用于计算底价能量以及化学势。我们发现，在所研究的区域中，海森堡模型采样足以在最多56个量子位的情况下收敛到接近底价，并且我们看到了莱德堡原子采样与随机采样相比的明显优势，即使有10倍以上的样本用于对角化。我们还针对56量子位哈伯德模型在IBM Quantum硬件上提出了基于门的SQD算法的基于门的实现作为基准。最后，我们为使用这种方法研究涌现的超导电性提供了差距分析。


### Abstract

We present a method for calculating the ground state energy of the Fermi-Hubbard model leveraging Rydberg atom processors and sample-based quantum diagonalization (SQD). By exploiting the perturbative relationship between the Fermi-Hubbard and Heisenberg models, the procedure samples from the Heisenberg model as prepared on the Rydberg atom processor, and uses the samples to diagonalize the Fermi-Hubbard model for large U. We include anisotropy and next-nearest-neighbor interactions and discuss the relevant regime for quasi-superconductivity in the 1-dimensional Fermi- Hubbard model. Numerical and experimental results on the Aquila quantum processor are presented for ground state energy calculations as well as the chemical potential. We find that the Heisenberg model sampling in the studied regime is sufficient to converge near to the ground state for up to 56 qubits, and we see a clear advantage of Rydberg atom sampling as opposed to random sampling even with 10x more samples for diagonalization. We also present a gate-based implementation of the gate-based SQD algorithm on IBM Quantum hardware for 56-qubit Hubbard model as a benchmark. Finally, we provide a gap analysis for studying emergent superconductivity using this method.

## 多维时态量子网络的原子接口

**Original Title:** An Atomic Interface for High-Dimensional Temporal Mode Quantum Networks

**Authors:** Shicheng Zhang, Aonan Zhang, Ilse Maillette de Buy Wenniger, Paul M. Burdekin, Jerzy Szuniewicz, Steven Sagona-Stophel, Sarah E. Thomas, Ian A. Walmsley

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06558v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.06558v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-06


### 摘要

光子的时间模由于其高信道容量和光纤兼容性而成为高维量子网络的一种很有前途的编码方案。然而，实现它们的全部潜力需要能够在光子和原子带宽上同步，处理和连接这些模式的设备。在这项工作中，我们展示了一个可编程的高维时间模式处理器使用拉曼量子存储器在温暖的铯蒸气。我们利用单模性质的拉曼相互作用内核，动态成形的控制字段合成一个可调谐的相干滤波器，选择性地解决特定的时间波形。该机制支持按需存储、过滤和转换，在MHz和GHz带宽模式之间提供一致的接口。我们在30个垂直厄米-高斯模式的基础上验证了该平台的选择性，并通过5维过程断层扫描来验证高保真度量子操作。通过将确定性模式转换与双向带宽接口相结合，我们将拉曼存储器建立为可扩展量子信息处理的关键活动节点。


### Abstract

Temporal modes of photons are a promising encoding scheme for high-dimensional quantum networks due to their high channel capacity and fiber compatibility. However, realizing their full potential requires devices capable of synchronizing, processing and interfacing these modes across photonic and atomic bandwidths. In this work, we demonstrate a programmable high-dimensional temporal mode processor using a Raman quantum memory in warm cesium vapor. We exploit the single-mode nature of the Raman interaction kernel, dynamically shaping the control field to synthesize a tunable coherent filter that selectively addresses specific temporal waveforms. This mechanism enables on-demand storage, filtering, and conversion, providing a coherent interface between MHz- and GHz-bandwidth modes. We validate the platform's selectivity across a basis of 30 orthogonal Hermite-Gaussian modes and certify high-fidelity quantum operation via 5-dimensional process tomography. By combining deterministic mode conversion with bidirectional bandwidth interfacing, we establish the Raman memory as a critical active node for scalable quantum information processing.

## 俘获离子量子计算机的射频侧通道分析

**Original Title:** Radio-Frequency Side-Channel Analysis of a Trapped-Ion Quantum Computer

**Authors:** Giorgio Grigolo, Dorian Schiffer, Lukas Gerster, Martin Ringbauer, Paul Erker

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06562v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.06562v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-06


### 摘要

与经典计算机类似，量子处理器表现出侧通道，可能会让攻击者访问潜在的专有算法。我们识别并利用俘获离子量子处理器中以前未探索的侧通道，该侧通道源自用于调制激光器以进行离子冷却、门执行和读出的射频（RF）信号。在这些量子处理器中，声光调制器（AOM）将相和频率调制印记到与离子相互作用的激光场上，以实现个体和集体的单位。AOM由强RF信号驱动，其中一小部分会泄漏到设备之外。我们讨论了利用该侧通道的一般策略，并演示如何使用现成的组件检测最先进的基于量子处理器的RF泄漏。从这些数据中，我们提取单离子和纠缠门的脉冲特征，从而实现对新型攻击载体的原理验证利用。最后，我们概述了减轻通过所呈现的侧通道信息泄露的方法。


### Abstract

Analogously to classical computers, quantum processors exhibit side channels that may give attackers access to potentially proprietary algorithms. We identify and exploit a previously unexplored side channel in trapped-ion quantum processors that arises from the radio-frequency (RF) signals used to modulate lasers for ion cooling, gate execution, and readout. In these quantum processors, acousto-optical modulators (AOMs) imprint phase and frequency modulations onto laser fields interacting with the ions to implement individual and collective unitaries. The AOMs are driven by strong RF signals, a fraction of which leaks out of the device. We discuss general strategies to exploit this side channel and demonstrate how to detect RF leakage from a state-of-the-art qudit-based quantum processor using off-the-shelf components. From this data, we extract pulse characteristics of single-ion and entangling gates, thereby implementing a proof-of-principle exploitation of the novel attack vector. Finally, we outline ways to mitigate the information leakage through the presented side channel.

## 对瞬间的颂歌

**Original Title:** An ode to instantons

**Authors:** Oliver Janssen, Joel Karlsson, Flavio Riccardi, Mattia Varrone

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06575v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.06575v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-06


### 摘要

我们在一个世纪的工作基础上，提出了量子力学中半经典时间演化的形式主义。我们识别实时的复杂鞍点、复杂时间中的真实鞍点以及复杂时间中的复杂鞍点，以重现经典问题中的已知答案。对于亚稳态的衰变，我们找到了“弹跳”的有限时间和有限能量类似物，它们不具有严格的零模式或负模式。讨论了波函数的单圈相和后期弹跳解的多重性。这项工作的动机是学习如何在具有非平凡时间依赖性的情况下计算量子场论中的衰变率，首先谦虚地倒退到迷人的量子力学世界。


### Abstract

We present a formalism for semiclassical time evolution in quantum mechanics, building on a century of work. We identify complex saddle points in real time, real saddle points in complex time, and complex saddle points in complex time that reproduce the known answers in classic problems. For the decay of a metastable state, we find finite time and finite energy analogs of the "bounce" which do not have strict zero or negative modes. The one-loop phase of the wave function and the multiplicity of bounce solutions at late times are discussed. The motivation of this work is to learn how to compute decay rates in quantum field theory in situations with non-trivial time dependence, by first taking a humble step backwards to the fascinating world of quantum mechanics.
