## 实践中最佳量子线性求解器的常因子分析

**Original Title:** Constant Factor Analysis of Optimal Quantum Linear Solvers in Practice

**Authors:** Pedro C. S. Costa, Alexander M. Dalzell, Dong An, Dominic W. Berry

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22185v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.22185v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-24


### 摘要

最佳量子线性方程求解器提供复杂性$O（k\log（1/e））$，其中$k $是条件数，$e $是允许误差。使用离散隔热方法的最佳求解器[PRX Quantum 3，040303（2022）]对于复杂性上限，具有较大的分析证明的常数因子。后来在数值测试中发现，常数因子约小1，200倍[Quantum 9，1887（2025）]。这意味着它比使用[PRX Quantum 6，040373（2025）]的随机方法效率大约高出一个数量级，后者的分析证明的常数因子要小得多。最近，人们发现了一种“捷径”方法可以提供一个最优解算器，该解算器也具有小的已证明的常数因子。在本工作中，我们对两类随机线性系统进行了全面的数值分析，将该方法与隔热求解器进行了比较。我们发现，在解规范未知的情况下，隔热求解器提供的性能稍好一些。如果已知解规范，那么捷径方法为非埃尔米特矩阵提供了明显更好的性能。


### Abstract

Optimal quantum linear equation solvers provide complexity $O(κ\log(1/ε))$, where $κ$ is the condition number and $ε$ is the allowable error. The optimal solver using a discrete adiabatic approach [PRX Quantum 3, 040303 (2022)] has large analytically proven constant factors for the upper bound on the complexity. The constant factors were later found to be about 1,200 times smaller in numerical testing [Quantum 9, 1887 (2025)]. This meant it is about an order of magnitude more efficient than using a randomised approach from [PRX Quantum 6, 040373 (2025)], which has far smaller analytically proven constant factors. Recently, a ``Shortcut'' method has been found to provide an optimal solver which also has small proven constant factors. In the present work, we conduct a comprehensive numerical analysis comparing this method with the adiabatic solver for two families of random linear systems. We find that, in the case where the solution norm is unknown, the adiabatic solver provides slightly better performance. If the solution norm is known, then the shortcut method provides significantly better performance for non-Hermitian matrices.

## 模块格点安全性（第二部分）：通过最佳符号选择进行模块格点简化

**Original Title:** Module Lattice Security (Part II): Module Lattice Reduction via Optimal Sign Selection

**Authors:** Ming-Xing Luo

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22900v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.22900v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-24


### 摘要

我们将CDPR格约简算法从理想格扩展到模块格，利用功率基的轨迹垂直性将模块分解为rank-1子模块，并将CDPR独立应用于每个子模块。在MLWE分布式基自动满足的平衡假设下，此基本模块约简实现了与理想情况相匹配的Hermite因子$\BEP（\tilde{O}（\SQRT{n}））$，其中模块约简因子$O（1）$独立于排名。为了控制精度，我们在完全分裂Prime时引入了ECT缩放的舍入，减少了Gram-Schmidt舍入误差并产生有界精度实现。我们进一步将CDPR符号选择子问题重新表述为混合整线性规划，将最佳平衡差异确定为普适常数$δ #*\大约0.4407$。所有结果都建立在本系列第一部分中建立的一类第一条件$h_k^+=1$之上。


### Abstract

We extend the CDPR lattice reduction algorithm from ideal to module lattices, leveraging the trace orthogonality of the power basis to decompose the module into rank-1 submodules and applying CDPR independently to each. This base module reduction achieves a Hermite factor $\exp(\tilde{O}(\sqrt{n}))$ matching the ideal case, with a module reduction factor $O(1)$ independent of the rank, under a balance hypothesis automatically satisfied for MLWE-distributed bases. To control precision, we introduce CRT-scaled rounding at totally split primes, reducing the Gram-Schmidt rounding error and yielding a bounded-precision implementation. We further reformulate the CDPR sign-selection subproblem as a mixed-integer linear program, determining the optimal balanced discrepancy to be a universal constant $δ^*\approx 0.4407$. All results build on the class number one condition $h_k^+=1$ established in Part I of this series.

## 原子约瑟夫森结中的弗劳恩和费图案

**Original Title:** Fraunhofer Patterns in Atomic Josephson Junctions

**Authors:** Kevin T. Geier, Giampiero Marchegiani, Vijay Pal Singh, Juan Polo, Luigi Amico

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22923v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.22923v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-24


### 摘要

驱动原子约瑟夫森结使人们能够通过对系统物理条件的前所未有的控制和灵活性来监控相调动态。虽然约瑟夫森效应的冷原子表现在各种环境中得到了广泛的研究，但合成电磁场中的原子约瑟夫森结在很大程度上仍未被探索。在这里，我们表明合成磁场可以诱导原子约瑟夫森结中临界电流的类似夫劳恩霍夫的调制。尽管这种效应与在超流体中发现的夫琅和费图案类似，但由于超流体的中性性质，出现了独特的特征。我们研究了潜在的空间干涉机制，并基于数值模拟阐明了约瑟夫森涡旋在空间调制电流分布形成中的作用。我们的结果为物质波电路开辟了新的途径，以加深我们对约瑟夫森结空间相关性的理解，这是新型量子技术发展的基础。


### Abstract

Driven atomic Josephson junctions allow one to monitor phase-coherent dynamics with unprecedented control and flexibility of the system's physical conditions. While cold-atom manifestations of the Josephson effect have been extensively studied in a wide variety of settings, atomic Josephson junctions in synthetic electromagnetic fields remain largely unexplored. Here, we show that synthetic magnetic fields can induce Fraunhofer-like modulations of the critical current in atomic Josephson junctions. Although this effect presents analogies to the Fraunhofer patterns found in superconducting devices, distinctive features emerge due to the neutral nature of the superfluid. We investigate the underlying spatial interference mechanisms and elucidate the role of Josephson vortices in the formation of spatially modulated current distributions based on numerical simulations. Our results open up new avenues for matter-wave circuits to deepen our understanding of spatial coherence in Josephson junctions, which are fundamental to the development of novel quantum technologies.

## 邓克尔非简振子中的量子动力学和崩溃复兴现象

**Original Title:** Quantum Dynamics and Collapse-and-Revival Phenomena in the Dunkl Anharmonic Oscillator

**Authors:** D. Ojeda-Guillén, R. D. Mota, M. Salazar-Ramírez

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22945v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.22945v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-24


### 摘要

我们从$SU（1，1）$群的代数方法研究Dunkl非简谐振子（Kerr媒介）Hamilton量。为了获得这个问题的确切能谱，我们用Dunkl产生和湮没操作符来写出它的Hamilton，这闭合了$su（1，1）$ Lie代数。这使我们能够准确地求解这个Hamilton量并获得其与宇称相关的能谱。然后，我们通过使用偶数和奇数邓克尔相干态叠加给出的初始状态来研究系统的量子动力学，特别是坍缩和复兴现象。我们计算了场积分和生存概率，表明Dunkl参数$μ$调制了分数复兴，并在特定变形值的半周期产生完美的状态重建。我们分析了交方差，表明邓克尔变形在$t \approximate pi $周围产生干扰诱导的压缩状态。标准Kerr媒介动力学在限制$μ\rightarrow 0$内完全恢复。


### Abstract

We study the Dunkl anharmonic oscillator (Kerr medium) Hamiltonian from an algebraic approach of the $SU(1,1)$ group. In order to obtain the exact energy spectrum of this problem, we write its Hamiltonian in terms of the Dunkl creation and annihilation operators, which close the $su(1,1)$ Lie algebra. This allows us to exactly solve this Hamiltonian and obtain its parity-dependent energy spectrum. Then, we investigate the quantum dynamics of the system, particularly the collapse and revival phenomena, by using an initial state given by a superposition of even and odd Dunkl coherent states. We compute the field quadrature and the survival probability, showing that the Dunkl parameter $μ$ modulates the fractional revivals and produces perfect state reconstructions at half-periods for specific deformation values. We analyze the quadrature variance to show that the Dunkl deformation generates interference-induced squeezed states around $t \approx π$. The standard Kerr medium dynamics are exactly recovered in the limit $μ\rightarrow 0$.

## 用超冷原子实现多轨道埃梅里模型

**Original Title:** Realizing multi-orbital Emery models with ultracold atoms

**Authors:** Conall McCabe, Jamie Boyd, Kaizhao Wang, Martin Lebrat, Cindy Regal, Adam Kaufman, Ana Maria Rey, Lukas Homeier

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22955v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.22955v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-24


### 摘要

过渡金属氧化物中强相关的电子会引发有趣的涌现现象，包括铜酸盐中的高温超导电性。虽然简化的单频段哈伯德模型捕捉了某些方面，但明确描述铜轨道和氧轨道的相互作用（如三频段埃梅里模型中的那样）对于捕捉铜酸盐的完整现象学至关重要。基于超冷原子的量子模拟器为在受控环境中研究此类系统提供了一条有希望的途径，但实现现实的多轨道哈伯德模型仍然具有挑战性。在这里，我们提出了一种光学超格子架构，用超冷费米子实现三带埃梅里模型。通过将格子束与可控干涉相结合，我们设计了轨道自由度，可以重现铜酸盐带结构的关键特征，同时能够独立控制轨道相关的相互作用和电荷转移能量。我们表明，单粒子量子行走可以对由此产生的紧束缚模型进行基准测试。使用决定性量子蒙特卡罗，我们进一步研究了未掺杂区的热力学性质，并发现有限温度金属-绝缘体交叉，伴随着当前实验中可获得的反铁磁相关性的开始。最后，我们应用汉密尔顿学习协议，能够从埃梅里模型的实验实现中推断出有效的单频段哈伯德模型。我们的结果为用量子气体显微镜模拟多轨道哈伯德物理学提供了一条实用途径。


### Abstract

Strongly-correlated electrons in transition-metal oxides give rise to intriguing emergent phenomena, including high-temperature superconductivity in cuprates. While simplified one-band Hubbard models capture some aspects, explicitly describing the interplay of copper and oxygen orbitals -- as in the three-band Emery model -- is essential to capture the full phenomenology of cuprates. Quantum simulators based on ultracold atoms offer a promising route to study such systems in a controlled setting, but realizing realistic multi-orbital Hubbard models remains challenging. Here we propose an optical superlattice architecture that implements the three-band Emery model with ultracold fermions. By combining lattice beams with controllable interference, we engineer orbital degrees of freedom that reproduce key features of the cuprate band structure, while enabling independent control of orbital-dependent interactions and charge-transfer energy. We show that single-particle quantum walks can benchmark the resulting tight-binding model. Using determinant quantum Monte Carlo, we further investigate thermodynamic properties in the undoped regime and find a finite-temperature metal-insulator crossover accompanied by the onset of antiferromagnetic correlations accessible in current experiments. Finally, we apply a Hamiltonian learning protocol enabling to infer effective single-band Hubbard models from experimental realizations of Emery models. Our results provide a practical pathway to simulate multi-orbital Hubbard physics with quantum gas microscopes.

## 没有单一扩展的克隆

**Original Title:** No cloning with unitary scaling

**Authors:** Dafa Li

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22960v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.22960v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-24


### 摘要

众所周知，比特串等经典信息是可以复制的。1982年，伍特斯和祖雷克提出了量子非克隆原则[1]。非克隆原则认为，不可能通过使用正进化来制造任意未知量子纯态的相同副本。在本文中，我们提出了一个一般的非克隆原则。我们打电话给你|si>，其中U是任何一元运算符，状态的U副本|磅/平方英寸>。一般的非克隆原则指出，不可能通过使用正进化来制造任意未知量子纯态的U复制。


### Abstract

It is known that the classical information like strings of bits can be copied. In 1982, Wootters and Zurek proposed the quantum no-cloning principle [1]. No-cloning principle says that it is impossible to make an identical copy of an arbitrary unknown quantum pure state by using unitary evolution. In this paper, we propose a general no-cloning principle. We call U|psi>, where U is any unitary operator, a U-copy of the state |psi>. The general no-cloning principle states that it is impossible to make a U-copy of an arbitrary unknown quantum pure state by using unitary evolution.

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.22974v1

## 加速量子吉布斯采样而无需量子行走

**Original Title:** Accelerating quantum Gibbs sampling without quantum walks

**Authors:** Jiaqi Leng, Jiaqing Jiang, Lin Lin

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22996v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.22996v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-24


### 摘要

塞格迪的量子行走为可逆经典马尔科夫链提供了通用的二次加速，但除了特殊情况之外，将这种机制扩展到量子吉布斯采样仍然具有挑战性。我们提出了一种无需行走的量子算法，用于制备纯化的吉布斯态，对于满足精确的库博-马丁-Schwinger详细平衡的一类量子吉布斯采样器来说，光谱间隙依赖性有二次改进。我们的主要结构结果是将相应的父Hamilton量显式分解为非交换的一阶运算符。这将纯化的吉布斯状态准备变成奇异值过滤问题，并在标准相关访问假设下实现具有二次改进间隙依赖性的量子奇异值变换算法。该框架适用于戴维斯设置之外的几个可高效实现的Gibbs采样器。我们还引入了一种基于相同因式分解的辅助消散动力学，它可用于在亚稳定区域中的双倍Hilbert空间中产生热启动。


### Abstract

Szegedy's quantum walk gives a generic quadratic speedup for reversible classical Markov chains, but extending this mechanism to quantum Gibbs sampling has remained challenging beyond special cases. We present a walk-free quantum algorithm for preparing purified Gibbs states with a quadratic improvement in spectral-gap dependence for a broad class of quantum Gibbs samplers that satisfy exact Kubo-Martin-Schwinger detailed balance. Our main structural result is an explicit factorization of the corresponding parent Hamiltonian into noncommutative first-order operators. This turns purified Gibbs-state preparation into a singular-value filtering problem and enables a quantum singular value transformation algorithm with quadratically improved gap dependence under standard coherent-access assumptions. The framework applies to several efficiently implementable Gibbs samplers beyond the Davies setting. We also introduce an auxiliary dissipative dynamics based on the same factorization, which can be used to generate warm starts in the doubled Hilbert space in metastable regimes.

## 具有位置相关噪音的增强量子输运的设计原则

**Original Title:** Design Principles for Enhanced Quantum Transport with Site-Dependent Noise

**Authors:** Maggie Lawrence, Elise Wang, Dvira Segal

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.23005v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.23005v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-24


### 摘要

环境噪音可以增强传输，这种效应被称为环境噪音辅助量子传输。大多数理论研究都集中在空间均匀系统与环境耦合下优化系统参数。相反，在这里，我们通过允许取决于地点的去相来优化环境噪音本身。我们研究了具有斜坡或无序能量景观的一维格子中的稳态输运，同时考虑了短程和远程相干隧道。在没有环境影响的情况下，在热力学极限下，这些系统可能会表现出局部化，从而抑制由相消干扰引起的传输。使用Lindblad主方程框架，我们实施优化的局部去相，以最大化稳态人口流量。我们发现，对于斜坡势，短程隧道有利于交替位点的选择性去相，而远程隧道受益于随着距注射位点的距离而增加的去相曲线。在能量无序的系统中，强烈失调的位点需要在短程隧道下增强局部去相以促进传输。在所有情况下，我们发现，网站优化的移相允许更高的传输效率比均匀的移相，它是伴随着增加的空间离域的稳定状态。我们的研究结果提供了微观洞察相干动力学和环境噪声之间的相互作用。失相扩大了局部能级，有助于克服失谐和相消干涉。更一般地说，我们建立了空间结构的环境噪声作为一种策略，用于控制开放系统中的量子传输和状态相干性。


### Abstract

Environmental noise can enhance transport, an effect known as environmental noise-assisted quantum transport. Most theoretical studies focus on optimizing system parameters under spatially uniform system-environment coupling. Here, instead, we optimize the environmental noise itself by allowing for site-dependent dephasing. We investigate steady-state transport in one-dimensional lattices with either ramped or disordered energy landscapes, considering both short- and long-range coherent tunneling. In the absence of environmental effects, in the thermodynamic limit these systems can exhibit localization, and thus suppressed transport, arising from destructive interference. Using a Lindblad master equation framework, we implement local dephasing optimized to maximize steady-state population flux. We find that for ramp potentials, short-range tunneling favors selective dephasing on alternating sites, whereas long-range tunneling benefits from a dephasing profile that increases with distance from the injection site. In energetically disordered systems, strongly detuned sites require enhanced local dephasing under short-range tunneling to facilitate transport. In all cases, we find that site-optimized dephasing allows higher transport efficiency than uniform dephasing, and it is accompanied by increased spatial delocalization of the steady state. Our results provide microscopic insight into the interplay between coherent dynamics and environmental noise. Dephasing broadens energy levels locally, helping to overcome detuning and destructive interference. More generally, we establish spatially-structured environmental noise as a strategy for controlling both quantum transport and state coherence in open systems.

## Qutrit Clifford+T门通过两体角动量耦合、旋转和单轴扭转

**Original Title:** Qutrit Clifford+T gates by two-body angular momentum couplings, rotations and one-axis-twistings

**Authors:** F. E. S. Steinhoff

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.23007v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.23007v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-24


### 摘要

我们开发了一个角动量表示和实施的克利福德+T集的酉qutrits。我们表明，从这个集合的本地门可以实现通过单独使用合适的旋转和单轴扭转操作，这是最多的角动量算子的二次，因此可以在许多量子系统中实验实现。控制旋转只需要线性角动量耦合，因此，完整的qutrit克利福德+T集示出仅表示在两体角动量耦合，旋转和单轴扭转操作。通过采用约旦-施温格映射，我们显示了一个类似的实施方式的玻色子模式，改进的模式的数量与以前的计划。此外，我们利用交叉克尔相互作用来获得玻色子模的任何quutrit Clifford+T门。我们用准备感兴趣的纠缠态的简单方案来说明我们的发现。


### Abstract

We develop an angular momentum representation and implementation of the Clifford+T set of unitaries for qutrits. We show that local gates from this set can be realized by the sole use of suitable rotations and one-axis-twisting operations, which are at most quadratic in the angular momentum operators and thus can be experimentally realized in many quantum systems. Controlled rotations are shown to only require linear angular momentum couplings and, as a consequence, the full qutrit Clifford+T set is shown to be expressed solely in terms of two-body angular momentum couplings, rotations and one-axis-twisting operations. By employing the Jordan-Schwinger map, we show an analogous implementation in terms of bosonic modes, improving on the number of modes with regard to a previous scheme. Moreover, we employ the cross-Kerr interaction in order to obtain any qutrit Clifford+T gate for bosonic modes. We illustrate our findings with simple schemes for preparing entangled states of interest.

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.23031v1

**Original Title:** Representability for Quantum Theory beyond Particle-Number Conservation

**Authors:** David A. Mazziotti

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.23869v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.23869v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-26


### 摘要

可代表性确定两粒子约化密度矩阵（2-RCM）何时对应于物理量子状态，从而能够使用2-RCM而不是波函数进行多粒子量子计算。在这封信中，我们提出了不含粒子数保守的量子系统的可代表性问题的解决方案。物理上允许的2-RCM集合可以从几何上“垂直”集合（极锥）来描述。我们推导出极锥（$p$-正锥与二体运算符空间的交线）中二体运算符的显式线性方程，以获得不依赖于更高的RCM或波函数的系统性可代表性条件分层结构。此外，通过用粒子数方差来增强这些条件，我们获得了处理粒子数保守系统和非保守系统的统一框架。我们用旋转系统和分子H$_4$来说明。


### Abstract

Representability determines when a two-particle reduced density matrix (2-RDM) corresponds to a physical quantum state, enabling many-particle quantum calculations with 2-RDMs rather than the wave function. In this Letter, we present a solution of the representability problem for quantum systems without particle-number conservation. The physically allowed set of 2-RDMs can be characterized from a geometrically `orthogonal' set, the polar cone. We derive explicit linear equations for the two-body operators in the polar cone -- the intersection of the $p$-positive cone with the two-body operator space -- to obtain a systematic hierarchy of representability conditions that do not depend on higher RDMs or the wave function. Moreover, by augmenting these conditions with the particle-number variance, we obtain a unified framework for treating both particle-number-conserving and nonconserving systems. We illustrate with a spin system and molecular H$_4$.

These results provide a unified description of matter-wave interferometric phases in time-dependent GW backgrounds and identify complementary dynamical, spin, and electromagnetic pathways through which spacetime curvature imprints itself on quantum interference.

## 利用广义信念传播契约张量网络

**Original Title:** Contracting Tensor Networks with Generalized Belief Propagation

**Authors:** Joseph Tindall, Grace M. Sommers, Hilbert Kappen

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.24760v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.24760v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-27


### 摘要

近年来，人们对使用信念传播（一种最初为对图形模型执行统计推断而引入的算法）进行近似但高效的张量网络收缩的兴趣越来越大。在这里，我们详细介绍了如何应用广义信念传播（GBP）（其中消息在张量网络的重叠区域的层次结构中传递）来大约收缩张量网络并获得准确的结果。原始的信念传播算法是这种方法的一个角落，对应于张量网络区域的特别简单的选择。我们在一系列二维和三维、无限和有限张量网络上针对许多不同的区域选择实施GBP算法，以数字方式求解相应的不稳定点方程，并在某些易于处理的情况下通过分析方式求解。我们的例子包括计算完全受挫的伊辛模型的配分函数、计算三维冰模型的底薪简并度、测量变形AKLT量子态的可观测值以及评估随机生成的张量网络状态的规范。


### Abstract

Recent years have seen a growing interest in the use of belief propagation - an algorithm originally introduced for performing statistical inference on graphical models - for approximate, but highly efficient, tensor network contraction. Here, we detail how to apply generalized belief propagation (GBP) - where messages are passed within a hierarchy of overlapping regions of the tensor network - to approximately contract tensor networks and obtain accurate results. The original belief propagation algorithm is a corner case of this approach, corresponding to a particularly simple choice of regions of the tensor network. We implement the GBP algorithm for a number of different region choices on a range of two- and three-dimensional, infinite and finite tensor networks, solving the corresponding fixed point equations both numerically and, in certain tractable cases, analytically. Our examples include calculating the partition function of the fully frustrated Ising model, computing the ground state degeneracy of three-dimensional ice models, measuring observables on the deformed AKLT quantum state and evaluating the norm of randomly generated tensor network states.
