## 表面代码实验的综合症数据的逻辑误差估计

**Original Title:** Logical error estimation from syndrome data of surface-code experiments

**Authors:** Evangelia Takou, Cesar Benito, Arian Vezvaee, Daniel A. Lidar, Kenneth R. Brown

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11496v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.11496v2

**Submitted:** 2026-06-09


### 摘要

量子错误纠正（QEC）实验的解码器依赖于检测器错误模型（DEC），该模型针对每个错误对其概率及其翻转的检测器和逻辑可观测量进行编码。在这里，我们表明，根据实验伴随式估计DEC事件概率是可行的，可以避免独立的设备基准测试，并产生有用的解码器先验以估计和减少解码的逻辑错误概率。我们使用在Google Willow芯片上执行的表面代码内存实验中的开源数据来评估我们的方法，并在IBM的\textttt {ibm\_miami}处理器上执行了类似的表面代码实验。尽管Google和IBM设备的物理错误规模不同，但在这两种情况下，我们估计的德明相对于基线设备通知的德明都提高了逻辑错误概率，通常在5美元-10美元的水平，并且在某些IBM情况下收益更大，无需额外的校准电路、解码器微调或对逻辑结果进行监督式匹配。


### Abstract

Decoders for quantum error correction (QEC) experiments rely on detector error models (DEMs), which encode, for each error, its probability and the detectors and logical observables it flips. Here we show that estimating DEM event probabilities from experimental syndromes is feasible, avoids independent device benchmarking, and produces useful decoder priors for estimating and reducing decoded logical error probabilities. We evaluate our methods using open-source data from surface-code memory experiments performed on Google's Willow chip, and we carry out analogous surface-code experiments on IBM's \texttt{ibm\_miami} processor. Despite the different physical error scales of the Google and IBM devices, in both cases our estimated DEMs improve logical error probabilities relative to baseline device-informed DEMs, typically at the $5\%-10\%$ level and with larger gains in some IBM cases, without additional calibration circuits, decoder fine-tuning, or supervised fitting to logical outcomes.

## 弱驱动二水平媒体中储层控制的电磁感应栅

**Original Title:** Reservoir-controlled electromagnetically induced gratings in a weakly driven two-level medium

**Authors:** Amjad Hussain, Hamid Arian Zad, Amjad Sohail, Hazrat Ali, Michal Jaščur, Saeed Haddadi

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.13085v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.13085v2

**Submitted:** 2026-06-11


### 摘要

我们从理论上研究了弱探测场在耦合到工程量子库的弱驱动二能级介质中形成的电磁感应光栅的透射和衍射。使用微扰解决方案的光学布洛赫方程在弱驱动制度，我们分析如何正常真空，热，和宽带压缩真空环境修改探头的敏感性，从而重塑空间传输函数和远场衍射图案。我们表明，水库统计有显着的影响，通过改变诱导光栅的振幅和相位的衍射响应。热储层增强了透射调制，并增加了占主导地位的衍射阶的强度，而挤压真空储层产生强烈的相位敏感的修改，选择性地重新分配衍射通道之间的光功率。我们进一步证明，压缩储层和驱动场之间的失调提供了一种有效的机制来控制折射方向性，从而导致所选角阶数的大幅放大。在二维几何结构中，压缩真空相关性产生高度结构化的相景和强各向异性的折射图案，从而能够定向增强特定的折射通道，同时抑制其他折射通道。这些结果确立了储层工程作为控制最小两级系统中传输、折射效率和角度选择性的通用方法，在可编程光学器件、束转向和量子光学平台中具有潜在应用。


### Abstract

We theoretically investigate the transmission and diffraction of a weak probe field from an electromagnetically induced grating formed in a weakly driven two-level medium coupled to engineered quantum reservoirs. Using a perturbative solution of the optical Bloch equations in the weak-driving regime, we analyze how normal-vacuum, thermal, and broadband squeezed-vacuum environments modify the probe susceptibility and consequently reshape both the spatial transmission function and the far-field diffraction patterns. We show that reservoir statistics have a pronounced impact on the diffraction response by altering the amplitude and phase of the induced grating. Thermal reservoirs enhance the transmission modulation and increase the intensity of the dominant diffraction orders, whereas squeezed-vacuum reservoirs generate strongly phase-sensitive modifications that selectively redistribute optical power among diffraction channels. We further demonstrate that the detuning between the squeezed reservoir and the driving field provides an efficient mechanism for controlling diffraction directionality, leading to substantial amplification of selected angular orders. In two-dimensional geometries, squeezed-vacuum correlations produce highly structured phase landscapes and strongly anisotropic diffraction patterns, enabling directional enhancement of specific diffraction channels while suppressing others. These results establish reservoir engineering as a versatile approach for controlling transmission, diffraction efficiency, and angular selectivity in minimal two-level systems, with potential applications in programmable photonic devices, beam steering, and quantum optical platforms.

## 与零模式相关的临界动态干扰

**Original Title:** Interference of critical dynamics associated with zero modes

**Authors:** Zhi-Han Zhang, Han-Chuan Kou, Peng Li

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.13200v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.13200v2

**Submitted:** 2026-06-11


### 摘要

我们使用连续通过两个临界点的封闭失准路径研究了广义Creutz梯中与零模相关的临界动力学（ICDZR）的干扰。通过读出最终的零模转移概率，我们发现依赖于抑制路径的丰富ICDZR干涉图案。特别是，当闭合路径连接两个非平凡的相时，ICDZR图案可能会消失或呈现周期加倍。在WKB分析的框架内，这种现象通过灭过程中积累的干扰相得到了很好的澄清。我们还证明，零模转移概率可以通过边界粒子数与其初始分数值的偏差来检测，该偏差是由于临界动力学中体模的混合引起的。作为边缘缺陷，零模转移概率捕捉到了非封闭失超路径中ICDZR振荡和已知的异常缺陷产生。这些结果将ICDZR和相应的边缘缺陷识别为与拓扑零模相关的关键动态的探针。


### Abstract

We study the interference of critical dynamics associated with zero modes (ICDZM) in the generalized Creutz ladders using closed quench paths that pass through two critical points successively. By reading out the final zero-mode transfer probability, we find rich ICDZM interference patterns dependent on the quench path. In particular, when the closed path links two topologically nontrivial phases, the ICDZM pattern may either vanish or exhibit period doubling. Within the framework of WKB analysis, this phenomenon is well clarified by the interference phase accumulated in the quench procedure. We also demonstrate that the zero-mode transfer probability can be detected by the deviation of the boundary particle number from its initial fractional value, which arises from the blending of bulk modes in the critical dynamics. As an edge defect, the zero-mode transfer probability captures both the ICDZM oscillation and the known anomalous defect production in a non-closed quench path. These results identify ICDZM and the corresponding edge defect as probes for critical dynamics associated with topological zero modes.

## 钛作为高温集成电路的基础材料

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.13750v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-11


### 摘要

用于量子应用的超导集成电路的性能从根本上受到与材料相关的损失的限制。作为下一代量子电路的新兴材料，近年来，在超导微波共振器和量子位中表现出突破性的性能后引起了相当大的关注。与此同时，越来越多的工作致力于铂基电路和相关制造技术的操作。这种兴趣进一步激发了这种兴趣，导致其各种晶体结构、超导性质、一致性等。此外，钛电路在低温实验中表现出独特的特征，这在铝或钼基实验中没有观察到。在这篇综述中，我们总结了各种基片上的钛薄膜生长和相选择机制的最新研究成果、高温电路制造和性能的关键方面，包括材料第一性原理理论研究。总之，我们解决了一些悬而未决的问题，包括\b{eta}相杂质的作用、氟酸溶液对链特征的影响以及低温下阿尔法-钛链的异常行为。


### Abstract

The performance of superconducting integrated circuits for quantum applications is fundamentally limited by material-related losses. Tantalum, as an emerging material for next-generation quantum circuits, has attracted considerable attention in recent years after demonstrating breakthrough performance in both superconducting microwave resonators and qubits. Concurrently, a growing body of work is devoted to the operation of tantalum-based circuits and related fabrication techniques. This interest is further stimulated by tantalum thin films polymorphism resulting in a variety of its crystalline structure, superconducting properties, coherence, etc. Furthermore, tantalum circuits exhibit distinctive features in cryogenic experiments, which have not been observed in aluminum- or niobium-based ones. In this review, we summarize the recent research of tantalum thin films growth and phase selection mechanisms on various substrates, key aspects of fabrication and performance of superconducting circuit, including a material first-principles theoretical study. In conclusion, we address a number of open issues, including the role of \b{eta}-phase impurities, the effect of hydrofluoric acid solutions on chain characteristics, and the anomalous behavior of α-tantalum chains at cryogenic temperatures.

## 弹跳和球体之间热转变的复兴

**Original Title:** Resurgence of the Thermal Transition between Bounce and Sphaleron

**Authors:** Shaun D. Hampton, Kyungsun Lee, Sungjay Lee

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.13778v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.13778v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-11


### 摘要

我们从博雷尔复活的观点出发，研究亚稳真空量子力学中弹跳与球体之间的热转变。对于代表二阶和一阶转变的两个模型，我们计算热自由能到高级的微扰展开，并提取领先的博雷尔奇异性数据$（A，b，S）$作为温度的函数。博雷尔奇性位置$A$再现了过渡两侧主导马鞍的壳上动作，在二阶情况下顺利连接，在一阶情况下发展出扭结。特征指数$b$在整个过渡期间在$0$和$1/2$之间跳跃，计算相应马鞍的零模式。斯托克斯常数$S$与马鞍周围的单循环决定性相匹配。因此，假真空周围的微扰膨胀决定了转变温度、转变顺序和衰变率，包括单回路预因子，而不依赖半经典输入。


### Abstract

We study the thermal transition between the bounce and the sphaleron in quantum mechanics with a metastable vacuum from the viewpoint of Borel resurgence. For two models representing a second-order and a first-order transition, we compute the perturbative expansion of the thermal free energy to high orders and extract the leading Borel singularity data $(A,b,S)$ as functions of temperature. The Borel singularity location $A$ reproduces the on-shell action of the dominant saddle on both sides of the transition, joining smoothly in the second-order case and developing a kink in the first-order case. The characteristic exponent $b$ jumps between $0$ and $1/2$ across the transition, counting the zero modes of the corresponding saddle. The Stokes constant $S$ matches the one-loop determinant around the saddle. The perturbative expansion around the false vacuum thus determines the transition temperature, the order of the transition, and the decay rate including the one-loop prefactor without relying on semiclassical inputs.

## 基于矩阵积状态的量子轨迹模拟中的计算机制

**Original Title:** Computational regimes in matrix-product-state-based quantum trajectory simulations

**Authors:** Aaron Sander, Simon Cichy, Martin Eigel, Jens Eisert, Maximilian Fröhlich, Tom Peham, Robert Wille

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.13779v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.13779v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-11


### 摘要

开放量子系统的有效模拟是建模有噪音的量子硬件和多体动力学的核心。在基于子索的张量网络方法中，成本通常与子索级量（例如纠缠增长或键维）相关。然而，固定精度模拟的总成本也取决于统计抽样，并且每个轨迹复杂性和抽样工作量之间的相互作用仍然知之甚少。在这里，我们引入了基于矩阵积状态（MPS）的量子轨迹模拟的成本分解框架，该框架将总成本分解为每个轨迹的内存、每个轨迹的运行时间和采样工作量。我们表明，相同Lindblad动态的物理等效随机解开不一定会降低总成本，而是在轨迹复杂性和统计收敛之间重新分配成本。这种权衡由两个无维膨胀因子量化：键维膨胀$a $和抽样膨胀$k $，它们共同确定了在硬件相关内存和并行性约束下的首选解包。我们提供了一种实用的协议，用于从适度的试点模拟中提取$（a，k）$，并使用跨多个噪音通道的基准进行演示。由此产生的决策图表明，计算上有利的解开可能会随着噪音强度、时间步分辨率、系统大小和可用并行性而变化。这些结果将解开选择确定为一个硬件感知的模拟设计问题，而不是单独对轨迹纠缠的内在优化。


### Abstract

Efficient simulation of open quantum systems is central to modeling noisy quantum hardware and many-body dynamics. In trajectory-based tensor network methods, cost is often associated with trajectory-level quantities such as entanglement growth or bond dimension. However, the total cost of a fixed-accuracy simulation also depends on statistical sampling, and the interplay between per-trajectory complexity and sampling effort remains poorly understood. Here we introduce a cost-resolved framework for matrix product state (MPS)-based quantum trajectory simulations that decomposes total cost into memory per trajectory, runtime per trajectory, and sampling effort. We show that physically equivalent stochastic unravelings of the same Lindblad dynamics do not necessarily reduce total cost, but instead redistribute cost between trajectory complexity and statistical convergence. This trade-off is quantified by two dimensionless inflation factors: a bond dimension inflation $α$ and a sampling inflation $κ$, which together determine the preferred unraveling under hardware-dependent memory and parallelism constraints. We provide a practical protocol for extracting $(α,κ)$ from modest pilot simulations and demonstrate it using benchmarks across multiple noise channels. The resulting decision maps show that the computationally favorable unraveling can change with noise strength, time-step resolution, system size, and available parallelism. These results establish unraveling choice as a hardware-aware simulation design problem rather than an intrinsic optimization of trajectory entanglement alone.

## 超越临界性的稳定器熵的普遍交叉

**Original Title:** Universal Crossovers of Stabilizer Entropy Beyond Criticality

**Authors:** Reyhaneh Khasseh, E. A. Ramirez Trino, M. A. Rajabpour

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.13810v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.13810v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-11


### 摘要

稳定器雷尼（Rényi）熵已成为量子多体系统中非稳定性的一种探索，但与纠缠论相比，其临界点以外的标度结构仍然知之甚少。最近的场论方法表明，稳定器信息包含普遍的关键数据和边界敏感项，这引发了这些结构如何扩展到大规模和交叉区域的问题。我们针对雷尼指数二分之一的一类有限范围旋转链解决了这个问题。我们为全周期链和无限链的有限间隔推导出精确的有限大小公式，使从临界行为到非临界行为的普遍交叉可以通过分析访问。在周期几何中，信息量遵循远离临界的体积定律，并表现出受系统大小和相关长度之间竞争控制的普适有限大小交叉。我们还表明，大规模SRE密度在场调谐临界线上形成了一个尖点，而XX端点则由与饱和点相关的独特标度机制决定。在子系统几何结构中，区间信息将体临界行为与有限区域切割无限链的方式产生的边界贡献分开。然后，从临界行为到大规模行为的交叉被编码在由相关长度控制的边界常数和普适函数中。通过精确的稳定子-熵对应关系，标度理论扩展到内部XY约化、超临界范围旋转链和集群-伊辛代表。我们的结果为孤立保形点以外的稳定器信息的新兴QFT描述提供了精确的格点基准。


### Abstract

Stabilizer Rényi entropy has emerged as a probe of nonstabilizerness in quantum many-body systems, but its scaling structure beyond critical points remains poorly understood compared with entanglement entropy. Recent field-theory approaches indicate that stabilizer entropy contains universal critical data and boundary-sensitive terms, raising the question of how these structures extend into massive and crossover regimes. We address this problem for a broad class of finite-range spin chains at Rényi index one-half. We derive exact finite-size formulas for both full periodic chains and finite intervals of the infinite chain, making the universal crossover from critical to noncritical behavior analytically accessible. In periodic geometry, the entropy obeys a volume law away from criticality and exhibits a universal finite-size crossover controlled by the competition between system size and correlation length. We also show that the large-scale SRE density develops a cusp across the field-tuned critical line, while the XX endpoint is governed by a distinct scaling regime associated with the saturation point. In the subsystem geometry, the interval entropy separates bulk critical behavior from boundary contributions generated by the way the finite region cuts the infinite chain. The crossover from critical to massive behavior is then encoded in boundary constants and universal functions controlled by the correlation length. Through exact stabilizer-entropy correspondences, the scaling theory extends to internal XY reductions, Finite-range spin chains, and Cluster--Ising representatives. Our results provide an exact lattice benchmark for the emerging QFT description of stabilizer entropy beyond isolated conformal points.

## 将量子运算符与大型语言模型对齐

### Abstract

## 用Connes谱距离标定Bloch球上的Helstrom几何

## 腔磁振子中均匀磁振子Hamilton量的集体旋转推导

**Original Title:** A Collective-Spin Derivation of the Uniform Magnon Hamiltonian in Cavity Magnonics
