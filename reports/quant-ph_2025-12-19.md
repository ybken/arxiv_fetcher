## 量子系统的关系出现时间：多观察者、引力和宇宙学框架

**Original Title:** Relational Emergent Time for Quantum System: A Multi-Observer, Gravitational, and Cosmological Framework

**Authors:** Amir Hossein Ghasemi

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.15789v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.15789v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-15


### 摘要

我们提出了一个关系框架，其中时间结构不是基本的，而是从全球静止量子状态内的相关性中产生的。每个子系统都包括一个内部时钟，条件状态相对于这些内部读数有效演变。该结构自然地扩展到相对论运动、引力红移和宇宙膨胀，导致在不同物理状态下有效的统一出现时间函数。该理论再现了经典的时间膨胀，预测了与标准进化的相关性偏差，并表明非相互作用或无质量的粒子表现出可忽略的内部时间。这些结果为时间物理学基础的概念和实验研究开辟了方向，从多时钟量子系统到精确计量和宇宙学设置。特别是，该框架表明高度纠缠系统与标准量子进化存在可测量的偏差，并预测无质量粒子的内部时间可以忽略不计。


### Abstract

We present a relational framework in which temporal structure is not fundamental but emerges from correlations within a globally stationary quantum state. Each subsystem includes an internal clock, and conditional states evolve effectively with respect to these internal readings. The construction naturally extends to relativistic motion, gravitational redshift, and cosmological expansion, leading to a unified emergent-time functional valid across diverse physical regimes. The theory reproduces classical time dilation, predicts correlation-dependent deviations from standard evolution, and suggests that non-interacting or massless particles exhibit negligible internal time. These consequences open directions for conceptual and experimental investigations in the foundations of temporal physics, from multi-clock quantum systems to precision metrology and cosmological settings. In particular, the framework suggests measurable deviation from standard quantum evolution for highly entangled systems and predicts negligible internal time for massless particles.

## 一维系统中量子临界点的平方变形方法

**Original Title:** A sine-square deformation approach to quantum critical points in one-dimensional systems

**Authors:** Yuki Miyazaki, Shiori Tanigawa, Giacomo Marmorini, Nobuo Furukawa, Daisuke Yamamoto

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.14149v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.14149v2

**Submitted:** 2025-12-16


### 摘要

我们提出了一种使用平方变形（SSD）确定一维系统量子相界的方法。基于这一命题，该命题得到了几个精确解决的情况的支持，尽管没有得到完全一般性的证明，即“如果一维系统是无间隙的，那么具有SSD的Hamilton的底态中任何局部观测值的期望值在热力学极限下表现出平移对称性”，我们确定了量子临界点作为局部观测值变得与站点无关的位置，通过有限规模缩放分析识别。作为案例研究，我们考虑了两个模型：具有最近邻和远程相互作用的混合横向和纵向磁场中的反铁磁伊辛链。我们使用密度矩阵重正化群算法通过SSD计算这些Hamilton体的底薪，并评估局部横向磁性。对于最近邻模型，我们表明，量子临界点可以通过我们的程序在多达84个位点甚至更小的系统中准确估计，这与文献的结果非常一致。对于远程模型，我们发现反铁磁相和顺磁相之间的相边界相对于最近邻的情况略有移动，导致反铁磁有序区域减少。此外，我们还提出了一种实验方法，在光钳中使用里德伯原子阵列实现反铁磁$J_1$-$J_2$伊辛耦合，这可以在很好的近似范围内实现。由于自然出现多个独立的标度条件，我们的方法能够精确确定量子临界点，甚至可能从相对小的系统大小中提取额外的临界现象，例如临界指数。


### Abstract

We propose a method to determine the quantum phase boundaries of one-dimensional systems using sine-square deformation (SSD). Based on the proposition, supported by several exactly solved cases though not proven in full generality, that "if a one-dimensional system is gapless, then the expectation value of any local observable in the ground state of the Hamiltonian with SSD exhibits translational symmetry in the thermodynamic limit," we determine the quantum critical point as the location where a local observable becomes site-independent, identified through finite-size scaling analysis. As case studies, we consider two models: the antiferromagnetic Ising chain in mixed transverse and longitudinal magnetic fields with nearest-neighbor and long-range interactions. We calculate the ground state of these Hamiltonians with SSD using the density-matrix renormalization-group algorithm and evaluate the local transverse magnetization. For the nearest-neighbor model, we show that the quantum critical point can be accurately estimated by our procedure with systems of up to 84 sites, or even smaller, in good agreement with results from the literature. For the long-range model, we find that the phase boundary between the antiferromagnetic and paramagnetic phases is slightly shifted relative to the nearest-neighbor case, leading to a reduced region of antiferromagnetic order. Moreover, we propose an experimental procedure to implement the antiferromagnetic $J_1$-$J_2$ Ising couplings with SSD using Rydberg atom arrays in optical tweezers, which can be achieved within a very good approximation. Because multiple independent scaling conditions naturally emerge, our approach enables precise determination of quantum critical points and possibly even the extraction of additional critical phenomena, such as critical exponents, from relatively small system sizes.

## 无穷小动力学分级的局部化：临界标度和Kibble-Zurek普适性

**Original Title:** Localization from Infinitesimal Kinetic Grading: Critical Scaling and Kibble-Zurek Universality

**Authors:** Argha Debnath, Ayan Sahoo, Debraj Rakshit

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.15795v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.15795v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-16


### 摘要

我们研究了一个一维网格模型，其最近邻跳幅度遵循乘势定律分布。跳频变化由分级指数$a $控制，该指数作为系统的调谐参数。在热力学极限下，底价变成局部化为$|α|\到0$，表明存在以分歧定位长度为特征的临界点。使用精确的对角化，我们执行有限大小缩放分析并提取管理这种分歧的相关临界指数，揭示了与著名的安德森、奥布里-安德烈和斯塔克局部化不同的普适性类。为了进一步描述临界行为，我们分析了逆参与比、基态和第一激发态之间的能差以及保真度敏感性。我们还通过以各种速率线性增加跳跃曲线并跟踪局部长度和逆参与比的演变来研究非平衡动力学。Kibble-Zurek机制使用从静态缩放分析中获得的临界指数成功捕获了由此产生的动态。我们的结果证明了一条干净、无无序的定位途径，并提供了一个与具有工程跳跃轮廓的光格子和超冷原子阵列相关的可调平台。


### Abstract

We study a one-dimensional lattice model with site-dependent nearest-neighbor hopping amplitudes that follow a power-law profile. The hopping variation is controlled by a grading exponent, $α$, which serves as the tuning parameter of the system. In the thermodynamic limit, the ground state becomes localized as $|α| \to 0$, signaling the presence of a critical point characterized by a diverging localization length. Using exact diagonalization, we perform finite-size scaling analysis and extract the associated critical exponent governing this divergence, revealing a universality class distinct from well-known Anderson, Aubry-Andre, and Stark localization. To further characterize the critical behavior, we analyze the inverse participation ratio, the energy gap between the ground and first excited states, and the fidelity susceptibility. We also investigate nonequilibrium dynamics by linearly ramping the hopping profile at various rates and tracking the evolution of the localization length and the inverse participation ratio. The Kibble-Zurek mechanism successfully captures the resulting dynamics using the critical exponents obtained from the static scaling analysis. Our results demonstrate a clean, disorder-free route to localization and provide a tunable platform relevant to photonic lattices and ultracold atom arrays with engineered hopping profiles.

## 用非负维格纳函数描述量子状态集

**Original Title:** Characterising the sets of quantum states with non-negative Wigner function

**Authors:** Nicolas J. Cerf, Ulysse Chabaud, Jack Davis, Nuno C. Dias, João N. Prata, Zacharie Van Herstraeten

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.14820v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.14820v2

**Submitted:** 2025-12-16


### 摘要

对于Hilbert空间$\mathcal H\subseteq L^2（\mathbb R）$，我们考虑Wigner正态（WPS）的凸集$\mathcal D_+（\mathcal H）$，即H$上的密度矩阵，具有非负Wigner函数。我们调查这些集的拓扑结构，即关于封闭性，紧性，内部和边界（在相对拓扑诱导的迹规范）。我们还研究了它们的几何结构，构造了通过凸组合生成$\mathcal D_+（\mathcal H）$的最小状态集。如果$\mathcal H$是有限维的，那么这些集合的存在性可以从凸分析中的一个中心结果得出，即Krein-Milman定理。在无限维的情况下，$\mathcal H=L^2（\mathbb R）$并非如此，因为集合$\mathcal D_+（\mathcal H）$缺乏紧性。然而，我们证明了Krein-Milman定理在这种情况下成立，这使我们能够将大部分关于生成元集的结果扩展到无限维环境。最后，我们研究了WPS的有限维集和无限维集之间的关系，证明了前者提供了一个闭子集的层次，它们也是后者的真面。这些结果提供了一个基础的操作特性的极端点的WPS，我们在一个配套文件中进行。我们的工作提供了一个统一的角度来看，在有限和无限维的WPS集的拓扑和几何性质，以及明确的构造最小集的发电机。


### Abstract

For Hilbert spaces $\mathcal H\subseteq L^2(\mathbb R)$ we consider the convex sets $\mathcal D_+(\mathcal H)$ of Wigner-positive states (WPS), i.e.~density matrices over $\mathcal H$ with non-negative Wigner function. We investigate the topological structure of these sets, namely concerning closure, compactness, interior and boundary (in a relative topology induced by the trace norm). We also study their geometric structure and construct minimal sets of states that generate $\mathcal D_+(\mathcal H)$ through convex combinations. If $\mathcal H$ is finite-dimensional, the existence of such sets follows from a central result in convex analysis, namely the Krein-Milman theorem. In the infinite-dimensional case $\mathcal H=L^2(\mathbb R)$ this is not so, due to lack of compactness of the set $\mathcal D_+(\mathcal H)$. Nevertheless, we prove that a Krein-Milman theorem holds in this case, which allows us to extend most of the results concerning the sets of generators to the infinite-dimensional setting. Finally, we study the relation between the finite and infinite-dimensional sets of WPS, and prove that the former provide a hierarchy of closed subsets, which are also proper faces of the latter. These results provide a basis for an operational characterisation of the extreme points of the sets of WPS, which we undertake in a companion paper. Our work offers a unified perspective on the topological and geometric properties of the sets of WPS in finite and infinite dimensions, along with explicit constructions of minimal sets of generators.

## 极端非负维格纳函数

**Original Title:** Extreme non-negative Wigner functions

**Authors:** Zacharie Van Herstraeten, Jack Davis, Nuno C. Dias, João N. Prata, Nicolas J. Cerf, Ulysse Chabaud

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.14831v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.14831v2

**Submitted:** 2025-12-16


### 摘要

提供维格纳正状态（OPS）的操作特征，即，具有非负维格纳函数的量子状态集是一个长期存在的悬而未决的问题。对于纯状态，唯一的WP是高斯状态，但对于混合状态，情况要微妙得多。在这里，我们使用凸几何来处理这个问题，将这个问题简化为对OPS集合的极点的描述。我们给出了一种生成一大类此类极端WP的构造性方法，该方法结合了以下步骤：（i）我们描述了维格纳正准状态超集（WPQS）的相不变极点;（ii）我们引入了一种新的量子地图，名为Verdum地图，它将极端WPQS映射到极端WP，同时保持相不变性;（iii）我们识别了极端保留地图的家族，并使用它们来获得极端WP，同时放松相不变性。我们的结构生成所有低维度的极端WP，从称为分束器状态的特定类型的WP开始。我们的结果建立在配套论文中推导出的WP集的新数学性质的基础上，并揭示了具有非负维格纳函数的混合状态的显着结构。


### Abstract

Providing an operational characterization of the Wigner-positive states (WPS), i.e., the set of quantum states with non-negative Wigner function, is a longstanding open problem. For pure states, the only WPS are Gaussian states, but the situation is considerably more subtle for mixed states. Here, we approach the problem using convex geometry, reducing the question to the characterization of the extreme points of the set of WPS. We give a constructive method to generate a large class of such extreme WPS, which combines the following steps: (i) we characterize the phase-invariant extreme points of the superset of Wigner-positive quasi-states (WPQS); (ii) we introduce a new quantum map, named Vertigo map, which maps extreme WPQS to extreme WPS while preserving phase invariance; (iii) we identify families of extremality-preserving maps and use them to obtain extreme WPS while relaxing phase invariance. Our construction generates all extreme WPS of low dimension, starting from a specific kind of WPS known as beam-splitter states. Our results build upon new mathematical properties of the set of WPS derived in a companion paper and unveil the remarkable structure of mixed states with non-negative Wigner functions.

## 具有保留米制自动编码器的几何潜空间断层扫描

**Original Title:** Geometric Latent Space Tomography with Metric-Preserving Autoencoders

**Authors:** S. M. Yousuf Iqbal Tomal, Abdullah Al Shafin

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.15801v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.15801v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-16


### 摘要

量子态层析成像面临着系统规模的指数缩放，而最近的神经网络方法实现多项式缩放的代价是失去量子态空间的几何结构。我们引入几何潜在空间层析成像，将经典神经编码器与参数化量子电路解码器相结合，这些解码器通过度量保持损失进行训练，该度量保持损失强制执行潜在欧几里得距离和量子Bures测地线之间的比例。在纯度为0.85- 0.95的NISQ年代退相干的两量子比特混合态上，我们实现了高保真度的重建（平均保真度F = 0.942pm 0.03$），得到了一个可解释的20维潜在结构.关键的是，隐测地线与Bures距离具有很强的线性相关性（Pearson r = 0.88，R^2 = 0.78），保持了78%的量子度规结构.几何分析揭示了内在的多维为6.35与20个环境维度和可测量的局部弯曲（$k = 0.011 \下午0.006$），证实了非平凡的Riemann几何，具有$O（d#2）$的计算优势优于$O（4#n）$密度矩阵运算。与先前的神经断层扫描不同，我们的几何感知潜在空间能够实现直接状态区分、根据欧几里得距离进行保真度估计，以及可解释的误差集，以缓解量子误差，而无需重复的全断层扫描，为具有有限相干时间的NISQ设备提供关键功能。


### Abstract

Quantum state tomography faces exponential scaling with system size, while recent neural network approaches achieve polynomial scaling at the cost of losing the geometric structure of quantum state space. We introduce geometric latent space tomography, combining classical neural encoders with parameterized quantum circuit decoders trained via a metric-preservation loss that enforces proportionality between latent Euclidean distances and quantum Bures geodesics. On two-qubit mixed states with purity 0.85--0.95 representing NISQ-era decoherence, we achieve high-fidelity reconstruction (mean fidelity $F = 0.942 \pm 0.03$) with an interpretable 20-dimensional latent structure. Critically, latent geodesics exhibit strong linear correlation with Bures distances (Pearson $r = 0.88$, $R^2 = 0.78$), preserving 78\% of quantum metric structure. Geometric analysis reveals intrinsic manifold dimension 6.35 versus 20 ambient dimensions and measurable local curvature ($κ= 0.011 \pm 0.006$), confirming non-trivial Riemannian geometry with $O(d^2)$ computational advantage over $O(4^n)$ density matrix operations. Unlike prior neural tomography, our geometry-aware latent space enables direct state discrimination, fidelity estimation from Euclidean distances, and interpretable error manifolds for quantum error mitigation without repeated full tomography, providing critical capabilities for NISQ devices with limited coherence times.

## 凸块约束下的熵稳定性和谱集中度

**Original Title:** Entropy Stability and Spectral Concentration under Convex Block Constraints

**Authors:** Hassan Nasreddine

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.16192v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.16192v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-17


### 摘要

我们研究了受凸块对角约束的量子状态的熵最小化问题。我们的主要结果是一个定量稳定性定理：如果一个状态在固定块约束下的最小可能值的√范围内，那么它必须位于熵最小化器的踪迹范中的O（√ '内。我们表明这个速率是最佳的。该分析完全是有限维的，依赖于将信息量精确分解为经典和内部成分，以及尖锐的相对信息量不等式。作为一个应用，我们研究了有限加性运算符，其谱分解会导致自然块约束。在这种情况下，稳定性定理产生了诱导光谱测量的定量非浓度界限。该框架是抽象的且独立于算术输入。它为线性谱约束下的熵最小化器提供了一般稳定性原则。


### Abstract

We investigate entropy minimization problems for quantum states subject to convex block-diagonal constraints. Our principal result is a quantitative stability theorem: if a state has entropy within epsilon of the minimum possible value under a fixed block constraint, then it must lie within O(epsilon^{1/2}) in trace norm of the manifold of entropy minimizers. We show that this rate is optimal. The analysis is entirely finite-dimensional and relies on a precise decomposition of entropy into classical and internal components, together with sharp relative entropy inequalities. As an application, we study finite additive operators whose spectral decomposition induces natural block constraints. In this setting, the stability theorem yields quantitative non-concentration bounds for induced spectral measures. The framework is abstract and independent of arithmetic input. It provides a general stability principle for entropy minimizers under linear spectral constraints.

## 量子信息中的优化技术

**Original Title:** Optimization Techniques in Quantum Information

**Authors:** Benjamin Desef

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.15831v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.15831v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-17


### 摘要

本论文重点关注数学和计算优化与量子信息的交叉。主要贡献是开源软件代码：混合“传统”非凸和凸方法的混合方法可以使困难的问题更容易理解。演示了如何有效地实现此类算法，避免界面瓶颈，找到通过有损通道建立纠缠的最佳协议。开发的中央软件包解决了多项优化问题。许多问题自然只涉及多项目标和约束多项。此类问题可以自动转化为提供外部逼近层次结构的半定程序。由此产生的问题往往如此之大，而且涉及的可变数量和程度也如此不利，以至于很快就达到了可行性的边界。然而，硬件和算法方面的技术进步已经突破了这一界限，但用于多项优化的软件框架并没有以同样的方式遵循，现在往往使它们成为以前解决方案的瓶颈。本文期间开发的PolynomialOptimizing.jl包旨在填补这一空白，并提供一个非常资源高效的中间层以及大量算法来减少问题规模，并自然支持量子信息问题中普遍存在的复数和半定约束。演示了它在纠缠分布问题上的应用，表明即使具有三位和四位大小的半定矩阵的松弛也可以方便地解决。最后，开发了一种以近乎时间最优的方式计算平方和矩阵锥的中点障碍的新方法，其有效实施有可能进一步减少资源消耗。


### Abstract

This thesis focuses on the intersection of mathematical and computational optimization and quantum information. Main contributions are open-source software code: A hybrid approach mixing "traditional" nonconvex and convex methods can make difficult problems more accessible. A demonstration of how to efficiently implement such an algorithm, avoiding interfacial bottlenecks, is provided, finding optimal protocols to establish entanglement through a lossy channel. The central software package developed addresses polynomial optimization problems. Many problems naturally involve only a polynomial objective and constraint polynomials. Such problems can automatically be cast into semidefinite programs that provide a hierarchy of outer approximations. The resulting problems are often so large and scale so unfavorably with respect to the variable number and degree involved that the boundary of the doable is reached quickly. However, technical progress both in hardware and algorithms has pushed this boundary - but software frameworks for polynomial optimization have not followed in the same manner, often now making them the bottleneck that before was the solver. The package PolynomialOptimization.jl developed during this thesis aims to fill the gap and provide a very resource-efficient intermediate layer together with a wide number of algorithms to reduce the problem size, and naturally supporting complex numbers and semidefinite constraints ubiquitous in quantum information problems. Its application on an entanglement distribution problem is demonstrated, showing that even relaxations with semidefinite matrices of three- and four-digit size can be solved conveniently. Finally, a new way to calculate interior-point barriers for the cone of sums-of-squares matrices in a nearly time-optimal way is developed, whose efficient implementation has the potential of further reducing resource consumption.

## 用于基于CCD的俘获离子量子位测量中实时神经网络处理的低延迟DSP控制系统

**Original Title:** Low-Latency FPGA Control System for Real-Time Neural Network Processing in CCD-Based Trapped-Ion Qubit Measurement

**Authors:** Binglei Lou, Gautham Duddi Krishnaswaroop, Filip Wojcicki, Ruilin Wu, Richard Rademacher, Zhiqiang Que, Wayne Luk, Philip H. W. Leong

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.15838v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.15838v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-17


### 摘要

准确且低延迟的量子位状态测量对于俘获离子量子计算至关重要。虽然深度神经网络（DNN）已被集成以增强检测保真度，但其在特定硬件平台上的延迟性能仍然未充分研究。这项工作对现场可编程门阵列（FPGAs）和图形处理单元（GPUs）上基于DNN的量子位检测的延迟进行了基准测试。该DSP解决方案直接将电子相乘电荷耦合器件（EMCCD）与后续数据处理逻辑连接，消除了缓冲和接口管理费用。作为基线，基于运算处理器的系统采用高速ASIC图像捕获器进行图像输入，并采用I/O卡进行状态输出。我们在硬件上部署多层感知器（MLP）和Vision Transformer（ViT）模型来评估测量性能。与传统的阈值处理相比，DNN将平均测量保真度（MF）误差降低了1.8-2.5x（一量子位情况）和4.2-7.6x（三量子位情况）。基于FPGA的MLP和ViT实现了微秒级和微秒级的推理延迟，而完整的单次测量过程则实现了超过100倍的加速。此外，时钟周期级信号分析揭示了通过Cameralink传输EMCCD数据的效率低下，这表明优化该接口可以进一步利用超低延迟DNN推理的优势，指导下一代量子位检测系统的开发。


### Abstract

Accurate and low-latency qubit state measurement is critical for trapped-ion quantum computing. While deep neural networks (DNNs) have been integrated to enhance detection fidelity, their latency performance on specific hardware platforms remains underexplored. **Authors:** Yanxuan Shao, Jannik L. Wyss, Don Towsley, Adilson E. Motter

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.15884v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.15884v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-17


### 摘要

量子通信网络的现有协议通常假设量子纠缠资源的初始分配，然后通过本地操作和经典通信（LOCC）来操纵这些资源，以在远程方之间建立高保真纠缠。人们普遍认为，由此产生的保真度将随着纠缠预算的增加而单调增加。在这里，我们表明，对于非合作LOCC协议，随着更多纠缠被添加到具有非纯状态的网络中，所得的保真度可能会降低。这种效应源于自私路由的量子模拟，并构成大型量子网络中资源优化利用的潜在障碍。


### Abstract

Existing protocols for quantum communication networks usually assume an initial allocation of quantum entanglement resources, which are then manipulated through local operations and classical communication (LOCC) to establish high-fidelity entanglement between distant parties. It is generally held that the resulting fidelity would increase monotonically with the entanglement budget. Here, we show that for noncooperative LOCC protocols, the resulting fidelity may decrease as more entanglement is added to a network with non-pure states. This effect results from a quantum analog of selfish routing and constitutes a potential obstacle to the optimal use of resources in large quantum networks.

## 压缩量子错误纠正码

**Original Title:** Deflating quantum error-correcting codes

**Authors:** Jaron Skovsted Gundersen, Rene Bødker Christensen, Petar Popovski, Rafał Wisniewski

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.15887v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.15887v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-17


### 摘要

在这项工作中，我们引入了一种缩短量子稳定器代码长度的技术，我们称之为代码紧缩。在去除多个qudit的情况下，缩小可以被视为众所周知的穿孔和缩短技术的概括。我们表明，泄气量子码的参数是可以控制的，并认为类似的方法在应用于经典线性码时并不那么有益。此外，研究表明，与仅连续应用穿孔和缩短相比，放气引入了额外的自由度。我们推测，通过缩小代码而不是连续使用穿孔和缩短来获得更好的参数。


### Abstract

In this work, we introduce a technique for reducing the length of a quantum stabilizer code, and we call this deflation of the code. Deflation can be seen as a generalization of the well-known puncturing and shortening techniques in cases where more than a single qudit is removed. We show that the parameters of the deflated quantum code can be controlled, and argue that a similar approach is not as beneficial when applied to classical linear codes. Furthermore, it is shown that deflation introduces additional freedom compared to applying just puncturing and shortening consecutively. We exemplify that it is possible to obtain better parameters by deflating a code rather than consecutively using puncturing and shortening.

## 癌症靶向光敏剂光反应性的量子算法

**Original Title:** Quantum Algorithms for Photoreactivity in Cancer-Targeted Photosensitizers

**Authors:** Yanbing Zhou, Pablo A. M. Casares, Diksha Dhawan, Ignacio Loaiza, Soran Jahangiri, Robert A. Lang, Juan Miguel Arrazola, Stepan Fomichev

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.15889v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.15889v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-17


### 摘要

光动力疗法（PDC）是一种靶向癌症治疗方法，使用光激活光敏剂产生活性氧物种，选择性地破坏肿瘤细胞，通常比传统治疗造成的附带损害更少。然而，它的临床成功取决于具有强光学敏感性和高效产生活性氧的光敏剂的可用性。虽然经典计算方法为光敏剂设计提供了有用的见解，但它们难以扩展，并且通常缺乏这些模拟所需的准确性。在这项工作中，我们展示了如何使用耐故障量子算法来识别有前途的光动力疗法候选光敏剂。为了预测光敏剂的性能，我们评估了两个计算性质。首先，我们通过使用阈值投影算法计算治疗窗口中的累积吸收来量化光敏度。其次，我们通过使用进化代理方法估计系统间穿越（ISC）率，并在适当的情况下辅之以振动动力学处理来确定活性氧产生的效率。我们将这些算法应用于临床相关且积极追求的一类光敏剂，即BODIPY衍生物，包括对经典方法具有挑战性的重原子和过渡金属取代体系。我们通过PennyLane获得的资源估计表明，可以使用180美元-350美元的逻辑量子位和107美元和109美元之间的Toffoli门深度来模拟活动空间范围从11到45个空间轨道的系统，使我们的算法在现实的可容忍量子设备的能力范围内。这为设计光敏剂的高效基于量子的工作流程铺平了道路，这些光敏剂可以加速新的光动力学试剂的发现。


### Abstract

Photodynamic therapy (PDT) is a targeted cancer treatment that uses light-activated photosensitizers to generate reactive oxygen species that selectively destroy tumor cells, generally causing less collateral damage than conventional treatments. However, its clinical success hinges on the availability of photosensitizers with strong optical sensitivity and high efficiency in generating reactive oxygen species. While classical computational methods have provided useful insights into photosensitizer design, they struggle to scale and often lack the accuracy needed for these simulations. In this work, we show how fault-tolerant quantum algorithms can be used to identify promising photosensitizer candidates for PDT. To predict photosensitizer performance, we assess two computational properties. First, we quantify light sensitivity by calculating the cumulative absorption in the therapeutic window with a threshold projection algorithm. Second, we determine the efficiency of reactive oxygen generation by estimating intersystem crossing (ISC) rates using the evolution-proxy approach, complemented by a vibronic dynamic treatment where appropriate. We apply these algorithms to a clinically relevant and actively pursued class of photosensitizers, BODIPY derivatives, including heavy-atom and transition-metal-substituted systems that are challenging for classical methods. Our resource estimates, obtained with PennyLane, suggest that systems with active spaces ranging from 11 to 45 spatial orbitals can be simulated using $180$-$350$ logical qubits and Toffoli gate depths between $10^7$ and $10^9$, placing our algorithms within reach of realistic fault-tolerant quantum devices. This paves the way to an efficient quantum-based workflow for designing photosensitizers that can accelerate the discovery of new PDT agents.

## 一般费米子高斯态中的普适和最大纠缠交换

**Original Title:** Universal and Maximal Entanglement Swapping in General Fermionic Gaussian States

**Authors:** Jiyuan Fang, Qicheng Tang, Xueda Wen

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.15890v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.15890v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-17


### 摘要

探索多体系统中的普适纠缠结构既基础又具有挑战性，特别是当系统经历非正操作时。在这项工作中，我们发现了在投射贝尔测量的费米子高斯态中实现最大纠缠交换的普遍机制。我们考虑任意维度的自由费米子系统的两个最初脱钩的、半填充的副本，并对这两个副本中的一半相应位点进行选择后贝尔测量。值得注意的是，测量后状态分解为贝尔对的积，建立了完全独立于初始高斯状态的最大层间纠缠。我们准确地推导出了一般粒子数保持费米子高斯状态的测量后状态，建立了该机制的有效性和普遍性，并利用数值模拟作为一致性检查。这种现象源于费米子统计和高斯性之间的强有力相互作用，揭示了测量引起的最大纠缠的独特费米子路径。


### Abstract

Exploring universal entanglement structure in many-body systems is both fundamental and challenging, particularly when the system undergoes non-unitary operations. In this work, we uncover a universal mechanism for realizing maximal entanglement swapping in fermionic Gaussian states subjected to projective Bell measurements. We consider two initially decoupled, half-filled copies of a free-fermion system in arbitrary dimensions and perform post-selective Bell measurements on half of the corresponding sites across the two copies. Remarkably, the post-measurement state factorizes into a product of Bell pairs, establishing maximal interlayer entanglement entirely independent of the initial Gaussian state. We derive this post-measurement state exactly for general particle-number-conserving fermionic Gaussian states, establishing both the validity and universality of the mechanism, with numerical simulations serving as consistency checks. This phenomenon arises from a robust interplay between fermionic statistics and Gaussianity, revealing a distinct fermionic route to measurement-induced maximal entanglement.

## 更高形式的纠缠不对称性。第一部分.对称性破缺的极限

**Original Title:** Higher-form entanglement asymmetry. Part I. The limits of symmetry breaking

**Authors:** Francesco Benini, Eduardo García-Valdecasas, Stathis Vitouladitis

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.15898v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.15898v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-17


### 摘要

纠缠不对称性是一种相对信息，可以忠实地诊断量子状态（可能是在空间子区域内）的对称性破缺。在这项工作中，我们将这样的框架扩展到更高形式的对称性，并计算具有自发破缺连续零和更高形式对称性的理论中的纠缠不对称性。我们的中心结果之一是一个关于$0$-和$p$-形式对称的信息。在子区域上也有效，它禁止连续$p$-形式对称性在时空维度$d\leq p+2$中自发破缺。我们的定理不仅限定了对称性破缺，还量化了它：自发破缺触发了一种不为零的纠缠不对称性，该不对称性向红外单调增长，并计算戈德斯通场的数量。一路上，我们推导出了有关戈德斯通玻色子和规范场的纠缠量的独立结果。


### Abstract

Entanglement asymmetry is a relative entropy that faithfully diagnoses symmetry breaking in quantum states, possibly within a spatial subregion. In this work, we extend such framework to higher-form symmetries and compute entanglement asymmetry in theories with spontaneously-broken continuous zero- and higher-form symmetries. One of our central results is an entropic Coleman--Mermin--Wagner theorem, for $0$- and $p$-form symmetries, valid also on subregions, which forbids spontaneous breaking of continuous $p$-form symmetries in spacetime dimensions $d\leq p+2$. Our theorem not only qualifies symmetry breaking, it also quantifies it: spontaneous breaking triggers a nonvanishing entanglement asymmetry that grows monotonically towards the infrared, and counts the number of Goldstone fields. Along the way, we derive standalone results concerning the entanglement entropy of Goldstone bosons and gauge fields.

## 布尔函数单查询识别的封闭形式最优量子电路

**Original Title:** Closed-Form Optimal Quantum Circuits for Single-Query Identification of Boolean Functions

**Authors:** Leonardo Bohac

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.15901v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.15901v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-17


### 摘要

我们研究了给定黑匣子（Oracle）访问且允许一个查询的未知单位布尔函数的最小错误识别。我们没有停在抽象的最佳测量上，而是给出了一个完全建设性的解决方案：显式状态准备和显式测量单位，其计算基础读出实现了区分四种可能函数的Helstrom最优成功概率3/4。由此产生的电路深度较低，使用固定门集，并且（在这个最小的设置中）在输入状态中不需要纠缠。除了具体示例之外，主要信息是可操作的。它强调了一种制度，其中最佳Oracle区分不仅定义良好，而且具有可实现性：最佳POVMs崩溃为一个紧凑的门级基元，可以在更大的例程中进行编译、验证和组成。受此启发，我们讨论了一个精神上开放的“如果”问题：对于固定的（n，m，k），最佳k查询识别（可能对于大型假设类）是否可以接受对查询间的确定性、封闭形式的描述和作用于自然n+ m-量子位输入输出寄存器（以及，如果需要的话，小工作寄存器）的最终测量单位？即使这样的描述并不紧凑并且没有回避通用布尔函数的已知电路复杂性障碍，在电路级别做出最佳构造性对于理论到硬件的翻译以及澄清哪些形式的“Oracle访问”在物理上是有意义的。


### Abstract

We study minimum-error identification of an unknown single-bit Boolean function given black-box (oracle) access with one allowed query. Rather than stopping at an abstract optimal measurement, we give a fully constructive solution: an explicit state preparation and an explicit measurement unitary whose computational-basis readout achieves the Helstrom-optimal success probability 3/4 for distinguishing the four possible functions. The resulting circuit is low depth, uses a fixed gate set, and (in this smallest setting) requires no entanglement in the input state. Beyond the specific example, the main message is operational. It highlights a regime in which optimal oracle discrimination is not only well-defined but implementably explicit: the optimal POVM collapses to a compact gate-level primitive that can be compiled, verified, and composed inside larger routines. Motivated by this, we discuss a "what if" question that is open in spirit: for fixed (n,m,k), could optimal k-query identification (possibly for large hypothesis classes) admit deterministic, closed-form descriptions of the inter-query unitaries and the final measurement unitary acting on the natural n+m-qubit input--output registers (and, if needed, small work registers)? Even when such descriptions are not compact and do not evade known circuit-complexity barriers for generic Boolean functions, making the optimum constructive at the circuit level would be valuable for theory-to-hardware translation and for clarifying which forms of "oracle access" are physically meaningful.

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.15928v1

### 摘要

### Abstract

We work within the end point measurement scheme, which avoids an initial energy measurement and allows quantum resources in the initial state to affect nonequilibrium energy statistics. ## 理解双缝现象的类面团模型

**Original Title:** A Dough-Like Model for Understanding Double-Slit Phenomena

**Authors:** Ping-Rui Tsai, Tzay-Ming Hong

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.15932v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.15932v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-17


### 摘要

双缝实验中观察到的概率干涉条纹生动地展示了量子叠加原理，但它们也凸显了一个基本的概念挑战：测量前后系统之间的关系。根据哥本哈根解释，未观察到的量子系统根据薛定格方程不断演变，而观察导致波函数瞬间坍缩为本征状态。连续进化和突然坍缩之间的这种对比使得单粒子的行为变得特别神秘，特别是考虑到量子力学本身是建立在整体而不是单个实体的统计行为之上的。在这项研究中，我们引入了一个基于深度学习的双缝折射代理模型DSM，旨在捕捉波函数和概率分布之间的映射。DSM探索多个潜在的传播路径，并使用梯度下降自适应地选择最佳传输信道，形成通过网络的信息的骨干。通过比较路径和干涉的可解释性，我们提出了一个直观的物理类比：粒子的行为就像一个可拉伸的面团，延伸穿过两个狭缝，传输后重新连接，允许在屏障前分离。蒙特卡罗模拟证实，该框架可以自然地再现特征干涉和衍射概率图案。我们的方法提供了一个新的，物理上可解释的角度量子叠加和测量引起的崩溃。面团类比预计将扩展到其他量子现象。最后，我们提供了一个基于面团的图片，试图将干扰、纠缠和隧道效应统一为同一潜在现象的表现。


### Abstract

The probabilistic interference fringes observed in the double slit experiment vividly demonstrate the quantum superposition principle, yet they also highlight a fundamental conceptual challenge: the relationship between a system before and after the measurement. According to Copenhagen interpretation, an unobserved quantum system evolves continuously based on the Schrodinger equation, whereas observation induces an instantaneous collapse of the wave function to an eigenstate. This contrast between continuous evolution and sudden collapse renders the single particle behavior particularly enigmatic, especially given that quantum mechanics itself is constructed upon the statistical behavior of ensembles rather than individual entities. In this study, we introduce a Double Slit Diffraction Surrogate Model DSM based on deep learning, designed to capture the mapping between wave functions and probability distributions. The DSM explores multiple potential propagation paths and adaptively selects optimal transmission channels using gradient descent, forming a backbone for the information through the network. By comparing the interpretability of paths and interference, we propose an intuitive physical analogy: the particle behaves like a stretchable dough, extending across both slits, reconnecting after transmission, allowing detachment before the barrier. Monte Carlo simulations confirm that this framework can naturally reproduce the characteristic interference and diffraction probability patterns. Our approach offers a novel, physically interpretable perspective on quantum superposition and measurement induced collapse. The dough analogy is expected to extend to other quantum phenomena. Finally, we provide a dough based picture, attempting to unify interference, entanglement, and tunneling as manifestations of the same underlying phenomenon.
