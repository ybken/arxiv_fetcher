## 手握诱导的旋转选择性调节太阳细菌光合作用中三重链的形成

**Original Title:** Chiral-Induced Spin Selectivity Regulates Triplet formation in Heliobacterial Photosynthesis

**Authors:** Parul Raghuvanshi, Vishvendra Singh Poonia

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.08307v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.08307v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-08


### 摘要

三重体的形成及其调节一直是理解生命系统物理行为的中心兴趣。在有机系统中，过度的三重线形成构成了重大挑战，因为它会促进光化学损伤并降低电荷分离过程的效率，使其调节变得至关重要。在这里，我们对调节太阳细菌反应中心中三重线形成的内在量子旋转动力学进行了理论研究，该系统在没有任何内部磁场的情况下运行。使用基于Lindblad形式主义的开放量子系统方法，我们模拟了太阳细菌反应中心电荷分离期间发生的旋转相关自由基对动力学。该研究系统地考察了三重体的形成如何通过两个关键参数（超精细耦合强度和重组率）的变化来调节，以及这种调节如何进一步受到手征诱导的旋转选择性（CISS）与自由基对机制（RP）的影响。我们的结果表明，CISS效应显着抑制了与太阳细菌分子环境相关的参数空间中的三重体形成，揭示了太阳细菌光合作用中通过旋转控制运作的固有量子保护机制。


### Abstract

Triplet formation and its regulation have always been of central interest in understanding the photophysical behavior of living systems. In organic systems, excessive triplet formation poses significant challenges, as it can promote photochemical damage and reduce the efficiency of charge separation processes, making its regulation critically important.Here, we present a theoretical investigation of the intrinsic quantum spin dynamics governing triplet formation in the heliobacterial reaction center, a system that operates without any internal magnetic field. Using an open quantum systems approach based on the Lindblad formalism, we simulate the spin-correlated radical pair dynamics occurring during charge separation in the heliobacterial reaction center. The study systematically examines how triplet formation is regulated by variations in two key parameters, hyperfine coupling strengths and recombination rates, and how this regulation is further influenced by the inclusion of chirality-induced spin selectivity (CISS) in conjunction with the radical pair mechanism (RPM). Our results demonstrate that the CISS effect significantly suppresses triplet formation across the parameter space relevant to the heliobacterial molecular environment, revealing an intrinsic quantum protective mechanism operating through spin control in heliobacterial photosynthesis.

## 通过分层参数调整实现量子近似优化的最佳搜索QON

**Original Title:** Optimal FALQON for Quantum Approximate Optimization via Layer-wise Parameter Tuning

**Authors:** Michael Mancini, Shabnam Sodagari

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.08332v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.08332v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-08


### 摘要

基于反馈的自适应量子优化（SYS QON）是一种很有前途的方法，用于解决有噪的中等规模量子（NISQ）设备上的组合问题，每层仅需要单个电路评估。然而，标准SYS QON依赖于固定的超参数，这严重限制了收敛速度，需要数百到数千层才能获得可接受的解决方案。本文提出了最优搜索QON，这是一种基于优化的公式，将每层时间步（$δ_k$）和比例因子（$M_k$）视为通过经典方法优化的决策变量。我们对所有94个具有12个点的非同质3-正规图进行了全面的实证研究，比较了最优SYS QON与标准SYS QON和多个QAOA变体。结果表明，在所评估的基准中，成功概率、评估效率和深度标准化成本在统计上显着提高。此外，与固定初始化相比，使用最佳RST QON的参数初始化QAOA可以产生更好的热启动性能。


### Abstract

Feedback-based adaptive quantum optimization (FALQON) is a promising approach for solving combinatorial problems on noisy intermediate-scale quantum (NISQ) devices, requiring only single circuit evaluations per layer. However, standard FALQON relies on fixed hyperparameters that severely limit convergence speed, requiring hundreds to thousands of layers for acceptable solutions. This paper proposes Optimal FALQON, an optimization-based formulation that treats the per-layer time step ($δ_k$) and scaling factor ($M_k$) as decision variables optimized via classical methods. We present a comprehensive empirical study on all 94 non-isomorphic 3-regular graphs with 12 vertices, comparing Optimal FALQON with standard FALQON and multiple QAOA variants. Results demonstrate statistically significant improvements in success probability, evaluation efficiency, and depth-normalized cost across the evaluated benchmarks. Furthermore, initializing QAOA with parameters from Optimal FALQON yields superior warm-start performance compared to fixed initialization.

## 探测器中一致增强的纠缠要求

**Original Title:** Entanglement Requirements for Coherent Enhancement in Detectors

**Authors:** Zachary Bogorad, Roni Harnik

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.08337v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.08337v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-08


### 摘要

相干增强是提高各种探测器灵敏度的强大机制，但其实际用途往往受到制备所需量子状态的难度的限制。我们表明，这种困难有一个基本根源：与检测器相互作用的信号的相干增强受到纠缠的定量限制。我们证明了相干效应的强度如何随着系统大小而缩放的一般界限，作为检测器的单一纠缠量的函数。这些界限在非相关和完全相关区域之间平滑地内插，并适用于参数估计问题和散射过程。我们从两个互补的角度讨论这些结果：首先，它们表现为多体状态量子Fisher信息的界限，这通过量子Cramér-Rao界限直接转化为参数敏感性的限制。其次，它们可以被解释为一类散射横截面积的限制，从而预测最小可检测的相互作用强度如何随着目标尺寸的变化而变化。总而言之，这些结果提供了计量和散射实验中相关增强的统一观点，并推动了产生纠缠检测器状态的新技术的开发。


### Abstract

Coherent enhancement is a powerful mechanism for improving the sensitivity of a wide range of detectors, but its practical use is often limited by the difficulty of preparing the required quantum states. We show that this difficulty has a fundamental origin: coherent enhancement of a signal interacting with a detector is quantitatively constrained by entanglement. We prove general bounds on how the strength of coherent effects can scale with system size, as a function of the single-mode entanglement entropy of the detector. These bounds smoothly interpolate between the incoherent and fully coherent regimes, and apply both to parameter-estimation problems and to scattering processes. We discuss these results from two complementary perspectives: First, they appear as bounds on the quantum Fisher information of many-body states, which translate directly into limits on parameter sensitivity via the quantum Cramér-Rao bound. Second, they can be interpreted as limits on a class of scattering cross sections, leading to predictions for how minimum detectable interaction strengths scale with target size. Together, these results provide a unified view of coherent enhancement in metrology and scattering experiments, and motivate the development of new techniques for generating entangled detector states.

## 通过部分量子误差修正的量子计量

**Original Title:** Quantum metrology via partial quantum error correction

**Authors:** Yinan Chen, Zongyuan Wang, Sisi Zhou

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.08341v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.08341v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-08


### 摘要

我们引入了一种新的误差修正量子计量方法，其中仅需要部分量子误差修正（QEC）来抑制局部噪音并维持探测状态的超标准量子极限（super-SQL）传感性能。这与Phys. Rev. Lett中现有的QEC辅助传感方案形成鲜明对比。112，080801（2014）和Phys. Rev. Lett. 112，150802（2014），其中探测状态被编码到量子码的逻辑子空间中，并且错误纠正涉及对代码的所有检查的测量。在这里，我们将探测状态编码为底层量子代码的能量不同状态的叠加。对于我们的探测状态，使用检查子集的误差纠正足以抑制相印之前和之后的噪音。我们分析了噪音抑制的权衡。对于与运算符权重$l $的相印记并行的噪音，我们实现了$p ^δ $的抑制，其中$p $是噪音强度，$δ =\lfloor（l +1）/2\rfloor $。我们提出了一种自适应的打印机权重增加策略，以在扩展系统时保持超级SQL性能。在我们所有的示例中，检查和阶段印记器都被选择为本地操作员，从而避免非本地连接。


### Abstract

We introduce a new method for error-corrected quantum metrology where only partial quantum error correction (QEC) is needed to suppress local noise and maintain the probe states' super-standard-quantum-limit (super-SQL) sensing performance. This stands in contrast to the existing QEC-assisted sensing schemes in Phys. Rev. Lett. 112, 080801 (2014) and Phys. Rev. Lett. 112, 150802 (2014), where a probe state is encoded into the logical subspace of a quantum code and error correction involves measurements on all checks of the code. Here, we encode the probe states into superpositions of energetically different states of the underlying quantum code. For our probe states, error correction using a subset of checks is enough to suppress noise both before and after phase imprinting. We analyze the tradeoff in noise suppression. For noise parallel to our phase imprinter of operator weight $l$, we achieve a suppression of $p^δ$, where $p$ is the noise strength and $δ= \lfloor (l+1)/2 \rfloor$. We propose an adaptive imprinter-weight-increasing strategy to maintain super-SQL performance as we scale up the system. In all our examples, checks and phase imprinters are chosen to be local operators, avoiding non-local connectivity.

## 涉及时空中封闭实验室的更高级量子过程具有量子控制的因果顺序

**Original Title:** Higher-order quantum processes respecting closed labs in a spacetime have quantum controlled causal order

**Authors:** Matthias Salzger, V. Vilasini

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.08351v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.08351v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-08


### 摘要

在量子因果关系和量子信息中，存在大量抽象量子协议，允许操作之间存在循环或非非循环因果结构，包括不确定因果顺序和过程矩阵等更高级量子过程的框架。一个长期存在的悬而未决的问题是，什么是最大的一类抽象过程，无需后选择就允许物理实现。在这项工作中，我们使用基于相对论因果关系原则的自上而下的方法提供了严格的答案。建立在因果盒框架（它简化了与固定背景时空兼容的最通用量子信息处理协议）的基础上，我们形式化了额外的约束（一次行动+局部顺序），以捕获细粒度时空水平的过程矩阵框架的封闭实验室假设。我们证明，经典非循环时空中满足这些条件的任何协议在行为上都等效于具有因果顺序量子控制（QC-QC）的量子电路，从而从物理原理中提供了QC-QC的自上而下的推导。我们的结果表明，QC-QC恰好构成了一类更高级的量子过程，包括那些具有不定阶的过程，这些过程可以在经典时空中物理实现，排除了封闭实验室假设下更一般的非因果过程。这澄清了抽象的更高级过程矩阵框架和实验可访问的量子协议之间的关系，以及粗粒度循环和细粒度非循环操作因果结构之间的相互作用。我们还开发了流程盒协议的特征化技术，从而引发有关时空量子协议和相对论量子实验的新的基于空度的开放问题。


### Abstract

In quantum causality and quantum information, there is a vast landscape of abstract quantum protocols permitting cyclic or non-acyclic causal structures between operations, including frameworks for indefinite causal order and higher-order quantum processes such as process matrices. A longstanding open question is what is the largest class of abstract processes that admit physical realisations without post-selection. In this work, we provide a rigorous answer using a top-down approach grounded in relativistic causality principles. Building on the framework of causal boxes, which characterise the most general quantum information-processing protocols compatible with fixed background spacetimes, we formalise additional constraints (Acting Once + Local Order) capturing the closed-laboratory assumptions of the process matrix framework at a fine-grained spacetime level. We prove that any protocol in a classical acyclic spacetime satisfying these conditions is behaviourally equivalent to a quantum circuit with quantum control of causal order (QC-QC), providing a top-down derivation of QC-QCs from physical principles. Our results show that QC-QCs constitute precisely the class of higher-order quantum processes, including those with indefinite order, that can be physically realised within classical spacetime, ruling out more general non-causal processes under the closed-labs assumption. This clarifies the relationship between abstract higher-order process matrix frameworks and experimentally accessible quantum protocols, as well as the interplay between coarse-grained cyclic and fine-grained acyclic operational causal structures. We also develop characterisation techniques for process box protocols that lead to new causality-based open questions concerning spacetime quantum protocols and relativistic quantum experiments.

## 量子信息处理的旋转链

**Original Title:** Spin Chains for Quantum Information Processing

**Authors:** Eduardo K. Soares

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.08402v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.08402v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-08


### 摘要

经典计算严重依赖于信息操纵。硬件的每个组件都需要与其他组件通信，这是通过将信息编码成比特串并应用逻辑运算来完成的。在处理量子技术时，出现了一套新的范式和设备，其基于量子位（传统位的量子类似物）的操纵。这项工作研究了量子纠缠（一种独特的非经典相关性）的产生和分布，量子纠缠是量子信息处理的有前途的平台。我们系统地比较了两种不同的纠缠生成协议：协议1（P1），基于交替的弱和强耦合，创建了能够有效三聚体模型逼近的能带结构，而协议2（P2），采用对称边界耦合和虚拟激励来建立链端之间的直接有效相互作用。我们的结果表明，基于虚拟激励和优化边界耦合的协议在速度、实现的纠缠以及针对制造缺陷和噪音的鲁棒性方面始终优于其对应协议。此外，通过采用有效的模型约简和开放量子系统技术，我们提供了一个全面的框架来理解固态量子设备中分布式纠缠的弹性。虚拟耦合协议的特征凸显了其在可扩展量子技术中实验实现的潜力。


### Abstract

Classical computation relies heavily on information manipulation. Each component of a hardware needs to communicate with others, and this is done by encoding information into strings of bits and application of logical operations. When dealing with quantum technologies, there arises a new set of paradigms and devices, based on manipulations of qubits, the quantum analogues of conventional bits. This work investigates the generation and distribution of quantum entanglement, a uniquely non-classical correlation, across spin chains, which serve as promising platforms for quantum information processing. We systematically compare two distinct entanglement generation protocols: Protocol 1 (P1), based on alternating weak and strong couplings that create a band structure enabling an effective trimer-model approximation, and Protocol 2 (P2), which employs symmetric boundary couplings and virtual excitations to establish a direct effective interaction between the chain ends. Our results demonstrate that a protocol based on virtual excitations and optimized boundary couplings consistently outperforms its counterpart in speed, achieved entanglement, and robustness against fabrication imperfections and noise. Furthermore, by employing effective model reductions and open quantum systems techniques we provide a comprehensive framework for understanding the resilience of distributed entanglement in solid-state quantum devices. The characteristics of the virtual-coupling protocol highlight its potential for experimental implementation in scalable quantum technologies.

**Authors:** Brent Russon, Bao Bach, Ed Younis, Ilya Safro

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.09237v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.09237v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-10


### 摘要

可扩展量子位映射和路由仍然是量子编译中的主要瓶颈，尤其是对于俘获离子量子电荷耦合器件（TI-QCCD）架构，其中量子位相互作用需要在严格的运动、拥挤和俘获容量限制下物理地穿梭离子。我们提出了一个围绕位置图抽象构建的编译框架，位置图抽象是可执行位置、移动路径和路由约束的统一表示，使启发式映射器能够直接在基于穿梭的硬件上操作。使用这个抽象，我们通过实现相对移动评分来加速基于SWAP的BidiRECtional启发式搜索（SABRE），该评分会缓存搜索期间出现的重复启发式移动评估，并记住拥堵解决方案，这会加速重复拥堵的解决。这种优化在不改变路由/穿梭决策的情况下消除了冗余计算，提高了TI-QCCD系统上基于SABER的方法的可扩展性。我们的结果表明，将架构感知抽象与记忆启发式评估相结合，可以产生一条实现跨异类量子架构的可扩展量子位映射和路由的实用有效途径。


### Abstract

Scalable qubit mapping and routing remain major bottlenecks in quantum compilation, especially for Trapped-Ion Quantum Charge-Coupled device (TI-QCCD) architectures, where qubit interactions require physically shuttling ions under strict movement, congestion, and trap-capacity constraints. We present a compilation framework built around the position graph abstraction, a unified representation of executable locations, movement paths, and routing constraints that enables heuristic mappers to operate directly on shuttling-based hardware. Using this abstraction, we accelerate the SWAP-based BidiREctional heuristic search (SABRE) by implementing relative move scoring, which caches repeated heuristic move evaluations that arise during search, and memoized congestion resolution, which speeds up the resolution of repeated congestion. This optimization removes redundant computation without changing routing/shuttling decisions, improving the scalability of SABRE-based methods on TI-QCCD systems. Our results show that combining an architecture-aware abstraction with memoized heuristic evaluation yields a practical and effective path toward scalable qubit mapping and routing across heterogeneous quantum architectures.

## （2+1）维Lifshitz型Dirac方程的有界状态谱

**Original Title:** Bound-State Spectra of a Lifshitz-Type Dirac Equation in (2+1) Dimensions

**Authors:** Lucas K. R. Queiroz, Van Sérgio Alves, Nilberto Bezerra, Luis Fernández, Francisco Peña

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.09249v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.09249v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-10


### 摘要

我们研究了一个由动态指数$z=2$的Lifshitz空间求导修改的（2+1）维Dirac型方程，重点研究了辐射约束下束缚态的光谱性质。对于恒定背景、硬壁限制和调和势获得分析解，而对log限制通过Numerov方法进行数值处理，并通过半经典WKB分析进行补充。所得光谱表现出由Lifshitz参数$b$决定的特征标度定律，包括硬壁约束的$E-M\propto b/R_0 ' 2 $，用于调和俘获的$E-M\propto \SQRT{2b}\、ω$，以及半经典半经典状态下的$E-M\sim a\lon\SQRT{b}$。这些结果为高级空间衍生物如何修改二维狄拉克系统中的束缚状态光谱提供了一致的描述，并且可能与具有二次低能量分散的材料（例如双层石墨烯和相关的各向异性2D系统）的有效描述相关。


### Abstract

We investigate a Dirac-type equation in (2+1) dimensions modified by Lifshitz spatial derivatives with dynamical exponent $z=2$, focusing on the spectral properties of bound states under radial confinement. Analytical solutions are obtained for constant backgrounds, hard-wall confinement, and harmonic potentials, while logarithmic confinement is treated numerically via the Numerov method and complemented by a semiclassical WKB analysis. The resulting spectra exhibit characteristic scaling laws governed by the Lifshitz parameter $b$, including $E - M \propto b/R_0^2$ for hard-wall confinement, $E - M \propto \sqrt{2b}\,ω$ for harmonic trapping, and $E - M \sim α\ln\sqrt{b}$ in the semiclassical regime of logarithmic confinement. These results provide a consistent characterization of how higher-order spatial derivatives modify bound-state spectra in two-dimensional Dirac systems and may be relevant for effective descriptions of materials with quadratic low-energy dispersion, such as bilayer graphene and related anisotropic 2D systems.

## 开关联格子中慢刘维扇形的微观共振壳机制

**Authors:** X. Z. Zhang

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.09274v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.09274v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-10


### 摘要

我们开发了一个微观理论，用于研究如何在开放相关格子中选择慢刘维部门。起点不是假设的非厄米特带，而是现场双胞胎和分支分辨的最近邻键之间的局部相互作用共振。这种共振定义了复合壳轨道，其双键重量控制储层可见性，其混合双键特征控制壳的移动性。将微观跳跃投射到选定的壳上，产生分支选择性二聚化通道。在稀释状态下，边界双损失通道通过Zeno型回归产生指数缓慢的边缘记忆极点。在壳临界点，边缘极点被具有代数相干间距的近零驻波双峰所取代。在有限壳填充时，同一局部壳变得密度修饰。数量保守的锁相跳跃消除了明亮的不匹配部分，留下缺陷作为渐进慢变量，并产生扩散的有限尺寸间隙。我们在一个舒尔投影框架内推导出局部壳层、投影分支布局、边缘记忆定律、壳临界双峰、密度修饰壳层Hamilton和缺陷生成器。由此产生的机制将火箭工程的快块识别为可观察到的慢区的选择器，而微观母壳保持固定。


### Abstract

We develop a microscopic theory for how slow Liouvillian sectors are selected in an open correlated lattice. The starting point is not a postulated non-Hermitian band, but a local interacting resonance between an on-site doublon and a branch-resolved nearest-neighbor bond. This resonance defines a composite shell orbital whose doublon weight controls reservoir visibility and whose mixed doublon-bond character controls shell mobility. Projecting the microscopic hopping onto the selected shell yields a branch-selective dimerized channel. In the dilute regime, a boundary doublon-loss channel yields an exponentially slow edge-memory pole through a Zeno-type return. At the shell-critical point, the edge pole is replaced by a near-zero standing-wave doublet with an algebraic coherent spacing. At finite shell filling, the same local shell becomes density dressed. A number-conserving phase-locking jump removes a bright mismatch sector, leaving defects as the asymptotic slow variables and producing a diffusive finite-size gap. We derive the local shell, the projected branch topology, the edge-memory law, the shell-critical doublet, the density-dressed shell Hamiltonian, and the defect generator within one Schur-projection framework. The resulting mechanism identifies the reservoir-engineered fast block as the selector of the observable slow sector, while the microscopic parent shell remains fixed.

## 神经信息因果关系

**Original Title:** Neural Information Causality

**Authors:** Jeongho Bang, Marcin Pawłowski

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.09316v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.09316v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-10


### 摘要

查询分离的计算迫使表示发挥操作作用：在查询已知之前对数据进行编码，并且稍后的解码器只能通过中间接口进行回答。在这种制度中，表示的功能是消息，而不仅仅是特征地图。我们通过将信息因果关系（IC）嵌入到表示学习中来形式化这一观察结果，获得一个称为神经信息因果关系（Neural-IC）的框架。修订后的公式区分了两个逻辑上不同的陈述。首先，每个查询分离的架构都会引发随机访问通信实验，并遵守嵌入不等式$I_{\mathrm{N\text{-}RAC}\le I（\vec a：H，B）$。其次，接口上绑定的任何独立认证的物理容量，例如硬$m$位字母表、有限精度寄存器或功率限制的有噪通道，都意味着$I_{\mathrm{N\text{-}RAC}\le C_H$。这种分离避免了将容量视为事后定义，并使Neural-IC成为查询泄漏、精度泄漏和特定剧集内存的操作诊断。我们还提供了一个精确的一位经典RAC基准，明确表明相关的量子增强不是超出瓶颈的全部信息，而是公平的查询条件访问。对于CHSH型相关层，嵌套的Neural-RAC协议在深度上乘以相关偏差;要求任意深度的一位瓶颈稳定性会选择Tsirelson阈值。我们将分析扩展到不对称种子偏差、多容量有限深度相图以及通过条件信息评分扩展到相关数据。受控模拟，包括直通式二进制瓶颈和故意泄露的消融，可以验证明显的违规是否是由查询分离中断或容量不足造成的。


### Abstract

Query-separated computation forces a representation to play an operational role: data are encoded before a query is known, and a later decoder can answer only through the intermediate interface. In this regime the representation functions as a message rather than merely as a feature map. We formalize this observation by embedding information causality (IC) into representation learning, obtaining a framework called neural information causality (Neural-IC). The revised formulation separates two logically distinct statements. First, every query-separated architecture induces a random-access communication experiment and obeys the embedding inequality $I_{\mathrm{N\text{-}RAC}}\le I(\vec a:H,B)$. Second, any independently certified physical capacity bound on the interface, such as a hard $m$-bit alphabet, a finite-precision register, or a power-constrained noisy channel, implies $I_{\mathrm{N\text{-}RAC}}\le C_H$. This separation avoids treating capacity as a post hoc definition and makes Neural-IC an operational diagnostic for query leakage, precision leakage, and episode-specific memory. We also provide an exact one-bit classical RAC benchmark, showing explicitly that the relevant quantum enhancement is not total information beyond the bottleneck, but fair query-conditioned access. For CHSH-type correlation layers, nested Neural-RAC protocols multiply correlation biases across depth; requiring stability of a one-bit bottleneck for arbitrary depth selects the Tsirelson threshold. We extend the analysis to asymmetric seed biases, to multi-capacity finite-depth phase diagrams, and to correlated data via a conditional information score. Controlled simulations, including straight-through binary bottlenecks and deliberately leaky ablations, verify that apparent violations are accounted for by broken query separation or undercounted capacity.

### 摘要

### Abstract

In this article, we introduce a coherence-based image distillation approach that separates object from noise by leveraging the difference in their temporal coherence properties. This overcomes the limitations of both intensity-based and quantum distillation methods. We further show that our method remains effective for fields with substantial spectral overlap, outperforming spectral filtering in regimes where the latter provides little noise suppression. This approach provides a robust framework for noise-resilient imaging with applications in optical communication, fluorescence microscopy, and biological imaging at both high and low light levels.

## Clifford量子电路综合的等变强化学习

**Original Title:** Equivariant Reinforcement Learning for Clifford Quantum Circuit Synthesis

**Authors:** Richie Yeung, Aleks Kissinger, Rob Cornish

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.10910v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.10910v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-11


### 摘要

我们考虑为具有全对全量子位连接性的设备合成Clifford量子电路的问题。我们将这项任务视为一个强化学习问题，其中代理学习发现一系列基本Clifford门，该序列将Clifford电路的给定辛矩阵表示简化为身份。这种公式允许基于身份随机游走的简单学习课程。我们引入了一种新型的神经网络架构，它与辛矩阵表示的量子位重新标记等变，并且是大小不可知的，允许在不同的量子位计数上应用单个学习策略，而无需电路拼接或网络重新参数化。在六量子位Clifford电路（可用最佳引用的最大机制）上，我们的代理在每个实例以毫秒为单位的一个两量子位最佳门内找到电路，并在每个实例的秒内在99.2%的实例中找到最佳电路。在对十个量子位实例进行持续训练后，代理扩展到具有多达三十个量子位的不可见Clifford画面，包括从具有超过一千个Clifford门的电路中生成的目标，在那里它实现的平均两个量子位门计数低于QisKit的Aaronson-Gottesman和贪婪的Clifford合成器。


### Abstract

We consider the problem of synthesizing Clifford quantum circuits for devices with all-to-all qubit connectivity. We approach this task as a reinforcement learning problem in which an agent learns to discover a sequence of elementary Clifford gates that reduces a given symplectic matrix representation of a Clifford circuit to the identity. This formulation permits a simple learning curriculum based on random walks from the identity. We introduce a novel neural network architecture that is equivariant to qubit relabelings of the symplectic matrix representation, and which is size-agnostic, allowing a single learned policy to be applied across different qubit counts without circuit splicing or network reparameterization. On six-qubit Clifford circuits, the largest regime for which optimal references are available, our agent finds circuits within one two-qubit gate of optimality in milliseconds per instance, and finds optimal circuits in 99.2% of instances within seconds per instance. After continued training on ten-qubit instances, the agent scales to unseen Clifford tableaus with up to thirty qubits, including targets generated from circuits with over a thousand Clifford gates, where it achieves lower average two-qubit gate counts than Qiskit's Aaronson-Gottesman and greedy Clifford synthesizers.

## 双物种里德堡阵列上的多量子位稳定器读出

**Original Title:** Multi-Qubit Stabilizer Readout on a Dual-Species Rydberg Array

**Authors:** Yu Wang, Ryan Cimmino, Kenneth Wang, Santiago Lopez, Jeffery Li, Jin Ming Koh, Jonathan N. Hallén, Anne Matthies, Norman Y. Yao, Kang-Kuen Ni

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.10924v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.10924v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-11


### 摘要

以高效且无串烧的方式本地控制和测量辅助量子位子集的能力是量子误差纠正（QEC）的关键要素。双物种中性原子阵列提供了这些能力的理想实现，能够对每个物种进行独立的状态准备、操纵和检测。在这项工作中，我们实现了这样一种双物种里德伯阵列，该阵列由捕获在共定域2D光镊子阵列中的钠和铯原子组成，使用钠作为辅助来测量周围Cs数据量子位的稳定剂。我们将有限的物种间里德伯格-里德伯格相互作用强度确定为高保真度多体纠缠的实际障碍，并表明，通过调节拉比频率和里德伯格驱动场的失调，可以补偿由此产生的几何相差。这产生了仅通过全局脉冲同时、非破坏性、现场稳定器读出多个数据量子位的协议。使用该协议，我们演示了通过单个全球里德伯脉冲序列对四量子位Cs斑块上的Pauli-Z稳定剂进行无损测量。我们的结果表明，双物种镊子阵列是实现可扩展QEC的一条有希望的途径，并为利用物种间和物种内相互作用的新量子控制协议打开了大门。


### Abstract

The ability to locally control and measure subsets of ancilla qubits in an efficient and crosstalk-free manner is a key ingredient in quantum error correction (QEC). Dual-species neutral atom arrays offer an ideal implementation of these capabilities, enabling independent state preparation, manipulation, and detection on each species. In this work, we realize such a dual-species Rydberg array of Na and Cs atoms trapped in co-localized 2D optical tweezer arrays, using Na as an ancilla to measure stabilizers of surrounding Cs data qubits. We identify the finite interspecies Rydberg-Rydberg interaction strength as a practical obstacle to high-fidelity multi-body entanglement and show that, by tuning the Rabi frequency and the detuning of the Rydberg driving field, the resulting geometric phase error can be compensated. This yields a protocol for simultaneous, non-destructive, in situ stabilizer readout of multiple data qubits via global pulses alone. Using this protocol, we demonstrate non-destructive measurement of Pauli-Z stabilizers on four-qubit Cs plaquettes via a single global Rydberg pulse sequence. Our results demonstrate dual-species tweezer arrays as a promising route towards scalable QEC and open the door to new quantum control protocols leveraging both interspecies and intraspecies interactions.

## 晶体对称性产生具有偏置擦除通道的音素全息门

**Original Title:** Crystallographic Symmetry Generates Phononic Holonomic Gates with Biased-Erasure Channels

**Authors:** El Mustapha Mansouri, Keigo Arai

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.10932v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.10932v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-11


### 摘要

固态处理器需要控制层，其错误对于量子错误纠正解码器来说是显而易见的。我们表明，晶体学对称性可以在应变活性Lambda多管中提供这样的层。当投影应变张量和Lambda转变操作符共享一个乘性为一的二维不可约表示时，对称性将线性应变相互作用固定为纯量点积。两个锁相机械模式合成圆形应变场，实现复杂的音素Lambda腿控制，而无需局部微波近场。在这个集合上，我们使用Lambda腿控制和共振双量子反热音构建了一个超隔热回声月全息门。氮空位中心的旋转框架模拟在1.833微秒内给出了99.88%的条件平均保真度，或者当泄漏算作误差时，给出了99.40%的条件平均保真度。共振千兆赫高泛音体声学共振器分析将Hamilton量转化为拉比速率、线宽和脉冲跟踪要求。明状态结构组织噪音：A2-扇形扰动被宇称过滤为光学可区分的辅助状态，而横向E-扇形故障被回声抑制并保留为解码器应力轴。提取的通道具有0.47%的擦除概率和0.168%的剩余Z误差。在XZZZ代码容量模拟中，相对于非结构化Rabi基线，这种偏置擦除模型产生了名义上64%的适合外推数据量子位减少。重复轮检测器模型诊断保留了名义距离9代理，并将错过的擦除、横向楼层、泄漏/标记定时和强串烧识别为验证限制。对轨道Lambda系统和亮投影仪音素总诊断的扩展将晶体学对称性确定为共同设计音素激励、泄漏、噪音偏置和量子解码的原则。


### Abstract

Solid-state processors require control layers whose errors are legible to quantum-error-correction decoders. We show that crystallographic symmetry can provide such a layer in strain-active Lambda manifolds. When the projected strain tensor and Lambda-transition operators share a multiplicity-one two-dimensional irreducible representation, symmetry fixes the linear strain interaction to a scalar dot product. Two phase-locked mechanical modes synthesize a circular strain field, enabling complex phononic Lambda-leg control without local microwave near fields. On this manifold we construct a superadiabatic echo-lune holonomic gate using Lambda-leg control and a resonant double-quantum counterdiabatic tone. Rotating-frame simulations of a nitrogen-vacancy center give 99.88% conditional average fidelity in 1.833 microseconds, or 99.40% when leakage is counted as error. A resonant gigahertz high-overtone bulk acoustic resonator analysis translates the Hamiltonian into Rabi-rate, linewidth, and envelope-tracking requirements. The bright-state structure organizes noise: A2-sector perturbations are parity-filtered into an optically distinguishable auxiliary state, whereas transverse E-sector faults are echo suppressed and retained as a decoder stress axis. The extracted channel has 0.47% erasure probability and 0.168% residual Z error. In XZZX code-capacity simulations, this biased-erasure model yields a nominal 64% fit-extrapolated data-qubit reduction relative to an unstructured Rabi baseline. Repeated-round detector-model diagnostics preserve the nominal distance-9 proxy and identify missed erasures, transverse floors, leakage/flag timing, and strong crosstalk as validation limits. Extensions to orbital Lambda systems and bright-projector phonon-bus diagnostics identify crystallographic symmetry as a principle for co-designing phononic actuation, leakage, noise bias, and quantum decoding.

## 三维被动自纠正量子存储器

**Original Title:** A passive self-correcting quantum memory in three dimensions

**Authors:** Shankar Balasubramanian, Margarita Davydova, Ting-Chun Lin

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.10943v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.10943v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-11


### 摘要

我们构建了一个3D泡利稳定器Hamilton，当与非零温度的浴相耦合时，其底价空间可以对指数时间的量子位进行编码。我们的构造将一系列变换迭代应用于种子Hamilton，这增加了编码量子位的存储寿命，同时保持$\mathbb{R}' 3 $中的几何局部性。


### Abstract

We construct a 3D Pauli stabilizer Hamiltonian whose ground state space can encode a qubit for exponential time when coupled to a bath at non-zero temperature. Our construction recursively applies a sequence of transformations to a seed Hamiltonian that increases the memory lifetime of the encoded qubit while maintaining geometric locality in $\mathbb{R}^3$.
