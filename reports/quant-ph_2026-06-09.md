## 能量调制的时间不对称自发坍缩：随机伊藤光量子和亮孤立子的前向-后向动力学

**Original Title:** Energy-Modulated Time-Asymmetric Spontaneous Collapse: Forward-Backward Dynamics from Stochastic Ito Reversal and Bright Solitons

**Authors:** Ikechukwu C. Okoro, Mike O. Osiele, Godfrey E. Akpojotor

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06452v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.06452v2

**Submitted:** 2026-06-04


### 摘要

我们提出了一个严格的理论框架，用于研究由五次非线性薛定格方程（CQ-NLSE）形式体系中随机伊藤场反转引起的对称性破缺和量子不可逆性。从三个物理动机考虑出发，通过伊藤演算推导出正向和反向非线性随机方程。运动学时间逆转被证明与伊藤随机结构从根本上不相容，从而产生了普遍不对称耦合参数2/3。引入了与噪音强度、局部概率密度和激发能量平方的积成正比的能量驱动的坍缩操作符，放大了高密度、高激发区域中的坍缩。对于具有吸引力的Li-7原子的准一维BEC，获得了精确的亮孤立子解，其前向和后向幅度比为1.870。对参数平面的热图分析表明，向前塌陷操作符随时间单调增长，而向后对应的操作符则衰变，达到约1030的比率，这使得该框架与传统的对称塌陷模型有鲜明的区别。


### Abstract

We present a rigorous theoretical framework for symmetry breaking and quantum irreversibility arising from stochastic Ito field reversal within a cubic-quintic nonlinear Schrodinger equation (CQ-NLSE) formalism. Starting from three physically motivated considerations, forward and backward nonlinear stochastic differential equations are derived via the Ito calculus. Kinematic time-reversal is shown to be fundamentally incompatible with the Ito stochastic structure, yielding the universal asymmetry-coupling parameter of 2/3. An energy-driven collapse operator proportional to the product of noise strength, local probability density, and excitation energy squared is introduced, amplifying the collapse in high-density, high-excitation regions. Exactly bright soliton solutions are obtained for a quasi-one-dimensional BEC of attractive Li-7 atoms, with forward and backward amplitude ratio of 1.870. Heat map analysis of the parameter planes reveals that the forward collapse operator grows monotonically in time while the backward counterpart decays, achieving a ratio approximately 1030, sharply distinguishing this framework from conventional symmetric collapse models.

## 非马尔科夫TLR噪音下耦合氟量子比特中的相关辅助奇宇称编码门

**Original Title:** Correlation-Assisted Odd-Parity Encoded Gates in Coupled Fluxonium Qubits under Non-Markovian TLS Noise

**Authors:** Chenghong Ji, Chaoying Zhao

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07699v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.07699v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-05


### 摘要

相关纵向噪音可以部分转化为奇宇称两量子位子空间中的共同模式波动。我们分析了由两个耦合的氟龙量子位中的状态形成的编码逻辑量子位。将交换耦合的两量子位Hamilton量投影到这个子空间上会产生一个有效的逻辑Hamilton量，其中交换相互作用驱动XL旋转，量子位失调驱动ZR旋转。我们通过使用具有有限存储时间的纵向随机过程来对相关两级系统（TLR）噪音进行建模，并通过平均门保真度来评估编码门性能。在投影模型中，正空间噪音相关性抑制了差异波动，从而提高了编码逻辑门的保真度。我们进一步比较高斯奥恩斯坦-乌伦贝克、马尔科夫和随机电报噪音模型，并研究逻辑动态脱钩的作用。这些结果为耦合氟氯铵设备中的奇宇称编码操作确定了一种自适应的噪音控制机制，并激励未来的多层模拟，包括泄漏和脉冲级约束。


### Abstract

Correlated longitudinal noise can be partially converted into common-mode fluctuations in an oddparity two-qubit subspace. We analyze an encoded logical qubit formed by the states in two coupled fluxonium qubits. Projecting the exchange-coupled two-qubit Hamiltonian onto this subspace yields an effective logical Hamiltonian in which the exchange interaction drives XL rotations and the qubit detuning drives ZL rotations. We model correlated two-levelsystem (TLS) noise by using longitudinal stochastic processes with finite memory time and evaluate encoded-gate performance through the average gate fidelity. Within the projected model, positive spatial noise correlation suppresses the differential fluctuation and thereby improves the fidelity of encoded logical gates. We further compare Gaussian Ornstein-Uhlenbeck, Markovian, and randomtelegraph noise models and examine the role of logical dynamical decoupling. These results identify a noise-adapted control mechanism for odd-parity encoded operations in coupled fluxonium devices and motivate future multilevel simulations including leakage and pulse-level constraints.

## 非埃尔米特物理合成光格子中异常Floquet角响应的复规范控制

**Original Title:** Complex-gauge control of anomalous Floquet corner responses in a non-Hermitian physical-synthetic photonic lattice

**Authors:** W. C. Ning, X. Z. Zhang

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07038v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.07038v2

**Submitted:** 2026-06-05


### 摘要

我们提出了一种由物理共振器坐标和合成频率坐标形成的非厄米特Floquet光格子。两步调制协议在这个物理合成平面中实现了手征行走，其中实合成通量控制环路干涉，虚规范场控制非可逆包线。我们表明，准能为零和\（pi/T\）的异常角对表现出三个不同的物理层。非布洛赫高级结构预测开放边界下是否存在\（0/pi\）角对。虚规范场选择正确本征模的累积位置。真实通量控制局部干涉矩阵元素，该元素确定双周期光学响应是否可见。因此，在局部光学测量中，相同的拓扑共存区可以是明亮的、肤色暗的或通量暗的。我们进一步表明，复规范可以调节两周期角传播子的异常点。此时，异常响应保持其双周期符号交替，但由于乔丹块，其信封变成了代数的。这些结果提供了一条光途径来分离非埃尔米特合成维度中的拓扑存在、皮肤选择定位、光学可见性和有缺陷的两周期动力学。


### Abstract

We propose a non-Hermitian Floquet photonic lattice formed by a physical resonator coordinate and a synthetic frequency coordinate. A two-step modulation protocol realizes a chiral walk in this physical-synthetic plane, with a real synthetic flux controlling loop interference and imaginary gauge fields controlling non-reciprocal envelopes. We show that anomalous corner pairs at quasienergies zero and \(π/T\) exhibit three distinct layers of physics. A non-Bloch higher-order construction predicts whether the \(0/π\) corner pair exists under open boundaries. The imaginary gauge fields select where the right eigenmodes accumulate. The real flux controls the local interference matrix element that determines whether the doubled-period optical response is visible. As a result, the same topological coexistence sector can be bright, skin-dark, or flux-dark in a local optical measurement. We further show that the complex gauge can tune an exceptional point of the two-period corner propagator. At this point the anomalous response keeps its doubled-period sign alternation, but its envelope becomes algebraic because of a Jordan block. These results provide a photonic route to separate topological existence, skin-selected localization, optical visibility, and defective two-period dynamics in a non-Hermitian synthetic dimension.

## CVaR投资组合优化的量子数学弹性基准：表达性-一致性权衡

**Original Title:** Benchmarking Quantum Algorithmic Resilience for CVaR Portfolio Optimization: The Expressibility-Coherence Trade-off

**Authors:** Prashik N. Somkuwar, K. Srinivasan, G. Raghavan

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07727v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.07727v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-05


### 摘要

量子组合优化为复杂的金融建模提供了理论优势，但有噪音的中间规模量子（NISQ）设备上的物理实现受到硬件布局的严重限制。本研究针对混合平均方差和条件风险价值（CVaR）投资组合目标，提出了硬件高效变分量子神经网络（HE-VQNN）和热启动量子近似优化算法（WS-QAOA）之间的硬件基准分析。通过实现一种新颖的经典量子混合代理矩阵来绕过CVaR辅助量子位瓶颈，我们将NIFTY 50索引中的多达16个资产映射到IBM重型十六进制处理器上。我们系统地量化算法对路由过程中产生的“SWAP税”的弹性。经验结果揭示了一个关键的操作权衡：WS-QAOA提供了精确的理论映射，但由于指数级非本地门开销而遭受灾难性的硬件去相关性。相反，HE-VQNN保留了硬件的一致性，但缺乏捕捉密集尾部风险资产相关性的数学表达能力。这项研究暴露了当前架构上密集财务优化的局限性，迫使在算法不可表达性和硬件去相关之间做出不可行的选择。这表明，对于缺乏全对全连接性的NISQ计算机可以做什么和不能做什么存在更深层次的限制。


### Abstract

Quantum combinatorial optimization offers theoretical advantages for complex financial modeling, but physical implementation on Noisy Intermediate Scale Quantum (NISQ) devices is severely constrained by hardware topology. This study presents a hardware benchmarking analysis between a Hardware Efficient Variational Quantum Neural Network (HE-VQNN) and the Warm Start Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm (WS-QAOA) for a hybrid Mean Variance and Conditional Value at Risk (CVaR) portfolio objective. By implementing a novel classical quantum hybrid proxy matrix to bypass the CVaR auxiliary qubit bottleneck, we map up to 16 assets from the NIFTY 50 index onto an IBM heavy hex processor. We systematically quantify algorithmic resilience to the "SWAP tax" incurred during routing. Empirical results reveal a critical operational trade-off: WS-QAOA provides exact theoretical mapping but suffers catastrophic hardware decoherence due to exponential nonlocal gate overhead. Conversely, HE-VQNN preserves hardware coherence but lacks the mathematical expressibility to capture dense tail risk asset correlations. This study exposes the limitations of dense financial optimization on current architectures forces an nonviable choice between algorithmic inexpressibility and hardware decoherence. This is indicative of a deeper limitation as to what can and cannot be done with NISQ computers lacking in all-to-all connectivity.

## 探索紧凑的魔法状态蒸馏工厂的景观

**Original Title:** Exploring the landscape of compact magic-state distillation factories

**Authors:** Hugo Jacinto, Xavier Valcarce, Victor Barizien, Élie Gouzien, Nicolas Sangouard

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07734v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.07734v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-05


### 摘要

使用尽可能少的物理量子位和操作产生高保真幻境是大规模实现容错量子计算的一个非常重要的挑战。除了培养等替代方法的新提案外，魔状态蒸馏对于实现极低的错误率仍然至关重要。已知的蒸馏协议通常是通过从三正交矩阵推导出的量子码构建的。在这里，我们利用魔状态蒸馏协议中存在的特定噪音结构，表明经典的错误纠正码为推导这些协议提供了一个更简单的框架。该公式特别适合涉及固定数量量子位的蒸馏协议的系统性数值和分析研究。具体来说，我们使用SAT求解器来推导出一系列与关键品质因子相关的不去定理，包括量子位数量、协议深度、工厂距离和输出错误率的预因子。例如，我们证明了任何使用少于八个量子位的$T$-to-$T$状态蒸馏协议最多可以检测三个错误，而任何使用少于八个量子位的$T$-to-$\mathrm{CC}Z$状态蒸馏协议最多可以检测两个错误。我们的结果还包括文献中对于给定距离具有最少量子位数的新蒸馏协议，即支持10和11个量子位的距离4和5 $T$-to-$T$状态协议，以及支持9和10个量子位的距离3和4 $T$-to-$\mathrm{CC}Z$状态蒸馏协议。


### Abstract

Producing high-fidelity magic states using the smallest possible amount of physical qubits and operations stands as a very important challenge to achieve fault-tolerant quantum computation at scale. Besides emerging proposals for alternative methods such as cultivation, magic state distillation remains essential for achieving very low error rates. Known distillation protocols are usually built through quantum codes derived from triorthogonal matrices. Here, exploiting the specific noise structure present in magic state distillation protocols, we show that classical error-correcting codes offer a simpler framework for deriving these protocols. This formulation is particularly well suited to systematic numerical and analytical studies of distillation protocols involving a fixed number of qubits. Specifically, we use a SAT solver to derive a series of no-go theorems that relate key figures of merit, including the number of qubits, the protocol depth, the factory distance, and the prefactor in the output error rate. For instance, we prove that any $T$-to-$T$ state distillation protocol using fewer than eight qubits can detect at most three errors, while any $T$-to-$\mathrm{CC}Z$ state distillation protocol using fewer than eight qubits can detect at most two errors. Our results also include new distillation protocols with the smallest number of qubits for a given distance in the literature, namely distance 4 and 5 $T$-to-$T$ state protocols supported on 10 and 11 qubits, as well as distance 3 and 4 $T$-to-$\mathrm{CC}Z$ state distillation protocols supported on 9 and 10 qubits.

## 一类驱动消耗量子多体系统的精确亚稳定性

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07736v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.07736v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-05


### 摘要

多体量子系统的亚稳定性及其相关的指数长时间尺度一直是最近相当感兴趣的主题。在这里，我们重点关注一类由林德布拉德学派描述的具有隐藏的时间逆转对称性（量子详细平衡的一种形式）的驱动消散多体开放量子系统。例子包括边界驱动的相互作用旋转链、玻色子格子模型和驱动消散集体旋转模型。我们认为，对于此类系统，可以使用非平衡稳态的特殊净化来分析预测消散一级相转变附近的慢时间尺度。我们通过对具有集体和局部衰变的消散横场伊辛模型以及驱动消散非线性腔模型的详细研究来证明我们猜想的准确性。我们的结果允许对一系列系统的亚稳定性和慢动力学进行定量见解，包括半经典或路径积分瞬间子方法难以处理的情况。


### Abstract

Metastability in many-body quantum systems and its associated exponentially-long timescales have been the subject of considerable recent interest. Here, we focus on a class of driven-dissipative many-body open quantum systems described by a Lindbladian having hidden time-reversal symmetry (a form of quantum detailed balance). Examples include boundary-driven interacting spin chains, bosonic lattice models and driven-dissipative collective spin models. We suggest that for such systems, slow timescales in the vicinity of a dissipative first-order phase transition can be analytically predicted using a special purification of the non-equilibrium steady state. We show the accuracy of our conjecture through detailed studies of a dissipative transverse-field Ising model with collective and local decay, and a driven-dissipative nonlinear cavity model. Our results allow quantitative insights into metastability and slow dynamics for a range of systems, including cases where semiclassical or path-integral instanton approaches are intractable.

## $100/PM £ t$多年的量子不确定性：从起源到现代洞察

**Original Title:** $100\pmΔt$ Years of Quantum Uncertainty: From Origins to Modern Insights

**Authors:** Lorcan O. Conlon, Biveen Shajilal, Jie Zhao, Tim C. Ralph, Gerd Leuchs, Ulrik L. Andersen, Syed M. Assad, Ping Koy Lam, Yunlong Xiao

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07747v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.07747v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-05


### 摘要

海森堡的不确定原理是量子力学的基石，标志着对经典物理学的决定性背离。它是在近一个世纪前通过一项思想实验构思的，表明测量电子的位置不可避免地会扰乱其动量，它最初是一个看似简单的想法，引发了无数的研究，并发展成为今天丰富的研究领域。本文回顾了其发展到一系列数学公式（称为不确定性关系），并探讨了它们的相互关联和广泛应用。我们强调了它在量子计量学中的核心作用，它支撑着以不断提高的精确度从量子系统中提取信息的策略，以及它与多参数估计和压缩状态的联系。这篇评论致力于纪念不确定性原理一百周年，反思了它如何加深了我们对量子理论的理解并推动了实践进步，并展望了一个世纪，即将迎来进一步令人惊讶和变革性的发现。


### Abstract

Heisenberg's uncertainty principle is a cornerstone of quantum mechanics, marking a decisive departure from classical physics. Conceived almost a century ago through a thought experiment showing that measuring an electron's position inevitably disturbs its momentum, it began as a deceptively simple idea that sparked countless studies and grew into the rich research field it is today. This review traces its development into a spectrum of mathematical formulations -- known as uncertainty relations -- and explores their interconnections and wide-ranging applications. We highlight its central role in quantum metrology, where it underpins strategies for extracting information from quantum systems with ever-increasing precision, and its links to multiparameter estimation and squeezed states. This review, dedicated to the centenary of the uncertainty principle, reflects on how it has deepened our understanding of quantum theory and driven practical advances, and looks ahead to a century poised for further surprising and transformative discoveries.

## 通过半导体异质结中的电荷输运探测激子凝聚

**Original Title:** Detecting Exciton Condensation through Charge Transport in Semiconductor Heterostructures

**Authors:** Caterina Zerba, Léo Mangeolle, Michael Knap

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07752v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.07752v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-05


### 摘要

半导体杂结构中激子凝聚的直接证据仍然难以捉摸。在这里，我们提出了掺杂子的电荷输运作为过渡金属二硫属化合物杂结构中激子凝聚的探针，并确定了不同的实验特征。首先，凝聚抑制了载体散射的相空间，导致电阻率降低，从而提供了激子凝聚的一般诊断。其次，在具有可调固体费什巴赫共振的异结构中，掺杂子和三子束缚态之间的凝聚物诱导的杂交定性地改变了输运。特别是，在接近共振时，这种杂交产生负的有效质量和霍尔电阻率的相应符号反转。这些结果确立了电荷输运作为检测和描述半导体杂结构中激子凝聚的有希望的途径。


### Abstract

Direct evidence of exciton condensation in semiconductor heterostructures remains elusive. Here we propose charge transport of doped carriers as a probe of exciton condensation in transition-metal dichalcogenide heterostructures and identify distinct experimental signatures. First, condensation suppresses the phase space for carrier scattering, leading to a reduction in resistivity, that provides a general diagnostic of exciton condensation. Second, in heterostructures with a tunable solid-state Feshbach resonance, condensate-induced hybridization between doped carriers and trion bound states qualitatively modifies transport. In particular, near resonance, this hybridization yields a negative effective mass and a corresponding sign reversal of the Hall resistivity. These results establish charge transport as a promising route for detecting and characterizing exciton condensation in semiconductor heterostructures.

## 参数驱动耦合超导量子位中Floquet纠缠的产生

**Original Title:** Floquet Entanglement Generation in Parametrically Driven Coupled Superconducting Qubits

**Authors:** Gustavo M. Meneses A., Daniel Dominguez, María José Sánchez

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07797v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.07797v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-05


### 摘要

我们研究了通过参数驱动的纵向相互作用耦合的两个超导量子位系统中纠缠的动态产生。使用Floquet理论和精确的数值模拟，我们分析了在可分离的基状态下初始化的系统的时间演变。我们的结果揭示了纠缠产生的一种非平凡机制，从根本上不同于传统的纠缠本征态的共振激发。我们表明，当两个最初可分离的本征态在多子共振条件下通过周期驱动混合时，这种机制就会出现。由于该效应无法在标准的回旋波逼近中捕捉，因此我们采用广义范弗莱克近简并微扰理论来推导有效的分析描述。在这个框架内，我们证明了持续纠缠源于主要Floquet状态的杂交，即与初始底态重叠最大的那些状态。此外，通过驱动幅度可以有效地控制纠缠度。特别是，对于特定的幅度，纠缠被完全抑制。我们将这种现象称为纠缠的连贯破坏。


### Abstract

We investigate the dynamical generation of entanglement in a system of two superconducting qubits coupled through a parametrically driven longitudinal interaction. Using Floquet theory and exact numerical simulations, we analyze the time evolution of the system initialized in a separable ground state. Our results reveal a nontrivial mechanism for entanglement generation, fundamentally distinct from the conventional resonant excitation to an entangled eigenstate. We show that this mechanism emerges when two initially separable eigenstates are mixed by the periodic driving under multiphoton resonance conditions. Since the effect cannot be captured within a standard rotating-wave approximation, we employ generalized Van Vleck near-degenerate perturbation theory to derive an effective analytical description. Within this framework, we demonstrate that the sustained entanglement originates from the hybridization of the dominant Floquet states, namely those with the largest overlap with the initial ground state. Furthermore, the degree of entanglement can be efficiently controlled through the driving amplitude. In particular, for specific amplitudes, the entanglement is fully suppressed. We term this phenomenon as coherent destruction of entanglement.
