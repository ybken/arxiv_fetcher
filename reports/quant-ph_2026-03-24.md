## 非埃尔米特Su-Schrieffer-Heeger链中的竞争集肤效应和准周期局部化：重入离域、谱布局破坏和纠缠抑制

**Original Title:** Competing skin effect and quasiperiodic localization in the non-Hermitian Su-Schrieffer-Heeger chain: Reentrant delocalization, spectral topology destruction, and entanglement suppression

**Authors:** Souvik Ghosh

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.20330v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.20330v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-20


### 摘要

我们研究了具有非互惠跳跃的一维Su-Schrieffer-Heeger（SSH）链中非厄米特皮肤效应和Aubry-André-Harper（AAH）准周期性疾病之间的相互作用。通过精确对角化、传递矩阵分析和分析相似变换论点，我们绘制了完整的（，$δ$）相图，其中A是AAH调制强度和非可逆参数。我们确定了五种不同的状态：（）具有扩展体积的topology，（II）AAH局部化，（III）皮肤局部化，（IV）完全局部化，以及之前未报告的（V）表现出再入部分离域的竞争状态，其中中间准周期性失调在最终安德森局部化之前破坏了定向皮肤积累。使用相平均诊断和有限大小缩放，我们确认可重入机制是鲁棒的，其特征是非单调逆参与比随着系统大小的增加而加剧。我们推导出修改后的局部化边界的解析式$_{c}（δ）= 2\SQRT {v_{eff}w}$，其中$v_{vff}=\SQRT{v^{2}-δ^{2}}$，这与数值李雅普诺夫指数计算一致。我们进一步表明，准周期无序逐渐解开复杂的谱环，以与能带-拓扑转变不同的临界强度破坏点间隙拓扑;集肤效应将纠缠论抑制到接近零的值，而足够强的AAH无序则部分恢复它;并且广泛研究的非埃尔米特AAH链中所缺乏的SH亚格子结构对于产生五相景观至关重要，如与非二聚化限度直接比较所证明的。


### Abstract

We investigate the interplay between the non-Hermitian skin effect and Aubry-André-Harper (AAH) quasiperiodic disorder in a one-dimensional Su-Schrieffer-Heeger (SSH) chain with nonreciprocal hopping. By exact diagonalization, transfer-matrix analysis, and an analytical similarity-transformation argument, we map the full ( , $δ$) phase diagram, where A is the AAH modulation strength and the nonreciprocity parameter. We identify five distinct regimes: ( ) topological with extended bulk, (II) AAH-localized, (III) skin-localized, (IV) fully localized, and a previously unreported (V) competition regime exhibiting reentrant partial delocalization, in which intermediate quasiperiodic disorder disrupts the directional skin accumulation before ultimately Anderson-localizing all states. Using phase-averaged diagnostics and finite-size scaling, we confirm that the reentrant regime is robust, characterized by a non-monotonic inverse participation ratio that sharpens with increasing system size. We derive an analytical expression for the modified localization boundary $λ_{c}(δ)=2\sqrt{v_{eff}w}$ with $v_{vff}=\sqrt{v^{2}-δ^{2}}$, which agrees with numerical Lyapunov exponent calculations. We further show that quasiperiodic disorder progressively unwinds the complex spectral loops, destroying the point-gap topology at a critical strength distinct from the band-topological transition ; that the skin effect suppresses entanglement entropy to near-zero values while sufficiently strong AAH disorder partially restores it ; and that the SSH sublattice structure absent in the widely studied non-Hermitian AAH chain is essential for producing the five-phase landscape, as demonstrated by direct comparison with the non-dimerized limit.

## 精化Bessis-Moussa-Villani猜想的单参数反例

**Original Title:** One-parameter counterexamples to the refined Bessis-Moussa-Villani conjecture

**Authors:** Hyunho Cha

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.19927v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.19927v2

**Submitted:** 2026-03-20


### 摘要

贝西-穆萨-维拉尼（BMV）猜想起源于量子统计力学，在利布和塞林格进行了有影响力的重新阐述后，由斯塔尔证明。稍后的细化询问具有$n$字母$A$和$m$字母$B$的所有单词的规范化平均值是否始终以$\mathrm{TR}（A ' nB ' m）$为上限，以$\mathrm{TR}\BEP（n\log A+m\log B）$为上限。我们研究一个特定的单参数族$（A_x，B_x）$，并推导出当$n=m=5$时第一个不等式两侧的精确封闭公式。特别是，$x=10^{-3}$给出了一个反例，值得注意的是，规范化的字平均值与轨迹$\mathrm{TR}（A ' nB ' m）$的比率可以变得任意大。


### Abstract

The Bessis-Moussa-Villani (BMV) conjecture, originating in quantum statistical mechanics, was proved by Stahl after an influential reformulation by Lieb and Seiringer. A later refinement asks whether the normalized average over all words with $n$ letters $A$ and $m$ letters $B$ is always bounded above by $\mathrm{tr}(A^nB^m)$ and below by $\mathrm{tr}\exp(n\log A+m\log B)$. We study a specific one-parameter family $(A_x, B_x)$ and derive exact closed formulas for both sides of the first inequality when $n=m=5$. In particular, $x=10^{-3}$ gives a counterexample and, remarkably, the ratio of the normalized word average to the trace $\mathrm{tr}(A^nB^m)$ can become arbitrarily large.

## 超深量子通道中的误差纠正转变

**Original Title:** Error-Correction Transitions in Finite-Depth Quantum Channels

**Authors:** Arman Sauliere, Guglielmo Lami, Pedro Ribeiro, Andrea De Luca, Jacopo De Nardis

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.20369v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.20369v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-20


### 摘要

我们研究了错误纠正型协议，其中量子通道将逻辑信息编码到扩大的希尔伯特空间中。具体来说，我们考虑由具有空间局部相互作用门的一维随机有噪量子电路实现的通道。我们分析了编码后作用的噪音和影响编码电路本身的噪音。使用相关信息作为衡量标准，我们表明，在这两种情况下，无限深度极限都由随机矩阵理论决定，该理论预测了临界噪音率下的普适相转变。这个临界点将保存编码信息的错误纠正阶段与编码信息不可挽回地丢失的阶段分开。超越无限深度限制，我们描述了与随机矩阵普适性的系统有限深度偏差。特别是，我们表明，这些偏差的表现在参数上有所不同，具体取决于噪音是在编码后起作用还是影响编码本身。对于无噪编码器，该方法在电路深度上呈指数级，尽管边界效应可能会相对于电路设计时间延迟完美编码。对于有噪的编码器，我们发现电路保真度有效地取代了哈希界限，并且完美编码是通过深度进行的。


### Abstract

We study error correction type protocols in which a quantum channel encodes logical information into an enlarged Hilbert space. Specifically, we consider channels realized by one dimensional random noisy quantum circuits with spatially local interaction gates. We analyze both noise acting after the encoding and noise affecting the encoding circuit itself. Using the coherent information as a metric, we show that in both cases the infinite depth limit is governed by random matrix theory, which predicts a universal phase transition at a critical noise rate. This critical point separates an error correcting phase, in which encoded information is preserved, from a phase in which it is irretrievably lost. Going beyond the infinite depth limit, we characterize the systematic finite depth deviations from random matrix universality. In particular, we show that these deviations behave parametrically differently depending on whether the noise acts after the encoding or also affects the encoding itself. For noiseless encoders, the approach is exponential in circuit depth, although boundary effects can delay perfect encoding relative to the circuit design time. For noisy encoders, we find that the circuit fidelity effectively replaces the Hashing bound, and perfect encoding is approached polynomially with depth.

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.20372v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-20


### 摘要

由于量子涨落增强，低维材料表现出奇异的性质，这使得对其微观起源的理解成为凝聚物质物理学的核心。模拟量子模拟器为在微观水平上研究这些系统提供了一种强大的方法，特别是在量子纠缠限制经典数值方法的大规模体系中。到目前为止，模拟模拟器主要关注普遍Hamilton主义，而不是特定材料的定量比较。在这里，我们使用基于里德伯格的量子模拟器来研究体层挫败量子磁铁TmMGGaO$_4$。从量子模拟器获得的磁化强度测量结果与在磁性实验室设施中进行的独立测量结果非常一致，验证了所提出的有效的二维微观哈密顿量。建立在这个定量对应关系，我们调查这两个平台上的反铁磁相变。我们通过快照分析进一步探索量子涨落的作用，将我们的结果与综合非弹性中子散射数据联系起来。最后，我们访问，与模拟器，皮秒材料时间尺度上的非平衡动力学，包括频率响应和热化的观测。


### Abstract

Low-dimensional materials exhibit exotic properties due to enhanced quantum fluctuations, making the understanding of their microscopic origin central in condensed matter physics. Analogue quantum simulators offer a powerful approach for investigating these systems at the microscopic level, particularly in large-scale regimes where quantum entanglement limits classical numerical methods. To date, analogue simulators have largely focused on universal Hamiltonians rather than material-specific quantitative comparisons. Here we use a Rydberg-based quantum simulator to study the bulk-layered frustrated quantum magnet TmMgGaO$_4$. Magnetisation measurements obtained from the quantum simulator show excellent agreement with independent measurements performed in a magnetic laboratory facility, validating the proposed effective two-dimensional microscopic Hamiltonian. Building on this quantitative correspondence, we investigate on both platforms the antiferromagnetic phase transition. We further probe the role of quantum fluctuations via snapshot analysis, connecting our results to integrated inelastic neutron scattering data. Finally, we access, with the simulator, non-equilibrium dynamics on picosecond material timescales, including frequency response and thermalisation of observables.

## 具有标志分解的参数最优么正综合

**Original Title:** Parameter-optimal unitary synthesis with flag decompositions

**Authors:** Korbinian Kottmann, David Wierichs, Guillermo Alonso-Linaje, Nathan Killoran

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.20376v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.20376v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-20


### 摘要

我们引入标志分解作为酉合成的核心工具。它让我们以这样一种方式划分出一个具有2^n $自由度的对角酉式，即剩余的标志电路由4^n-2^n$旋转的最佳数量参数化。这使我们能够产生参数优化的量子电路的通用幺正和矩阵产品状态的准备。我们的方法改进了现有技术，无论是在编译到{Clifford + Rot}门集时，我们称之为选择性解复用，还是在使用相位梯度资源状态与量子算术一起实现复用旋转时。我们所有的合成方法都可以通过标准线性代数例程实现的循环Cartan分解来有效实现，使它们适用于所有实际相关的系统大小。


### Abstract

We introduce the flag decomposition as a central tool for unitary synthesis. It lets us carve out a diagonal unitary with $2^n$ degrees of freedom in such a way that the remaining flag circuit is parametrized by the optimal number of $4^n-2^n$ rotations. This enables us to produce parameter-optimal quantum circuits for generic unitaries and matrix product state preparation. Our approach improves upon the state of the art, both when compiling down to the {Clifford + Rot} gate set with what we call selective de-multiplexing, and when using phase gradient resource states together with quantum arithmetic to implement multiplexed rotations. All of our synthesis methods are efficiently implementable in terms of recursive Cartan decompositions realized by standard linear algebra routines, making them applicable to all practically relevant system sizes.

## 非厄米特式无序系统

**Original Title:** Non-Hermitian Disordered Systems

**Authors:** Kohei Kawabata, Shinsei Ryu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.20393v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.20393v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-20


### 摘要

非埃尔米特无序系统已成为现代物理学的中心舞台，其影响跨越凝聚物质、量子、统计和高能背景。同样的原则也是物理学以外的现象的基础，例如网络科学、复杂系统和生物物理学，其中消散、非互惠和随机性无处不在。在这里，我们回顾了非埃尔米特无序系统的物理和数学，特别强调非埃尔米特随机矩阵理论。我们首先提出非埃尔米特系统的38重对称分类，并将其与埃尔米特系统的10重对称分类进行对比。在介绍了非厄米特随机矩阵的经典吉尼布尔集合后，我们研究了复谱统计量的各种诊断方法和通过对称性实现的不同普适性类。作为物理学的关键应用，我们讨论了非厄米随机矩阵理论如何描述开放量子系统中的混乱和可积性。然后，我们转向由于无序和非厄米性相互作用而产生的临界性，包括波野-尼尔森模型中的安德森转变及其更高维度的扩展。我们还讨论了非线性西格玛模型对非厄米特无序系统的有效场论描述。


### Abstract

Non-Hermitian disordered systems have emerged as a central arena in modern physics, with ramifications spanning condensed matter, quantum, statistical, and high energy contexts. The same principles also underlie phenomena beyond physics, such as network science, complex systems, and biophysics, where dissipation, nonreciprocity, and stochasticity are ubiquitous. Here, we review the physics and mathematics of non-Hermitian disordered systems, with particular emphasis on non-Hermitian random matrix theory. We begin by presenting the 38-fold symmetry classification of non-Hermitian systems, contrasting it with the 10-fold way for Hermitian systems. After introducing the classic Ginibre ensembles of non-Hermitian random matrices, we survey various diagnostics for complex-spectral statistics and distinct universality classes realized by symmetry. As a key application to physics, we discuss how non-Hermitian random matrix theory characterizes chaos and integrability in open quantum systems. We then turn to the criticality due to the interplay of disorder and non-Hermiticity, including Anderson transitions in the Hatano-Nelson model and its higher-dimensional extensions. We also discuss the effective field theory description of non-Hermitian disordered systems in terms of nonlinear sigma models.

## 量子场论能从时间对称随机力学中脱离出来吗？第一部分：推广刘维尔方程

**Original Title:** Can Quantum Field Theory be Recovered from Time-Symmetric Stochastic Mechanics? Part I: Generalizing the Liouville Equation

**Authors:** Simon Friederich, Mritunjay Tyagi

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.20399v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.20399v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-20


### 摘要

我们探索量子场论是否可以被理解为汉密尔顿动力学的时间逆转不变随机概括的统计力学。该项目的动机始于本文，是为所有可观测量赋予尖锐的值，从而避免量子测量问题。在经典力学中，运动是确定性的，并且对应于根据刘维尔方程的相空间概率密度的演变，该方程由相空间中汉密尔顿量的一阶求导决定。我们推导出具有自然约束的刘维尔方程的推广--即，简化为经典Hamilton动力学作为随机性参数$\hbar\Mapsto 0 $、概率密度演变的福克-普朗克形式、局部Hamilton依赖性、时间逆转不变性、能量保存和极小性--这是一个具有广义扩散矩阵的福克-普朗克方程，该矩阵是对称的、无痕迹的，并由汉密尔顿的黑森人建造。然后，我们表明，某些玻色子QFT的相干状态相空间公式中的薛定格方程正是这种形式，其中胡西米函数扮演相空间概率密度的角色。在另一篇论文中讨论了这个方程可以在多大程度上用客观随机场理论来解释的问题。


### Abstract

We explore whether quantum field theory can be understood as the statistical mechanics of a time-reversal-invariant stochastic generalization of Hamiltonian dynamics. The motivation for this project, started with this paper, is to assign sharp values to all observables and thereby avoid the quantum measurement problem. In classical mechanics, motion is deterministic and corresponds to an evolution of the phase space probability density according to Liouville's equation that is governed by first derivatives of the Hamiltonian in phase space. We derive a generalization of the Liouville equation with natural constraints -- namely, reduction to classical Hamiltonian dynamics as the stochasticity parameter $\hbar\mapsto0$, Fokker-Planck form for the probability density evolution, local Hamiltonian dependence, time-reversal invariance, energy conservation, and minimality -- which turns out to be a Fokker-Planck equation with a generalized diffusion matrix that is symmetric, traceless, and constructed from the Hessian of the Hamiltonian. We then show that the Schrödinger equation in the coherent-state phase-space formulation of certain bosonic QFTs has precisely this form, with the Husimi function playing the role of the phase space probability density. The question to what extent this equation can be interpreted in terms of objective stochastic field theories is discussed in a companion paper.

## 有噪随机电路和Lindbladian动力学中噪音诱导的MPO截断误差压缩

**Original Title:** Noise-induced contraction of MPO truncation errors in noisy random circuits and Lindbladian dynamics

**Authors:** Zhi-Yuan Wei, Joel Rajakumar, Jon Nelson, Daniel Malz, Michael J. Gullans, Alexey V. Gorshkov

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.20400v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.20400v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-20


### 摘要

我们研究在模拟两种设置时矩阵积运算符（MPO）截断误差如何演变：（1）去极化噪音或幅度衰减噪音下的1D哈尔随机电路，（2）去极化或幅度衰减噪音下的不可积量子伊辛模型的1D Lindbladian动力学。我们首先表明，系统密度矩阵的平均纯度在时间尺度上放松到稳定值，该值与噪音率成正比。然后，我们表明，当有噪的动态将不同的密度矩阵映射到相同的稳态时，截断误差在系统大小$N$和进化时间$t$上呈指数级收缩。这产生了$L_1$截断误差的经验界，该界在$N$中比现有界呈指数级紧密。总而言之，这些结果提供了经验证据，表明MPO模拟算法可以有效地从任意电路深度的1D有噪随机电路的输出[set（1）]以及从1D Lindbladian动态的稳态[set（2）]进行采样。


### Abstract

We study how matrix-product-operator (MPO) truncation errors evolve when simulating two setups: (1) 1D Haar-random circuits under either depolarizing noise or amplitude-damping noise, and (2) 1D Lindbladian dynamics of a non-integrable quantum Ising model under either depolarizing or amplitude-damping noise. We first show that the average purity of the system density matrix relaxes to a steady value on a timescale that scales inversely with the noise rate. We then show that truncation errors contract exponentially in both system size $N$ and the evolution time $t$, as the noisy dynamics maps different density matrices toward the same steady state. This yields an empirical bound on the $L_1$ truncation error that is exponentially tighter in $N$ than the existing bound. Together, these results provide empirical evidence that MPO simulation algorithms may efficiently sample from the output of 1D noisy random circuits [setup (1)] at arbitrary circuit depth, and from the steady state of 1D Lindbladian dynamics [setup (2)].

对于字符串的字母从一个小的字母表，如DNA序列，我们提出了一个量子编码，经验提供了一个很强的相关性之间的Levenshtein编辑距离和量子状态之间的保真度定义的编码。它基于现代大型语言模型中采用的旋转位置嵌入（RoPE）原则。传统上，这种编码产生RotorMap -一种GPU加速的DNA映射算法，在人类和玉米基因组的概念验证测试中，该算法比单线程Minimap 2实现了50- 700倍的加速。为了在量子设备上使用，我们引入了Angular编码，它是从RoPE构建的，直接输出状态准备电路。为了验证其在NISQ设备上的特性和实用性，我们报告了在Quantinuum量子计算机上进行的实验结果：56量子位H2-1、H2-2和最新的98量子位Helios-1。作为一个潜在的应用，我们考虑了量子DNA认证问题，并推测与任何类似的经典解决方案相比，可以实现单向通信复杂性的量子优势。


### Abstract

For strings of letters from a small alphabet, such as DNA sequences, we present a quantum encoding that empirically provides a strong correlation between the Levenshtein edit distance and the fidelity between quantum states defined by the encodings. It is based on the principles of Rotary Position Embeddings (RoPE), employed in modern large language models. Classically, this encoding yields RotorMap - a GPU-accelerated DNA mapping algorithm that achieves speedups of 50-700x over single-thread Minimap2 in proof-of-concept tests on human and maize genomes. For use on quantum devices, we introduce the Angular encoding, which is built from RoPE and directly outputs state preparation circuits. To verify its properties and utility on NISQ devices, we report results of experiments conducted on quantum computers from Quantinuum: the 56-qubit H2-1, H2-2 and the latest 98-qubit Helios-1. As a potential application, we consider a quantum DNA authentication problem and conjecture that a quantum advantage in one-way communication complexity could be achieved over any comparable classical solution.

## 使用低足迹非线性干涉仪通过未检测到的量子识别聚合物

**Original Title:** Polymer identification via undetected photons using a low footprint nonlinear interferometer

**Authors:** Atta Ur Rehman Sherwani, Emma Pearce, Philipp Hildenstein, Felix Mauerhoff, Alexander Sahm, Katrin Paschke, Helen M. Chrzanowski, Sven Ramelow

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22253v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.22253v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-23


### 摘要

塑料污染已成为一个严峻的全球挑战，微塑料弥漫在生态系统中并进入人类食物链。有效监测这种广泛的污染需要快速、可靠且便携式的材料识别技术，而这些技术通常无法实现传统的拉曼和傅里叶变换光谱。非线性干涉仪（NLI）内的未检测到的量子光谱提供了一种解决方案，允许通过使用标准硅基技术仅检测近红外信号量子来检索中红外吸收光谱。在这里，我们展示了一种高度紧凑、微集成、热稳定的NLI，具有类似迈克尔逊的几何形状，专为塑料的快速光谱分析而设计。我们对其室温性能进行了基准测试，结果表明信噪比为34，测量速率为100 Hz，光谱分辨率为6 cm$^{-1}$。我们表明，我们可以准确，快速地检索常见的聚合物，如聚丙烯，聚乙烯和聚苯乙烯的特征振动吸收光谱，而不使用中红外技术。这些结果使我们的紧凑型模块成为一个有前途的现场部署平台，用于强大的实时环境监测系统和其他中红外光谱应用。


### Abstract

Plastic pollution has become a critical global challenge, with microplastics pervading ecosystems and entering human food chains. Effectively monitoring this widespread contamination demands rapid, reliable, and portable material identification techniques that often elude conventional Raman and FTIR spectroscopy. Undetected photon spectroscopy within a nonlinear interferometer (NLI) offers a solution, allowing the retrieval of mid-infrared absorption spectra by detecting only near-infrared signal photons using standard silicon-based technology. Here, we demonstrate a highly compact, micro-integrated, thermally-stabilised NLI with a Michelson-like geometry designed for the rapid spectroscopy of plastics. We benchmarked its room-temperature performance, demonstrating a signal-to-noise ratio of 34 with a measurement rate of 100 Hz and a spectral resolution of 6 cm$^{-1}$. We show that we can accurately and rapidly retrieve the characteristic vibrational absorption spectra of common polymers such as polypropylene, polyethene, and polystyrene, without using mid-infrared technology. These results establish our compact module as a promising field-deployable platform for robust, real-time environmental monitoring systems and other mid-infrared spectroscopy applications.

## 精密的时间之箭

**Original Title:** Precision's arrow of time

**Authors:** Luis E. F. Foa Torres, G. Pappas, V. Achilleos, D. Bautista Avilés

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22284v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.22284v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-23


### 摘要

时间之箭通常归因于两种机制：通过环境纠缠产生的去相干，以及通过非线性动力学产生的混乱。在这里，我们展示了第三种途径，即精确诱导的不可逆性（PIR），两者都不需要。没有纠缠。没有非线性。只有三种成分：放大性、非正态性和有限动态范围，其相互作用产生操作时间箭头;删除任何一个，即可恢复可逆性。非埃尔米特进化在数学上仍然是可逆的，但在以可用精度线性缩放的尖锐时间可预测性视野之外，不同的状态就会崩溃为相同的表示。回声保真度测试证实了任意精度计算和硬件之间的这种转变，揭示了形式可逆性和物理可逆性的分歧。


### Abstract

The arrow of time is usually attributed to two mechanisms: decoherence through environmental entanglement, and chaos through nonlinear dynamics. Here we demonstrate a third route, Precision-Induced Irreversibility (PIR), requiring neither. No entanglement. No nonlinearity. Just three ingredients: amplification, non-normality, and finite dynamic range, whose interplay yields an operational arrow of time; remove any one and reversibility can be restored. Non-Hermitian evolution remains mathematically invertible, yet beyond a sharp temporal predictability horizon scaling linearly with available precision, distinct states collapse onto identical representations. Echo-fidelity tests confirm this transition across arbitrary-precision calculations and hardware, revealing where formal invertibility and physical reversibility diverge.
