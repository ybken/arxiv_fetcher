## 加速宇宙体解码器以进行量子错误纠正

**Original Title:** Accelerating the Tesseract Decoder for Quantum Error Correction

**Authors:** Dragana Grbic, Laleh Aghababaie Beni, Noah Shutty

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.02985v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.02985v2

**Submitted:** 2026-02-03


### 摘要

量子错误纠正（QEC）对于构建稳健、耐故障的量子计算机至关重要;然而，解码过程通常存在严重的计算瓶颈。Tesseract是一款新型的QEC最可能错误（MLE）解码器，它采用A* 搜索算法来探索指数级大的错误假设图，实现高解码速度和准确性。本文提出了一种通过低级性能增强来优化Tesseract解码器的系统方法。基于广泛的分析，我们实施了四种有针对性的优化策略，包括替换低效的数据结构、重组内存布局以提高缓存命中率以及使用硬件加速的逐位操作。我们在各种代码系列和配置中实现了显着的解码加速，包括颜色代码、双变量自行车代码、表面代码和跨性别C诺特协议。我们的结果表明，大多数代码系列的一致加速比约为2倍，通常超过2.5倍。值得注意的是，对于计算要求最高的双变量自行车代码配置，我们实现了超过5倍的峰值性能增长。这些改进使Tesseract解码器更加高效和可扩展，作为一个实践案例研究，凸显了高性能软件工程在QEC中的重要性，并为未来的研究提供了坚实的基础。


### Abstract

Quantum Error Correction (QEC) is essential for building robust, fault-tolerant quantum computers; however, the decoding process often presents a significant computational bottleneck. Tesseract is a novel Most-Likely-Error (MLE) decoder for QEC that employs the A* search algorithm to explore an exponentially large graph of error hypotheses, achieving high decoding speed and accuracy. This paper presents a systematic approach to optimizing the Tesseract decoder through low-level performance enhancements. Based on extensive profiling, we implemented four targeted optimization strategies, including the replacement of inefficient data structures, reorganization of memory layouts to improve cache hit rates, and the use of hardware-accelerated bit-wise operations. We achieved significant decoding speedups across a wide range of code families and configurations, including Color Codes, Bivariate-Bicycle Codes, Surface Codes, and Transversal CNOT Protocols. Our results demonstrate consistent speedups of approximately 2x for most code families, often exceeding 2.5x. Notably, we achieved a peak performance gain of over 5x for the most computationally demanding configurations of Bivariate-Bicycle Codes. These improvements make the Tesseract decoder more efficient and scalable, serving as a practical case study that highlights the importance of high-performance software engineering in QEC and providing a strong foundation for future research.

## 增强的量子扩散模型在复杂图像生成中的应用

**Original Title:** Enhancing Quantum Diffusion Models for Complex Image Generation

**Authors:** Jeongbin Jo, Santanam Wishal, Shah Md Khalil Ullah, Shan Zeng, Dikshant Dulal

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.03405v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.03405v2

**Submitted:** 2026-02-03


### 摘要

量子生成模型提供了一种探索多维Hilbert空间的新颖方法，但应用于多模式分布时在可扩展性和可表达性方面面临重大挑战。在这项研究中，我们探索了一种与自适应非本地可观测量（ANO）集成的混合量子经典U-Net架构，作为这些障碍的潜在解决方案。通过将经典数据压缩到密集的量子潜空间中并利用可训练的可观测量，我们的模型旨在提取补充经典处理的非局部特征。我们还研究了跳过连接在反向扩散过程中保留语义信息方面的作用。完整MNIST数据集（数字0-9）的实验结果表明，所提出的架构能够为所有数字类别生成结构一致且可识别的图像。虽然硬件限制仍然对分辨率造成限制，但我们的研究结果表明，具有自适应测量的混合架构为在NISQ时代缓解模式崩溃和增强生成能力提供了可行的途径。


### Abstract

Quantum generative models offer a novel approach to exploring high-dimensional Hilbert spaces but face significant challenges in scalability and expressibility when applied to multi-modal distributions. In this study, we explore a Hybrid Quantum-Classical U-Net architecture integrated with Adaptive Non-Local Observables (ANO) as a potential solution to these hurdles. By compressing classical data into a dense quantum latent space and utilizing trainable observables, our model aims to extract non-local features that complement classical processing. We also investigate the role of Skip Connections in preserving semantic information during the reverse diffusion process. Experimental results on the full MNIST dataset (digits 0-9) demonstrate that the proposed architecture is capable of generating structurally coherent and recognizable images for all digit classes. While hardware constraints still impose limitations on resolution, our findings suggest that hybrid architectures with adaptive measurements provide a feasible pathway for mitigating mode collapse and enhancing generative capabilities in the NISQ era.

## 通过使用大型语言模型的测试时学习生成量子电路

**Original Title:** Quantum Circuit Generation via test-time learning with large language models

**Authors:** Adriano Macarone-Palmieri, Rosario Lo Franco

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.03466v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.03466v2

**Submitted:** 2026-02-03


### 摘要

大型语言模型（LLM）可以生成结构化工件，但将它们用作科学设计的可靠优化器需要一种在黑匣子评估下进行迭代改进的机制。在这里，我们将量子电路合成视为一个闭环、测试时优化问题：LLM提出对固定长度门列表的编辑，外部模拟器使用迈耶-沃拉赫（MW）全局纠缠度量评估所得状态。我们引入了一种轻量级的测试时学习食谱，可以将之前的高性能候选人重复使用作为显式记忆跟踪，通过分数差异反馈来增强提示，并应用从最佳抽样重新开始以避免潜在的平稳期。在固定的20量子位设置中，没有反馈和从头开始的循环可以在一定范围内改进随机初始电路。为了提高绩效和成功率，我们使用完整的学习策略。对于25量子位，当使用原始查询时，它可以缓解明显的性能平台。除了原始分数之外，我们还分析了合成状态的结构，发现高MW解决方案可以对应于稳定剂或类图形状态的结构，但由于度量性质和即时设计，无法保证完全的连接性。这些结果说明了记忆评估者指导的电路合成LLM优化的前景和陷阱，强调了先前人造理论定理在优化设计定制工具以支持研究方面的关键作用。


### Abstract

Large language models (LLMs) can generate structured artifacts, but using them as dependable optimizers for scientific design requires a mechanism for iterative improvement under black-box evaluation. Here, we cast quantum circuit synthesis as a closed-loop, test-time optimization problem: an LLM proposes edits to a fixed-length gate list, and an external simulator evaluates the resulting state with the Meyer-Wallach (MW) global entanglement measure. We introduce a lightweight test-time learning recipe that can reuse prior high-performing candidates as an explicit memory trace, augments prompts with a score-difference feedback, and applies restart-from-the-best sampling to escape potential plateaus. Across fixed 20-qubit settings, the loop without feedback and restart-from-the-best improves random initial circuits over a range of gate budgets. To lift up this performance and success rate, we use the full learning strategy. For the 25-qubit, it mitigates a pronounced performance plateau when naive querying is used. Beyond raw scores, we analyze the structure of synthesized states and find that high MW solutions can correspond to stabilizer or graph-state-like constructions, but full connectivity is not guaranteed due to the metric property and prompt design. These results illustrate both the promise and the pitfalls of memory evaluator-guided LLM optimization for circuit synthesis, highlighting the critical role of prior human-made theoretical theorems to optimally design a custom tool in support of research.

## 量子误差修正的可扩展测试

**Original Title:** Scalable testing of quantum error correction

**Authors:** John Zhuoyang Ye, Jens Palsberg

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.04921v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.04921v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-04


### 摘要

量子误差修正基准测试的标准方法是随机错误注入测试。最先进的工具\stim对于距离高达10的错误纠正实施非常有效，但对于更大距离的扩展性较差，物理错误率较低。本文中，我们提出了一种可扩展的方法，将分层故障注入与外推相结合。我们的见解是，可以有效地对一些故障空间进行采样，之后外推足以完成测试任务。因此，我们的工具可以扩展到距离17，物理错误率为0.0005，桌面上的时间预算为两小时。对于这种情况，它以高置信度估计逻辑错误率为1.51美元\x 10 '{-11}$。


### Abstract

The standard method for benchmarking quantum error-correction is randomized fault-injection testing. The state-of-the-art tool \stim is efficient for error correction implementations with distances of up to 10, but scales poorly to larger distances for low physical error rates. In this paper, we present a scalable approach that combines stratified fault injection with extrapolation. Our insight is that some of the fault space can be sampled efficiently, after which extrapolation is sufficient to complete the testing task. As a result, our tool scales to distance 17 for a physical error rate of 0.0005 with a two-hour time budget on a desktop. For this case, it estimated a logical error rate of $1.51 \times 10^{-11}$ with high confidence.

## 大旋转的不可知参数估计

**Original Title:** Agnostic Parameter Estimation with Large Spins

**Authors:** Huining Zhang, X. X. Yi

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.04934v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.04934v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-04


### 摘要

量子状态相对于某个参数的量子费舍尔信息量化了量子状态对该参数变化的敏感性。最大化量子费舍尔信息对于实现量子传感器的最佳估计精度至关重要。典型的量子传感器涉及经历未知旋转的量子位（例如1/2旋转）探针，这里未知旋转角是要估计的参数。一个众所周知的局限性是，如果旋转轴未知，则不可能获得最大量子费舍尔信息。最近，通过利用探测量子位和辅助量子位之间的纠缠，这一限制被解除了。也就是说，通过在轴暴露后测量支臂，可以准备适合任何未知旋转轴的最佳探针。然而，该提案仅适用于旋转-1/2。考虑到大型旋转探针可以获得更大的量子费舍尔信息，提供更强的拉曼优势，我们在这里利用大型旋转探针和辅助之间的纠缠来获得最佳量子费舍尔信息来估计旋转角，而无需事先了解旋转轴。与之前的自旋-1/2情况不同，在大自旋情况下实现最佳精度通常需要后选择，导致成功概率取决于希尔伯特空间的维度。此外，我们扩展的编码状态从最大纠缠的情况下，一般的纠缠态，表明最佳的计量仍然可以实现一定的成功概率。


### Abstract

The quantum Fisher information of a quantum state with respect to a certain parameter quantifies the sensitivity of the quantum state to changes in that parameter. Maximizing the quantum Fisher information is essential for achieving the optimal estimation precision of quantum sensors. A typical quantum sensor involves a qubit(e.g. a spin-1/2) probe undergoing an unknown rotation, here the unknown rotation angle is the parameter to be estimated. A well known limitation is that if the rotation axis is unknown, the maximal quantum Fisher information is impossible to attain. This limitation has been lifted recently by leveraging entanglement between the probe qubit and an ancilla qubit. Namely, through measurement of the ancilla after the axis is revealed, one can prepare the probe that is optimal for any unknown rotation axis. This proposal, however, works only for a spin-1/2. Considering large spin probes can achieve a larger quantum Fisher information, offering enhanced metrological advantage, we here utilize the entanglement between a large spin probe and an ancilla to achieve optimal quantum Fisher information for estimating the rotation angle, without prior knowledge of the rotation axis. Different from the previous spin-1/2 case, achieving the optimal precision with large spins generally requires post-selection, resulting in a success probability dependent on the dimension of the Hilbert space. Furthermore, we extend the encoding state from the maximally entangled case to general entangled states, showing that optimal metrology can still be achieved with a certain success probability.

## 经典共振与量子共振之间的对应

**Original Title:** Correspondence between classical and quantum resonances

**Authors:** F. J. Arranz, R. M. Benito, F. Borondo

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.04793v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.04793v2

**Submitted:** 2026-02-04


### 摘要

随着激发能的增加，分子Hamilton非线性系统发生分叉，从而导致不同经典共振的出现。本文中，我们研究了这些经典共振在同质化体系CN-Li$\leftrightarrows$Li-CN中的量子表现。通过使用本征能与普朗克常数的相关图，我们展示了不同系列的避免交叉的存在，从而导致相应的量子共振系列，这代表了经典共振的量子表现。此外，将这些系列外推到$\hbar=0$揭示了经典共振的分叉能量与半经典极限$\hbar\to 0 $中量子共振系列能量之间的对应关系。此外，为了获得我们结果的分析表达，我们开发了半经典理论。


### Abstract

Bifurcations take place in molecular Hamiltonian nonlinear systems as the excitation energy increases, this leading to the appearance of different classical resonances. In this paper, we study the quantum manifestations of these classical resonances in the isomerizing system CN-Li$\leftrightarrows$Li-CN. By using a correlation diagram of eigenenergies versus Planck constant, we show the existence of different series of avoided crossings, leading to the corresponding series of quantum resonances, which represent the quantum manifestations of the classical resonances. Moreover, the extrapolation of these series to $\hbar=0$ unveils the correspondence between the bifurcation energy of classical resonances and the energy of the series of quantum resonances in the semiclassical limit $\hbar\to0$. Additionally, in order to obtain analytical expressions for our results, a semiclassical theory is developed.

## 图--海森堡反铁磁铁变分波函数相优化复杂性的理论分析

**Original Title:** Graph--Theoretic Analysis of Phase Optimization Complexity in Variational Wave Functions for Heisenberg Antiferromagnets

**Authors:** Mahmud Ashraf Shamim, Moshiur Rahman, Mohamed Hibat-Allah, Paulo T Araujo

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.04943v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.04943v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-04


### 摘要

尽管进行了广泛的研究，但受挫折的海森堡反铁磁铁（HAF）中波函数的相结构尚未得到系统的描述。在这项工作中，我们将HAF的Hilbert空间表示为加权图，我们将其称为Hilbert图（HG），其顶部是旋转配置，其边由海森堡Hamilton的非对角旋转翻转项生成，权重由波函数幅度的积设置。保持幅度固定并将相限制为$\mathbb{Z}_2$值，依赖于相的变分能量可以被重新铸造为HG上的经典伊辛反铁磁体，从而使接地状态的相重建减少到加权Max-Cut实例。这表明相重建HAF在最坏情况下是NP难的，并且在波函数符号结构和组合优化之间提供了直接联系。


### Abstract

Despite extensive study, the phase structure of the wavefunctions in frustrated Heisenberg antiferromagnets (HAF) is not yet systematically characterized. In this work, we represent the Hilbert space of an HAF as a weighted graph, which we term the Hilbert graph (HG), whose vertices are spin configurations and whose edges are generated by off-diagonal spin-flip terms of the Heisenberg Hamiltonian, with weights set by products of wavefunction amplitudes. Holding the amplitudes fixed and restricting phases to $\mathbb{Z}_2$ values, the phase-dependent variational energy can be recast as a classical Ising antiferromagnet on the HG, so that phase reconstruction of the ground state reduces to a weighted Max-Cut instance. This shows that phase reconstruction HAF is worst-case NP-hard and provides a direct link between wavefunction sign structure and combinatorial optimization.

## 具有少量副本测量的实例最佳高精度阴影断层扫描：可重写方法

**Original Title:** Instance-optimal high-precision shadow tomography with few-copy measurements: A metrological approach

**Authors:** Senrui Chen, Weiyuan Gong, Sisi Zhou

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.04952v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.04952v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-04


### 摘要

We study the sample complexity of shadow tomography in the high-precision regime under realistic measurement constraints. Given an unknown $d$-dimensional quantum state $ρ$ and a known set of observables $\{O_i\}_{i=1}^m$, the goal is to estimate expectation values $\{\mathrm{tr}(O_iρ)\}_{i=1}^m$ to accuracy $ε$ in $L_p$-norm, using possibly adaptive measurements that act on $O(\mathrm{polylog}(d))$ number of copies of $ρ$ at a time. We focus on the regime where $ε$ is below an instance-dependent threshold.   Our main contribution is an instance-optimal characterization of the sample complexity as $\tildeΘ(Γ_p/ε^2)$, where $Γ_p$ is a function of $\{O_i\}_{i=1}^m$ defined via an optimization formula involving the inverse Fisher information matrix. Previously, tight bounds were known only in special cases, e.g. Pauli shadow tomography with $L_\infty$-norm error. Concretely, we first analyze a simpler oblivious variant where the goal is to estimate an observable of the form $\sum_{i=1}^m α_i O_i$ with $\|α\|_q = 1$ (where $q$ is dual to $p$) revealed after the measurement. For single-copy measurements, we obtain a sample complexity of $Θ(Γ^{\mathrm{ob}}_p/ε^2)$. We then show $\tildeΘ(Γ_p/ε^2)$ is necessary and sufficient for the original problem, with the lower bound applying to unbiased, bounded estimators. Our upper bounds rely on a two-step algorithm combining coarse tomography with local estimation. Notably, $Γ^{\mathrm{ob}}_\infty = Γ_\infty$. In both cases, allowing $c$-copy measurements improves the sample complexity by at most $Ω(1/c)$.   Our results establish a quantitative correspondence between quantum learning and metrology, unifying asymptotic metrological limits with finite-sample learning guarantees.

  Our main contribution is an instance-optimal characterization of the sample complexity as $\tildeΘ(Γ_p/ε^2)$, where $Γ_p$ is a function of $\{O_i\}_{i=1}^m$ defined via an optimization formula involving the inverse Fisher information matrix. ## 在冷原子量子模拟器上迈向2美元+1$D量子电动力学

### 摘要

冷原子已成为更高空间维度量子模拟格点规范理论的强大平台。然而，这样的实现仅限于规范场的最低可能截断，这限制了人们与格子量子电动力学的联系。在这里，我们提出了一个可行的冷原子量子模拟器，该模拟器适用于旋转$S=1$截断中的$（2+1）$维U$（1）$格点规范理论，以动态物质和规范场为特色。我们将这个理论映射到玻色子计算基础上，并通过量子芝诺动力学的新兴规范保护机制稳定下来。该实现基于在倾斜光学超格子中实现的单物种Bose-Hubbard模型。这种方法只需要当前超冷原子平台中已经可用的适度实验资源。使用无限矩阵积状态模拟，我们对全局熄灭下的实时动态进行基准测试。结果证明了目标规范理论的忠实演变和规范约束的稳健保留。我们的工作显着推进了使用更大规范场截断模拟更高维格点规范理论的实验前景。

## Krylov复杂性通过量子行走的出现：复杂性的量子起源探索

**Original Title:** Emergence of Krylov complexity through quantum walks: An exploration of the quantum origins of complexity

**Authors:** Dimitrios Patramanis, Watse Sybesma

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.04949v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.04949v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-04


### 摘要

在这项工作中，我们研究了图上的量子随机游动与Krylov/扩展复杂性之间的关系。我们表明，后者的定义是通过将图简化为链的规范方法自然出现的，在该方法上我们可以识别通常的Krylov结构。我们使用这种识别来构建与具有已知复杂性特征的特殊系统类别相对应的图形族，并相反，计算物理感兴趣的图形的Krylov复杂性。两个主要结果是对任意数量$q$相互作用费米子的SYK模型的Lanczos系数的分析计算，以及对任意数量的超立方体图的Krylov复杂性的完整描述。后者是深入比较克雷洛夫和电路复杂性的起点，据称它们是在黑洞的背景下出现的。我们发现，虽然在某些条件下Krylov复杂性遵循归因于此类系统的增长和饱和模式，但饱和发生的时间尺度通常可能比随机么正电路预测的要短，这是由于量子加速的影响，在比较量子和经典随机游走时通常会发生。


### Abstract

In this work we study the relationship between quantum random walks on graphs and Krylov/spread complexity. We show that the latter's definition naturally emerges through a canonical method of reducing a graph to a chain, on which we can identify the usual Krylov structure. We use this identification to construct families of graphs corresponding to special classes of systems with known complexity features and conversely, to compute Krylov complexity for graphs of physical interest. ### 摘要

光子量子计算是容错竞赛中的有力竞争者。最近的提议使用以频率模式编码的量子比特，承诺大大减少硬件占用空间，并引起了广泛的关注。在这种编码中，线性光学，即，分束器和移相器不一定是节能的，并且实施起来成本高。在这项工作中，我们提出了基于调制耦合共振器阵列的频率模式分束器的设计。我们开发了一种基于量子输入输出网络的SHH形式主义来构建有效转移矩阵的方法。我们的方法是灵活的且高度可组合的，允许我们定义$N$模式分束器，无论是本地基于任意连接性的$N$-共振器阵列，还是定义为互连的$l$模式分束器的网络，其中$l<N$。我们应用我们的方法来分析由组成这些设备、四个共振器设备获得的双共振器设备、频域移器和马赫-曾德干涉仪，并提出了一个关于本地生成某些$N$模式频域分束器的可能性的正式不去定理。N$-共振器阵列。


### Abstract

Photonic quantum computing is a strong contender in the race to fault-tolerance. Recent proposals using qubits encoded in frequency modes promise a large reduction in hardware footprint, and have garnered much attention. In this encoding, linear optics, i.e., beam splitters and phase shifters, is necessarily not energy-conserving, and is costly to implement. In this work, we present designs of frequency-mode beam splitters based on modulated arrays of coupled resonators. We develop a methodology to construct their effective transfer matrices based on the SLH formalism for quantum input-output networks. Our methodology is flexible and highly composable, allowing us to define $N$-mode beam splitters either natively based on arrays of $N$-resonators of arbitrary connectivity or as networks of interconnected $l$-mode beam splitters, with $l<N$. We apply our methodology to analyze a two-resonator device, a frequency-domain phase shifter and a Mach-Zehnder interferometer obtained from composing these devices, a four-resonator device, and present a formal no-go theorem on the possibility of natively generating certain $N$-mode frequency-domain beam splitters with arrays of $N$-resonators.
