## 量子通道合奏的时刻

**Original Title:** Moments of quantum channel ensembles

**Authors:** Matthew Duschenes, Diego García-Martín, Zoë Holmes, M. Cerezo

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.12700v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.12700v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-16


### 摘要

正元集合的时刻在量子信息理论中发挥着核心作用，因为它们捕捉了具有某种形式随机性的系统动力学的统计性质。事实上，当比较给定么正系综的相关矩操作符与另一个参考系综的相关矩操作符的接近程度时，就会出现诸如大约$t$-设计之类的概念。尽管动量运算符很重要，但它们的性质尚未像量子通道那样被探索。在这项工作中，我们开发了一个理论框架，来计算量子通道集合的动量操作符，适用于所有动量阶$t$，特别关注于确定可用作参考点的集合。通过推导系综之间的层次，通过他们的时刻运营商规范的不等式，我们给他们的业务意义，并定义有用的概念，如通道$T$-设计。最后，我们进行了理论和数值研究，表明不同类型的噪声可以降低矩算子的范数（例如，去极化噪声），以及增加它（例如，振幅阻尼），并将噪声引起的集中现象推广到通道设计引起的现象。一路上，我们找到了排列的块垂直基础，这极大地简化了我们的分析，并且可能具有独立的兴趣。


### Abstract

Moments of ensembles of unitaries play a central role in quantum information theory as they capture the statistical properties of dynamics of systems with some form of randomness. Indeed, concepts such as approximate $t$-designs arise when comparing how close an associated moment operator of a given unitary ensemble is to that of another, reference ensemble. Despite the importance of moment operators, their properties have not been as explored for quantum channels. In this work we develop a theoretical framework to compute moment operators for ensembles of quantum channels, for all moment orders $t$, with a special focus on determining ensembles that can be used as points of reference. By deriving hierarchies between ensembles, via inequalities of their moment operator norms, we give them operational meaning, and define useful concepts such as that of channel $t$-designs. Finally, we perform theoretical and numerical studies which show that different types of noise can decrease the norm of the moment operators (e.g., depolarizing noise), as well as increase it (e.g., amplitude damping), and generalize noise-induced concentration phenomena to channel-design-induced phenomena. Along the way, we find a block-orthogonal basis for permutations, which greatly simplifies our analyses, and may be of independent interest.

## 快衰落通道上CV-QKD平均值的作用

**Original Title:** The role of averages in CV-QKD over fast fading channels

**Authors:** Miguel Castillo-Celeita, Matteo Schiavon

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.12721v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.12721v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-16


### 摘要

这项工作对快衰落通道上的连续变量量子密钥分发（CV-QKD）协议进行了研究，该通道通常在自由空间通信链路中找到。考虑两种窃听模型来评估其在集体攻击下的安全性：\textit{Holevo bound average}（HSA）和\textit{协方差矩阵平均}（CMA）。在HPA方法中，Holevo界限在通道透过率上求平均。相比之下，CMA方法根据平均协方差矩阵计算Holevo界限。为这两种策略开发了分析公式。这两种方法在计算合法各方之间的互信息的方式上也有所不同。结果表明，SKR受到如何处理渠道波动的显着影响，凸显了选择更好地描述协议实际实现的模型的重要性。


### Abstract

This work presents a study of continuous-variable quantum key distribution (CV-QKD) protocols over fast-fading channels, typically found in free-space communication links. Two eavesdropping models are considered to evaluate their security under collective attacks: \textit{Holevo bound average} (HBA) and \textit{covariance matrix average} (CMA). In the HBA approach, the Holevo bound is averaged over the channel transmittance. In contrast, the CMA method calculates the Holevo bound from the average covariance matrix. Analytical expressions are developed for both strategies. The two methods also differ in how they calculate the mutual information between the legitimate parties. The results demonstrate that the SKR is significantly influenced by how you treat channel fluctuations, highlighting the importance of choosing the model that better describes the actual implementation of the protocol.

## Lutinger液体中的巨大数宇称效应和可扩展的旋转压缩

**Original Title:** Giant number-parity effect and scalable spin squeezing in Luttinger liquids

**Authors:** Filippo Caleca, Saverio Bocini, Fabio Mezzacapo, Tommaso Roscilde

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.12746v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.12746v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-16


### 摘要

粒子大小的量子旋转系统可以通过应用量子破场来被磁性化，但一般来说，一旦量子破场，它们的对称性预计就会恢复。最近（F. Caleca等人，arXiv：2412.15493）我们已经表明，具有奇数个位点和保宇称Hamilton量的半整旋转系统可以保持有限的磁性，因此在有限大小下表现出自发对称性破缺（TSB）。在这里，我们将这种现象推广到旋转链，其低能量物理学（零场中）实现了卢廷格-液体相。我们观察到，奇数大小的链可以表现出有限大小准单边带现象，其中保留净次延磁强度$M \sim N^{1-1/（4K）}$，其中$N$是位点数量，$K $是卢廷格指数。有趣的是，通过准外交地关闭分裂场而准备的状态显示出可扩展的旋转压缩--即系统越大--无论N$的宇称如何。压缩参数的缩放再次由Lutinger指数$ð_R^2 \sim N^{-1+1/（2K）}$决定。这一结果表明，通常与高维系统相关联的可扩展量子关联在无间隙的一维系统中也可以找到;它们是Luttinger液体临界性质的直接结果。


### Abstract

Finite-size quantum spin systems can be magnetized by the application of a symmetry-breaking field, but in general their symmetry is expected to be restored once the field is turned off adiabatically. Recently (F. Caleca et al., arXiv:2412.15493) we have shown that systems of half-integer spins with an odd number of sites and a parity-preserving Hamiltonian can retain a finite magnetization, hence exhibiting spontaneous symmetry breaking (SSB) at finite size. Here we generalize this phenomenon to spin chains whose low-energy physics (in zero field) realizes a Luttinger-liquid phase. We observe that odd-sized chains can exhibit a phenomenon of finite-size quasi-SSB, in which a net sub-extensive magnetization, $M \sim N^{1-1/(4K)}$ is retained, where $N$ is the number of sites and $K$ the Luttinger exponent. Interestingly, the states prepared by turning off the symmetry-breaking field quasi-adiabatically display scalable spin squeezing -- namely stronger the bigger the system -- regardless of the parity of $N$. The scaling of the squeezing parameter is dictated again by the Luttinger exponent, $ξ_R^2 \sim N^{-1+1/(2K)}$. This result shows that scalable quantum correlations with metrological significance, associated typically with high-dimensional systems, can be found as well in gapless one-dimensional ones; and they are a direct consequence of the critical nature of Luttinger liquids.

## Sdim：Qudit稳定器模拟器

**Original Title:** Sdim: A Qudit Stabilizer Simulator

**Authors:** Adeeb Kabir, Steven Nguyen, Sohan Ghosh, Tijil Kiran, Isaac H. Kim, Yipeng Huang

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.12777v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.12777v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-16


### 摘要

量子计算机在过去十年中稳步发展，但开发有用的通用计算所需的容错量子计算（FTQC）技术仍然是一项持续的努力。FTQC的关键元素，如纠错码和解码，得到了Stim和CHP等丰富的稳定器仿真软件的支持，这些软件对于在现实规模上对这些协议进行数值表征至关重要。最近，实验小组已经建立了新生的高维量子硬件，称为qudits，它对算法和FTQC具有无数有吸引力的特性。尽管如此，还没有广泛使用的量子稳定器模拟器。我们引入了此类所有维度模拟器的第一个开源实现。我们针对现有的状态向量模拟证明了它的正确性，并对它在评估和采样量子电路方面的性能进行了基准测试。该模拟器是探索新型量子比特错误纠正的重要计算基础设施，就像早期的稳定器模拟器用于量子比特一样。


### Abstract

Quantum computers have steadily improved over the last decade, but developing fault-tolerant quantum computing (FTQC) techniques, required for useful, universal computation remains an ongoing effort. Key elements of FTQC such as error-correcting codes and decoding are supported by a rich bed of stabilizer simulation software such as Stim and CHP, which are essential for numerically characterizing these protocols at realistic scales. Recently, experimental groups have built nascent high-dimensional quantum hardware, known as qudits, which have a myriad of attractive properties for algorithms and FTQC. Despite this, there are no widely available qudit stabilizer simulators. We introduce the first open-source realization of such a simulator for all dimensions. We demonstrate its correctness against existing state vector simulations and benchmark its performance in evaluating and sampling quantum circuits. This simulator is the essential computational infrastructure to explore novel qudit error correction as earlier stabilizer simulators have been for qubits.

## 具有硬件代表性噪音模型的量子门GRAPE脉冲优化的验证实现

**Original Title:** Verified Implementation of GRAPE Pulse Optimization for Quantum Gates with Hardware-Representative Noise Models

**Authors:** Rylan Malarchick

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.12799v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.12799v2

**Submitted:** 2025-11-16


### 摘要

有噪的中等规模量子（NISQ）计算机中的门保真度仍然是限制实际量子计算的主要瓶颈，受到去相关和控制噪音的限制。量子最优控制（QOC）技术，例如梯度上升脉冲工程（GRAPE）算法，为设计积极减轻这些影响的抗噪脉冲提供了一种强大的方法。然而，大多数QOC实现都在理想化的模拟环境中运行，这些环境无法捕捉物理量子硬件固有的实时参数漂移，从而产生了关键的“与真实”差距。在这项工作中，我介绍了QubitPulseOpt，这是一个开源、经过严格测试的Python框架，旨在通过硬件代表性的最佳控制来弥合这一差距。该框架演示了与IQM的Garnet量子处理器（20量子位超导设备）的API连接性，并实现了使用硬件代表性参数构建高保真度“数字孪生子”的工作流程。使用此模拟框架，我证明与标准高斯脉冲相比，GRAPE优化的脉冲实现了77 $\x $的模拟门误差降低。该框架的可靠性通过864个测试验证套件（74%代码覆盖率）和遵守NASA JPL Power-of-10安全关键编码标准来确保，为值得信赖的量子控制软件建立了新范式。所有结果均来自经过验证的GRAPE优化以及完整的出处文档。


### Abstract

Gate fidelity in noisy intermediate-scale quantum (NISQ) computers remains the primary bottleneck limiting practical quantum computation, constrained by decoherence and control noise. Quantum optimal control (QOC) techniques, such as the gradient ascent pulse engineering (GRAPE) algorithm, offer a powerful approach to designing noise-robust pulses that actively mitigate these effects. However, most QOC implementations operate in idealized simulation environments that fail to capture the real-time parameter drift inherent to physical quantum hardware, creating a critical ``sim-to-real'' gap. In this work, I present QubitPulseOpt, an open-source, rigorously-tested Python framework designed to bridge this gap through hardware-representative optimal control. The framework demonstrates API connectivity to IQM's Garnet quantum processor (20-qubit superconducting device) and implements a workflow that constructs a high-fidelity ``digital twin'' using hardware-representative parameters. Using this simulation framework, I demonstrate that GRAPE-optimized pulses achieve a simulated gate error reduction of 77$\times$ compared to standard Gaussian pulses. The framework's reliability is ensured through a 864-test verification suite (74\% code coverage) and adherence to NASA JPL Power-of-10 safety-critical coding standards, establishing a new paradigm for trustworthy quantum control software. All results are from verified GRAPE optimizations with full provenance documentation.

## 来自超对称Hamilton平方根的$\mathCal{PT}$类相转变

**Original Title:** $\mathcal{PT}$-like phase transitions from square roots of supersymmetric Hamiltonians

**Authors:** Jacob L. Barnett, Ramy El-Ganainy

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.12833v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.12833v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-16


### 摘要

我们引入了一个通用框架，用于在非埃尔米特系统中实现类似$\mathCal{PT}$的相转变，而无需强加显式的宇称时间（$\mathCal{PT}$）对称性。该方法基于将汉密尔顿量构造为能量移动了一个常数的超对称伙伴的平方根。这种公式自然会导致具有平衡损益的双方动力学，并且可以包含非互惠耦合。当相应的被动Hamilton缺乏零模时，最终的系统在有限参数范围内表现出完全真实的光谱。随着代表收益和损失的非埃尔米特参数的增加，频谱在二阶异常点经历受控的实到复转变。我们通过几个例子来展示这个框架的多功能性，包括众所周知的模型，例如Hatano--Nelson（HN）和复杂的Su--Schrieffer--Heeger（cSSH）格子。将形式主义扩展到$q$-交换矩阵进一步使得能够在具有单向耦合的系统中系统地实现更高级例外点。总的来说，这项工作揭示了非埃尔米特物理学和超对称性之间的新联系，提供了一条实用的途径来设计具有可调光谱特性的光学阵列，超出了显式$\mathcal{PT}$-对称性所能实现的范围。


### Abstract

We introduce a general framework for realizing $\mathcal{PT}$-like phase transitions in non-Hermitian systems without imposing explicit parity--time ($\mathcal{PT}$) symmetry. The approach is based on constructing a Hamiltonian as the square root of a supersymmetric partner energy-shifted by a constant. This formulation naturally leads to bipartite dynamics with balanced gain and loss and can incorporate non-reciprocal couplings. The resulting systems exhibit entirely real spectra over a finite parameter range precisely when the corresponding passive Hamiltonian lacks a zero mode. As the non-Hermitian parameter representing gain and loss increases, the spectrum undergoes controlled real-to-complex transitions at second-order exceptional points. We demonstrate the versatility of this framework through several examples, including well-known models such as the Hatano--Nelson (HN) and complex Su--Schrieffer--Heeger (cSSH) lattices. Extending the formalism to $q$-commuting matrices further enables the systematic realization of higher-order exceptional points in systems with unidirectional couplings. Overall, this work uncovers new links between non-Hermitian physics and supersymmetry, offering a practical route to engineer photonic arrays with tunable spectral properties beyond what is achievable with explicit $\mathcal{PT}$-symmetry.

## 量子状态断层扫描的近似消息传递

**Original Title:** Approximate Message Passing for Quantum State Tomography

**Authors:** Noah Siekierski, Kausthubh Chandramouli, Christian Kümmerle, Bojko N. Bakalov, Dror Baron

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.12857v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.12857v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-17


### 摘要

量子状态断层扫描（QST）是描述多体量子系统不可或缺的工具。然而，由于协议随着系统规模而呈指数级缩放成本，因此针对具有特定结构的量子状态（例如低阶状态）开发了许多方法。在本文中，我们展示了如何使用近似消息传递（MP）（一种压缩感知技术）来执行低等级QST。MP为大型系统提供渐进最优的性能保证，这表明其对QST的实用性。我们讨论了将AMP应用于QST的设计挑战，并表明通过正确设计AMP算法，与现有的低秩QST方法相比，我们可以将重建失真降低一个数量级以上。我们还进行了IBM金士顿的断层实验，并考虑了设备噪声的可靠性的预测保真度的状态准备的影响。我们的工作推进了低秩QST的状态，并可能适用于其他量子层析协议。


### Abstract

Quantum state tomography (QST) is an indispensable tool for characterizing many-body quantum systems. However, due to the exponential scaling cost of the protocol with system size, many approaches have been developed for quantum states with specific structure, such as low-rank states. In this paper, we show how approximate message passing (AMP), a compressed sensing technique, can be used to perform low-rank QST. AMP provides asymptotically optimal performance guarantees for large systems, which suggests its utility for QST. We discuss the design challenges that come with applying AMP to QST, and show that by properly designing the AMP algorithm, we can reduce the reconstruction infidelity by over an order of magnitude compared to existing approaches to low-rank QST. We also performed tomographic experiments on IBM Kingston and considered the effect of device noise on the reliability of the predicted fidelity of state preparation. Our work advances the state of low-rank QST and may be applicable to other quantum tomography protocols.

## 两个具有弱连接桥的任意图之间量子行走的脉动

**Original Title:** Pulsation of quantum walk between two arbitrary graphs with weakly connected bridge

**Authors:** Taisuke Hosaka, Etsuo Segawa

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.12872v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.12872v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-17


### 摘要

我们考虑有限图上的Grover行走，该图由两个由一条边连接的任意简单图组成，称为桥。桥上分配的参数$e>0$代表连接性的强度：如果$e =0$，那么图完全分离。我们表明，对于足够小的$e $值，就会出现一种称为脉动的现象。脉动的特征是量子行者在两个图之间的周期性转移。推导出在两个图中的任何一个上找到步行者的概率关于小$$的渐进式。这个公式表明，脉动仅取决于每个图中边的数量，无论它们的结构如何。此外，我们还得到了量子行者在两个图之间周期性地转移，周期为$O（e &{-1/2}）$。此外，当两个图的边数相等时，量子行者几乎完全转移。


### Abstract

We consider the Grover walk on a finite graph composed of two arbitrary simple graphs connected by one edge, referred to as a bridge. The parameter $ε>0$ assigned at the bridge represents the strength of connectivity: if $ε=0$, then the graph is completely separated. We show that for sufficiently small values of $ε$, a phenomenon called pulsation occurs. The pulsation is characterized by the periodic transfer of the quantum walker between the two graphs. An asymptotic expression with respect to small $ε$ for the probability of finding the walker on either of the two graphs is derived. This expression reveals that the pulsation depends solely on the number of edges in each graph, regardless of their structure. In addition, we obtain that the quantum walker is transferred periodically between the two graphs, with a period of order $O(ε^{-1/2})$. Furthermore, when the number of edges of two graphs is equal, the quantum walker is almost completely transferred.

## Floquet过热

**Original Title:** Floquet Superheating

**Authors:** Yang Hou, Andrea Pizzi, Huike Jin, Johannes Knolle, Roderich Moessner, Hongzheng Zhao

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.12877v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.12877v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-17


### 摘要

周期性驱动的多体系统通常会向无特征的“无限温度”状态加热。作为清洁系统中均匀加热的替代方案，我们在这里建立了Floquet过热机制，其中快速加热在由局部能量相对于适当有效Hamilton的罕见波动产生的“热点”处成核。引人注目的宏观后果包括异常长寿命的预热化和宏观可观测量的非历经双峰分布。过热是基于强烈取决于局部波动的升温率;在我们的例子中，这是由尖锐的状态选择性旋转回声提供的，其中能量吸收对于低能量状态被强烈抑制，而热波动则打开了过度的加热通道。发展了一个简单的唯象理论来表明临界液滴尺寸的存在，该尺寸结合了驱动场的加热以及液滴流出的热量。我们的结果揭示了一种新的加热机制，并提出了稳定驱动系统中物质非平衡相的新途径。


### Abstract

Periodically driven many-body systems generally heat towards a featureless 'infinite-temperature' state. As an alternative to uniform heating in a clean system, here we establish a Floquet superheating regime, where fast heating nucleates at ''hot spots" generated by rare fluctuations in the local energy with respect to an appropriate effective Hamiltonian. Striking macroscopic consequences include exceptionally long-lived prethermalization and non-ergodic bimodal distributions of macroscopic observables. Superheating is predicated on a heating rate depending strongly on the local fluctuation; in our example, this is supplied by a sharp state-selective spin-echo, where the energy absorption is strongly suppressed for low-energy states, while thermal fluctuations open up excessive heating channels. A simple phenomenological theory is developed to show the existence of a critical droplet size, which incorporates heating by the driving field as well as the heat current out of the droplet. Our results shine light on a new heating mechanism and suggest new routes towards stabilizing non-equilibrium phases of matter in driven systems.

## 基于对称测量的施密特数证人注释

**Original Title:** A note on Schmidt-number witnesses based on symmetric measurements

**Authors:** Xiao-Qian Mu, Hao-Fan Wang, Shao-Ming Fei

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.12887v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.12887v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-17


### 摘要

施密特数是量子纠缠的一种重要特征。具有较高施密特数的量子状态在各种量子信息处理任务中表现出显着优势。通过基于对称测量推导出一类k-正线性映射，我们给出了类（k + 1）的新施密特数见证人。通过详细的例子，我们表明我们的施密特数见证者可以更好地识别多维系统中量子状态的施密特数。此外，我们注意到，Fedorov比与纯高斯状态的施密特数一致，并在自发参数下转换等非高斯情况下提供了接近的逼近，可以作为验证所提出的（k +1）级施密特数证人的实验可用工具。


### Abstract

The Schmidt number is an important kind of characterization of quantum entanglement. Quantum states with higher Schmidt numbers demonstrate significant advantages in various quantum information processing tasks. By deriving a class of k-positive linear maps based on symmetric measurements, we present new Schmidt-number witnesses of class (k + 1). By detailed example, we show that our Schmidt number witnesses identify better the Schmidt number of quantum states in high-dimensional systems. Furthermore, we note that the Fedorov ratio, which coincides with the Schmidt number for pure Gaussian states and provides a close approximation in non-Gaussian cases such as spontaneous parametric down-conversion, serves as an experimentally accessible tool for validating the proposed (k +1)-class Schmidt-number witnesses.

## 快速量子多体状态合成

**Original Title:** Fast Quantum Many Body State Synthesis

**Authors:** Prashasti Tiwari, Dylan Lewis, Sougato Bose

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.12923v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.12923v1

**Authors:** Mikołaj Myszkowski, Mattia Damia Paciarini, Francesco Sannino

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.14547v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.14547v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-18


### 摘要

我们提出了量子力学系统，其中空间维度的数量被提升为动态量子变量。因此，有效维度取决于系统的物理状态。有趣的是，与固定维的系统相比，这种形式的系统表现出增强的对称性。作为一个明确的例子，我们分析了空间维度由量子操作符表示的谐振子。通过评估相应的配分函数，我们发现了与温度相关的有效维度。我们的框架为构建物理系统开辟了一条新的途径，从重力到凝聚物质，其中维度的概念本身就变成了量子。


### Abstract

We propose quantum-mechanical systems in which the number of spatial dimensions is promoted to a dynamical quantum variable. As a consequence, the effective dimension depends on the physical state of the system. Interestingly, systems of this form exhibit enhanced symmetries compared to their fixed-dimensional counterparts. As an explicit example, we analyze a harmonic oscillator for which the spatial dimension is represented by a quantum operator. By evaluating the corresponding partition function, we uncover a temperature-dependent effective dimension. Our framework opens a new avenue for constructing physical systems, from gravity to condensed matter, where the very notion of dimensionality becomes quantum.

## 真正量子姆彭巴效应的实验观察和应用

**Original Title:** Experimental observation and application of the genuine Quantum Mpemba Effect

**Authors:** Bruno P. Schnepper, Jefferson L. D. de Oliveira, Carlos H. S. Vieira, Krissia Zawadzki, Roberto M. Serra

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.14552v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.14552v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-18


### 摘要

相干性是一种固有的量子性质，它深刻影响微观过程，包括热化现象。一个引人注目的例子是量子Mpemba效应（QME），其中系统可以表现出异常松弛，从最初距离平衡较远的状态比从较近的状态热化更快。在这里，我们通过实验研究真正的QME，并观察与散热器相互作用的1/2旋转系统的动力学如何加速到平衡。此外，我们将QME应用于量子奥托冰箱中，从而提高其冷却能力。这项概念验证实验揭示了改进量子热任务的新实践路径。


### Abstract

Coherence is an inherently quantum property that deeply affects microscopic processes, including thermalization phenomena. A striking example is the quantum Mpemba effect (QME), in which a system can exhibit anomalous relaxation, thermalizing faster from a state initially farther from equilibrium than from one closer. Here, we experimentally investigate the genuine QME and observe how the dynamics of a spin-1/2 system interacting with a heat sink can be sped-up to equilibrium. Furthermore, we apply the QME in a quantum Otto refrigerator, thereby increasing its cooling power. This proof-of-concept experiment unveils new practical paths for improving quantum thermal tasks.

## 量子探测器断层扫描的后继下降方法

**Original Title:** Gradient-descent methods for quantum detector tomography

**Authors:** Amanuel Anteneh, Olivier Pfister

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.14579v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.14579v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-18


### 摘要

我们提出了一种用于对相不敏感量子探测器进行量子探测器断层扫描（QDT）的技术，使用基于梯度下降的优化来学习正操作者值测量（POVC），该测量最好地描述使用所研究的探测器收集的数据。我们针对约束凸优化（CCO）对我们的方法进行了数字基准测试，并表明即使在存在噪音和有限的探测器状态资源的情况下，它也能在更短的时间内达到更高或相当的重建保真度。我们还通过复Stiefel上的POVMs参数化，提出了对相敏感情况的方法的可能扩展，这使得基于梯度的优化仅限于此Manifel。


### Abstract

We present a technique for performing quantum detector tomography (QDT) of phase insensitive quantum detectors using gradient descent-based optimization to learn the positive operator-valued measure (POVM) that best describes the data collected using the detector under study. We numerically benchmark our method against constrained convex optimization (CCO) and show that it reaches higher or comparable reconstruction fidelity in much less time even in the presence of noise and limited probe state resources. We also present a possible extension of our approach to the phase sensitive case via a parametrization of POVMs on the complex Stiefel manifold which enables gradient based optimization restricted to this manifold.

## 瓦片码的逻辑算子与导自同构

**Original Title:** Logical Operators and Derived Automorphisms of Tile Codes

**Authors:** Nikolas P. Breuckmann, Shin Ho Choe, Jens Niklas Eberhardt, Francisco Revson Fernandes Pereira, Vincent Steffan

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.14589v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.14589v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-18


### 摘要

最近推出的磁贴代码是表面代码的一个有希望的替代方案，将二维局部性与更高的编码效率结合起来。虽然表面代码在其逻辑运算符和边界行为方面已经得到了很好的理解，但对磁贴代码的了解却要少得多。在这项工作中，我们建立了对其逻辑运算符空间的自然而精确的描述。我们证明，在温和的假设下，任何瓦片代码都承认沿着网格边界支持的逻辑运算符的典型辛基，它可以由简单的元胞自动机有效地生成，更新规则的数量仅取决于瓦片代码的非局部性。此外，我们开发了瓦片码的代数和代数几何框架，通过用变换不变的泡利稳定器模型来求解它们，并表明它们是作为$\mathbb {P}' s上Koszul复形的衍生部分出现的。' s。' s上的Koszul复形的衍生部分出现的。最后，我们引入量子码的派生自同射概念。这些都是类似自同质的操作，即使对于不具有对称性的代码也可以存在。我们解释了如何通过在一侧扩展网格并在另一侧缩小网格，以低开销和耐故障的方式为瓦片代码实现派生自同射。虽然这个操作对于表面代码来说很简单，但它在编码信息上引发了逻辑Cnot门的积。我们的结果为瓦片代码提供了新的结构见解，并为瓦片代码作为容错量子计算的构建模块奠定了基础。


### Abstract

The recently introduced tile codes are a promising alternative to surface codes, combining two-dimensional locality with higher encoding efficiency. While surface codes are well understood in terms of their logical operators and boundary behavior, much less is known about tile codes. In this work, we establish a natural and precise description of their logical operator space. We prove that, under mild assumptions, any tile code admits a canonical symplectic basis of logical operators supported along lattice boundaries, which can be generated efficiently by a simple cellular automaton with the number of update rules only depending on the non-locality of the tile code. Further, we develop algebraic and algebro-geometric frameworks for tile codes, by resolving them by translationally invariant Pauli stabilizer models and showing that they arise as derived sections of a Koszul complex on $\mathbb{P}^1 \times \mathbb{P}^1$. Finally, we introduce the concept of derived automorphisms for quantum codes. These are automorphism-like operations that can exist even for codes that do not have symmetries. We explain how derived automorphisms can be implemented for tile codes in a low-overhead and fault-tolerant manner by extending the lattice on one side and shrinking it on the other. While this operation is trivial for the surface code, it induces a product of logical CNOT gates on the encoded information. Our results provide new structural insights into tile codes and lay the groundwork for tile codes as building blocks for fault-tolerant quantum computation.

## 使用超出微扰极限的传输线谐振器测量无功负载阻抗

**Original Title:** Measuring Reactive-Load Impedance with Transmission-Line Resonators Beyond the Perturbative Limit

**Authors:** Xuanjing Chu, Jinho Park, Jesse Balgley, Sean Clemons, Ted S. Chung, Kenji Watanabe, Takashi Taniguchi, Leonardo Ranzani, Martin V. Gustafsson, Kin Chung Fong, James Hone

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.14621v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.14621v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-18


### 摘要

我们开发了一个分析框架，以从终止于无功负载的超导传输线谐振器中提取电路参数和损耗角切角，将分析扩展到微扰范围之外。该公式产生了共振频率、参与比和内部质量因子之间的封闭关系，无需全波模拟。我们通过范德华平行板电容器的电路模拟、有限元素建模和实验测量来验证该框架，并使用它来提取六方CBN的介电常数和损耗角切。多个参考谐振器的统计分析，加上多模式自校准，证明了电容和损耗角切的一致且可重复的提取，与文献值非常一致。除了参数提取外，分析关系还为最大限度地提高负载中的能量参与率并提高基于共振器的材料计量的精度提供了实用的设计指南。


### Abstract

We develop an analytic framework to extract circuit parameters and loss tangent from superconducting transmission-line resonators terminated by reactive loads, extending analysis beyond the perturbative regime. The formulation yields closed-form relations between resonant frequency, participation ratio, and internal quality factor, removing the need for full-wave simulations. We validate the framework through circuit simulations, finite-element modeling, and experimental measurements of van der Waals parallel-plate capacitors, using it to extract the dielectric constant and loss tangent of hexagonal boron nitride. Statistical analysis across multiple reference resonators, together with multimode self-calibration, demonstrates consistent and reproducible extraction of both capacitance and loss tangent in close agreement with literature values. In addition to parameter extraction, the analytic relations provide practical design guidelines for maximizing energy participation ratio in the load and improving the precision of resonator-based material metrology.

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.14628v1

### 摘要

我们为显式几何条件下的变分量子特征解算器提供了具有噪音稳健性、可能大约正确（PAC）的全局$\varepð$-最优性保证。对于具有有界生成元的周期性分析--在环形参数空间上产生全球Lipschitz成本景观--我们假设包含全球最小值的低能量区域是莫尔斯-博特子模，其正规Hessian的rank $r = O（\log p）$p $参数，并且满足关于坐标对齐的嵌入平面的多项纤维规则性。这种低弯曲维结构可以作为只有少数方向控制能量变化的机制的模型，并且与特定多尺度和束缚浅层结构中强参数与局部性联系在一起等机制一致。   在此假设下，找到置信度为1-δ $的$\varepð$-最优区域所需的样本复杂性与弯曲维度$r $而不是环境维度$p $成比例。该算法以至少$1-δ $的概率输出一个区域，其中所有点都是$\varepð$-最优的，并且至少有一个点位于全局最小值的有界附近。由此产生的复杂性在$p $和$\varepð^{-1}$中是准多项的，在$δ ^{-1}$中是log的。这识别了一种几何机制，在该机制中，尽管散粒噪音，高概率全局优化仍然可行。


### Abstract

We give noise-robust, Probably Approximately Correct (PAC) guarantees of global $\varepsilon$-optimality for the Variational Quantum Eigensolver under explicit geometric conditions. For periodic ansatzes with bounded generators -- yielding a globally Lipschitz cost landscape on a toroidal parameter space -- we assume that the low-energy region containing the global minimum is a Morse--Bott submanifold whose normal Hessian has rank $r = O(\log p)$ for $p$ parameters, and which satisfies polynomial fiber regularity with respect to coordinate-aligned, embedded flats. This low-curvature-dimensional structure serves as a model for regimes in which only a small number of directions control energy variation, and is consistent with mechanisms such as strong parameter tying together with locality in specific multiscale and tied shallow architectures.
  Under this assumption, the sample complexity required to find an $\varepsilon$-optimal region with confidence $1-δ$ scales with the curvature dimension $r$ rather than the ambient dimension $p$. With probability at least $1-δ$, the algorithm outputs a region in which all points are $\varepsilon$-optimal, and at least one lies within a bounded neighborhood of the global minimum. The resulting complexity is quasi-polynomial in $p$ and $\varepsilon^{-1}$ and logarithmic in $δ^{-1}$. This identifies a geometric regime in which high-probability global optimization remains feasible despite shot noise.

## 基于Divide-et-impera启发式的量子比特路由问题随机搜索

**Original Title:** Divide-et-impera Heuristic-based Randomized Search for the Qubit Routing Problem

**Authors:** Marco Baioletti, Fabrizio Fagiolo, Angelo Oddi, Riccardo Rasconi

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.14644v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.14644v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-18


### 摘要

本文介绍了解决量子位路由问题（QRP）的DIRSH算法，使用启发式随机分治策略。该方法将电路分成块，并通过随机选择门和交换来优化每个块。它通过重新启动和对bandt参数的自适应调整以及深度敏感的本地修剪来平衡全局搜索。在映射到20量子位IBMQ东京布局的RevLib基准测试上进行测试，DIRSH在不同时间预算下的表现优于三个LightSABRE变体，实现了更短的深度和更少的交换。这些结果证实，将基于块的分解与带宽驱动启发式方法相结合对于在NISQ设备上路由量子电路是有效的。


### Abstract

This paper introduces the DIRSH algorithm for the Qubit Routing Problem (QRP), using a heuristic-guided randomized divide-and-conquer strategy. The method splits the circuit into chunks and optimizes each one with a stochastic selection of gates and swaps. It balances global search, via restarts and adaptive tuning of bandit parameters with depth-sensitive local pruning. Tested on RevLib benchmarks mapped to the 20-qubit IBMQ Tokyo topology, DIRSH outperformed three LightSABRE variants across different time budgets, achieving shorter depths and fewer swaps. These results confirm that combining chunk-based decomposition with bandit-driven heuristics is effective for routing quantum circuits on NISQ devices.

## 使用施密特分解确定量子纠缠

**Original Title:** Using the Schmidt Decomposition to Determine Quantum Entanglement

**Authors:** Lane Boswell, Ying Cao

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.14648v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.14648v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-18


### 摘要

量子信息论是数学和物理学中一个快速发展的领域，结合了量子力学和信息论这两个独立的理论。量子纠缠是最早在EPR悖论中提出的一个概念。在量子力学中，粒子可以处于叠加状态，这意味着它们同时处于多个不同的状态。直到粒子被测量后，它才会被迫进入单一状态。然而，粒子也有可能与其他粒子联系在一起，这意味着一个粒子的测量将决定另一个粒子的测量。纠缠是量子信息理论的核心。它是允许计算能力大幅提高的核心部分之一。在本文中，我们决定重点展示用于确定系统是否纠缠的数学方法（施密特分解），以及量子纠缠的用途（量子隐形传输）的演示，以及如何扩展施密特分解的用途。


### Abstract

Quantum information theory is a rapidly growing area of math and physics that combines two independent theories, quantum mechanics and information theory. Quantum entanglement is a concept that was first proposed in the EPR paradox. In quantum mechanics, particles can be in superposition, meaning they are in multiple different states at once. It is not until the particle is measured that it is forced into a single state. However, it is possible that particles can be tied to other particles, meaning that the measurement of one particle will determine the measurement of the other particle. Entanglement is at the very core of quantum information theory. It is one of the core pieces that allows for the massive increase in computing power. For this paper, we decided to focus on demonstrating the mathematical method (the Schmidt decomposition) for determining if a system is entangled, and a demonstration of quantum entanglement's use (quantum teleportation) as well as a quick look at how to extend the uses of the Schmidt decomposition.
