## 相驱动Andreev束缚态量子电池中的相互作用增强的功磁效应

**Original Title:** Interaction-Enhanced Ergotropy in Phase-Driven Andreev Bound State Quantum Batteries

**Authors:** Disha Verma, B. Vigneshwar, R. Sankaranarayanan

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.24456v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.24456v2

**Submitted:** 2026-06-23


### 摘要

我们研究了由两个相互作用的安德烈夫束缚态（ABS）单元组成的相驱动量子电池，为相干能量存储提供了最小的超导体平台。通过分析超相斜坡下的各向异性动力学，我们表明避免的交叉激发和相互作用诱导的杂交之间的相互作用强烈地改变了充电过程。在与石墨烯SEN结相关的高透明度区域中，相互作用增强了存储的可提取功，并产生与耦合ABS扇形之间的一致重新分配相关的明显振荡充电动力学。相分辨的演变进一步揭示了约瑟夫森循环期间的最佳充电窗口，表明通过部分循环操作实现相可编程能量提取的可能性。总体而言，我们的结果将相互作用辅助避免交叉动力学确定为超导量子电池中可控能量存储的微观机制。


### Abstract

We investigate a phase-driven quantum battery composed of two interacting Andreev bound state (ABS) units, providing a minimal superconducting platform for coherent energy storage. By analyzing the ergotropy dynamics under a superconducting phase ramp, we show that the interplay between avoided-crossing excitation and interaction-induced hybridization strongly modifies the charging process. In the high-transparency regime relevant for graphene SNS junctions, the interaction enhances the stored extractable work and generates pronounced oscillatory charging dynamics associated with coherent redistribution between coupled ABS sectors. The phase-resolved evolution further reveals optimal charging windows during the Josephson cycle, indicating the possibility of phase-programmable energy extraction through partial-cycle operation. Overall, our results identify interaction-assisted avoided-crossing dynamics as a microscopic mechanism for controllable energy storage in superconducting quantum batteries.

## 具有采样反馈的消散量子乘权：通过工程开放系统动力学实现的经典硬基元

**Original Title:** Dissipative Quantum Multiplicative Weights with Sampling Feedback: A Classically Hard Primitive Realized via Engineered Open-System Dynamics

**Authors:** Agung Trisetyarso, Lenny Putri Yulianti, Kridanto Surendro

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.26162v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.26162v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-24


### 摘要

我们引入了\{具有采样反馈的消散量子乘权}（DQMW-Sample），这是一种在线学习基元，其中工程化的开放量子系统动力学准备了吉布斯状态，其计算基础测量提供了损失反馈。核心概念贡献是将恒温Gibbs采样的计算难度提升为物理上可实现的在线学习原始值。通过设计一个每轮反馈无法有效地经典模拟的戴维斯型相关器，我们获得了学习理论分离，其中DQMW-Sample实现渐进次线性遗憾，而每个高效的经典学习器在适当构建的实例上遭受恒定的平均遗憾。我们进一步证明，工程脉动器的频谱间隙会收缩硬件噪音，在平衡的消散时间表下产生次线性噪音引起的遗憾，并且我们加强了单轮对完全自适应交互的硬度：整个$T$轮反馈过程的高效经典模拟器将崩溃多元多元体系。我们以显式的形式陈述了所需的可实现性假设，并报告了IBM Heron~r2处理器上的初始硬件特性。这些结果将DQMW-Sample定位为在线学习中实现计算优势的具体途径，该学习基于复杂性理论并与近期的高温硬件兼容。


### Abstract

We introduce \emph{Dissipative Quantum Multiplicative Weights with Sampling Feedback} (DQMW-Sample), an online-learning primitive in which engineered open quantum-system dynamics prepare a Gibbs state whose computational-basis measurement supplies the loss feedback. The central conceptual contribution is to lift the computational hardness of constant-temperature Gibbs sampling into a physically realizable online-learning primitive. By engineering a Davies-type dissipator whose per-round feedback cannot be efficiently simulated classically, we obtain a learning-theoretic separation in which DQMW-Sample achieves asymptotically sublinear regret while every efficient classical learner suffers constant average regret on a suitably constructed instance. We further prove that the spectral gap of the engineered dissipator contracts hardware noise, yielding sublinear noise-induced regret under a balanced dissipation schedule, and we strengthen the single-round hardness to the full adaptive interaction: an efficient classical simulator of the entire $T$-round feedback process would collapse the polynomial hierarchy. We state the required realizability assumption in explicit form and report an initial hardware characterization on the IBM Heron~r2 processor. These results position DQMW-Sample as a concrete route toward computational advantage in online learning that is grounded in complexity theory and compatible with near-term superconducting hardware.

## 接近海森堡标度的量子位辅助量子计量准则

**Original Title:** Criterion for qubit-assisted quantum metrology approaching Heisenberg scaling

**Authors:** Peng Chen, Jun Jing

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.26167v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.26167v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-24


### 摘要

在这项工作中，我们研究了耦合到辅助量子位的探针系统的计量精度。限制探针-量子比特的耦合仅沿一个或两个方向被发现是一个足够的标准，有效的动力学发电机，以达到海森堡极限的精度。在该准则下，待估计参数的量子Fisher信息（QFI）成为组合系统有效生成元相对于初始状态的均方期望值.我们的标准在两个不同的系统中是合理的。对于玻色子探头，关于位移估计的QFI被发现与探头的平均激励次数成正比。这导致了一个违反直觉的结果，即可以通过增加探针系统的温度来增强量子计量灵敏度。对于旋转系综探针，关于旋转相和磁场估计的QFI表现出对探针旋转数的二次依赖性。研究发现，即使将旋转系综准备为有限温度状态，也远离所谓的资源状态，例如，压缩状态或格林伯格-霍恩-蔡林格状态，QFI仍然可以表现出海森堡标度行为。


### Abstract

We study in this work the metrology precision of a probe system coupled to an ancillary qubit. Restricting the probe-qubit coupling along only one or two directions is found to be a sufficient criterion for the effective dynamical generator to achieve the Heisenberg limit in precision. Under the criterion, the quantum Fisher information (QFI) about the to-be-estimated parameter becomes the expectation value of mean square of the effective generator with respect to the initial state of the composite system. Our criterion is justified in two distinct systems. For a bosonic probe, QFI about the displacement estimation is found to be proportional to the mean excitation number of probe. It renders a counterintuitive result that quantum metrology sensitivity can be enhanced by increasing the temperature of the probe system. For a spin-ensemble probe, QFI about both rotation-phase and magnetic-field estimation exhibit a quadratic dependence on the probe-spin number. It is found that even when the spin-ensemble is prepared as a finite-temperature state, faraway from the so-called resource states, e.g., the squeezed state or the Greenberger-Horne-Zeilinger state, QFI can still manifest a Heisenberg scaling behavior.

## 自主热力学网络中基于规则的可解释学习

**Original Title:** Interpretable rule-based learning in an autonomous thermodynamic network

**Authors:** Sparrow Suderman, Giulia Rubino

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.26220v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.26220v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-24


### 摘要

机器学习通常用确定性逻辑操作来描述，而物理系统通常在存在噪音、消散和不可逆性的情况下运行。在这里，我们将这些物理效应转化为自主、可解释的学习架构的计算资源。我们开发了一个基于热力学神经元的分类器，热力学神经元是通过热流动实现逻辑操作的自主量子热机，并使用这些来构建Tsetlin机的随机版本，这是一种可解释的基于规则的学习架构。通过将热力学AND、Not和OR门与自主耦合机制相结合，我们实现了一个学习系统，其计算无需外部时间相关控制即可展开。尽管其成分有噪音，但最终的分类器实现了在统计上与标准Tsetlin机器相当的分类准确性。可靠性源于阈值和冗余等架构机制，而不是精确的逻辑操作。我们的结果强调，准确且可解释的学习可以从自主随机动力学中产生，并将热力学计算建立为物理机器学习的可行框架。


### Abstract

Machine learning is typically described in terms of deterministic logical operations, whereas physical systems generally operate in the presence of noise, dissipation and irreversibility. Here, we turn these physical effects into computational resources for an autonomous, interpretable learning architecture. We develop a classifier based on thermodynamic neurons, which are autonomous quantum thermal machines that implement logical operations through heat flow, and use these to construct a stochastic version of the Tsetlin machine, an interpretable rule-based learning architecture. By combining thermodynamic AND, NOT and OR gates with an autonomous coupling mechanism, we realise a learning system whose computation unfolds without the need for external time-dependent control. Despite its noisy components, the resulting classifier achieves classification accuracy that is statistically comparable to that of the standard Tsetlin machine. Reliability arises from architectural mechanisms such as thresholding and redundancy, rather than exact logical operations. Our results highlight that accurate and interpretable learning can emerge from autonomous stochastic dynamics, and establish thermodynamic computation as a viable framework for physical machine learning.

**Original Title:** Toric code made subsystem: a framework for topological subsystem codes using anticommuting quantum spin liquids

**Authors:** Vaibhav Sharma, Sumiran Pujari

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.26226v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.26226v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-24


### 摘要

我们引入了一个基于[Phys. Rev. B 113，064402（2026）]中描述的反对易量子旋转液体类构建拓扑子系统代码的框架。此类中的规范模型可以被认为是复曲面码的空间修改，其稳定器码属性无效。相反，这些模型包含一组广泛的反对易局部保守操作符，从而导致广泛的接地状态简并。这种简并性构成了相关量子错误纠正代码中子系统自由度的基础。该代码继承了量子旋转液体的多体拓扑序，使其成为一个拓扑子系统代码。我们给出了两个具体而详细的例子，用于在方形网格和kagome网格几何上构建这些代码，分别需要权重4和权重3的局部检查操作符测量。与其他子系统代码相比，这些代码的一个独特性质是存在大量的局部规范量子位，除了逻辑量子位之外，这些量子位不受检查运算符的干扰。我们的结构提供了一个模板，用于在不同的网格或图形几何上生成这类新的拓扑子系统代码，适合在各种量子硬件平台上实现。


### Abstract

We introduce a framework of constructing topological subsystem codes based on the class of anticommuting quantum spin liquids described in [Phys. Rev. B 113, 064402 (2026)]. A canonical model from this class can be considered as a spatial modification of the toric code that voids its stabilizer code property. Rather, these models contain an extensive set of anticommuting local conserved operators that lead to an extensive ground state degeneracy. This degeneracy forms the basis of the subsystem degrees of freedom in the associated quantum error correcting code. The code inherits the many-body topological order of the quantum spin liquid, making it a topological subsystem code. We present two concrete and detailed examples for constructing these codes on a square lattice and a kagome lattice geometry, requiring weight-4 and weight-3 local check operator measurements respectively. In contrast to other subsystem codes, a unique property of these codes is the presence of an extensive number of local gauge qubits that are left undisturbed by the check operators apart from the logical qubits. Our construction provides a template for generating this new category of topological subsystem codes on different lattice or graph geometries, suitable for implementation on various quantum hardware platforms.

## 以极端尺度量子蒙特卡罗模拟为基准的量子退变保真度指标

**Original Title:** A fidelity metric for quantum annealing benchmarked by extreme scaling quantum Monte-Carlo simulations

**Authors:** Gabriel Gouraud, Miha Srdinsek, Xavier Waintal

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.26233v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.26233v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-24


### 摘要

量子退热器应该以热力学方式遵循系统的底薪，因为系统的Hamilton量在平凡相和非平凡相之间缓慢内插;非平凡的底薪是优化问题的解决方案。绝大多数情况下，它们的性能是根据优化问题解决的好坏或速度来衡量的。虽然务实，但这种方法本质上很脆弱，因为它强烈依赖于所考虑的问题和用作参考基准的经典算法。在这里，我们提出了一个量，不仅衡量最终结果，还衡量实际量子退场过程本身的质量。我们的指标是基于门的量子计算机的每门平均数的量子退变对应物。它的形式是退火炉状态方程的精确度$e $。我们使用里德堡原子系统模拟量子退变技术的两种变体计算$的基准值。我们的第一种方法使用变分量子蒙特卡罗，并采用受热退变启发的类比。它表明，在$e\sim 10 '-10 ' s范围内，量子退火炉与其热经典对应物没有区别。至关重要的是，我们可以在单个中央处理器上达到高达100，000，000美元原子的精确度。我们的第二种方法（基于格林函数量子蒙特卡罗）的准确度约为$e\sim 10 '{-4}$，并且我们已经将其运行到高达100，000美元的原子。这些结果在精度和尺寸上都优于当前的里德伯原子量子退变实验平台，并对未来的硬件提出了严格的限制。


### Abstract

Quantum annealers are supposed to follow adiabatically the ground state of a system as its Hamiltonian slowly interpolates between a trivial phase and a non-trivial one; the non-trivial ground state being the solution to an optimization problem. Overwhelmingly, their performances are measured in terms of how well or fast the optimization problem is solved. While pragmatic, this approach is inherently brittle as it strongly depends on the problem considered and the classical algorithm used as the reference benchmark. Here, we propose a quantity that not only measures the end result but also the quality of the actual quantum annealing process itself. Our metric is the quantum annealing counterpart of the fidelity-per gate of gate-based quantum computers. It takes the form of an accuracy $ε$ for the equation of state of the annealer. We calculate benchmark values of $ε$ using two variants of the simulated quantum annealing technique for Rydberg atoms systems. Our first approach uses variational quantum Monte-Carlo with an ansatz inspired by thermal annealing. It suggests that within $ε\sim 10^{-2}-10^{-3}$, a quantum annealer is indistinguishable from its thermal classical counterpart. Critically, we could reach this precision up to $100,000,000$ atoms on a single CPU. Our second approach (based on Green function quantum Monte-Carlo) reaches accuracies around $ε\sim 10^{-4}$ and we have run it up to $100,000$ atoms. These results outperform current Rydberg atom quantum annealing experimental platforms in both precision and size by orders of magnitude and put severe constraints for future hardware.

## 混合状态布局阶段的手征性度量

**Original Title:** Measures of Chirality in Mixed-State Topological Phases

**Authors:** Shijun Sun, Bader Aldossari, Rasmit Devkota, Zhu-Xi Luo

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.26235v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.26235v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-24


### 摘要

混合态拓扑相是手性的意味着什么？在数学上，手征性可以通过混合态的对称代数来精确地表征。然而，身体上的问题要微妙得多。在纯态中，拓扑相位中的手征性与熟悉的诊断网络联系在一起，包括体边界对应、无间隙纠缠谱、非平凡的模块化换向器和量化的热霍尔响应。我们表明，这些诊断仍然是可靠的混合状态。相反，对于一个已知的无错误的父状态的decocerant拓扑相位，我们提出了两个相对熵为基础的措施，可以诊断手性，其中一个进一步提取手性中心电荷。我们的研究结果强调了混合状态拓扑结构的需求本质上是新的诊断超越纯状态探针的直接模拟。


### Abstract

What does it mean for a mixed-state topological phase to be chiral? Mathematically, chirality can be sharply characterized through the symmetry algebra of the mixed state. Physically, however, the question is far more subtle. In pure states, chirality in topological phase is tied to a web of familiar diagnostics, involving bulk-boundary correspondence, a gapless entanglement spectrum, a nontrivial modular commutator, and a quantized thermal Hall response. We show that none of these diagnostics remain reliable in mixed states. Instead, for decohered topological phases with a known error-free parent state, we propose two relative-entropy-based measures that can diagnose chirality, with one of them further extracting the chiral central charge. Our results emphasize how mixed-state topology demands intrinsically new diagnostics beyond direct analogues of pure-state probes.

## 排列不对称性在随机Bell检验中释放涌现优势

**Original Title:** Permutation asymmetry unlocks emergent advantage in randomized Bell tests

**Authors:** Wieslaw Laskowski, Tamas Vertesi, Jan Wojcik

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.26242v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.26242v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-24


### 摘要

在一致随机投影测量下，所有最大纠缠的两量子位状态都以相同的概率违反局部实在论，但它们在随机贝尔测试中的表现不需要完全相同。我们表明，当序列贝尔实验中各方之间交换测量设置时，共享状态的排列对称性决定了两个实现之间的统计关系。置换不变状态在两个实验中产生相同的非局部结果，而不对称状态在一种实现中可能违反局部实在论，但在另一种实现中则不会。这种区别会导致两个操作后果。首先，它能够通过联合违规统计来检测Alice和Bob的测量选择之间的相关性。其次，在有限测量池的贝尔测试中，不对称最大纠缠态可以显着增加观察到非定域性的概率，而不需要额外的资源。我们的结果将排列不对称性确定为随机贝尔实验中的一个有用特征，并强调了对称性在量子非定域中的新作用。


### Abstract

All maximally entangled two-qubit states violate local realism with the same probability under uniformly random projective measurements, yet they need not behave identically in randomized Bell tests. We show that when measurement settings are exchanged between the parties in sequential Bell experiments, permutation symmetry of the shared state determines the statistical relation between the two realizations. Permutationally invariant states yield identical nonlocality outcomes in both experiments, whereas asymmetric states can violate local realism in one realization but not in the other. This distinction leads to two operational consequences. First, it enables the detection of correlations between the measurement choices of Alice and Bob through the joint violation statistics. Second, in Bell tests with finite measurement pools, asymmetric maximally entangled states can significantly increase the probability of observing nonlocality without requiring additional resources. Our results identify permutation asymmetry as a useful feature in randomized Bell experiments and highlight a new role of symmetry in quantum nonlocality.

## 量子增强传感的最佳可观测量及其在Floquet时间晶体传感器中的应用

**Original Title:** Optimal observables for quantum-enhanced sensing and applications in a Floquet time crystal sensor

**Authors:** M. A. Manya, Andrei Tsypilnikov, Fernando Iemini

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.26248v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.26248v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-24


### 摘要

在这项工作中，我们讨论了如何为饱和量子费舍尔信息（QFI）界限的计量协议确定和实现可行的最佳可观测量。特别是，我们的研究重点是一个简单的协议，即矩量法（MoM）。我们首先证明对称对数求导（SLD）操作符（Hermitian Observer）一旦在MoM中实现，就饱和了QFI界限。然而，SLD通常过于复杂，并且通常是非本地的，使得其直接实验实现不可行。为了克服这一限制，我们在特定的传感模型中探索了它的结构--充当交流场传感器的Floquet时间晶体（FTC）--并表明SLD可以通过相当简单的可观测量来逼近，例如裸旋转磁性或宇称可观测值，用于不同的相关初始状态准备。我们在作为FTC传感器运行的核磁共振系统中进一步证实了我们的理论预测，采用实验激励参数来模拟其在最先进的实现中的性能。总的来说，我们的结果建立了一条在FTC传感器中实现近乎最佳计量的实用途径，其中不可访问的SLD操作符可以被更简单的可观测值取代，同时保留量子增强的灵敏度。


### Abstract

In this work, we discuss how to determine and implement feasible optimal observables for a metrology protocol that saturates the quantum Fisher information (QFI) bound. In particular, we focus our study on a simple protocol, namely the method of moments (MoM). We first demonstrate that the symmetric logarithmic derivative (SLD) operator, a Hermitian observable, once implemented in the MoM, saturates the QFI bound. However, the SLD is generally too complex and typically non-local, rendering its direct experimental realization unfeasible. To overcome this limitation, we explore its structure in a specific sensing model - a Floquet time crystal (FTC) acting as an ac field sensor - and show that the SLD can be approximated by substantially simpler observables, such as the bare spin magnetization or a parity observable, for different relevant initial state preparations. We further corroborate our theoretical predictions in a nuclear magnetic resonance system operating as an FTC sensor, employing experimentally motivated parameters to simulate its performance in a state-of-the-art implementation. In general, our results establish a practical route toward near-optimal metrology in FTC sensors, where the inaccessible SLD operator can be replaced by simpler observables while retaining quantum-enhanced sensitivity.
