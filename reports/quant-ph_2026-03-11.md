## 学习检测光学非经典性

**Original Title:** Learning to detect optical nonclassicality

**Authors:** Martina Jung, Suchitra Krishnaswamy, Timon Schapeler, Annabelle Bohrdt, Tim J. Bartley, Jan Sperling, Martin Gärttner

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06319v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.06319v2

**Submitted:** 2026-03-06


### 摘要

从量子光学意义上定义的非经典性是基于光的量子技术的资源。因此，证明量子状态的非经典性对于衡量其量子优势的潜力至关重要。然而，假设完全了解证人可观测值的传统非古典证人在统计数据和有限分辨率的量子探测器的现实场景中往往会失败。此外，这些证人并没有利用这样一个事实：某些状态在特定实验中不太可能被观察到。在这里，我们训练了一个变分模型，以使用多个用不同的光数分辨检测方案探测的多个多模式量子状态的测量样本来区分经典状态和非经典状态。然后，学习到的决策规则是非经典性的指标，专为一组给定的物理相关状态量身定制。我们的方法既是数据驱动的，又是可解释的，因为可以提取学到的分析决策规则。根据用（i）超纳米线单量子探测器和（ii）时间点多路传输检测方案测量的实验数据训练模型，证明了该方法的通用性，为高效的非经典性检测铺平了道路。


### Abstract

Nonclassicality, defined in the quantum optical sense, serves as a resource for photon-based quantum technologies. Therefore, certifying the nonclassicality of a quantum state is crucial for gauging its potential for quantum advantage. However, traditional nonclassicality witnesses that assume perfect knowledge of the witness observables often fail in realistic scenarios with limited statistics and finite-resolution photon detectors. Furthermore, these witnesses do not exploit the fact that certain states are unlikely to be observed in a given experiment. Here, we train a variational model to distinguish classical from nonclassical states using finitely many measurement samples of multimode quantum states that are probed with different photon-number-resolving detection schemes. The learned decision rule is then an indicator of nonclassicality, tailored to a given set of physically relevant states. Our approach is both data-driven and interpretable in the sense that the learned analytical decision rule can be extracted. Training the model on experimental data measured with (i) a superconducting nanowire single-photon detector and (ii) a time-bin multiplexing detection scheme demonstrates the versatility of the approach, paving the way for efficient nonclassicality detection.

## 对于分子极化子来说，无序和音素时间尺度控制着热力学极限下暗态的激活

**Original Title:** For molecular polaritons, disorder and phonon timescales control the activation of dark states in the thermodynamic limit

**Authors:** Tianchu Li, Pranay Venkatesh, Qiang Shi, Andrés Montoya-Castillo

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06868v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.06868v2

**Submitted:** 2026-03-06


### 摘要

集体光物质系统拥有广泛多样的暗态，其在热力学行为出现中的作用仍然知之甚少，特别是在存在无序和结构化环境的情况下。在这里，我们开发了一个混合矩阵产品状态层次运动方程（MPS-HEOM）的方法，使数值精确模拟的极化激元动力学从几个发射器的热力学极限下的静态和动态无序。这使我们能够，第一次，提供一个定量和操作的答案，长期存在的问题，什么是所需的最小系统尺寸达到热力学极限集体极化激子系统。通过引入收敛尺度N_{T}$，根据光动力学达到热力学极限所需的分子数量，我们表明动态无序通常比静态无序构成更大的计算挑战。我们将这种行为归因于无序对集体光物质动力学的抑制，从而动态地激活了非集体自由度。我们进一步发现，随着浴变得更加马尔科夫，$N_{T}$表现出周转行为，因为浴时间尺度调节了亮到暗的能量转移以及暗状态和灰色状态的参与。因此，音素时间尺度既控制着集体行为的崩溃，也控制着$N_{T}$的增长。我们的结果确立了集体行为的抑制是无序光物质系统中控制热力学收敛的关键机制。


### Abstract

Collective light-matter systems host an extensive manifold of dark states whose role in the emergence of thermodynamic behavior remains poorly understood, especially in the presence of disorder and structured environments. Here, we develop a hybrid matrix product state-hierarchical equations of motion (MPS-HEOM) approach that enables numerically exact simulations of polariton dynamics from a few emitters to the thermodynamic limit under both static and dynamic disorder. This allows us, for the first time, to provide a quantitative and operational answer to the long-standing question of what is the minimum system size required to reach the thermodynamic limit in collective polaritonic systems. By introducing a convergence scale, $N_{T}$, i.e., the number of molecules required for the photonic dynamics to reach the thermodynamic limit, we show that dynamic disorder generally poses a greater computational challenge than static disorder. We attribute this behavior to the suppression of collective light-matter dynamics by disorder, which dynamically activates non-collective degrees of freedom. We further find that $N_{T}$ exhibits a turnover behavior as the bath becomes more Markovian, as the bath timescales regulate bright-to-dark energy transfer and the involvement of dark and gray states. Hence, phonon timescales control both the breakdown of collective behavior and the growth of $N_{T}$. Our results establish the suppression of collective behavior as the key mechanism governing thermodynamic convergence in disordered light-matter systems.

## d=3保形场论的量子位离散化

**Original Title:** Qubit discretizations of d=3 conformal field theories

**Authors:** Hansen S. Wu, Ribhu K. Kaul

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.07420v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.07420v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-08


### 摘要

我们提出d=3共形场论的标度维数可以在量子比特系统上用近期量子模拟平台进行研究。我们的建议选择耦合的量子多体问题的多面体晶格上的共形态算符对应可以观察到最准确的频谱。我们验证了我们的协议的伊辛模型，我们提取的标度尺寸的一些标度算子的百分之几的准确性从一个系统的光谱只有20量子位。这个过程只做了一般共形不变性以外的小假设--因此它可以被广泛应用。讨论了在量子计算机上实现这一提议的要求和挑战。我们的结果表明，对于当前或近期的量子位平台来说，三维共形场论提供了一个独特的机会--这是一个在经典计算机上很困难但可以通过量子模拟来解决的前沿问题。


### Abstract

We propose that scaling dimensions of d=3 conformal field theories can be studied on a system of qubits with near term quantum simulation platforms. Our proposal chooses couplings of quantum many-body problems on a polyhedral lattice at which the conformal state-operator correspondence can be observed most accurately in the spectrum. We validate our protocol on the Ising model where we extract the scaling dimensions of a number of scaling operators with a few percent accuracy from the spectrum of a system of just 20 qubits. The procedure makes only minor assumptions beyond general conformal invariance -- it may hence be applied widely. Requirements and challenges to realize this proposal on quantum computers are discussed. Our results demonstrate that for current or near term qubit platforms, three dimensional conformal field theories present a unique opportunity -- a forefront problem that is difficult on classical computers but may be solved through quantum simulation.

## 正式启动具有1，000+个量子位的量子相估计电路

**Original Title:** Formally Verifying Quantum Phase Estimation Circuits with 1,000+ Qubits

**Authors:** Arun Govindankutty, Sudarshan K. Srinivasan

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.08762v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.08762v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-09


### 摘要

我们提出了一种用于量子阶段估计（QPE）电路的可扩展形式验证方法。我们的方法使用基于无量化器的位向逻辑的符号量子位抽象，捕捉关键的量子现象，包括叠加、旋转和测量。所提出的方法将量子电路功能行为从希尔伯特空间映射到位向域。我们开发与此抽象一致的形式属性，以确保QPE电路的功能正确性。该方法可高效扩展，使用不到3.5~GB的存储器验证具有高达6个精度量子位和1，024个相量子位的QPE电路。


### Abstract

We present a scalable formal verification methodology for Quantum Phase Estimation (QPE) circuits. Our approach uses a symbolic qubit abstraction based on quantifier-free bit-vector logic, capturing key quantum phenomena, including superposition, rotation, and measurement. The proposed methodology maps quantum circuit functional behaviour from Hilbert space to a bit-vector domain. We develop formal properties aligned with this abstraction to ensure functional correctness of QPE circuits. The method scales efficiently, verifying QPE circuits with up to 6 precision qubits and 1,024 phase qubits using under 3.5~GB of memory.

## 局部自由度和非局部自由度分离的三量子位纯状态的可视化

**Original Title:** Visualization of Three-Qubit Pure States with Separation of Local and Nonlocal Degrees of Freedom

**Authors:** Satoru Shoji

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.07942v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.07942v2

**Submitted:** 2026-03-09


### 摘要

了解多量子位量子状态的结构对于量子信息研究和教育都至关重要，但单量子位布洛赫球体之外的直观可视化仍然具有挑战性。在这项工作中，我们提出了一个统一的几何框架，用于可视化两个和三个量子位纯状态，其中局部自由度和纠缠自由度被明确分离。对于两个量子位，我们将约化密度操作符的布洛赫球表示与在复平面上绘制的复并发相结合，从而能够同时可视化纠缠强度和相结构。对于三个量子位，在广义施密特分解的基础上，我们引入了二部和GZZ型三重复并发，它们与局部布洛赫载体一起提供了状态的完整坐标表示。与基于分类的方法不同，我们的方法专注于表示给定的具体状态，揭示局部属性和非局部相关性如何共存。该框架区分了纠缠幅度相同但干扰结构不同的状态，并直观地洞察了成对纠缠和真正三重纠缠之间的平衡。这种方法既清晰了概念，又在量子教育和状态分析中的潜在应用。


### Abstract

Understanding the structure of multi-qubit quantum states is essential for both quantum information research and education, yet intuitive visualization beyond the single-qubit Bloch sphere remains challenging. In this work, we propose a unified geometric framework for visualizing two- and three-qubit pure states in which local degrees of freedom and entanglement degrees of freedom are explicitly separated. For two qubits, we combine Bloch-sphere representations of reduced density operators with a complex concurrence plotted on the complex plane, enabling simultaneous visualization of entanglement strength and phase structure. For three qubits, building on the generalized Schmidt decomposition, we introduce bipartite and GHZ-type tripartite complex concurrences, which, together with local Bloch vectors, provide a complete coordinate representation of the state. Unlike classification-based approaches, our method focuses on representing a given concrete state, revealing how local properties and nonlocal correlations coexist. The framework distinguishes states with identical entanglement magnitudes but different interference structures and provides intuitive insight into the balance between pairwise and genuinely tripartite entanglement. This approach offers both conceptual clarity and potential applications in quantum education and state analysis.

## 有限资源下量子传感的现实框架

**Original Title:** A Realistic Framework for Quantum Sensing under Finite Resources

**Authors:** Zdeněk Hradil, Jaroslav Řeháček

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.08306v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.08306v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-09


### 摘要

量子增强传感通常使用量子费舍尔信息（QFI）进行基准测试，该信息通常被解释为可实现精度的直接指标。然而，这个量只有在完全指定的推理框架内才具有操作意义，该框架一致地结合了状态准备、测量设计、资源核算、估计器构建、先验信息和有限数据效应。在这里，我们在有限资源下为量子传感建立了一个现实的端到端框架，并确定了具有运营意义的性能评估所需的一般原则。一个核心概念点是，相关估计单位不是单个检测事件，而是构建一致估计器所需的推断数据集。我们将这种方法应用于文献中经常引用的几种范式感知策略。在相等的总量子资源下，在Bayesian框架内重新审视了NOON状态的阶段估计，我们显式地构建了最佳估计器，并表明此类方案与重复经典干涉测量术相比，对于有限先验宽度的全球阶段估计，没有提供任何性能优势。明显的海森堡式缩放主要来自先前的约束，而不是来自测量中获得的信息，在这里考虑的资源标准化意义上，测量中获得的信息在操作上是可以忽略的。我们进一步分析了Holland-Burnett干涉测量法和压缩状态的零差检测，展示了估计器结构和重复数如何确定可达到的精度以及QFI何时提供可靠的诊断。我们的研究结果澄清了条件下，非经典资源导致真正的量子优势，并提供了一个实用的方法来设计和评估量子传感协议在现实的实验约束。


### Abstract

Quantum-enhanced sensing is commonly benchmarked using the quantum Fisher information (QFI), often interpreted as a direct indicator of achievable precision. However, this quantity acquires operational meaning only within a fully specified inference framework that consistently incorporates state preparation, measurement design, resource accounting, estimator construction, prior information, and finite data effects. Here we establish a realistic end-to-end framework for quantum sensing under finite resources and identify general principles required for operationally meaningful performance assessment. A central conceptual point is that the relevant unit of estimation is not a single detection event but the inference data set required to construct a consistent estimator. We apply this approach to several paradigmatic sensing strategies frequently cited in the literature. Revisiting phase estimation with NOON states within a Bayesian framework under equal total photon resources, we explicitly construct optimal estimators and show that such schemes offer no performance advantage over repeated classical interferometry for global phase estimation with finite prior width. The apparent Heisenberg-like scaling arises predominantly from prior constraints rather than from information gained in the measurement, which is operationally negligible in the resource-normalized sense considered here. We further analyse Holland-Burnett interferometry and homodyne detection with squeezed states, demonstrating how estimator construction and repetition number determine the attainable precision and when QFI provides a reliable diagnostic. Our results clarify the conditions under which nonclassical resources lead to genuine metrological advantages and provide a practical methodology for designing and evaluating quantum sensing protocols under realistic experimental constraints.

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.08346v1

### 摘要

三子--由两个同电荷的载体和一个带相反电荷的载体组成的库伦束缚三粒子激发--是二维半导体中的中心准粒子。减少的介电屏蔽和量子限制极大地增强了它们的结合能，使它们稳健且在实验上易于使用。这篇评论回顾了三子物理学的理论和实验进展，强调严格的少体方法以及介电环境、各向异性以及外部电场和磁场的作用。我们分析了描述二维配置空间中三重子的计算方法，并讨论了降维如何修改它们的结构和稳定性。还强调了与多体现象的联系，包括筛选、兰道级混合和激子-极化子交叉。

We analyze computational methods for describing trions in two-dimensional configuration spaces and discuss how reduced dimensionality modifies their structure and stability. Connections to many-body phenomena, including screening, Landau-level mixing, and exciton--polaron crossover, are also highlighted.

## 半无限方形势井中的束缚态

**Original Title:** Bound states in a semi-infinite square potential well

**Authors:** Nivaldo A. Lemos

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.08394v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.08394v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-09


### 摘要

有限平方势井是量子力学入门中的一个主要问题。关于允许能量的确定有大量文献，这需要通过数值、图形或近似分析方法来求解超越方程。在这里，我们研究了半无限势井中粒子的探索较少的问题。能量本征值（也由超越方程确定）是通过标准图形方法找到的，并提供了产生稳态数量的简单规则。接下来，尝试简化上述超越方程。在这个过程中遇到了陷阱，并且优秀教科书的解决方案手册中给出的据称更简单的问题图形处理被证明是有缺陷的。更仔细的分析可以得出正确的简化，这被证明特别适合寻找能量级的高度准确的近似值。最后，产生一类精确解，构造相关的正规化特征函数，并计算找到井内粒子的概率。


### Abstract

The finite square potential well is a staple problem in introductory quantum mechanics. There is an extensive literature on the determination of the allowed energies, which requires the solution of a transcendental equation by numerical, graphical or approximate analytic methods. Here we investigate the less explored problem of a particle in a semi-infinite potential well. The energy eigenvalues, which are also determined by a transcendental equation, are found by a standard graphical method, and a simple rule that yields the number of stationary states is provided. Next a simplification of the aforementioned transcendental equation is attempted. During the process pitfalls are encountered and a purportedly simpler graphical treatment of the problem given in the solutions manual to a fine textbook is shown to be flawed. A more careful analysis brings forth the correct simplification, which is shown to be particularly suitable for finding highly accurate approximations to the energy levels. Finally, a class of exact solutions is produced, the associated normalized eigenfunctions are constructed and the probability of finding the particle inside the well is computed.

In this work, we experimentally use encodings of Markov chains to prepare quantum states and run a quantum Markov Chain Monte Carlo algorithm (qMCMC) on Quantinuum's H2 and Helios quantum computers. We demonstrate that it is possible to obtain accurate results on current Noisy Intermediate Scale Quantum (NISQ) hardware, operating directly on the physical qubits.
