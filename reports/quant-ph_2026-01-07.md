## 周期驱动系统中基于Floquet-Bloch态的无监督拓扑相位发现

**Original Title:** Unsupervised Topological Phase Discovery in Periodically Driven Systems via Floquet-Bloch State

**Authors:** Chen-Yang Wang, Jing-Ping Xu, Ce Wang, Ya-Ping Yang

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.24822v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.24822v2

**Submitted:** 2025-12-31


### 摘要

Floquet工程为实现新型非平衡拓扑相提供了无与伦比的平台。然而，Floquet系统的独特结构（包括多个准能间隙）对使用传统分析方法的分类构成了重大挑战。我们提出了一种新型的无监督机器学习框架，该框架采用在动量-时间（$\boldSymbol{k}，t$）空间中定义的核，直接从Floquet-Bloch本征状态构建。这种方法本质上是数据驱动的，不需要对底层的拓扑不变量的先验知识，与依赖于微动运算符或同伦变换等抽象概念的现有方法相比，具有根本优势。至关重要的是，这项工作成功地揭示了Floquet本征态本身中编码的内在拓扑特征。我们证明，我们的方法能够稳健且同时地识别出与各种对称类（1D AIII、1D D和2D A）的$0$-gap和$pi $-gap相关的拓扑不变量，为系统性分类和发现复杂非平衡拓扑物质建立了稳健的方法论。


### Abstract

Floquet engineering offers an unparalleled platform for realizing novel non-equilibrium topological phases. However, the unique structure of Floquet systems, which includes multiple quasienergy gaps, poses a significant challenge to classification using conventional analytical methods. We propose a novel unsupervised machine learning framework that employs a kernel defined in momentum-time ($\boldsymbol{k},t$) space, constructed directly from Floquet-Bloch eigenstates. This approach is intrinsically data-driven and requires no prior knowledge of the underlying topological invariants, providing a fundamental advantage over prior methods that rely on abstract concepts like the micromotion operator or homotopic transformations. Crucially, this work successfully reveals the intrinsic topological characteristics encoded within the Floquet eigenstates themselves. We demonstrate that our method robustly and simultaneously identifies the topological invariants associated with both the $0$-gap and the $π$-gap across various symmetry classes (1D AIII, 1D D, and 2D A), establishing a robust methodology for the systematic classification and discovery of complex non-equilibrium topological matter.

## 拓扑量子场论和广义统计的泡利稳定剂形式主义

**Original Title:** Pauli stabilizer formalism for topological quantum field theories and generalized statistics

**Authors:** Yitao Feng, Hanyu Xue, Ryohei Kobayashi, Po-Shen Hsin, Yu-An Chen

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.00064v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.00064v2

**Submitted:** 2025-12-31


### 摘要

拓学量子场论（TQFT）为描述物质的拓学相和构造量子误差纠正码提供了统一的框架，在高能物理、凝聚物质和量子信息中发挥着核心作用。一个核心挑战是在格上制定topology order并从微观Hamilton中提取topology激励的属性。在这项工作中，我们构建了新类别的格点规范理论作为泡利稳定器模型，实现了一般维度的广泛TQFT。我们开发了扩展激励的网格描述，并系统地确定其广义统计量。我们的主要例子是（4+1）D费米子循环复律码，通过压缩（4+1）D $\mathbb{Z}_4$复律码中的$e#2m#2 $-循环获得。我们表明环激励表现出费米子环统计数据：24步环翻转过程产生$-1$的相。我们的泡利稳定器模型实现了（4+1）D中的所有扭曲2形式规范理论，即按$H & 5（B & 2G，U（1））$分类的更高级形式Dijkgraaf-Witten TQFT。在（4+1）D之外，费米子环复曲面码形成了任意维度上的一个$\mathbb{Z}_2$toporder族，使用$\mathbb{Z}_4$quidits实现为显式泡利稳定码。最后，我们开发了一个基于Pauli的框架，该框架定义了任何维度中扩展激励的广义统计量，产生可计算的格么正过程来检测非平凡统计量。例如，我们提出了（6+1）D中的任意子膜统计，以及任意维度中的费米子膜和体积统计。我们构建了新的$\mathbb{Z}_2$拓扑序族：费米-膜复律码和费米-体积复律码。此外，我们证明了$2 p +2$空间维度中的$p$-维激发可以仅支持$p$的任意$p$-brane统计。


### Abstract

Topological quantum field theory (TQFT) provides a unifying framework for describing topological phases of matter and for constructing quantum error-correcting codes, playing a central role across high-energy physics, condensed matter, and quantum information. A central challenge is to formulate topological order on lattices and to extract the properties of topological excitations from microscopic Hamiltonians. In this work, we construct new classes of lattice gauge theories as Pauli stabilizer models, realizing a wide range of TQFTs in general dimensions. We develop a lattice description of extended excitations and systematically determine their generalized statistics. Our main example is the (4+1)D fermionic-loop toric code, obtained by condensing the $e^2m^2$-loop in the (4+1)D $\mathbb{Z}_4$ toric code. We show that the loop excitation exhibits fermionic loop statistics: the 24-step loop-flipping process yields a phase of $-1$. Our Pauli stabilizer models realize all twisted 2-form gauge theories in (4+1)D, the higher-form Dijkgraaf-Witten TQFT classified by $H^5(B^2G,U(1))$. Beyond (4+1)D, the fermionic-loop toric codes form a family of $\mathbb{Z}_2$ topological orders in arbitrary dimensions, realized as explicit Pauli stabilizer codes using $\mathbb{Z}_4$ qudits. Finally, we develop a Pauli-based framework that defines generalized statistics for extended excitations in any dimension, yielding computable lattice unitary processes to detect nontrivial statistics. For example, we propose anyonic membrane statistics in (6+1)D, as well as fermionic membrane and volume statistics in arbitrary dimensions. We construct new families of $\mathbb{Z}_2$ topological orders: the fermionic-membrane toric code and the fermionic-volume toric code. In addition, we demonstrate that $p$-dimensional excitations in $2p+2$ spatial dimensions can support anyonic $p$-brane statistics for only even $p$.

## 最小长度：量子力学中非厄米性和非定域性的根源

**Original Title:** Minimal length: a source of non-Hermiticity and non-locality in quantum mechanics

**Authors:** H. Moradpour, S. Jalalzadeh

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.02413v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.02413v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-03


### 摘要

首先，该研究试图阐明纯量子力学动量测量（规范动量空间）和广义动量操作符测量之间的关系，包括最小长度效应。此外，由于长度最小，量子力学中复数的存在似乎是合理的。最后，还报道了一种受最小长度启发产生复量子数量子纠缠态的新方法。因此，包括最小长度的理论，例如引力的一些量子场景，似乎能够丰富当前对非定域性的理解。


### Abstract

First, the study tries to shed light on the relationship between purely quantum mechanical momentum measurements (canonical momentum space) and measurements of the generalized momentum operator, including minimal length effects. Additionally, the existence of complex numbers in quantum mechanics seems justifiable as a consequence of minimal length. Finally, a novel method for generating quantum entangled states with complex quantum numbers inspired by the minimal length is also reported. Therefore, theories including a minimal length, like some quantum scenarios of gravity, seem to be able to enrich the current understanding of non-locality.

## 爱丽丝如何在她的时代之前推导出量子力学的原理

**Original Title:** How Alice, long before her time, derived the principles of quantum mechanics

**Authors:** Marcello Poletti

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.02419v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.02419v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-03


### 摘要

这场哲学对话探讨了这样一个想法：量子力学的基本原理不需要被解释为描述新的物理学，而是可能源于在连贯的代数框架内形式化测量行为的逻辑必要性。通过将这一观点推向极致，对话认为，量子力学的核心结构可以独立于原子粒子的任何特定量子性质而推导出来，并且一旦认真考虑可观察性和测量背景的限制，就可以在其他经典理论中公式化。


### Abstract

This philosophical dialogue explores the idea that the foundational principles of quantum mechanics need not be interpreted as describing a new physics, but may instead arise from the logical necessity of formalising the act of measurement within a coherent algebraic framework. By pushing this perspective to its extreme, the dialogue argues that the core structures of quantum mechanics can be derived independently of any specifically quantum properties of atomic particles, and can be formulated within an otherwise classical theory once limitations of observability and measurement context are taken seriously.

## 哈代型悖论与逻辑语境的等效性

**Original Title:** The Equivalence between Hardy-type paradox and Logical Contextuality

**Authors:** Songyi Liu, Yongjun Wang, Baoshan Wang, Chang He, Yunyi Jia

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.01445v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.01445v2

**Submitted:** 2026-01-04


### 摘要

哈代型悖论为量子语境性提供了优雅的、无不等式的证明。在这项工作中，我们引入了一般哈代型悖论的统一逻辑公式，我们将其称为逻辑哈代型悖论。我们证明，对于任何有限场景，逻辑哈代型悖论的存在等同于逻辑上下文。特别地，强上下文相当于成功概率SP = 1的逻辑哈代型悖论。这些结果推广了先前关于（2，k，2），（2，2，d）和n-循环场景的工作，并解决了这样的等价性不适用于一般场景的误解[1]。我们分析了（2，2，2）和（2，3，3）Bell方案以及Klyachko-Can-Binicioglu-Shumovsky（KCBS）方案上的逻辑Hardy型悖论。我们表明，KCBS的情况下承认只有一种哈代型悖论，实现SP \约10.56%的成功概率为一个特定的参数设置。


### Abstract

Hardy-type paradoxes offer elegant, inequality-free proof of quantum contextuality. In this work, we introduce a unified logical formulation for general Hardy-type paradoxes, which we term logical Hardy-type paradoxes. We prove that for any finite scenario, the existence of a logical Hardy-type paradox is equivalent to logical contextuality. Specially, strong contextuality is equivalent to logical Hardy-type paradoxes with success probability SP = 1. These results generalize prior work on (2,k,2), (2,2,d), and n-cycle scenarios, and resolve a misconception that such equivalence does not hold for general scenarios [1]. We analyse the logical Hardy-type paradoxes on the (2,2,2) and (2,3,3) Bell scenarios, as well as the Klyachko-Can-Binicioglu-Shumovsky (KCBS) scenario. We show that the KCBS scenario admits only one kind of Hardy-type paradox, achieving a success probability of SP \approx 10.56% for a specific parameter setting.

## 克尔介体模腔中协同光参数放大和驱动力的光阻挡效应

**Original Title:** Photon blockade effect from synergistic optical parametric amplification and driving force in Kerr-medium single-mode cavity

**Authors:** Zhang Zhiqiang

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.01819v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.01819v2

**Submitted:** 2026-01-05


### 摘要

这项工作研究了包含与光参数放大器（OPA）耦合的克尔非线性腔的混合量子系统中的量子阻塞控制。动力学由一个主方程决定，该方程源自有效Hamilton（包括腔衰变）。为了获得分析解，系统的量子状态在Fock基中被扩展到双量子水平。求解稳态薛定格方程会产生最佳量子封锁的概率幅度和分析条件。结果证实，通过合适的参数可以实现量子阻断。稳态等时二阶相关函数的分析解和数值模拟之间存在良好的一致性，验证了分析方法和封锁效应。从数字上看，共振下平均腔内量子数显着增加，为增强单量子源亮度提供了理论途径。此外，驱动阶段被证明可以调节最佳封锁区域：它在驱动强度和OPA非线性的二维参数空间内移动了椭圆区域，甚至可以逆转其打开方向。还研究了克尔非线性的影响。在克尔的广泛优势中，光封锁仍然强大。物理分析将这种影响归因于抑制双量子状态的两个不同激发路径之间的破坏性量子干涉。虽然克尔非线性改变了系统的能量水平，但它不会破坏这种干扰机制，从而解释了该效应在广泛参数范围内的稳定性。


### Abstract

This work investigates photon blockade control in a hybrid quantum system containing a Kerr-nonlinear cavity coupled to an optical parametric amplifier (OPA). The dynamics are governed by a master equation derived from an effective Hamiltonian that includes cavity decay. To obtain analytical solutions, the system's quantum state is expanded in the Fock basis up to the two-photon level. Solving the steady-state Schrodinger equation yields probability amplitudes and the analytical conditions for optimal photon blockade. Results confirm that photon blockade is achievable with suitable parameters. Excellent agreement is found between the analytical solutions and numerical simulations for the steady-state, equal-time second-order correlation function, validating both the analytical method and the blockade effect. Numerically, the average intracavity photon number increases significantly under resonance, providing a theoretical pathway for enhancing single-photon source brightness. Furthermore, the driving phase is shown to regulate the optimal blockade region: it shifts the parabolic region within the two-dimensional parameter space of driving strength and OPA nonlinearity and can even reverse its opening direction. The influence of Kerr nonlinearity is also examined. Photon blockade remains robust across a wide range of Kerr strengths. Physical analysis attributes the effect to destructive quantum interference between two distinct excitation pathways that suppress two-photon states. While Kerr nonlinearity shifts the system's energy levels, it does not disrupt this interference mechanism, explaining the effect's stability over a broad parameter range.

## 光学磁力学的量子信息：半经典Cramer-Rao界违背和海森堡标度

**Original Title:** Quantum information of optical magnetometry: Semiclassical Cramer-Rao bound violation and Heisenberg scaling

**Authors:** Georg Engelhardt, Ming Li, Xingchang Wang, JunYan Luo, J. F. Chen

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.01820v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.01820v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-05


### 摘要

光学磁力计利用法拉第效应引起的线极化激光的旋转进行高精度磁场测量。在这里，我们进行了深入的量子信息研究，部署了两个不同的模型：第一个，半经典模型可能会违反量子Cramer-Rao界限几个数量级，因为弱消散和大原子数，从而使半经典方法无效。在这个参数制度。第二个模型将原子描述为集体旋转，尊重所有参数的克莱默-拉奥界限。有趣的是，集体模型还预测了量子费舍尔信息的海森堡缩放。两个模型的比较表明，海森堡标度是非相互作用量子系统中测量引起的量子相关性的结果。由于海森堡标度出现在宏观量子系统的稳态中，因此可以将其视为量子传感中的一种新范式。有趣的是，这两个模型与实验数据的比较可以构成对宏观原子系系中量子力学基础的测试。


### Abstract

Optical magnetometers use the rotation of linearly polarized laser light induced by the Faraday effect for high precision magnetic field measurements. Here, we carry out an in-depth quantum information investigation, deploying two distinct models: The first, semiclassical model can violate the quantum Cramer-Rao bound by several orders of magnitude for weak dissipation and large atom numbers, invalidating the semiclassical approach in this parameter regime. The second model, describing the atoms as a collective spin, respects the Cramer-Rao bound for all parameters. Interestingly, the collective model also predicts Heisenberg scaling for the quantum Fisher information. The comparison of both models shows that Heisenberg scaling is a result of measurement-induced quantum correlation in an otherwise non-interacting quantum system. As the Heisenberg scaling appears in a stationary state of a macroscopic quantum system, it can be thus viewed as a new paradigm in quantum sensing. Intriguingly, the comparison of both models with experimental data can constitute a test for the foundations of quantum mechanics in a macroscopic ensemble of atoms.

## 多量子位纠缠的全局参数门

**Original Title:** Global Parametric Gates for Multi-qubit Entanglement

**Authors:** Jize Yang, Lin Guo, Haonan Xiong, Jiahui Wang, Yan Li, Yunfan Yang, Chenjie An, Hongyi Zhang, Luyan Sun, Yipu Song, Luming Duan

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.01826v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.01826v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-05


### 摘要

我们提出并通过实验证明了一种全局参数门，可以在一步中生成多量子位纠缠态。通过以相对于计算量子位的精确失调方式对常见量子位应用参数驱动，我们直接产生状态可靠度分别为99.4%/pm0.2%，93.4%%/pm0.3%，91.4%/pm0.3%，91.4%/pm0.3%，%。该方案仅使用微波驱动器即可实现高效、可重新配置的控制，并且与固定频率量子位兼容。误差分析表明，不忠实性主要源于去相干和一致控制误差，静态ZZ耦合和通量噪音的影响可以忽略不计。此外，采用最先进参数的模拟预测该全局门可以在多达六个量子位的系统中产生高保真度（99.70%）纠缠。


### Abstract

We propose and experimentally demonstrate a global parametric gate that generates multi-qubit entangled states in a single step. By applying a parametric drive to a common qubit at precise detunings relative to computational qubits, we directly produce two-, three-, and four-qubit entanglement with state fidelities of 99.4\%\pm0.2\%, 93.4\%\pm0.3\%, and 91.4\%\pm0.3\%, respectively. This scheme enables efficient, reconfigurable control using only microwave drives and is compatible with fixed-frequency qubits. Error analyses indicate that infidelity stems primarily from decoherence and coherent control errors, with negligible contributions from static ZZ coupling and flux noise. Furthermore, simulations with state-of-the-art parameters predict this global gate can generate high-fidelity (99.70\%) entanglement in systems of up to six qubits.

## Grover算法的形式化建模与验证

**Original Title:** Formal Modeling and Verification of Grover's Algorithm

**Authors:** H. Sun, Z. Shi, S. Chen, G. Wang, X. Li, Y. Guan, Q. Zhang, Z. Shao

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.02435v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.02435v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-05


### 摘要

Grover的算法依赖于量子力学的叠加和干涉，在搜索未排序的数据库等特定任务中，这比经典计算更有效。由于量子力学的高度复杂性，量子算法的正确性很难通过传统的模拟方法得到保证。相比之下，Grover算法的基本概念和数学结构可以形式化为逻辑表达式，并通过高阶逻辑推理进行验证。在本文中，我们正式建模和验证的HOL光定理证明器中的Grover的算法。我们专注于证明关键属性，例如其预言和扩散运算符的正性、成功概率相对于迭代次数的单调性以及最佳迭代计数的精确表达。通过分析整因子分解的具体应用，我们展示了我们工作的实用性和前景。


### Abstract

Grover's algorithm relies on the superposition and interference of quantum mechanics, which is more efficient than classical computing in specific tasks such as searching an unsorted database. Due to the high complexity of quantum mechanics, the correctness of quantum algorithms is difficult to guarantee through traditional simulation methods. By contrast, the fundamental concepts and mathematical structure of Grover's algorithm can be formalized into logical expressions and verified by higher-order logical reasoning. In this paper, we formally model and verify Grover's algorithm in the HOL Light theorem prover. We focus on proving key properties such as the unitarity of its oracle and diffusion operators, the monotonicity of the success probability with respect to the number of iterations, and an exact expression for the optimal iteration count. By analyzing a concrete application to integer factorization, we demonstrate the practicality and prospects of our work.

## 自由电子与光之间涉及一级和二级过程的量子相互作用

**Original Title:** Quantum Interaction Between Free Electrons and Light Involving First-order and Second-order Process

**Authors:** Hongteng Lin, Xiaotong Xiong, Junjie Liu, Yidong Huang, Fang Liu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.01846v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.01846v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-05


### 摘要

光致近场电子显微镜（PINEM）效应揭示了自由电子与光学近场之间的量子相互作用，揭示了许多操纵自由电子波包和检测/整形量子光态的新现象。然而，自由电子通常一次只吸收/发射一个量子，而自由电子与双量子相互作用的物理机制和现象尚未得到研究。此外，PINEM和Kapitza-Dirac（KD）效应与非线性康普顿散射之间的关系仍不清楚。在这里，我们发展了考虑双量子过程的电子-量子相互作用的完整量子理论。研究表明，通过操纵光学近场的电场分量可以大大增强电子对两个量子的发射/吸收，并且在某些情况下会发生单量子过程和双量子过程之间的量子干涉，从而影响量子数状态、电子能状态和电子-光纠缠。同时，发现KD效应（弹性电子-量子散射）和非线性康普顿散射（非弹性电子-量子散射）也是一种双量子过程，电子的分布可以基于全量子理论解析推导。我们的工作揭示了自由电子与两个量子相互作用时可能存在的丰富现象，为未来更深入地研究电子-量子相互作用中的非线性过程铺平了道路。


### Abstract

Photon-induced Near-field Electron Microscopy (PINEM) effect has revealed the quantum interaction between free electrons and optical near filed, which demonstrated plenty of novel phenomena of manipulating free electron wave packet and detecting/shaping quantum photonic states. However, free electrons generally only absorb/emit one photon at a time, while the physical mechanism and phenomena of free electron-two-photon interaction have not been studied yet. Moreover, the relationship between PINEM and Kapitza-Dirac (KD) effect and nonlinear Compton scattering is still unclear. Here we develop the full quantum theory of electron-photon interaction considering the two-photon process. It is revealed that the emission/absorption of two photons by electrons can be greatly enhanced by manipulating the electric field component of optical near field, and the quantum interference between single-photon and two-photon processes can occur in some circumstances, which affects the photon number state, electron energy states and electron-photon entanglement. Meanwhile, it is found that the KD effect (elastic electron-photon scattering) and nonlinear Compton scattering (inelastic electron-photon scattering) are also a kind of two-photon process and the distribution of electrons can be deduced analytically based on the full quantum theory. Our work uncovers the possible abundant phenomena when free electron interacting with two photons, paves the way for more in-depth studies of nonlinear processes in electron-photon quantum interactions in the future.

## 巴格曼不变量概览：几何基础和应用

**Original Title:** A Survey of Bargmann Invariants: Geometric Foundations and Applications

**Authors:** Lin Zhang, Bing Xie

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.01858v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.01858v1

## 引力纠缠何时意味着引力子？

**Original Title:** When does entanglement through gravity imply gravitons?

**Authors:** Nikolaos Mitrakos, Maria Papageorgiou, T. Rick Perche, Marios Christodoulou

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.03214v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.03214v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-06


### 摘要

人们声称，通过牛顿势检测纠缠可以支持引力子的存在，这是通过外推到一个思想实验，该实验表明，除非存在量子涨落，否则互补性和因果关系将发生冲突。我们使用纯量场模型批判性地评估了这个一致性论点。我们表明，当忽略量子涨落时，是否违反了互补性或无信号传递取决于如何采取这种逼近，而在这两种情况下，纠缠都是在时空中局部产生的。我们澄清，正确解读这个悖论需要明确区分因果关系违反的两个概念：牛顿远距离作用和量子力学无信号传递;后者是相关的，而前者则不然。我们得出的结论是，如果在引力纠缠中检测到阻滞效应，思想实验（a）并没有增加牛顿势纠缠的认识论相关性（b）支持引力子的存在。


### Abstract

Detection of entanglement through the Newtonian potential has been claimed to support the existence of gravitons, by extrapolating to a thought experiment which demonstrates that complementarity and causality would be in conflict unless quantum fluctuations exist. We critically assess this consistency argument using scalar field models. We show that whether complementarity or no-signalling is violated when quantum fluctuations are neglected, depends on how this approximation is taken, while in both cases entanglement is generated locally in spacetime. We clarify that the correct reading of the paradox requires making a clear distinction between two notions of causality violation: Newtonian action-at-a-distance and the quantum mechanical no-signalling; the latter is pertinent while the former is not. We conclude that the thought experiment (a) does not add to the epistemological relevance of entanglement through Newtonian potentials (b) lends support for the existence of gravitons, if retardation effects are detected in entanglement through gravity.

## $\mathsf{QAC}^0$包含$\mathsf{TC}^0$（具有输入的许多副本）

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.03243v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.03243v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-06


### 摘要

$\mathsf{QAC}^0$ is the class of constant-depth polynomial-size quantum circuits constructed from arbitrary single-qubit gates and generalized Toffoli gates. It is arguably the smallest natural class of constant-depth quantum computation which has not been shown useful for computing any non-trivial Boolean function. Despite this, many attempts to port classical $\mathsf{AC}^0$ lower bounds to $\mathsf{QAC}^0$ have failed.   We give one possible explanation of this: $\mathsf{QAC}^0$ circuits are significantly more powerful than their classical counterparts. We show the unconditional separation $\mathsf{QAC}^0\not\subset\mathsf{AC}^0[p]$ for decision problems, which also resolves for the first time whether $\mathsf{AC}^0$ could be more powerful than $\mathsf{QAC}^0$. Moreover, we prove that $\mathsf{QAC}^0$ circuits can compute a wide range of Boolean functions if given multiple copies of the input: $\mathsf{TC}^0 \subseteq \mathsf{QAC}^0 \circ \mathsf{NC}^0$. Along the way, we introduce an amplitude amplification technique that makes several approximate constant-depth constructions exact.

## 大经典典型性

**Original Title:** Grand-Canonical Typicality

**Authors:** Cedric Igelspacher, Roderich Tumulka, Cornelia Vogel

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.03253v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.03253v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-06


### 摘要

We study how the grand-canonical density matrix arises in macroscopic quantum systems. ``Canonical typicality'' is the known statement that for a typical wave function $Ψ$ from a micro-canonical energy shell of a quantum system $S$ weakly coupled to a large but finite quantum system $B$, the reduced density matrix $\hatρ^S_Ψ=\mathrm{tr}^B |Ψ\rangle\langle Ψ|$ is approximately equal to the canonical density matrix $\hatρ_\mathrm{can}=Z^{-1}_\mathrm{can} \exp(-β\hat{H}^S)$. Here, we discuss the analogous statement and related questions for the \emph{grand-canonical} density matrix $\hatρ_\mathrm{gc}=Z^{-1}_\mathrm{gc} \exp(-β(\hat{H}^S-μ_1 \hat{N}_{1}^S-\ldots-μ_r\hat{N}_{r}^S))$ with $\hat{N}_{i}^S$ the number operator for molecules of type $i$ in the system $S$. This includes (i) the case of chemical reactions and (ii) that of systems $S$ defined by a spatial region which particles may enter or leave. It includes the statements (a) that the density matrix of the appropriate (generalized micro-canonical) Hilbert subspace $H_\mathrm{gmc} \subset H^S \otimes H^B$ (defined by a micro-canonical interval of total energy and suitable particle number sectors), after tracing out $B$, yields $\hatρ_\mathrm{gc}$; (b) that typical $Ψ$ from $H_\mathrm{gmc}$ have reduced density matrix $\hatρ^S_Ψ$ close to $\hatρ_\mathrm{gc}$; and (c) that the conditional wave function $ψ^S$ of $S$ has probability distribution $\mathrm{GAP}_{\hatρ_\mathrm{gc}}$ if a typical orthonormal basis of $H^B$ is used. That is, we discuss the foundation and justification of both the density matrix and the distribution of the wave function in the grand-canonical case. We also extend these considerations to the so-called generalized Gibbs ensembles, which apply to systems for which some macroscopic observables are conserved.
