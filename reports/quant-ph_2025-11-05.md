## 通过受控消散增强Kerr-Cat量子位一致性

**Original Title:** Enhancing Kerr-Cat Qubit Coherence with Controlled Dissipation

**Authors:** Francesco Adinolfi, Daniel Z. Haxell, Alessandro Bruno, Laurent Michaud, Venus Hasanuzzaman Kamrul, Preeti Pandey, Alexander Grimm

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.01027v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.01027v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-02


### 摘要

量子计算关键依赖于在计算期间保持量子一致性。玻色子量子误差修正通过将量子位编码到抗噪振荡器状态的叠加中来保护这种一致性。就克尔猫量子位（KCQ）而言，这些状态的稳定性源自驱动非线性振荡器中工程Hamilton的准简并底态。KCQ在实验上与片上架构和高保真操作兼容，使它们成为可扩展玻色子量子处理器的有希望的候选者。然而，它们的位翻转时间必须进一步增加才能充分利用这些优势。在这里，我们提供了直接证据，表明KCQ中的位翻转时间受到量子位集管泄漏的限制，并通过实验减轻了这一过程。我们一致地控制泄漏总数，并测量其> 9%，比非驱动系统高十二倍。然后，我们通过设计的消散将该种群冷却回KCQ管中，确定抑制位翻转的条件，并证明位翻转时间增加至3.6毫秒。通过采用汉密尔顿约束和工程消散，我们的实验结合了Schr\' odinger-cat量子位稳定的两种范式。我们的结果阐明了这些稳定过程之间的相互作用，并指出了充分实现这些量子位量子误差修正潜力的途径。


### Abstract

Quantum computing crucially relies on maintaining quantum coherence for the
duration of a calculation. Bosonic quantum error correction protects this
coherence by encoding qubits into superpositions of noise-resilient oscillator
states. In the case of the Kerr-cat qubit (KCQ), these states derive their
stability from being the quasi-degenerate ground states of an engineered
Hamiltonian in a driven nonlinear oscillator. KCQs are experimentally
compatible with on-chip architectures and high-fidelity operations, making them
promising candidates for a scalable bosonic quantum processor. However, their
bit-flip time must increase further to fully leverage these advantages. Here,
we present direct evidence that the bit-flip time in a KCQ is limited by
leakage out of the qubit manifold and experimentally mitigate this process. We
coherently control the leakage population and measure it to be > 9%, twelve
times higher than in the undriven system. We then cool this population back
into the KCQ manifold with engineered dissipation, identify conditions under
which this suppresses bit-flips, and demonstrate increased bit-flip times up to
3.6 milliseconds. By employing both Hamiltonian confinement and engineered
dissipation, our experiment combines two paradigms for Schr\"odinger-cat qubit
stabilization. Our results elucidate the interplay between these stabilization
processes and indicate a path towards fully realizing the potential of these
qubits for quantum error correction.

## 感知器存储容量中的伪量子优势

**Original Title:** Pseudo quantum advantages in perceptron storage capacity

**Authors:** Fabio Benatti, Masoud Gharahi, Giovanni Gramegna, Stefano Mancini, Vincenzo Parisi

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.01028v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.01028v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-02


### 摘要

我们研究了一种广义量子感知器架构，其特征在于振荡激活函数，其频率范围从零到无穷大。利用统计力学的分析技术，我们推导出最佳存储容量，并证明经典结果在消失频率的极限内可以恢复。然而，随着频率的增加，该架构展现出增强的量子存储能力。值得注意的是，这种改进仅源于激活函数的特定形式，并且原则上可以在经典框架内模拟。因此，我们将这种增强称为伪量子优势。


### Abstract

We investigate a generalized quantum perceptron architecture characterized by
an oscillating activation function with a tunable frequency ranging from zero
to infinity. Employing analytical techniques from statistical mechanics, we
derive the optimal storage capacity and demonstrate that the classical result
is recovered in the limit of vanishing frequency. As the frequency increases,
however, the architecture exhibits enhanced quantum storage capabilities.
Notably, this improvement stems solely from the specific form of the activation
function and, in principle, could be emulated within a classical framework.
Accordingly, we refer to this enhancement as a pseudo quantum advantage.

## ECCentric：量子纠错码的实证分析

**Original Title:** ECCentric: An Empirical Analysis of Quantum Error Correction Codes

**Authors:** Aleksandra Świerkowska, Jannik Pflieger, Emmanouil Giortamis, Pramod Bhatotia

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.01062v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.01062v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-02


### 摘要

量子错误纠正（QEC）对于构建可扩展量子计算机至关重要，但缺乏系统性的端到端评估方法使得难以评估不同QEC代码在现实条件下的表现。代码的巨大多样性、广阔的实验搜索空间以及标准化框架的缺乏阻碍了彻底、全面的分析。为了解决这个问题，我们引入了ECCentric，这是一个端到端基准测试框架，旨在系统地评估整个量子计算堆栈中的QEC代码。ECCentric设计为模块化、可扩展和通用性，允许在不同硬件布局、噪音模型和编译策略下对QEC代码系列进行全面分析。   使用ECCentric，我们针对现实的中期量子设备参数对主要QEC代码系列进行了首次系统性基准测试。我们的实证分析表明，QPU内执行的表现显着优于分布式方法，量子位连接性对于减少逻辑错误来说是比增加代码距离更重要的因素，而且编译器负载仍然是错误的主要来源。此外，我们的研究结果表明，具有量子位穿梭的捕获离子架构是最有前途的近期平台，并且在有噪音的设备上，有必要战略性地和选择性地应用QEC，以避免引入的错误多于纠正的错误。这项研究为硬件设计师和从业者提供了至关重要的、可操作的见解，指导耐故障量子系统的开发。


### Abstract

Quantum error correction (QEC) is essential for building scalable quantum
computers, but a lack of systematic, end-to-end evaluation methods makes it
difficult to assess how different QEC codes perform under realistic conditions.
The vast diversity of codes, an expansive experimental search space, and the
absence of a standardized framework prevent a thorough, holistic analysis. To
address this, we introduce ECCentric, an end-to-end benchmarking framework
designed to systematically evaluate QEC codes across the full quantum computing
stack. ECCentric is designed to be modular, extensible, and general, allowing
for a comprehensive analysis of QEC code families under varying hardware
topologies, noise models, and compilation strategies.
  Using ECCentric, we conduct the first systematic benchmarking of major QEC
code families against realistic, mid-term quantum device parameters. Our
empirical analysis reveals that intra-QPU execution significantly outperforms
distributed methods, that qubit connectivity is a far more critical factor for
reducing logical errors than increasing code distance, and that compiler
overhead remains a major source of error. Furthermore, our findings suggest
that trapped-ion architectures with qubit shuttling are the most promising
near-term platforms and that on noisy devices, a strategic and selective
application of QEC is necessary to avoid introducing more errors than are
corrected. This study provides crucial, actionable insights for both hardware
designers and practitioners, guiding the development of fault-tolerant quantum
systems.

## 具有SPAM错误的一般布局的量子网络断层扫描

**Original Title:** Quantum Network Tomography for General Topology with SPAM Errors

**Authors:** Xuchuang Wang, Matheus Guedes De Andrade, Guus Avis, Yu-zhen Janice Chen, Mohammad Hajiesmaili, Don Towsley

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.01074v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.01074v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-02


### 摘要

量子网络断层扫描（QNT）的目标是根据外部外围操作来描述量子网络中的内部量子通道。之前的研究主要集中在具有位翻转和去极化通道的星型网络上，而更广泛的问题--例如QNT，适用于具有任意布局的网络和一般Pauli通道--基本上没有被探索。此外，即使在简化的量子星网络中，建立通道可识别性仍然是一个重大挑战。   在本文的第一部分中，我们介绍了量子网络中的一种新型网络断层扫描方法，称为Mergecast。我们证明，Mergecast与渐进蚀刻过程一起，能够独特地识别以任意布局和泡利通道为特征的网络中的所有内部量子通道。作为附带贡献，我们引入了泡利通道的一个子集，称为可旁路泡利通道，并针对专门包含这些通道的网络提出了一种更有效的基于单播的断层扫描方法，称为ThomasUnicast。在第二部分中，我们将调查扩展到更现实的QNT场景，其中包含状态准备和测量（SPAM）错误。我们在QNT中严格制定了SPAM错误，在QNT中提出了此类错误的估计协议，并随后调整我们的Mergecast方法来处理受SPAM错误影响的网络。最后，我们进行了基于NetSquid的模拟，以证实我们提出的协议在识别内部量子通道和估计量子网络中SPAM错误方面的有效性。特别是，我们证明了Mergecast在实际条件下（例如量子损失和量子存储去相干）保持良好的性能。


### Abstract

The goal of quantum network tomography (QNT) is the characterization of
internal quantum channels in a quantum network from external peripheral
operations. Prior research has primarily focused on star networks featuring
bit-flip and depolarizing channels, leaving the broader problem -- such as QNT
for networks with arbitrary topologies and general Pauli channels -- largely
unexplored. Moreover, establishing channel identifiability remains a
significant challenge even in simplified quantum star networks.
  In the first part of this paper, we introduce a novel network tomography
method, termed Mergecast, in quantum networks. We demonstrate that Mergecast,
together with a progressive etching procedure, enables the unique
identification of all internal quantum channels in networks characterized by
arbitrary topologies and Pauli channels. As a side contribution, we introduce a
subclass of Pauli channels, termed bypassable Pauli channels, and propose a
more efficient unicast-based tomography method, called BypassUnicast, for
networks exclusively comprising these channels. In the second part, we extend
our investigation to a more realistic QNT scenario that incorporates state
preparation and measurement (SPAM) errors. We rigorously formulate SPAM errors
in QNT, propose estimation protocols for such errors within QNT, and
subsequently adapt our Mergecast approaches to handle networks affected by SPAM
errors. Lastly, we conduct NetSquid-based simulations to corroborate the
effectiveness of our proposed protocols in identifying internal quantum
channels and estimating SPAM errors in quantum networks. In particular, we
demonstrate that Mergecast maintains good performance under realistic
conditions, such as photon loss and quantum memory decoherence.

## 量子误差修正期间的么正运算现场校准

**Original Title:** In situ calibration of unitary operations during quantum error correction

**Authors:** Jonathan Kunjummen, Jacob M. Taylor

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.01080v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.01080v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-02


### 摘要

量子错误纠正使用对伴随式的测量和经典解码算法来估计错误的位置和类型，同时保护编码的量子位。在这里，我们考虑先验信息和Bayesian更新如何在特别有噪音的量子位的情况下在提高QEC的性能方面发挥关键作用。这允许利用偶数距离码（其在QEC中通常价值较低）来处理有噪量子位，从而改变逻辑错误率与基线物理错误率的乘势律缩放。其中的一个关键部分是通过将解码器输出实时反馈到近似卡尔曼过滤器来更新先验。因此，我们的方法为实际错误率提供了引导。我们通过模拟全闭环系统来证明这一点：从统一先验开始，更新过程逐渐学习特定于站点的错误率，使解码器能够优于固定先验基线。反过来，我们表明，这使得通过注入门集断层扫描操作来对单位操作进行现场校准，在低噪音量子位的更典型场景中仅需要适度的额外费用。


### Abstract

Quantum error correction uses the measurement of syndromes and classical
decoding algorithms to estimate the location and type of errors while
protecting the encoded quantum bits. Here we consider how prior information and
Bayesian updates can play a critical role in improving the performance of QEC
in the scenario of a particularly noisy qubit. This allows for leveraging even
distance codes, which typically are less valued in QEC, to handle the noisy
qubit, changing the power-law scaling of the logical error rate with the
baseline physical error rate. A crucial component of this is updating the prior
by real time feeding of decoder outputs into a approximate Kalman filter. Thus
our approach provides a bootstrap to the actual error rates. We show this via
simulation of the full closed-loop system: starting from uniform priors, the
update procedure gradually learns site-specific error rates, enabling the
decoder to outperform a fixed-prior baseline. In turn, we show that this
enables in situ calibration of unitary operations via injection of gate set
tomography operations with only moderate overhead in the more typical scenario
of low noise qubits.

## 对称旋转网络中鲁棒的量子状态生成

**Original Title:** Robust Quantum State Generation in Symmetric Spin Networks

**Authors:** Andre Luiz P. de Lima, Luke S. Baker, Anatoly Zlotnik, Andrew K. Harter, Michael J. Martin, Jr-Shin Li

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.01085v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.01085v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-02


### 摘要

在这项工作中，我们考虑了一个参数化的伊辛模型，该模型在旋转$\fRAC{1}{2}$粒子网络上具有远程对称成对相互作用。该系统采用对称动力学设计，允许将状态空间简化为由迪克状态集定义的子空间。我们提出了一种基于矩量化方法设计鲁棒电磁幅度脉冲的方法。引入的参数考虑了电磁场的不确定性，从而产生了一系列不同的汉密尔顿族。通过采用离散化的基于矩的量化技术，我们设计了一个控制脉冲，能够同时转向一个无限的动态系统的集合，以补偿参数的变化。该方法利用了无限维参数化系统与有限维截断矩动力学之间的对偶性。仿真结果表明，该方法在实现量子传感中的重要感兴趣的状态，包括GHZ和W状态的有效性。


### Abstract

In this work, we consider a parameterized Ising model with long-range
symmetric pairwise interactions on a network of spin $\frac{1}{2}$ particles.
The system is designed with symmetric dynamics, allowing for the reduction of
the state space to a subspace defined by the set of Dicke states. We propose a
method for designing robust electromagnetic amplitude pulses based on a moment
quantization approach. The introduced parameter accounts for uncertainties in
the electromagnetic field, resulting in a family of distinct Hamiltonians. By
employing a discretized moment-based quantization technique, we design a
control pulse capable of simultaneously steering an infinite collection of
dynamical systems to compensate for parameter variations. This approach
benefits from the duality between the infinite-dimensional parameterized system
and its finite-dimensional trucnated moment dynamics. Simulation results
demonstrate the efficacy of this method in achieving states of significant
interest in quantum sensing, including the GHZ and W states.

## 描述量子计算Max-k-Cut问题的QUBO重新公式

**Original Title:** Characterizing QUBO Reformulations of the Max-k-Cut Problem for Quantum Computing

**Authors:** Adrian Harkness, Hamidreza Validi, Ramin Fakhimi, Illya V. Hicks, Tamás Terlaky, Luis F. Zuluaga

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.01108v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.01108v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-02


### 摘要

量子计算为解决经典计算机无法企及的NP难组合（优化）问题提供了巨大的潜力。利用这一潜力的一种方法是将组合问题重新定义为二次无约束二元优化（QUBO）问题。然后，可以使用隔热量子计算设备或基于门的量子计算设备上的适当量子计算算法来解决QUBO重新公式的解决方案。一般来说，通过适当惩罚违反原问题目标中问题约束的行为，可以轻松获得组合问题的QUBO重新公式。然而，描述紧密（即，为此目的，最小但足够的）惩罚系数对于在当前和近期的量子计算设备中解决所得QUBO至关重要。沿着这些思路，我们在这里重点讨论（加权）max $k$-cut问题，这是一个具有广泛应用的基本组合问题，它推广了众所周知的max $-cut问题。我们为max $k$-cut问题的两个不同的QUBO重新公式给出了紧罚系数的封闭形式刻画，其值取决于定义该问题的图的点的（加权）度。这些发现有助于使量子计算成为大规模解决组合问题的可行工具的持续努力。我们通过说明性例子支持我们的理论结果。此外，我们对拟议的QUBO重新公式进行了基准测试，以解决量子计算机模拟器上的max $k$-cut问题。


### Abstract

Quantum computing offers significant potential for solving NP-hard
combinatorial (optimization) problems that are beyond the reach of classical
computers. One way to tap into this potential is by reformulating combinatorial
problems as a quadratic unconstrained binary optimization (QUBO) problem. The
solution of the QUBO reformulation can then be addressed using adiabatic
quantum computing devices or appropriate quantum computing algorithms on
gate-based quantum computing devices. In general, QUBO reformulations of
combinatorial problems can be readily obtained by properly penalizing the
violation of the problem's constraints in the original problem's objective.
However, characterizing tight (i.e., minimal but sufficient) penalty
coefficients for this purpose is critical for enabling the solution of the
resulting QUBO in current and near-term quantum computing devices. Along these
lines, we here focus on the (weighted) max $k$-cut problem, a fundamental
combinatorial problem with wide-ranging applications that generalizes the
well-known max cut problem. We present closed-form characterizations of tight
penalty coefficients for two distinct QUBO reformulations of the max $k$-cut
problem whose values depend on the (weighted) degree of the vertices of the
graph defining the problem. These findings contribute to the ongoing effort to
make quantum computing a viable tool for solving combinatorial problems at
scale. We support our theoretical results with illustrative examples. Further,
we benchmark the proposed QUBO reformulations to solve the max $k$-cut problem
on a quantum computer simulator.

## 多状态迭代量子位耦合团簇（MS-iQCC）：一种基于量子启发的状态平均方法，以获取基态和激发态能量

**Original Title:** Multistate iterative qubit coupled cluster (MS-iQCC): a quantum-inspired, state-averaged approach to ground- and excited-state energies

**Authors:** Robert A. Lang, Shashank G. Mehendale, Ilya G. Ryabinkin, Artur F. Izmaylov

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.01121v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.01121v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-03


### 摘要

我们引入了多状态迭代量子位耦合团簇（MS-iQCC）方法，这是一种量子启发的算法，在经典硬件上高效运行，旨在预测分子的基电子状态和激发电子状态。准确的激发态能量学对于解释光谱学和化学反应性至关重要，但标准电子结构方法对于较大的系统来说要么计算成本太高，要么在存在强电子相关性的情况下失去可靠性。MS-iQCC通过在单一的状态平均程序中同时优化多个电子状态来解决这一挑战，该程序平等地对待基态和激发态。这消除了当一次计算一个激发状态并限制为保持与之前优化的状态垂直时引入的能量偏差。该方法通过允许多决定性初始猜测并通过从量子位激励生成器池自适应地构建紧凑的指数类比来支持多参考电子结构。我们将MS-iQCC应用于H$_4$、H$_2$O、N$_2$和C$_2$，包括强相关的几何结构，并观察到所有目标状态能在其潜在能量表面上稳健收敛到化学上有意义的准确性。


### Abstract

We introduce the multistate iterative qubit coupled cluster (MS-iQCC) method,
a quantum-inspired algorithm that runs efficiently on classical hardware and is
designed to predict both ground and excited electronic states of molecules.
Accurate excited-state energetics are essential for interpreting spectroscopy
and chemical reactivity, but standard electronic structure methods are either
too computationally expensive for larger systems or lose reliability in the
presence of strong electron correlation. MS-iQCC addresses this challenge by
simultaneously optimizing multiple electronic states in a single,
state-averaged procedure that treats ground and excited states on equal
footing. This removes the energetic bias that is introduced when excited states
are computed one at a time and constrained to remain orthogonal to previously
optimized states. The approach supports multireference electronic structure by
allowing multideterminantal initial guesses and by adaptively building a
compact exponential ansatz from a pool of qubit excitation generators. We apply
MS-iQCC to H$_4$, H$_2$O, N$_2$, and C$_2$, including strongly correlated
geometries, and observe robust convergence of all targeted state energies to
chemically meaningful accuracy across their potential energy surfaces.

## 海特勒-伦敦模型中的筛选：重新审视氢分子的成键和反键状态

**Original Title:** Screening in the Heitler-London Model: Revisiting the Bonding and Antibonding States of the Hydrogen Molecule

**Authors:** Washington P. da Silva, Daniel Vieira, Jonas Maziero, Edgard P. M. Amorim

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.01132v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.01132v1

**Authors:** Caleb Blumenfeld

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.02556v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.02556v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-04


### 摘要

我们试图修改无时间卷积主方程（TCL-ME）以更抗崩溃。我们通过采用Moore-Penrose逆来消除生成器的一部分可逆的标准假设。我们使用Israel和Charnes的结果重新推导出微扰展开，并分别在Jaynes-Cummings和Ising模型上测试该方程高达六阶和五阶。我们发现，在这两种情况下，由于修改后的方程的项随着浴的维度呈指数级缩放，因此与标准TLC相比，修改后的方程未能捕捉到精确解的动态，并将这种失败与扰动膨胀的收敛损失联系起来。


### Abstract

We attempt to modify the time-convolutionless master equation (TCL-ME) to be
more resistant to breakdown. We remove the standard assumption that a portion
of the generator is invertible by instead taking the Moore-Penrose inverse. We
rederive the perturbative expansion using Israel and Charnes' result, and test
the equation up to sixth and fifth orders on the Jaynes-Cummings and Ising
models, respectively. We find that in both cases, the modified equation fails
to capture the dynamics of the exact solution compared to the standard TCL due
to the terms of the modified equation scaling exponentially with the dimension
of the bath, and connect this failure to a loss of convergence of the
perturbative expansion.

maximal. As a result, non-chaotic systems can generate exponential or even
