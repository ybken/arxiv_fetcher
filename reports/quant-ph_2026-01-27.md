## 嵌入共振器的超导量子比特网络中的玻色凝聚和Bogoliubov激发

**Original Title:** Bose condensation and Bogoliubov excitation in resonator-embedded superconducting qubit network

**Authors:** Patrick Navez, Valentina Di Meo, Berardo Ruggiero, Claudio Gatti, Fabio Chiarello, Alessandro D'Elia, Alessio Rettaroli, Emanuele Enrico, Luca Fasolo, Mikhail Fistul, Ilya Eremin, Alexandre Zagoskin, Paolo Vanacore, Paolo Silvestrini, Mikhail Lisitskiy

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.15101v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.15101v2

**Submitted:** 2026-01-21


### 摘要

嵌入低损耗共振器中的导电量子位网络（SQN）是一种有前途的设备，不仅可以在宏观尺度上建立集体量子动力学，还可以大大提高微波量子探测器的灵敏度。由SQN和共振器的微波量子之间的耦合提供的量子ac Stark效应导致了量子之间的强烈非线性相互作用。在这里，我们提出了一个双音光谱实验，其中一组10个超导通量量子比特被耦合到输入R-共振器和输出T-传输线。接近共振频率的外部微波泵场在宏观上填充共振器模式，作为玻色-爱因斯坦凝聚体，而第二个探测束扫描共振，也称为博戈柳博夫样激发。从传输系数测量的相应激励频率，|S21（f）|一旦泵场的功率超过临界值Pcl，则显示出共振骤降位置的突然变化。这种急剧的漂移发生在泵频的狭窄区域中，并且可以通过施加的磁场进行调节。这是共振器内部量子数双稳性的标志，与理论一致。


### Abstract

Superconducting qubit networks (SQNs) embedded in a low-dissipative resonator is a promising device allowing one not only to establish the collective quantum dynamics on a macroscopic scale but also to greatly enhance the sensitivity of detectors of microwave photons. A quantum ac Stark effect provided by coupling between an SQN and microwave photons of a resonator, leads to a strong nonlinear interaction between photons. Here, we present a two-tone spectroscopy experiment in which a set of 10 superconducting flux qubits is coupled to the input R- resonator and the output T- transmission line. An external microwave pump field close to the resonance frequency populates macroscopically the resonator mode as a Bose-Einstein condensate, while a second probe beam scans the resonances referred also as Bogoliubov-like excitations. The corresponding excitation frequency measured from the transmission coefficient, |S21(f)| displays an abrupt change of the resonant dip position once the power of the pump field overcomes a critical value Pcr. This sharp shift occurs in a narrow region of pump frequencies, and can be tuned by an applied magnetic field. It is a signature of bistability of the photon number inside the resonator, in agreement with theory.

## 稳定器码通道转换超越重复码以改善哈希界

**Original Title:** Stabilizer-Code Channel Transforms Beyond Repetition Codes for Improved Hashing Bounds

**Authors:** Tyler Kann, Matthieu R. Bloch, Shrinivas Kudekar, Ruediger Urbanke

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.15505v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.15505v2

**Submitted:** 2026-01-21


### 摘要

量子哈希界限保证无记忆泡利通道可以实现高达$1-H（p_I，p_X，p_Y，p_Z）$的速率，但通常并不严格。提高某些不对称泡利通道可实现速率的已知方法是将小的内部稳定器代码应用于少数通道使用、解码并将产生的逻辑噪音视为诱导泡利通道;将哈希参数重新应用于此诱导通道可以超过基线哈希界限。我们将这种诱导通道观点推广到纯粹用作通道变换的任意稳定器代码。给定任何$ [\！[ n，k ]\！] $稳定器生成器集，我们构建了一个全辛表，计算物理泡利通道下逻辑泡利错误和伴随式的诱导联合分布，并通过具有解码器侧信息的哈希界获得可实现的速率。我们对小型转换和报告实例执行结构化搜索，以改善Pauli通道家族的基线哈希界限，这些通道具有在之前的工作中研究过的倾斜和独立误差。


### Abstract

The quantum hashing bound guarantees that rates up to $1-H(p_I, p_X, p_Y, p_Z)$ are achievable for memoryless Pauli channels, but it is not generally tight. A known way to improve achievable rates for certain asymmetric Pauli channels is to apply a small inner stabilizer code to a few channel uses, decode, and treat the resulting logical noise as an induced Pauli channel; reapplying the hashing argument to this induced channel can beat the baseline hashing bound. We generalize this induced-channel viewpoint to arbitrary stabilizer codes used purely as channel transforms. Given any $ [\![ n, k ]\!] $ stabilizer generator set, we construct a full symplectic tableau, compute the induced joint distribution of logical Pauli errors and syndromes under the physical Pauli channel, and obtain an achievable rate via a hashing bound with decoder side information. We perform a structured search over small transforms and report instances that improve the baseline hashing bound for a family of Pauli channels with skewed and independent errors studied in prior work.

## 宽带拉姆齐干涉法揭示了碳化硅中3/2旋转硅空位中的观众跳变串烧

**Original Title:** Spectator-transition crosstalk in a spin-3/2 silicon vacancy qudit in silicon carbide revealed by broadband Ramsey interferometry

**Authors:** Jun-Jae Choi, Seung-Jae Hwang, Seoyoung Paik, Juhwan Kim, Jawad UI-Hassan, Nguyen Tien Son, Hiroshi Abe, Takeshi Ohshima, Jaekwon Suk, Hyeon-Ho Jeong, Dong-Hee Kim, Sang-Yun Lee

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.15559v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.15559v2

**Submitted:** 2026-01-22


### 摘要

4 H-SiC中的色心旋转提供了芯片级材料成熟度与长旋转相关性和芯片级光电子学的罕见组合，使其成为可扩展量子技术的有前途的构建模块。特别是，硅空位具有S=3/2的接地状态，这是一种本地qudit，可以实现紧凑的编码和子空间选择性控制，但也引入旁观者跳变：短的、去调谐的脉冲可以连贯地驱动非编址的水平对并产生串话。在这里，我们使用宽带拉姆齐干涉术来揭示和量化这种旁观者过渡串烧。在实验上，拉姆齐傅里叶光谱显示了超出所解决的单量子转变的多条线。从分析上讲，我们将每条线映射到旋转框架Hamilton量之间的成对能量差，并通过由准备状态和微波脉冲参数设置的紧凑幅度来分配其权重，从而预测确定性的六分支结构。实验采样的数值时间域传播再现了失调图，并且测量的峰值位置与未经频率匹配的分析分支线重合。这些结果共同为硅空位中的多层控制提供了一个实用的、观众感知的框架。该方法为抑制串话或相反利用观众线提供了明确的指导，例如作为现场脉冲校准以及相敏量子状态和过程估计的额外约束。


### Abstract

Color center spins in 4H-SiC offer a rare combination of wafer-scale materials maturity with long spin coherence and chip-level photonics, making them promising building blocks for scalable quantum technologies. In particular, the silicon vacancy hosts an S=3/2 ground state, a native qudit that enables compact encodings and subspace-selective control, but also introduces spectator transitions: short, detuned pulses can coherently drive non-addressed level pairs and create crosstalk. Here we use broadband Ramsey interferometry to reveal and quantify such spectator-transition crosstalk. Experimentally, the Ramsey Fourier spectra display multiple lines beyond the addressed single-quantum transition. Analytically, we map each line to a pairwise energy difference between qudit levels of the rotating-frame Hamiltonian and assign its weight via compact amplitudes set by the prepared state and the microwave pulse parameters, predicting a deterministic six-branch structure. Numerical time-domain propagation with the experimental sampling reproduces the detuning map, and the measured peak positions coincide with the analytic branch lines without frequency fitting. Together these results provide a practical, spectator-aware framework for multilevel control in the silicon vacancy qudit. The approach offers clear guidance to suppress crosstalk or, conversely, to exploit spectator lines, for example as additional constraints for in situ pulse calibration and for phase-sensitive quantum state and process estimation.

## Rabi驱动电子旋转量子位的修正Lindblad方程，具有隧道效应

**Original Title:** A modified Lindblad equation for a Rabi driven electron-spin qubit with tunneling to a Markovian lead

**Authors:** Emily Townsend, Joshua Pomeroy, Garnett W. Bryant

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.16306v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.16306v2

**Submitted:** 2026-01-22


### 摘要

当量子点的旋转状态由振荡磁场驱动时，我们推导出了与马尔科夫引线耦合的量子点隧道状态的修改Lindblad方程。我们证明该方程是一个完全正的、保留痕迹的映射，并找到跳跃操作符。这是一个驱动-消散状态，其中相干驱动与隧道效应相关，并且不能被视为简单地用静态磁场下产生的浴来修改系统的旋转。这项工作的动机是确定量子点（旋转量子位）上电子旋转的塞曼分裂的实验愿望，在相关工作中，我们表明可以通过在扫描驱动场的频率时测量点的电荷占有率来找到这种分裂能量\ arXiv：2503.17481。在这里，我们涵盖了方程的全部推导过程，并给出了跳跃算子。这些跳跃算子是潜在的有用的描述更复杂的系统的随机行为与相干驱动的自旋能够隧穿或关闭的设备，如在电子自旋共振扫描隧道显微镜。跳跃算子有一个有趣的特性，即把电子跳跃到器件上和从器件上跳下来结合起来。


### Abstract

We derive a modified Lindblad equation for the state of quantum dot tunnel coupled to a Markovian lead when the spin state of the dot is driven by an oscillating magnetic field. We show that the equation is a completely positive, trace-preserving map and find the jump operators. This is a driven-dissipative regime in which coherent driving is relevant to the tunneling and cannot be treated as simply a rotation modifying the system with a bath derived under a static magnetic field. This work was motivated by an experimental desire to determine the Zeeman splitting of an electron spin on a quantum dot (a spin qubit), and in a related work we show that this splitting energy can be found by measuring the charge occupancy of the dot while sweeping the frequency of the driving field \ arXiv:2503.17481. Here we cover the full derivation of the equation and give the jump operators. These jump operators are potentially useful for describing the stochastic behavior of more complex systems with coherent driving of a spin capable of tunneling on or off of a device, such as in electron spin resonance scanning tunneling microscopy. The jump operators have the interesting feature of combining jumps of electrons onto and off of the device.

## 不同噪音环境下双量子干涉传感的最佳策略

**Original Title:** The optimal strategy of two-photon interferometric sensing in diverse noise environments

**Authors:** Teng-fei Yan, Zhuo-zhuo Wang, Qi-qi Li, Peng-long Wang, Rui-Bo Jin, Bai-hong Li

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.16517v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.16517v2

**Submitted:** 2026-01-23


### 摘要

基于双量子干涉测量的量子传感体现了超越经典精度极限的量子优越性。然而，这种优势通常不可避免地会被噪音削弱。在这里，我们分析了两种典型的双量子干涉术，即洪欧曼德尔（HOM）和N 00 N状态干涉术对噪音的敏感性。研究发现，HOM（N 00 N状态）干扰取决于双子频率差（和），实际上无论是光谱非分辨检测还是分辨检测，对相噪都不敏感（敏感），这表明了它们在不同噪音场景下的潜在应用。此外，对于两种干涉测量术，光谱分辨检测优于光谱非分辨检测，特别是对于超过双子相干时间的范围。这些发现为双量子干涉传感在不同噪音环境中的实际应用提供了最佳策略。


### Abstract

Quantum sensing based on two-photon interferometry manifests quantum superiority beyond the classical precision limit. However, this superiority is usually diminished inevitably by the noise. Here, we analyze the sensitivity of two typical two-photon interferometries to the noise, that is, Hong-Ou-Mandel (HOM) and N00N state interferometry. Furthermore, spectrally resolved detection outperforms spectrally non-resolved one for the two interferometries, especially for the scope that exceeds the coherence time of biphotons. The findings provide an optimal strategy for the practical applications of two-photon interferometric sensing in diverse noise environments.

## 量化超冷分子碰撞和化学反应中纠缠产生的通用框架

**Original Title:** General framework for quantifying entanglement production in ultracold molecular collisions and chemical reactions

**Authors:** Adrien Devolder, Paul Brumer, Timur Tscherbul

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.17144v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.17144v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-23


### 摘要

纠缠是量子力学的一个定义特征，自然产生于分子系统之间的相互作用。然而，分子碰撞和反应产物中纠缠的精确性质和量化在很大程度上仍然没有探索。在这里，我们表明碰撞分子的外部（运动）自由度和内部自由度之间的耦合会产生各种形式的产物状态纠缠：离散-离散、连续-连续体和混合离散-连续体。我们开发了一个通用的理论框架来直接从散射S矩阵元素量化这些纠缠形式，并识别出一类新型纠缠状态--多模式混合猫状态，其表现出多模式离散-连续体纠缠。尽管适用于任意碰撞能量，但形式主义在非弹性RB+SrF和RB+ SrF $^+$碰撞的超冷和冷状态以及化学反应F+HD $\rightarrow$ HF+D、DF+H中得到了说明。我们证明，纠缠可以在费什巴赫磁共振附近有效控制，为超冷分子碰撞中产物状态纠缠产生的精确磁控制铺平了道路。


### Abstract

Entanglement, a defining feature of quantum mechanics, arises naturally from interactions between molecular systems. Yet the precise nature and quantification of entanglement in the products of molecular collisions and reactions remain largely unexplored. Here, we show that coupling between the external (motional) and internal degrees of freedom of the colliding molecules generates diverse forms of product-state entanglement: discrete-discrete, continuum-continuum, and hybrid discrete-continuum. We develop a general theoretical framework to quantify these entanglement forms directly from scattering S-matrix elements and identify a novel class of entangled states-multimode hybrid cat states, that exhibit multimode discrete-continuum entanglement. Although applicable at arbitrary collision energies, the formalism is illustrated in the ultracold and cold regimes for inelastic Rb+SrF and Rb+Sr$^+$ collisions, as well as the chemical reaction F+HD $\rightarrow$ HF+D, DF+H. We demonstrate that entanglement can be efficiently controlled near magnetic Feshbach resonances, paving the way for precise magnetic control of product-state entanglement generation in ultracold molecular collisions.

## 高次调和产生的高精度半经典强场Herman-Kluk传播子方法

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.17164v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.17164v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-23


### 摘要

我们扩展了我们最近开发的半经典强场赫尔曼-克鲁克传播子（SFHK）方法来计算强激光中原子的高次谐振产生（HHG）。我们表明，我们的方法基于赫尔曼-克鲁克传播子和强场逼近的组合，可以为HHG产额和相提供高度准确的结果，与依赖于时的薛定汉方程的精确数值解的结果几乎相同。我们对我们的方法及其在HHG过程中的应用进行了详细分析，特别是重组时间。该方法的主要计算任务是求解原子势和激光-电子相互作用组合中活性电子的经典牛顿方程组。由相干状态建模的电子波包中心的运动由独立的经典轨迹控制，因此计算可以非常有效地并行化。


### Abstract

The main computational task in this method is to solve the classical Newton equations for the active electron in the combined atomic potential and laser-electron interaction. The motion of the centers of the electron wave packets, modeled by coherent states, is governed by independent classical trajectories so that the computation can therefore be parallelized very efficiently.

The exposition emphasized clarity and physical insight, making effective Hamiltonian methods accessible for teaching and learning in multimode light-matter interactions.

## Bayesian推理协议的熵效率

**Original Title:** Entropic Efficiency of Bayesian Inference Protocols

**Authors:** Nathan Shettell, Alexia Auffèves

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.17282v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.17282v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-24


### 摘要

推理是一种多功能工具，是科学发现、机器学习和日常决策的基础：它描述了代理如何在从多次测量中获取部分信息时更新概率分布，从而减少对系统潜在状态的无知。我们将推理效率定义为信息收益与累积存储擦除成本的比率，低效率是由测量系统与存储器之间和/或存储器与环境（噪音）之间未利用的相关性引起的。利用这种效率，我们对两种限制性测量范式进行基准测试：顺序（迭代利用相同的内存）和并行（同时利用许多内存）。在这两种情况下，最小擦除成本反映了记忆之间的相关性：时间顺序，空间并行。值得注意的是，当利用所有系统记忆相关性进行推理时，即使存在噪音，两种范式也能获得相同的最小擦除成本。相反，平行范式在存在未利用的相关性时表现得更好，这些相关性源于隐藏记忆的自由度。这种方法提供了一个量化的、基于物理的标准来比较推理策略、确定其效率并将目标信息收益与其最小的熵成本联系起来。


### Abstract

Inference is a versatile tool that underlies scientific discovery, machine learning, and everyday decision-making: it describes how an agent updates a probability distribution as partial information is acquired from multiple measurements, reducing ignorance about a system's latent state. We define an inferential efficiency as the ratio of information gain to cumulative memory erasure cost, with inefficiency arising from unexploited correlations between the measured system and memories, and/or between memories and environment (noise). Using this efficiency, we benchmark two limiting measurement paradigms: sequential, in which the same memory is exploited iteratively, and parallel, in which many memories are exploited simultaneously. In both cases, the minimal erasure cost reflects correlations across memories: temporal in sequential, spatial in parallel. Remarkably, when all system-memory correlations are exploited for inference, both paradigms attain the same minimal erasure cost, even in the presence of noise. Conversely, the parallel paradigm performs better in the presence of unexploited correlations, stemming from hidden memories' degrees of freedom. This approach provides a quantitative, physically grounded criterion to compare inference strategies, determine their efficiency, and link target information gains to their minimal entropic cost.

**Submitted:** 2026-01-25


### 摘要

## 量子密钥分发安全证明中的认证

**Original Title:** Authentication in Security Proofs for Quantum Key Distribution

**Authors:** Devashish Tupkary, Shlok Nahar, Ernest Y. -Z. Tan

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.17960v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.17960v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-25


### 摘要

量子密钥分发（QKD）协议依赖于认证的经典通信。典型的QKD安全证明是在理想化的环境中执行的，其中假设身份验证行为诚实：它永远不会中止，并且所有经典消息都忠实地传递，并保留其原始时间。在实践中可以构建的经过验证的渠道具有不同的属性。最关键的是，此类通道可能会不对称地中止，这样只有接收方可以检测到认证失败，而发送方却不知道。此外，对手可能会延迟、重新排序或阻止经典消息。这种差异使得标准QKD安全定义和现有QKD安全证明在实际认证设置中无效。在这项工作中，我们解决了这个问题。我们的主要结果是一个约简定理表明，在温和的和容易满足的协议条件下，任何QKD协议被证明是安全的诚实认证设置下仍然是安全的，在一个实际的认证设置。该结果允许所有现有的QKD证明通过轻微的协议调整可追溯提升到实际的身份验证设置。


### Abstract

Quantum Key Distribution (QKD) protocols rely on authenticated classical communication. Typical QKD security proofs are carried out in an idealized setting where authentication is assumed to behave honestly: it never aborts, and all classical messages are delivered faithfully with their original timing preserved. Authenticated channels that can be constructed in practice have different properties. Most critically, such channels may abort asymmetrically, such that only the receiving party may detect an authentication failure while the sending party remains unaware. Furthermore, an adversary may delay, reorder, or block classical messages. This discrepancy renders the standard QKD security definition and existing QKD security proofs invalid in the practical authentication setting. In this work we resolve this issue. Our main result is a reduction theorem showing that, under mild and easily satisfied protocol conditions, any QKD protocol proven secure under the honest authentication setting remains secure under a practical authentication setting. This result allows all existing QKD proofs to be retroactively lifted to the practical authentication setting with a minor protocol tweak.
