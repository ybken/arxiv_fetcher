## 4D中的个位二次量子引力

**Original Title:** Unitary Quadratic Quantum Gravity in 4D

**Authors:** K. Sravan Kumar, João Marto

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19707v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.19707v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-21


### 摘要

在二次引力中，具有正的Weyl平方系数，额外的2号旋区被证明对应于双倒置谐振子，而不是幽灵。使用怀特曼谱条件，我们证明了相关的Källén-Lehmann谱密度消失，反映了可正规化的基本状态的缺乏和传播子极点的类空性质。这将传播子唯一地固定到主值形式，作为一个定理，而不是一个规定。光学定理得到满足，双重IHO spin-2不是渐进状态，并且在所有循环阶次下仅给出虚拟贡献。因此，统一性与可再规范化性保持一致。


### Abstract

In quadratic gravity, with a positive Weyl squared coefficient, the extra spin-2 sector is shown to correspond to a dual inverted harmonic oscillator, instead of a ghost. Using the Wightman spectrum condition, we prove that the associated Källén--Lehmann spectral density vanishes, reflecting the absence of a normalizable ground state and the spacelike nature of the propagator pole. This uniquely fixes the propagator to a principal value form as a theorem, not a prescription. The optical theorem is satisfied, the dual IHO spin-2 is not an asymptotic state, and gives only virtual contributions at all loop orders. As a result, unitarity is preserved consistently with renormalizability.

## 仿射陪集结构的高围长规则量子低脂码

**Original Title:** High-Girth Regular Quantum LDPC Codes from Affine-Coset Structures

**Authors:** Koki Okada, Kenta Kasai

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20838v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.20838v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-22


### 摘要

我们从长度为-$512$ Calderbank--Shor--Steane基矩阵对构建量子低密度宇称检查码族。碱基对与已知的SPC（3）产品CSS代码置换等效，并且当前的仿射陪集描述直接证明了两个坦纳图都是$（3，8）$-正规的，周长为$8$。基本代码具有参数$[[512，174，8]]$。然后我们应用循环置换矩阵（CPM）提升。主要解码实验在代码容量去极化模型下使用提升因子$P=32$的CPM提升码，其参数为$[[16384，4142，\le 40]]$。具有后处理的信念传播解码器在$p=0.085$时实现了约10 ' s的帧错误率，并且观察到的一个权重为$40$的逻辑剩余给出了解码器派生的上界$d\le 40$。


### Abstract

We construct a quantum low-density parity-check code family from a length-$512$ Calderbank--Shor--Steane base matrix pair. The base pair is permutation-equivalent to the known SPC(3) product CSS code, and the present affine-coset description gives a direct proof that both Tanner graphs are $(3,8)$-regular with girth $8$. The base code has parameters $[[512,174,8]]$. We then apply circulant permutation matrix (CPM) lifts. The main decoding experiment uses the CPM-lifted code with lift factor $P=32$, which has parameters $[[16384,4142,\le 40]]$, under the code-capacity depolarizing model. A belief-propagation decoder with post-processing achieved frame error rate about $10^{-8}$ at $p=0.085$, and one observed logical residual of weight $40$ gives a decoder-derived upper bound $d\le 40$.

## 一款针对贫瘠高原感知量子模拟器设计的四人潜在游戏

**Original Title:** A four-player potential game for barren-plateau-aware quantum ansatz design

**Authors:** Rubén Darío Guerrero

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21955v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.21955v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-23


### 摘要

我们将参数化量子电路的设计视为一个四人潜在游戏，其状态是电路有向无环图（DAB），其参与者编码可训练性、非稳定性、任务性能和硬件成本。每个玩家的限制动作集将移动空间分解为附加、删除、重新输入和重新布线操作;块坐标$\varepð $-Nash剩余$δ_\text{Nash}$证明没有单个玩家可以单方面改进。MaxCut $K_4$上的单次权重扫描追踪从Clifford端点$（M_2/n，\langle H\rangle）=（0，4.00）$到非Clifford端点$（0.48，3.30）$的帕累托边界。基于三种四量子位硬件布局（重十六进制，$2乘以2$网格，Rydberg all to all），纳什搜索实现了最高的平均潜力;在$2乘以2$网格上，纳什在五个种子中的两个上达到了理论上限$®_\text{max}=4.10$，而模拟-退变基线在一个上达到了这一点;对五个种子的配对Wilcoxon测试不能拒绝任何单个拓扑上的空值（$p\ge 0.22$）。在LiH/STO-3G上，从58门Givens双打模拟器中播种Nash会产生48操作、深度25的电路，保留97.7%美元的相关能量，同时减少门数、增加非稳定性并控制可训练性。该框架是ADAPT-VQE和k-UpCCGSD等纯能量搜索的补充，它们可以通过更少的操作达到化学准确度，但不会优化其他三个轴。


### Abstract

We cast the design of parameterized quantum circuits as a four-player potential game whose state is a circuit directed acyclic graph (DAG) and whose players encode trainability, non-stabilizerness, task performance, and hardware cost. Per-player restricted action sets factorize the move space into append, remove, retype, and rewire operations; a block-coordinate $\varepsilon$-Nash residual $δ_\text{Nash}$ certifies that no single player can improve unilaterally. A single weight sweep on MaxCut $K_4$ traces a Pareto frontier from a Clifford endpoint $(M_2/n,\langle H\rangle)=(0,4.00)$ to a non-Clifford endpoint $(0.48,3.30)$. On three four-qubit hardware topologies (heavy-hex, $2\times 2$ grid, Rydberg all-to-all), Nash search achieves the highest mean potential; on the $2\times 2$ grid Nash reaches the theoretical ceiling $Φ_\text{max}=4.10$ on two of five seeds while the simulated-annealing baseline does so on one; paired Wilcoxon tests over five seeds cannot reject the null on any single topology ($p\ge 0.22$). On LiH/STO-3G, seeding Nash from a 58-gate Givens-doubles ansatz produces a 48-operation, depth-25 circuit retaining $97.7\%$ of the correlation energy while simultaneously reducing gate count, increasing non-stabilizerness, and controlling trainability. The framework is complementary to energy-only searches such as ADAPT-VQE and k-UpCCGSD, which reach chemical accuracy with fewer operations but do not optimize the other three axes.

## 复合量子门同时补偿多个错误

**Original Title:** Composite quantum gates simultaneously compensated for multiple errors

**Authors:** Hristo Tochev, Nikolay Vitanov

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21594v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.21594v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-23


### 摘要

系统控制错误仍然是实现高保真度单量子位门的主要障碍。我们引入复合脉冲序列，实现X门和阿达玛门，同时补偿幅度（拉比频率）、失调（频率）和持续时间误差。我们的结构使用两种补充策略：（i）通过凯莱-克莱因参数化公式化，在全单位（而不仅仅是转移概率）中基于求导的误差项抵消，以及（ii）在规定的误差范围内直接最小化平均门不忠实度。我们推导出具有封闭形式相的对称五脉冲解，可以抵消所有一阶项（包括混合求导），并对较长的序列（最多15个脉冲）进行数值优化，以实现更高阶抑制。我们还表明，标准的“通用”五脉冲序列（U 5 a/U 5 b）作为我们的对称解决方案的简单移相实例出现，从而对失调和幅度误差产生广泛的鲁棒性。最后，我们为$R_x（pi/2）$构建变面积序列，该序列在虚拟Z旋转之前对阿达玛门进行基准测试。在所有系列中，我们观察到序列长度和稳健性窗口之间的预期权衡，在大误差域上，保真度大幅提高。


### Abstract

Systematic control errors remain a primary obstacle to realizing high-fidelity single-qubit gates. We introduce composite pulse sequences that implement X and Hadamard gates while simultaneously compensating amplitude (Rabi-frequency), detuning (frequency), and duration errors. Our construction uses two complementary strategies: (i) derivative-based cancellation of error terms in the full unitary (not just the transition probability), formulated via the Cayley-Klein parametrization, and (ii) direct minimization of the average gate infidelity over prescribed error ranges. We derive symmetric five-pulse solutions with closed-form phases that cancel all first-order terms (including the mixed derivative), and numerically optimize longer sequences -- up to 15 pulses -- to achieve higher-order suppression. We also show that standard ``universal'' five-pulse sequences (U5a/U5b) emerge as simple phase-shifted instances of our symmetric solutions, yielding broad robustness to both detuning and amplitude errors. Finally, we construct variable-area sequences for $R_x(π/2)$, which, up to virtual Z rotations, benchmark the Hadamard gate. Across all families we observe the expected trade-off between sequence length and robustness window, with substantial boosts in fidelity over large error domains.

## 对角线上的奇异物理学：用量子断层扫描限制违反CP的SMEFT

**Original Title:** Odd Physics Off the Diagonal: Constraining CP-violating SMEFT with Quantum Tomography

**Authors:** Avalon Roberts, Patrick Dougan, Alexander Oh, Savanna Shaw

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21857v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.21857v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-23


### 摘要

除了标准模型（SM）中描述的之外，需要新的电荷宇称（CP）违反来源来解释观察到的物质--宇宙的反物质不对称性。标准模型有效场论（SMEFT）提供了一个框架，以与模型无关的方式引入CP奇物理学的额外电弱源。然而，这些违反CP的签名大多退化为极化盲可观测量中的CP甚至SMEFT操作员，仅在使用方位角衰减角的SM-New Physics（NP）干涉中才能区分。使用量子断层扫描技术，我们提出了一种限制这些NP效应的新方法。通过重建双玻色子系统的旋转密度矩阵（STM），我们超越了“干涉复活”，利用Beyond-SM物理学的完整特征，包括纯二次NP项。我们表明，这种方法对CP偶数和CP奇数贡献的特征特征（包括传统角度可观测量未完全捕捉到的影响）提供了卓越的同时敏感性。


### Abstract

New sources of charge-parity (CP) violation beyond those described in the Standard Model (SM) are required to explain the observed matter--antimatter asymmetry of the Universe. The Standard Model Effective Field Theory (SMEFT) provides a framework to introduce additional electroweak sources of CP-odd physics in a model-independent manner. However, these CP-violating signatures are mostly degenerate to CP-even SMEFT operators in polarisation-blind observables, distinguishable only in the SM-New Physics (NP) interference using the azimuthal decay angle. Using Quantum Tomography techniques, we present a new approach to constraining these NP effects. Reconstructing the spin density matrix (SDM) of a diboson system, we go beyond `interference resurrection' to exploit the full signature of the Beyond-SM physics, including the pure quadratic NP terms. We show that this approach provides superior simultaneous sensitivity to characteristic features of CP-even and CP-odd contributions, including effects not fully captured by traditional angular observables.

## 现实量子网络上的随机纠缠渗流

We illustrate this map for representative PDL models and discuss the resulting implications for entanglement percolation.

## 周期驱动广义Aubry-André模型中的Floquet移动性边缘和传输

**Original Title:** Floquet mobility edges and transport in a periodically driven generalized Aubry-André model

**Authors:** Jayashis Das, Vatsana Tiwari, Manish Kumar, Auditya Sharma

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.21992v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.21992v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-23


### 摘要

我们研究了周期性电场驱动对广义Aubry-André模型的影响，该模型也称为Ganeshan-Pixley-Das Sarma（GPD）模型，该模型以众多移动性边缘而闻名。我们对驱动GPD模型的Floquet谱的研究揭示了两个不同的Floquet迁移率边的出现，一个有界区域中的离域-局部（DL）边，一个无界区域中的多重分维域-局部（ML）边。使用应用于高频有效Hamilton的阿维拉整体理论得出的分析结果，以及分数维和逆参与比等数值诊断，我们证明这些迁移率边缘可以通过电场驱动的幅度和频率有效控制。我们还在驱动参数的特定值下识别驱动诱导的定位，对应于不存在准周期势的动态定位点。此外，周期性驱动GPD模型的动力学研究表明，在与DL边缘对应的有界区域中存在超扩散到几乎弹道传输，而在与ML边缘相关的无界区域中观察到亚扩散传输。我们还通过明确检查低频驱动机制来分析与高频有效描述的偏差，其中观察到光谱属性和传输行为的显着且违反直觉的偏差。我们的研究强调了准周期势和周期性变化的电场驱动的相互作用，作为在具有丰富光谱特征的系统中设计移动性边缘和控制传输的强大机制。


### Abstract

We investigate the effect of a periodic electric field drive on the generalized Aubry-André model, also known as the Ganeshan-Pixley-Das Sarma (GPD) model, which is well known as a host of mobility edges. Our study of the Floquet spectrum of the driven GPD model uncovers the emergence of two distinct Floquet mobility edges, a delocalized--localized (DL) edge in the bounded regime, and a multifractal--localized (ML) edge in the unbounded regime. Using analytical results derived from Avila's global theory applied to the high frequency effective Hamiltonian, together with numerical diagnostics such as the fractal dimension and inverse participation ratio, we demonstrate that these mobility edges can be effectively controlled by the amplitude and frequency of the electric field drive. We also identify drive-induced localization at specific values of the driving parameters, corresponding to dynamical localization points in the absence of quasiperiodic potential. Furthermore, the dynamical study of the periodically driven GPD model demonstrates superdiffusive to almost ballistic transport in the bounded regime corresponding to the DL edges, whereas subdiffusive transport is observed in the unbounded regime associated with the ML edges. We also analyze deviations from the high-frequency effective description by explicitly examining the low-frequency driving regime, where significant and counterintuitive deviations in both spectral properties and transport behavior are observed. Our study highlights the interplay of a quasiperiodic potential and a periodically varying electric field drive as a powerful mechanism to engineer mobility edges and control transport in systems with rich spectral features.

## 无时间卷积非马尔科夫量子主方程的展开：使用Fano-Anderson模型的案例研究

**Original Title:** Expansion of time-convolutionless non-Markovian quantum master equations: A case study using the Fano-Anderson model

**Authors:** Tim Alhäuser, Heinz-Peter Breuer

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22010v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.22010v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-23


### 摘要

我们使用Fano-Anderson模型作为测试用例来探索无时间卷积（TLC）投影运算符技术的性能。将精确的TLC主方程与系统-环境耦合强度的乘方展开进行比较，我们分析了瞬时动力学以及稳态行为。对于洛伦兹谱密度，我们证明了无因次展开参数对应于环境相关时间与系统松弛时间的比率，并推导出了TLC展开的收敛半径，这取决于失调比和谱密度的宽度。我们进一步研究了该模型的量子非马尔科夫性，该模型基于量子状态之间布雷斯距离的演变以及它如何由二阶和四阶展开来表示。我们的结果强调了TLC形式主义在捕捉开放量子系统关键特征方面的优势和局限性，特别是准确描述强耦合系统和非马尔科夫动力学的挑战。


### Abstract

We explore the performance of the time-convolutionless (TCL) projection operator technique using the Fano-Anderson model as a test case. Comparing the exact TCL master equation with an expansion in powers of the strength of the system-environment coupling, we analyze the transient dynamics as well as the steady-state behavior. For a Lorentzian spectral density we demonstrate that the dimensionless expansion parameter corresponds to the ratio of the environmental correlation time to the relaxation time of the system, and we derive the convergence radius for the TCL expansion, which is seen to depend on the ratio of detuning and width of the spectral density. We further study the quantum non-Markovianity of the model based on the evolution of the Bures distance between quantum states and how it is represented by the second and fourth order of the expansion. Our results highlight both the strengths and the limitations of the TCL formalism in capturing key features of open quantum systems and, in particular, the challenges of accurately describing strongly coupled systems and non-Markovian dynamics.

## 远程仅测量电路中的纠缠和信息扰乱

**Original Title:** Entanglement and information scrambling in long-range measurement-only circuits

**Authors:** Abigail McClain Gomez, Fiona Abney-McPeek, Hong-Ye Hu, Susanne F. Yelin, Ceren B. Dağ

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22022v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.22022v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-23


### 摘要

纯测量电路提供了一个最小设置，其中重复的局部投影可以生成或抑制多体纠缠，从而引起测量引起的相转变和动力学机制，而这些机制可能没有单位对应物。在这里，我们研究了具有远程两量子位宇称检查的一维纯测量Clifford电路中的纠缠和信息转变。通过调整每层的测量范围和密度，我们发现了一组广泛的相，其分类需要超越纠缠量的探测，例如互信息、三方互信息、辅助净化和贝尔簇统计。我们使用两种协议的大规模Clifford模拟来绘制相图：随机基设计，其中每个测量从$\lbrace XX、YY、ZZ \rbrace$中随机选择，以及单基设计，其中基在每层内固定，但在层之间变化，从而为电路引入更多结构。   我们通过将基于复制的方法扩展到仅随机基测量的电路，将量子平均纠缠量映射到二维统计力学模型，并表明连续时间限制在稳态下产生有效的远程XX Hamilton。这种联系将观察到的体积定律到亚体积定律纠缠的转变联系到连续对称性破缺相和临界XY相之间的边界。引人注目的是，在结构化（单基）电路中，我们发现了一个阶段，其中体积定律和远程纠缠与辅助量子位的快速、与大小无关的纯化共存，并且没有加扰，强调了仅测量的电路作为有效准备高度纠缠和技术上有用的量子状态的有希望的途径。


### Abstract

Measurement-only circuits provide a minimal setting in which repeated local projections can either generate or suppress many-body entanglement, giving rise to measurement-induced phase transitions and dynamical regimes, that might have no unitary counterpart. Here we investigate entanglement and information transitions in one-dimensional measurement-only Clifford circuits with long-range two-qubit parity checks. By tuning both the measurement range and density per layer, we uncover a broad set of phases whose classification requires probes beyond entanglement entropy, such as mutual information, tripartite mutual information, purification from an ancilla, and Bell-cluster statistics. We map phase diagrams using large-scale Clifford simulations for two protocols: a random-basis design in which each measurement is randomly chosen from $\lbrace XX,YY,ZZ \rbrace$, and a single-basis design in which the basis is fixed within each layer but varies between layers, hence introducing more structure to the circuit.
  We map the trajectory-averaged entanglement entropy to a two-dimensional statistical mechanics model by extending a replica-based method to random-basis measurement-only circuits, and show that a continuous-time limit yields an effective long-range XX hamiltonian in the steady state. This connection links the observed volume-law to sub-volume-law entanglement transition to the boundary between a continuous symmetry broken phase and a critical XY phase. Strikingly, in structured (single-basis) circuits we find a phase in which volume-law and long-range entanglement coexists with rapid, size-independent purification of an ancilla qubit, and the absence of scrambling, highlighting measurement-only circuits as a promising route to efficiently preparing highly entangled and technologically useful quantum states.
