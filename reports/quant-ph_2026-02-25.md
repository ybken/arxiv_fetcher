## 用超精细相互作用探测原子核的微观结构

**Original Title:** The hyperfine interaction as a probe of the microscopic structure of the atomic nucleus

**Authors:** Denis Janković, Jean-Gabriel Hartmann, Johann Bartel, Hervé Molique, Ludovic Bonneau, Paul-Antoine Hervieux

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.18144v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.18144v2

**Submitted:** 2026-02-20


### 摘要

The study of highly charged electronic and muonic hydrogen-like ions, provides an intriguing way to probe the internal structure of their atomic nuclei. In this work, we use nuclear structure calculations to accurately calculate the hyperfine splitting of electronic and muonic hydrogen-like ions, focusing in particular on the incorporation of finite-volume corrections, such as Bohr-Weisskopf and Breit-Rosenthal, due to the penetration of the electron and muon wavefunction into the nuclear electric charge and magnetic dipole densities. These corrections are essential for refining our understanding of the nuclear magnetic dipole and electric quadrupole moments. Our simulations use a Skyrme-Hartree-Fock-BCS model known for its effectiveness in modeling well-deformed nuclei such as ${}^{159}\mathrm{Tb}^{64+}$ and ${}^{165}\mathrm{Ho}^{66+}$, with particular emphasis on ${}^{161,163}\mathrm{Dy}^{65+}$ isotopes. It can also be generalised to multi-electron ions by studying the hyperfine anomaly between two isotopes.

## 量子单例界的辛证明

**Original Title:** A Symplectic Proof of the Quantum Singleton Bound

**Authors:** Frederick Dehmel, Shilun Li

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.20186v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.20186v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-21


### 摘要

我们提出了稳定器量子误差纠正码的量子辛线性代数证明，以及线性代数参数的Lean 4形式化。该证明是用建模泡利运算符的有限维辛向量空间的语言来表达的，并依赖于基于距离的擦除可纠正性和清洁引理。使用辛稳定器框架内的计维参数，我们推导出任何[[n，k，d]]稳定器代码的界限\（k + 2（d - 1）\le n \）。这种方法隔离了该边界下的代数结构，并避免了基于信息量的证明中出现的较重的分析机制，同时仍然非常适合形式验证。


### Abstract

We present a symplectic linear-algebraic proof of the Quantum Singleton Bound for stabiliser quantum error-correcting codes together with a Lean4 formalisation of the linear-algebraic argument. The proof is formulated in the language of finite-dimensional symplectic vector spaces modelling Pauli operators and relies on distance-based erasure correctability and the cleaning lemma. Using a dimension-counting argument within the symplectic stabiliser framework, we derive the bound \( k + 2(d - 1) \le n \) for any [[n, k, d]] stabiliser code. This approach isolates the algebraic structure underlying the bound and avoids the heavier analytic machinery that appears in entropy-based proofs, while remaining well-suited to formal verification.

## trainsum --用于量子张量序列的Python包

**Original Title:** trainsum -- A Python package for quantics tensor trains

**Authors:** Paul Haubenwallner, Matthias Heller

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.20226v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.20226v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-23


### 摘要

我们介绍trainsum，这是一个多功能的Python包，用于使用多维量子张量序列进行计算：https://github.com/fh-igd-iet/trainsum。trainsum将Array API标准与opp_einsum一起使用，允许通过张量序列轻松逼近张量或函数，而与其形状或维度无关。一旦逼近，我们的包就可以使用量子张量序列执行正常的算术运算，包括加法、爱因斯坦总和和元素变换。因此，它可以用于通用计算，并应用于模拟、数据压缩、机器学习和数据分析。


### Abstract

We present trainsum, a versatile Python package for doing computations with multidimensional quantics tensor trains: https://github.com/fh-igd-iet/trainsum. Using the Array API standard together with opt_einsum, trainsum allows the effortless approximation of tensors or functions by tensor trains independent of their shape or dimensionality. Once approximated, our package can perform normal arithmetic operations with quantics tensor trains, including addition, Einstein summations and element-wise transformations. It can be therefore used for generic computations with applications in simulation, data compression, machine learning and data analysis.

## CQM：循环量子位映射

**Original Title:** CQM: Cyclic Qubit Mappings

**Authors:** Maxwell Poster, Sayam Sethi, Jonathan Baker

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.20123v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.20123v2

**Submitted:** 2026-02-23


### 摘要

量子计算机有望解决经典计算机上难以解决的选定问题。然而，有噪音的中规模量子（NISQ）时代设备目前容易出现各种错误来源。量子错误纠正（QEC）显示出作为实现容错量子计算的途径的希望。特别是，表面代码因其作为量子错误纠正代码的功效而在文献中无处不在，并且可以通过格子手术操作执行量子电路。如果有空间的话，格子手术还允许逻辑量子位在架构中操纵。用于短期演示的硬件在逻辑量子位中具有空间和时间变化的误差。通过围绕该布局操纵逻辑量子位，可以强制执行平均逻辑错误率（LER）。我们提出了循环量子位映射（CQM），这是一种在编译期间实施的动态重新映射技术，旨在通过扩展和收缩逻辑量子位来减轻硬件的异类性。除了LER平均之外，CQM还表现出了最初的承诺，因为它的执行时间负担和有效的资源利用率。


### Abstract

Quantum computers show promise to solve select problems otherwise intractable on classical computers. However, noisy intermediate-scale quantum (NISQ) era devices are currently prone to various sources of error. Quantum error correction (QEC) shows promise as a path towards fault tolerant quantum computing. Surface codes, in particular, have become ubiquitous throughout literature for their efficacy as a quantum error correcting code, and can execute quantum circuits via lattice surgery operations. Lattice surgery also allows for logical qubits to maneuver around the architecture, if there is space for it. Hardware used for near-term demonstrations have both spatially and temporally varying error results in logical qubits. By maneuvering logical qubits around the topology, an average logical error rate (LER) can be enforced. We propose cyclic qubit mappings (CQM), a dynamic remapping technique implemented during compilation to mitigate hardware heterogeneity by expanding and contracting logical qubits. In addition to LER averaging, CQM shows initial promise given it's minimal execution time overhead and effective resource utilization.

## 极紫外光蚀刻术的量子模拟

**Original Title:** Quantum Simulations for Extreme Ultraviolet Photolithography

**Authors:** Tyler D. Kharazi, Stepan Fomichev, Shu Kanno, Takao Kobayashi, Juan Miguel Arrazola, Qi Gao, Torin F. Stetina

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.20234v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.20234v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-23


### 摘要

极紫外线（UVA）曝光是半导体制造中最先进的工艺，但其空间分辨率从根本上受到源自92 eV下的吸收的“模糊”的限制，这会通过激发状态过程和电子级联在光致抗蚀剂中引发物理和化学变化。这些现象的准确建模需要高能衰变通道的精确从头算数据，特别是光吸收和光电子发射。由于模拟电子动力学时的禁止缩放，或者由于无法以有效的方式解析电离连续体，这些对于经典方法来说在计算上很困难。在这项工作中，我们提出了量子模拟算法来计算这些关键可观测量。首先，我们引入了一种经过优化的一致域光谱算法，以求解92 eV工作频率下的光吸收横截面积。其次，我们开发了一种第一量子化平面波模拟来计算光电子动能谱，利用实时动力学和能量窗口来平等地处理束缚和离域散射状态。此外，我们还为模型光致抗蚀剂单体4-碘-2-甲基苯酚（IMeRh）提供了逻辑资源估计，并证明可以使用大约200美元的逻辑量子位和每个电路总价值10亿美元的非Clifford门来解决92 eV的吸收灵敏度，最小实例的发射量约为103美元。更复杂的光电子发射算法对连续体进行了显式建模，每个电路的门成本为$\geq $$非Clifford门总数，$10#4 $发射，并且需要数千个逻辑量子位。这些结果建立了高保真度量子模拟，作为参数化克服半导体小型化中的电子模糊瓶颈所需的多尺度宏观模型的关键组成部分。


### Abstract

Extreme Ultraviolet (EUV) lithography is the state-of-the-art process in semiconductor fabrication, yet its spatial resolution is fundamentally limited by the ``blur'' originating from absorption of photons at 92 eV, which induce physical and chemical changes in the photoresist via excited state processes and electron cascades. Accurate modeling of these phenomena requires precise ab initio data for high-energy decay channels, specifically photoabsorption and photoelectron emission. These are computationally difficult for classical methods due to prohibitive scaling in simulating electron dynamics, or due to the inability to resolve the ionization continuum in an efficient manner. In this work, we present quantum simulation algorithms to compute these key observables. First, we introduce a coherent time-domain spectroscopy algorithm optimized to resolve the photoabsorption cross-section at the 92 eV operating frequency. Second, we develop a first-quantized plane-wave simulation to compute the photoelectron kinetic energy spectrum, utilizing real-time dynamics and energy windowing to treat bound and delocalized scattering states on equal footing. Additionally, we provide logical resource estimation for a model photoresist monomer, 4-iodo-2-methylphenol (IMePh), and demonstrate that 92 eV absorption sensitivity can be resolved using roughly $200$ logical qubits and $10^{9}$ total non-Clifford gates per circuit with approximately $10^3$ shots for the smallest instance. The more sophisticated photoemission algorithm that models the continuum explicitly, incurs gate costs of $\geq 10^{13}$ total non-Clifford gates per circuit, $10^4$ shots, and requires a few thousand logical qubits. These results establish high-fidelity quantum simulations as a key component to parameterize the multi-scale macroscopic models required to overcome the electron blur bottleneck in semiconductor miniaturization.

## 联合查找解码器有限阈值的证明

**Original Title:** Proof of a finite threshold for the union-find decoder

**Authors:** Satoshi Yoshida, Ethan Lake, Hayata Yamasaki

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.20238v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.20238v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-23


### 摘要

从实践和理论角度来看，实现强大错误抑制的快速解码器对于容差量子计算（FTQC）至关重要。表面代码的联合查找（UF）解码器被广泛认为是一个有前途的候选者，它提供几乎线性的时间复杂性和由数字证据支持的有利的经验错误抑制。然而，由于缺乏严格的阈值定理，UF解码器是否可以实现超出数值模拟中测试的错误模型和参数机制的故障容忍度。在这里，我们在电路级局部随机误差模型下为表面代码上的UF解码器提供了有限阈值的严格证明。为此，我们开发了一个改进的错误集群框架，该框架扩展了以前用于分析细胞自动机和重正化组解码器的技术，通过表明错误集群可以被更大的缓冲区分开，从而能够对UF解码器的行为进行分析控制。使用这一保证，我们进一步证明了并行UF解码器在代码大小方面的平均运行时间的准多对数上界。我们还表明，这个框架为贪婪解码器提供了有限阈值，贪婪解码器是一个更简单的解码器，复杂性较低，但经验错误抑制较弱。这些结果为基于UF的解码器在开发耐故障量子计算机中的实际使用提供了坚实的理论基础，同时为研究这些实际解码器的耐故障提供了统一的框架。


### Abstract

Fast decoders that achieve strong error suppression are essential for fault-tolerant quantum computation (FTQC) from both practical and theoretical perspectives. The union-find (UF) decoder for the surface code is widely regarded as a promising candidate, offering almost-linear time complexity and favorable empirical error suppression supported by numerical evidence. However, the lack of a rigorous threshold theorem has left open whether the UF decoder can achieve fault tolerance beyond the error models and parameter regimes tested in numerical simulations. Here, we provide a rigorous proof of a finite threshold for the UF decoder on the surface code under the circuit-level local stochastic error model. To this end, we develop a refined error-clustering framework that extends techniques previously used to analyze cellular-automaton and renormalization-group decoders, by showing that error clusters can be separated by substantially larger buffers, thereby enabling analytical control over the behavior of the UF decoder. Using this guarantee, we further prove a quasi-polylogarithmic upper bound on the average runtime of a parallel UF decoder in terms of the code size. We also show that this framework yields a finite threshold for the greedy decoder, a simpler decoder with lower complexity but weaker empirical error suppression. These results provide a solid theoretical foundation for the practical use of UF-based decoders in the development of fault-tolerant quantum computers, while offering a unified framework for studying fault tolerance across these practical decoders.

## 量子多体Hamilton的光谱抽取

**Original Title:** Spectral Decimation of Quantum Many-Body Hamiltonians

**Authors:** Feng He, Arthur Hutsalyuk, Giuseppe Mussardo, Andrea Stampiggi

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.20256v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.20256v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-23


### 摘要

我们发展了一个系统的理论，光谱抽取量子多体哈密顿，并表明它提供了一个定量的探测统计混合光谱的涌现对称性。统计混合物的分析描述的基础上，我们推导出一个明确的表达式的特征对称部门（CSS）的大小，定义为表现出非泊松相关性的水平的最大子序列。CSS尺寸被证明是潜在的对称部门的大小偏置的平均值，建立光谱统计和希尔伯特空间结构之间的直接联系。我们将这个框架应用到两个范例设置：希尔伯特空间碎片化和无序诱导的多体定位（MBL）。在碎片化的系统中，即使全谱看起来几乎是Poissonian，CSS也会重现混合预测并隔离相关子部门。在无序的海森堡链中，谱抽取揭示了通过缩小的CSS逐渐出现的可积性，CSS的统计数据表现出与运动的局部积分一致的特征。我们引入特征对称性熵（CSE）作为有限大小缩放可观察值，并在可访问的系统大小内提取交叉指数。我们的结果将谱抽取建立为对多体谱中隐藏结构的受控、无偏且计算成本低的诊断，能够区分混乱动力学、统计混合和涌现可积性。


### Abstract

We develop a systematic theory of spectral decimation for quantum many-body Hamiltonians and show that it provides a quantitative probe of emergent symmetries in statistically mixed spectra. Building on an analytical description of statistical mixtures, we derive an explicit expression for the size of a characteristic symmetry sector (CSS), defined as the largest subsequence of levels exhibiting non-Poissonian correlations. The CSS dimension is shown to be the size-biased average of the underlying symmetry sectors, establishing a direct link between spectral statistics and Hilbert-space structure. We apply this framework to two paradigmatic settings: Hilbert-space fragmentation and disorder-induced many-body localization (MBL). In fragmented systems, the CSS reproduces the mixture prediction and isolates correlated subsectors even when the full spectrum appears nearly Poissonian. In the disordered Heisenberg chain, spectral decimation reveals the gradual emergence of integrability through a shrinking CSS, whose statistics exhibit signatures consistent with local integrals of motion. We introduce a characteristic symmetry entropy (CSE) as a finite-size scaling observable and extract, within accessible system sizes, the crossover exponents. Our results establish spectral decimation as a controlled, unbiased and computationally inexpensive diagnostic of hidden structure in many-body spectra, capable of distinguishing between chaotic dynamics, statistical mixtures, and emergent integrability.

## 时间晶体作为被动保护的振荡量子位

**Original Title:** Time Crystals as Passively Protected Oscillating Qubits

**Authors:** Mert Esencan, A. I. Lvovsky, Berislav Buča

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.20269v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.20269v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-23


### 摘要

在开放量子系统中保护信息免受去相干仍然是量子计算的核心挑战。特别是，被动错误纠正方案迄今为止仅限于静态存储器，而不是动态量子位。我们证明，驱动消散玻色子系统可以在无噪音子系统中编码持续振荡的量子位，该子系统明确地在玻色-哈伯德二聚体（BHD）中实现。该模型的强宇称对称性导致简并稳态。这种对称性在热力学极限下被进一步分解为非稳态，表现出持续的振荡。随着驱动力的增加，这些状态的刘维尔光谱具有相转变的特征。在转变点以上，非稳态对量子信息进行编码，将其保存在无噪音子系统中。除了同样影响两种玻色子模式的全局损失之外，我们还添加了全局去相并表明振荡量子位被保留。最后，为了获得额外的物理见解，我们研究了相扰动对两种模式的影响，并观察到它们同样受到被动保护，大约返回到其初始配置。这些结果建立了消散时间晶体动力学作为动态量子信息被动保护的机制，从而实现自主稳定的振荡量子位。


### Abstract

Protecting information against decoherence in open quantum systems remains a central challenge for quantum computing. In particular, passive error correction schemes have so far been limited to static memories rather than dynamical qubits. We demonstrate that a driven-dissipative bosonic system can encode a persistently oscillating qubit within a noiseless subsystem, realized explicitly in the Bose-Hubbard dimer (BHD). The strong parity symmetry of the model leads to degenerate stationary states. This symmetry is further broken into non-stationary states in the thermodynamic limit, which exhibit persistent oscillations. As the driving force increases, the Liouvillian spectrum of these states features a phase transition. Above the transition point, the non-stationary state encodes quantum information, preserving it in a noiseless subsystem. In addition to global loss that affects both bosonic modes identically, we further add global dephasing and show that the oscillating qubit is preserved. Finally, in order to gain additional physical insight, we study the effect of phase perturbation to both modes and observe that likewise they are passively protected, returning approximately to their initial configurations. These results establish dissipative time-crystalline dynamics as a mechanism for passive protection of dynamical quantum information, enabling autonomously stabilized oscillating qubits.

### 摘要

共振非弹性X射线散射（RIXS）是探测高容量阴极材料结构退化的主力实验技术。然而，由于缺乏准确的模拟，实验光谱的解释具有挑战性。在这项工作中，我们提出了一种量子算法来模拟假设在锂过量的阴极中形成的分子团簇的RIXS光谱。该算法使用量子相估计来对来自编码团簇价激发的散射转变幅度的状态的光谱进行采样。我们在量子计算机中使用偶极操作符的块编码和量子信号处理来准备这个状态，以在中间核心激发状态上实现格林函数传播子。为了展示该算法，我们使用了最近实验工作中提出的模型集群，该集群由键合到锰原子的氧二聚体组成。使用PennyLane软件平台，我们报告了模拟尺寸不断增加的化学激发活性空间的RIXS光谱的资源估计。对于具有20个轨道的经典挑战性活动空间，该算法需要2.0美元\x 10^{10}$ Toffoli门和414美元$逻辑量子位。


### Abstract

Resonant inelastic X-ray scattering (RIXS) is the workhorse experimental technique for probing the structural degradation of higher-capacity cathode materials. However, the interpretation of experimental spectra is challenging due to the lack of accurate simulations. In this work, we propose a quantum algorithm for simulating the RIXS spectrum of molecular clusters hypothesized to form in Li-excess cathodes. The algorithm uses quantum phase estimation to sample the spectrum from a state encoding the scattering transition amplitudes of the cluster valence excitations. We prepare this state in the quantum computer using a block-encoding of the dipole operator and quantum signal processing to implement the Green's function propagator over intermediate core-excited states. To showcase the algorithm, we use a model cluster proposed in recent experimental works consisting of an oxygen dimer bonded to a manganese atom. Using the PennyLane software platform, we report resource estimation for simulating RIXS spectra for chemically motivated active spaces of increasing sizes. For a classically challenging active space with 20 orbitals, the algorithm requires $2.0 \times 10^{10}$ Toffoli gates and $414$ logical qubits.

## 量子资源准备的脉冲级控制

### 摘要

最大限度地减少量子状态准备所需的时间对于减轻退相干并在近期硬件上实现实用量子算法至关重要。在这项工作中，我们引入了一种使用优化的电磁脉冲序列而不是离散量子门在跨元量子位系统中准备量子状态的技术。通过直接针对量子相关性而不是特定目标状态，我们确定了优化相关纠缠资源的最小时间脉冲协议，例如分别针对两个和三个量子位系统的并发性和三个纠缠。对于所考虑的品质因子，这种方法在每种情况下都成功实现了最大纠缠：Bell、GZZ和W类状态。除了状态准备之外，该方法的面向资源的性质导致控制方案的有效表达能力降低，这一功能在已知过度控制自由度会阻碍性能的算法环境中代表了优势。


### Abstract

For the figures of merit considered, this approach successfully achieves maximal entanglement in each case: Bell, GHZ and W like states. Beyond state preparation, the resource-oriented nature of the approach leads to a reduced effective expressivity of the control scheme, a feature that represents an advantage in algorithmic settings where excessive control freedom is known to hinder performance.

**Authors:** J. Thirunirai Selvam, S. Saravana Veni, Ria Rushin Joseph

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.20614v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.20614v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-24


### 摘要

这项工作基于Jaynes-Cummings模型，在IBM Quantum超导体处理器上实现，对与单一模式电磁场相互作用的三原子系统进行了数字量子模拟。qutrit使用两个物理量子位来编码来表示原子状态，而另一个量子位对截断场模式进行编码，从而实现有效的$~$型原子动力学。连续时间光与物质相互作用是通过使用Suzuki-Trotter分解离散化进化，以数字形式实现的。与模拟实现相反，数字模拟用参数受到显式控制的量子门序列取代了连续进化。使用校准的$R_Z$门对相互作用Hamilton量产生的相演变进行数字编码，其旋转角由物理相关的耦合尺度和选择的Trotter时间步固定。状态准备使用Hadamard和参数化旋转门实现，而相互作用动力学则通过受控操作实现。一阶和二阶Trotter实现之间的比较分析揭示了数字准确性和硬件引起的噪音之间的权衡。总体而言，结果表明，校准门操作和噪音感知电路设计能够可靠地数字模拟有噪音的中等规模量子平台上的多水平光与物质相互作用。


### Abstract

This work presents a digital quantum simulation of a three-level atomic system interacting with a single-mode electromagnetic field based on the Jaynes-Cummings model, implemented on IBM Quantum superconducting processors. A qutrit is encoded using two physical qubits to represent the atomic states, while an additional qubit encodes the truncated field mode, enabling the realization of effective $Λ$-type atomic dynamics.The continuous-time light-matter interaction is implemented in a digital form by discretizing the evolution using Suzuki-Trotter decomposition. In contrast to an analog realization, the digital simulation replaces the continuous evolution with a sequence of quantum gates whose parameters are explicitly controlled. Phase evolution arising from the interaction Hamiltonian is digitally encoded using calibrated $R_Z$ gates, whose rotation angles are fixed by the physically relevant coupling scale and the chosen Trotter time step.State preparation is achieved using Hadamard and parametrized rotation gates, while the interaction dynamics are implemented through controlled operations. A comparative analysis between first- and second-order Trotter implementations reveals a trade-off between digital accuracy and hardware-induced noise. Overall, the results demonstrate that calibrated gate operations and noise-aware circuit design enable reliable digital simulation of multi-level light-matter interactions on noisy intermediate-scale quantum platforms.

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.20674v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.20674v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-24


### 摘要

### Abstract

Numerical simulations demonstrate this potential, with $(G,1)$-compatibility achieving a 40%-55% gain in simultaneously supported tasks relative to the single-task baseline. By incorporating compatibility as a fundamental design objective, quantum networks can move beyond single-task optimization towards scalable, robust architectures that effectively balance proactive entanglement distribution and supplemental reactive coordination.

### 摘要

低密度Parity-Check（Low-Dose Parity-Check，简称Low Dose）码的解码是编码理论中的一个基本问题，而置信度传播（Belief Propagation，简称BP）是最流行的低密度Parity-Check码解码方法之一。然而，BP可能会遇到收敛问题和次优性能，特别是对于短长度码和高噪音通道。量子逼近优化算法（QAOA）是一种变分量子算法（VQA），旨在通过最小化特定问题的成本函数来解决组合优化问题。在本文中，我们通过制定一个结合了宇称检验约束和软信道可靠性信息的解码成本函数，提出了一种基于QAOA的低密度码解码框架。使用QAOA来解决由此产生的优化问题，以搜索与有效代码字对应的低能量配置。我们通过大量的数值实验测试了所提出的方法，并将其性能与BP解码进行了比较。实验结果表明，在多个实验设置中，基于QAOA的解码器比BP实现了更高的正确恢复传输的代码字的可能性。


### Abstract

Decoding Low-Density Parity-Check (LDPC) codes is a fundamental problem in coding theory, and Belief Propagation (BP) is one of the most popular methods for LDPC code decoding. However, BP may encounter convergence issues and suboptimal performance, especially for short-length codes and in high-noise channels. The Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm (QAOA) is a type of Variational Quantum Algorithm (VQA) designed to solve combinatorial optimization problems by minimizing a problem-specific cost function. In this paper, we present a QAOA-based decoding framework for LDPC codes by formulating a decoding cost function that incorporates both parity-check constraints and soft channel reliability information. The resulting optimization problem is solved using QAOA to search for low-energy configurations corresponding to valid codewords. We test the proposed method through extensive numerical experiments and compare its performance with BP decoding. The experimental results demonstrate that the QAOA-based decoder achieves a higher probability of correctly recovering the transmitted codeword than BP across multiple experimental settings.

### Abstract

We observe that, unlike the pristine case, these FNCs have the potential to manifest anomalous Hall conductivities (AHC), while the Néel vector orients along both in-plane and out-of-plane directions. ## Topological Floquet Green的函数为零

**Original Title:** Topological Floquet Green's function zeros

**Authors:** Elio J. König, Aditi Mitra

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.21199v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.21199v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-24


### 摘要

受使用有噪的中等规模量子（NISQ）设备的数字量子模拟的最新进展以及人们对凝聚物质系统中的拓学格林函数零点兴趣的增长的激励，我们在这里研究了拓学Floquet系统中的格林函数零点。我们专注于相互作用的类Kitaev Floquet链（或等效的横向场伊辛电路），并为相应的对称类BDI引入基于Floquet Green函数的拓扑不变量。在自由费米子相图中的特殊点附近，并使用定制的相互作用（导致对称质量生成的Floquet版本），我们分析计算了边缘和体格林函数。正如在连续体时间进化的情况下一样，格林函数零点的拓学带也可能对拓学不变量有贡献。然而，与连续体时间演变的情况相反，Floquet Green的函数即使在没有相互作用的情况下也可以有零。最后，我们还讨论了这个Floquet系统在数字量子模拟器中的实现：我们提出了一个电路，该电路对所考虑的相互作用进行编码，并确定携带有关拓扑格林函数边界零点信息的可观测量。


### Abstract

Motivated by recent advances in digital quantum emulation using noisy intermediate-scale quantum (NISQ) devices and an increased interest in topological Green's function zeros in condensed matter systems, we here study Green's function zeros in topological Floquet systems. Finally, we also discuss an implementation of this Floquet system in a digital quantum emulator: We present a circuit which encodes the interaction under consideration and pinpoint the observables carrying information about the topological Green's function boundary zeros.
