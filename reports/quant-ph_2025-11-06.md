## 萨格纳克音素干涉法测量旋转费米超流体的角动量

**Original Title:** Angular momentum of rotating fermionic superfluids by Sagnac phonon interferometry

**Authors:** Marcia Frómeta Fernández, Diego Hernández Rajkov, Giulia Del Pace, Nicola Grani, Massimo Inguscio, Francesco Scazza, Sandro Stringari, Giacomo Roati

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.02664v2

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.02664v2

**Submitted:** 2025-11-04


### 摘要

费米子多体系统提供了一个无与伦比的舞台来研究相互作用如何驱动集体量子行为的出现，例如宏观相干性和超流性。这些现象的核心是库珀对的形成，库珀对是两个费米子的相关态，它们表现为复合玻色子，并在临界温度以下凝聚。然而，与基本玻色子不同的是，这些对保留了由潜在的费米子相关性设定的内部结构，这对于理解所谓的玻色-爱因斯坦凝聚体（BEC）到巴丁-库珀-施里弗（BCS）交叉的超流体性质至关重要-这是强相关费米子物质的基石。在这里，我们利用光学Sagnac效应的声波模拟，以揭示跨BEC-BCS交叉的费米子凝聚体的复合性质。我们通过相干激发具有可调粒子间相互作用的环形费米子超流体的两个反向传播长波音素来实现原位环路干涉仪。在超流环中可控地注入量子化的超流后，时钟和逆时钟声音模式之间的频率简并会被解除，从而产生可测量的多普勒频移，使我们能够探测循环的基本量子和每个粒子携带的角动量。费米流体。我们的观察直接表明，超流环流以$h/2 m $量化，其中$m$是成分的质量，与玻色子凝聚物形成鲜明对比，其中$h/m$是相关的环流量子。此外，通过在可调温度下操作我们的干涉仪，我们测量了单位费米气体中超流体的热耗尽，证明了音素干涉术是探测强相关量子系统基本性质的一种强大技术。


### Abstract

Fermionic many-body systems provide an unrivaled arena to investigate how
interactions drive the emergence of collective quantum behavior, such as
macroscopic coherence and superfluidity. Central to these phenomena is the
formation of Cooper pairs, correlated states of two fermions that behave as
composite bosons and condense below a critical temperature. However, unlike
elementary bosons, these pairs retain their internal structure set by
underlying fermionic correlations, essential for understanding superfluid
properties throughout the so-called Bose-Einstein condensate (BEC) to
Bardeen-Cooper-Schrieffer (BCS) crossover-- a cornerstone of strongly
correlated fermionic matter. Here, we harness a sonic analog of the optical
Sagnac effect to disclose the composite nature of fermionic condensates across
the BEC-BCS crossover. We realize an in-situ loop interferometer by coherently
exciting two counter-propagating long-wavelength phonons of an annular
fermionic superfluid with tuneable interparticle interactions. The frequency
degeneracy between clock- and anticlock-wise sound modes is lifted upon
controllably injecting a quantized supercurrent in the superfluid ring,
resulting in a measurable Doppler shift that enables us to probe the elementary
quantum of circulation and the angular momentum carried by each particle in the
fermionic fluid. Our observations directly reveal that the superflow
circulation is quantized in terms of $h/2m$, where $m$ is the mass of the
constituents, in striking contrast to bosonic condensates where $h/m$ is the
relevant circulation quantum. Further, by operating our interferometer at
tunable temperature, we measure the thermal depletion of the superfluid in the
unitary Fermi gas, demonstrating phonon interferometry as a powerful technique
for probing fundamental properties of strongly-correlated quantum systems.

## 经典引力可以纠缠的简单原因

**Original Title:** The simple reason why classical gravity can entangle

**Authors:** Andrea Di Biagio

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.02683v2

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.02683v2

**Submitted:** 2025-11-04


### 摘要

自从引力诱导纠缠（GIE）实验被提出作为引力量子本质的见证以来，越来越多的与量子物质相结合的经典引力理论被证明可以预测GIE，尽管存在一些独立于理论的不去定理据称声称这不应该是可能的。这篇笔记解释了为什么这是可能的，以及为什么这使得GIE实验成为量子引力研究中更加紧迫的问题。


### Abstract

Ever since gravity-induced entanglement (GIE) experiments have been proposed
as a witness of the quantum nature of gravity, more and more theories of
classical gravity coupled to quantum matter have been shown to predict GIE,
despite the existence of several theory-independent no-go theorems purportedly
claiming that it should not be possible. This note explains why this is
possible, and why this makes the GIE experiments an even more urgent matter in
quantum gravity research.
