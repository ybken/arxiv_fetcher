## Rib 1-xV 2 Te 2 O中的G型反铁磁结构

**Original Title:** G-type antiferromagnetic structure in Rb1-xV2Te2O

**Authors:** Wu Xie, Changchao Liu, Fayuan Zhang, Zhenhong Tan, Wenhai Ji, Nan Zhao, Lingxiang Bao, Dong Zhang, Feiran Shen, Lunhua He, Hao Wang, Rong Du, Guanghan Cao, Chaoyu Chen, Ping Miao

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.17365v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.17365v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-19


### 摘要

交变磁性以其非相对论性的自旋分裂带结构而闻名，但补偿矩，正在被深入研究。发现新的具有适合实际应用的特性的交变磁性材料仍然是一个重要的正在进行的任务。最近，基于自旋分辨光电子能谱和扫描隧道显微镜/光谱（STM/STS）测量的实验结果，报道了一种具有d波自旋对称性的层状结构金属室温交流磁体候选物Rb 1-xV 2 Te 2 O。本文报道了用中子粉末衍射（NPD）研究Rb_（1-x）V_2 Te_2 O的磁结构，发现在337 K以下的转变温度下，Rb_（1-x）V_2 Te_2 O具有G型反铁磁结构。结果与最初的理论预期不同，这可能会导致对这种互磁候选物的物理学有新的见解。


### Abstract

Altermagnetism, known for its non-relativistic spin-split band structures with yet compensated moments, is being intensively investigated. Discovering new altermagnetic materials with characteristics suitable for practical use remains an important ongoing task. Recently a metallic room-temperature altermagnet candidate Rb1-xV2Te2O with a layered structure and d-wave spin symmetry has been reported based on experimental results from the spin-resolved photoemission spectroscopy and scanning tunnelling microscopy/spectroscopy (STM/STS) measurements. Here we report neutron powder diffraction (NPD) investigations on the magnetic structure of Rb1-xV2Te2O, which shows a G-type antiferromagnetic structure below the transition temperature of 337 K. The result is different from the original theoretical expectation, which might lead to new insights on the physics of this altermagnet candidate.

## 噪音中等规模量子计算机上的期权定价：量子神经网络方法

**Original Title:** Option Pricing on Noisy Intermediate-Scale Quantum Computers: A Quantum Neural Network Approach

**Authors:** Sebastian Zając, Rafał Pracht

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19832v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.19832v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-20


### 摘要

在名义价值高达数百万亿美元的全球衍生品市场中，定价模型的准确性和效率至关重要，对风险管理、资本配置和监管合规性有直接影响。在这项工作中，我们采用Black-Scholes-Merton（BSM）框架本身并不是目的，而是作为一个受控基准环境，在其中严格评估量子机器学习方法的能力。   我们提出了一种基于量子神经网络（QNN）的完全量子期权定价方法，并且据我们所知，在当前可用的量子硬件上展示了这种方法的首批实现之一。具体来说，我们研究QNN是否可以通过利用Hilbert空间的几何结构来有效地逼近期权定价函数。   我们的实现利用了紧凑的2量子位QNN架构，该架构在多个最先进的量子处理器上进行了评估，包括IBM Fez、IQM Garnet、IonQ Forte和Rigetti Ankaa-3。这项跨平台研究揭示了明显的硬件相关性能特征，同时证明，尽管受到噪音中等规模量子（NISQ）硬件的限制，但可以在不同设备上一致地实现准确的定价逼近。   结果提供了经验证据，证明基于QNN的方法构成了衍生品定价的可行框架。虽然分析是在BSM环境中进行的，但更广泛的意义在于这些方法的潜在扩展到更现实和计算要求更高的模型，包括局部波动率、随机波动率和实践中常用的利率框架。


### Abstract

In a global derivatives market with notional values in the hundreds of trillions of dollars, the accuracy and efficiency of pricing models are of fundamental importance, with direct implications for risk management, capital allocation, and regulatory compliance. In this work, we employ the Black-Scholes-Merton (BSM) framework not as an end in itself, but as a controlled benchmark environment in which to rigorously assess the capabilities of quantum machine learning methods.
  We propose a fully quantum approach to option pricing based on Quantum Neural Networks (QNNs), and, to the best of our knowledge, present one of the first implementations of such a methodology on currently available quantum hardware. Specifically, we investigate whether QNNs, by exploiting the geometric structure of Hilbert space, can effectively approximate option pricing functions.
  Our implementation utilizes a compact 2-qubit QNN architecture evaluated across multiple state-of-the-art quantum processors, including IBM Fez, IQM Garnet, IonQ Forte, and Rigetti Ankaa-3. This cross-platform study reveals distinct hardware-dependent performance characteristics while demonstrating that accurate pricing approximations can be achieved consistently across different devices despite the constraints of Noisy Intermediate-Scale Quantum (NISQ) hardware.
  The results provide empirical evidence that QNN-based approaches constitute a viable framework for derivative pricing. While the analysis is conducted within the BSM setting, the broader significance lies in the potential extension of these methods to more realistic and computationally demanding models, including local volatility, stochastic volatility, and interest rate frameworks commonly used in practice.

## Kerr-EEDA黑洞带电纯量场的灰体因子、共振频率和熵量子化

**Original Title:** Greybody Factor, Resonant Frequencies, and Entropy Quantization of Charged Scalar Fields in the Kerr-EMDA Black Hole

**Authors:** Nazım Sertkan, İzzet Sakallı

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19848v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.19848v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-21


### 摘要

我们研究了爱因斯坦-麦克斯韦-迪拉顿-轴子（EEDA）理论（称为Kerr-EEDA BH）旋转黑洞（BH）背景上的带电大质量纯量场扰动。从规范协变克莱因-戈登方程（KGE）开始，我们执行变量的完全分离，并根据汇合Heun函数（CHF）获得角度部分和半径部分的精确分析解。与Senjaya和Ponglertsakul早期的中性量处理不同[Eur. Phys. J. C \textBF{85}，352（2025）]，电磁耦合$q$从根本上改变了Heun参数的结构，并产生了定性的新物理学。应用HF多项条件，我们推导出虚部与$等距的共振频谱|Δω_I| = 1/（2 M）$，仅由BH质量确定的普适间距。通过Maggiore规定和BH热力学第一定律，这产生了一个依赖于参数的量子量子$δS_{\text{BH}} = 4 pi r_+/（r_+ - r_-）$，对于Schwarzschild来说，它减少到$4 pi $，但在极端情况下偏离-我们构造了控制纯量波散射的有效势，并分析了它对动量参数$D$、旋转$a$和纯量电荷$q$的依赖性。在无质量不带电极限下，HF简化为高斯超几何函数，{\colour {black}使我们能够计算Kerr-EEDA几何的第一分析灰体因子（GF）;我们表明这种简化扩展到无质量带电纯量，产生捕获超辐射放大的封闭形式GF。我们研究了线粒体变形如何将Kerr-EEDA光谱与标准Kerr和Kerr-Newman情况区分开来。


### Abstract

We study charged massive scalar field perturbations on the rotating black hole (BH) background of Einstein-Maxwell-Dilaton-Axion (EMDA) theory, known as the Kerr-EMDA BH. Starting from the gauge-covariant Klein-Gordon equation (KGE), we perform a full separation of variables and obtain exact analytical solutions for both the angular and radial parts in terms of confluent Heun functions (CHFs). Unlike the earlier neutral scalar treatment by Senjaya and Ponglertsakul [Eur. Phys. J. C \textbf{85}, 352 (2025)], the electromagnetic coupling $q$ fundamentally alters the structure of the Heun parameters and produces qualitatively new physics. Applying the CHF polynomial condition, we derive the resonant frequency spectrum whose imaginary parts are equispaced with $|Δω_I| = 1/(2M)$, a universal spacing determined solely by the BH mass. Via the Maggiore prescription and the first law of BH thermodynamics, this yields a parameter-dependent entropy quantum $δS_{\text{BH}} = 4πr_+/(r_+ - r_-)$, which reduces to $4π$ for Schwarzschild but diverges at extremality -- {\color{black}in contrast to the universal $2π$ obtained for the rotating linear dilaton BH (RLDBH).} We construct the effective potential governing scalar wave scattering and analyze its dependence on the dilaton parameter $D$, rotation $a$, and scalar charge $q$. In the massless uncharged limit, the CHF reduces to the Gauss hypergeometric function, {\color{black}enabling us to compute the first analytical greybody factor (GF) for the Kerr-EMDA geometry; we show that this reduction extends to massless charged scalars, yielding a closed-form GF that captures superradiant amplification.} We examine how the dilaton deformation distinguishes the Kerr-EMDA spectrum from the standard Kerr and Kerr-Newman cases.

## 半有限冯诺伊曼代数中通过Nussbaum-Szkoğa分布的量子$f$-分歧

**Original Title:** Quantum $f$-divergences via Nussbaum-Szkoła Distributions in Semifinite von Neumann Algebras

**Authors:** Theodoros Anastasiadis, George Androulakis

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19853v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.19853v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-21


### 摘要

本文证明了半有限vonNeumann代数上两个正常态之间的量子f-发散等于两个相应经典态之间的经典f-发散，称之为Nussbaum-Szkoenza分布.这一结果已被第二作者和T.C.~ John对于Hilbert空间$\mathscr {H}$上所有有界算子的von~Neumann代数$\mathbb{B}（\mathscr {H}）$上的正常态。我们将他们的结果推广到任何半有限von~Neumann代数上的正常态，而不仅仅是$\mathbb{B}（\mathscr{H}）$。


### Abstract

In this article, we prove that the quantum $f$-divergence between two normal states on a semifinite von~Neumann algebra is equal to the classical $f$-divergence between two corresponding classical states, which are called Nussbaum-Szkoła distributions. This result has been proved by the second named author and T.C.~John for normal states on the von~Neumann algebra $\mathbb{B}(\mathscr{H})$ of all bounded operators on a Hilbert space $\mathscr{H}$. We extend their result for normal states on any semifinite von~Neumann algebra, not only $\mathbb{B}(\mathscr{H})$.

## 迈向设计工作负载感知的Surface Code架构

**Original Title:** Toward designing workload-aware Surface Code Architectures

**Authors:** Archisman Ghosh, Avimita Chatterjee, Swaroop Ghosh

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19855v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.19855v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-21


### 摘要

实际的量子优势预计将取决于容差量子计算，尽管支持容差所需的架构负担仍然极高。先前的FTQC设计通常强调快速逻辑量子位可访问性，代价是大量量子位开销，或者强调高逻辑量子位密度，代价是增加工作负载延迟。我们提出了一种架构，通过在以辅助为中心的区域周围放置表面代码补丁来平衡这些相互竞争的目标，这为所有数据量子位提供了几乎统一的辅助访问。在此设计的基础上，我们引入了一种新的工作负载驱动的放置方法，该方法使用应用程序的$T$-gate配置文件来确定有效的布图规划。我们进一步提供了可重新配置的优化，以根据每个工作负载减少$Y$-gate测量的延迟。为了提高灵活性，我们还研究了在同一架构上并发执行多个程序。数值评估表明，我们的方法将每条指令的周期保持在最佳机制附近，同时将所需的数据切片数量减少高达$\sim21\%$，并在并发运行10个程序时实现高达$\sim90\%$的效率。


### Abstract

Practical quantum advantage is expected to depend on fault-tolerant quantum computing, although the architectural overhead needed to support fault tolerance is still extremely high. Prior FTQC designs generally emphasize either fast logical-qubit accessibility at the cost of significant qubit overhead, or high logical-qubit density at the cost of added workload latency. We propose an architecture that balances these competing objectives by placing surface-code patches around an ancilla-centric region, which yields nearly uniform ancilla access for all data qubits. Building on this design, we introduce a new workload-driven placement method that uses the $T$-gate profile of an application to determine an effective floorplan. We further provide a reconfigurable optimization for reducing the latency of $Y$-gate measurements on a per-workload basis. To improve flexibility, we also study concurrent execution of multiple programs on the same architecture. Numerical evaluation indicates that our approach keeps cycles per instruction near the optimal regime while reducing the number of required data tiles by up to $\sim21\%$, and achieves up to $\sim90\%$ efficiency when running 10 programs concurrently.

## 逻辑量子位的协同设计错误缓解和错误检测

**Original Title:** Co-Designing Error Mitigation and Error Detection for Logical Qubits

**Authors:** Rohan S. Kumar, Takahiro Tsunoda, Sophia H. Xue, Dantong Li, Robert J. Schoelkopf, Yongshan Ding

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19871v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.19871v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-21


### 摘要

近期量子工作负载需要错误管理，但两种最轻量级的技术--量子错误检测（QED）和概率错误消除（AEC）--具有互补的成本特征，其联合架构设计空间仍未被开发。QED编码逻辑量子位并丢弃错误标记的运行，以较低的量子位负载过滤噪音，但留下残余错误; AEC可以在软件中纠正这些错误，但噪音强度的代价是指数级。如果QED有效地降低了每个门的噪音，那么FEC的成本节省可以超过QED的丢弃费用;然而，实现这一点需要解决两个系统级设计挑战。   首先，\texttit {QED interval} --插入检测周期的频率--是管理成本准确性权衡的可调架构参数。我们推导出一个效率条件，并表明规范的每个门一个周期频率在我们评估的任何代码中都不会实现收支平衡，而高速率Iceberg代码上的优化间隔却能实现收支平衡。其次，我们发现天真的AEC +QED集成\textit{降低}准确性低于仅QED的基线。根本原因是第一个检测周期中的暂时错误配置文件破坏了FEC的噪音模型。我们开发了\texttit {稳态提取}，这是一种共同设计的特征化协议，可以隔离稳态错误行为，将估计偏差降低高达10.2美元\x $。在以固定射击预算运行QAOA（$p{=}4$--$8$）的$[[6，4，2]$ Iceberg代码上，相对于物理量子位的FEC，FEC +QED实现了2美元--$11，times $较低的绝对误差和高达31，000美元的MBE，每个间隔的节省随着间隔深度的增加而复合。


### Abstract

Near-term quantum workloads demand error management, yet the two lightest-weight techniques, Quantum Error Detection (QED) and Probabilistic Error Cancellation (PEC), have complementary cost profiles whose joint architectural design space remains unexplored. QED encodes logical qubits and discards error-flagged runs, filtering noise with low qubit overhead but leaving residual errors; PEC can correct these in software, but at exponential cost in noise strength. If QED efficiently reduces per-gate noise, PEC's cost savings can outweigh QED's discard overhead; realizing this, however, requires solving two system-level design challenges.
  First, the \textit{QED interval} -- how often detection cycles are inserted -- is a tunable architectural parameter governing the cost-accuracy tradeoff. We derive an efficiency condition and show that the canonical one-cycle-per-gate frequency does not achieve break-even in any code we evaluate, while optimized intervals on high-rate Iceberg codes do. Second, we discover that naive PEC+QED integration \textit{degrades} accuracy below the QED-only baseline. The root cause is a transient error profile in the first detection cycle that corrupts PEC's noise model. We develop \textit{steady-state extraction}, a co-designed characterization protocol that isolates steady-state error behavior, reducing estimation bias by up to $10.2\times$. On a $[[6,4,2]]$ Iceberg code running QAOA ($p{=}4$--$8$) with a fixed shot budget, PEC+QED achieves $2$--$11\times$ lower absolute error and up to $31\times$ lower MSE versus PEC on physical qubits, with per-interval savings compounding over interval depth.

Using IQM superconducting systems as a hardware case study, we implement an IQM-backed QDMI layer and connect it to two software layers that HPC centers working with quantum computers already care about: Slurm-based job execution and Qiskit-facing user workflows. The implementation is publicly available at https://github.com/iqm-finland/QDMI-on-IQM. The key message is simple: integrating quantum hardware into HPC does not have to be a bespoke engineering effort for each backend. Once the software-hardware boundary is standardized, large parts of the stack become reusable across providers and deployment styles. Our results do not claim that standardization eliminates all HPCQC challenges. They show that this specific boundary can already be standardized today in a way that is practical for users, operators, and vendors.

## 高级张量和代数的边界子阶

### 摘要

我们确定了几个代数族的高级结构张量的边界子阶，特别得到了以下结果。(1)我们确定所有$k$的$k$-fold矩阵相乘和$k$-fold上三角矩阵相乘的边界子阶的紧界。(2)我们确定了二次曲面的截头多项代数、零代数和极代数的高级结构张量的边界子阶。(3)我们确定所有阶的李代数$\mathfrak{sl}_2$的高级结构张量的边界子阶。(4)我们证明了代数结构张量的退化从高阶传播到低阶。一路上，我们研究了哪些上限方法（几何排序、$G$-稳定排序、索圈度）在哪些环境中有效，以及它们如何相互关联。我们的工作扩展了斯特拉森（J.~）的结果雷恩·安吉。~数学。，1987年、1991年），他在两个方向上确定了这些代数对于三阶张量的渐进子阶：我们确定边界子阶本身而不是其渐进版本，并且我们考虑更高级的结构张量。


### Abstract

Our work extends the results of Strassen (J.~Reine Angew.~Math., 1987, 1991), who determined the asymptotic subrank of these algebras for tensors of order three, in two directions: we determine the border subrank itself rather than its asymptotic version, and we consider higher order structure tensors.

We discuss the relevance of this construction for semiclassical simulation of many-body spin systems and the characterization of non-classicality through Wigner negativity.
