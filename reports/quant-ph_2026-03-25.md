## 基于调和变换的偏置量子容量的几何分类

**Original Title:** Geometric Classification of Biased Quantum Capacity via Harmonic Translation

**Authors:** Eliseo Sarmiento Rosales, Egor Maximenko, Dionisio Manuel Tun Molina, Juan Carlos Jimenez Cervantes, Jose Alberto Guzman Vega, Rodrigo Leon Morales

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22336v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.22336v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-21


### 摘要

我们建立了对角局部相噪音下量子误差修正的精确噪音模型推导的描述。在均匀局部性下，t-局部相差下的最大逻辑维度等于Aq（n，2 t +1），即经典的q元包装函数。由于没有强加仿射或稳定剂结构，因此每当Aq（n，2 t +1）>Bq（n，2 t +1）时，非线性谱支持就实现了这一界限，并严格超过所有仿射构造。这源于调和平移原理：对角相运算符充当傅里叶域中的刚性平移，将Knill-Laflamme条件精确简化为添加性非碰撞约束（S-S）cap Et={0}。对于结构化的相噪，精确的修正相当于加性凯莱图中的独立性，将偏置量子容量与经典零误差理论和洛瓦什西塔函数联系起来。在混合泡利噪音下，在共形域中的同时保护会产生固有的速率惩罚R <= 1-（gamma_X+gamma_Z）/2，暴露了离散调和不确定性原理。与稳定器或基于图形的框架相比，这种经典对应关系直接从相噪模型本身而不是从辅助的代数结构中推导出来。


### Abstract

We establish an exact noise-model-derived characterization of quantum error correction under diagonal local phase noise. Under uniform locality, the maximal logical dimension under t-local phase errors equals Aq(n,2t+1), the classical q-ary packing function. Because no affine or stabilizer structure is imposed, nonlinear spectral supports achieve this bound and strictly exceed all affine constructions whenever Aq(n,2t+1)>Bq(n,2t+1). This follows from a harmonic translation principle: diagonal phase operators act as rigid translations in the Fourier domain, reducing the Knill-Laflamme conditions exactly to an additive non-collision constraint (S-S) cap Et={0}. For structured phase noise, exact correction is equivalent to independence in an additive Cayley graph, connecting biased quantum capacity to classical zero-error theory and the Lovasz theta function. Under mixed Pauli noise, simultaneous protection in conjugate domains incurs an intrinsic rate penalty R <= 1-(gamma_X+gamma_Z)/2, exposing a discrete harmonic uncertainty principle. In contrast with stabilizer- or graph-based frameworks, this classical correspondence is derived directly from the phase-noise model itself rather than from an auxiliary algebraic construction.

## 开放量子电池中羔羊移动诱导的能量转移切换

**Original Title:** Lamb-shift-induced switching of energy transfer in open quantum batteries

**Authors:** Liang Luo, Shun-Cai Zhao

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22337v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.22337v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-21


### 摘要

开放量子电池（QB）在不可避免的系统-环境相互作用下运行，其中耗散和相干重整化都会影响其性能。虽然大多数以前的研究集中在耗散效应，环境引起的频率重整化的作用，如兰姆位移，仍然没有充分explored.In这项工作中，我们调查了外部驱动的QB组成的两个相干耦合的量子谐振子，代表充电器和电池。通过将耗散和兰姆位移修正内的Lindblad主方程，我们表明，兰姆位移有效地重整化系统的本征频率，从而修改与外部驱动器的共振条件。我们表明，调整驱动频率相对于重整化本征模导致模式选择性能量转移过程，导致充电器和电池之间的能量的可控再分配。这种行为表现为主导能量存储通道的切换，并且可以通过耦合系统的超模分解来定量理解。我们的结果澄清了开放量子电池中环境引起的频移的动力学作用，并为在现实操作条件下优化功提取提供了物理透明的框架。


### Abstract

Open quantum batteries (QBs) operate under unavoidable system-environment interactions, where both dissipation and coherent renormalization influence their performance. While most previous studies focus on dissipative effects, the role of environment-induced frequency renormalization, such as the Lamb shift, remains insufficiently explored.In this work, we investigate an externally driven QB composed of two coherently coupled quantum harmonic oscillators, representing the charger and the battery. By incorporating both dissipation and Lamb-shift corrections within a Lindblad master equation, we show that the Lamb shift effectively renormalizes the system eigenfrequencies and thereby modifies the resonance condition with the external drive. We demonstrate that tuning the driving frequency relative to the renormalized eigenmodes leads to a mode-selective energy transfer process, resulting in a controllable redistribution of energy between the charger and the battery. This behavior manifests as a switching of the dominant energy storage channel and can be quantitatively understood through a supermode decomposition of the coupled system. Our results clarify the dynamical role of environment-induced frequency shifts in open quantum batteries and provide a physically transparent framework for optimizing work extraction under realistic operating conditions.

## 球体上的路径积分蒙特卡洛

**Original Title:** Path Integral Monte Carlo on a Sphere

**Authors:** Riccardo Fantoni

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22338v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.22338v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-21


### 摘要

我们在数字上精确地解决了量子广义相对论的一个简单玩具模型，或者更准确地说，解决了弯曲空间上的路径积分。我们考虑球体（恒定正弯曲的表面）上量子多体问题的热平衡。我们使用路径积分蒙特卡罗来测量球体上低温下玻色子、费米子和任意子流体的动能、内能和静态结构。对于玻色子，我们还测量了超流分数，并将其在临界温度下的行为与尼尔森和科斯特利茨在超流相转变的热力学极限下在平坦空间中预测的普适跳跃进行比较。对于费米子和任意子来说，有必要使用限制路径积分公式来克服符号问题。即使这个配方对于非相互作用流体是精确的，它也简化为相互作用系统的近似值。我们以低温下的电子气为例。计算机实验发展过程中许多体路径配置的快照表明，由于庞加莱的“毛茸茸的球定理”，两极附近的单粒子路径的“速度”减慢。还研究了弯曲对多种体液热力学和结构性质的影响。


### Abstract

We solve numerically exactly a simple toy model to quantum general relativity or more properly to path integral on a curved space. We consider the thermal equilibrium of a quantum many body problem on the sphere, the surface of constant positive curvature. We use path integral Monte Carlo to measure the kinetic energy, the internal energy and the static structure of a bosons, fermions and anyons fluid at low temperatures on the sphere. For bosons we also measure the superfluid fraction and compare its behavior at the critical temperature with the universal jump predicted by Nelson and Kosterlitz in flat space in the thermodynamic limit at the superfluid phase transition. For fermions and anyons it is necessary to use the restricted path integral recipe in order to overcome the sign problem. Even if this recipe is exact for the non interacting fluid it reduces to just an approximation for an interacting system. And we make the example of the electron gas at low temperature. Snapshots of the many body path configuration during the evolution of the computer experiment show that the ``speed'' of the single particle path near the poles slows down as a consequence of the ``hairy ball theorem'' of Poincaré. The influence of curvature on the thermodynamic and structural properties of the many body fluid is also studied.

## 量子位系统中幻数的相空间几何测量

**Original Title:** A Phase-Space Geometric Measure of Magic in Qubit Systems

**Authors:** Soumyojyoti Dutta, Tushar

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.20792v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.20792v2

**Submitted:** 2026-03-21


### 摘要

量子魔法（在稳定器电路之外实现计算优势的资源）的特征在量子位系统中是微妙的，因为既定的措施可能会提供有关同一状态的相互冲突的信息。我们引入C（rho），即状态的离散维格纳函数到稳定剂维格纳函数的凸壳的l1距离，并通过紧度比kappa（rho）研究其与稳定剂范围Gamma（rho）的关系：=（Gamma（rho）-1）/C（rho）。对于重复码子空间跨度中的三个两量子位家族{|00>，|11>}，证明了kappa在每个族上取正整数常数：对于R_y和Bell+R_z族，kappa=1;对于R_x族，kappa=2。因子2的间隙出现是因为虚相干性将维格纳负性集中在16个相空间点中的2个而不是4个，使得伽马不变。最佳对偶见证是重复码的逻辑泡利算子，揭示了C是可纠正错误下的容错可观测不变量-相空间几何和量子纠错之间的意外联系。我们证明了Gamma >= 1 + C/Mn的一个严格界，建立了张量积行为的半球二分法，其中对于亏损约为0.335 C（rho）的北半球态，C的超可加性失效，并证明了C在满Clifford群下不是魔单调的，因此渐近蒸馏率需要Gamma.


### Abstract

Characterizing quantum magic -- the resource enabling computational advantage beyond stabilizer circuits -- is subtle in qubit systems because established measures can give conflicting information about the same state. We introduce C(rho), the l1 distance from a state's discrete Wigner function to the convex hull of stabilizer Wigner functions, and study its relationship to the stabilizer extent Gamma(rho) via the tightness ratio kappa(rho) := (Gamma(rho)-1)/C(rho). For three two-qubit families in the repetition-code subspace span{|00>,|11>}, we prove kappa takes exact integer values constant over each family: kappa=1 for the R_y and Bell+R_z families, kappa=2 for the R_x family. The factor-of-2 gap arises because imaginary coherence concentrates Wigner negativity at 2 of 16 phase-space points rather than 4, leaving Gamma unchanged. The optimal dual witnesses are logical Pauli operators of the repetition code, revealing that C is a fault-tolerant observable invariant under correctable errors -- an unexpected connection between phase-space geometry and quantum error correction. We prove a sharp bound Gamma >= 1 + C/M_n, establish a hemispheric dichotomy in tensor-product behavior where superadditivity of C fails for northern-hemisphere states with deficit approximately 0.335 C(rho), and show C is not a magic monotone under the full Clifford group, so asymptotic distillation rates require Gamma.

## 作为左伴随的上下文：正结节结构的分类生成

**Original Title:** Contextuality as a Left Adjoint: A Categorical Generation of Orthomodular Structure

**Authors:** Yukio-Pegio Gunji, Yoshihiko Ohzawa, Yuki Tokuyama, Yu Huang, Kyoko Nakamura

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22353v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.22353v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-22


### 摘要

上下文性被广泛认为是量子信息的标志，但其结构起源往往被概率或操作公式所掩盖。在这项工作中，我们表明非分配正调制结构不需要假设，而是作为经典布尔上下文中的左伴随而规范地产生。我们引入了一个胶合函子，它采用布尔代数对，并通过类别推出仅识别它们的最小和最大元素。由此产生的网格是正模的，但一般是非分配的。我们证明，这个建设是左伴随一个健忘的函子选择布尔子代数，从而提供了一个自由的，但有约束的量子逻辑结构从经典的背景下一代。此外，我们证明，这个推出保持布尔的失败是相当于缺乏全球部分的层理论框架中的Abramsky和Brandenburger。这建立了一个精确的对应关系作为一个层的障碍和非分配性作为一个colimit失败的上下文。我们的研究结果提供了一个分类和格理论的上下文重建之前的概率概念，并澄清了量子逻辑在信息理论设置的结构必要性。


### Abstract

Contextuality is widely regarded as a hallmark of quantum information, yet its structural origin is often obscured by probabilistic or operational formulations. In this work, we show that non-distributive orthomodular structure need not be postulated, but arises canonically as a left adjoint from classical Boolean contexts. We introduce a gluing functor that takes pairs of Boolean algebras and identifies only their minimal and maximal elements via a categorical pushout. The resulting lattice is orthomodular but generically non-distributive. We prove that this construction is left adjoint to a forgetful functor selecting Boolean subalgebras, thereby providing a free but constrained generation of quantum-logical structure from classical contexts. Furthermore, we demonstrate that the failure of this pushout to remain Boolean is equivalent to the absence of global sections in the sheaf-theoretic framework of Abramsky and Brandenburger. This establishes a precise correspondence between contextuality as a sheaf obstruction and non-distributivity as a colimit failure. Our results offer a categorical and lattice-theoretic reconstruction of contextuality that precedes probabilistic notions and clarifies the structural necessity of quantum logic in information-theoretic settings.

## 辛框架下的几何量子力学：度仿射扩张和变形量子动力学

**Original Title:** Geometric Quantum Mechanics in a Symplectic Framework: Metric-Affine Extensions and Deformed Quantum Dynamics

**Authors:** Hoshang Heydari

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22354v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.22354v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-22


### 摘要

我们基于投射希尔伯特空间的辛结构提出了量子力学的几何公式。在标准Kähler框架的基础上，我们引入了一种扩展，其中辛结构被允许与度量仿射背景几何耦合，从而导致状态空间上汉密尔顿流的变形。   我们表明，在适当的条件下，变形的结构保持辛并定义了适定的Hamilton系统。在几何变形消失的极限下，该公式简化为标准薛定格动力学。   构建了显式的分析示例来说明变形的影响。特别是，与弯曲相关的变形会导致Hamilton流的重新缩放，而扭转引起的贡献会产生与方向相关的修正。此外，几何相获得由变形的辛结构确定的修正。   这些结果为探索背景弯曲和仿射结构引起的量子进化的几何修改提供了数学上一致的框架。


### Abstract

We present a geometric formulation of quantum mechanics based on the symplectic structure of the projective Hilbert space. Building upon the standard Kähler framework, we introduce an extension in which the symplectic structure is allowed to couple to a metric-affine background geometry, leading to a deformation of the Hamiltonian flow on the state space.
  We show that, under suitable conditions, the deformed structure remains symplectic and defines a well-posed Hamiltonian system. The formulation reduces to standard Schrödinger dynamics in the limit where the geometric deformation vanishes.
  Explicit analytical examples are constructed to illustrate the effect of the deformation. In particular, curvature-dependent deformations lead to a rescaling of Hamiltonian flows, while torsion-induced contributions produce direction-dependent corrections. In addition, geometric phases acquire corrections determined by the deformed symplectic structure.
  These results provide a mathematically consistent framework for exploring geometric modifications of quantum evolution induced by background curvature and affine structure.

## 使用siZZle-CZ门的固定频率超导量子位系统的高产量集成设计

**Original Title:** High-yield integration design of fixed-frequency superconducting qubit systems using siZZle-CZ gates

**Authors:** Kazuhisa Ogawa, Yutaka Tabuchi, Makoto Negoro

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.21537v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.21537v2

**Submitted:** 2026-03-23


### 摘要

固定频率传输子量子位以简单的架构和长的相关时间为特点，是大规模量子计算的有前途的平台。然而，快速增加的频率冲突直接降低了制造良率，阻碍了缩放，尤其是在基于交叉共振（CR）门的架构中，其中受限的驱动频率严重限制了可用的设计空间。我们通过水平漂移（siZZZle）门来研究斯塔克引起的ZZ，该门通过允许任意驱动频率选择来放宽这一限制。针对广泛参数范围的广泛数值分析（包括先前受到忽略不计的远失调状态）揭示了支持受控Z（CZ）一致性> 99.6%的广泛操作窗口。利用这些窗口，我们设计了包含>1000个量子位的网格架构，表明即使在0.25%的制造引起的频率分散下，方形和重六边形网格中的零碰撞产率也分别达到80%和100%。因此，siZZle-CZ门是CR门的可扩展且碰撞稳健的替代方案，为高产量固定频率转换子量子处理器提供了可行的途径。


### Abstract

Fixed-frequency transmon qubits, characterized by simple architectures and long coherence times, are promising platforms for large-scale quantum computing. However, the rapidly increasing frequency collisions, which directly reduce the fabrication yield, hinder scaling, especially in cross-resonance (CR) gate-based architectures, wherein the restricted drive frequency severely limits the available design space. We investigate the Stark-induced ZZ by level excursions (siZZle) gate, which relaxes this limitation by allowing arbitrary drive-frequency choices. Extensive numerical analyses across a broad parameter range -- including the far-detuned regime that has received negligible prior attention -- reveal wide operating windows that support controlled-Z (CZ) fidelities >99.6%. Leveraging these windows, we design lattice architectures containing >1000 qubits, showing that even under 0.25% fabrication-induced frequency dispersion, the zero-collision yields in square and heavy-hexagonal lattices reach 80% and 100%, respectively. Thus, the siZZle-CZ gate is a scalable and collision-robust alternative to the CR gate, offering a viable route toward high-yield fixed-frequency transmon quantum processors.

## 双布拉格折射的稳健原子干涉术

**Original Title:** Robust Atom Interferometry with Double Bragg Diffraction

**Authors:** Rui Li

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22385v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.22385v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-23


### 摘要

本论文旨在建立一个基于双布拉格衍射（DBD）的高对比度原子干涉测量的理论和数值框架。虽然DBD提供了内在的对称性，降低了对内部状态系统学的敏感性，并适用于微重力实验，但其性能长期以来受到不完美衍射和对比度损失的限制。这项工作克服了这些限制，通过构建一个分析的哈密顿描述DBD -包括多普勒效应和极化缺陷-并通过推导减少两个和五个级别的模型，通过截断的马格努斯展开方法。这些模型澄清了AC斯塔克位移，偏振引起的错误，和多普勒选择性的起源，他们提供了准确的预测现实的输入动量分布。   在此理论基础上，论文介绍了一种具有动态可调失调的三频激光器方案，并使用五层S矩阵形式评估了不同的失调控制策略。线性失调扫描和最佳控制脉冲被证明可以分别提供近乎理想的分束器和反射镜性能，确保在广泛的实验缺陷中具有稳健的对比度。使用GPU加速的通用原子干涉仪模拟器（UATIS）进行的补充全三维模拟结合了相互作用的玻色-爱因斯坦凝聚物和现实的光学势，揭示了横向效应和极化诱导的失真，扩展了一维非相互作用模型的预测。总而言之，本论文建立了一个连贯的理论和数值框架，证明通过适当的失调控制，双布拉格原子干涉仪可以实现精确惯性传感和未来基础物理学的空基量子测试所需的鲁棒性。


### Abstract

This thesis develops a general theoretical and numerical framework for achieving high-contrast atom interferometry based on double Bragg diffraction (DBD). While DBD offers intrinsic symmetry, reduced sensitivity to internal-state systematics, and suitability for microgravity experiments, its performance has long been limited by imperfect diffraction and contrast loss. This work overcomes these limitations by constructing an analytic Hamiltonian description of DBD -- including Doppler effects and polarization imperfections -- and by deriving reduced two- and five-level models via a truncated Magnus-expansion approach. These models clarify the origin of AC-Stark shifts, polarization-induced errors, and Doppler selectivity, and they provide accurate predictions for realistic input momentum distributions.
  Building on this theoretical foundation, the thesis introduces a tri-frequency laser scheme with dynamically tunable detuning and evaluates different detuning-control strategies using a five-level S-matrix formalism. Linear detuning sweeps and optimal-control pulses are shown to provide near-ideal beam-splitter and mirror performance, respectively, ensuring robust contrast across a wide range of experimental imperfections. Complementary full three-dimensional simulations using the GPU-accelerated Universal Atom Interferometer Simulator (UATIS) incorporate interacting Bose-Einstein condensates and realistic optical potentials, revealing transverse effects and polarization-induced distortions that extend the predictions of the one-dimensional non-interacting models. Taken together, this thesis establishes a coherent theoretical and numerical framework demonstrating that, with appropriate detuning control, double-Bragg atom interferometers can achieve the robustness required for precision inertial sensing and future space-based quantum tests of fundamental physics.

## 散装非埃尔米特系统的边界斑点控制

**Original Title:** Boundary Floquet Control of Bulk non-Hermitian Systems

**Authors:** Yu-Min Hu, Yu-Bo Shi, Linhu Li, Gianluca Teza, Ching Hua Lee, Roderich Moessner, Shu Zhang, Sen Mu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22396v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.22396v1

**Authors:** Dan-Fang Zhang, Jing-Ting Li, Wen-Zhang Wang, Wei-Hao Xu, Jia-Yi Wei, Xiao Li, Yi-Bo Wang, Dong-Feng Gao, Jia-Qi Zhong, Biao Tang, Lin Zhou, Run-Bing Li, Huan-Yao Sun, Qun-Feng Chen, Lei Qin, Mei-zhen An, Zong-Feng Li, Shu-Quan Wang, Xiao-Xiao Guo, Yao Tian, Xi-He Yu, Hong-En Zhong, Xi Chen, Jin Wang, Ming-Sheng Zhan

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22981v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.22981v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-24


### 摘要

弱等效原理（BEP）是广义相对论的核心支柱。它对太空量子系统的精确测试为了解新物理学提供了一个独特的窗口。在这里，我们报告了BEP的首次轨道量子测试。中国空间站上实现了双物种（85 Ra/87 Ra）原子干涉仪。开发了平台运动抑制、荧光检测切换和双量子失调切换等方法，以消除相噪并提高测量准确性。根据280天的BEP测试数据得出的测试不确定度为2.8*10-8，经误差估计后得出的测试结果为（-3.1+/-4.6）*10-7。这将先前的微重力原子干涉BEP测试改进了三个数量级。这项工作为星载量子惯性传感器及其在未来太空基础物理学中的应用铺平了道路。


### Abstract

The Weak Equivalence Principle (WEP) is a central pillar of general relativity. Its precise test with quantum systems in space offers a unique window onto new physics. Here we report the first in-orbit quantum test of the WEP. A dual-species (85Rb/87Rb) atom interferometer is realized aboard the China Space Station. Methods of platform motion suppression, fluorescence detection switching, and two-photon detuning switching are developed to eliminate phase noise and improve measurement accuracy. A test uncertainty of 2.8*10-8 is obtained from 280 days of WEP test data, and a test result of (-3.1+/-4.6)*10-7 is achieved after error estimation. This improves prior atom-interferometric WEP tests in microgravity by three orders of magnitude. This work paves the way for space-borne quantum inertial sensors and their application to future fundamental physics in space.

## 三方系统中的不精确量子引导不平等

**Authors:** Yan Zhao, Li-Juan Li, Zheng-Peng Xu, Liu Ye, Dong Wang

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.22986v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-24


### 摘要

量子操纵作为非局部量子相关性的表现，在实现各种量子信息处理任务方面发挥着至关重要的作用。然而，实际实施通常会受到不完美或不受信任的测量设备带来的重大挑战的阻碍。这项研究调查了测量不准确性对量子操纵的影响，特别关注不受信任方测量设备中的错误。我们首先分析这些误差如何影响转向不等式的评估，然后在不完美测量下基于相关矩阵推导出双方转向不等式。   我们的研究结果表明，即使是很小的测量误差也会显着损害量子可操纵性的认证，随着系统维度的增加，这种影响变得特别明显。此外，通过相关矩阵将拟议的引导不等式扩展到修改后的三方场景，我们证明了测量不完善的影响在多方量子引导中比在双方情况下严重得多。我们的结果强调了解决实验量子操纵中测量缺陷的迫切需要，并为描述和减轻多维量子系统中的这些影响提供了理论框架。


### Abstract

Quantum steering, as a manifestation of nonlocal quantum correlations, plays a crucial role in enabling various quantum information processing tasks. However, practical implementations are often hindered by significant challenges arising from imperfect or untrusted measurement devices. This study investigates the impact of measurement inaccuracies on quantum steering, with a particular focus on errors in the untrusted party's measurement devices. We first analyze how such errors affect the evaluation of steering inequalities, and then derive bipartite steering inequalities based on correlation matrices under imperfect measurements.
  Our findings show that even small measurement errors can significantly compromise the certification of quantum steerability, an effect that becomes particularly pronounced as the system dimension increases. Furthermore, by extending the proposed steering inequality to a modified tripartite scenario via correlation matrices, we demonstrate that the influence of measurement imperfections is far more severe in multipartite quantum steering than in the bipartite case. Our results underscore the critical need to account for measurement imperfections in experimental quantum steering and provide a theoretical framework for characterizing and mitigating these effects in high-dimensional quantum systems.

## 耦合振子与热库相互作用中量子关联和退相干的动力学演化

**Original Title:** Dynamical Evolution of Quantum Correlations and Decoherence in Coupled Oscillators Interacting with a Thermal Reservoir

**Authors:** Somayeh Mehrabankar, Farkhondeh Abbasnezhad, Davood Afshar, Aurelian Isar

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22994v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.22994v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-24


### 摘要

我们研究了由两个耦合的不对称谐振子与热环境相互作用组成的开放量子系统中量子不和谐、纠缠和纯度的动态演变。使用科萨科夫斯基-林德布拉德主方程，我们分析了从压缩真空状态开始的时间演变。与我们之前对不对称振子中纠缠演变的研究相反，本工作引入了XY型位置-位置耦合，并对量子不和谐和纠缠的纯度进行了系统联合分析。我们考察了压缩参数、不对称参数、耦合系数、消散率和温度的综合影响。我们发现，一般来说，量子不和谐和纠缠随着时间的推移呈现出非单调的减少。升高温度会持续加速量子相关性和纯度的退化，而增加消散会加速量子相关性的退化，但会导致更高的稳态纯度。增加压缩参数通过增强初始相关性和延长纠缠生存时间来提供保护效应，而增加耦合系数会导致更高的量子相关性。不对称参数对相关性演变的影响很小。我们的分析表明，量子不和谐表现出比纠缠更强的弹性，纠缠可以呈现更复杂的行为，包括纠缠突然死亡以及可能的暂时复活和重新维持。这些发现为开发稳健的量子信息协议和策略提供了宝贵的见解，以在现实开放量子系统中保留量子相关性，并有可能扩展到非马尔科夫机制和多模式架构。


### Abstract

We investigate the dynamical evolution of quantum discord, entanglement and purity in an open quantum system of two coupled asymmetric harmonic oscillators interacting with a thermal environment. Using the Kossakowski-Lindblad master equation we analyze the time evolution starting with a squeezed vacuum state. In contrast to our previous study on entanglement evolution in asymmetric oscillators, the present work introduces XY-type position-position coupling together with a systematic joint analysis of quantum discord and purity alongside entanglement. We examine the combined effects of the squeezing parameter, asymmetry parameter, coupling constant, dissipation rate and temperature. We find that quantum discord and entanglement exhibit, in general, a non-monotonic decrease over time. Increasing temperature consistently accelerates the degradation of both quantum correlations and purity, whereas increasing dissipation accelerates the degradation of quantum correlations but leads to higher steady-state purity. Increasing the squeezing parameter provides a protective effect by enhancing initial correlations and prolonging entanglement survival time, while increasing the coupling constant leads to higher quantum correlations. The asymmetry parameter exhibits only a weak influence on the correlation evolution. Our analysis reveals that quantum discord demonstrates stronger resilience than entanglement, which can present more complex behaviour including entanglement sudden death and possible temporary revivals and re-suppressions. These findings provide valuable insights for developing robust quantum information protocols and strategies for preserving quantum correlations in realistic open quantum systems, with potential extensions to non-Markovian regimes and multi-mode architectures.

This behavior is particularly interesting and commonly seen in potential DSM candidates. Consequently, we report for the first time that a dichroic DSM generates 12 unique topological laser types. We discover that surface currents are generated by topological terms on the surface of the DSM slab. Furthermore, we examine how the θ term associated with axions in topological materials contributes to these topological properties. Our study reveals distinct topological role of the θ term more clearly than ever before. Our results confirm that the topological properties of DSMs with a single Dirac cone remain stable under external influences and that a topologically robust DSM laser can be developed accordingly

## 具有互惠和非互惠耦合的量子电池网络中依赖于连接布局的能量传输和各向异性

**Authors:** Bing-Bing Liu, Rui Chen, Jin-Lei Wu, Gang Chen, Shi-Lei Su

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.23009v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.23009v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-24


### 摘要

实现可扩展的量子电池架构不仅需要关注可以存储多少能量，还需要关注如何在连接的电池节点之间传输、分配和转化为可提取的功。虽然之前的研究主要集中在多电池量子电池中的集体充电，但量子电池网络的依赖于布局的传输定律和相应的工作导向性能在很大程度上仍然没有被探索。在这项工作中，我们研究了具有工程互惠和非互惠耦合的量子电池网络，并在统一的传输框架内比较了不同的连接布局，包括级联和并行架构。在非互惠机制中，最佳耦合对于两种连接布局遵循不同的缩放定律，即对于级联传输来说，$J_{\rm op}\propto N$，对于大-$N$限制下的并行充电，$J_{\rm op}\propto N ''''。在倒易级联网络中，依赖于宇称的光谱响应会产生非倒易和并行配置中不存在的奇-偶传输效应。我们进一步分析了热储层和压缩储层的作用，并表明热噪音主要增加被动能，而压缩增强了各向异性，从而增加了储存能量的有用比例。这些结果将重点从充电增强转移到传输工程，并为量子电池网络提供了架构级设计原则。


### Abstract

The realization of scalable quantum battery architectures requires concern not only with how much energy can be stored, but also with how energy is transported, distributed, and converted into extractable work across connected battery nodes. While previous studies mainly focused on collective charging in multi-cell quantum batteries, the topology-dependent transport law and the corresponding work-oriented performance of quantum battery networks remain largely unexplored. In the nonreciprocal regime, the optimal coupling follows distinct scaling laws for the two connection topologies, namely $J_{\rm op}^{c}\propto N$ for cascaded transport and $J_{\rm op}^{p}\propto N^{-1/2}$ for parallel charging in the large-$N$ limit. In reciprocal cascaded networks, a parity-dependent spectral response produces an odd-even transport effect that is absent in the nonreciprocal and parallel configurations. We further analyze the role of thermal and squeezed reservoirs and show that thermal noise mainly increases passive energy, whereas squeezing enhances ergotropy and thus the useful fraction of stored energy. These results shift the emphasis from charging enhancement to transport engineering and provide architecture-level design principles for quantum battery networks.

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.23026v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.23026v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-24


### 摘要

玻色-爱因斯坦凝聚最初排列在一个长链中，自由膨胀和干涉。如果冷凝物的初始相是相同的，则初始密度分布在膨胀期间周期性地恢复，从而引起Talbot效应。即使在初始相位中有轻微的无序，也会导致干涉图样的转变。响应于相位紊乱，空间密度分布的谱获得在相同相位的情况下不存在的峰。我们推导出任意相无序的空间密度分布谱的解析表达。我们表明，由于相无序而出现的新峰源于凝聚物的成对干扰。这些峰的位置与这种成对干扰产生的密度调制（子波）的波矢重合。当初始阶段相同时，这些峰值的缺失可以通过重叠子波在叠加期间的相互破坏来解释。


### Abstract

Bose-Einstein condensates initially arranged in a long chain freely expand and interfere. If the initial phases of the condensates are identical, the initial density distribution is restored periodically during the expansion, giving rise to the Talbot effect. In response to the phase disorder, the spectrum of the spatial density distribution acquires peaks that are absent in the case of identical phases. We show that the new peaks emerging due to the phase disorder originate from pairwise interferences of the condensates. The positions of these peaks coincide with the wave vectors of the density modulations (wavelets) generated by such pairwise interferences. The absence of these peaks, when the initial phases are identical, is explained by the mutual destruction of the overlapping wavelets during their summation.

### 摘要

我们为一般自回归神经量子状态建立了信息论标度定律，该定律由波函数幅度的中间互信息确定。通过将虚拟键形式化为序列二分区中的有效信息通道，我们严格证明了量子状态的精确自回归表示需要虚拟键维度随幅度互信息缩放。对于稳定国家家庭，我们表明该定律产生了一个显式的分析等级公式。将这个框架应用到量子状态断层扫描、底态和有限温度学习中，我们的数值实验揭示了精确的指数匹配、循环和Transformer神经量子状态之间的结构相关缩放差异，以及自回归基排序的关键作用。这些结果在量子多体状态的内在结构与其忠实表示所需的相应神经网络容量之间建立了严格的物理联系。


### Abstract

We establish an information-theoretic scaling law for generic autoregressive neural quantum states, determined by the middle-cut mutual information of the wavefunction amplitude. By formalizing the virtual bond as an effective information channel across a sequence bipartition, we rigorously prove that exact autoregressive representation of a quantum state requires the virtual-bond dimension to scale with the amplitude mutual information. For stabilizer-state families, we show that this law yields an explicit, analytical rank formula. Applying this framework across quantum-state tomography, ground-state and finite-temperature learning, our numerical experiments expose precise exponent matching, architecture-dependent scaling differences between recurrent and Transformer neural quantum state, and the critical role of autoregressive basis ordering. These results establish a rigorous physical link between the intrinsic structure of a quantum many-body state and the corresponding neural-network capacity required for its faithful representation.

## 有限随机砖砌电路中的初始状态记忆

**Original Title:** Initial State Memory in Finite Random Brickwork Circuits

**Authors:** Jakob Bannister, Katja Klobas, Colin Rylands, Bruno Bertini

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.23469v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.23469v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-24


### 摘要

我们询问随机门的有限砖砌电路在什么条件下保留有关初始状态的局部信息。为了回答这个问题，我们测量了通过进化两个任意初始状态并追踪系统的一部分而获得的约化状态之间的平均弗罗贝尼乌斯距离。通过始终准确地描述这个距离，我们发现，如果环境（所追踪的子系统）小于系统的一半，否则就会被冲走，那么信息就会被保留。我们还发现，虽然弗罗贝尼乌斯距离的动态取决于所选择的特定初始状态，但这种依赖性对于大规模来说变得越来越弱，最终弗罗贝尼乌斯距离获得了作为时间函数的普适形式。最后，我们表明，通过引入足够弱的边界消散，可以观察到记忆保留阶段和信息完全丢失阶段之间的相转变。


### Abstract

We ask under what conditions a finite brickwork circuit of random gates retains local information about the initial state. To answer this question we measure the averaged Frobenius distance between the reduced states obtained by evolving two arbitrary initial states and tracing out a portion of the system. By characterising this distance exactly at all times we find that the information is retained if the environment -- the subsystem traced out -- is smaller than half of the system and washed away otherwise. We also find that, while the dynamics of the Frobenius distance depends on the specific initial states chosen, this dependence becomes increasingly weak for large scales and eventually the Frobenius distance attains a universal form as a function of time. Finally, we show that by introducing weak enough boundary dissipation, one can observe a phase transition between a memory preserving phase and one where the information is completely lost.
