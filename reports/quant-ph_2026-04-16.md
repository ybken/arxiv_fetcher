## 电荷激发的量子力学模型：表面束缚和分散

**Original Title:** Quantum mechanical model for charge excitation: Surface binding and dispersion

**Authors:** Dionisios Margetis

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12111v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.12111v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-13


### 摘要

通过理想化的量子力学模型，我们正式描述了非延迟电磁波的频散，该电磁波在零温度下在三维空间（3D）中表达固定平面附近的电荷密度振荡。我们的目标是捕捉微观尺度的相互作用，其中包括表面等离子体出现时的限制长度，即平面附近的集体低能量电荷激发。我们从3D中的与时间相关的Hartree型方程开始。该模型考虑了粒子与平面的结合以及与相对于接地状态的感应电荷密度相关的排斥性库伦相互作用。通过线性化运动方程，我们制定了一个齐次积分方程的散射振幅的粒子波函数在（z-）坐标垂直于平面。对于与负δ函数和z波函数成比例的结合势，我们应用关于正z的拉普拉斯变换，并将积分方程转换成涉及变换后的解的几个值的函数方程。利用Mittag-Leffler定理，精确地导出了散射振幅和色散关系的快速收敛级数形式。在半经典区域，我们的结果给出了能量激发谱的渐近展开式。该首阶项与基于投影欧拉-Poisson系统的经典流体动力模型的预测一致。


### Abstract

By an idealized quantum mechanical model, we formally describe the dispersion of nonretarded electromagnetic waves that express charge density oscillations near a fixed plane in three spatial dimensions (3D) at zero temperature. Our goal is to capture the interplay of microscopic scales that include a confinement length in the emergence of the surface plasmon, a collective low-energy charge excitation in the vicinity of the plane. We start with a time-dependent Hartree-type equation in 3D. This model accounts for particle binding to the plane and the repulsive Coulomb interaction associated with the induced charge density relative to the ground state. By linearizing the equation of motion, we formulate a homogeneous integral equation for the scattering amplitude of the particle wave function in the (z-) coordinate vertical to the plane. For a binding potential proportional to a negative delta function and symmetric-in-z wave function, we apply the Laplace transform with respect to positive z and convert the integral equation into a functional equation that involves several values of the transformed solution. The scattering amplitude and dispersion relation are derived exactly in terms of rapidly convergent series via the Mittag-Leffler theorem. In the semiclassical regime, our result furnishes an asymptotic expansion for the energy excitation spectrum. The leading-order term is found in agreement with the prediction of a classical hydrodynamic model based on a projected-Euler-Poisson system.

## 关于一些不等式、由此产生的不确定性关系和相关性的注释。1.一般数学形式主义

**Original Title:** Notes on some inequalities, resulting uncertainty relations and correlations. 1. General mathematical formalism

**Authors:** Krzysztof Urbanowski

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12410v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.12410v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-14


### 摘要

我们分析了Schwarz不等式及其推广，以及由Jensen不等式产生的不等式。它们在量子理论中用于导出两个非对易观测量的海森堡-罗伯逊（HR）和薛定谔-罗伯逊（SR）不确定关系，以及它们对三个或更多非对易观测量的推广。詹森不等式反过来又有助于导出两个或多个观测量的各种“和不确定性关系”。利用这些不等式，我们导出了两个以上非对易可观量的各种广义测不准关系，并分析了它们的性质和临界点。我们还研究了两个及更多可观测量的HR和SR不确定性关系的概括与这些可观测量在所研究量子系统状态下的相关性之间的联系。在这项分析中，我们特别关注三个非交换可观测量的广义SR不确定关系对其在量子系统给定状态下相关性的影响，以及这种关系与适当的相关矩阵的联系，其矩阵元素是量子版本的皮尔逊系数。我们还表明，SR不确定关系（包括广义关系）可以使用这些皮尔逊系数以等效的方式写成。


### Abstract

We analyze the Schwarz inequality and its generalizations, as well as inequalities resulting from the Jensen inequality. They are used in quantum theory to derive the Heisenberg-Robertson (HR) and Schroedinger-Robertson (SR) uncertainty relation for two non-commuting observables and their generalizations to three or more non-commuting observables. Jensen's inequality, in turn, is helpful in deriving various the "sum uncertainty relations" for two or more observables. Using these inequalities, we derive various types of generalized uncertainty relations for more than two non--commuting observables and analyze their properties and critical points. We also study the connections between the generalizations of the HR and SR uncertainty relations for two and more observables and the correlations of these observables in the state of the quantum system under study. In this analysis, we pay special attention to the consequences of the generalized SR uncertainty relation for three non--commuting observables on their correlations in a given state of a quantum system and to the connections of this relation with the appropriate correlation matrix, whose matrix elements are the quantum versions of the Pearson coefficient. We show also that the SR uncertainty relation (including the generalized ones) can be written in an equivalent way using these Pearson coefficients.

## 对arXiv的评论：2604.09826：发现“爱因斯坦--波多尔斯基--罗森悖论”的解决方案

**Authors:** Mikołaj Sienicki, Krzysztof Sienicki

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.13135v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.13135v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-14


### 摘要

罗曼·施纳贝尔（Roman Schnabel）的文章认为，爱因斯坦-波多尔斯基-罗森（Einstein-Podolsky-Rosen）悖论可以通过识别作者所谓的“EPR含义”中的缺陷并使用放射性阿尔法衰变作为例子来解决，表明可预测性并不排除真正的随机性。该论文写得很清楚，并解决了一个重要的基础问题。然而，我们认为，其主要结论并不如下。本文缩小了最初的EPR论点的范围，将太多地归因于违反贝尔不平等的行为，并用相关随机事件的更简单案例取代了核心的EPR结构--该结构涉及不相容的可观测值和基于本地性的推理。这个结果是一个有趣的解释性评论，但我们认为，这并不是对EPR问题的令人满意的科学解决方案。


### Abstract

Roman Schnabel's article argues that the Einstein-Podolsky-Rosen (EPR) paradox can be resolved by identifying a flaw in what the author calls the "EPR implication" and by using radioactive alpha decay as an example showing that predictability does not exclude genuine randomness. The paper is clearly written and addresses an important foundational question. In our view, however, its main conclusion does not follow. The article narrows the original EPR argument, attributes too much to Bell-inequality violations, and replaces the central EPR structure - which involves incompatible observables and locality-based reasoning - with a simpler case of correlated random events. The result is an interesting interpretive remark, but not, we think, a satisfactory scientific resolution of the EPR problem.

## 指数灵敏度的量子类似物：从洛施切尔回声到克雷洛夫复杂性

**Original Title:** Quantum analogues of exponential sensitivity: from Loschmidt echo to Krylov complexity

**Authors:** Ignacio García-Mata, Diego A. Wisniacki

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12707v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.12707v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-14


### 摘要

经典混乱的基本表现之一是对初始条件的指数敏感性，即从几乎相同的初始状态开始的两个轨迹随着时间的推移呈指数分布。这种行为由李雅普诺夫指数量化。由于量子力学的唯一性，这种指数分歧在量子系统中是难以捉摸的。因此，近年来人们提出并研究了几种替代量来捕捉类似的行为。   在本文中，我们对近年来密集研究的三个量进行了教学概述：洛施切尔回声、超时序相关器（OTOCs）和Krylov复杂性。


### Abstract

One of the fundamental manifestations of classical chaos is exponential sensitivity to initial conditions that is, two trajectories starting from nearly identical initial states diverge exponentially over time. This behavior is quantified by the Lyapunov exponents. Due to the unitary nature of quantum mechanics, such exponential divergence is elusive in quantum systems. As a result, several alternative quantities have been proposed and studied in recent years to capture analogous behavior.
  In this article, we present a pedagogical overview of three such quantities that have been the focus of intense research in recent years: the Loschmidt echo, out-of-time-order correlators (OTOCs), and Krylov complexity.

## 通过随机时间进化进行量子启发的经典模拟

**Original Title:** Quantum-inspired classical simulation through randomized time evolution

**Authors:** Fredrik Hasselgren, Bálint Koczor

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.13144v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.13144v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-14


### 摘要

量子多体动力学的张量网络模拟从根本上受到纠缠积累的限制，这导致计算成本呈指数级增长。此外，这些经典模拟算法本质上是连续的，因为量子状态的张量网络表示通常在每个时间步增量更新。我们以最近引入的随机量子时间进化算法（TE-PRI）为基础，并将其调整到经典模拟环境中，以实现大规模并行化。我们的MPS TE-PRI方法实现了平均精确的时间演变（无偏估计器），并通过将随机化浅Trottter变体电路的集合表示为张量网络来进行。由于每个电路实例都会产生确定性量子状态（或可观察的期望值），随机性的唯一来源是电路变体的采样;因此，相对于TE-PRI的量子硬件实现，散粒噪音的缺乏会产生更小的估计器方差。我们模拟了代表性的无序一维旋转环Hamilton，并在数字上观察到相对于Trotterized MPS进化，每个样本门计数减少了高达10 ' 3美元的因子，从而在现实的并行化水平下产生了数量级的减少。最后，我们从数字上观察到，MPS TE-PRI对于严重的键维截断比积公式更稳健，这可能使其对于模拟实际中需要截断的强相关系统有用。我们还证明，该方法可以自然地与现有的时间进化算法结合使用，通过并行化有效地扩展其时间深度。


### Abstract

Tensor-network simulations of quantum many-body dynamics are fundamentally limited by entanglement build-up, which leads to exponentially growing computational costs. Furthermore, these classical simulation algorithms are inherently sequential as typically a tensor network representation of the quantum state is updated incrementally at each time step. We build on recently introduced randomized quantum algorithms for time evolution (TE-PAI), and adapt them to the classical simulation context with the purpose of enabling massive parallelisation. Our MPS TE-PAI approach achieves exact time evolution on average (unbiased estimator) and proceeds by representing an ensemble of randomized shallow Trotter-variant circuits as tensor networks. As each circuit instance yields a deterministic quantum state (or observable expecation value), the only source of randomness is the sampling of circuit variants; the absence of shot noise therefore yields a reduced estimator variance relative to quantum hardware implementations of TE-PAI. We simulate representative disordered one-dimensional spin-ring Hamiltonians, and numerically observe reductions in the per-sample gate-count by a factor of up to $10^3$ relative to Trotterized MPS evolution, yielding orders of magnitude reduction in the time-to-solution under realistic levels of parallelisation. Finally, we numerically observe that MPS TE-PAI is substantially more robust against severe bond-dimension truncation than product formulas, potentially making it useful for the simulation of strongly correlated systems where truncation is necessary in practice. We also demonstrate that the approach can be used naturally in combination with existing time evolution algorithms, effectively extending their time depth via parallelisation.

## 混乱与量子隧道

**Original Title:** Chaos and Quantum Tunneling

**Authors:** Akira Shudo

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12926v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.12926v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-14


### 摘要

在既不是完全可积也不是完全混乱的通用Hamilton系统中，相空间由规则和混乱成分的混合物组成。在经典动力学中，相空间中不同不变集之间的转变是严格禁止的，这些集充当彼此的动力学屏障。相比之下，在量子力学中，波动效应允许通过此类动力学屏障进行转变。这个过程被称为动态隧道效应，指的是穿透相空间中的动态屏障，并于20世纪80年代初首次被发现。从那时起，动力学隧穿的各个方面都得到了阐明，大大推进了我们对这种新量子现象的理解。在这篇文章中，我们提供了一个概述的动力学隧穿，包括混沌辅助和共振辅助隧道的几个唯象的观点，并介绍了基于经典力学扩展到复杂的领域的方法。特别是，我们试图澄清什么是常见的说法，即“混沌导致增强的隧穿概率”，这是经常动态隧穿时，穿着。我们讨论什么制度，这是指，如果这样的增强发生，它可能的起源是什么。


### Abstract

In generic Hamiltonian systems that are neither completely integrable nor fully chaotic, phase space consists of a mixture of regular and chaotic components. In classical dynamics, transitions between different invariant sets in phase space are strictly forbidden, and these sets act as dynamical barriers to one another. In quantum mechanics, in contrast, wave effects allow transitions through such dynamical barriers. This process, known as dynamical tunneling, refers to penetration through dynamical barriers in phase space and was first recognized in the early 1980s. Since then, various aspects of dynamical tunneling have been elucidated, significantly advancing our understanding of such a novel quantum phenomenon. In this article, we provide an overview of several phenomenological perspectives of dynamical tunneling, including chaos-assisted and resonance-assisted tunneling, and also introduce approaches based on classical mechanics extended into the complex domain. In particular, we seek to clarify what is meant by the common claim that "chaos leads to an enhancement of the tunneling probability", which is often made when dynamical tunneling is dressed. We discuss what regime this refers to and, if such an enhancement occurs, what its likely origin is.

## 具有手征异常的调味格子Schwinger模型

**Original Title:** Flavoured Lattice Schwinger Model with Chiral Anomaly

**Authors:** Dogukan Bakircioglu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.13146v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.13146v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-14


### 摘要

我们引入了\{风味格子Schwinger模型}，这是一种$（1{+}1）$维$U（1）$格点规范理论，其中费米子加倍问题是通过错开$\mathbb{Z}_{2}$风味自由度而不是错开手征性来解决的。与所有标准方法不同，调味结构在有限格点间距下保留了精确的轴向$U（1）$对称性。我们推导出连续体极限，表明该模型简化为标记为$a\in\{0，1\}$的无质量Schwinger模型的两个副本。核心结果是，调味结构承认定义良好、规则化、规范不变的格子轴向电荷$Q_{G}^{A}$，具有手征异常方程$\langle dQ_{G}^{A}/dt\rangle = -（2g/pi）\int Dx\，\langle E（x）\rangle$在连续体极限中，作为有限格间距下最小规范耦合的直接动力学结果而推导。限制到$a =0$部门可以恢复标准的单一口味结果。我们进一步表明，风味区的空间分离可以通过生活在带状$（2{+}1）$维Bernevig--Hughes--Zhang拓扑绝缘体边界上的螺旋边缘状态来实现。这为手征异常和费米子加倍提供了体边界图像解决方案。


### Abstract

We introduce the \emph{flavoured lattice Schwinger model}, a $(1{+}1)$-dimensional $U(1)$ lattice gauge theory in which the fermion doubling problem is resolved by staggering a $\mathbb{Z}_{2}$ flavour degree of freedom rather than staggering chirality. Unlike all standard approaches, the flavoured construction preserves an exact axial $U(1)$ symmetry at finite lattice spacing. We derive the continuum limit, showing the model reduces to two copies of the massless Schwinger model labelled by $α\in\{0,1\}$. The central result is that the flavoured construction admits a well-defined, regularized, gauge-invariant lattice axial charge $Q_{G}^{A}$ with chiral anomaly equation $\langle dQ_{G}^{A}/dt\rangle = -(2g/π)\int dx\,\langle E(x)\rangle$ in the continuum limit, derived as a direct dynamical consequence of minimal gauge coupling at finite lattice spacing. Restricting to the $α=0$ sector recovers the standard single-flavour result. We further show that spatial separation of the flavour sectors can be realised as a helical edge states living on the boundaries of a ribbon shaped $(2{+}1)$-dimensional Bernevig--Hughes--Zhang topological insulator. This provides a bulk-boundary picture solution to both the chiral anomaly and fermion doubling.

## 用多原子门快速测量中性原子

**Original Title:** Fast measurement of neutral atoms with a multi-atom gate

**Authors:** Yotam Vaknin, Ran Finkelstein, Ofer Firstenberg, Alex Retzker

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.13158v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.13158v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-14


### 摘要

测量时间是限制中性原子量子计算机运算速度的一个关键瓶颈，因为它无法像其他量子操作一样通过并行化来加速。我们提出了一种基于新的快速多原子里德堡门的中性原子快速测量协议，该协议显着减少了测量积分时间并提高了测量保真度。我们的方法在单个里德伯封锁区域内使用$N$辅助原子的多量子位寄存器来测量单个数据量子位。这可以将光发射收集提高N倍，同时降低测量对损失的敏感性。该方案需要数据量子位和辅助之间的光谱分离，这可以通过双物种架构或有针对性的光漂移来实现。除此之外，该方案实施起来很简单：它仅依赖于全局脉冲、全局量子收集，并避免原子穿梭和数字优化脉冲。Cs--RB平台的模拟表明，仅使用五个支腿（$N=5$），即可在6美元内实现低于$10 &{-3}$的测量不忠实度\ μ\text{s}$。


### Abstract

Measurement time represents a critical bottleneck limiting the operational speed of neutral atom quantum computers, as it cannot be accelerated through parallelization like other quantum operations. We present a protocol for fast measurement of neutral atoms based on a new, fast multi-atom Rydberg gate that significantly reduces the measurement integration time and improves the measurement fidelity. Our approach employs a multi-qubit register of $N$ ancilla atoms within a single Rydberg blockade region to measure a single data qubit. This enables an $N$-fold enhancement in photon emission collections, while reducing the measurement's sensitivity to loss. The scheme requires spectral separation between the data qubit and the ancillae, achievable through either a dual-species architecture or a targeted light shift. Beyond this, the scheme is straightforward to implement: it relies only on global pulses, global photon collection, and avoids both atom shuttling and numerically optimized pulses. Simulations of a Cs--Rb platform demonstrate that with only five ancillae ($N=5$), measurement infidelity below $10^{-3}$ within $6\ μ\text{s}$ is achievable.

## Floquet混乱多体系统中真正的量子伤疤

**Original Title:** Genuine quantum scars in Floquet chaotic many-body systems

**Authors:** Harald Schmid, Andrea Pizzi, Johannes Knolle

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.13164v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.13164v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-14


### 摘要

不稳定的周期轨道是经典混沌系统的组织结构，也是量子疤痕的基础。在单粒子系统中，基于不稳定周期轨道的真正的量子伤疤早已为人所知，最近已经扩展到孤立的多体系统中的时间无关的哈密顿量。然而，它们在周期性驱动下的命运在很大程度上仍然是未知的，受到这些系统通常应该加热到无限温度的期望的挑战。在这里，我们调查如何真正的疤痕竞争的驱动器在Floquet多体系统。使用混沌自旋链，我们证明了Floquet状态仍然疤痕在高频极限。除了这种静态的对应关系，我们发现额外的，驾驶引起的Floquet疤痕没有静态的模拟。我们构建了一个丰富的动态稳定图与中频制度的增强和淬火疤痕，我们理解与经典的分析的李雅普诺夫指数。我们的研究结果将Floquet系统定位为调整量子多体系统疤痕行为的天然平台。


### Abstract

Unstable periodic orbits act as organizing structures for classical chaotic systems and underpin quantum scarring. Long known in single-particle systems, genuine quantum scars based on unstable periodic orbits have been recently extended to isolated many-body systems for time-independent Hamiltonians. Their fate under periodic driving, however, remains largely uncharted, challenged by the expectation that these systems should in general heat to infinite temperature. Here, we investigate how genuine scarring competes with the drive in a Floquet many-body system. Using chaotic spin chains, we demonstrate that Floquet states remain scarred in the high-frequency limit. Beyond this static correspondence, we uncover additional, driving-induced Floquet scars with no static analog. We construct a rich dynamical stability diagram with intermediate-frequency regimes of enhanced and quenched scarring, which we understand with a classical analysis of the Lyapunov exponent. Our results position Floquet systems as a natural platform for tuning the scarring behavior of quantum many-body systems.

We also show that applying this design in order to sort a set of Orbital Angular Momentum (OAM) modes with zero radial index reproduces the well-known Fork grating configuration. Furthermore, by taking the limit of M -> inf, we present an analytical expression for sorting all the modes of a given family. By operating this device in reverse, it can be used to generate arbitrary modes, by illuminating it with a Gaussian beam. The power transmission coefficient for this sorter goes as 1/M and we provide a mathematical proof that this is optimal for any typical arrangement of the detector positions. We further study the sorter sensitivity to wavelength and random phase noise.

## 量子物质在低能量下弱纠缠

**Original Title:** Quantum matter is weakly entangled at low energies

**Authors:** Samuel J. Garratt, Dmitry A. Abanin

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14143v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.14143v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-15


### 摘要

我们构建了多体量子状态的纠缠性翁的上界，这些量子状态相对于几何局部Hamilton具有固定的能量期望值。我们的重点是约占整个系统一半的子系统的纠缠性信息。冯·诺伊曼纠缠量的上限是两个虚拟系统在相同温度下的热熵之和的一半，在某些系统中还有额外的面积定律贡献。选择有效温度，使两个虚拟系统的热能之和与原始问题中状态能量的限制相匹配;在次延能下，该温度随着系统尺寸的增加而降低。我们对雷尼纠缠量的上界采取类似的形式。作为第一个应用，我们表明，无挫败（FF）系统中的接地状态施密特等级是由作用于子系统的汉密尔顿体的接地状态简并度为上界的。因此，当子系统的零温热熵随表面积而不是随子系统体积而变化时，态冯·诺伊曼和雷尼纠缠量遵循面积定律。该结果与光谱间隙无关。对于具有明确定义的比热容量（并且不一定是FF）的量子物质物理模型来说，我们的边界提供了一种将热力学数据转换为对亚延能量和广泛能量下纯状态纠缠的约束的方法。我们还表明，在各种系统中，我们对半系统纠缠量的上界是最优的，直到次领先修正。我们的结果将物理热力学性质与低能量多体希尔伯特空间的结构联系起来。


### Abstract

We construct upper bounds on entanglement entropies of many-body quantum states that have fixed energy expectation values with respect to geometrically local Hamiltonians. Our focus is on entanglement entropies of subsystems that make up approximately half of the full system. The upper bound on the von Neumann entanglement entropy is half the sum of the thermal entropies of two fictitious systems at the same temperature as one another, with an additional area-law contribution in some systems. The effective temperature is chosen such that the sum of the thermal energies of the two fictitious systems matches the constraint on the energy of the state in the original problem; at subextensive energies, this temperature decreases with increasing system size. Our upper bounds on Rényi entanglement entropies take an analogous form. As a first application we show that ground-state Schmidt ranks in frustration-free (FF) systems are upper bounded by the ground-state degeneracies of Hamiltonians acting on subsystems. Ground-state von Neumann and Rényi entanglement entropies therefore follow an area law when the zero-temperature thermal entropies of subsystems scale with surface areas, rather than with subsystem volumes. This result holds independently of the spectral gap. For physical models of quantum matter, which have well-defined specific heat capacities (and are not necessarily FF), our bounds provide a way to convert this thermodynamic data into constraints on pure-state entanglement at both subextensive and extensive energies. We also show that our upper bounds on half-system entanglement entropies are optimal, up to subleading corrections, in wide varieties of systems. Our results relate physical thermodynamic properties to the structure of many-body Hilbert space at low energies.

## 通过量子电容研究狄拉克材料中非厄密性的热力学特征

**Original Title:** Thermodynamic signatures of non-Hermiticity in Dirac materials via quantum capacitance

**Authors:** Juan Pablo Esparza, Francisco J. Peña, Patricio Vargas, Vladimir Juričić

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14150v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.14150v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-15


### 摘要

非埃尔米特能带描述捕捉了损失、收益和环境耦合如何重塑量子物质，但大多数实验测试依赖于基于波的或动态探测器。在这里，我们在狄拉克材料中建立了一条通往异常物理的新平衡路径：在弱非厄米特区中，热力学状态密度和量子电容表现出异常点的普适平衡方法。在我们的最小非互惠石墨烯模型中，跳跃不平衡将狄拉克速度降低为$v_F=v\squtt {1-β #2}$，这意味着低能量密度的状态、热力学状态密度和量子电容均按比例缩放为$（1-β #2）'| β|\到1-$。因此，在电荷中性时，量子电容在温度上保持线性，但前置因子存在分歧，而逆响应在接近异常点时线性软化。在磁场中，这表现为兰道级间距的崩溃和相应的热活跃级聚集。补充地，双垂直布洛赫状态表现出彼得曼因子$K=（1-β #2）'#+[-1}$，它隔离了本征向非垂直性的不可约非厄米特效应。这些结果将量子电容确定为狄拉克材料中有效非厄米性的实验可用体平衡探测器。


### Abstract

Non-Hermitian band descriptions capture how loss, gain, and environmental coupling reshape quantum matter, yet most experimental tests rely on wave-based or dynamical probes. Here we establish a new equilibrium route to exceptional physics in Dirac materials: in the weakly non-Hermitian regime, the thermodynamic density of states and the quantum capacitance exhibit a universal equilibrium approach to the exceptional point. In our minimal non-reciprocal graphene model, the hopping imbalance reduces the Dirac velocity as $v_F=v\sqrt{1-β^2}$, implying that the low-energy density of states, the thermodynamic density of states, and the quantum capacitance all scale as $(1-β^2)^{-1}$ as $|β|\to 1^-$. Consequently, at charge neutrality the quantum capacitance remains linear in temperature but with a diverging prefactor, while the inverse response softens linearly on approaching the exceptional point. In a magnetic field, this manifests as a collapse of the Landau-level spacing and a corresponding crowding of thermally active levels. Complementarily, the biorthogonal Bloch states exhibit a Petermann factor $K=(1-β^2)^{-1}$, which isolates the irreducibly non-Hermitian effect of eigenvector non-orthogonality. These results identify quantum capacitance as an experimentally accessible bulk equilibrium probe of effective non-Hermiticity in Dirac materials.
