## 当1D中平移对称性被打破时，非零动量意味着远程纠缠

**Original Title:** Non-zero Momentum Implies Long-Range Entanglement When Translation Symmetry is Broken in 1D

**Authors:** Amanda Gatto Lamas, Taylor L. Hughes

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.15345v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.15345v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-20


### 摘要

Gioia和Wang的结果[Phys Rev X 12，031007（2022）]表明，具有非零动量的对称状态必然是远程纠缠（LRE）。在这里，我们考虑这样一个问题：非平移对称状态的动量概念能否像平移对称状态那样直接编码其纠缠的性质？我们表明，对于一维系统来说，答案是肯定的，而更高维度的扩展和布局有序的系统需要进一步的工作。虽然Gioia和Wang的结果适用于通过有限深度量子电路连接到平移对称状态的状态，但找到这样的电路来确定打破平移对称性的状态纠缠的性质通常是不切实际的。在这里，我们关注的不是平移本征态，而是多体动量分布和具有破坏平移对称性的系统的多体状态中的平移操作符的期望值。我们表明，在连续体中限制了翻译运算符$的期望值的大小|<T>|$ necessarily goes to $1$ for delocalized states, a proxy for LRE states in 1D systems.这个结果可以被视为雷斯塔局部化长度公式的动量空间版本。我们研究了在具有和不具有明确定义的连续体限制的不同网格模型中我们的结果的准确性。为此，我们引入了两个模型：随机二聚体模型的确定性版本，说明了热力学和连续体极限对我们在格子水平上的结果的作用，以及奥布里-安德烈模型的简化版本，动量和位置空间都有相称的跳跃。最后，我们使用随机二聚体模型作为$准确性的测试案例|<T>|$ as a localization (and thus entanglement) probe for 1D periodic lattice models without a well-defined continuum limit.


### Abstract

A result by Gioia and Wang [Phys Rev X 12, 031007 (2022)] showed that translationally symmetric states having nonzero momentum are necessarily long range entangled (LRE). Here, we consider the question: can a notion of momentum for non-translation symmetric states directly encode the nature of their entanglement, as it does for translation symmetric states? We show the answer is affirmative for 1D systems, while higher dimensional extensions and topologically ordered systems require further work. While Gioia and Wang's result applies to states connected via finite depth quantum circuits to a translation symmetric state, it is often impractical to find such a circuit to determine the nature of the entanglement of states that break translation symmetry. Here, instead of translation eigenstates, we focus on the many-body momentum distribution and the expectation value of the translation operator in many-body states of systems having broken translation symmetry. We show that in the continuum limit the magnitude of the expectation value of the translation operator $|<T>|$ necessarily goes to $1$ for delocalized states, a proxy for LRE states in 1D systems. This result can be seen as a momentum-space version of Resta's formula for the localization length. We investigate how accurate our results are in different lattice models with and without well-defined continuum limits. To that end, we introduce two models: a deterministic version of the random dimer model, illustrating the role of the thermodynamic and continuum limits for our result at a lattice level, and a simplified version of the Aubry-Andre model, with commensurate hopping for both momentum and position space. Finally, we use the random dimer model as a test case for the accuracy of $|<T>|$ as a localization (and thus entanglement) probe for 1D periodic lattice models without a well-defined continuum limit.

## 辅助量子位弱耦合下的精度限制

**Original Title:** Precision limit under weak-coupling with ancillary qubit

**Authors:** Peng Chen, Jun Jing

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.15354v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.15354v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-21


### 摘要

我们在复合模型中提出了一种基于测量的量子计量协议，其中探针系统（旋转系综）与具有一般海森堡XXZ相互作用的辅助两级系统（量子位）耦合。通过优化且较弱的探针-辅助耦合强度和适当的联合进化持续时间，对量子位的无条件测量引发的探针系统的两条平行进化路径可以将旋转系综集体角动量操作子的本征状态转化为本征空间中距离较大的二分量状态。因此，关于极化状态或其叠加的探针系统中编码的相的量子费舍尔信息（可以放宽到混合状态）可以表现出相对于探针尺寸（旋转数）$N$的精确或渐进二次缩放。发现二次缩放行为对不完美的编码操作符和耦合强度不敏感。通过辅助量子位或探测系统上的宇称检测，相灵敏度可以接近海森堡极限。我们认为，对量子位的无条件测量可以成为取代类似格林伯格-霍恩-蔡林格状态和压缩Hamilton的有效资源，以超过计量精度的标准量子极限。


### Abstract

We propose a measurement-based quantum metrology protocol in a composite model, where the probe system (a spin ensemble) is coupled to an ancillary two-level system (qubit) with a general Heisenberg XXZ interaction. With an optimized and weak probe-ancilla coupling strength and a proper duration of joint evolution, the two parallel evolution paths of the probe system induced by the unconditional measurement on qubit can transform an eigenstate of the collective angular momentum operator of spin ensemble to be a two-component state with a large distance in eigenspace. The quantum Fisher information about the phase encoded in the probe system of polarized states or their superposition, that could be relaxed to mixed states, can therefore manifest an exact or asymptotic quadratic scaling with respect to the probe size (spin number) $N$. The quadratic scaling behavior is found to be insensitive to the imperfect encoding operator and coupling strength. By virtue of the parity detection on the ancillary qubit or the probe system, the phase sensitivity can approach the Heisenberg limit. We suggest that the unconditional measurement on qubit could become an efficient resource to replace Greenberger-Horne-Zeilinger-like states and squeezing Hamiltonian for exceeding the standard quantum limit in metrology precision.

## 利用宽场量子金刚石显微镜进行金刚石hBN场效应晶体管电流的基片内成像

**Original Title:** In-Substrate Imaging of Diamond hBN FET Current via Widefield Quantum Diamond Microscopy

**Authors:** Anuj Bathla, Subrat Kumar Pradhan, Ajit Kumar Dash, Prabhat Anand, M. Girish Chandra, Kenji Watanabe, Takashi Taniguchi, Akshay Singh, Veeresh Deshpande, Kasturi Saha

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.15355v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.15355v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-21


### 摘要

我们展示了氢端金刚石场效应晶体管（场效应晶体管）中电流通过基片内氮空位（NV）中心的宽场磁成像。金刚石表面的氢终止会引发二维空洞气（2DHG），而位于表面下方1~μm的近表面NV中心的集合使得能够以微米级空间分辨率对电流进行无创磁性成像。在漏极源极偏置$V_{ds}= 0$至$-15 V $和栅电压$V_{gs}=+3 $至$-9V$范围内对场效应晶体管进行了电气特征，然后在器件操作期间进行现场宽场NV磁力测量。磁场图和重建的电流密度分布直接可视化源漏接触处的电流注入和hBN门控沟道下方的传输。磁场图揭示了由于栅电介质中的不均匀性或缺陷而导致的沟道区域中的电流密度变化。此外，我们观察到激光照射期间漏极电流（$\sim 600-900 μA$）的显着增强和表观阈值电压的漂移，反映了通道静电的光致变化。通过将门相关磁图像与同时的电气测量相关联，我们将空间电流分布与场效应晶体管传输特性直接联系起来，为晶体管沟道中的埋入界面输运和非均匀门控效应提供了新的见解。由于该方法与顶部门控场效应晶体管兼容，因此可以用于以微米分辨率绘制新兴沟道材料（例如2D材料和宽带间隙沟道）中的沟道电流分布，并建立宽场NV磁力测量法作为探测晶体管和范德华电介电杂结构中电荷输运的强大平台。


### Abstract

We demonstrate widefield magnetic imaging of current flow in hydrogen terminated diamond field effect transistors (FETs) through in-substrate nitrogen vacancy (NV) centers. Hydrogen termination of the diamond surface induces a two dimensional hole gas (2DHG), while an ensemble of near surface NV centers located $ \sim 1~μm$ below the surface enables noninvasive magnetic imaging of current flow with micrometer scale spatial resolution. The FETs were electrically characterized over a range of drain source biases $V_{ds}= 0$ to $-15V$ and gate voltages,$V_{gs}= +3$ to $-9V$ followed by in situ widefield NV magnetometry during device operation. Magnetic field maps and reconstructed current density distributions directly visualize current injection at the source drain contacts and transport beneath the hBN gated channel. Magnetic field maps reveal current density variations in the channel region owing to non-uniformities or defects in the gate dielectric. In addition, we observe a pronounced enhancement of the drain current ($\sim 600-900 μA$) and a shift in the apparent threshold voltage during laser illumination, reflecting photo induced changes in channel electrostatics. By correlating gate dependent magnetic images with simultaneous electrical measurements, we directly link spatial current distributions to FET transfer characteristics, providing new insight into buried interface transport and non-uniform gating effects in the transistor channel. As the methodology is compatible with top gated FETs, it can be used to map channel current distributions with micrometer resolution in emerging channel materials, such as 2D materials and wide bandgap channels, and establish widefield NV magnetometry as a powerful platform for probing charge transport in transistors and Van der Waals dielectric heterostructures.

## 两个非相关源超分辨率的多参数估计

**Original Title:** Multiparameter estimation for the superresolution of two incoherent sources

**Authors:** Antonin Grateau, Alexander Boeschoten, Tanguy Favin-Lévêque, Isael Herrera, Nicolas Treps

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.14876v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.14876v2

**Submitted:** 2026-01-21


### 摘要

我们通过实验证明了在亚Rayleigh区域中同时估计描述一对非相干光源的三个参数，从而实现超分辨率场景描述。使用空间模式解多路分解（SPADE）和两个解多路分解（其中一个是故意移动的），我们确定远低于折射极限的分离度，并在单一实验环境中在广泛的场景配置范围内实现分离度、重心和相对亮度的敏感联合估计。我们使用基于费舍尔信息的Cramér-Rao界限来基准测试我们的性能，并讨论相应的量子极限。我们调查了两种补充的情况：来源略有不同的现实情况，以及来源难以区分的理想情况。


### Abstract

Using spatial-mode demultiplexing (SPADE) with two demultiplexers--one deliberately shifted--we determine separations well below the diffraction limit and achieve sensitive joint estimation of separation, centroid, and relative brightness over a broad range of scene configurations in a single experimental setting. We benchmark our performance using Fisher-information-based Cramér-Rao bounds, and discuss the corresponding quantum limits. We investigate two complementary scenarios: a realistic case with slightly non-identical sources, and an idealized case of indistinguishable sources.

## USDS：使用对称性的通用稳定器解码器框架

**Original Title:** USDs: A universal stabilizer decoder framework using symmetry

**Authors:** Hoshitaro Ohnishi, Hideo Mukai

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.15361v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.15361v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-21


### 摘要

量子误差纠正对于实现可靠的量子计算不可或缺。当量子信息被冗余编码时，使用多个物理量子位构建更大的希尔伯特空间，并在指定的子空间内执行计算。当将深度学习应用于量子错误纠正码的解码时，一个关键挑战来自提供给解码器的伴随式测量与构成地面真值标签的相应错误模式之间的非唯一性。在之前的工作的基础上，通过根据宇称检查结构固有的对称性重新优化解码器来解决复曲面码的这个问题，我们将这种方法推广到任意稳定器码。在我们的实验中，我们采用多层感知器来逼近连续函数，以补充Color代码和Golay代码的综合症测量。使用这些模型，我们对每个代码进行了解码器重新优化。对于Color码，我们在物理错误率为5%的情况下实现了约0.8%的解码准确性提高，而对于Golay码，准确性提高了约0.1%。此外，从每个代码的连续函数逼近中的几何和代数结构的评估来看，我们表明广义连续函数的设计有利于学习代码中固有的几何结构。我们的结果还表明，忠实地复制代码结构的逼近可以对重新优化的有效性产生显着影响。这项研究表明，之前被证明对Toric码有效的重新优化技术可以被推广，以解决将深度学习应用于稳定器码解码时出现的标签简并性挑战。


### Abstract

Quantum error correction is indispensable to achieving reliable quantum computation. When quantum information is encoded redundantly, a larger Hilbert space is constructed using multiple physical qubits, and the computation is performed within a designated subspace. When applying deep learning to the decoding of quantum error-correcting codes, a key challenge arises from the non-uniqueness between the syndrome measurements provided to the decoder and the corresponding error patterns that constitute the ground-truth labels. Building upon prior work that addressed this issue for the toric code by re-optimizing the decoder with respect to the symmetry inherent in the parity-check structure, we generalize this approach to arbitrary stabilizer codes. In our experiments, we employed multilayer perceptrons to approximate continuous functions that complement the syndrome measurements of the Color code and the Golay code. Using these models, we performed decoder re-optimization for each code. For the Color code, we achieved an improvement of approximately 0.8% in decoding accuracy at a physical error rate of 5%, while for the Golay code the accuracy increased by about 0.1%. Furthermore, from the evaluation of the geometric and algebraic structures in the continuous function approximation for each code, we showed that the design of generalized continuous functions is advantageous for learning the geometric structure inherent in the code. Our results also indicate that approximations that faithfully reproduce the code structure can have a significant impact on the effectiveness of reoptimization. This study demonstrates that the re-optimization technique previously shown to be effective for the Toric code can be generalized to address the challenge of label degeneracy that arises when applying deep learning to the decoding of stabilizer codes.

## 量子二聚体模型中精确可解的拓扑相转变

**Original Title:** Exactly solvable topological phase transition in a quantum dimer model

**Authors:** Laura Shou, Jeet Shah, Matthew Lerner-Brecher, Amol Aggarwal, Alexei Borodin, Victor Galitski

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.15377v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.15377v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-21


### 摘要

我们引入了一个家庭的广义Rokhsar-Kivelson（RK）哈密顿量，这是反向工程有一个任意的边缘加权叠加的二聚体覆盖作为其确切的基态在RK点。然后，我们专注于一个量子二聚体模型的三角晶格，具有双周期的边缘权重。为了简单起见，我们考虑一个2\times1 $周期性模型，其中所有的权重都设置为1，除了一个可调的水平边缘权重标记为$α$。我们解析地证明了该模型在$α=3$处表现出连续的量子相变，从拓扑的$\mathbb{Z}_2$量子自旋液体（$α<3$）转变为柱状有序态（$α>3$）.二聚体-二聚体相关器在转变的两侧呈指数级衰减，相关长度为$RST\propto 1/|阿尔法-3| $并作为临界点的权力定律。维森相关器在旋转液体相中表现出指数衰减，但在有序相中变成一个常数。我们根据双二聚体模型的循环统计来解释恒维ison相关器。使用vison相关器的有限大小缩放，我们提取与2D Ising普适性类一致的临界指数。


### Abstract

We introduce a family of generalized Rokhsar-Kivelson (RK) Hamiltonians, which are reverse-engineered to have an arbitrary edge-weighted superposition of dimer coverings as their exact ground state at the RK point. We then focus on a quantum dimer model on the triangular lattice, with doubly-periodic edge weights. For simplicity we consider a $2\times1$ periodic model in which all weights are set to one except for a tunable horizontal edge weight labeled $α$. We analytically show that the model exhibits a continuous quantum phase transition at $α=3$, changing from a topological $\mathbb{Z}_2$ quantum spin liquid ($α<3$) to a columnar ordered state ($α>3$). The dimer-dimer correlator decays exponentially on both sides of the transition with the correlation length $ξ\propto1/|α-3|$ and as a power-law at criticality. The vison correlator exhibits an exponential decay in the spin liquid phase, but becomes a constant in the ordered phase. We explain the constant vison correlator in terms of loops statistics of the double-dimer model. Using finite-size scaling of the vison correlator, we extract critical exponents consistent with the 2D Ising universality class.

## 在长基线中微子实验中探索量子性

**Original Title:** Exploring Quantumness at Long-Baseline Neutrino Experiments

**Authors:** Murshed Alam, Vedran Brdar, Dibya S. Chattopadhyay

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.15375v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.15375v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-21


### 摘要

正如莱格特和加格提出的那样，可以通过在不同时间对系统进行的测量来探索经典性的违反。具体来说，违反莱格特-加格不等式表明宏观系统中存在量子效应。长基线中微子实验提供了一些可以执行此类测试的最长可用传播距离。之前对中微子领域莱格特-加格测试的研究主要集中在表明振荡概率可能违反某些参数选择的经典界限。在这项工作中，我们开发了一个更完整的数据驱动框架，该框架既处理代表经典和量子行为的分布，也处理实验不确定性。我们考虑了MINOS、T2 K、NOvA以及即将推出的DUNE，并提出了在这些长基线中微子实验中区分量子行为与经典场景的各自统计意义。其中，我们发现T2 K对经典性的违反最为严重，达到$\sim 14西格玛$，NOvA和DUNE的预测也产生了超过5美元西格玛$的重要性。


### Abstract

Violations of classicality can be probed through measurements performed on a system at different times, as proposed by Leggett and Garg. Specifically, violations of Leggett-Garg inequalities suggest the presence of quantum effects in macroscopic systems. Long-baseline neutrino experiments provide some of the longest available propagation distances over which such tests can be performed. Previous studies of Leggett-Garg tests in the neutrino sector have largely focused on showing that the oscillation probabilities can violate classical bounds for certain parameter choices. In this work, we develop a more complete and data-driven framework that treats both the distributions representing the classical and quantum behavior, as well as the experimental uncertainties. We consider MINOS, T2K, NOvA, as well as the upcoming DUNE, and present the respective statistical significance for distinguishing quantum behavior from classical scenarios at these long-baseline neutrino experiments. Among them, we find that T2K yields the most significant violation of classicality, at the level of $\sim 14 σ$, with NOvA and projections for DUNE also resulting in a significance of more than $5σ$.

## 计算双向量子容量

**Original Title:** The computational two-way quantum capacity

**Authors:** Johannes Jakob Meyer, Jacopo Rizzo, Asad Raza, Lorenzo Leone, Sofiene Jerbi, Jens Eisert

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.15393v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.15393v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-21


### 摘要

量子通道容量是量子信息理论的基础。然而，它们的定义并不限制发送者和接收者的计算资源。在这项工作中，我们启动了计算量子能力的研究。这些量化了在施加加密和解码必须具有计算效率的自然要求时可以可靠地传输多少信息。我们重点关注计算双向量子容量，并展示它与通道Choi态的计算可纠缠密切相关。这种联系使我们能够展示出明显的计算能力分离。在标准密码假设下，存在一个具有多项复杂度的量子通道，其计算双向量子容量消失，而其无界量子容量几乎是最大的。更重要的是，我们表明，当通道复杂性离开多项领域时，计算量子容量存在从接近最大值到零的急剧转变。我们的结果表明，计算效率的自然要求可以从根本上改变量子通信的局限性。


### Abstract

Quantum channel capacities are fundamental to quantum information theory. Their definition, however, does not limit the computational resources of sender and receiver. In this work, we initiate the study of computational quantum capacities. These quantify how much information can be reliably transmitted when imposing the natural requirement that en- and decoding have to be computationally efficient. We focus on the computational two-way quantum capacity and showcase that it is closely related to the computational distillable entanglement of the Choi state of the channel. This connection allows us to show a stark computational capacity separation. Under standard cryptographic assumptions, there exists a quantum channel of polynomial complexity whose computational two-way quantum capacity vanishes while its unbounded counterpart is nearly maximal. More so, we show that there exists a sharp transition in computational quantum capacity from nearly maximal to zero when the channel complexity leaves the polynomial realm. Our results demonstrate that the natural requirement of computational efficiency can radically alter the limits of quantum communication.

## 二次张量作为克利福德、高斯和自由费米子物理学的统一

**Original Title:** Quadratic tensors as a unification of Clifford, Gaussian, and free-fermion physics

**Authors:** Andreas Bauer, Seth Lloyd

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.15396v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.15396v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-21


### 摘要

某些系列的量子力学模型可以在经典计算机上有效地描述和求解，包括量子位或qudit Clifford电路和稳定器代码、自由玻色子或自由费米子模型以及某些旋转体和GKP代码。我们表明，所有这些族都可以描述为相同代数结构的实例，即阿贝尔群上的二次函数，或者更一般地说，（超）霍普夫代数上的二次函数。不同类型的自由度对应于不同的“基本”阿贝尔群或霍普夫代数：$\mathbb{Z}_2$表示量子位，$\mathbb {Z}_d$表示量子位，$\mathbb{R}$表示连续变量，$\mathbb{Z}$和$\mathbb{R}/\mathbb{Z}$表示旋转子，超霍普夫代数$\mathcalF $表示费米子模式。状态、运算符、超级运算符或投影运算符值测量等对象都是张量。对于上面的可解模型，这些张量是基于二次函数的二次张量。具有$n$自由度的二次张量仅由$O（n#2）$系数完全指定。二次张量的张量网络可以在这些系数的水平上有效地收缩，使用令人想起舒尔补的操作。我们的形式主义自然包括具有混合自由度的模型，例如不同维度的qudits。我们还使用二次函数来定义任意阿贝尔群的广义稳定器码和Clifford门。最后，我们给出了从二次（或二阶）到$i$阶张量的推广，这些张量由$O（n ' i）$系数指定，但一般不能有效地收缩。


### Abstract

**Authors:** Jiahao Cao, Rohan Joshi, Yizhuo Tian, N. S. Srivatsa, Jad C. Halimeh

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.16166v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.16166v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-22


### 摘要

通量弦的物理学是量子束缚和强子化的核心，最近已成为高能物理学（HEP）量子模拟领域的焦点。尽管取得了相当大的进展，但对规范理论的量子模拟相关格子公式中通量弦行为的详细了解仍然局限于规范场的最低截断，而这些截断在得出有关量子场论极限的结论的能力方面受到严重限制。在这里，我们采用张量网络模拟来研究2美元+1$D量子电动力学（QED）的量子链接公式中通量串的行为，其中规范场的旋转为$1$。我们首先绘制出存在两个空间分离的静电荷的情况下该模型的接地状态图，揭示了导致弦断裂的不同微观过程，包括在spin-$\fRAC{1}{2}$公式中不可能实现的两阶段断裂机制。然后，我们从不同的初始产物状态弦配置开始，探索各种参数区域中远非平衡的激灭动力学，展示了真正的$2+$1$D实时弦断裂和类胶球束缚状态的形成，而后者在spin-$\fRAC{1}{2}$公式中是不可能的。在平衡状态和平衡状态下，我们考虑不同的静电荷值和位置。最后，我们为量子模拟实验提供了高效的qudit电路，其中我们的结果可以在最先进的离子陷阱设置中观察到。我们的发现为通量弦的量子模拟达到量子场论极限奠定了基础。


### Abstract

At the heart of quark confinement and hadronization, the physics of flux strings has recently become a focal point in the field of quantum simulation of high-energy physics (HEP). Despite considerable progress, a detailed understanding of the behavior of flux strings in quantum simulation-relevant lattice formulations of gauge theories has remained limited to the lowest truncations of the gauge field, which are severely limited in their ability to draw conclusions about the quantum field theory limit. Here, we employ tensor network simulations to investigate the behavior of flux strings in a quantum link formulation of $2+1$D quantum electrodynamics (QED) with a spin-$1$ representation of the gauge field. We first map out the ground-state phase diagram of this model in the presence of two spatially separated static charges, revealing distinct microscopic processes responsible for string breaking, including a two-stage breaking mechanism not possible in the spin-$\frac{1}{2}$ formulation. Starting in different initial product state string configurations, we then explore far-from-equilibrium quench dynamics across various parameter regimes, demonstrating genuine $2+1$D real-time string breaking and glueball-like bound state formation, with the latter not possible in the spin-$\frac{1}{2}$ formulation. In and out of equilibrium, we consider different values and placements of the static charges. Finally, we provide efficient qudit circuits for a quantum simulation experiment in which our results can be observed in state-of-the-art ion-trap setups. Our findings lay the groundwork for quantum simulations of flux strings towards the quantum field theory limit.

## 无限温度下的稳定剂热本征状态

**Original Title:** Stabilizer Thermal Eigenstates at Infinite Temperature

**Authors:** Akihiro Hokkyo

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.16177v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.16177v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-22


### 摘要

了解如何分析高度纠缠的热本征态是量子多体系统研究的核心挑战。在这封信中，我们引入了一种基于稳定器的方法来构建不可积多体Hamilton的分析易处理的能量本征状态。我们关注无限温度下的零能量本征态，证明了一个尖锐的不去定理：对于任何$k\ge 4 $，两体Hamilton的稳定本征态都不能满足$k$-体微观热平衡。我们通过显式地构造二体不可积Hamilton，进一步表明这个界限是紧的，其稳定器本征态再现了所有二体和所有三体可观测量的热期望值。最后，我们通过描述稳定器状态可以出现为Hamilton的零能量本征状态的条件来确定这种限制的结构起源，从而揭示了相互作用的少体性质所施加的基本约束。


### Abstract

Understanding how to analyze highly entangled thermal eigenstates is a central challenge in the study of quantum many-body systems. In this Letter, we introduce a stabilizer-based approach to construct analytically tractable energy eigenstates of nonintegrable many-body Hamiltonians. Focusing on zero-energy eigenstates at infinite temperature, we prove a sharp no-go theorem: stabilizer eigenstates of two-body Hamiltonians cannot satisfy $k$-body microscopic thermal equilibrium for any $k\ge4$. We further show that this bound is tight by explicitly constructing two-body nonintegrable Hamiltonians whose stabilizer eigenstates reproduce thermal expectation values for all two-body and all three-body observables. Finally, we identify the structural origin of this limitation by characterizing the conditions under which a stabilizer state can appear as a zero-energy eigenstate of a Hamiltonian, thereby revealing a fundamental constraint imposed by the few-body nature of interactions.

## 在量子计算机上利用波包动力学研究能量分辨传输

**Original Title:** Studying energy-resolved transport with wavepacket dynamics on quantum computers

**Authors:** Melody Lee, Roland C. Farrell

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.16180v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.16180v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-22


### 摘要

在量子模拟器中探测能量依赖的输运需要准备具有可调能量和小能量方差的状态。现有的方法通常研究简单初始状态（例如计算基本状态）的激冷动力学，这些初始状态远离能量本征状态，因此限制了可实现的能量分辨率。在这项工作中，我们建议使用波包来探测传输特性，并提高能量分辨率。为了证明这种方法的实用性，我们在Quantinuum的H2-2量子计算机上准备并进化了波包，并在8 x 7格子上识别了安德森模型中的能量依赖性局部化转变--有限大小的迁移率边缘。我们观察到，以低能量初始化的波包在时间演变下保持空间局部化，而高能波包则去局部化，这与移动性边缘的存在一致。对我们的实验至关重要的是一种错误缓解策略，该策略使用最大似然估计来推断无噪输出位串分布。与后选择相比，该方法消除了系统误差，并将统计不确定性降低了5倍。我们通过开发一种量子算法来在相互作用费米子的一维模型中准备准粒子波包，将我们的方法扩展到多粒子区域。该技术对量子资源的需求适度，使得基于波包的多体系统传输研究成为近期量子计算机的一个有前途的应用。


### Abstract

Probing energy-dependent transport in quantum simulators requires preparing states with tunable energy and small energy variance. Existing approaches often study quench dynamics of simple initial states, such as computational basis states, which are far from energy eigenstates and therefore limit the achievable energy resolution. In this work, we propose using wavepackets to probe transport properties with improved energy resolution. To demonstrate the utility of this approach, we prepare and evolve wavepackets on Quantinuum's H2-2 quantum computer and identify an energy-dependent localization transition in the Anderson model on an 8x7 lattice--a finite-size mobility edge. We observe that a wavepacket initialized at low energy remains spatially localized under time evolution, while a high-energy wavepacket delocalizes, consistent with the presence of a mobility edge. Crucial to our experiments is an error mitigation strategy that infers the noiseless output bit string distribution using maximum-likelihood estimation. Compared to post-selection, this method removes systematic errors and reduces statistical uncertainty by up to a factor of 5. We extend our methods to the many-particle regime by developing a quantum algorithm for preparing quasiparticle wavepackets in a one-dimensional model of interacting fermions. This technique has modest quantum resource requirements, making wavepacket-based studies of transport in many-body systems a promising application for near-term quantum computers.

## 来自哥茨曼-基塔耶夫-普雷斯基尔州的鲁棒贝尔非本地性

**Original Title:** Robust Bell Nonlocality from Gottesman-Kitaev-Preskill States

**Authors:** Xiaotian Yang, Santiago Zamora, Rafael Chaves, Ulrik L. Andersen, Jonatan Bohr Brask, A. de Oliveira Junior

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.16189v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.16189v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-22


### 摘要

基于零差检测的贝尔测试在连续变量系统中受到严格限制。Gottesman-Kitaev-Preskill（GKP）编码能否将零差检测变成揭示贝尔非定域性的实用工具？我们考虑了一个物理激励模型，其中每一方都执行零差检测，并通过固定的周期性分类（对应于逻辑泡利测量）将连续结果数字化。在这个框架内，我们推导出一个双方禁忌：对于贝尔对状态来说，CHSH不能被违反。超越两个政党，我们表明，超压缩的GKP编码的GZZ和W状态仍然表现出强烈的多方非局部性，违反了仅同频元读出的多方贝尔不等式。我们量化了所需的挤压阈值和对损失的鲁棒性，为在连续变量系统中进行基于高频同步的贝尔测试提供了一条途径。


### Abstract

Bell tests based on homodyne detection are strongly constrained in continuous-variable systems. Can Gottesman-Kitaev-Preskill (GKP) encoding turn homodyne detection into a practical tool for revealing Bell nonlocality? We consider a physically motivated model in which each party performs homodyne detection and digitizes the continuous outcome via a fixed periodic binning, corresponding to logical Pauli measurements. Within this framework, we derive a bipartite no-go: CHSH cannot be violated for Bell-pair states. Moving beyond two parties, we show that finitely squeezed GKP-encoded GHZ and W states nevertheless exhibit strong multipartite nonlocality, violating multipartite Bell inequalities with homodyne-only readout. We quantify the required squeezing thresholds and robustness to loss, providing a route toward homodyne-based Bell tests in continuous-variable systems.
