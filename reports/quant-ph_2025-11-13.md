## 任意平面之间的相关全光成像

**Original Title:** Correlation Plenoptic Imaging between Arbitrary Planes

**Authors:** Francesco Di Lena, Gianlorenzo Massaro, Alessandro Lupo, Augusto Garuccio, Francesco V. Pepe, Milena D'Angelo

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2007.12033v2

**PDF:** None

**Submitted:** 2020-07-23


### 摘要

我们提出了一种新的方法，通过测量感兴趣的三维场景内任意选择的两个参考平面之间的光的二阶相关性，在折射极限处执行全光成像。我们表明，对于混乱光和纠缠量子照明，该协议能够在后处理中改变聚焦平面，并实现图像分辨率和景深的前所未有的组合。特别是，景深结果相对于之前的相关全光成像协议大3倍，相对于标准成像大一个数量级，而分辨率保持在折射极限。这些结果引领了基于混乱光的相关全光成像设备以及基于纠缠量子照明的高SNR全光成像设备的紧凑设计的开发，从而有助于使相关全光成像与商业全光设备有效竞争。


### Abstract

We propose a novel method to perform plenoptic imaging at the diffraction limit by measuring second-order correlations of light between two reference planes, arbitrarily chosen, within the tridimensional scene of interest. We show that for both chaotic light and entangled-photon illumination, the protocol enables to change the focused planes, in post-processing, and to achieve an unprecedented combination of image resolution and depth of field. In particular, the depth of field results larger by a factor 3 with respect to previous correlation plenoptic imaging protocols, and by an order of magnitude with respect to standard imaging, while the resolution is kept at the diffraction limit. The results lead the way towards the development of compact designs for correlation plenoptic imaging devices based on chaotic light, as well as high-SNR plenoptic imaging devices based on entangled photon illumination, thus contributing to make correlation plenoptic imaging effectively competitive with commercial plenoptic devices.

## 贝尔非局部性的测量依赖成本：因果与逆因果模型

**Original Title:** Measurement-dependence cost for Bell nonlocality: causal vs retrocausal models

**Authors:** Michael J. W. Hall, Cyril Branciard

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2007.11903v1

**PDF:** None

**Submitted:** 2020-07-23


### 摘要

基于Bell非局域性的设备无关协议，如量子密钥分配和随机性生成，必须确保没有对手可以事先知道测量结果。这需要一个计量独立性假设：计量的选择与影响计量结果的任何其他基本变量无关。相反，放松测量的独立性允许一个完全的“因果”模拟贝尔非局域性。我们构建了最有效的这样的模拟，作为测量的基础变量和测量设置之间的互信息，为Clauser-Horne-Shimony-Holt（CHSH）的情况下，并发现，最大的量子违反需要一个互信息只有$\sim 0.080$位。任何为实现此模拟而构建的物理设备都允许对手完全了解基于违反CHSH不等式的设备独立协议生成的密钥或“随机”数。我们还表明，CHSH场景的先前模型仅需要$\sim 0.046$比特来模拟最大量子破坏，对应于最有效的“回溯”模拟，其中未来的测量设置必然会影响早期的源变量。这可以被视为先前模型的非物质限制，也可以被视为基于其更高效率的回溯因果关系的论点。还讨论了最大纠缠两量子位状态的因果模型和逆因果模型，以及超确定性、单边和锯齿形因果模型。


### Abstract

Device independent protocols based on Bell nonlocality, such as quantum key distribution and randomness generation, must ensure no adversary can have prior knowledge of the measurement outcomes. This requires a measurement independence assumption: that the choice of measurement is uncorrelated with any other underlying variables that influence the measurement outcomes. Conversely, relaxing measurement independence allows for a fully `causal' simulation of Bell nonlocality. We construct the most efficient such simulation, as measured by the mutual information between the underlying variables and the measurement settings, for the Clauser-Horne-Shimony-Holt (CHSH) scenario, and find that the maximal quantum violation requires a mutual information of just $\sim 0.080$ bits. Any physical device built to implement this simulation allows an adversary to have full knowledge of a cryptographic key or `random' numbers generated by a device independent protocol based on violation of the CHSH inequality. We also show that a previous model for the CHSH scenario, requiring only $\sim 0.046$ bits to simulate the maximal quantum violation, corresponds to the most efficient `retrocausal' simulation, in which future measurement settings necessarily influence earlier source variables. This may be viewed either as an unphysical limitation of the prior model, or as an argument for retrocausality on the grounds of its greater efficiency. Causal and retrocausal models are also discussed for maximally entangled two-qubit states, as well as superdeterministic, one-sided and zigzag causal models.

## 通过Duffing非线性和腔内压缩光在高度未解析的边带区域中对光机系统进行大而鲁棒的机械压缩

**Original Title:** Large and robust mechanical squeezing of optomechanical systems in a highly unresolved sideband regime via Duffing nonlinearity and intracavity squeezed light

**Authors:** Jian-Song Zhang, Ai-Xi Chen

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2007.11833v2

**PDF:** None

**Submitted:** 2020-07-23


### 摘要

我们提出了一种方案，借助Duffing非线性和腔内压缩光，在高度未分辨边带（HURSB）区域中的光机系统中产生强大且鲁棒的机械压缩。该系统由具有Duffing非线性（机械非线性）和二阶非线性媒介（光学非线性）的标准光机系统组成。在分解边带区域中，二阶非线性媒介可能在机械压缩的产生中发挥破坏性作用。然而，它可以显着增加HURSB方案中的机械压缩（大于3分贝）。最后，我们表明机械压缩对机械共振器的热波动具有鲁棒性。HURSB区域中大而稳健的机械压缩的产生是机械非线性和光学非线性的综合效应。


### Abstract

We propose a scheme to generate strong and robust mechanical squeezing in an optomechanical system in the highly unresolved sideband (HURSB) regime with the help of the Duffing nonlinearity and intracavity squeezed light. The system is formed by a standard optomechanical system with the Duffing nonlinearity (mechanical nonlinearity) and a second-order nonlinear medium (optical nonlinearity). In the resolved sideband regime, the second-order nonlinear medium may play a destructive role in the generation of mechanical squeezing. However, it can significantly increase the mechanical squeezing (larger than 3dB) in the HURSB regime. Finally, we show the mechanical squeezing is robust against thermal fluctuations of the mechanical resonator. The generation of large and robust mechanical squeezing in the HURSB regime is a combined effect of the mechanical and optical nonlinearities.

## 重温旋转谐振子

**Original Title:** The rotating harmonic oscillator revisited

**Authors:** Francisco M. Fernández

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2007.11695v2

**PDF:** None

**Submitted:** 2020-07-22


### 摘要

我们利用弗罗贝尼乌斯方法分析了量子机械旋转谐振子的本征值分布。合适的类比会导致展开系数的三项回归关系。该系列的截断会产生一些特定的特征值和精确分析形式的特征函数。前者可以以获得有关模型整个范围的适当信息的方式组织。


### Abstract

We analyze the distribution of the eigenvalues of the quantum-mechanical rotating harmonic oscillator by means of the Frobenius method. A suitable ansatz leads to a three-term recurrence relation for the expansion coefficients. Truncation of the series yields some particular eigenvalues and eigenfunctions in exact analytical form. The former can be organized in such a way that one obtains suitable information about the whole spectrum of the model.

## 使用量子计算的程序生成

**Original Title:** Procedural generation using quantum computation

**Authors:** James R. Wootton

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2007.11510v1

**PDF:** None

**Submitted:** 2020-07-22


### 摘要

量子计算是一项新兴技术，有望成为许多领域的强大工具。尽管在显着的量子优势被证明之前可能还需要几年时间，但该技术的发展带来了一系列宝贵的资源。其中包括公开可用的原型量子硬件、小型量子程序的高级模拟器以及测试和开发量子软件的编程框架。在这篇挑衅性论文中，我们试图证明这些资源足以在程序生成领域提供第一个有用的结果。这是通过引入原理证明方法来实现的：模糊过程的量子概括，其中使用量子干涉来提供独特的效果。通过这一点，我们希望表明，在该技术对程序生成有用之前，不需要进一步开发该技术。相反，现在就可以开始对这项新技术进行富有成效的实验。


### Abstract

Quantum computation is an emerging technology that promises to be a powerful tool in many areas. Though some years likely still remain until significant quantum advantage is demonstrated, the development of the technology has led to a range of valuable resources. These include publicly available prototype quantum hardware, advanced simulators for small quantum programs and programming frameworks to test and develop quantum software. In this provocation paper we seek to demonstrate that these resources are sufficient to provide the first useful results in the field of procedural generation. This is done by introducing a proof-of-principle method: a quantum generalization of a blurring process, in which quantum interference is used to provide a unique effect. Through this we hope to show that further developments in the technology are not required before it becomes useful for procedural generation. Rather, fruitful experimentation with this new technology can begin now.

## 单个俘获中性原子的亚ms、非破坏性、时间分辨量子状态读出

**Original Title:** Sub-ms, nondestructive, time-resolved quantum-state readout of a single, trapped neutral atom

**Authors:** Margaret E. Shea, Paul M. Baker, James A. Joseph, Jungsang Kim, Daniel J. Gauthier

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2007.09422v2

**PDF:** None

**Submitted:** 2020-07-18


### 摘要

我们通过荧光检测光学偶极陷阱中5$S_{1/2}$（$F=2$）态的单个$##{87}$RB原子来实现快速、无损的量子状态读出。原子由线极化读出激光束驱动，使该方案对磁次级中原子布居分布不敏感。我们证明，在160美元20美元的读出时间内，读出保真度为97.6/pm0.2美元，在$>97/ %$的试验中保留了原子，这代表了比其他磁状态不敏感技术的进步。我们证明，通过各种跳变的偶极矩阵元素的分布和光陷阱的AC-Stark位移，$F=2$状态部分地保护其免受光抽运。我们的结果可能会应用于中性原子量子计算和模拟。


### Abstract

We achieve fast, nondestructive quantum-state readout via fluorescence detection of a single $^{87}$Rb atom in the 5$S_{1/2}$ ($F=2$) ground state held in an optical dipole trap. The atom is driven by linearly-polarized readout laser beams, making the scheme insensitive to the distribution of atomic population in the magnetic sub-levels. We demonstrate a readout fidelity of $97.6\pm0.2\%$ in a readout time of $160\pm20$ $μ$s with the atom retained in $>97\%$ of the trials, representing an advancement over other magnetic-state-insensitive techniques. We demonstrate that the $F=2$ state is partially protected from optical pumping by the distribution of the dipole matrix elements for the various transitions and the AC-Stark shifts from the optical trap. Our results are likely to find application in neutral-atom quantum computing and simulation.

## 位切片Hilbert空间：将精确的量子电路模拟提升到新水平

**Original Title:** Bit-Slicing the Hilbert Space: Scaling Up Accurate Quantum Circuit Simulation to a New Level

**Authors:** Yuan-Hung Tsai, Jie-Hong R. Jiang, Chiao-Shan Jhang

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2007.09304v1

**PDF:** None

**Submitted:** 2020-07-18


### 摘要

近年来，量子计算取得了巨大进步，预计将在不久的将来改变计算范式。量子电路模拟在量子硬件和软件系统开发的工具链中发挥着关键作用。然而，由于量子状态的巨大Hilbert空间，尽管已经做出了显着的努力，但用经典计算机模拟量子电路仍然极具挑战性。本文从两个方面增强了量子电路模拟：准确性和可扩展性。前者是通过使用复数的代数表示来实现的;后者是通过对数字表示进行位切片并用符号布尔函数操作取代矩阵-载体相乘来实现的。实验结果表明，我们的方法对于各种量子电路来说优于最新的方法，并且可以模拟具有多达数万个量子位的某些基准系列。


### Abstract

Quantum computing is greatly advanced in recent years and is expected to transform the computation paradigm in the near future. Quantum circuit simulation plays a key role in the toolchain for the development of quantum hardware and software systems. However, due to the enormous Hilbert space of quantum states, simulating quantum circuits with classical computers is extremely challenging despite notable efforts have been made. In this paper, we enhance quantum circuit simulation in two dimensions: accuracy and scalability. The former is achieved by using an algebraic representation of complex numbers; the latter is achieved by bit-slicing the number representation and replacing matrix-vector multiplication with symbolic Boolean function manipulation. Experimental results demonstrate that our method can be superior to the state-of-the-art for various quantum circuits and can simulate certain benchmark families with up to tens of thousands of qubits.

## 量子网络的量子匿名性

**Original Title:** Quantum Anonymity for Quantum Networks

**Authors:** Awais Khan, Junaid ur Rehman, Hyundong Shin

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2007.11176v1

**PDF:** None

**Submitted:** 2020-07-22


### 摘要

我们提出了第一个量子匿名通知（QAN）协议，该协议引入了匿名性，并为量子网络中的匿名安全量子通信铺平了道路。QAN协议的应用范围从多方量子计算到量子互联网。我们利用QAN协议在$n$-节点量子网络中提出了一种匿名量子私人比较协议。该协议可以在剩余的$n-k$方和半诚实的第三方的帮助下比较任何$2 \leq k \leq n$方的私人信息。这些协议具有无痕迹属性，即编码操作无法追溯到其起源。安全分析表明，该协议对外部对手和恶意参与者具有鲁棒性。


### Abstract

We present the first quantum anonymous notification (QAN) protocol that introduces anonymity and paves the way for anonymous secure quantum communication in quantum networks. QAN protocol has applications ranging from multiparty quantum computation to quantum internet. We utilize the QAN protocol to propose an anonymous quantum private comparison protocol in an $n$-node quantum network. This protocol can compare private information of any $2 \leq k \leq n$ parties with the help of the remaining $n-k$ parties and a semi-honest third party. These protocols feature a traceless property, i.e., encoding operations cannot be traced back to their originating sources. Security analysis shows that this protocol is robust against external adversaries and malicious participants.

## 从分子离子中编码的兆赫兹到太赫兹量子位：N$\mathBF{_2^+}$中偶极禁止光谱跳变的理论分析

**Original Title:** From megahertz to terahertz qubits encoded in molecular ions: theoretical analysis of dipole-forbidden spectroscopic transitions in N$\mathbf{_2^+}$

**Authors:** Kaveh Najafian, Ziv Meir, Stefan Willitsch

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2007.11097v1

**PDF:** None

**Submitted:** 2020-07-21


### 摘要

量子技术的最新进展使得能够在量子层面上精确控制单个被困分子。在探索这些新技术的范围时，我们从理论上研究了分子氮离子的旋转、旋转和振动自由度中量子位和时钟跳变的实施，包括磁场的影响。相关的光谱转变在频率上跨越六个数量级，说明了分子光谱用于编码量子信息的多功能性。我们确定了两种具有非常低（“伸展”状态量子位）甚至零（“神奇”磁场量子位）线性塞曼位移的磁不敏感量子位。预计，对于几mG量级的磁场波动幅度，相应的光谱跃变将仅移动几mHz，转化为编码在分子的旋转和振动中的数十分钟塞曼限制的相干时间。我们还发现，基本振动转变的Q（0）线是与分子的第一激发电子状态相互作用所允许的磁偶极。Q（0）转变受益于时钟操作的小系统漂移以及对质子与电子质量比可能变化的高敏感性，迄今为止在单量子光谱中尚未考虑。最后，我们探索了通过核-旋转状态的磁增强混合来连贯控制N$_2^+$的核-旋转配置的可能性。


### Abstract

Recent advances in quantum technologies have enabled the precise control of single trapped molecules on the quantum level. Exploring the scope of these new technologies, we studied theoretically the implementation of qubits and clock transitions in the spin, rotational, and vibrational degrees of freedom of molecular nitrogen ions including the effects of magnetic fields. The relevant spectroscopic transitions span six orders of magnitude in frequency illustrating the versatility of the molecular spectrum for encoding quantum information. We identified two types of magnetically insensitive qubits with very low ("stretched"-state qubits) or even zero ("magic" magnetic-field qubits) linear Zeeman shifts. The corresponding spectroscopic transitions are predicted to shift by as little as a few mHz for an amplitude of magnetic-field fluctuations on the order of a few mG translating into Zeeman-limited coherence times of tens of minutes encoded in the rotations and vibrations of the molecule. We also found that the Q(0) line of the fundamental vibrational transition is magnetic-dipole allowed by interaction with the first excited electronic state of the molecule. The Q(0) transitions, which benefit from small systematic shifts for clock operation and high sensitivity to a possible variation in the proton-to-electron mass ratio, were so far not considered in single-photon spectra. Finally, we explored possibilities to coherently control the nuclear-spin configuration of N$_2^+$ through the magnetically enhanced mixing of nuclear-spin states.

## 通过量子机器学习预测毒性

**Original Title:** Predicting toxicity by quantum machine learning

**Authors:** Teppei Suzuki, Michio Katouda

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2008.07715v3

**PDF:** None

**Submitted:** 2020-08-18


### 摘要

近年来，参数化量子电路被视为混合量子经典方法框架内的机器学习模型。量子机器学习（QML）已应用于二元分类问题和无监督学习。然而，非线性回归任务的实际量子应用受到的关注要少得多。在这里，我们开发了QML模型，旨在根据定量结构活性关系预测221种苯酚的毒性。结果表明，我们通过量子纠缠增强的数据编码比之前的编码提供了更多的表达能力，这意味着量子相关可能有利于经典数据的特征地图表示。我们的QML模型的表现明显优于多元线性回归方法。此外，我们的模拟表明，QML模型与使用放射基函数网络获得的模型相当，同时提高了概括性能。本研究表明，QML可能是化学信息学等非线性回归任务的替代方法。


### Abstract

In recent years, parameterized quantum circuits have been regarded as machine learning models within the framework of the hybrid quantum-classical approach. Quantum machine learning (QML) has been applied to binary classification problems and unsupervised learning. However, practical quantum application to nonlinear regression tasks has received considerably less attention. Here, we develop QML models designed for predicting the toxicity of 221 phenols on the basis of quantitative structure activity relationship. The results suggest that our data encoding enhanced by quantum entanglement provided more expressive power than the previous ones, implying that quantum correlation could be beneficial for the feature map representation of classical data. Our QML models performed significantly better than the multiple linear regression method. Furthermore, our simulations indicate that the QML models were comparable to those obtained using radial basis function networks, while improving the generalization performance. The present study implies that QML could be an alternative approach for nonlinear regression tasks such as cheminformatics.

## 模拟量子搜索进化的几何方面

**Original Title:** Geometric aspects of analog quantum search evolutions

**Authors:** Carlo Cafaro, Shannon Ray, Paul M. Alsing

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2008.07675v2

**PDF:** None

**Submitted:** 2020-08-18


### 摘要

我们使用Anandan和Aharonov最初提出的几何概念来表明，两个垂直量子状态之间的Farhi-Gutmann时间最优模拟量子搜索进化的特征是在投射Hilbert空间上追踪的单位效率动态轨迹。特别是，我们证明了这些最佳动力学轨迹是连接量子进化初始状态和最终状态的最短测地路径。此外，我们还验证了它们描述了由不确定性不等式指定的最小不确定性演变，该不等式比普通的时间-能量不确定性关系更严格。我们还从我们提出的雷曼几何角度研究偏离时间最优性条件的影响。此外，在指出我们的量子搜索几何方法提供的一些物理直观方面之后，我们提到了一些实际相关的物理见解，这些见解可能会从我们的几何分析应用于更现实的时间相关量子搜索进化中产生。最后，我们简要讨论了我们的工作对量子计算任务中相关热轨迹效率的几何分析的可能扩展。


### Abstract

We use geometric concepts originally proposed by Anandan and Aharonov to show that the Farhi-Gutmann time optimal analog quantum search evolution between two orthogonal quantum states is characterized by unit efficiency dynamical trajectories traced on a projective Hilbert space. In particular, we prove that these optimal dynamical trajectories are the shortest geodesic paths joining the initial and the final states of the quantum evolution. In addition, we verify they describe minimum uncertainty evolutions specified by an uncertainty inequality that is tighter than the ordinary time-energy uncertainty relation. We also study the effects of deviations from the time optimality condition from our proposed Riemannian geometric perspective. Furthermore, after pointing out some physically intuitive aspects offered by our geometric approach to quantum searching, we mention some practically relevant physical insights that could emerge from the application of our geometric analysis to more realistic time-dependent quantum search evolutions. Finally, we briefly discuss possible extensions of our work to the geometric analysis of the efficiency of thermal trajectories of relevance in quantum computing tasks.

## 合并元素转换

**Original Title:** Merged-element transmon

**Authors:** R. Zhao, S. Park, T. Zhao, M. Bal, C. R. H. McRae, J. Long, D. P. Pappas

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2008.07652v5

**PDF:** None

## 操作概率理论中对称状态的区分

**Authors:** Kenji Nakahira

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2012.13845v1

**PDF:** None

**Submitted:** 2020-12-27


### 摘要

## 通过选择性超精细调节最大化动态核极化

**Submitted:** 2020-12-23


### 摘要

动态核极化（DNP）是指一类用于通过将高度极化电子的集合的旋转极化转移到目标核分析物来增加核磁共振测量中的信号的技术。然而，这些技术需要应用强磁场来最大化电子的旋转极化，从而限制电子-核（超精细）旋转耦合和转移的路径。在这项工作中，我们表明，对于具有本征零场分裂的电子旋转系统，基于实验室框架交叉松弛的一类单独的更强超精细相互作用可以用于提高DNP效率和产率，同时在中等场操作。我们分析审查了现有的方法，并确定这种方法比现有技术将极化转移到核系综的速率提高了一个数量级。这一结果是在室温下使用金刚石中氮空位（NV）缺陷的光学可极化$S=1$电子旋转系统作为电子旋转极化的来源进行实验证明的。最后，我们评估了这些基于NV的方法对于核磁共振和核磁共振应用中金刚石外部分子旋转宏观量的极化的实用性。

## 具有在线算法缓冲模型的量子问答游戏

**Original Title:** Quantum Request-Answer Game with Buffer Model for Online Algorithms

**Authors:** Kamil Khadiev

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2012.12321v1

**PDF:** None

**Submitted:** 2020-12-22


### 摘要

我们将在线算法视为一个问答游戏。对手生成输入请求，然后在线算法进行回答。我们考虑游戏的通用版本，其缓冲区大小有限。对手将数据加载到缓冲区，并且算法可以随机访问缓冲区的元素。我们考虑该模型的量子和经典（确定性或随机化）算法。   在论文中，我们提供了一个特定的问题（最频繁的关键字问题）和一种量子算法，该算法在竞争比方面比任何经典（确定性或随机）算法更好。同时，对于该问题，标准模型中的经典在线算法等效于带缓冲模型的问答游戏中的经典算法。

We consider quantum and classical (deterministic or randomized) algorithms for the model.
  In the paper, we provide a specific problem (The Most Frequent Keyword Problem) and a quantum algorithm that works better than any classical (deterministic or randomized) algorithm in terms of competitive ratio. At the same time, for the problem, classical online algorithms in the standard model are equivalent to the classical algorithms in the request-answer game with buffer model.
