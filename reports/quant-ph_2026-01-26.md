## 二次玻色子系统非厄米动力学研究进展

**Original Title:** Advances in non-Hermitian dynamics of quadratic bosonic systems

**Authors:** Huawei Zhao, Xinlei Liu, Xinyao Huang, Guofeng Zhang

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.14329v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.14329v2

**Submitted:** 2026-01-20


### 摘要

非埃尔米特物理学已成为一个快速发展的研究领域，揭示了一系列新颖的现象和潜在的应用。传统的非埃尔米特Hamilton通常通过构建不对称耦合或通过引入消散和收益来实现非埃尔米特系统来模拟。具有压缩相互作用的二次玻色子系统（QBS）本质上是埃尔米特式的;然而，其在实空间和动量空间中的动力学演化矩阵都是非埃尔米特式的。基于此，对动态进化矩阵应用场运算符变换XP，产生x和p运算符之间的交反传输。这种非可逆特性可以在信号放大器中利用。另一方面，在动量空间的博戈柳博夫-德根尼斯框架内，可以观察到非埃尔米特式的拓学现象，例如点间隙拓学和非埃尔米特式的集肤效应，两者都是由具有非零缠绕数的光谱引起的。此外，QBS还可以用于实现非埃尔米特阿哈罗诺夫-博姆笼并扩展非布洛赫能带理论。之前对非埃尔米特物理学的研究主要集中在经典系统上。非埃尔米特性质对量子效应的影响仍然是一个有待探索的关键问题，并已演变为非埃尔米特性质与量子物理学界面的一个研究方向。


### Abstract

Non-Hermitian physics has emerged as a rapidly advancing field of research, revealing a range of novel phenomena and potential applications. Traditional non-Hermitian Hamiltonians are typically simulated by constructing asymmetric couplings or by introducing dissipation and gain to realize non-Hermitian systems. The quadratic bosonic system (QBS) with squeezing interaction is intrinsically Hermitian; however, its dynamical evolution matrix in both real and momentum spaces is non-Hermitian. Based on this, applying a field-operator transformation xp to the dynamical evolution matrix yields quadrature nonreciprocal transmission between the x and p operators. This nonreciprocal characteristic can be utilized in signal amplifiers. On the other hand, within the Bogoliubov-de Gennes framework in momentum space, one can observe non-Hermitian topological phenomena such as point-gap topology and the non-Hermitian skin effect, both induced by spectra with nonzero winding numbers. Additionally, QBS can be employed to realize non-Hermitian Aharonov-Bohm cages and to extend non-Bloch band theory. Previous studies in non-Hermitian physics have largely concentrated on classical systems. The influence of non-Hermitian properties on quantum effects remains a key issue awaiting exploration and has evolved into a research direction at the interface of non-Hermitian and quantum physics.

## 受驱动量子线量子位中的限制可调谐合成规范场和Floquet布局现象

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.13859v2

**Submitted:** 2026-01-20


### 摘要

理论分析表明，当由二色场驱动时，抛式量子线中的旋转量子位表现出限制可调的合成规范场，从而导致新颖的Floquet topology现象。提出了量子位状态免受时间周期扰动的拓扑保护机制。该分析揭示了一种限制引起的拓-齐纳转变，其特征是在Landau-Zener-St$\ddot{u}$ckelberg-Majorana干涉测量中从保留的对称性转变为手征干涉图案。确定了弯曲约束和相参数空间中循环演变下非阿贝尔几何相的出现，从而实现了完整量子计算。此外，准能量和共振转移概率谱中非常规Floquet-Bloch振荡作为双调和相的函数的预测表明了奇异的性质，例如分数形谱和分数Floquet隧道效应。这些现象提供了合成维度中一致传输的直接证据。提出了在半导体杂合结构中实现这些效应的具体实验路径，并将该框架扩展到多量子位纠缠的产生，并对其固有的去相干弹性进行了定量分析。总的来说，这些发现将量子线材料定位为Floquet工程、拓扑量子控制和耐故障量子信息处理的多功能且可扩展的平台。


### Abstract

Theoretical analysis demonstrates that a spin qubit in a parabolic quantum wire, when driven by a bichromatic field, exhibits a confinement-tunable synthetic gauge field leading to novel Floquet topological phenomena. The underlying mechanism for topological protection of qubit states against time-periodic perturbations is presented. The analysis reveals a confinement-induced topological Landau-Zener transition, characterized by a shift from preserved symmetries to chiral interference patterns in Landau-Zener-St$\ddot{u}$ckelberg-Majorana interferometry. The emergence of non-Abelian geometric phases under cyclic evolution in curved confinement and phase-parameter space is identified, enabling holonomic quantum computation. Furthermore, the prediction of unconventional Floquet-Bloch oscillations in the quasi-energy and resonance transition probability spectra as a function of the biharmonic phase indicates exotic properties, such as fractal spectra and fractional Floquet tunnelling. These phenomena provide direct evidence of coherent transport in the synthetic dimension. Concrete experimental pathways for realizing these effects in semiconductor heterostructures are proposed, and the framework is extended to multi-qubit entanglement generation with a quantitative analysis of its inherent resilience to decoherence. Collectively, these findings position quantum wire materials as a versatile and scalable platform for Floquet engineering, topological quantum control, and fault-tolerant quantum information processing.

## 无限远并返回--光物质系统的1美元/N美元图形扩展

**Original Title:** To infinity and back -- $1/N$ graph expansions of light-matter systems

**Authors:** Andreas Schellenberger, Kai P. Schmidt

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.13860v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.13860v2

**Submitted:** 2026-01-20


### 摘要

我们提出了一种利用连锁群理论对轻物质系统进行完整图扩展的方法。这种方法使我们能够探索对粒子数量的热力学极限$N\到\infty$的1/N$修正，使我们能够进入介观状态。虽然由于用既定方法研究它的挑战，这个机制在很大程度上尚未被探索，但它包含了有趣的特征，例如光与物质之间的纠缠，在热力学极限下消失。作为代表性应用，我们通过结合精确对角化（NLCE）和微扰理论（pcst++）进行图形展开，计算顺磁正相范式Dicke-Ising链的低能量状态的物理量，并与其他技术进行基准测试我们的方法。我们研究了激发态能量密度和量子密度，显示了从微观到宏观的平稳转变，直至热力学极限。在量子临界点附近，我们提取对底价能量密度的1/N$修正，以使用外推技术获得临界点和临界指数。


### Abstract

We present a method for performing a full graph expansion for light-matter systems, utilizing the linked-cluster theorem. This method enables us to explore $1/N$ corrections to the thermodynamic limit $N\to \infty$ in the number of particles, giving us access to the mesoscopic regime. While this regime is yet largely unexplored due to the challenges of studying it with established approaches, it incorporates intriguing features, such as entanglement between light and matter that vanishes in the thermodynamic limit. As a representative application, we calculate physical quantities of the low-energy regime for the paradigmatic Dicke-Ising chain in the paramagnetic normal phase by accompanying the graph expansion with both exact diagonalization (NLCE) and perturbation theory (pcst++), benchmarking our approach against other techniques. We investigate the ground-state energy density and photon density, showing a smooth transition from the microscopic to the macroscopic regime up to the thermodynamic limit. Around the quantum critical point, we extract the $1/N$ corrections to the ground-state energy density to obtain the critical point and critical exponent using extrapolation techniques.

## LiDMaS：GKP量子位中容差魔态注入的架构级建模

**Original Title:** LiDMaS: Architecture-Level Modeling of Fault-Tolerant Magic-State Injection in GKP Photonic Qubits

**Authors:** Dennis Delali Kwesi Wayo

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.16244v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.16244v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-22


### 摘要

光体系结构中的容错量子计算依赖于在有限压缩和量子损失施加的现实约束下高效地准备高保真逻辑幻境。在这项工作中，我们使用重复直至成功注入协议结合外表面代码保护，研究了GKP编码的量子比特中的逻辑T门魔状态准备。我们基于用标准数值线性代数实现的轻量级密度矩阵模拟器开发了一个架构级建模框架。有限压缩被映射到有效的逻辑去相，去极化噪音被包含在逻辑层面，而量子损失被视为预示的擦除过程。这种方法避免了显式连续变量波函数模拟，硬件特定的光子模型和量子软件框架，从而实现了对架构权衡的透明和计算效率的探索。我们进行系统的参数扫描压缩值从8到16分贝，基线损失概率在0.01和0.03之间，和表面代码的距离d = 1，3，5和7。在这个制度中，我们评估重复，直到成功的概率，平均注入开销，和逻辑魔术状态保真度。我们发现，成功概率超过0.94在所有研究的参数，平均开销接近统一。经过外码保护后，逻辑一致性约达到0.77至0.80，并且对中等的量子损失表现出弱的敏感性，但对压缩的依赖性强。相界分析确定了同时实现高成功概率和逻辑保真度所需的最小挤压要求。这些结果为可扩展的光量子容差架构提供了定量设计指导。


### Abstract

Fault-tolerant quantum computation in photonic architectures relies on the efficient preparation of high-fidelity logical magic states under realistic constraints imposed by finite squeezing and photon loss. In this work, we study logical T-gate magic-state preparation in GKP-encoded photonic qubits using a repeat-until-success injection protocol combined with outer surface-code protection. We develop an architecture-level modeling framework based on a lightweight density-matrix simulator implemented with standard numerical linear algebra. Finite squeezing is mapped to effective logical dephasing, depolarizing noise is included at the logical level, and photon loss is treated as a heralded erasure process. This approach avoids explicit continuous-variable wavefunction simulation, hardware-specific photonic models, and quantum software frameworks, enabling transparent and computationally efficient exploration of architectural trade-offs. We perform systematic parameter sweeps over squeezing values from 8 to 16 dB, baseline loss probabilities between 0.01 and 0.03, and surface-code distances d = 1, 3, 5, and 7. Across this regime, we evaluate repeat-until-success probability, average injection overhead, and logical magic-state fidelity. We find that success probabilities exceed 0.94 across all studied parameters, with an average overhead close to unity. After outer-code protection, logical fidelities reach approximately 0.77 to 0.80 and show weak sensitivity to moderate photon loss but a strong dependence on squeezing. Phase-boundary analysis identifies minimum squeezing requirements needed to simultaneously achieve high success probability and logical fidelity. These results provide quantitative design guidance for scalable photonic fault-tolerant quantum architectures.

## 通过反馈量子控制的实验性素因式分解

**Original Title:** Experimental prime factorization via the feedback quantum control

**Authors:** K. B. Hari Krishnan, Vishal Varma, T. S. Mahesh

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.16116v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.16116v2

**Submitted:** 2026-01-22


### 摘要

量子处理器上的素因式分解通常通过基于电路的方法（例如Shor算法）或通过基于隔热、模拟或变分技术的汉密尔顿优化方法来实现。虽然Shor的算法需要高保真度量子门，但Hamilton优化方案（将素因子编码为问题Hamilton的简并底态）通常需要大量的经典后处理来确定控制参数。我们提出了一种全量子、基于测量的反馈方法，该方法迭代地将量子系统引导到目标接地状态，一旦确定并实现问题Hamilton，就无需对驱动参数进行经典计算。作为原理证明，我们使用三量子位核磁共振量子寄存器对双质551进行了实验性分解，并数值分析了该方法对控制场误差的鲁棒性。我们通过分别使用5个和9个量子位对更大的双素（9，167和2，106，287）进行数字化实现RST QON因式分解，进一步证明了可扩展性。


### Abstract

Prime factorization on quantum processors is typically implemented either via circuit-based approaches such as Shor's algorithm or through Hamiltonian optimization methods based on adiabatic, annealing, or variational techniques. While Shor's algorithm demands high-fidelity quantum gates, Hamiltonian optimization schemes, with prime factors encoded as degenerate ground states of a problem Hamiltonian, generally require substantial classical post-processing to determine control parameters. We propose an all-quantum, measurement-based feedback approach that iteratively steers a quantum system toward the target ground state, eliminating the need for classical computation of drive parameters once the problem Hamiltonian is determined and realized. As a proof of principle, we experimentally factor the biprime 551 using a three-qubit NMR quantum register and numerically analyze the robustness of the method against control field-errors. We further demonstrate scalability by numerically implementing the FALQON factorization of larger biprimes, 9,167 and 2,106,287, using 5 and 9 qubits, respectively.

随着量子设备的尺寸越来越大，相应地扩展其一致控制的重大挑战出现了。量子元胞自动机（QCA）构成了一个有前途的框架，可以绕过这个控制问题：仅使用静态量子位阵列和全局控制操作就可以实现普适动力学。我们在镭和铯原子的双物种里德堡阵列上实现了QA，利用对每个物种的独立全球控制来执行无数的量子协议。通过简单的脉冲序列，我们探索多体动力学并生成各种纠缠态，包括GZZ状态、96.7（1.7）%保真度贝尔状态、17量子位团簇状态和高连通性图状态。QCA的多功能性和可扩展性为通过全局控制扩展量子信息系统提供了令人信服的途径，并为量子多体动力学提供了新的视角。

## 稳定器状态中的多不变量

**Original Title:** Multi-invariants in stabilizer states

**Authors:** Sriram Akella, Abhijit Gadde, Jay Pandey

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.16258v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.16258v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-22


### 摘要

多方纠缠是双方纠缠的自然概括，但人们的理解相对较少。本文中，我们开发了计算稳定器状态的一类多方纠缠度量的工具--称为多重不变量。我们给出了一种有效的数值算法，可以计算稳定器状态的多重不变量。对于三方稳定器状态，我们还使用GZZ提取定理获得了任何多重不变量的显式公式。然后，我们提出了一个计数参数，用于计算q部稳定器状态的任何Coxeter多重不变量。我们推测同样的封闭形式表达。我们发现了多元不变量、稳定器状态和布局之间有趣联系的线索。我们展示了如何针对一类有限的稳定器状态进一步简化我们的公式，这些稳定器状态表现为复曲面代码和X立方体模型等有趣模型的基状态。


### Abstract

Multipartite entanglement is a natural generalization of bipartite entanglement, but is relatively poorly understood. In this paper, we develop tools to calculate a class of multipartite entanglement measures - known as multi-invariants - for stabilizer states. We give an efficient numerical algorithm that computes multi-invariants for stabilizer states. For tripartite stabilizer states, we also obtain an explicit formula for any multi-invariant using the GHZ-extraction theorem. We then present a counting argument that calculates any Coxeter multi-invariant of a q-partite stabilizer state. We conjecture a closed form expression for the same. We uncover hints of an interesting connection between multi-invariants, stabilizer states and topology. We show how our formulas are further simplified for a restricted class of stabilizer states that appear as ground states of interesting models like the toric code and the X-cube model.

## 模拟金属表面非隔热动力学的量子算法

**Original Title:** Quantum algorithm for simulating non-adiabatic dynamics at metallic surfaces

**Authors:** Robert A. Lang, Paarth Jain, Juan Miguel Arrazola, Danial Motlagh

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.16264v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.16264v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-22


### 摘要

分子-金属界面的非隔热动力学支配着各种具有技术重要性的现象，从非均相催化到染料增敏太阳能转化和分子结上的电荷传输。这种动力学的现实建模需要考虑各种电荷和能量转移通道，涉及核运动与大量电子状态的耦合，这导致使用经典计算方法的成本过高。在这项工作中，我们引入了安德森-牛顿汉密尔顿量的推广，并开发了一种高度优化的量子算法来模拟现实分子-金属界面的非隔热动力学。使用PennyLane软件平台，我们对算法进行资源估计，表明其对于代表各种科学和工业相关分子金属系统的模型系统的实施成本非常低。具体来说，我们发现，包括100美元的金属轨道、8美元的分子轨道和20美元的核自由度在内的模型的时间进化仅需要271美元的量子位和7.9美元\x 107美元的Toffoli门即可实现1000美元的Trotter步骤，这表明非隔热分子-金属动力学是第一代容错量子计算机的富有成效的应用。


### Abstract

Non-adiabatic dynamics at molecule-metal interfaces govern diverse and technologically important phenomena, from heterogeneous catalysis to dye-sensitized solar energy conversion and charge transport across molecular junctions. In this work we introduce a generalization of the Anderson-Newns Hamiltonian and develop a highly optimized quantum algorithm for simulating the non-adiabatic dynamics of realistic molecule-metal interfaces. Using the PennyLane software platform, we perform resource estimations of our algorithm, showing its remarkably low implementation cost for model systems representative of various scientifically and industrially relevant molecule-metal systems. Specifically, we find that time evolution for models including $100$ metal orbitals, $8$ molecular orbitals, and $20$ nuclear degrees of freedom, requires only $271$ qubits and $7.9 \times 10^7$ Toffoli gates for $1000$ Trotter steps, suggesting non-adiabatic molecule-metal dynamics as a fruitful application of first-generation fault-tolerant quantum computers.
