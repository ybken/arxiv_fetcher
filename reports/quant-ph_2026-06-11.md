## $e^+e^-$碰撞中$Z$-玻色子共振的强场控制

**Original Title:** Strong-field control of the $Z$-boson resonance in $e^+e^-$ collisions

**Authors:** Fengye Chen, Qingzheng Lv, Libin Fu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.09394v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.09394v2

**Submitted:** 2026-06-08


### 摘要

共振$Z$-玻色子的产生是精确电弱物理学的基石，其真空线形状由$Z$质量、宽度和碰撞运动学决定。我们表明，强激光场可以显着改变这一图像。通过非微扰处理场，我们发现对进入的费米子的激光修整改变了有效的碰撞运动学，并在$e^{+}e^{-}$碰撞中打开了激光-量子交换通道，包括多量子过程。结果，$Z$-共振轮廓发展出不同的强度依赖机制，从真空极限演变为中等强度下的饱和，并演变为较高强度下的大约二次增强。此外，所产生的$Z$玻色子的偏振成分被重新分配。特别是，在高强度的激光诱导的贡献，可以补偿固有的手征不对称性的电弱相互作用，导致近宇称平衡的$Z$-玻色子生产。我们的结果表明，强经典场可以动态地控制电弱共振现象，打开了强场QED和高能对撞机物理之间的桥梁。


### Abstract

Resonant $Z$-boson production is a cornerstone of precision electroweak physics, with its vacuum line shape set by the $Z$ mass, width, and collision kinematics. We show that a strong laser field can significantly alter this picture. By treating the field nonperturbatively, we find that laser dressing of the incoming fermions alters the effective collision kinematics and opens laser-photon exchange channels, including multiphoton processes, in $e^{+}e^{-}$ collisions. As a result, the $Z$-resonance profile develops distinct intensity-dependent regimes, evolving from the vacuum limit to saturation at intermediate field strengths and to an approximately quadratic enhancement at higher intensities. Additionally, the polarization composition of the produced $Z$ bosons is redistributed. In particular, at high intensities the laser-induced contribution can compensate the intrinsic chiral asymmetry of the electroweak interaction, leading to nearly parity-balanced $Z$-boson production. Our results identify that strong classical fields can dynamically control electroweak resonance phenomena, opening a bridge between strong-field QED and high-energy collider physics.

## JGRA：NISQ噪音感知量子神经网络中的Jacobian几何鲁棒性评估

**Original Title:** JGRA: Jacobian Geometry Robustness Assessment in NISQ Noise-Aware Quantum Neural Networks

**Authors:** Gianluca Scanu, Luca Barletta, Stefano Rini

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.09964v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.09964v2

**Submitted:** 2026-06-08


### 摘要

NISQ时代对量子计算提出了严格的限制，其中噪音和去相干从根本上限制了性能。在经典深度学习中，模型稳健性和对扰动的弹性得到了充分的研究：尽管存在修剪、噪音注入和结构扰动，深度神经网络（DNN）仍能保持高性能，因为其表示中固有的冗余。量子机器学习的一个核心挑战是将鲁棒性概念转移到现实NISQ噪音下的量子神经网络（QNN）。虽然经典深度学习通过结构冗余表现出鲁棒性，但QNN的类似原理仍然不发达。我们提议JGRA：一个用于通过雅可比几何评估噪音感知QNN的鲁棒性的框架，捕捉模型对噪音引起的参数扰动的敏感性。我们的方法包括熵匹配的噪音校准、噪音感知训练和噪音条件雅可比提取，产生将干净状态结构与噪音推断行为联系起来的几何描述符。我们还通过经验证明，这些描述符编码了有关不可见噪音下鲁棒性的预测信息。


### Abstract

The NISQ era places stringent constraints on quantum computation, where noise and decoherence fundamentally limit performance. In classical deep learning, model robustness and resilience to perturbations are well studied: deep neural networks (DNNs) maintain high performance despite pruning, noise injection, and structural perturbations due to inherent redundancy in their representations. A central challenge in quantum machine learning is to transfer this notion of robustness to quantum neural networks (QNNs) under realistic NISQ noise. While classical deep learning exhibits robustness through structural redundancy, analogous principles for QNNs remain underdeveloped. We propose JGRA: a framework for assessing robustness in noise-aware QNNs via Jacobian geometry, capturing model sensitivity to parameter perturbations induced by noise. Our method includes entropy-matched noise calibration, noise-aware training, and noise-conditioned Jacobian extraction, yielding geometric descriptors that link clean-regime structure to noisy inference behaviour. We also empirically demonstrate that these descriptors encode predictive information about robustness under unseen noise.

## 迈向量子分类器的实现

**Original Title:** Towards the implementation of a quantum classifier

**Authors:** Lorenzo Confalonieri

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.10150v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.10150v2

**Submitted:** 2026-06-08


### 摘要

在这项工作中，我们研究了量子电路作为量子机器学习背景下的二进制分类模型的使用。我们称这个模型为二元量子分类器。   首先，我们描述量子计算的基本概念并介绍所使用的计算工具：Qibo，一个用于高效量子模拟和量子硬件控制的开源框架。然后，我们描述了如何通过展示如何在电路中输入数据、定义具有可训练参数和损失函数的量子电路模型模拟器以及实现多个最小化器来设计用于图像和小变量阵列的分类的二元量子分类器。我们使用两个数据集测试我们的量子分类器。第一个是MNIST数据集，它由手写数字组成（简化为仅手写零和手写一用于二进制分类）。我们通过增加模拟器的层次数量来研究不同最小化器的行为。第二个数据集代表可能在LHC（CERN）等对撞机上发生的两种不同的高能碰撞。由于被称为堆积的质子-质子相互作用，我们区分了两个不同的数据集：“无堆积”和“有堆积”。这些碰撞可以用大小为32 x32的图像或我们称之为特征的六个高级变量来表示。通过增加训练数据集的大小和模拟器的层数，我们搜索最佳最小化器。将数据集拆分为训练集和测试集，我们计算：ROC曲线、UC评分、混淆矩阵和测试集准确性。对于“有堆积”的图像，我们将量子分类器获得的结果与小型卷积神经网络进行比较。   我们得出的结论是，用量子电路构建二元量子分类器是可能的，并强调了与经典技术相比其性能和局限性。


### Abstract

In this work, we investigate the use of a quantum circuit as a binary classification model in the context of quantum machine learning. We call this model, binary quantum classifier.
  First, we describe fundamental concepts of quantum computing and introduce the computational tool used: Qibo, an open-source framework for efficient quantum simulations and quantum hardware control. Then, we describe how to design a binary quantum classifier for the classification of images and small arrays of variables by showing how to input data in the circuit, defining a quantum circuit model Ansatz with trainable parameters and a loss function, and implementing multiple minimizers. We test our quantum classifier with two data sets. The first one is the MNIST data set which is composed of handwritten digits (reduced to only handwritten zeros and handwritten ones for binary classification). We study the behavior of different minimizers by increasing the number of layers of the Ansatz. The second data set represents two different high energy collisions that can occur at colliders such as LHC (CERN). Due to in-time proton-proton interactions known as pile-up, we distinguish two different data sets: "without pile-up" and "with pile-up". These collisions can be represented by images of size 32x32 or by six high-level variables that we call features. By increasing the size of the training data set and the number of layers of the Ansatz, we search for the best minimizer. Splitting the data set in training set and test set, we compute: ROC curve, AUC score, confusion matrices and test set accuracy. For "with pile-up" images, we compare the results obtained with the quantum classifier with a small convolutional neural network.
  We conclude that is possible to build a binary quantum classifier with a quantum circuit and we highlight its performances and limitations in comparison with classical technologies.

## CDW--STP过渡中拓序的精确动力学

**Original Title:** Exact Dynamics of Topological Order Across a CDW--SPT Transition

**Authors:** Pradip Kattel, Yicheng Tang, Natan Andrei

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11303v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.11303v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-09


### 摘要

我们研究了一维相互作用系统从电荷密度波（CDW）相到溶剂保护的topology（SPP）相转变的非平衡动力学。从CDW初始状态开始，我们研究了突然熄灭和缓慢上升到SPL状态。虽然CDW秩序在两种协议下都融化了，但拓扑秩序的命运却截然不同。突然猝灭后，不会出现远程SPL有序，因为后猝灭状态包含在拓扑基状态之上有限密度的激发。相比之下，缓慢的斜坡允许系统跟随瞬时的接地状态远离临界区域，从而能够在受Kibble-Zurek缺陷产生控制的偏差的情况下建立STP秩序。该动力学可以通过到二次费米子Hamilton的么正映射来求解，从而使我们能够计算洛施密特回声、相关函数和弦相关器。洛施切尔率函数表现出信号动态量子相转变的尖点，而相关动力学则揭示了控制转变中的猝灭和斜坡的对比机制。这些结果表明，进入拓扑区域并不足以出现拓扑秩序;决定性因素是进化过程中激发产生的抑制。


### Abstract

We investigate the nonequilibrium dynamics of a one-dimensional interacting system across a transition from a charge-density-wave (CDW) phase to a symmetry-protected topological (SPT) phase. Starting from a CDW initial state, we study both sudden quenches and slow ramps into the SPT regime. While the CDW order melts under both protocols, the fate of topological order is sharply different. Following a sudden quench, long-range SPT order does not emerge because the post-quench state contains a finite density of excitations above the topological ground state. In contrast, slow ramps allow the system to follow the instantaneous ground state away from the critical region, enabling the buildup of SPT order with deviations governed by Kibble-Zurek defect production. The dynamics is solvable via a unitary mapping to a quadratic fermionic Hamiltonian, allowing us to compute the Loschmidt echo, correlation functions, and string correlator. The Loschmidt rate function exhibits cusps signaling dynamical quantum phase transitions, while the correlation dynamics reveal the contrasting mechanisms governing quenches and ramps across the transition. These results demonstrate that entering the topological regime is not sufficient for the emergence of topological order; the decisive factor is the suppression of excitation production during the evolution.

## 用VAPOR在量子计算机上实现哈密顿重整化群流

### 摘要

虽然哈密尔顿格点规范理论越来越受欢迎，但今天有限的数值能力使模拟受到离散化误差的影响。这促使重正化群（RG）技术的实施，以找到离散化错误的运营商。为此，我们引入VAPOR，变分量子算法，分解成泡利字符串的运营商，确定RG流轨道，并确定一个天真的离散运营商的不动点。我们说明了这一点，使用一个玩具模型的运动算子在SU（2）杨米尔斯理论的限制。


### Abstract

While Hamiltonian Lattice Gauge Theory is gaining traction, today's limited numerical capacity leaves simulations affected by discretization errors. To this end, we introduce VAPOR, a variational quantum algorithm that decomposes operators into Pauli strings, identifies RG flow orbits, and determines fixed points of a naively discretized operator. We illustrate this using a toy model of a kinematic operator in a symmetry-restricted SU(2) Yang-Mills theory.

## 最近邻双单位Floquet系统之外的精确纠缠动力学

**Original Title:** Exact Entanglement Dynamics Beyond Nearest-Neighbor Dual-Unitary Floquet Systems

**Authors:** Tanay Pathak

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11311v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.11311v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-09


### 摘要

使用二元正性的精确结果在很大程度上依赖于最近邻结构，而有限范围相互作用通常会导致复杂化。超越通常的近邻设置，我们引入了一个分析上易于处理的有限范围踢伊辛模型家族，该模型承认精确的封闭形式纠缠动力学。该结构基于交错结构，其中二元单位性存在于子格上，然后相互耦合。核心观察是，这些亚格子间的耦合不会阻碍所得模型的二元统一性。对于$r= 2$的最小相互作用范围，我们推导出所有$n-$Rényi纠缠量在所有时间的精确表达，并表明结果是两个耦合子格贡献的和。我们的框架自然地扩展到更大的有限相互作用范围和具有异类局部Hilbert空间的系统，无需额外的假设。因此，它为研究严格最近邻双正模型之外的精确纠缠增长提供了受控设置。


### Abstract

Exact results using dual-unitarity largely rely on nearest-neighbor structures, while finite-range interactions typically lead to complications. Going beyond the usual nearest-neighbor setting, we introduce an analytically tractable family of finite-range kicked Ising models that admit exact closed-form entanglement dynamics. The construction is based on a staggered structure in which dual-unitarity is present on sublattices that are then coupled to each other. The central observation is that these inter-sublattice couplings do not obstruct the dual-unitarity of the resulting model. For the minimal interaction range of $r= 2$, we derive exact expressions for all the $n-$Rényi entanglement entropies at all times and show that the result is the sum of the two coupled sublattice contributions. Our framework extends naturally to larger finite interaction ranges and to systems with heterogeneous local Hilbert spaces, without additional assumptions. It thus provides a controlled setting for studying exact entanglement growth beyond strictly nearest-neighbor dual-unitary models.

## 左右本征状态区分的异常点的可观察特征

**Original Title:** Observable signatures of exceptional points from left-right eigenstate distinction

**Authors:** Leela Ganesh Chandra Lakkaraju, Soumik Bandyopadhyay, Sudipto Singha Roy, Philipp Hauke

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11333v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.11333v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-09


### 摘要

与厄米特量子系统相比，非厄米特量子系统表现出本质上不同的物理行为，一个典型的例子是被称为异常点的光谱奇异性。它们的相关性例如量子传感、单向传输和稳健的激光发射使得能够通过多体系统的可观察特征识别异常点变得重要。在这里，以实现旋转时间RT和宇称时间PT对称机制的一维复XY旋转链为例，我们开发了一个基于汉密尔顿量左右本征量之间的区别来检测异常点的框架，这些本征量在非埃尔米特系统中不再是彼此的伴随。我们首先表明，根据汉密尔顿量及其伴随点之间的差异构建的全局度量通过独特的非分析行为来定位异常点。在可观测水平上，在左右本征态上评估的局部旋转相关性的差异提供了可靠的静态检测方案。相比之下，静态双方纠缠测量未能捕捉到这种区别，促使我们研究该模型的量子动力学。在突然的抑制之后，我们证明了时间平均的左右纠缠量差直接编码了例外点的签名。在RT对称区域中，它在异常点表现出明显的峰值，而在PT对称区域中，它表现为类序参数的量，在一个相中保持有限并在转变时消失。我们的结果在非厄米特本征态的结构与异常点的可观察签名之间建立了直接联系，为在现有量子模拟器中识别它们提供了一种实用的途径。


### Abstract

Non-Hermitian quantum systems exhibit qualitatively distinct physical behavior compared to Hermitian systems, a prime example being spectral singularities known as exceptional points. Their relevance in, e.g., quantum sensing, unidirectional transport, and robust lasing makes it important to be able to identify exceptional points through observable features of a many-body system. Here, using as an example a one-dimensional complex XY spin chain realizing both rotation-time RT- and parity-time PT-symmetric regimes, we develop a framework for detecting exceptional points based on the distinction between left and right eigenvectors of the Hamiltonian, which in a non-Hermitian system are no longer the adjoint of each other. We first show that a global measure constructed from the difference between the Hamiltonian and its adjoint locates exceptional points via distinct non-analytic behavior. At the level of observables, differences in local spin correlations evaluated on the right and left eigenstates provide a reliable static detection scheme. In contrast, static bipartite entanglement measures fail to capture this distinction, urging us to study the quantum dynamics of the model. Following a sudden quench, we demonstrate that the time-averaged right-left entanglement entropy difference directly encodes signatures of the exceptional point. In the RT-symmetric regime, it exhibits a pronounced peak at the exceptional point, whereas in the PT-symmetric regime it behaves as an order-parameter-like quantity, remaining finite in one phase and vanishing at the transition. Our results establish a direct link between the structure of non-Hermitian eigenstates and observable signatures of exceptional points, providing a practical route to identify them in existing quantum simulators.

## Q-DICE：量子分布式互连调度器和模拟器

**Original Title:** Q-DICE: Quantum Distributed Interconnect Compiler and Emulator

**Authors:** Michael Silver, Zachary Vernec, Hans-Arno Jacobsen

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11340v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.11340v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-09


### 摘要

由于分布式量子计算（DQC）提供了可扩展量子计算的领先途径，因此在现实条件下对分布式算法进行基准测试的能力对于系统协同设计变得至关重要。然而，由于无法访问物理系统，研究人员缺乏评估分发协议的工具。我们引入Q-DICE（量子分布式互连调试器和模拟器），这是一种硬件感知的仿真环境，用于在经典模拟器和NISQ时代的单片硬件上对分布式量子电路进行基准测试。这项工作提供了三个核心贡献：（1）利用两种新颖技术-- QPU切片和缝合--构建分布式QPU后台的编程方案，以促进分布式电路映射，（2）使用物理激励Kraus操作符和随机误差通道对非本地链路噪音建模的方法，以及（3）边界感知电路映射算法，在蒸发期间强制执行分布式QPU布局约束。这些组件共同构成了一个分布感知的编译器和噪音建模引擎，可以忠实地在现有执行环境中强制执行分布式量子硬件的物理限制。我们针对大量实验证明的量子电路验证了Q-DICE，包括在光学链接捕获离子硬件上的分布式Grover搜索，实现了模拟和实验结果之间4%的最坏情况保真度偏差。这些发现证明了Q-DICE能够准确地复制跨平台的真实分布式量子系统行为，简化分布式量子算法和架构的实验。


### Abstract

As distributed quantum computing (DQC) offers a leading path towards scalable quantum computation, the ability to benchmark distributed algorithms under realistic conditions becomes critical for system co-design. However, without access to physical systems, researchers lack tools to evaluate distribution protocols. We introduce Q-DICE (Quantum Distributed Interconnect Compiler and Emulator), a hardware-aware emulation environment for benchmarking distributed quantum circuits on classical simulators and on NISQ-era monolithic hardware. This work provides three core contributions: (1) a programmatic scheme to construct distributed QPU backends, utilizing two novel techniques - QPU slicing and stitching - to facilitate distributed circuit mapping, (2) a methodology for modeling nonlocal link noise using physically motivated Kraus operators and stochastic error channels, and (3) a boundary-aware circuit mapping algorithm enforcing distributed QPU topology constraints during transpilation. Together, these components constitute a distribution-aware compiler and noise-modeling engine that faithfully enforces the physical limitations of distributed quantum hardware within existing execution environments. We validate Q-DICE against a multitude of experimentally demonstrated quantum circuits, including a distributed Grover's search on optically linked trapped-ion hardware, achieving a worst-case fidelity deviation of 4% between simulated and experimental results. These findings demonstrate Q-DICE's capacity to accurately reproduce real distributed quantum system behavior across platforms, streamlining experimentation with distributed quantum algorithms and architectures.

## 费舍尔几何重塑了多参数量子估计中不相容的影响

**Original Title:** Fisher geometry reshapes the effect of incompatibility in multiparameter quantum estimation

**Authors:** Jiayu He, Matteo G. A. Paris

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11343v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.11343v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-09


### 摘要

多参数量子估计面临两个基本障碍：草率，即量子费舍尔信息矩阵（QRST）的各向异性使得某些参数方向不敏感且不相容，不同参数的最佳测量的非交换性。权衡界限$C_T$捕捉了它们对精度的联合影响，但目前尚不清楚参数平面之间的不兼容性分布如何影响其总体成本。在这里，我们将不兼容的总量与其位置分开。我们引入了一个无因次量$G_n^{（F）}$，用于测量不相容性分布和QSYS特征值之间的一致性，并展示不相容性贡献的弗罗贝尼乌斯量表如何进行因子分解。我们获得一个界并证明不相容成本位于这个界及其依赖等级的倍数之间。我们还证明，在固定的松散度或等效固定的Fisher体积下，将不相容性集中到单个参数平面中可以降低优化的权衡成本，因为然后可以重新塑造Fisher几何形状，以将更多的Fisher面积分配给该平面。quutrit $SU（2）$编码在数字上证实，如果匹配因子$G$足够小，具有较大不兼容强度的状态仍然会产生较小的成本。我们的研究结果表明，相对于Fisher特征基的不相容分布是多参数估计的中心诊断，超出了总的不相容强度。


### Abstract

Multiparameter quantum estimation faces two fundamental obstacles: sloppiness, i.e., anisotropy of the quantum Fisher information matrix (QFIM) that renders some parameter directions insensitive, and incompatibility, the non-commutativity of optimal measurements for different parameters. The trade-off bound $C_T$ captures their joint impact on precision, but it has remained unclear how the distribution of incompatibility across parameter planes affects its overall cost. Here we separate the total amount of incompatibility from its location. We introduce a dimensionless quantity $G_n^{(F)}$ that measures the alignment between the incompatibility distribution and the eigenvalues of the QFIM, and show how the Frobenius scale of the incompatibility contribution factorizes. We obtain a bound and prove the incompatibility cost lies between this bound and a rank-dependent multiple thereof. We also prove that at fixed sloppiness, or equivalently fixed Fisher volume, concentrating incompatibility into a single parameter plane reduces the optimized trade-off cost because the Fisher geometry can then be reshaped to allocate more Fisher area to that plane. A qutrit $SU(2)$ encoding numerically confirms that states with larger incompatibility strength can nevertheless incur a smaller cost if the matching factor $G$ is sufficiently small. Our results establish that the distribution of incompatibility relative to the Fisher eigenbasis is a central diagnostic for multiparameter estimation, beyond the total incompatibility strength.
