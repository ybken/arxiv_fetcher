## 广义不确定关系和量子速度极限

**Original Title:** Generalized Uncertainty Relations and Quantum Speed Limits

**Authors:** M. W. AlMasri

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.24791v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.24791v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-25


### 摘要

我们为混合量子力学提出了一个数学上严格的统一框架，该框架系统地将代数变形和空间非定域性结合在单一操作员形式主义中。通过谱演算构造自伴随混合动力学操作员，我们推导出精确的广义不确定关系，该关系在$q$-变形和分数量子力学边界之间进行插值。此外，我们为混合Hamilton建立了严格的量子速度极限定理，揭示了变形参数、分数阶和外部势如何调节量子状态的基本进化速率。我们证明了代数变形通过离散动量量子化加速了连贯动力学，而分数非局部性会导致谱增宽，从而抑制进化速度。该框架将标准量子力学、$q$-量子力学和分数量子力学恢复为限制情况，并为俘获离子、超导和冷原子平台中的实验区分提供了明确的现象学签名。


### Abstract

We propose a mathematically rigorous unified framework for hybrid quantum mechanics that systematically combines algebraic deformation and spatial non-locality within a single operator formalism. By constructing a self-adjoint hybrid kinetic operator through spectral calculus, we derive exact generalized uncertainty relations that interpolate between $q$-deformed and fractional quantum mechanical bounds. Furthermore, we establish a rigorous quantum speed limit theorem for the hybrid Hamiltonian, revealing how deformation parameters, fractional orders, and external potentials tune the fundamental evolution rate of quantum states. We prove that algebraic deformation accelerates coherent dynamics through discrete momentum quantization, while fractional non-locality induces spectral broadening that suppresses evolution speed. The framework recovers standard quantum mechanics, $q$-quantum mechanics, and fractional quantum mechanics as limiting cases, and provides explicit phenomenological signatures for experimental discrimination in trapped-ion, superconducting, and cold-atom platforms.

## 具有固有量子时间不确定性的量子重量法

**Original Title:** Quantum gravimetry with intrinsic quantum time uncertainty

**Authors:** Salman Sajad Wani, Sundus Abdi, Rushda Naik, Saif Al-Kuwari

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.24792v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.24792v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-25


### 摘要

当询问时间具有固有的不确定性时，我们研究量子重力测量，其动机是与能量-时间不确定性关系相关的时间分辨率的基本限制。对于线性重力耦合重力仪，我们通过分析双参数量子费舍尔信息（QFI）矩阵的询问时间来获得有效重力信息。在这个类别中，时间信息块在引力参数中是二次的，对于二次背景动力学，重力-时间交叉项在$g$中变成仿射。这些属性为一旦询问时间被视为干扰参数，剩余的标准单参数重力QFI分数产生了一个标准化表达，具有仿射分子和洛伦兹分母。我们在三个基准模型中计算出这些结果：自由下落的高斯波包、Kasevich-Chu光脉冲原子干涉仪和理想化的闭单位光学机械模型。高斯自由落体基准为有效重力信息提供了精确的封闭形式表达，并明确显示了滋扰时间剖面如何抑制标准单参数重力QFI中动量传播相关部分。在Kasevich-Chu干涉仪中，除非提供独立的计时信息，否则内部状态布居读出会给出一个等级不足的测量双参数几何，而完全访问最终的运动和内部状态可以恢复一个全等级几何，保留力由初始速度传播和重力累积运动之间的竞争控制。在原子干涉基准中，该框架为最大限度地减少滋扰时间信息损失提供了明确的条件，以及对动量传播、空间定位和长询问时间操作的相应约束。


### Abstract

We study quantum gravimetry when the interrogation time carries intrinsic uncertainty, motivated by a fundamental limit on temporal resolution associated with the energy--time uncertainty relation. For linearly gravity-coupled gravimeters, we obtain the effective gravity information by profiling the interrogation time from the two-parameter quantum Fisher information (QFI) matrix. In this class, the time-information block is quadratic in the gravitational parameter, and for quadratic background dynamics, the gravity--time cross term becomes affine in $g$. These properties yield a normalized expression for the fraction of standard single-parameter gravity QFI that remains once interrogation time is treated as a nuisance parameter, with an affine numerator and a Lorentzian denominator. We work out these results in three benchmark models: a freely falling Gaussian wavepacket, the Kasevich--Chu light-pulse atom interferometer, and an idealized closed-unitary optomechanical model. The Gaussian free-fall benchmark yields an exact closed-form expression for the effective gravity information and shows explicitly how nuisance-time profiling suppresses the momentum-spread-dependent part of the standard single-parameter gravity QFI. In the Kasevich--Chu interferometer, internal state population readout gives a rank-deficient measured two-parameter geometry unless independent timing information is supplied, whereas full access to the final motional and internal states restores a full-rank geometry with retention controlled by the competition between initial velocity spread and gravitationally accumulated motion. In atom-interferometric benchmarks, the framework yields explicit conditions for minimizing nuisance-time information loss, together with corresponding constraints on momentum spread, spatial localization, and long-interrogation-time operation.

## 架构感知的统一合成

**Original Title:** Architecture-aware Unitary Synthesis

**Authors:** Frans Perkkola, Arianne Meijer-van de Griend, Jukka K. Nurminen

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.23777v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.23777v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-26


### 摘要

我们提出了一种新颖的架构感知蒸发方法，用于在高温量子硬件上进行精确的一般元门合成。我们的方法与优化的块ZXZ分解紧密集成，利用其迭代结构在迭代的每个级别做出硬件感知决策，而不是将蒸发视为独立的后处理步骤。该方法引入了三项关键技术：贪婪量子位映射策略，最大限度地减少物理量子位之间的成对距离，自适应格雷码选择与量子位交换相结合，优化目标布局的统一控制Rz门的构造，以及通过利用远程C诺特阶梯的结构来减少C诺特门的启发式。我们在20量子位IQM Garnet（方形网格）和156量子位IBM Marrakesh（重十六进制）架构上针对TKet、QisKit和Pennylane对我们的方法进行基准测试，量子位计数范围从3到11。与最佳竞争的翻译器相比，我们的方法在IQM Garnet上实现了高达36%的COT计数减少，在IBM Marrakesh上实现了高达34%的COT计数减少，同时实现了高达553倍的翻译加速。此外，我们的方法是唯一一种能够在30分钟的时间限制内在两种架构中编译超过10个量子位的电路的方法。


### Abstract

We present a novel architecture-aware transpilation method for exact general unitary gate synthesis on superconducting quantum hardware. Our approach is tightly integrated with the optimized block-ZXZ decomposition, exploiting its recursive structure to make hardware-aware decisions at each level of the recursion rather than treating transpilation as an independent post-processing step. The method introduces three key techniques: a greedy qubit mapping strategy that minimizes pairwise distances between physical qubits, an adaptive Gray code selection combined with qubit swapping that optimizes the construction of uniformly controlled Rz gates for the target topology, and a heuristic for reducing CNOT gates by exploiting the structure of long-range CNOT ladders. We benchmark our method against TKet, Qiskit, and Pennylane on the 20-qubit IQM Garnet (square lattice) and the 156-qubit IBM Marrakesh (heavy-hex) architectures with qubit counts ranging from 3 to 11. Our method achieves CNOT count reductions of up to 36 percent on the IQM Garnet and up to 34 percent on the IBM Marrakesh compared to the best competing transpiler, while simultaneously achieving transpilation speedups of up to 553x. Furthermore, our method is the only one capable of transpiling circuits beyond 10 qubits within a 30-minute time limit across both architectures.

## 基于图条件信任区域的查询高效量子逼近优化

**Original Title:** Query-Efficient Quantum Approximate Optimization via Graph-Conditioned Trust Regions

**Authors:** Molena Huynh

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.24803v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.24803v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-27


### 摘要

在量子逼近优化算法（QAOA）的低深度实现中，主要成本通常是客观评估的数量，而不是电路深度。我们引入了一种图形条件信任域方法来减少这种查询成本。图神经网络预测QAOA角度上的高斯分布N（μ，Sigma）。平均值初始化局部优化器，协方差定义了约束搜索的椭圆形信任区域，预测的不确定性确定了取决于实例的评估预算。因此，学习到的分布定义了搜索策略，而不仅仅是初始参数估计。   在局部光滑度、弯曲度、校准和噪音的明确假设下，我们推导出信任区内目标退化的界限、梯度方差的下限、去极化噪音下预期目标排序的保留以及有限样本覆盖保证。我们在n = 8-16个点的Erdos-Renyi、3-正规、Barabasi-Albert和Watts-Strogatz图上评估深度p = 2处MaxCut的方法。相对于随机重启和最强的学习点预测基线，该方法将电路评估的平均数量从343和85减少到45 +/- 7，同时将采样逼近比保持在基于浓度的启发式方法的3个百分点内。   该方法不会提高绝对逼近比;它的优点是在相当的解决方案质量下降低了查询成本。在实验中校准了预测不确定性，ECT = 0.052，Spearman相关性rho = 0.770，并且学习到的信任区域转移到训练期间未使用的图大小。结果确定了一个低深度、查询主导的机制，其中图形条件信任区域在不修改类比的情况下降低了QAOA的查询成本。


### Abstract

In low-depth implementations of the Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm (QAOA), the dominant cost is often the number of objective evaluations rather than circuit depth. We introduce a graph-conditioned trust-region method for reducing this query cost. A graph neural network predicts a Gaussian distribution N(mu, Sigma) over QAOA angles. The mean initializes a local optimizer, the covariance defines an ellipsoidal trust region that constrains the search, and the predicted uncertainty determines an instance-dependent evaluation budget. Thus the learned distribution defines a search policy rather than only an initial parameter estimate.
  Under explicit assumptions on local smoothness, curvature, calibration, and noise, we derive bounds on objective degradation within the trust region, lower bounds on gradient variance, preservation of expected objective ordering under depolarizing noise, and finite-sample coverage guarantees. We evaluate the method for MaxCut at depth p = 2 on Erdos-Renyi, 3-regular, Barabasi-Albert, and Watts-Strogatz graphs with n = 8-16 vertices. Relative to random restarts and the strongest learned point-prediction baseline, the method reduces the mean number of circuit evaluations from 343 and 85 to 45 +/- 7, while maintaining sampled approximation ratios within 3 percentage points of concentration-based heuristics.
  The method does not improve absolute approximation ratios; its advantage is reduced query cost at comparable solution quality. The predictive uncertainty is calibrated in the experiments, with ECE = 0.052 and Spearman correlation rho = 0.770, and the learned trust regions transfer to graph sizes not used during training. The results identify a low-depth, query-dominated regime in which graph-conditioned trust regions reduce the query cost of QAOA without modifying the ansatz.

## 恩里科·费米给瓦雷娜的遗产

**Original Title:** The Legacy of Enrico Fermi to Varenna

**Authors:** Vladislav Gavryusev, Massimo Inguscio

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.24829v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.24829v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-27


### 摘要

瓦雷纳学派是一个中心，包括众多诺贝尔奖获得者在内的几代物理学家在这里塑造了这一领域，通常是通过跨越政治和文化界限的合作交流。我们探讨了恩里科·费米的科学遗产及其对现代原子、分子和光学物理学的影响。从费米1954年在瓦雷纳学校的演讲开始，从高能物理学到激光光谱学、精确计量学和超冷原子控制的关键发展被追溯到。无Doppler光谱学、光学频率梳、玻色-爱因斯坦凝聚和简并费米气体等里程碑被强调为导致量子模拟和量子计算的转折点。费米早期倡导制造计算机而不是购买计算机，可以被视为当今量子科学和技术努力的先驱。这一历史轨迹和遗产继续为当前的量子物质和信息科学研究提供信息。


### Abstract

The Varenna school is a hub where generations of physicists, including numerous Nobel laureates, have shaped the field, often through collaborative exchanges across political and cultural boundaries. We examine the scientific legacy of Enrico Fermi and its influence on modern atomic, molecular, and optical physics. Beginning with Fermi's 1954 lectures at the Varenna school, key developments are traced from high-energy physics to laser spectroscopy, precision metrology, and the control of ultracold atoms. Milestones such as Doppler-free spectroscopy, optical frequency combs, Bose-Einstein condensation, and degenerate Fermi gases are highlighted as turning points leading to quantum simulation and quantum computation. Fermi's early advocacy for building a computer, rather than buying it, can be viewed as a precursor to today's efforts in quantum science and technologies. This historical trajectory and legacy continues to inform current research in quantum matter and information science.

## CAbLECAR：在可拼接的旋转量子位芯片上高效调度QDLC码，并通过穿梭

**Original Title:** CAbLECAR: efficiently scheduling QLDPC codes on a tileable spin qubit chip with shuttling

**Authors:** Jason D. Chadwick, Frederic T. Chong

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.24739v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.24739v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-27


### 摘要

半导体旋转量子位是大规模量子计算的一个有前途的平台，但尚未充分利用广泛的量子低密度宇称检验（QDLC）码，这些码承诺高编码率和高效的逻辑，但需要物理量子位之间的非本地连接性。在这项工作中，我们研究了Q低脂码在可拼接、基于穿梭的旋转量子位架构上的实现。通过针对穿梭噪音模型定制综合征提取电路，我们显着改进了之前的表面代码提案，并将架构的可行穿梭范围扩展了5- 10倍，从而能够实现具有远程交互的更复杂代码。我们从机器人领域汲取灵感，开发了一种协调的航天飞机调度算法，该算法支持任意代码，并使用它来基准测试各种有前途的代码系列的逻辑性能。我们发现，对于某些代码系列，优化的计划比手动优化的计划快86%。通过详细的电路级模拟，我们识别出特定的Q低脂码，这些码在先前的表面码实现的基础上改进了几个数量级，提高了编码效率并降低了逻辑错误率。这项工作展示了基于穿梭的旋转量子位硬件平台用于可扩展和高效的容错量子计算的潜力。


### Abstract

Semiconductor spin qubits are a promising platform for large-scale quantum computing, but have yet to take full advantage of the broad class of quantum low-density parity check (QLDPC) codes, which promise high encoding rates and efficient logic but require nonlocal connectivity between physical qubits. In this work, we investigate the implementation of QLDPC codes on a tileable, shuttling-based spin qubit architecture. By tailoring syndrome extraction circuits to the shuttling noise model, we significantly improve on previous surface code proposals and extend the feasible shuttling range of the architecture by 5-10x, enabling the implementation of more complex codes with long-range interactions. Taking inspiration from the field of robotics, we develop a coordinated shuttle scheduling algorithm that supports arbitrary codes and use it to benchmark the logical performance of a variety of promising code families. We find that the optimized schedules are up to 86% faster than hand-optimized schedules for certain code families. Through detailed circuit-level simulations, we identify specific QLDPC codes that improve upon prior surface code implementations by orders of magnitude, increasing encoding efficiency and reducing logical error rates. This work demonstrates the potential of shuttling-based spin qubit hardware platforms for scalable and efficient fault-tolerant quantum computation.

## 泡利稳定器码的分类：格和连续体说明

**Original Title:** The Classification of Pauli Stabilizer Codes: A Lattice and Continuum Treatise

**Authors:** Bowen Yang, Matthew Yu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.24847v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.24847v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-27


### 摘要

我们使用代数$\mathrm{L}$-理论的框架将移动Pauli稳定器代码分类为间隙接口和粗粒度。我们将这种分类与框架TQFT的分类进行比较，框架TQFT是在连续体中自然产生的理论，强调了两者之间密切的结构关系。我们的方法是在洛朗多项环上配备二次函子的完美链复合体的范畴中制定的$R = \mathbb{Z}/p[x_1^{\PM 1}，\ldots，x_n^{\PM 1}]$，其中泡利稳定码的拓扑运算符集合自然地作为对象出现。特别是，我们为格论建立了一种体边界对应：Pauli稳定器代码直到有间隙界面的等效类由更高一维的Clifford QCA描述。这是使用稳定器码的通用目标类别来完成的，该类别是分类谱，其存在性和通用性质在本文中介绍了。我们通过强调泡利稳定码和TQFT的分类之间的细微差异来得出结论，这导致了格理论和连续体理论之间的定性差异。


### Abstract

We classify mobile Pauli stabilizer codes up to gapped interfaces and coarse-graining using the framework of algebraic $\mathrm{L}$-theory. We compare this classification with that of framed TQFTs, theories that arise naturally in the continuum, highlighting a close structural relationship between the two. Our approach is formulated in the category of perfect chain complexes equipped with quadratic functor over the Laurent polynomial ring $R = \mathbb{Z}/p[x_1^{\pm 1}, \ldots, x_n^{\pm 1}]$, within which the collection of topological operators of Pauli stabilizer codes arise naturally as objects. In particular, we establish a bulk-boundary correspondence for lattice theories: the equivalence class of a Pauli stabilizer code up to gapped interface is described by a Clifford QCA in one dimension higher. This is done using the universal target category for stabilizer codes, which is the categorical spectrum whose existence and universal properties are introduced in this work. We conclude by highlighting subtle differences between the classification of Pauli stabilizer codes and TQFTs, leading to qualitative distinctions between lattice and continuum theories.

## 周期驱动Rydberg链中的新兴预热Bethe可积性

**Original Title:** Emergent prethermal Bethe integrability in a periodically driven Rydberg chain

**Authors:** Saptadip Roy, Arnab Sen, Diptiman Sen, K. Sengupta

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.24850v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.24850v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-27


### 摘要

我们研究了周期性驱动的里德伯原子链，并确定了一类驱动协议，其中系统在特殊驱动频率下表现出新兴的热前Bethe可集成性。我们提供了大驱动幅度区域下其Floquet Hamilton量的微扰分析表达。我们通过将其映射到范式旋转的汉密尔顿量链-$1/2$ ${\rm XXZ}$链来证明这个Floquet Hamilton量的主导项在特殊驱动频率下的可积性。我们通过对有限链的精确对角化研究来支持我们的分析结果。我们关于驱动链的水平统计、半链纠缠量和纵向磁性的数值结果揭示了其在特殊驱动频率下出现的可积性，这种特性持续到大的预热时间尺度。


### Abstract

We study a chain of periodically driven Rydberg atoms and identify a class of drive protocols for which the system exhibits emergent prethermal Bethe integrability at special drive frequencies. We provide a perturbative analytic expression of its Floquet Hamiltonian in the large drive amplitude regime. We demonstrate integrability of the leading term of this Floquet Hamiltonian at special drive frequencies, which we identify, by mapping it to the Hamiltonian of the paradigmatic spin-$1/2$ ${\rm XXZ}$ chain. We support our analytical results by exact diagonalization studies on finite chains. Our numerical results on level statistics, half-chain entanglement entropy, and longitudinal magnetization of the driven chain brings out its emergent integrable nature at the special drive frequencies which persists up to a large prethermal timescale.

## 中性原子量子处理器中无序诱导纠缠转变的随机测量

**Original Title:** Randomised measurements of a disorder-induced entanglement transition in a neutral atom quantum processor

**Authors:** Apollonas S. Matsoukas-Roubeas, Oscar Scholin, Lucas Sá, Arinjoy De, Majd Hamdan, Alexei Bylinskii, Andrew J. Daley, Dorian A. Gangloff

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.24854v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.24854v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-27


### 摘要

复杂量子系统中纠缠的发展和传播对于探索脱离平衡的多体现象至关重要。测量纠缠动力学可以揭示信息扰乱和热化，即从多体量子混乱到无序相互作用系统中的局部化的转变。在量子计算系统中，最近测量了密度矩阵的纠缠态和其他非线性函数，特别是通过使用随机测量工具箱。然而，如果没有通用的本地控制，很难在特定子系统上实现所需的任意单位旋转。在这里，我们使用随机测量协议在可编程模拟量子模拟器中设计并演示了纠缠量的测量，该协议利用局部能量调谐和全局场来绕过对局部门控制的需要。我们在市售的中性原子量子模拟器QuEra的Aquila上实现了这一点，并使用它来展示局部Hamilton参数中的可编程无序如何导致从混乱到局部纠缠动力学的转变。给定当前的去相干时间，我们清楚地解决了小型系统中特定于无序的、时间相关的纠缠传播问题。我们的工作扩展了可编程模拟量子模拟器的实用性，并为一系列其他模拟系统中更广泛的随机测量工具箱开辟了进一步的机会。


### Abstract

The development and spread of entanglement in complex quantum systems is central to exploring many-body phenomena out of equilibrium. Measuring entanglement dynamics can shed light on information scrambling and thermalisation, namely on transitions from many-body quantum chaos to localisation in disordered, interacting systems. In quantum computing systems, entanglement entropy and other nonlinear functions of the density matrix have been recently measured, in particular by using the randomised measurement toolbox. However, it is difficult to implement the required arbitrary unitary rotations on specific subsystems without universal local control. Here we devise and demonstrate the measurement of entanglement entropy in a programmable analogue quantum simulator using a randomised measurement protocol that leverages local energy tuning together with a global field to bypass the need for local gate control. We implement this on a commercially available neutral-atom quantum simulator, QuEra's Aquila, and use it to show how programmable disorder in the local Hamiltonian parameters leads to a transition from chaotic to localised entanglement dynamics. Given current decoherence times, we clearly resolve disorder-specific, time-dependent entanglement spreading in small systems. Our work extends the utility of programmable analogue quantum simulators, and opens further opportunities for wider randomised measurement toolboxes in a range of other analogue systems.

## 使用氮空位中心生成量子随机数的演示

**Original Title:** Demonstration of quantum random number generation using nitrogen vacancy centres

**Authors:** Conrad Strydom, Mark Tame

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.24870v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.24870v1

### 摘要

光学共振器增强的非线性相互作用对于连续波二次谐振的有效产生、光学参数振荡、频率混合和压缩光的产生非常重要。为了最大限度地提高腔内非线性材料内的这些相互作用，需要高强度、最佳相匹配和所有相互作用场的同时共振。然而，光学共振器的分散通常会阻止多个波长的共共振。在这里，我们提出了一种新颖的实现方式，使用单片双稳态散热器来根据直接施加到非线性晶体一部分的浅温度梯度来控制谐振器分散。该方法能够实现精确的分散控制，并旨在最大限度地减少非线性晶体中的机械和热应力，从而为设计高效且可靠的共振器增强非线性器件提供了一种额外的方法，用于引力波检测、量子光学和频率转换等高要求的应用。


### Abstract

Optical resonator-enhanced nonlinear interactions are of great importance for the efficient generation of continuous-wave second harmonic generation, optical parametric oscillation, frequency mixing, and the generation of squeezed light. In order to maximize these interactions within the intra-cavity nonlinear material, high intensities, optimal phase matching, and simultaneous resonance of all interacting fields are required. However, the dispersion of the optical resonator often prevents the co-resonance of multiple wavelengths. Here, we present a novel implementation using a monolithic bimetallic heat sink for controlling the resonator dispersion based on a shallow temperature gradient directly applied to a section of the nonlinear crystal. This method enables precise dispersion control and is designed to minimize mechanical and thermal stresses in the nonlinear crystal, thus providing an additional method for designing highly efficient and reliable resonator-enhanced nonlinear devices for demanding applications such as gravitational wave detection, quantum optics, and frequency conversion.

## QCalEval：量子校准图理解的视觉语言模型基准

**Original Title:** QCalEval: Benchmarking Vision-Language Models for Quantum Calibration Plot Understanding

**Authors:** Shuxiang Cao, Zijian Zhang, Abhishek Agarwal, Grace Bratrud, Niyaz R. Beysengulov, Daniel C. Cole, Alejandro Gómez Frieiro, Elena O. Glen, Hao Hsu, Gang Huang, Raymond Jow, Greshma Shaji, Tom Lubowe, Ligeng Zhu, Luis Mantilla Calderón, Nicola Pancotti, Joel Pendleton, Brandon Severin, Charles Etienne Staub, Sara Sussman, Antti Vepsäläinen, Neel Rajeshbhai Vora, Yilun Xu, Varinia Bernales, Daniel Bowring, Elica Kyoseva, Ivan Rungger, Giulia Semeghini, Sam Stanwyck, Timothy Costa, Alán Aspuru-Guzik, Krysta Svore

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25884v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.25884v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-28


### 摘要

量子计算校准取决于解释实验数据，校准图为这项任务提供了最通用的人类可读表示，但还没有对视觉语言模型（VLM）解释它们的能力进行系统评估。我们引入了QCalEval，这是第一个量子校准图的VLM基准：来自22个实验系列的87个场景类型的243个样本，涵盖了超量子位和中性原子，在零激发和背景学习环境中对六个问题类型进行了评估。最好的通用零射击模型的平均得分达到72.3，许多开权模型在多图像上下文学习下会退化，而前沿封闭模型则大幅提高。90亿参数规模的监督微调消融表明，SFT提高了零激发性能，但无法缩小多模式上下文学习差距。作为参考案例研究，我们发布了NVIDIA Ising Calibration 1，这是一款基于Qwen 3.5 - 35 B-A3 B的开重模型，可达到74.7的零拍摄平均分。


### Abstract

Quantum computing calibration depends on interpreting experimental data, and calibration plots provide the most universal human-readable representation for this task, yet no systematic evaluation exists of how well vision-language models (VLMs) interpret them. We introduce QCalEval, the first VLM benchmark for quantum calibration plots: 243 samples across 87 scenario types from 22 experiment families, spanning superconducting qubits and neutral atoms, evaluated on six question types in both zero-shot and in-context learning settings. The best general-purpose zero-shot model reaches a mean score of 72.3, and many open-weight models degrade under multi-image in-context learning, whereas frontier closed models improve substantially. A supervised fine-tuning ablation at the 9-billion-parameter scale shows that SFT improves zero-shot performance but cannot close the multimodal in-context learning gap. As a reference case study, we release NVIDIA Ising Calibration 1, an open-weight model based on Qwen3.5-35B-A3B that reaches 74.7 zero-shot average score.

## 用混合编码系统测试连续变量Bell类不等式

**Original Title:** Testing a continuous-variable Bell-like inequality with a hybrid-encoded system

**Authors:** Yu Meng, Ying Wang, Clara Henke, Nikolai Bart, Arne Ludwig, Peter Lodahl, Jonas S. Neergaard-Nielsen, Ulrik L. Andersen, Leonardo Midolo, Zheng-Hao Liu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25901v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.25901v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-28


### 摘要

连续变量量子系统是量子计算和量子信息处理的有前途的候选者。众所周知，高斯连续变量系统上的积分测量可以用非上下文隐变量模型来描述，并且不能违反贝尔不等式。在这里，我们证明当涉及顺序测量时，观察会失败。我们的实验是通过将InAs/Gaas量子发射器确定性产生的单量子的空间模式映射到Gottesman--Kitaev--Preskill代码空间中的逻辑运算来实现的。采用黑匣子式的方法，我们观察到贝尔式非上下文隐变量不平等违反了380个标准差。我们的结果解决了之前工作中的概念漏洞，并为使用光编码连续变量系统研究基础量子物理学开辟了新的可能性。


### Abstract

Continuous-variable quantum systems are promising candidates for quantum computing and quantum information processing. It is widely known that quadrature measurements on Gaussian continuous-variable systems can be described by a noncontextual hidden-variable model and cannot violate a Bell inequality. Here, we demonstrate that the observation fails when sequential measurements are involved. Our experiment is realized by mapping the spatial modes of a single photon, deterministically generated from an InAs/GaAs quantum emitter, to the logical operations in the Gottesman--Kitaev--Preskill code space. Employing a black-box-style approach, we observe a violation of the Bell-like noncontextual hidden-variable inequality by 380 standard deviations. Our results address the conceptual loopholes in previous works and open up new possibilities for studying fundamental quantum physics using photonic-encoded continuous-variable systems.

## 多模式高斯态上的量子计数测量产生的非经典光的预示概率优化

**Original Title:** Heralding probability optimization for nonclassical light generated by photon counting measurements on multimode Gaussian states

**Authors:** Jaromír Fiurášek

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25910v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.25910v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-28


### 摘要

高度非经典的光量子态的产生对于光学量子信息处理和量子计量至关重要。鉴于光场之间缺乏足够强的非线性相互作用，常用的光学量子状态制备方案是有条件的，基于在一部分的多模式压缩高斯状态上预示着量子数测量引起的非线性。此类概率量子状态工程方案的开发和优化是当前量子光学的核心挑战之一。随着技术的进步和实验的进步，探测更多数量的量子，预示概率的最大化对于确保足够高的状态准备率至关重要。在这里，我们表明，对于基于高斯状态和量子数测量的条件量子状态准备方案，预示概率的最大化可以用公式表示为寻找方程组的解，这提供了一种找到最佳配置的有效方法，并使我们能够应用专门用于解决此类方程组的技术。我们的方法可以无缝地结合可用的单模式四角压缩的界限，这与实验高度相关。我们主要考虑Fock状态有限叠加的产生，但表明该方法可以直接扩展到Fock状态压缩叠加的产生。我们关注的是具有消失的相干位移的高斯态，因此有条件生成的状态具有明确定义的量子数宇称。我们以具有两种预示模式的单模式和双模式状态的生成示例来说明我们的一般方法论。


### Abstract

Generation of highly non-classical quantum states of light is essential for optical quantum information processing and quantum metrology. Given the lack of sufficiently strong nonlinear interactions between optical fields, the commonly employed optical quantum-state preparation schemes are conditional, based on nonlinearity induced by heralding photon number measurement on a part of a multimode squeezed Gaussian state. Development and optimization of such probabilistic quantum-state engineering schemes represents one of the central challenges in current quantum optics. As technology advances and experiments progress to detection of higher numbers of photons, the maximization of the heralding probability becomes essential to ensure sufficiently high state-preparation rates. Here, we show that for the conditional quantum state preparation schemes based on Gaussian states and photon number measurements the maximization of the heralding probability can be formulated as finding solution to a system of polynomial equations, which offers an efficient way to find the optimal configuration and allows us to apply techniques dedicated specifically to solving such systems of equations. Our approach can seamlessly incorporate bounds on the available single-mode quadrature squeezing, which is highly experimentally relevant. We mainly consider generation of finite superpositions of Fock states but show that the approach can be straightforwardly extended to generation of squeezed superpositions of Fock states. We focus on Gaussian states with vanishing coherent displacements, hence the conditionally generated states have well defined photon number parity. We illustrate our general methodology on examples of generation of single-mode and two-mode states with two heralding modes.
