## 作为一维XY旋转链的脂肪族链

**Original Title:** Aliphatic Chains as One-Dimensional XY Spin Chains

**Authors:** Kirill F. Sheberstov

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.23759v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.23759v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-28


### 摘要

旋转波是在具有最近邻相互作用的格子中传播的旋转序扰动。传统上，它们是在磁有序固体中使用非弹性中子、光或电子散射和铁磁共振来观察的。在这里，我们表明，类似的旋转动力学可以在含有脂肪族链的分子的液态核磁共振（核磁共振）中出现。在此类分子中，每个CH_2基团必须具有独特的化学位移，并且通过对外耦合而产生磁不等效。在此条件下，双子质子的单重态布居沿着（CH_2）_n段传播，形成磁无声的旋转波。对于具有平移对称性的链，旋转Hamilton因子分解为形式上等效于一维XY模型的子空间。这种对应关系产生了光谱学中本征态和本征能量的分析表达，我们称之为旋转链零量子核磁共振。我们识别出满足这些条件的分子系统。它们的集体动力学迅速超越了经典的计算可处理性，使它们成为量子计算机模拟旋转输运和多体动力学的目标。


### Abstract

Spin waves are propagating disturbances of spin order in lattices with nearest-neighbor interactions. They are traditionally observed in magnetically ordered solids using inelastic neutron, light, or electron scattering, and ferromagnetic resonance. Here, we show that analogous spin dynamics can arise in liquid-state nuclear magnetic resonance (NMR) of molecules containing aliphatic chains. In such molecules, each CH_2 group must have a distinct chemical shift and be magnetically inequivalent via out-of-pair couplings. Under these conditions, singlet state populations of geminal protons propagate along (CH_2)_n segments forming magnetically silent spin waves. For a chain with translational symmetry, the spin Hamiltonian factorizes into subspaces formally equivalent to the one-dimensional XY model. This correspondence yields analytic expressions for eigenstates and eigenenergies in a spectroscopy we term spin-chain zero-quantum NMR. We identify molecular systems in which these conditions are met. Their collective dynamics rapidly exceed classical computational tractability, making them targets for quantum-computer simulations of spin transport and many-body dynamics.

## Grover-mixer量子交替运算符模拟算法在高级无约束二元优化问题中的应用

**Original Title:** Applying Grover-mixer Quantum Alternating Operator Ansatz Algorithm to High-order Unconstrained Binary Optimization Problems

**Authors:** Evgeniy O. Kiktenko, Elizaveta V. Krendeleva, Aleksey K. Fedorov

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.23026v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.23026v2

**Submitted:** 2025-12-28


### 摘要

量子逼近优化算法（QAOA）是在近期处理器上实现量子优势的主要候选算法之一。虽然通常使用横向混合器（XM-QAOA）来实现，但Grover-mixer变体（GM-QAOA）因其全球搜索功能而提供了一种引人注目的替代方案。这项工作研究了GM-QAOA在更高级无约束二元优化（HUBO）问题中的应用，也称为多元无约束二元优化（PUBO），它构成了一类广义的组合优化任务，其特征在于本质上是多变量相互作用。我们提出了一项全面的数值研究，证明GM-QAOA与XM-QAOA不同，GM-QAOA表现出随电路深度的单调性能改进，并在HUBO问题上实现了更好的结果。我们方法的一个重要组成部分是用于建模GM-QAOA动态的分析框架，它能够实现最佳参数的经典逼近，并有助于减少优化成本。我们的资源高效的参数化GM-QAOA几乎与完全优化算法的性能相匹配，同时要求低得多，使其成为复杂优化任务的高效方法。这些发现凸显了GM-QAOA的潜力，并为其在当前量子硬件上的实施提供了实用途径。


### Abstract

The Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm (QAOA) is among leading candidates for achieving quantum advantage on near-term processors. While typically implemented with a transverse-field mixer (XM-QAOA), the Grover-mixer variant (GM-QAOA) offers a compelling alternative due to its global search capabilities. This work investigates the application of GM-QAOA to Higher-Order Unconstrained Binary Optimization (HUBO) problems, also known as Polynomial Unconstrained Binary Optimization (PUBO), which constitute a generalized class of combinatorial optimization tasks characterized by intrinsically multi-variable interactions. We present a comprehensive numerical study demonstrating that GM-QAOA, unlike XM-QAOA, exhibits monotonic performance improvement with circuit depth and achieves superior results for HUBO problems. An important component of our approach is an analytical framework for modeling GM-QAOA dynamics, which enables a classical approximation of the optimal parameters and helps reduce the optimization overhead. Our resource-efficient parameterized GM-QAOA nearly matches the performance of the fully optimized algorithm while being far less demanding, establishing it as a highly effective approach for complex optimization tasks. These findings highlight GM-QAOA's potential and provide a practical pathway for its implementation on current quantum hardware.

## LogosQ：Rust中的高性能和类型安全量子计算库

**Original Title:** LogosQ: A High-Performance and Type-Safe Quantum Computing Library in Rust

**Authors:** Shiwen An, Jiayi Wang, Konstantinos Slavakis

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.23183v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.23183v2

**Submitted:** 2025-12-29


### 摘要

由于现有基于Python的框架的动态性质，开发稳健且高性能的量子软件具有挑战性，这些框架经常遭受运行时错误和可扩展性瓶颈。在这项工作中，我们介绍了LogosQ，这是一个在Rust中实现的高性能后台不可知量子计算库，通过编译时类型安全来强制执行正确性。与现有工具不同，LogosQ利用Rust静态分析来消除整个类的运行时错误，特别是在变分算法的参数转移规则梯度计算中。我们引入了新颖的优化技术，包括直接状态载体操纵、自适应并行处理和经过快速傅里叶变换，这些技术共同为状态准备（QFT）提供高达900倍的加速比，为变化工作负载提供2到5倍的加速比Python框架（PennyLane、QisKit），比Julia实现（Yao）提供6到22倍，以及Q夏普具有竞争力的性能。除了性能之外，我们还通过分子氢和XYZ海森堡模型的变分量子本征解算器（VQE）实验来验证数值稳定性，即使在其他库失败的边缘情况下也能实现化学准确性。通过将系统编程的安全性与高级电路优化相结合，LogosQ为可靠有效的量子模拟建立了新标准。


### Abstract

Developing robust and high performance quantum software is challenging due to the dynamic nature of existing Python-based frameworks, which often suffer from runtime errors and scalability bottlenecks. In this work, we present LogosQ, a high performance backend agnostic quantum computing library implemented in Rust that enforces correctness through compile time type safety. Unlike existing tools, LogosQ leverages Rust static analysis to eliminate entire classes of runtime errors, particularly in parameter-shift rule gradient computations for variational algorithms. We introduce novel optimization techniques, including direct state-vector manipulation, adaptive parallel processing, and an FFT optimized Quantum Fourier Transform, which collectively deliver speedups of up to 900 times for state preparation (QFT) and 2 to 5 times for variational workloads over Python frameworks (PennyLane, Qiskit), 6 to 22 times over Julia implementations (Yao), and competitive performance with Q sharp. Beyond performance, we validate numerical stability through variational quantum eigensolver (VQE) experiments on molecular hydrogen and XYZ Heisenberg models, achieving chemical accuracy even in edge cases where other libraries fail. By combining the safety of systems programming with advanced circuit optimization, LogosQ establishes a new standard for reliable and efficient quantum simulation.

## Euler-Korteweg涡旋：薛定格和克莱因-戈登方程的流体力学模拟

**Original Title:** Euler-Korteweg vortices: A fluid-mechanical analogue to the Schrödinger and Klein-Gordon equations

**Authors:** D. M. F. Bischoff van Heemskerck

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.23771v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.23771v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-29


### 摘要

量子理论和相对论与流体力学表现出几种形式上的相似之处。本文探讨了经典流体模型在哪些条件下可以重现这两种理论最基本的数学形式主义。假设音速为c的无粘、正压、恒温流体中无旋涡旋的角动量大小等于约化普朗克常数，并结合Korteweg毛管应力，得到描述Euler-Korteweg涡旋动量和连续性方程的复波动方程。当引入均匀对流时，该波动方程的弱场逼近相当于薛定汉方程。该模型被证明产生了德布罗意波长、爱因斯坦-普朗克关系、博恩规则和不确定性原理的经典类似物。考虑到对流中涡旋波场的延迟传播，就会产生洛伦兹变换和克莱因-戈登方程，薛定格方程出现为低马赫数极限。这些结果表明，在明确的假设下，经典连续体可以以其最简单的形式复制量子和相对论理论的数学形式主义，而无需假设这些理论的主要假设。


### Abstract

Quantum theory and relativity exhibit several formal analogies with fluid mechanics. This paper examines under which conditions a classical fluid model may reproduce the most basic mathematical formalism of both theories. By assuming that the angular momentum of an irrotational vortex in an inviscid, barotropic, isothermal fluid with sound speed c is equal in magnitude to the reduced Planck constant, and incorporating Korteweg capillary stress, a complex wave equation describing the momentum and continuity equations of a Euler-Korteweg vortex is obtained. When uniform convection is introduced, the weak field approximation of this wave equation is equivalent to Schrödinger's equation. The model is shown to yield classical analogues to de Broglie wavelength, the Einstein-Planck relation, the Born rule and the uncertainty principle. Accounting for the retarded propagation of the wavefield of a vortex in convection produces the Lorentz transformation and the Klein-Gordon equation, with Schrödinger's equation appearing as the low-Mach-number limit. These results demonstrate that, under explicit assumptions, a classical continuum can reproduce the mathematical formalism of quantum and relativistic theory in their simplest form, without assuming the postulates principal to those theories.

## DifGA：量子量子光电路中多模式高斯和非高斯噪音的可区分误差抑制

**Original Title:** DifGa: Differentiable Error Mitigation for Multi-Mode Gaussian and Non-Gaussian Noise in Quantum Photonic Circuits

**Authors:** Dennis Delali Kwesi Wayo, Rodrigo Alves Dias, Leonardo Goliatt, Sven Groppe

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.23776v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.23776v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-29


### 摘要

我们引入了DifGA，这是一个完全可微的误差缓解框架，用于在高斯损耗和弱非高斯噪音下工作的连续变量（CV）量子光电路。该方法是使用PennyLane默认的.高斯后台的分析模拟进行演示的，其中量子状态由一阶和二阶矩表示，并通过自动求导进行优化的端到端。高斯损失被建模为分束器与环境真空模式的相互作用为穿透率$n\in [0.3，0.95]$，而非高斯相噪通过随机相旋转与抖动幅度$δ\in [0，0.7]$的可微蒙特卡罗混合来纳入。核心架构采用由信号、辅助和环境模式组成的多模式高斯电路。输入状态是使用参数$（r_s，\varphy_s，a）=（0.60，0.30，0.80）$和$（r_a，\varphy_a）=（0.40，0.10）$的压缩和位移操作来准备的，然后是角度$（0，？）=（0.70，0.20）$的纠缠分束器。通过添加包括局部相旋转和位移的六参数可训练高斯恢复层来实现误差缓解，该恢复层通过使用固定学习率$0.06$的梯度下降和实验中相同的初始化来最小化信号模式四元$上的二次损失来优化。在纯高斯损失下，优化的恢复将重建误差抑制到接近机器精度（$<10 &' s），以中等损失（$n\ge 0.5$）。当存在非高斯相噪时，使用蒙特卡罗平均的噪音感知训练会产生鲁棒的概括，与大相抖动下的高斯训练恢复相比，将误差减少了一个数量级以上。收件箱基准确认了蒙特卡罗样本数量的线性缩放。


### Abstract

We introduce DifGa, a fully differentiable error-mitigation framework for continuous-variable (CV) quantum photonic circuits operating under Gaussian loss and weak non-Gaussian noise. The approach is demonstrated using analytic simulations with the default.gaussian backend of PennyLane, where quantum states are represented by first and second moments and optimized end-to-end via automatic differentiation. Gaussian loss is modeled as a beam splitter interaction with an environmental vacuum mode of transmissivity $η\in [0.3,0.95]$, while non-Gaussian phase noise is incorporated through a differentiable Monte-Carlo mixture of random phase rotations with jitter amplitudes $δ\in [0,0.7]$. The core architecture employs a multi-mode Gaussian circuit consisting of a signal, ancilla, and environment mode. Input states are prepared using squeezing and displacement operations with parameters $(r_s,\varphi_s,α)=(0.60,0.30,0.80)$ and $(r_a,\varphi_a)=(0.40,0.10)$, followed by an entangling beam splitter with angles $(θ,φ)=(0.70,0.20)$. Error mitigation is achieved by appending a six-parameter trainable Gaussian recovery layer comprising local phase rotations and displacements, optimized by minimizing a quadratic loss on the signal-mode quadratures $\langle \hat{x}_0\rangle$ and $\langle \hat{p}_0\rangle$ using gradient descent with fixed learning rate $0.06$ and identical initialization across experiments. Under pure Gaussian loss, the optimized recovery suppresses reconstruction error to near machine precision ($<10^{-30}$) for moderate loss ($η\ge 0.5$). When non-Gaussian phase noise is present, noise-aware training using Monte Carlo averaging yields robust generalization, reducing error by more than an order of magnitude compared to Gaussian-trained recovery at large phase jitter. Runtime benchmarks confirm linear scaling with the number of Monte Carlo samples.

## 规范论量子模拟器中的普遍性打破遇到临界性

**Original Title:** Ergodicity breaking meets criticality in a gauge-theory quantum simulator

**Authors:** Ana Hudomal, Aiden Daniel, Tiago Santiago do Espirito Santo, Milan Kornjača, Tommaso Macrì, Jad C. Halimeh, Guo-Xian Su, Antun Balaž, Zlatko Papić

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.23794v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.23794v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-29


### 摘要

量子模拟的最新进展打开了人们对格点规范理论的实时动力学的了解，为探索量子临界性如何影响远离平衡的热化和历经性提供了一个新的环境。使用QuEra的可编程里德伯原子阵列，我们通过熄灭费米子质量绘制了一维空间上的旋转-1/2 U（1）量子链模型的动态相图。我们揭示了由于量子多体伤痕而引起的各次历经破缺的可调状态，表现为长寿命的相干振荡，其在比之前观察到的更广泛的参数范围内持续存在，包括在平衡相转变点。我们进一步分析了通过Kibble-Zurek机制在状态准备过程中产生的电子-正电子对，它强烈影响了灭后动力学。我们的结果为格点规范理论中的非热动力学提供了新的见解，并建立了里德伯原子阵列作为探索历经破缺和量子临界之间相互作用的强大平台。


### Abstract

Recent advances in quantum simulations have opened access to the real-time dynamics of lattice gauge theories, providing a new setting to explore how quantum criticality influences thermalization and ergodicity far from equilibrium. Using QuEra's programmable Rydberg atom array, we map out the dynamical phase diagram of the spin-1/2 U(1) quantum link model in one spatial dimension by quenching the fermion mass. We reveal a tunable regime of ergodicity breaking due to quantum many-body scars, manifested as long-lived coherent oscillations that persist across a much broader range of parameters than previously observed, including at the equilibrium phase transition point. We further analyze the electron-positron pairs generated during state preparation via the Kibble-Zurek mechanism, which strongly affect the post-quench dynamics. Our results provide new insights into nonthermal dynamics in lattice gauge theories and establish Rydberg atom arrays as a powerful platform for probing the interplay between ergodicity breaking and quantum criticality.

## 逻辑魔状态准备协议的高效模拟

**Original Title:** Efficient simulation of logical magic state preparation protocols

**Authors:** Samyak Surti, Lucas Daguerre, Isaac H. Kim

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.23799v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.23799v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-29


### 摘要

开发空间和时间效率高的逻辑魔状态准备协议可能是构建大规模容错量子计算机的重要一步。出于这一需求，我们引入了一种可扩展的方法，用于在标准电路级噪音模型下模拟逻辑魔状态准备协议。当应用于基于代码交换、魔状态培育和魔状态蒸馏的协议时，我们的方法会产生复杂性多项：（i）量子位的数量和（ii）非稳定性，例如，目标编码魔法状态的稳定器等级或泡利等级。我们模拟方法的效率源于一个奇怪的事实：这些协议中的每个电路级泡利错误最终都会传播为Clifford错误。这一性质由一大类协议来满足，包括那些重复测量与泡利平方的横向Clifford的协议。我们提供了一个原理验证的数值模拟，使用这种逻辑Clifford测量来准备魔法状态。我们的工作能够对逻辑神奇状态准备协议进行实际模拟，而无需诉诸逼近或资源密集型状态载体模拟。


### Abstract

Developing space- and time-efficient logical magic state preparation protocols will likely be an essential step towards building a large-scale fault-tolerant quantum computer. Motivated by this need, we introduce a scalable method for simulating logical magic state preparation protocols under the standard circuit-level noise model. When applied to protocols based on code switching, magic state cultivation, and magic state distillation, our method yields a complexity polynomial in (i) the number of qubits and (ii) the non-stabilizerness, e.g., stabilizer rank or Pauli rank, of the target encoded magic state. The efficiency of our simulation method is rooted in a curious fact: every circuit-level Pauli error in these protocols propagates to a Clifford error at the end. This property is satisfied by a large family of protocols, including those that repeatedly measure a transversal Clifford that squares to a Pauli. We provide a proof-of-principle numerical simulation that prepares a magic state using such logical Clifford measurements. Our work enables practical simulation of logical magic state preparation protocols without resorting to approximations or resource-intensive state-vector simulations.

## 广义迪克状态的Mermin装置

**Original Title:** Mermin Devices for Generalized Dicke States

**Authors:** Roman V. Buniy, Thomas W. Kephart

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.23803v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.23803v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-29


### 摘要

我们在这里给出了许多纠缠态的几个新的精确结果：三个量子位的W-状态及其推广--超过三个量子位的迪克状态。我们通过限制Bell-Mermin运算符的期望值来得出这些结果。我们回顾了三个量子位GHZ Mermin装置，将其推广到四个量子位，然后为三个和四个量子位的广义Dicke状态构建类似的Mermin装置。作为研究它们的操作是否可以完全解释Mermin的指令集的结果，我们表明三个量子位的GZZ和Dicke状态以及四个量子位的GZZ状态不允许这样的描述。然而，在四个量子位的两个广义迪克状态中，一个允许这样的描述，另一个不允许这样的描述。


### Abstract

We present here several new exact results for a number of entangled states: the W-state of three qubits and its generalization -- Dicke states for more than three qubits. We derive these results by bounding the expected values of the Bell-Mermin operators. We review the three qubit GHZ Mermin device, make its generalization to four qubits, and then construct analogous Mermin devices for the generalized Dicke states of three and four qubits. As a result of studying if their operations can be fully explained by Mermin's instructional sets, we show that the GHZ and Dicke states of three qubits and the GHZ state of four qubits do not allow such a description. However, among the two generalized Dicke states of four qubits, one does allow and the other does not allow such a description.

**Authors:** Samyak Surti, Lucas Daguerre, Isaac H. Kim

**Authors:** Patryk Michalski, Andrzej Dragan

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.24424v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.24424v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-30


### 摘要

我们研究区分两个量子波包的可分离和纠缠状态的问题，其中一个量子波包落入黑洞。直观地说，人们可能会认为这两种情况难以区分，因为一个波包携带的信息隐藏在事件视界之外。然而，我们表明，量子状态局部化的根本限制使得这两种情况原则上是可区分的。利用量子状态辨别理论的工具，我们分析了具体实现，并讨论了最大化成功区分这两种情况的可能性的配置。


### Abstract

We investigate the problem of distinguishing between separable and entangled states of two quantum wave packets, one of which falls into a black hole. Intuitively, one might expect the two scenarios to be indistinguishable, since the information carried by one wave packet is hidden beyond the event horizon. We show, however, that fundamental limitations on the localizability of quantum states render the two scenarios, in principle, distinguishable. Employing tools from quantum state discrimination theory, we analyze a concrete realization and discuss the configurations that maximize the probability of successfully distinguishing between the two cases.

## 非埃尔米特场景中的不确定性不等式：指标问题

**Original Title:** Uncertainty inequalities in a non-Hermitian scenario: the problem of the metric

**Authors:** Yanet Alvarez, Mariela Portesi, Romina Ramirez, Marta Reboiro

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.24437v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.24437v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-30


### 摘要

我们研究了在非埃尔米特Hamilton下演变的量子可观测量的不确定性关系，特别强调度量运算符的作用。通过在每个动态机制（即未破对称阶段、对称破对称阶段和异常点）中构建适当的指标，我们在克莱恩空间框架内提供了期望值、方差和时间演变的一致定义。在这种方法中，我们推导出了一个广义的海森堡-罗伯逊不确定不等式，该不等式在所有谱区中都有效。作为应用，我们分析了具有宇称时间反转对称性的两层模型，并表明，虽然不确定性测量在未破碎阶段表现出振荡行为，但它在对称性破碎阶段和异常点向最小不确定性稳态演变。我们进一步将基于指标的描述与Lindblad主方程方法进行比较，并表明它们在稳态下的一致性。我们的结果强调了结合适当的度量结构以从非厄米特量子动力学中提取物理上有意义的预测的必要性。


### Abstract

We investigate uncertainty relations for quantum observables evolving under non-Hermitian Hamiltonians, with particular emphasis on the role of metric operators. By constructing appropriate metrics in each dynamical regime, namely the unbroken-symmetry phase, the broken-symmetry phase, and at exceptional points, we provide a consistent definition of expectation values, variances, and time evolution within a Krein-space framework. Within this approach, we derive a generalized Heisenberg-Robertson uncertainty inequality which is valid across all spectral regimes. As an application, we analyze a two level model with parity-time reversal symmetry and show that, while the uncertainty measure exhibits oscillatory behavior in the unbroken phase, it evolves toward a minimum-uncertainty steady state in the broken symmetry phase and at exceptional points. We further compare our metric-based description with a Lindblad master-equation approach and show their agreement in the steady state. Our results highlight the necessity of incorporating appropriate metric structures to extract physically meaningful predictions from non-Hermitian quantum dynamics.

## Maxwell-Schrödinger数值方法中考虑跨子量子位之间的多量子位交换耦合效应

**Original Title:** Incorporating multi-qubit exchange coupling effects between transmon qubits in Maxwell-Schrödinger numerical methods

**Authors:** Ghazi Khan, Thomas E. Roth

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.24448v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.24448v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-30


### 摘要

超导量子位已成为实现量子计算机的领先平台。这些设备的准确建模对于预测性能、改进设计和优化控制至关重要。目前，许多建模方法依赖于集总电路逼近或其他简化处理，这些处理在解决量子位动力学和电磁电路之间的相互作用方面可能受到限制，有时导致实验与数值预测存在显着偏差。为了解决其中许多局限性，已经开发出了用经典麦克斯韦方程自协调地求解量子位动力学薛定格方程的方法，并被证明可以准确预测与超导量子位控制和读出相关的广泛影响。尽管取得了这些成功，但这些方法还不能考虑引起量子比特-量子比特纠缠的多量子比特效应。在这里，我们通过严格推导如何将transmon量子位之间的多量子位耦合效应嵌入麦克斯韦-薛定谔方法来解决这个问题。为了支持这一点，我们建立在早期的麦克斯韦-薛定谔方法的第一性原理推导的具体情况下，两个transmon量子比特耦合在一起，通过一个共同的电磁系统中的色散制度。为了帮助验证麦克斯韦-薛定谔框架的各个方面，我们还提供了麦克斯韦-薛定谔方法的一个新的解释，作为一种有效的模拟策略，以捕捉类非马尔可夫开放量子系统动力学。我们的结果表明，这些效应可能会引起强烈的经典串烧，从而显着改变多量子位动态，我们为交叉共振门证明了这一点。这些经典的串话效应在交叉共振实验中已经被注意到，但之前的量子理论和器件分析无法解释它们的起源。


### Abstract

Superconducting qubits have emerged as a leading platform for realizing quantum computers. Accurate modeling of these devices is essential for predicting performance, improving design, and optimizing control. Many modeling approaches currently rely on lumped circuit approximations or other simplified treatments that can be limited in resolving the interplay between the qubit dynamics and the electromagnetic circuitry, leading to significant experimental deviations from numerical predictions at times. To address many of these limitations, methods that self-consistently solve the Schrödinger equation for qubit dynamics with the classical Maxwell's equations have been developed and shown to accurately predict a wide range of effects related to superconducting qubit control and readout. Despite these successes, these methods have not been able to consider multi-qubit effects that give rise to qubit-qubit entanglement. Here, we address this by rigorously deriving how multi-qubit coupling effects between transmon qubits can be embedded into Maxwell-Schrödinger methods. To support this, we build on earlier first-principles derivations of Maxwell-Schrödinger methods for the specific case of two transmon qubits coupled together through a common electromagnetic system in the dispersive regime. To aid in validating aspects of the Maxwell-Schrödinger framework, we also provide a new interpretation of Maxwell-Schrödinger methods as an efficient simulation strategy to capture the class of non-Markovian open quantum system dynamics. Our results demonstrate that these effects can give rise to strong classical crosstalk that can significantly alter multi-qubit dynamics, which we demonstrate for the cross-resonance gate. These classical crosstalk effects have been noted in cross-resonance experiments, but previous quantum theory and device analysis could not explain their origin.

## 作为协方差几何的子集合相关性

**Original Title:** Sub-Ensemble Correlations as a Covariance Geometry

**Authors:** Zuoxian Wang, Yuhao Zhang, Gaopu Hou, Zihua Liang, Gen Hu, Lu Liu, Yuan Sun, Feilong Xu, Mao Ye

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.24451v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.24451v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-30


### 摘要

扩散耦合热原子蒸气中空间分辨检测的传统实践隐含地将局部响应视为相互独立的。然而，在这项研究中，表明可观测的相关性是由全球旋转涨落场的内在时空协方差决定的，因此空间分离仅指定重叠的统计投影，而不是独立的物理分量。建立了统一的场论描述，其中子集合被定义为单个随机场的测量引起的统计投影。在此公式中，子系综相关性由协方差运算符确定，从而引入自然几何形状，其中统计独立性对应于测量函式的垂直性。对于由扩散松弛Ornstein-Uhlenbeck随机场描述的集体旋转涨落，协方差谱在有界域中仅允许一组有限的涨落模式，从而对统计上可区分的子集合的数量施加了固有的场级限制。子系综独立性的丧失通过空间采样重叠的概念形式化，该概念量化了因共享访问常见的低级波动模式而产生的不可避免的统计耦合。虽然多通道原子磁力测量提供了一个具体的物理环境，其中这些约束变得明确，但该框架一般适用于扩散耦合随机场。


### Abstract

Conventional practice of spatially resolved detection in diffusion-coupled thermal atomic vapors implicitly treat localized responses as mutually independent. However, in this study, it is shown that observable correlations are governed by the intrinsic spatiotemporal covariance of a global spin-fluctuation field, such that spatial separation specifies only overlapping statistical projections rather than independent physical components. A unified field-theoretic description is established in which sub-ensembles are defined as measurement-induced statistical projections of a single stochastic field. Within this formulation, sub-ensemble correlations are determined by the covariance operator, inducing a natural geometry in which statistical independence corresponds to orthogonality of the measurement functionals. For collective spin fluctuations described by a diffusion-relaxation Ornstein-Uhlenbeck stochastic field, the covariance spectrum admits only a finite set of fluctuation modes in a bounded domain, imposing an intrinsic, field-level limit on the number of statistically distinguishable sub-ensembles. The loss of sub-ensemble independence is formalized through the notion of spatial sampling overlap, which quantifies the unavoidable statistical coupling arising from shared access to common low-order fluctuation modes. While multi-channel atomic magnetometry provides a concrete physical setting in which these constraints become explicit, the framework applies generically to diffusion-coupled stochastic fields.

## 光机系统中通过库伦相互作用增强量子相同步

**Original Title:** Quantum phase synchronisation enhanced via Coulomb interaction in an optomechanical system

**Authors:** E. K. Berinyuy, P. Djorwé, J. -X. Peng, A. Sohail, J. Ghosh, A. -H. Abdel-Aty, S. G. N. Engo, S. K. Singh

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.24454v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.24454v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-30


### 摘要

在这项工作中，我们研究了四模光机系统中量子同步的动力学，重点关注两个机械共振器之间的库伦相互作用的影响。我们分析了库伦耦合对三个不同同步机制的影响，即完整的量子同步、$ph $-同步和量子相同步。我们的结果表明，虽然库伦相互作用通过促进能量交换和相一致性而在显着增强量子相同步方面发挥着关键作用，但它对完全同步和$$-同步影响很小。这表明幅度和频率锁定主要由光学驱动决定，而相对准则严重取决于腔间耦合。我们还证明，通过机械共振器间接耦合的两个光学腔的振荡可以随着时间的推移而对齐，从而导致经典同步。这些发现为控制集体量子动力学提供了一种稳健的机制，并为量子通信、精确传感和同步量子网络开发的应用奠定了基础。


### Abstract

In this work, we investigate the dynamics of quantum synchronization in a four-mode optomechanical system, focusing on the influence of the Coulomb interaction between two mechanical resonators. We analyze the effect of the Coulomb coupling on three distinct synchronization regimes, i.e., complete quantum synchronization, $φ$-synchronization, and quantum phase synchronization. Our results show that while the Coulomb interaction plays a pivotal role in significantly enhancing quantum phase synchronization by facilitating energy exchange and phase coherence, it has little impact on complete and $φ$-synchronization. This indicates that amplitude and frequency locking are primarily determined by the optical driving, whereas phase alignment depends critically on inter-resonator coupling. We also demonstrate that the oscillations of the two optical cavities, which are indirectly coupled via the mechanical resonators, can become aligned over time, resulting in classical synchronization. These findings provide a robust mechanism for controlling collective quantum dynamics and offer a foundation for applications in quantum communication, precision sensing, and the development of synchronized quantum networks.

## 电路QED分散读出的边界条件观点

**Original Title:** A Boundary Condition Perspective on Circuit QED Dispersive Readout

**Authors:** Mustafa Bakr

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.24466v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.24466v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-30


### 摘要

受限几何中的边界条件和量子力学中的测量相互作用具有共同的结构作用：两者都通过确定哪些状态与施加的约束兼容来选择首选基础。本文通过从电路拉格朗日出发的第一性原理推导，发展了电路QED频散读出的这一观点。终止传输线共振器的传输子量子位提供了一个与频率相关的边界条件，其极点结构编码量子位的转变频率;不同的量子位状态产生不同的共振器频率。线性响应和极点主导展开这两种逼近在共振附近有效，将边界函数简化为Sturm-Liouville特征参数中的有理形式。然后，富尔顿-沃尔特谱理论的扩展Hilbert空间为以量子位状态为条件的修饰模式本征值问题提供了框架。频移和真空Rabi分裂由超越本征值方程产生，剩余数通过与分裂匹配来确定：$δ_{ge} = 2Lg#2 ω_q#2/v#4 $，其中$g$是真空Rabi耦合。水平排斥定理保证没有修饰模频率与跨激子转变重合。对于具有匹配频移的两个量子位，奇宇称状态变得频率简并;真正的纯宇称测量需要对线性频移项进行工程抑制。


### Abstract

Boundary conditions in confined geometries and measurement interactions in quantum mechanics share a common structural role: both select a preferred basis by determining which states are compatible with the imposed constraint. This paper develops this perspective for circuit QED dispersive readout through a first-principles derivation starting from the circuit Lagrangian. The transmon qubit terminating a transmission line resonator provides a frequency-dependent boundary condition whose pole structure encodes the qubit's transition frequencies; different qubit states yield different resonator frequencies. Two approximations, linear response and a pole-dominated expansion valid near resonance, reduce the boundary function to a rational form in the Sturm-Liouville eigenparameter. The extended Hilbert space of the Fulton-Walter spectral theory then provides a framework for the dressed-mode eigenvalue problem conditional on the qubit state. The dispersive shift and vacuum Rabi splitting emerge from the transcendental eigenvalue equation, with the residues determined by matching to the splitting: $δ_{ge} = 2Lg^2ω_q^2/v^4$, where $g$ is the vacuum Rabi coupling. A level repulsion theorem guarantees that no dressed mode frequency coincides with a transmon transition. For two qubits with matched dispersive shifts, odd-parity states become frequency-degenerate; true parity-only measurement requires engineered suppression of linear dispersive terms.

## 与激发态量子相变相关的三轴自旋压缩态

**Original Title:** Three-Axis Spin Squeezed States Associated with Excited-State Quantum Phase Transitions

**Authors:** Chon-Fai Kam

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.24472v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.24472v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-30


### 摘要

集体原子系群中的旋转压缩通过非线性相互作用将标准量子极限以下的噪音减少，从而实现量子增强计量。扩展了Kitagawa和Ueda的单轴和两轴扭转范式，我们在各向异性Lipkin-Meshkov-Glick模型中引入了一类一般的三轴旋转压缩态。该模型的特点是方向相关的二次耦合，可以在单轴和双轴区域之间进行插值，并且可以被解释为不对称量子旋转体。使用半经典动力学、Majorana表示和Husimi-Q分布，我们分析了所得状态的结构和可扩展性。三轴框架再现了已知的单轴扭曲的N^（-2/3）标度和双轴扭曲的海森堡限制的N^（-1）标度，同时允许额外的可调谐性和增强的纠缠产生在低自旋系统中。我们进一步表明，调整各向异性参数诱导基态和激发态的量子相变，包括与水平集群和临界动力学的二阶过渡。这些结果统一了自旋压缩，量子临界性和转子类比，并建议在里德伯阵列和腔QED平台的精密传感和量子模拟的实现。


### Abstract

Spin squeezing in collective atomic ensembles enables quantum-enhanced metrology by reducing noise below the standard quantum limit through nonlinear interactions. Extending the one-axis and two-axis twisting paradigms of Kitagawa and Ueda, we introduce a general class of three-axis spin squeezed states within the anisotropic Lipkin-Meshkov-Glick model. The model features direction-dependent quadratic couplings that interpolate between uniaxial and biaxial regimes and can be interpreted as an asymmetric quantum rotor. Using semiclassical dynamics, Majorana representations, and Husimi-Q distributions, we analyze the structure and metrological properties of the resulting states. The three-axis framework reproduces the known N^(-2/3) scaling of one-axis twisting and the Heisenberg-limited N^(-1) scaling of two-axis twisting, while allowing additional tunability and enhanced entanglement generation in low-spin systems. We further show that tuning the anisotropy parameters induces ground-state and excited-state quantum phase transitions, including a second-order transition associated with level clustering and critical dynamics. These results unify spin squeezing, quantum criticality, and rotor analogies, and suggest implementations in Rydberg arrays and cavity-QED platforms for precision sensing and quantum simulation.

### 摘要

我们证明，arXiv：2412.18018中建立的“弦性条件”也是充分的，该条件是通过全息简单树图模型实现的信息。该证明是建设性的，表明arXiv：2512.18702中引入的用于构建满足该条件的给定信息量的简单树图模型实现的算法总是成功。我们强调，这些结果适用于任意数量的方，并且，鉴于任何可由全息图模型实现的信息量也可以至少大致地由稳定器状态实现，它们强调了最初在全息中开发的技术如何能够提供对纠缠和信息理论的广泛见解更一般地，特别是稳定器和量子信息锥的结构。此外，如果arXiv：2204.00075的猜想的强形式成立，即如果所有全息信息量都可以通过（不一定是简单的）树图模型来实现，那么这项工作的结果表明，编码任意数量方的全息信息锥结构的基本数据是次可加性锥的“弦”极端射线集。

## 量子相跳的光谱学：复杂的实时瞬间子的可视化

**Original Title:** Spectroscopy of Quantum Phase Slips: Visualizing Complex Real-Time Instantons

**Authors:** Foster Thompson, Daniel K. J. Boneß, Mark Dykman, Alex Kamenev

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.24495v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.24495v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-30


### 摘要

参数驱动振荡器可以成为下一代量子位的基础。经典地，这些系统表现出两个相相反的稳定振荡状态。量子化后，这些状态变成一对紧密间隔的Floquet状态，可以作为量子位的逻辑基础。然而，与环境的相互作用会引发相滑事件，从而限制量子位的一致性。由于称为量子激活的机制，这种相跳即使在零温度下也会持续存在。与传统的隧道效应相反，量子激活由系统复杂化相空间中的{\em实时}瞬间轨迹来描述。在这项工作中，我们表明相滑率对弱AC扰动呈指数敏感。系统响应的频谱--由所谓的log易感性（LS）捕获--使得可以直接观察实时瞬间子的特征。研究这一光谱提出了有效量子位控制的新方法。


### Abstract

Parametrically driven oscillators can emerge as a basis for the next generation of qubits. Classically, these systems exhibit two stable oscillatory states with opposite phases. Upon quantization, these states turn into a pair of closely spaced Floquet states, which can serve as the logical basis for a qubit. However, interaction with the environment induces phase-slip events which set a limit on qubit coherence. Such phase slips persist even at zero temperature due to a mechanism known as quantum activation \cite{QuantumActivation}. In contrast to conventional tunneling, the quantum activation is described by a {\em real-time} instanton trajectory in the complexified phase space of the system. In this work, we show that the phase-slip rate is exponentially sensitive to weak AC perturbations. The spectrum of the system's response -- captured by the so-called logarithmic susceptibility (LS) -- enables a direct observation of characteristic features of real-time instantons. ## Lindbladian PT相转变

**Original Title:** Lindbladian PT phase transitions

### Abstract

A parity-time (PT) transition is a spectral transition characteristic of non-Hermitian generators; it typically occurs at an exceptional point, where multiple eigenvectors coalesce. The concept of a PT transition has been extended to Markovian open quantum systems, which are described by the GKSL equation. Interestingly, the PT transition in many-body Markovian open quantum systems, the so-called \textit{Lindbladian PT (L-PT) phase transition}, is closely related to two classes of exotic nonequilibrium many-body phenomena: \textit{continuous-time crystals} and \textit{non-reciprocal phase transitions}. In this review, we describe the recent advances in the study of L-PT phase transitions. First, we define PT symmetry in three distinct contexts: non-Hermitian systems, nonlinear dynamical systems, and Markovian open quantum systems, highlighting the interconnections between these frameworks. Second, we develop mean-field theories of L-PT phase transitions for collective-spin systems and for bipartite bosonic systems with particle-number conservation. Within these classes of models, we show that L-PT symmetry can induce a breaking of continuous time-translation symmetry down to a discrete one, leading to persistent periodic dynamics. We further demonstrate that the L-PT phase transition point is typically \textit{a critical exceptional point}, where multiple collective excitation modes with zero excitation spectrum coalesce. These findings establish an explicit connection to continuous-time crystals and non-reciprocal phase transitions. Third, going beyond the mean-field theory, we analyze statistical and quantum properties, such as purity and quantum entanglement indicators of time-independent steady states for several specific models with the L-PT symmetry. Finally, we discuss future research directions for L-PT phase transitions.
