## 具有可调次近邻耦合的四量子位各向同性海森堡XXX链中的么正动力学和资源权衡

**Original Title:** Unitary dynamics and resource trade-offs in a four-qubit isotropic Heisenberg XXX chain with tunable next-nearest-neighbor coupling

**Authors:** Seyed Mohsen Moosavi Khansari

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04429v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.04429v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-06


### 摘要

本研究从Bell型初始状态开始，推导出具有可调次近邻耦合$a $的四量子比特海森堡XXX链的正动力学，并分析了相$ph =（a + 1）t$下量子资源的演变。我们为子系统$12$和$34$提供了保真度$F（p（0），p（t））$、相关性$C_{l_1}（p（t））$和形成的两个量子位纠缠$E_F（t）$的封闭形式表达，所有这些都由$ph $支配。Fidelity表现出周期性行为，$F = \lvert \cos（ph/2）\rvert$，并且冻结状态为$a =-1 $，其中$F \等于1$。一致性遵循$C_{l_1}（p（t））=\sin ' 2（ph/2）$，显示出随着$\lvert a + 1 \rvert$而增加的敏感性，并在$a =-1$时消失。地层的纠缠$E_F（t）$是$ph $的一个熵函数，在$a =-1$处表现出带状振荡和冻结。阶段$统一了所有诊断的行为，将更快的动态与更大的$\lvert a + 1 \rvert$联系起来，并揭示了$（a + 1）t = pi/4 + k pi/2$处的最大灵敏度。这个集成框架为小型量子设备提供了精确的基准，以及通往噪音、有限温度和更大系统扩展的清晰途径。


### Abstract

This study derives the unitary dynamics of a four qubit Heisenberg XXX chain with tunable next nearest neighbor coupling $α$, starting from a Bell type initial state, and analyzes the evolution of quantum resources under the phase $φ= (α+ 1)t$. We provide closed form expressions for fidelity $F(ρ(0),ρ(t))$, coherence $C_{l_1}(ρ(t))$, and two qubit entanglement of formation $E_F(t)$ for subsystems $12$ and $34$, all of which are governed by $φ$. Fidelity exhibits periodic behavior with $F = \lvert \cos(φ/2) \rvert$ and a frozen regime at $α= -1$ where $F \equiv 1$. Coherence follows $C_{l_1}(ρ(t)) = \sin^2(φ/2)$, showing increasing sensitivity with $\lvert α+ 1 \rvert$ and vanishing at $α= -1$. Entanglement of formation $E_F(t)$ is an entropic function of $φ$, displaying banded oscillations and freezing at $α= -1$. The phase $φ$ unifies the behavior of all diagnostics, linking faster dynamics to larger $\lvert α+ 1 \rvert$ and revealing maximal sensitivity at $(α+ 1)t = π/4 + kπ/2$. This integrated framework provides exact benchmarks for small quantum devices and a clear pathway to noise, finite temperature, and larger system extensions.

## 隐形传送下稳定的概率理论的量子可实现性

**Original Title:** Quantum Realizability of Probabilistic Theories Stable under Teleportation

**Authors:** Miguel A. A. Lisboa

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04931v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.04931v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-06


### 摘要

Dmello和Gross的工作给出了在任意轮的量子隐形传态和纠缠交换下CHSH值稳定的广义概率论的分类，它由七个族组成，分别用Klein四群$K_4$，循环群$\mathbb{Z}_4$和二面体群$D_4$的特征标来索引.这些家族中的哪一个承认标准量子力学中的实现的问题尚未解决。在这项工作中，我们彻底解决了这个问题。利用基本表示理论，我们证明了恰好有两个族是可量子实现的，即$xi #{K_4}_{1234}$和$xi #{D_4}_{125}$，而其余五个族不允许量子实现。


### Abstract

The classification of general probabilistic theories (GPTs) whose CHSH value is stable under arbitrary rounds of teleportation and entanglement swapping was obtained in Dmello and Gross work and consists of seven families, indexed by characters of the Klein four-group $K_4$, the cyclic group $\mathbb{Z}_4$, and the dihedral group $D_4$. The question of which of these families admits a realization within standard quantum mechanics was left open. In this work we resolve this question completely. Using elementary representation theory, we prove that exactly two families are quantum-realizable, namely $χ^{K_4}_{1234}$ and $χ^{D_4}_{125}$, while the remaining five admit no quantum realization.

## 门控QKAN-FWP：可扩展的量子启发序列学习

**Original Title:** Gated QKAN-FWP: Scalable Quantum-inspired Sequence Learning

**Authors:** Kuo-Chung Peng, Samuel Yen-Chi Chen, Jiun-Cheng Jiang, Chen-Yu Liu, En-Jui Kuo, Yun-Yuan Wang, Prayag Tiwari, Andrea Ceschini, Chi-Sheng Chen, Yu-Chao Hsu, Chun-Hua Lin, Tai-Yue Li, Antonello Rosato, Massimo Panella, Simon See, Saif Al-Kuwari, Kuan-Cheng Chen, Nan-Yow Chen, Hsi-Sheng Goan

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06734v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.06734v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-07


### 摘要

快速权重编程器（FWP）通过动态更新的参数而不是反复出现的隐藏状态来编码时间依赖性。量子FWP（QFWP）通过变分量子电路（VQC）扩展了这一想法，但现有的实现依赖于多量子位架构，这些架构难以在有噪的中等规模量子（NISQ）设备上扩展，并且经典模拟也很昂贵。我们提出了门控QKAN-FWP，这是一个快速权重框架，将FWP与量子启发的Kolmogorov-Arnold网络（QKAN）集成在一起，使用单量子位数据重新上传电路作为可学习的非线性激活，称为DatA重新加载ActivatioN（DARUAN）。我们进一步引入了一种可稳定参数进化的纯量门控快速权重更新规则，并对其自适应记忆核、几何有界性和可并行梯度路径的理论分析提供了支持。我们跨时间序列基准、MiniGrid强化学习评估框架，并强调现实世界的太阳周期预测是我们的主要实践结果。在输入窗口为528个月和预测范围为132个月的长期环境中，我们的12.5k参数模型实现的均方误差（SSE）、峰值幅度误差和峰值定时误差比一套参数多13倍（包括长短期记忆（LSTM）网络）的经典回归基线更低（25.9k-89.1k参数）、WaveNet-LSTM（167 k）、Vanilla循环神经网络（11.5k）和修改的回声状态网络（132 k）。为了验证NISQ的兼容性，我们进一步在IonQ和IBM Quantum处理器上部署了训练有素的快速编程器，在1024次拍摄时将预测准确性恢复在无噪音模拟器的0.1%相对SSE范围内。这些结果将门控QKAN-FWP定位为一种可扩展、参数高效且与NISQ兼容的量子启发序列建模方法。


### Abstract

Fast Weight Programmers (FWPs) encode temporal dependencies through dynamically updated parameters rather than recurrent hidden states. Quantum FWPs (QFWPs) extend this idea with variational quantum circuits (VQCs), but existing implementations rely on multi-qubit architectures that are difficult to scale on noisy intermediate-scale quantum (NISQ) devices and expensive to simulate classically. We propose gated QKAN-FWP, a fast-weight framework that integrates FWP with Quantum-inspired Kolmogorov-Arnold Network (QKAN) using single-qubit data re-uploading circuits as learnable nonlinear activation, known as DatA Re-Uploading ActivatioN (DARUAN). We further introduce a scalar-gated fast-weight update rule that stabilizes parameter evolution, supported by a theoretical analysis of its adaptive memory kernel, geometric boundedness, and parallelizable gradient paths. We evaluate the framework across time-series benchmarks, MiniGrid reinforcement learning, and highlight real-world solar cycle forecasting as our main practical result. In the long-horizon setting with 528-month input window and 132-month forecast horizon, our 12.5k-parameter model achieves lower scaled Mean Square Error (MSE), peak amplitude error, and peak timing error than a suite of classical recurrent baselines with up to 13x more parameters, including Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks (25.9k-89.1k parameters), WaveNet-LSTM (167k), Vanilla recurrent neural network (11.5k), and a Modified Echo State Network (132k). To validate NISQ compatibility, we further deploy the trained fast programmer on IonQ and IBM Quantum processors, recovering forecasting accuracy within 0.1% relative MSE of the noiseless simulator at 1024 shots. These results position gated QKAN-FWP as a scalable, parameter-efficient, and NISQ-compatible approach to quantum-inspired sequence modeling.

## 用于简并感知量子错误纠正的仿射子码集合解码

**Original Title:** Affine Subcode Ensemble Decoding for Degeneracy-Aware Quantum Error Correction

**Authors:** Leo Wursthorn, Jonathan Mandelbaum, Sisi Miao, Hedongliang Liu, Holger Jäkel, Stergios Koutsioumpas, Laurent Schmalen

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06547v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.06547v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-07


### 摘要

量子低密度Pary-check码是低开销的容错量子计算的有希望的候选者，但众所周知，简并性会损害这些码的信念传播（BP）解码的收敛性。在这项工作中，我们表明将线性独立行添加到稳定器码的检查矩阵可以减少有效退化解的搜索空间。受此启发，我们将最近提出的仿射子码系综解码技术从经典环境扩展到量子环境。此外，我们对每条解码路径采用过完全矩阵。对复曲面码和广义自行车码的蒙特卡罗模拟表明，收敛性得到了改善，逻辑错误率得到了降低。


### Abstract

Quantum low-density parity-check codes are promising candidates for low-overhead fault-tolerant quantum computing, but degeneracy is known to impair the convergence of belief-propagation (BP) decoding of these codes. In this work, we show that appending linearly independent rows to a check matrix of a stabilizer code can reduce the search space for a valid degenerate solution. Motivated by this, we extend the recently proposed affine subcode ensemble decoding technique from the classical to the quantum setting. Moreover, we employ overcomplete matrices for each decoding path. Monte-Carlo simulations on toric and generalized bicycle codes demonstrate improved convergence and reduced logical error rate.

## 具有谷极化量子的巨原子使能量子光学

**Original Title:** Giant-atom-enabled quantum optics with valley-polarized photons

**Authors:** Marcel A. Pinto, Giovanni Luca Sferrazza, Silvia Casulleras, Alejandro Gonzalez-Tudela, Daniele De Bernardis, Francesco Ciccarello

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06771v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.06771v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-07


### 摘要

Valleytronics和Valley Photonics利用山谷自由度来编码和操纵信息。在这里，我们表明，只要它非局部地耦合到场，就可以使用简单的两级发射器在量子光学中选择性地解决量子谷，从而实现所谓的巨原子。具体来说，我们考虑一个量子位在多个点上耦合到具有失调子网格频率的工程蜂窝共振器网格。通过调整耦合点的几何形状，可以使巨型原子选择性地发射到单个谷中。因此，发射的量子获得了明确的谷特征并继承了相关的贝瑞弯曲。通过将量子位放置在相对子格子失调区域之间的域壁附近（其界面支持谷极化边缘模式），发射沿着域壁变成手征。这为实现单量子无序稳健的手征发射提供了一条有希望的途径，而不会破坏电路QED等平台中电磁媒体的时间反对称性。


### Abstract

Valleytronics and valley photonics exploit the valley degree of freedom to encode and manipulate information. Here we show that photonic valleys can be selectively addressed in quantum optics using a simple two-level emitter, provided it is coupled nonlocally to the field, thereby realizing a so-called giant atom. Specifically, we consider a qubit coupled at multiple points to an engineered honeycomb lattice of resonators with detuned sublattice frequencies. By tailoring the geometry of the coupling points, the giant atom can be made to emit selectively into a single valley. The emitted photons thereby acquire a well-defined valley character and inherit the associated Berry curvature. By placing the qubit near a domain wall between regions of opposite sublattice detuning, whose interface supports valley-polarized edge modes, emission becomes chiral along the domain wall. This provides a promising route toward implementation of single-photon disorder-robust chiral emission without breaking time-reversal symmetry of the electromagnetic medium in platforms such as circuit QED.

## 物理启发的QCD分裂函数量子算法

**Original Title:** Physics inspired quantum algorithm for QCD splitting functions

**Authors:** Gabriel Rouxinol, Yacine Haddad, Cenk Tüysüz, Sofia Vallecorsa, Michele Grossi

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06789v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.06789v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-07


### 摘要

我们引入了一个模块化的量子电路原语来模拟QCD部分子分裂中的纠缠动力学，并将其用作数据驱动的物理一致性事件生成的可组合构建块。对于纯胶子通道，我们推导出了分裂顶点处产生的螺旋纠缠的解析表达式，通过并发度量化，并构建了一个两量子比特电路，其测量结果编码了外出胶子之间共享的动量，同时再现了QCD预测的纠缠结构。校准电路参数的大型强子对撞机射流子结构数据图，重建的动量共享分数是直接相关的电路旋转角度。组成多个分裂原语产生多叉动量分数分布，我们验证了三叉和四叉的情况下对实验数据，并找到良好的协议。对于三通配置，我们在超导量子硬件上执行电路，并通过低量子位数和浅电路深度实现标准质量削减后获得与模拟一致的结果。这项工作为量子原生部分簇射模块提供了一个具体框架，该模块在分裂动力学水平上编码量子相关性，并为未来的QCD量子算法提供了基于物理学的解答。


### Abstract

We introduce a modular quantum circuit primitive to model entanglement dynamics in QCD parton splitting and use it as a composable building block for data-driven, physics-consistent event generation. For the pure-gluon channel, we derive an analytic expression for the helicity entanglement generated at the splitting vertex, quantified via the concurrence, and construct a two-qubit circuit whose measurement outcomes encode the momentum shared between outgoing gluons while reproducing the QCD-predicted entanglement structure. Calibrating the circuit parameters to LHC jet substructure data maps, reconstructed momentum-sharing fractions are directly related to circuit rotation angles. Composing multiple splitting primitives yields multi-prong momentum-fraction distributions; we validate the three- and four-prong cases against experimental data and find good agreement. For the three-prong configuration, we execute the circuit on superconducting quantum hardware and obtain results consistent with simulation after standard quality cuts, enabled by the low qubit count and shallow circuit depth. This work provides a concrete framework for quantum-native parton-shower modules that encode quantum correlations at the level of splitting dynamics, and offers physics-informed ansätze for future quantum algorithms for QCD.

## Hamilton模拟中Clifford降噪的中间电路测量

**Original Title:** Mid-Circuit Measurements for Clifford Noise Reduction in Hamiltonian Simulations

**Authors:** James Brown, Jason Iaconis, Yuri Alexeev, Linta Joseph, Spencer Churchill, Kenny Heitritter, William Aguilar-Calvo, Martin Roetteler, Martin Suchara

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06792v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.06792v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-07


### 摘要

费米子Hamilton的量子模拟是量子计算的领先应用，但在当今硬件上的准确执行受到深度Trotter电路中错误积累的限制。我们提出了一种用于编码Hamilton模拟的设备匹配降噪框架，该框架将广义超快编码（GSE）中基于对称穿透的Trotter合成与Clifford降噪（CliNR）和通过中间电路测量实现的Short-style稳定器验证相结合。我们在类似于即将推出的IonQ Tempo系列的Barium开发系统上为六量子位编码的Clifford Trotter步骤实现了这种方法，并使用硬件实验和校准的设备级噪音模型针对直接执行对其进行基准测试。编码的CliNR执行可降低高达54%的逻辑错误率。至关重要的是，当稳定器读出被推迟到电路末端时，这一优势就会消失，这表明及时的中间电路故障检测而不是仅仅验证费用能够推动改进。作为概念证明，我们进一步表明，机器学习引导的稳定器选择可以识别优于随机选择的验证操作符。这些结果表明，编码本地验证与动态电路基元相结合可以极大地改善应用驱动的量子模拟，而无需量子错误纠正的全部费用。


### Abstract

Quantum simulation of fermionic Hamiltonians is a leading application of quantum computing, but accurate execution on present-day hardware is limited by error accumulation in deep Trotter circuits. We present a device-matched noise-reduction framework for encoded Hamiltonian simulation that combines symplectic-transvection-based Trotter synthesis in the Generalized Superfast Encoding (GSE) with Clifford Noise Reduction (CliNR) and Shor-style stabilizer verification enabled by mid-circuit measurement. We implement this approach for a six-qubit encoded Clifford Trotter step on a Barium development system similar to the forthcoming IonQ Tempo line and benchmark it against direct execution using both hardware experiments and a calibrated device-level noise model. The encoded CliNR execution achieves up to 54% lower logical error rate. Crucially, this advantage disappears when stabilizer readout is deferred to the end of the circuit, showing that timely mid-circuit fault detection, rather than verification overhead alone, drives the improvement. As a proof of concept, we further show that machine-learning-guided stabilizer selection can identify verification operators that outperform random choices. These results demonstrate that encoding-native verification combined with dynamic-circuit primitives can materially improve application-motivated quantum simulation without the full overhead of quantum error correction.

## 量子达尔文主义和Petz恢复的质量

**Original Title:** Quantum Darwinism and the quality of Petz recovery

**Authors:** Juha Torvinen, Esko Keski-Vakkuri, Nicola Pranzini

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06848v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.06848v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-07


### 摘要

根据量子达尔文主义，系统与环境的相互作用既选择特定的系统属性，又将它们冗余地编码在环境的许多独立子集（称为片段）中。这种冗余意味着观察者可以通过仅访问一个此类片段来恢复所选信息。然而，进行这种重建的协议通常没有具体说明。考虑与多方环境$ð$交互的系统$γ $，我们研究是否以及在什么条件下可以使用Petz恢复地图从环境碎片中恢复$γ $的一个选定状态。我们表明，系统的初始状态和通过Petz恢复重建的状态之间的保真度随着碎片大小的变化而发展出平台。我们的结果得到了大型但易于处理的模型的分析论点和数值模拟的支持。


### Abstract

According to Quantum Darwinism, system-environment interactions both einselect particular system properties and encode them redundantly in many independent subsets of the environment, called fragments. This redundancy implies that an observer can recover the einselected information by accessing just one such fragment. However, the protocol by which such reconstruction should occur is often left unspecified. Considering a system $Γ$ interacting with a multipartite environment $Ξ$, we investigate whether, and under what conditions, the einselected state of $Γ$ can be recovered from environmental fragments using the Petz recovery map. We show that the fidelity between the system's initial state and the state reconstructed via Petz recovery develops a plateau as a function of the fragment size. Our results are supported by both analytical arguments and numerical simulations of large but tractable models.

## 追踪量子生存幅度的复零点：能量分布如何控制动态相转变

**Original Title:** Tracing complex zeros of the quantum survival amplitude: How the energy distribution controls dynamical phase transitions

**Authors:** Jakub Novotný, Jan Střeleček, Pavel Stránský, Pavel Cejnar

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06849v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.06849v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-07


### 摘要

受动态量子相转变（DQPTs）进步的启发，我们分析了有限量子系统中复时生存（LoschSYS）幅度的零点，并基于全纯函数零点的稳定性开发了其逼近的一般框架。我们表明，零点分布的大规模性质由初始状态能量分布的信封决定，并且可以由与其主要贡献相关的周期零点链构建。在这张图中，当两个或更多本征态在包封的最大值处均匀填充时，零到达实时轴，从而提供了有限大小的DQPT前身。我们将该方法应用于具有可调相互作用范围的伊辛模型中的激冷态，并证明了零点的近似和精确分布之间的密切一致。我们证明了两带模型中BCCS接地激灭的近似构造变得精确。为了描述短期动态，我们引入了具有几乎等距光谱的最小高斯模型。缓慢去相连续地将初始零模式变形为渐进的两级结构，将异常DQPT解释为零延迟接近实时轴。我们的结果将能量包封确定为塑造动态临界行为的关键因素，并提供了复时间生存幅度的整个零分布的普遍解释。


### Abstract

Motivated by the advance of dynamical quantum phase transitions (DQPTs), we analyze the zeros of the complex-time survival (Loschmidt) amplitude in finite quantum systems and develop a general framework for their approximation based on the stability of zeros of holomorphic functions. We show that the large-scale properties of the distribution of zeros are governed by the envelope of the energy distribution of the initial state and can be constructed from chains of periodic zeros associated with its dominant contributions. In this picture, zeros reach the real-time axis when two or more eigenstates become equally populated at the maximum of the envelope, providing a finite-size precursor of DQPTs. We apply the method to quenched ground states in the Ising model with tunable interaction range and demonstrate close agreement between the approximate and exact distributions of zeros. We prove that the approximate construction becomes exact for BCS ground-state quenches in two-band models. To describe short-time dynamics, we introduce a minimal Gaussian model with a nearly equidistant spectrum. Slow dephasing continuously deforms the initial zero pattern into the asymptotic two-level structure, explaining anomalous DQPTs as a delayed approach of zeros to the real-time axis. Our results identify the energy envelope as the key ingredient shaping dynamical critical behavior and provide a universal interpretation of the whole zero distribution of the complex-time survival amplitude.

## 量子安妮：优化、采样和多体动力学

**Original Title:** Quantum Annealing: Optimisation, Sampling, and Many-Body Dynamics

**Authors:** Steven Abel, Andrei Constantin, Luca A. Nutricati

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06857v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.06857v1

Here, we study sparse signaling over lossy thermal-noise bosonic channels, which describe quantumly many practical channels (including optical, microwave, and radio-frequency). We characterize the input signal state that minimizes detectability. We find an unintuitive optimal quantum state structure: a mixture of just two consecutive photon-number states. In particular, in the low-brightness regime, the optimal signal state is a mixture of vacuum and a single photon. Since these states are generally suboptimal for both communication and active sensing, we explore the resulting trade-off and identify input-power thresholds for transitions between optimizing for covertness vs. performance in communication and sensing tasks.

## 解锁真空纠缠

**Original Title:** Unlocking vacuum entanglement

**Authors:** Andrew Steane, Haru Ishizaka

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.08076v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.08076v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-08


### 摘要

研究了调和链的基本纠缠结构。确定了一类对此有用的双模式压缩状态。在追踪到中心后，链两端的局部模的纠缠随着链长度的增加迅速降至零。然而，如果测量中心模，并将结果传递给与外模相互作用的系统，则后者表现出大大增强的纠缠，包括在没有其他方式可用的情况下。这些想法可以在俘获离子等系统的实验中得到证实。对连续情况的扩展产生了从玻色子量子场的真空状态提取的增强的纠缠。


### Abstract

The structure of entanglement in the ground state of the harmonic chain is studied. A class of two-mode squeezed states, useful for this purpose, is identified. The entanglement of the local modes at the ends of the chain, after tracing out the centre, rapidly falls to zero as the length of the chain increases. However, if the central modes are measured, and the result communicated to systems interacting with the outer modes, the latter exhibit greatly enhanced entanglement, including in conditions where none was otherwise available. These ideas can be demonstrated in experiments in trapped ions, among other systems. The extension to the continuous case yields enhanced entanglement extracted from the vacuum state of a bosonic quantum field.

## 玻色子系统量子学习理论的进展

**Original Title:** Advances in quantum learning theory with bosonic systems

**Authors:** Francesco Anna Mele

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.08082v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.08082v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-08


### 摘要

本文回顾了连续变量（CV）系统量子学习理论的最新进展。量子学习理论研究如何尽可能有效地从量子系统中提取经典信息。CV系统在自然界和量子技术中无处不在，因为它们描述了玻色子和量子光学系统。虽然有限维系统的量子学习理论已经得到了广泛的研究，但CV系统的相应理论最近才开始发展;在这里我们提供了一个简洁的回顾。我们重点关注以下问题：学习非高斯状态（可能是在能量限制下）所需的最小副本数（样本复杂度）是多少？学习高斯状态的样本复杂性是多少？CV状态学习的性能如何取决于非高斯性？如何测试一个状态是高斯状态还是远离高斯状态集？如何有效地学习高斯过程？对于这些主题来说，我们还根据协方差矩阵回顾了CV状态之间轨迹距离的几个界限，这可能是独立的兴趣。总体而言，这项工作总结了CV系统断层扫描的选定发展，并强调了一系列悬而未决的问题。


### Abstract

This paper reviews recent advances in quantum learning theory for continuous-variable (CV) systems. Quantum learning theory investigates how to extract classical information from quantum systems as efficiently as possible. CV systems are ubiquitous in nature and in quantum technologies, as they describe bosonic and quantum-optical systems. While quantum learning theory for finite-dimensional systems has been extensively studied, the corresponding theory for CV systems has only recently begun to develop; here we provide a concise review. We focus on the following questions: what is the minimum number of copies (the sample complexity) required to learn a non-Gaussian state, possibly under energy constraints? What is the sample complexity for learning Gaussian states? How does the performance of CV state learning depend on non-Gaussianity? How can one test whether a state is Gaussian or far from the set of Gaussian states? And how can Gaussian processes be learned efficiently? Central to these topics, we also review several bounds on the trace distance between CV states in terms of their covariance matrices, which may be of independent interest. Overall, this work summarises selected developments in tomography of CV systems and highlights a selection of open problems.
