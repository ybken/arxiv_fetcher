## 平均场区域中电子气的集体行为

**Original Title:** Collective behaviors of an electron gas in the mean-field regime

**Authors:** Dong Hao Ou Yang

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.24666v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.24666v2

**Submitted:** 2025-12-31


### 摘要

本文研究了3维环中电子气的动量分布。目标是计算通过随机相逼近获得的某些试验状态的傅里叶模式的占据数。我们得到了[Daniel and Voskov，Phys. Rev. \textbf{120}，（1960）]和[Lam，Phys. Rev. \textbf{3}，（1971）]中在高密度极限下电子气动量分布公式的平均场近似。本文中的分析主要基于工作[Christiansen，Hainzl，Nam，Comm. Math. Phys. \textbf{401}，（2023）]。我们的发现与Benedikter、Lill和Naidu独立获得的最近结果相关，该分析适用于一般一类奇异势，而不仅仅是Coulomb情况。


### Abstract

In this paper, we study the momentum distribution of an electron gas in a $3$-dimensional torus. The goal is to compute the occupation number of Fourier modes for some trial state obtained through random phase approximation. We obtain the mean-field analogue of momentum distribution formulas for electron gas in [Daniel and Voskov, Phys. Rev. \textbf{120}, (1960)] in high density limit and [Lam, Phys. Rev. \textbf{3}, (1971)] at metallic density. The analysis in the present paper is majorly based on the work [Christiansen, Hainzl, Nam, Comm. Math. Phys. \textbf{401}, (2023)]. Our findings are related to recent results obtained independently by Benedikter, Lill and Naidu, and the analysis applies to a general class of singular potentials rather than just the Coulomb case.

## 非埃尔米特基塔耶夫链中的对称性和布局

**Original Title:** Symmetry and Topology in the Non-Hermitian Kitaev chain

**Authors:** Ayush Raj, Soham Ray, Sai Satyam Samal

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.00951v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.00951v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-02


### 摘要

我们研究了具有非倒数跳变幅度和不对称超对的非埃尔米特基塔耶夫链。我们计算出模型的对称结构，并表明粒子-洞对称性（PHS）在整个参数范围内都得到了保留。由于PHS，有限开链的拓扑相点与周期（无限）系统的拓扑相点重合。通过显式地构造零能量波函数（马约拉纳模式），我们表明马约拉纳模式必然会作为在相反边界上积累的相互局部化对发生，其组合概率密度表现出零能量模式的非厄米特皮肤效应的精确抵消。相比之下，激发状态通常显示出集肤效应局部化，粒子和空洞成分在系统的相对两端积累。在体拓学层面，我们进一步在限制参数区域中构造了一个$\mathbb{Z}_2$的拓扑不变量，该变量能够正确区分拓扑相和平凡相。最后，我们在广泛的复杂参数范围内呈现了非埃尔米特基塔耶夫链的拓相图，并描绘了相关的相边界。


### Abstract

We investigate the non-Hermitian Kitaev chain with non-reciprocal hopping amplitudes and asymmetric superconducting pairing. We work out the symmetry structure of the model and show that particle-hole symmetry (PHS) is preserved throughout the entire parameter regime. As a consequence of PHS, the topological phase transition point of a finite open chain coincides with that of the periodic (infinite) system. By explicitly constructing the zero-energy wave functions (Majorana modes), we show that Majorana modes necessarily occur as reciprocal localization pairs accumulating on opposite boundaries, whose combined probability density exhibits an exact cancellation of the non-Hermitian skin effect for the zero energy modes. Excited states, by contrast, generically display skin-effect localization, with particle and hole components accumulating at opposite ends of the system. At the level of bulk topology, we further construct a $\mathbb{Z}_2$ topological invariant in restricted parameter regimes that correctly distinguishes the topological and trivial phases. Finally, we present the topological phase diagram of the non-Hermitian Kitaev chain across a broad range of complex parameters and delineate the associated phase boundaries.

## 一致时间的传统图景完整吗？暗物质同同性

**Original Title:** Is the Conventional Picture of Coherence Time Complete? Dark Matter Recoherence

**Authors:** Chaitanya Paranjape, Gilad Perez, Wolfram Ratzinger, Somasundaram Sankaranarayanan

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.00955v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.00955v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-02


### 摘要

局部太阳引力势形成了一个超轻暗物质（ULDM）的盆地，具有离散的能级。即使几乎没有填充，它也在DM动态中引入了一个新的特征时间尺度。这就需要对相干时间的概念进行推广。我们发现，在很长一段时间内，出现的现象，即ULDM的一个子组件表现出正式发散的相干时间。这个广义相干时间可以显著超过朴素估计的事实意味着在延长的观测期内积累数据的暗物质搜索的灵敏度增强。


### Abstract

The local solar gravitational potential forms a basin for ultralight dark matter (ULDM), with discrete energy levels. Even if barely populated, it introduces a new characteristic timescale in DM dynamics. This necessitates a generalization of the notion of coherence time. We find that, at long times, the phenomenon of recoherence emerges, whereby a subcomponent of ULDM exhibits a formally divergent coherence time. The fact that this generalized coherence time can significantly exceed the naive estimate implies an enhanced sensitivity for dark matter searches that accumulate data over extended observation periods.

## 高效量子点源的四光干涉

**Original Title:** Four-Photon Interference with a High-Efficiency Quantum Dot Source

**Authors:** Alistair J. Brash, Luke Brunswick, Mark R. Hogg, Catherine L. Phillips, Malwina A. Marczak, Timon L. Baltisberger, Sascha R. Valentin, Arne Ludwig, Richard J. Warburton

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.00966v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.00966v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-02


### 摘要

虽然双量子Hong-Ou-Mandel干涉可见性已成为单量子源的标准指标，但许多光学量子技术需要产生和操纵更大的光态。迄今为止，效率限制阻碍了将基于量子点的干涉扩展到单个分束器处两个以上的光合并。我们通过将最先进的量子点源与确定性解多路分解相结合来克服这一限制，从而能够直接观察由多达四个量子产生的量子干涉条纹。我们测量到两个光的高平均干涉对比度为93.0美元0.1~ %，四个光的高平均干涉对比度为84.1美元1.0~ %，复杂的条纹结构通过理论模型完全再现。这些结果揭示了“深条纹”的存在，其最小值不受可区分的量子的影响，使得四量子干涉的最大对比度对多量子发射高度敏感，但对量子可区分性很强。我们预测，这些现象将扩展到更大量量子的干涉，与一系列潜在的光量子技术相关。费舍尔信息分析表明，来自我们光源的干涉条纹可以表现出超出标准量子极限的相灵敏度，说明了量子计量学中的潜在应用。


### Abstract

While two-photon Hong-Ou-Mandel interference visibility has become a standard metric for single-photon sources, many optical quantum technologies require the generation and manipulation of larger photonic states. To date, efficiency limitations have prevented scaling quantum dot-based interference to the coalescence of more than two photons at a single beamsplitter. We overcome this limitation by combining a state-of-the-art quantum dot source with deterministic demultiplexing, enabling the direct observation of quantum interference fringes arising from up to four photons. We measure high mean interference contrasts of $93.0 \pm 0.1~\%$ for two photons, and $84.1 \pm 1.0~\%$ for four photons, with the complex fringe structure fully reproduced by a theoretical model. These results reveal the existence of "deep fringes" whose minima are unaffected by distinguishable photons, rendering the maximum contrast of four-photon interference highly sensitive to multi-photon emission but robust against photon distinguishability. We predict that these phenomena will extend to interference of larger numbers of photons, with relevance across a range of potential optical quantum technologies. A Fisher information analysis demonstrates that interference fringes from our source can exhibit phase sensitivity beyond the standard quantum limit, illustrating potential applications in quantum metrology.

## 独立位翻转和相翻转误差下表面码的改进解码算法

**Original Title:** Improved decoding algorithms for surface codes under independent bit-flip and phase-flip errors

**Authors:** Louay Bazzi

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.00972v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.00972v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-02


### 摘要

我们研究了标准独立\（X/Z\）噪音模型下复曲面码以及平面和旋转表面码的精确解码，重点关注分离最小权重（SMW）解码和分离最可能陪集（SMLC）解码。对于SMW解码问题，我们表明，对于表面码和复曲面码来说，\（O（n）\log n）\）时间解码器是可以实现的，比基于完整解码图的标准方法的\（O（n）\log n）\）最坏情况时间有所改进。我们的方法基于使用Fisher小工具将SMW解码局部归约为最小权重完美匹配问题，它保留了平面和旋转表面码的平面性，并保留了复曲面码的亏格~\（1\）。这种简化使得可以使用Lipton--Tarjan平面分离器方法，并暗示SMW解码位于\（\mathrm{NC}\）中。对于SMLC解码，我们表明平面代码允许具有\（O（n））代数复杂性的精确解码器，并且问题在于\（\mathrm{NC}\），比Bravyi的\（O（n）\）代数复杂性有所提高我们的方法通过陪集概率的双循环公式进行，并使用Fisher--Kasteleyn--Temperley结构显式简化平面Pfloan评估。相同的复杂性指标适用于旋转表面代码的SMLC解码。对于复曲面码，我们得到了一个具有\（O（n^{3}）\）代数复杂度的精确的多项时间SMLC解码器。此外，虽然SMLC制定的动机是连接到统计力学，我们提供了一个纯粹的代数推导的基础上MacWilliams对偶和傅立叶分析的对偶性。最后，我们讨论了扩展的框架去极化噪声模型，并确定由此产生的开放问题。


### Abstract

We study exact decoding for the toric code and for planar and rotated surface codes under the standard independent \(X/Z\) noise model, focusing on Separate Minimum Weight (SMW) decoding and Separate Most Likely Coset (SMLC) decoding. For the SMW decoding problem, we show that an \(O(n^{3/2}\log n)\)-time decoder is achievable for surface and toric codes, improving over the \(O(n^{3}\log n)\) worst-case time of the standard approach based on complete decoding graphs. Our approach is based on a local reduction of SMW decoding to the minimum weight perfect matching problem using Fisher gadgets, which preserves planarity for planar and rotated surface codes and genus~\(1\) for the toric code. This reduction enables the use of Lipton--Tarjan planar separator methods and implies that SMW decoding lies in \(\mathrm{NC}\). For SMLC decoding, we show that the planar surface code admits an exact decoder with \(O(n^{3/2})\) algebraic complexity and that the problem lies in \(\mathrm{NC}\), improving over the \(O(n^{2})\) algebraic complexity of Bravyi \emph{et al.} Our approach proceeds via a dual-cycle formulation of coset probabilities and an explicit reduction to planar Pfaffian evaluation using Fisher--Kasteleyn--Temperley constructions. The same complexity measures apply to SMLC decoding of the rotated surface code. For the toric code, we obtain an exact polynomial-time SMLC decoder with \(O(n^{3})\) algebraic complexity. In addition, while the SMLC formulation is motivated by connections to statistical mechanics, we provide a purely algebraic derivation of the underlying duality based on MacWilliams duality and Fourier analysis. Finally, we discuss extensions of the framework to the depolarizing noise model and identify resulting open problems.

## 蓝宝石上硅超颖表面产生中性原子的暗阱和亮阱阵列

**Authors:** Chengyu Fang, Minjeong Kim, Hongyan Mei, Xuting Yang, Zhaoning Yu, Yuzhe Xiao, Sanket Deshpande, Preston Huft, Alan M. Dibos, David A. Czaplewski, Mark Saffman, Jennifer T. Choy, Mikhail A. Kats

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.01038v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-03


### 摘要

我们展示了晶体蓝宝石上硅（c-SOS）元表面，它将高斯束转换为复杂的光学陷阱阵列，包括与明亮镊子阵列交错的黑暗区域中捕获原子的光学瓶束阵列。晶体硅的高折射率和间接带间隙使得设计高分辨率近红外（$A>700$纳米）元表面成为可能，可以使用与MOS兼容的工艺大规模制造。与已广泛用于生成陷阱阵列的空间光调制器（STM）等主动元件相比，元表面提供了无限可扩展的像素数量，从而能够以非常小的形状因子形成复杂陷阱的大型阵列，并降低了动态噪音。为了设计可以生成三维瓶形束作为暗陷阱的元表面，我们修改了Gerchberg-Saxton算法，以在元表面的焦平面上实施复幅度轮廓，并优化整个阵列陷阱的均匀性。我们制造并测量了c-SOS元表面，将高斯激光束转换为亮陷阱、暗陷阱和交错明/暗陷阱阵列。


### Abstract

We demonstrated crystalline silicon-on-sapphire (c-SOS) metasurfaces that convert a Gaussian beam into arrays of complex optical traps, including arrays of optical bottle beams that trap atoms in dark regions interleaved with bright tweezer arrays. The high refractive index and indirect band gap of crystalline silicon makes it possible to design high-resolution near-infrared ($λ>700$ nm) metasurfaces that can be manufactured at scale using CMOS-compatible processes. Compared with active components like spatial light modulators (SLMs) that have become widely used to generate trap arrays, metasurfaces provide an indefinitely scalable number of pixels, enabling large arrays of complex traps in a very small form factor, as well as reduced dynamic noise. To design metasurfaces that can generate three-dimensional bottle beams to serve as dark traps, we modified the Gerchberg-Saxton algorithm to enforce complex-amplitude profiles at the focal plane of the metasurface and to optimize the uniformity of the traps across the array. We fabricated and measured c-SOS metasurfaces that convert a Gaussian laser beam into arrays of bright traps, dark traps, and interleaved bright/dark traps.

## 牛顿和蒙德启发引力势中玻色-爱因斯坦凝聚体的底态和集体模

**Original Title:** Ground State and Collective Modes of Bose-Einstein Condensates in Newtonian and MOND-inspired gravitational potentials

**Authors:** Ning Liu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.01039v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.01039v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-03


### 摘要

我们分析和数值研究了玻色-爱因斯坦凝聚体在两个陷阱中的底价和集体动力学：牛顿势和受修正牛顿动力学（MOND）启发的对数势。在底价下，MOND势仅在深MOND体系中支持束缚态，此时凝聚物变得比牛顿对应物显着大。在两个势中，尺寸随着排斥耦合参数$β$的增加而增加。在MOND情况下出现了$β '#$大小的明确标度定律，并在很宽的参数范围内得到了数字证实，而对于牛顿势来说，由于托马斯-费米逼近不再有效，因此不存在简单的标度定律。对于动力学，我们推导并求解子集体模式的方程。较大的MOND束缚凝结物以较低的频率振荡，在强相互作用极限下，其缩放为$β #+{-1/3}$。这些标度定律为旨在探索冷原子修改重力场景的量子模拟实验提供了见解。


### Abstract

We analytically and numerically study the ground state and collective dynamics of Bose-Einstein condensates in two traps: a Newtonian potential and a logarithmic potential inspired by Modified Newtonian Dynamics (MOND). In the ground state, the MOND potential supports bound states only in the deep-MOND regime, where the condensate becomes significantly larger than its Newtonian counterpart. The size increases with repulsive coupling parameter $β$ in both potentials. A clear scaling law of the size with $β^{1/3}$ emerges in the MOND case and is confirmed numerically over a wide parameter range, while for the Newtonian potential no simple scaling law exists as the Thomas-Fermi approximation ceases to be valid. For the dynamics, we derive and solve equations for the monopole collective mode. The larger MOND-bound condensate oscillates at a lower frequency, which scales as $β^{-1/3}$ in the strong-interaction limit. These scaling laws provide insights for quantum-simulation experiments aiming to probe modified-gravity scenarios with cold atoms.

## 在经典通道上模拟量子秘密

**Original Title:** Impersonating Quantum Secrets over Classical Channels

**Authors:** Luowen Qian, Mark Zhandry

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.01058v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.01058v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-03


### 摘要

我们表明，一个简单的窃听者监听潜在纠缠的量子方之间的经典通信，最终将能够模仿任何一方。此外，如果单向谜题不存在，则攻击是有效的。作为直接结果，具有潜在演变的量子预共享秘密的经典通道上的可重复使用的认证方案隐含了单向谜题。   作为一个额外的应用，我们表明，任何只能通过对任何Oracle的经典查询来验证的量子货币方案都不可能是信息理论上安全的。这极大地概括了Ananth、Hu和Yuen的先前工作（ASIACRYPT ' 23），他们表现出相同的结果，但仅针对随机先知的特定情况。因此，验证量子货币的黑匣子结构本质上需要连贯地评估底层加密工具，这对于短期量子设备来说可能很困难。


### Abstract

We show that a simple eavesdropper listening in on classical communication between potentially entangled quantum parties will eventually be able to impersonate any of the parties. Furthermore, the attack is efficient if one-way puzzles do not exist. As a direct consequence, one-way puzzles are implied by reusable authentication schemes over classical channels with quantum pre-shared secrets that are potentially evolving.
  As an additional application, we show that any quantum money scheme that can be verified through only classical queries to any oracle cannot be information-theoretically secure. This significantly generalizes the prior work by Ananth, Hu, and Yuen (ASIACRYPT'23) where they showed the same but only for the specific case of random oracles. Therefore, verifying black-box constructions of quantum money inherently requires coherently evaluating the underlying cryptographic tools, which may be difficult for near-term quantum devices.

## 莫尔查诺夫公式和量子行走：概率方法

**Original Title:** Molchanov's Formula and Quantum Walks: A Probabilistic Approach

**Authors:** Hoang Vu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.01071v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.01071v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-03


### 摘要

本文通过推导连续时间和离散时间量子游动的概率表示，在量子动力学和经典动力学之间建立了鲁棒的联系。我们首先采用最初用于研究多维整格上薛定格操作的莫尔查诺夫公式来描述连续时间量子行走的演变。扩展了这个框架，我们开发了一种概率方法来表示无限整线上的离散时间量子行走，绕过了通常禁止直接应用莫尔查诺夫公式的局部性限制。通过对阿达玛步行的基准分析，我们的代表的有效性得到了经验上的证实，证明了传统单一进化的高保真度。我们的结果表明，这种概率镜头为学习多维量子行走提供了一个强大的替代方案，并为通过经典随机过程研究量子系统提供了新的分析途径。


### Abstract

This paper establishes a robust link between quantum dynamics and classical ones by deriving probabilistic representation for both continuous time and discrete time quantum walks. We first adapt Molchanov formula, originally employed in the study of Schrodinger operators on multidimensional integer lattice, to characterize the evolution of continuous time quantum walks. Extending this framework, we develop a probabilistic method to represent discrete time quantum walks on an infinite integer line, bypassing the locality constraints that typically inhibit direct application of Molchanov formula. The validity of our representation is empirically confirmed through a benchmark analysis of the Hadamard walk, demonstrating high fidelity with traditional unitary evolution. Our results suggest that this probabilistic lens offer a powerful alternative for learning multidimensional quantum walks and provides new analytical pathways for investigating quantum systems via classical stochastic processes.

## 使用猫状态量子位的多方W状态的一步混合CV-DV转移

**Original Title:** Single-Step Hybrid CV-DV Transfer of Multipartite W States Using Cat-State Qubits

**Authors:** Muhammad Nehal Khan, Sumrah Hussain

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.01078v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.01078v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-03


### 摘要

我们提出了一种确定性混合连续变量-离散变量（CV-DV）方案，用于在电路QED架构内对以光Schr$\ddot{o}$dinger猫状态量子位编码的$n$-量子位W状态进行一步传输。逻辑量子位被编码在玻色子模的偶数和奇数宇称猫状态中，而共振器对之间的有效拉曼型相互作用是由在频散区工作的单个超导通量四重奏介导的。该协议在单个集体操作中连贯地传输多方W状态，而无需填充更高的激发原子级，从而强烈抑制退散。基于完整Lindblad主方程的数值模拟，包括现实的腔体消散、qutrit松弛和去相以及腔间串烧，表明三量子位猫态W状态可以以大约0.92美元的最大保真度进行转移。这些结果证明了使用当前电路QED技术进行可扩展混合CV-DV纠缠转移的可行性。


### Abstract

We propose a deterministic hybrid continuous-variable-discrete-variable (CV-DV) scheme for the single-step transfer of an $n$-qubit W state encoded in photonic Schr$\ddot{o}$dinger cat-state qubits within a circuit QED architecture. Logical qubits are encoded in even- and odd-parity cat states of bosonic modes, while effective Raman-type interactions between resonator pairs are mediated by a single superconducting flux qutrit operating in the dispersive regime. The protocol coherently transfers the multipartite W state in a single collective operation without populating higher excited atomic levels, thereby strongly suppressing decoherence. Numerical simulations based on the full Lindblad master equation, including realistic cavity dissipation, qutrit relaxation and dephasing, and inter-cavity crosstalk, show that a three-qubit cat-state W state can be transferred with a maximum fidelity of approximately $0.92$. These results demonstrate the feasibility of scalable hybrid CV-DV entanglement transfer using current circuit QED technology.

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.01104v1
