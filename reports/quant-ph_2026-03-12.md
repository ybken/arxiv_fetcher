## 正式启动具有1，000+个量子位的量子相估计电路

**Original Title:** Formally Verifying Quantum Phase Estimation Circuits with 1,000+ Qubits

**Authors:** Arun Govindankutty, Sudarshan K. Srinivasan

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.08762v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.08762v2

**Submitted:** 2026-03-09


### 摘要

我们提出了一种用于量子阶段估计（QPE）电路的可扩展形式验证方法。我们的方法使用基于无量化器的位向逻辑的符号量子位抽象，捕捉关键的量子现象，包括叠加、旋转和测量。所提出的方法将量子电路功能行为从希尔伯特空间映射到位向域。我们开发与此抽象一致的形式属性，以确保QPE电路的功能正确性。该方法可高效扩展，使用不到3.5~GB的存储器验证具有高达6个精度量子位和1，024个相量子位的QPE电路。


### Abstract

We present a scalable formal verification methodology for Quantum Phase Estimation (QPE) circuits. Our approach uses a symbolic qubit abstraction based on quantifier-free bit-vector logic, capturing key quantum phenomena, including superposition, rotation, and measurement. The proposed methodology maps quantum circuit functional behaviour from Hilbert space to a bit-vector domain. We develop formal properties aligned with this abstraction to ensure functional correctness of QPE circuits. The method scales efficiently, verifying QPE circuits with up to 6 precision qubits and 1,024 phase qubits using under 3.5~GB of memory.

## 信息几何中利玛窦弯曲的量化

**Original Title:** Quantization of Ricci Curvature in Information Geometry

**Authors:** Carlos C. Rodriguez

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10054v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.10054v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-09


### 摘要

2004年，在研究二进制Bayesian网络（Bitnets）的信息几何时，作者推测，相对于Fisher信息度量计算的体积平均Ricci纯量<R>普遍量化为正半整：<R>在（1/2）Z中。本文时隔20年解决了这个猜想。我们通过通用Beta函数消除机制证明了树结构和完全图位网的这一点，并通过展示显式循环反例来总体上证明了这一点。   我们将该程序扩展到高斯DAB网络，其中符号二分法成立：离散位网络具有正弯曲，而高斯网络形成具有负弯曲的可解李群。


### Abstract

In 2004, while studying the information geometry of binary Bayesian networks (bitnets), the author conjectured that the volume-averaged Ricci scalar <R> computed with respect to the Fisher information metric is universally quantized to positive half-integers: <R> in (1/2)Z. This paper resolves the conjecture after 20 years. We prove it for tree-structured and complete-graph bitnets via a universal Beta function cancellation mechanism, and disprove it in general by exhibiting explicit loop counterexamples.
  We extend the program to Gaussian DAG networks, where a sign dichotomy holds: discrete bitnets have positive curvature, while Gaussian networks form solvable Lie groups with negative curvature.

## 四个否定和光谱预层

**Original Title:** Four negations and the spectral presheaf

**Authors:** Benjamin Engel, Ryshard-Pavel Kostecki

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.08699v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.08699v2

**Submitted:** 2026-03-09


### 摘要

利用瓦卡雷洛夫的带否定格逻辑理论，我们引入了（co）准直觉逻辑，并证明了它关于（co）准直觉代数类的合理性和完整性。将这些代数结合起来，我们得到了双拟直觉代数和双拟直觉逻辑。它们进一步的扩展与Skolem代数结构定义了Akchurin代数和相应的逻辑，它是双拟直觉主义和双直觉主义逻辑的产物，具有四个不同的否定。接下来我们概括光谱预沉的框架（这是Butterfield-Isham-Döring topos量子力学理论方法中的一个主要对象），证明了任意完备正交补格上的正交补决定了谱预层上的两个否定算子（一个仿协调，另一个仿完全），用双拟直觉代数的结构来装备谱预层的所有开闭子预层的集合。结合该集合的通用Skolem（即Heyting和Brouwer）代数结构，这给出了Akchurin代数的一个特定实例。我们还表明，底层的正射补格可以重建为谱预层的内部对象，从而产生双重余准直觉的图像（分别，准直觉主义）否定单元（分别，commonad）。最后，我们证明了谱预层是辩证（或任何其他）关联逻辑的模型这一说法的一个禁忌定理。


### Abstract

Using Vakarelov's theory of lattice logics with negation, we introduce the (co)quasiintuitionistic logic, and prove its soundness and completeness with respect to the class of (co)quasiintuitionistic algebras. Combining these algebras together, we obtain biquasiintuitionistic algebras and the biquasiintuitionistic logic. Their further extension with the Skolem algebra structure defines Akchurin algebras and the respective logic, which is a product of biquasiintuitionistic and biintuitionistic logics, featuring four distinct negations. Next we generalise the framework of spectral presheaves (which is a main object in the Butterfield--Isham--Döring topos theoretic approach to quantum mechanics) to arbitrary complete orthocomplemented lattices, and show that the orthocomplementation determines two negation operators on the spectral presheaf (one paraconsistent, another paracomplete), equipping the set of all closed-and-open subpresheaves of a spectral presheaf with the structure of a biquasiintuitionistic algebra. Combined with the generic Skolem (i.e. Heyting and Brouwer) algebra structure of this set, this gives a particular instance of an Akchurin algebra. We also show that the underlying orthocomplemented lattice can be reconstructed as an internal object of the spectral presheaf, resulting as the image of a double coquasiintuitionistic (resp., quasiintuitionistic) negation monad (resp., comonad). Finally, we prove a no-go theorem for the claim that the spectral presheaf is a model of a dialectical (or any other) relevance logic.

## 通过多阶段剩余学习减轻量子模型中的频率学习偏差

**Original Title:** Mitigating Frequency Learning Bias in Quantum Models via Multi-Stage Residual Learning

**Authors:** Ammar Daskin

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10083v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.10083v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-10


### 摘要

基于参数化电路的量子机器学习模型可以被视为傅里叶数列逼近器。然而，他们经常很难学习具有多个频率分量的函数，特别是高频或非主导频率分量的函数;我们将这种现象称为量子傅里叶参数化偏差。受经典傅里叶神经运算符（FNO）最新进展的启发，我们将多阶段剩余学习思想适应量子领域，根据前一阶段的剩余迭代训练额外的量子模块。我们在合成基准上评估我们的方法，该基准由具有各种包封形状（高斯、洛伦兹、三角形）的空间局部频率分量组成。系统实验表明，量子位的数量、编码方案和剩余学习对于解析多个频率都至关重要;单独的剩余学习就可以比针对相同的历元总数训练的单阶段基线显着提高测试均方差。我们的工作为增强量子模型的光谱表达能力提供了一个实用框架，并为其频率学习行为提供了新的见解。


### Abstract

Quantum machine learning models based on parameterized circuits can be viewed as Fourier series approximators. However, they often struggle to learn functions with multiple frequency components, particularly high-frequency or non-dominant ones; a phenomenon we term the quantum Fourier parameterization bias. Inspired by recent advances in classical Fourier neural operators (FNOs), we adapt the multi-stage residual learning idea to the quantum domain, iteratively training additional quantum modules on the residuals of previous stages. We evaluate our method on a synthetic benchmark composed of spatially localized frequency components with diverse envelope shapes (Gaussian, Lorentzian, triangular). Systematic experiments show that the number of qubits, the encoding scheme, and residual learning are all crucial for resolving multiple frequencies; residual learning alone can improve test MSE significantly over a single-stage baseline trained for the same total number of epochs. Our work provides a practical framework for enhancing the spectral expressivity of quantum models and offers new insights into their frequency-learning behavior.

## 定域本征键轨道解码相关电荷迁移动力学

**Original Title:** Localized intrinsic bond orbitals decode correlated charge migration dynamics

**Authors:** Imam S. Wahyutama, Madhumita Rano, Henrik R. Larsson

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10105v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.10105v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-10


### 摘要

几十年来，科学家们一直在研究复杂的电荷迁移动力学，电离后，局部电荷分布（“空穴”）在飞秒时间尺度上穿过分子迁移。这有可能控制分子中的电子，但对电荷迁移的许多方面仍然缺乏全面的了解。在这项工作中，我们分析了电荷迁移使用的本地化本征键轨道（IBO）的扩展。这些轨道导致动力学的紧凑表示，并将复杂的相关多电子电荷迁移映射到化学概念，如卷曲箭头和轨道-轨道相互作用。通过分析多种具有挑战性的场景，我们展示了IBO如何使我们能够识别电荷迁移的关键机制。例如，我们表明不同的机制负责将$pi $形孔转换为$Sigma $形孔，反之亦然。我们用超共激相互作用和具有不同对称性的轨道耦合的配置来解释这些。我们进一步展示了如何使用IBO来寻找具有高电荷迁移效率的分子。我们使用有效的时间相关密度矩阵重整化群（TDDMRG）设置来进行所有模拟，关联50个轨道中多达45个电子。我们相信我们的结果将有助于设计未来的实验。提出的IBO分析适用于其他类型的实时电子动力学和光谱学。


### Abstract

For decades, scientists have studied the intricate charge migration dynamics, where after ionization a localized charge distribution ("hole") migrates across the molecule on a femtosecond timescale. This has the potential for controlling electrons in molecules, yet a comprehensive understanding of the many aspects of charge migration is still missing. In this work, we analyze charge migration using an extension of localized intrinsic bond orbitals (IBOs). These orbitals lead to a compact representation of the dynamics and map the complex, correlated many-electron charge migration to chemical concepts such as curly arrows and orbital-orbital interactions. By analyzing multiple challenging scenarios, we show how IBOs enable us to identify key mechanisms in charge migration. For example, we show that different mechanisms are responsible for converting a $π$-shaped hole to a $σ$-shaped hole and vice versa. We explain these in terms of hyperconjugation interactions and configurations that couple orbitals with different symmetries. We further demonstrate how IBOs can be used to find molecules with high charge migration efficiency. We carry out all simulations using an efficient set up of the time-dependent density matrix renormalization group (TDDMRG), correlating as many as 45 electrons in 50 orbitals. We believe that our results will be useful to design future experiments. The proposed IBO analysis is applicable to other types of real-time electron dynamics and spectroscopy.

## 无阻挫无带隙量子系统的数字耗散态制备

**Original Title:** Digital dissipative state preparation for frustration-free gapless quantum systems

**Authors:** Johannes Feldmeier, Yu-Jie Liu, Mikhail D. Lukin, Soonwon Choi

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10119v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.10119v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-10


### 摘要

准备量子多体系统的代数相关的底薪对于量子模拟来说是一项重要但具有挑战性的任务。我们引入了一种采用局部投影测量和单位反馈的协议，用于无挫折无间隙系统。我们的方法在时间上准备了先验未知的基本状态，该状态与系统大小成正比。我们分析地展示了我们的单粒子动力学协议的性能;我们认为，相同的机制可以推广到基于准粒子物理学的多体系统。我们的理论预测，瞬时冷却动力学直接揭示了系统的普遍临界性质。特别是，当系统的动力学临界指数大于或等于准粒子探索的有效空间维度时，状态准备时间在有限尺寸间隙的倒数中呈线性关系（直到log修正）。我们在一维和二维铁磁海森堡模型、弗雷德金旋转链和共振价键状态的二维模型的数值模拟中验证了这些预测。我们的协议完全数字化地稳定无间隙多体基态，而不需要模拟旋转，从而在短期实验中能够超越传统隔热方法访问高保真度状态。


### Abstract

Preparing algebraically correlated ground states of quantum many-body systems is an important, yet challenging task for quantum simulation. We introduce a protocol that employs local projective measurements and unitary feedback for frustration-free gapless systems. Our approach prepares a priori unknown ground states in time that scales polynomially with system size. We analytically show the performance our protocol for the dynamics of a single-particle; we argue the same mechanism generalizes to many-body systems based on the physics of quasiparticles. Our theory predicts that a transient cooling dynamics directly reveals the system's universal critical properties. In particular, the state preparation time is linear in the inverse of the finite-size gap (up to log correction) when the system's dynamical critical exponent is larger or equal the effective spatial dimension explored by the quasiparticles. We verify these predictions in numerical simulations of ferromagnetic Heisenberg models in one- and two dimensions, a Fredkin spin chain, and a two-dimensional model of resonating valence bond states. Our protocol stabilizes gapless many-body ground states fully digitally without requiring analog rotations, enabling access to high-fidelity states beyond conventional adiabatic approaches in near-term experiments.

## 正规化温启动量子逼近优化及其在最大切割问题上超越经典求解器的条件

**Original Title:** Regularized Warm-Started Quantum Approximate Optimization and Conditions for Surpassing Classical Solvers on the Max-Cut Problem

**Authors:** Zichang He, Anuj Apte, Brandon Augustino, Arman Babakhani, Abid Khan, Sivaprasad Omanakuttan, Ruslan Shaydulin

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10191v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.10191v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-10


### 摘要

演示在大规模优化方面优于强大经典求解器的量子启发论仍然是一个悬而未决的挑战。在这里，我们引入了正规化温启动QAOA（RWS-QAOA），它通过使用惩罚近位串状态的正规化器最小化预期能量来初始化量子位，防止QAOA停滞。我们进一步提出了一种产生固定的、与实例无关的参数的协议，使RWS-QAOA能够作为非变分算法运行，其中量子电路参数是固定的，并且只有经典的热启动步骤是与实例相关的。我们在随机规则图的Max-Cut问题上评估了RWS-QAOA，其中该协议在三个补充设置中产生了恒定深度的量子电路。首先，在Quantinuum的捕获离子处理器上，RWS-QAOA优于经典算法，在$3$-正则图上具有最大割的最佳可证明保证，即Goemans-Williamson和Halperin-Livnat-Zwick，在$96$-节点实例上。其次，张量网络模拟图与高达$N{=}10{，}000$节点显示，深度为6 $ RWS-QAOA，实现了$0.9167$的平均切割分数，超过了最好的经典的匹配限制下（没有本地搜索后处理，没有迭代细化）。第三，我们删除这些限制和基准对最强的不受限制的经典算法，包括优化的并行Burer-Monteiro求解器，改进MQLib的实现。即使对这个更强的基线，我们项目的表面代码RWS-QAOA达到低于0.2 $秒的量子经典运行时交叉在$3{，}000$节点图与少于130万$的物理量子位。我们的结果表明，当在未来的量子计算机上执行时，恒深量子电路与经典热启动相结合，具有超越经典求解器的潜力。


### Abstract

Demonstrating quantum heuristics that outperform strong classical solvers on large-scale optimization remains an open challenge. Here we introduce Regularized Warm-Started QAOA (RWS-QAOA), which initializes qubits by minimizing expected energy with a regularizer that penalizes near-bitstring states, preventing QAOA from stalling. We further propose a protocol that yields fixed, instance-independent parameters, enabling RWS-QAOA to operate as a non-variational algorithm in which the quantum circuit parameters are fixed and only a classical warm starting step is instance-dependent. We evaluate RWS-QAOA on the Max-Cut problem for random regular graphs, where this protocol yields a constant-depth quantum circuit, across three complementary settings. First, on Quantinuum's trapped-ion processor, RWS-QAOA outperforms the classical algorithms with the best provable guarantees for Max-Cut on $3$-regular graphs, namely Goemans-Williamson and Halperin-Livnat-Zwick, on $96$-node instances. Second, tensor-network simulations on graphs with up to $N{=}10{,}000$ nodes show that depth-$6$ RWS-QAOA, achieving an average cut fraction of $0.9167$, surpasses the best classical heuristics under matched restrictions (no local-search post-processing and no iterative refinement). Third, we remove these restrictions and benchmark against the strongest unrestricted classical heuristics, including an optimized parallel Burer-Monteiro solver that improves upon the MQLib implementation. Even against this stronger baseline, we project that surface-code RWS-QAOA reaches a quantum-classical runtime crossover below $0.2$ seconds on $3{,}000$-node graphs with fewer than $1.3$ million physical qubits. Our results show that constant-depth quantum circuits combined with a classical warm start have a credible potential to surpass classical solvers on the Max-Cut problem when executed on future quantum computers.

## 量子轨迹适合半经典逼近吗？

**Original Title:** Are quantum trajectories suitable for semiclassical approximations?

**Authors:** Alfredo M. Ozorio de Almeida

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10206v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.10206v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-10


### 摘要

量子力学德布罗格-博姆公式中的量子轨迹取决于从薛定汉方程的全波解中推导出的额外量子势。集体提供所有正确的量子结果的任务强烈改变了相应经典轨迹的特征，而经典轨迹是对演变波函数的半经典逼近的基础。经典轨迹和量子轨迹在这里都被认为是保守的，不受外部环境的影响，尽管这是量子系统最终经典性（即退相干）的来源。可积性的概念与经典力学和量子力学密切相关，但量子轨迹并没有保留下来。一般的系统中，经典的混沌运动参与，是很难处理半经典，但量子轨道可以是混沌的，即使是可积系统。德布罗意-玻姆解释中经典轨道和量子轨道之间的这种差异并没有澄清奇异的经典-量子跃迁。


### Abstract

The quantum trajectories in the de Broglie-Bohm formulation of quantum mechanics depend on an additional quantum potential derived from the full wave solution of Schrödinger's equation. The task of supplying collectively all the correct quantum results strongly alters the characteristics of the corresponding classical trajectories, which underlie semiclassical approximations to the evolving wave function. Both classical and quantum trajectories are here considered to be conservative with no influence of an external environment, even though this is the source of eventual classicality in quantum systems, that is, decoherence. The concept of integrability, closely correspondent in classical and quantum mechanics, is not preserved by the quantum trajectories. General systems, in which classical chaotic motion participates, are much harder to treat semiclassically, but quantum trajectories can be chaotic even for integrable systems. This discrepancy between the character of classical and quantum trajectories in the de Broglie-Bohm interpretation does not clarify the singular classical-quantum transition.

## 使用可验证的基准电路减少量子误差缓解偏差

**Original Title:** Reducing Quantum Error Mitigation Bias Using Verifiable Benchmark Circuits

**Authors:** Joseph Harris, Kevin Lively, Peter Schuhmacher

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10224v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.10224v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-10


### 摘要

我们提出了一种简单、可扩展且低负担的方法来改进通用的偏置量子误差缓解（QEM）方法，在具有多达2000个纠缠门的100量子位电路上，比标准QEM实现高达15%的保真度改进。我们通过构建可验证的基准电路来做到这一点，这些基准电路反映了应用电路的本征门结构，从而反映了噪音分布。这些电路可用于基准测试和减轻底层错误缓解方法的偏差，仅需要应用电路和硬件原生门集。我们提出了两种生成基准电路的方法;一种方法对目标硬件不可知，但代价是单量子位门的少量开销，而另一种方法是特定于IBM超导体硬件的，并且没有门的开销。作为推论，我们引入基准噪音零噪音外推（bnZNE），作为零噪音外推（ZNE）的简单改编，ZNE是最受欢迎的错误缓解方法之一。我们以Trotered Hamilton模拟的减轻偏置的ZNE和bnZNE为例，观察到我们的方法在IBM超导体硬件上使用小规模经典模拟和100量子位实用规模实验时优于标准ZNE。我们考虑一维量子位链上的单点可观测量以及两点相关性的测量。我们还提供了一个软件包来实施本研究中使用的错误缓解技术。


### Abstract

We present a simple, malleable and low-overhead approach for improving generic biased quantum error mitigation (QEM) methods, achieving up to 15% fidelity improvements over standard QEM on 100-qubit circuits with up to 2000 entangling gates. We do so by constructing verifiable benchmark circuits which mirror the application circuit's native-gate structure and thus noise profile. These circuits can be used to benchmark and mitigate the bias of the underlying error mitigation method, requiring only the application circuit and hardware native gate set. We present two methods for generating benchmark circuits; one is agnostic to the target hardware at the expense of a small overhead of single-qubit gates, while the other is specific to the IBM superconducting hardware and has no gate overhead. As a corollary, we introduce benchmarked-noise zero-noise extrapolation (bnZNE) as a simple adaptation of zero-noise extrapolation (ZNE), one of the most popular error mitigation methods. We consider as an example the bias-mitigated ZNE and bnZNE of Trotterized Hamiltonian simulations, observing that our approaches outperform standard ZNE using both small-scale classical simulations and 100-qubit utility-scale experiments on the IBM superconducting hardware. We consider the measurement of both single-site observables as well as two-site correlations along a one-dimensional qubit chain. We also provide a software package for implementing the error mitigation techniques used in this research.

**Original Title:** Learning from Radio using Variational Quantum RF Sensing

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10239v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.10239v1

### 摘要

铜酸盐高温超导电性的微观起源仍然是凝聚物质物理学中的核心悬而未决问题之一。越来越多的实验和理论证据表明，裸露的单带费米-哈伯德模型可能无法完全捕捉铜酸盐的特性，例如超导电性，这促使我们重新审视铜氧化物平面的典型三带模型-埃梅里模型-单带模型最初就是从该模型中衍生出来的。在这里，我们提出并分析了一种量子模拟方案，用于利用当今的超冷原子量子模拟平台在与铜酸盐和无限层镍酸盐相关的区域中实现埃梅里模型，从而能够探索系统尺寸上的三带物理学，这对当前的数值方法具有挑战性。具体来说，我们表明，具有叠加排斥势图案的二维光学格子可以被设计来研究与铜酸盐和无限层镍酸盐相关的埃梅里模型的可变参数区域的低温性质。我们的结果为使用超冷原子量子模拟器进行真实材料模拟以及更好地理解非常规超级导体的物理学铺平了道路。


### Abstract

The microscopic origin of high-temperature superconductivity in cuprates remains one of the central open questions in condensed matter physics. Growing experimental and theoretical evidence suggests that the bare single-band Fermi-Hubbard model may not fully capture properties of cuprates such as superconductivity, motivating us to revisit the canonical three-band model of the copper-oxide planes - the Emery model - from which the single-band counterpart was originally derived. Here, we propose and analyze a quantum simulation scheme for realizing the Emery model in regimes relevant to cuprates and infinite-layer nickelates with today's ultracold atom quantum simulation platforms, enabling the exploration of the three-band physics on system sizes that are challenging for current numerical methods. Specifically, we show that a two-dimensional optical lattice with a superimposed pattern of repulsive potentials can be designed to study low-temperature properties for variable parameter regimes of the Emery model relevant to cuprates as well as infinite-layer nickelates. Our results pave the way for real material simulations with ultracold atom quantum simulators and a better understanding of the physics of unconventional superconductors.
