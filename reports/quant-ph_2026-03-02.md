## 识别量子退火炉中的量子一致性

**Original Title:** Identifying quantum coherence in quantum annealers

**Authors:** Connor Aronoff, Travis Howard, David Nicholaeff, Alejandro Lopez-Bezanilla, Wade DeGottardi

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.21355v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.21355v2

**Submitted:** 2026-02-24


### 摘要

在大规模量子处理器中展示真正的多体量子一致性仍然是近期量子技术的核心挑战。最近对D波量子退热器的实验研究了伊辛链的猝灭，并观察到的缺陷密度，显示出Kibble-Zurek标度，与连贯量子动力学一致。然而，经典或热过程可能会产生相同的缩放。在这里，我们建议使用多体相干振荡（MBCO）作为识别模拟量子模拟器中系统范围内相关性的诊断方法。通过求解与时相关的Schrodinger方程，我们表明，跨越量子临界点的交错一维伊辛链的灭灭会在缺陷可观测量中产生振荡特征。我们在D-Wave优势量子退火炉上实现了这个模型。使用快速退欧协议，我们发现，尽管缺陷密度遵循Kibble-Zurek缩放，但不存在预期的振荡行为。我们证明，与单个量子位相关的静态混乱不太可能导致MBCO的缺失。对退火炉计划的适度修改可以显着提高振荡可见性。这项工作为在有噪音的大规模量子平台中寻找量子一致性提供了一般路线图。


### Abstract

Demonstrating genuine many-body quantum coherence in large-scale quantum processors remains a central challenge for near-term quantum technologies. Recent experiments on D-Wave quantum annealers have investigated quenches of Ising chains and observed defect densities that show Kibble-Zurek scaling, consistent with coherent quantum dynamics. However, identical scaling can arise from classical or thermal processes. Here we propose the use of many-body coherent oscillations (MBCO) as a diagnostic for the identification of system-wide coherence in analog quantum simulators. Solving the time-dependent Schrodinger equation, we show that quenches of a staggered one-dimensional Ising chain across a quantum critical point produce oscillatory signatures in defect observables. We implement this model on the D-Wave Advantage quantum annealer. Using fast-anneal protocols, we find that, although defect densities follow Kibble-Zurek scaling, the expected oscillatory behavior is absent. We demonstrate that static disorder associated with individual qubits is not likely responsible for the absence of MBCO. Modest modifications to annealing schedules can dramatically enhance oscillation visibility. This work gives a general roadmap for the search for quantum coherence in noisy, large-scale quantum platforms.

## 被动环境辅助量子通信

**Original Title:** Passive Environment-Assisted Quantum Communication

**Authors:** Evelyn Voss, Bikun Li, Zhaoyou Wang, Liang Jiang

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.21549v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.21549v2

**Submitted:** 2026-02-25


### 摘要

随着量子信息系统的成熟，通过有噪通道有效且连贯地传输量子信息变得越来越重要。我们研究被动环境辅助量子通信如何提高直接量子信息传输效率。玻色子纯损耗通道被建模为通过在暗端口具有真空输入状态的分束器的传输，当透过率低于50%时，量子容量为零。通过通道的量子通信可以通过被动环境辅助来增强，被动环境辅助是通过为辅助端口选择适当的输入状态来实现的。尽管理想的Gottesman-Kitaev-Preskill（GKP）状态能够以任意小的传输率实现完美的量子信息传输，但它们在实验上实现具有挑战性。因此，我们探索实验上更容易获得的非高斯辅助状态，例如Fock、cat和压缩cat状态，并通过数字方式确定最佳编码和解码策略。我们还构建了产生高保真传输和良好信息率的分析方案。


### Abstract

As quantum information systems mature, efficient and coherent transfer of quantum information through noisy channels becomes increasingly important. We examine how passive environment-assisted quantum communication enhances direct quantum information transfer efficiency. A bosonic pure-loss channel, modeled as transmission through a beam splitter with a vacuum input state at the dark port, has zero quantum capacity when transmissivity is below 50%. Quantum communication through the channel can be enhanced by passive environment assistance, achieved via the selection of an appropriate input state for the ancilla port. Although ideal Gottesman-Kitaev-Preskill (GKP) states enable perfect quantum information transmission at arbitrarily small transmissivity, they are challenging to realize experimentally. We therefore explore more experimentally accessible non-Gaussian ancilla states, such as Fock, cat, and squeezed cat states, and numerically determine the optimal encoding and decoding strategies. We also construct analytical schemes that yield high-fidelity transmission and good information rates.

### Abstract

Identifying the boundary between classical and quantum computation is a central challenge in quantum information. In multi-qubit systems, entanglement and magic are the key resources underlying genuinely quantum behaviour. While entanglement is well understood, magic - essential for universal quantum computation - remains relatively poorly characterised. Here we show that determining membership in the stabilizer polytope, which defines the free states of magic-state resource theory, requires super-exponential time $\class{exp} ( n^2)$ in the number of qubits $n$, even approximately. We reduce the problem to solving a $3$-\class{SAT} instance on $n^2$ variables and, by invoking the exponential time hypothesis, the result follows. As a consequence, both quantifying and certifying magic are fundamentally intractable: any magic monotone for general states must be super-exponentially hard to compute, and deciding whether an operator is a valid magic witness is equally difficult. As a corollary, we establish the robustness of magic as computationally optimal among monotones. This barrier extends even to classically simulable regimes: deciding whether a state lies in the convex hull of states generated by a logarithmic number of non-Clifford gates is also super-exponentially hard. Together, these results reveal intrinsic computational limits on assessing classical simulability, distilling pathological magic states, and ultimately probing and exploiting magic as a quantum resource.

## 带收益的宏观量子电动力学：修正的波动及其后果

宏观量子电动力学（MQED）提供了一个统一的框架来描述任意宏观环境下的量子电磁场。该理论的核心是场相关性，它控制辐射（例如，兰姆位移和珀塞尔效应）和机械现象，例如范德华力和卡西米尔力。在本教程中，我们概述了MQED及其对活性媒体的扩展，重点介绍了波动诱导的力作为收益修改场相关性的表现。

### 摘要

使用角度编码的量子机器学习模型自然地表示截断的傅里叶序列，提供具有足够电路深度的普适函数逼近能力。对于一元固定频率编码，电路深度缩放为O（欧米茄_max *（欧米茄_max + ð '{-2}）），目标频率幅度欧米茄_max和精确度RST。理论上，可训练频率方法可以减少这一点，以匹配目标频谱大小，只需要与目标频谱中的频率一样多的编码门。尽管效率令人信服，但它们的实际有效性取决于一个关键假设：基于梯度的优化可以将预因子驱动到任意目标值。我们通过系统实验证明，频率预因子表现出有限的可训练性：运动被限制在典型学习率的大约+/-1单位。当目标频率位于此可达范围之外时，优化经常失败。为了克服这种频率可达性限制，我们提出使用三进制编码的基于网格的初始化，从而生成密集的整频谱。虽然这种方法需要O（log_3（欧米茄_max））编码门--比理论上的最佳值更多，但比固定频率方法呈指数级减少--但它确保目标频率位于本地可达范围内。在具有三个高频移动的合成目标上，三进制网格初始化的R2评分中位数为0.9969，而可训练频率基线的R2评分中位数为0.1841。对于现实世界的Flight Passengers数据集，三进制网格初始化的R#2得分中位数为0.9671，比可训练频率初始化（R#2中位数= 0.7876）提高了22.8%。


### Abstract

Quantum machine learning models using angle encoding naturally represent truncated Fourier series, providing universal function approximation capabilities with sufficient circuit depth. For unary fixed-frequency encodings, circuit depth scales as O(omega_max * (omega_max + epsilon^{-2})) with target frequency magnitude omega_max and precision epsilon. Trainable-frequency approaches theoretically reduce this to match the target spectrum size, requiring only as many encoding gates as frequencies in the target spectrum. Despite this compelling efficiency, their practical effectiveness hinges on a key assumption: that gradient-based optimization can drive prefactors to arbitrary target values. We demonstrate through systematic experiments that frequency prefactors exhibit limited trainability: movement is constrained to approximately +/-1 units with typical learning rates. When target frequencies lie outside this reachable range, optimization frequently fails. To overcome this frequency reachability limitation, we propose grid-based initialization using ternary encodings, which generate dense integer frequency spectra. While this approach requires O(log_3(omega_max)) encoding gates -- more than the theoretical optimum but exponentially fewer than fixed-frequency methods -- it ensures target frequencies lie within the locally reachable range. On synthetic targets with three shifted high frequencies, ternary grid initialization achieves a median R^2 score of 0.9969, compared to 0.1841 for the trainable-frequency baseline. For the real-world Flight Passengers dataset, ternary grid initialization achieves a median R^2 score of 0.9671, representing a 22.8% improvement over trainable-frequency initialization (median R^2 = 0.7876).

## 从QED$_3$到Fradkin-Shenker模型中的自二元多元临界性

**Original Title:** From QED$_3$ to Self-Dual Multicriticality in the Fradkin-Shenker Model

**Authors:** Thomas T. Dumitrescu, Pierluigi Niro, Ryan Thorngren

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23420v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.23420v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-26


### 摘要

我们考虑2+1维的Fradkin-Shenker ${\mathbb Z}_2$规范-希格斯格子模型，即由面内磁场变形的复曲面码。它的相图包含一个多临界CFT，具有无间隙的、相互非局部的电和磁粒子，通过${\mathbb Z}_2^{\mathsf{D}}$自二元对称性交换。我们引入了模型的交错推广，其中这些粒子分别携带全局$U（1）_e$和$U（1）_m$电荷，并提出了一个连续体QFT描述，以QED$_3$，$N_f = 2$ Dirac费米子口味和具有Yukawa耦合的二电荷希格斯场。假设的相图包含具有$（O（2）_e \times O（2）_m）\r times\mathbb{Z}_2 ' mathsf {D}$对称性的多临界CFT，其中一些在QFT描述中是新兴的。我们使用大的-$N_f$展开计算一些操作符的缩放维度，并发现与紧急选择规则的一致性。交错模型承认原始Fradkin-Shenker模型的变形，该模型映射到Higgs-Yukawa-QED$_3$中的单位电荷RST操作员，打破了$U（1）_e \乘U（1）_m$对称性。我们明确地表明，这种变形再现了弗拉迪金-申克相图的所有特征。最后，我们提出了Higgs-Yukawa-QED$_3$和易平面$\mathbb{ CP}' 1 $模型（即具有合适势的两味纯量QED$_3$）之间的多临界二元性，该模型描述了正方形网格上的1/2反铁磁体。这种二元性意味着Néel-VBS转变的一级线结束于去精细的量子多临界点，由交错Fradkin-Shenker模型中出现的相同的$O（2）_e \乘O（2）_m$对称CFT描述，该模型将其与有间隙的${\mathbb Z}_2$旋转液体相分离。


### Abstract

We consider the Fradkin-Shenker ${\mathbb Z}_2$ gauge-Higgs lattice model in 2+1 dimensions, i.e. the toric code deformed by an in-plane magnetic field. Its phase diagram contains a multicritical CFT with gapless, mutually non-local electric and magnetic particles, exchanged by a ${\mathbb Z}_2^{\mathsf{D}}$ self-duality symmetry. We introduce a staggered generalization of the model in which these particles carry global $U(1)_e$ and $U(1)_m$ charges, respectively, and we propose a continuum QFT description in terms of QED$_3$ with $N_f = 2$ Dirac fermion flavors and a charge-two Higgs field with Yukawa couplings. The conjectured phase diagram harbors a multicritical CFT with $(O(2)_e \times O(2)_m)\rtimes\mathbb{Z}_2^\mathsf{D}$ symmetry, some of which is emergent in the QFT description. We compute the scaling dimensions of some operators using a large-$N_f$ expansion and find agreement with the emergent selection rules. The staggered model admits a deformation to the original Fradkin-Shenker model, which maps to unit-charge monopole operators in Higgs-Yukawa-QED$_3$ that break the $U(1)_e \times U(1)_m$ symmetry. We show explicitly that this deformation reproduces all features of the Fradkin-Shenker phase diagram. Finally, we propose a multicritical duality between Higgs-Yukawa-QED$_3$ and the easy-plane $\mathbb{ CP}^1$ model (i.e. two-flavor scalar QED$_3$ with a suitable potential), which describes spin-1/2 anti-ferromagnets on a square lattice. This duality implies a first-order line of Néel-VBS transitions ending in a deconfined quantum multicritical point, described by the same $O(2)_e \times O(2)_m$ symmetric CFT that arises in the staggered Fradkin-Shenker model, which separates it from a gapped ${\mathbb Z}_2$ spin liquid phase.

## Koopman-Krylov空间中半经典弦的可积性破缺

**Original Title:** Integrability breaking in semiclassical strings in Koopman-Krylov space

**Authors:** Rathindra Nath Das, Saskia Demulder

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23421v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.23421v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-26


### 摘要

虽然非常强大，但半经典弦解的可积性是一种罕见的性质。出于了解和描述不可积弦动力学的广阔图景的需要，我们将探测混乱的Krylov方法扩展到经典系统。我们引入了一个Koopman-Krylov框架，该框架在经典力学的Koopman-von Neumann描述中制定，并通过作用于可观测量的Koopman生成器的生成器扩展动态模式分解（gEDMZ）逼近来实现。使用这个框架，我们研究了可积弦动力学的可积性破坏变形如何引起谱权重的特征重新分布，从而导致Krylov空间中的可观测的离域和扩展。我们通过三类不可积的半经典弦解说明了库普曼-Krylov诊断。


### Abstract

While very powerful, integrability in semiclassical string solutions is known to be a rare property. Motivated by the need to understand and characterise the large landscape of non-integrable string dynamics, we extend Krylov methods for probing chaos to classical systems. We introduce a Koopman-Krylov framework, formulated in the Koopman-von Neumann description of classical mechanics and implemented via a generator extended dynamic mode decomposition (gEDMD) approximation of the Koopman generator acting on observables. Using this framework, we study how integrability-breaking deformations of integrable string dynamics induce characteristic redistributions of spectral weight, leading to observable-dependent delocalisation and spreading in Krylov space. We illustrate the Koopman-Krylov diagnostics across three classes of non-integrable semiclassical string solutions.

## 量子东方模型中的底价和持续振荡

**Original Title:** Ground state and persistent oscillations in the quantum East model

**Authors:** Adway Kumar Das, Achilleas Lazarides

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23422v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.23422v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-26


### 摘要

对于具有开放边界的1D量子东方模型，我们表明，在极限$s \to -\infty$下，底态被简单的旋转-相干积态准确捕获。我们进一步确定了一个低纠缠激发本征态，它与底价的区别仅在于边界旋转的$-旋转，并保持与旋转相干态很好地接近。对于$-\infty<s<0$的范围，边缘相干积态与两个由尺寸无关的能间隙分开的本征状态重叠，导致热力学极限下全球和局部可观测量的持续相干振荡。这些振荡源于边界物理学，与量子多体疤痕或类超立方体的福克斯空间机制不同。


### Abstract

For the 1D quantum East model with open boundaries, we show that in the limit $s \to -\infty$, the ground state is accurately captured by a simple spin-coherent product state. We further identify a low-entanglement excited eigenstate that differs from the ground state only by a $π$-rotation of the boundary spin, remaining well approximated by a spin-coherent state. For a range of $-\infty<s<0$, the edge-coherent product state overlaps with two eigenstates separated by a size-independent energy gap, leading to persistent coherent oscillations of both global and local observables in the thermodynamic limit. These oscillations originate from boundary physics and are distinct from quantum many-body scars or hypercube-like Fock-space mechanisms.

## 概率轨道、轨道上的概率和随机-量子对应

**Original Title:** Trajectory of Probabilities, Probability on Trajectories, and the Stochastic-Quantum Correspondence

**Authors:** Győző Egri, Marton Gomori, Balazs Gyenis, Gábor Hofer-Szabó

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.23491v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.23491v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-26


### 摘要

物理系统时间演变的概率描述可以采取两种概念上不同的形式：概率轨迹，指定概率如何随着时间演变，以及轨迹上的概率，将概率分配给可能的历史。这两种概率描述之间缺乏明确的区分导致了一些概念上的困难，特别是在最近对随机量子对应关系的分析中。本文系统地描述了它们的关系。我们定义了概率动态学和随机过程族，以及连接这两个描述的精确实现概念。我们表明，实现一般是非唯一的，每一个概率动力学承认马尔可夫实现，并表征非马尔可夫实现是可能的。我们揭露常见的论点的线性概率动力学的基础上的法律全概率的谬误，并澄清正确的解释“过渡矩阵”，通过区分动态水平的地图从实施过程的条件概率矩阵。我们进一步引入分解性作为逐步演化（可能是非线性的）概率动力学的适当的一般概念，将其与线性情况下的可分性联系起来-表明两者可以分开-并将这两个概念从马尔可夫性和时间均匀性中解脱出来。最后，我们将这些结果与我们所谓的统计动力学联系起来，在统计动力学中，线性确实是物理动机，并将框架与量子力学进行对比。


### Abstract

The probabilistic description of the time evolution of a physical system can take two conceptually distinct forms: a trajectory of probabilities, which specifies how probabilities evolve over time, and a probability on trajectories, which assigns probabilities to possible histories. A lack of a clear distinction between these two probabilistic descriptions has given rise to a number of conceptual difficulties, particularly in recent analyses of stochastic-quantum correspondence. This paper provides a systematic account of their relationship. We define probability dynamics and stochastic process families together with a precise notion of implementation that connects the two descriptions. We show that implementations are generically non-unique, that every probability dynamics admits a Markovian implementation, and characterize when non-Markovian implementations are possible. We expose fallacies in common arguments for the linearity of probability dynamics based on the law of total probability and clarify the proper interpretation of ``transition matrices'' by distinguishing dynamics-level maps from the conditional probability matrices of implementing processes. We further introduce decomposability as the appropriate general notion of stepwise evolution for (possibly nonlinear) probability dynamics, relate it to divisibility in the linear case -- showing that the two can come apart -- and disentangle both notions from Markovianity and time-homogeneity. Finally, we connect these results to what we call statistical dynamics, in which linearity is indeed physically motivated, and contrast the framework with quantum mechanics.

Furthermore, using Monte-Carlo simulations of a fading channel, we show that $W_2$ acts as a high-fidelity estimator of instantaneous link quality, exhibiting a wide dynamic range immune to the numerical instabilities of fidelity-based metrics. This geometric framework bridges the gap between quantum optimal transport and practical receiver design, paving the way for adaptive sensing in scattering media.
