## 通过深度学习用时间相关汉密尔顿元插值一元元

**Original Title:** Interpolation of unitaries with time-dependent Hamiltonians via Deep Learning

**Authors:** Antonio Guerra, Daniel Uzcategui-Contreras, Aldo Delgado, Esteban S. Gómez

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.12619v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.12619v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-18


### 摘要

由含时Hamilton体控制的量子系统对正时间演化操作符的准确计算提出了重大挑战，而这对于预测量子状态动力学至关重要。在这项工作中，我们引入了一种基于物理信息神经网络的物理信息深度学习方法，以在全时间域内估计这些操作符。通过结合正性等物理约束并利用进化操作符的二阶马格努斯展开，提出的框架能够在不同时间间隔估计正矩阵。该模型使用模拟的么正操作符进行训练，并在2至6个量子比特范围内的量子系统上进行评估。对于更大的多体系统，特别是那些具有7和8个量子位的系统，采用相同的方法来重建有效的时间相关Hamilton，从中计算整个时间域上相应的时间进化操作符。所提出的框架使用有限数量的单位样本实现了超过0.92的一致性，这表明测量和数据获取成本的潜在降低。这些结果凸显了该方法用于量子动力学系统的数据驱动模拟和识别的有效性，与量子计算和量子模拟应用直接相关。


### Abstract

Quantum systems governed by time-dependent Hamiltonians pose significant challenges for the accurate computation of unitary time-evolution operators, which are essential for predicting quantum state dynamics. In this work, we introduce a physics-informed deep learning approach based on Physics-Informed Neural Networks to estimate these operators over the full time domain. By incorporating physical constraints such as unitarity and leveraging the second-order Magnus expansion on the evolution operator, the proposed framework enables the estimation of unitary matrices at different time intervals. The model is trained using simulated unitary operators and evaluated on quantum systems ranging from 2 to 6 qubits. For larger many-body systems, specifically those with 7 and 8 qubits, the same methodology is employed to reconstruct an effective time-dependent Hamiltonian, from which the corresponding time-evolution operator is computed over the entire temporal domain. The proposed framework achieves fidelities exceeding 0.92 using a limited number of unitary samples, indicating a potential reduction in measurement and data acquisition costs. These results highlight the effectiveness of the approach for data-driven simulation and identification of quantum dynamical systems, with direct relevance to quantum computing and quantum simulation applications.

## 通过次协调神经网络无方程发现开放量子系统

**Original Title:** Equation-Free Discovery of Open Quantum Systems via Paraconsistent Neural Networks

**Authors:** Aleyna Ceyran, Jair Minoro Abe

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.12635v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.12635v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-19


### 摘要

在有噪的中等规模量子（NISQ）设备上对开放量子系统的动力学进行建模构成了一项重大挑战，因为高噪音水平和环境退化会导致纯量子状态的衰变（去相干）和能量损失。这种情况代表了量子信息技术领域最重要的问题之一。虽然现有的数据驱动方法很难在训练数据之外进行概括（外推），但物理信息神经网络（PINN）需要预定义的控制方程，当基础物理不完整或未知时，这限制了它们的发现能力。在这项工作中，我们介绍了ParaQNN（ParaQuantum神经网络）架构，这是一个用于物理发现的无方程框架。ParaQNN通过采用二元神论逻辑层来解开多尺度动态，而不依赖先验定律，将连贯信号和去相干噪音建模为独立但相互作用的通道。通过对Rabi振荡、Lindblad动力学以及放松和去相过程相互竞争的特别复杂“混合方案”进行的广泛基准测试，我们表明ParaQNN与随机森林、XGBOP和PINN模型相比表现出一致的性能优势。物理信息不完整。与其竞争对手不同的是，ParaQNN通过从有噪的测量中“捕捉”潜在的结构不变量，即使在训练数据不可用的外推区域中，也成功地以高精度保持振荡和衰减动态。这些结果表明，在数学方程被证明不充分的情况下，次协调逻辑为学习量子行为提供了结构上更稳定的认识基础。


### Abstract

Modeling the dynamics of open quantum systems on noisy intermediate-scale quantum (NISQ) devices constitutes a major challenge, as high noise levels and environmental degradations lead to the decay of pure quantum states (decoherence) and energy losses. This situation represents one of the most important problems in the field of quantum information technologies. While existing data-driven methods struggle to generalize beyond the training data (extrapolation), physics-informed neural networks (PINNs) require predefined governing equations, which limit their discovery capability when the underlying physics is incomplete or unknown. In this work, we present the ParaQNN (ParaQuantum neural network) architecture, an equation-free framework for physical discovery. ParaQNN disentangles multi-scale dynamics without relying on a priori laws by employing a dialetheist logic layer that models coherent signal and decoherent noise as independent yet interacting channels. Through extensive benchmark tests performed on Rabi oscillations, Lindblad dynamics, and particularly complex ``mixed regimes'' where relaxation and dephasing processes compete, we show that ParaQNN exhibits a consistent performance advantage compared to Random Forest, XGBoost, and PINN models with incomplete physical information. Unlike its competitors, ParaQNN succeeds in maintaining oscillatory and damping dynamics with high accuracy even in extrapolation regions where training data are unavailable, by ``discovering'' the underlying structural invariants from noisy measurements. These results demonstrate that paraconsistent logic provides a structurally more stable epistemic foundation than classical methods for learning quantum behavior in situations where mathematical equations prove insufficient.

## 因果边缘的学习：无信号约束下的最佳学习样本复杂性

**Original Title:** Learning at the Edge of Causality: Optimal Learning-Sample Complexity from No-Signaling Constraints

**Authors:** Jeongho Bang, Kyoungho Cho, Jeongwoo Jae

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.12651v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.12651v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-19


### 摘要

什么最终决定了量子学习的样本成本--算法独创性还是物理定律？我们在计算、学习和因果关系相互冲突的领域研究这个问题。Grover搜索中反映先验未知状态的扭曲可能会将搜索空间$N$上的查询复杂性从$O（\SQRT{N}）$崩溃到$O（\log N）$，即，指数级加速。然而，标准量子理论禁止这种未知状态反射（无反射定理）。因此，我们构建了一个状态学习辅助架构，称为“放大学习”，它将连贯幅度放大与状态学习交替。将这种放大学习嵌入宝-布兰-乔丹无信号框架中，我们表明，除非每轮花费学习样本和反射电路预算至少达到$（\SQRT{N}/\log N）$，否则超微型轮梦想将打开一个超光速通信通道。与此同时，我们为学习电路生成的纯状态推导出了严格的计算学习理论样本边界，揭示了在最坏的情况下处于$N$级的状态泛反式“锁”。戏剧性的结局是，无信号传递不仅否决了非物理基元，而且它修复了唯一一致的反射电路复杂性，并将这种偶然性强制的复杂性输入到计算学习界限中，使其崩溃到完全相同的$\SQRT{N}/\log N$缩放上。仅无信号传递所需。因此，无信号充当可学习性的调节器：一种在物理和计算之间进行调解的约束，将查询、门和样本复杂性焊接到一个单一的因果兼容三角形中。


### Abstract

What ultimately fixes the sample cost of quantum learning -- algorithmic ingenuity or physical law? We study this question in an arena where computation, learning, and causality collide. A twist on Grover's search that reflects about an a priori unknown state can collapse the query complexity from $O(\sqrt{N})$ to $O(\log N)$ over a search space $N$, i.e., an exponential speedup. Yet, standard quantum theory forbids such a unknown-state reflection (no-reflection theorem). We therefore build a state-learning-assisted architecture, called ``amplify-learn,'' which alternates the coherent amplitude amplification with state learning. Embedding this amplify-learn into the Bao-Bouland-Jordan no-signaling framework, we show that the logarithmic-round dream would open a super-luminal communication channel unless each round expends the learning-sample and reflection-circuit budgets scaling at least as $Ω(\sqrt{N}/\log N)$. In parallel, we derive tight computational learning-theoretic sample bounds for learning circuit-generated pure states, revealing a state-universal ansatz ``lock'' at order $N$ in the worst case. The dramatic closure is that no-signaling does not merely veto the unphysical primitive, but it fixes the only consistent reflection-circuit complexity, and feeding this causality-enforced complexity into the computational learning bound makes it collapse onto the very same $\sqrt{N}/\log N$ scaling demanded by no-signaling alone. No-signaling thus acts as a regulator of learnability: a constraint that mediates between physics and computation, welding query, gate, and sample complexities into a single causality-compatible triangle.

## 构建区间内自由1D KFGM粒子的Hamilton量

**Original Title:** Constructing the Hamiltonian for a free 1D KFGM particle in an interval

**Authors:** Techapon Kampu, Salvatore De Vincenzo

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.12739v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.12739v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-19


### 摘要

我们分析了一个区间内自由1D Klein-Fock-Gordon-Majorana（KFGM）粒子的问题。所谓自由，我们的意思是间隔内没有势，并且其壁是可穿透的;因此，相关的能流密度不会在壁处消失。当然，区间中的量化并不是微不足道的，因为会出现所涉及的操作符域所施加的某些限制。在这里，我们的目标是获得这些粒子的汉密尔顿量。在实践中，Feshbach-Villars（GV）-Free Hamilton量是描述它们的合适操作符，并且是动量操作符的函数。此外，还必须对这两个操作符可以作用的波函数施加马约拉纳条件。因此，我们首先计算伪自伴随动量运算符。一组三参数边界条件（BC）构成了其域。到目前为止，汉密尔顿量的域是由动量操作符的域引起的;但是，我们确保只有能流密度在区间两端具有相同值的BC才在其域中。所有这些BC本质上属于单参数BC集。而且，由于GV方程在宇称运算下是不变的，因此宇称变换的波函数也是该方程的解，这进一步限制了自由GV Hamilton量的域。最后，知道一个区间内1D KFGM粒子的伪自伴随的GV Hamilton量的最一般的BC三参数集，我们发现只有两个BC可以留在GV自由Hamilton量的范围内：周期BC和反周期BC。这些BC由两分量GV波函数（这些分量是相关的）和一分量KFG波函数（可以是实的或虚的）满足。


### Abstract

We analyze the problem of a free 1D Klein-Fock-Gordon-Majorana (KFGM) particle in an interval. By free, we mean that there is no potential within the interval and that its walls are penetrable; hence, the pertinent energy current density does not vanish at the walls. Certainly, quantization in an interval is not trivial because certain restrictions imposed by the domains of the operators involved arise. Here, our objective is to obtain the Hamiltonian for these particles. In practice, the Feshbach-Villars (FV)--free Hamiltonian is the proper operator for characterizing them and is a function of the momentum operator. Additionally, a Majorana condition must also be imposed on the wavefunctions on which these two operators can act. Thus, we start by calculating the pseudo self-adjoint momentum operator. A three-parameter set of boundary conditions (BCs) constitutes its domain. Up to this point, the domain of the Hamiltonian is induced by the domain of the momentum operator; however, we ensure that only the BCs for which the energy current density has the same value at each end of the interval are in its domain. All these BCs essentially belong to a one-parameter set of BCs. Moreover, because the FV equation is invariant under the operation of parity, the parity-transformed wavefunction is also a solution of this equation, which further restricts the domain of the free FV Hamiltonian. Finally, knowing the most general three-parameter set of BCs for the pseudo self-adjoint FV Hamiltonian for a 1D KFGM particle in an interval, we find that only two BCs can remain within the domain of the FV--free Hamiltonian: the periodic BC and the antiperiodic BC. These BCs are satisfied by both the two-component FV wavefunction, with these components being related, and the one-component KFG wavefunction, which can be real or imaginary.

## 将热场双态中的魔力动力学与光谱形状因子联系起来

**Original Title:** Connecting Magic Dynamics in Thermofield Double States to Spectral Form Factors

**Authors:** Ning Sun, Pengfei Zhang

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.12787v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.12787v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-19


### 摘要

在正进化下，以简单状态初始化的混乱量子系统迅速发展成高复杂性，排除了任何有效的经典描述。量子混乱传统上由Hamilton的光谱性质来描述，最值得注意的是通过光谱形式因子，而稳定器形式主义中经典模拟的难度（通常称为量子魔法）可以通过稳定器雷尼（Rényi）熵来量化。在这封信中，我们基于具有全对全相互作用的混乱系统的一般论点，提出了热场双态稳定器Rényi的动态动态特性与光谱形状因子之间的关系。这种关系意味着稳定器雷尼（Rényi）的饱和度由一级动态转变决定。然后，我们在Sachdev-Ye-Kitaev模型中明确证明了这种关系，使用稳定器Rényi的辅助旋转表示，该稳定器表现出紧急的$Z_2$对称性。我们进一步发现，在SYK模型的高温区域中，转变发生在有限时间，长时间阶段以自发的$Z_2$对称性破缺为标志。相比之下，在低温下，转变被推到系统大小呈指数级长的时间。我们的结果揭示了量子混乱和量子魔法之间有趣的相互作用。


### Abstract

Under unitary evolution, chaotic quantum systems initialized in simple states rapidly develop high complexity, precluding any efficient classical description. Quantum chaos is traditionally characterized by spectral properties of the Hamiltonian, most notably through the spectral form factor, while the hardness of classical simulation within the stabilizer formalism, commonly referred to as quantum magic, can be quantified by the stabilizer Rényi entropy. In this Letter, we propose a relation between the dynamics of the stabilizer Rényi entropy for thermofield double states and the spectral form factor, based on general arguments for chaotic systems with all-to-all interactions. This relation implies that the saturation of the stabilizer Rényi entropy is governed by a first-order dynamical transition. We then demonstrate this relation explicitly in the Sachdev-Ye-Kitaev model, using an auxiliary-spin representation of the stabilizer Rényi entropy that exhibits an emergent $Z_2$ symmetry. We further find that, in the high-temperature regime of the SYK model, the transition occurs at a finite time, with the long-time phase marked by spontaneous $Z_2$ symmetry breaking. In contrast, at low temperatures, the transition is pushed to times exponentially long in the system size. Our results reveal an intriguing interplay between quantum chaos and quantum magic.

### Abstract

We address the quantumness of molecular nanomagnets using a recently formulated criterion [cf. Krisnanda et al., Phys. Rev. Lett. 119, 120402 (2017)] demonstrating that these systems exhibit an intrinsic quantum nature, as evidenced by their ability to generate and enhance quantum correlations between two non-interacting probes. ## 参数驱动法制备铁磁子量子状态

### 摘要

我们提出了一种利用纵向驱动制备和验证铁磁体中铁磁共振自旋波模的高斯量子态的方法。与基于量子光学的策略相反，我们的方法利用纯粹的磁振子特征-自旋波非线性-来产生磁振子压缩。这种资源用于制备真空压缩态，以及通过微波腔耦合的不同磁体模式之间的纠缠态。我们提出的方法来检测这样的状态与经典的方法，如铁磁共振或本地拾波线圈，并量化所需的检测效率。我们分析解决了椭圆形Y铁石榴子铁磁铁的情况，但我们的方法适用于多种形状和尺寸。我们的工作使得无需单磁振子源或检测器（量子位）即可实现量子磁振子实验，从而使量子机制触手可及。


### Abstract

We analytically solve the case of ellipsoidal yttrium iron garnet ferrimagnets, but our method applies to a vast range of shapes and sizes. ## 修正牛顿动力学中玻色气体的各向异性集体激发

**Original Title:** Anisotropic Collective Excitations of Bose Gases in Modified Newtonian Dynamics

**Authors:** Ning Liu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.12848v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.12848v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-19


### 摘要

集体激发是量子多体物理学中的基础，但传统上它们的光谱是在牛顿动力学中研究的。在这封信中，我们在修正牛顿动力学（MOND）下研究了玻色气体中的集体激发。我们推导出MOND区域中的各向异性激发光谱。这种各向异性直接源于MOND Poisson方程的固有非线性结构，形成了修改后的引力响应的独特特征。然后，我们分析Jeans不稳定性，获得与方向相关的临界波长和质量的解析式。这些结果促进了我们对修改动力学下量子系统集体行为的理解，并为在量子模拟器中测试类MOND效应建立了明确的理论签名。


### Abstract

Collective excitations are fundamental in quantum many-body physics, yet their spectra have traditionally been studied within Newtonian dynamics. In this Letter, we investigate collective excitations in Bose gases under Modified Newtonian Dynamics (MOND). We derive an anisotropic excitation spectrum in the MOND regime. This anisotropy arises directly from the intrinsic nonlinear structure of the MOND Poisson equation, forming a distinctive signature of the modified gravitational response. We then analyze the Jeans instability, obtaining analytic expressions for the direction-dependent critical wavelength and mass. These results advance our understanding of collective behavior in quantum systems under modified dynamics and establish clear theoretical signatures for testing MOND-like effects in quantum simulators.

## 具有三模参数相互作用的光机系统中强光场和弱量子信号的非可逆性

**Original Title:** Nonreciprocity of intense light field and weak quantum signal in optomechanical systems with three-mode parametric interactions

**Authors:** Yao Dong, Xin-Yao Huang, Guo-Feng Zhang

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.12855v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.12855v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-19


### 摘要

我们在由三模参数相互作用驱动的可重新配置光机平台内展示了强经典场和弱量子信号的非互惠光传输。该平台是模块化的，其中每个三模式光机系统都作为基本构建模块。单个块独立操作，实现经典场的非互惠性。具体来说，来自固有光学机械非线性的不对称辐射压力会引起非可逆机械位移，通过光学机械反馈调制腔强度。这可以完全隔离反向传输，而无需参数初始化。或者，对于量子信号，通过激活两个模块之间的光和音素交换通道来重新配置平台。在这种配置中，非可逆性源于直接量子跳跃和间接转换路径之间的量子干涉。相长干涉实现单向低损耗传输，而相长干涉则完全抑制反向传输。在以热力方式消除辅助模式后，可以通过工程光学机械引起的机械损耗来控制最佳非可逆频率以及插入损耗和非可逆带宽之间的权衡。此外，在分解边带条件下，基于三模的设备比双模系统需要更少的控制场功率，这展示了跨越经典和量子域的光学非互惠应用的广泛潜力。


### Abstract

We demonstrate nonreciprocal optical transmission for both intense classical fields and weak quantum signals within a reconfigurable optomechanical platform driven by three-mode parametric interactions. The platform is modular, where each three-mode optomechanical system serves as a fundamental building block. Operating independently, a single block achieves nonreciprocity for classical fields. Specifically, asymmetric radiation pressure from intrinsic optomechanical nonlinearity induces nonreciprocal mechanical displacement, modulating the cavity intensity through optomechanical feedback. This enables full isolation of backward transmission without requiring parameter initialization. Alternatively, for quantum signals, the platform is reconfigured by activating photonic and phononic exchange channels between the two blocks. In this configuration, nonreciprocity arises from quantum interference between direct photon hopping and indirect conversion pathways. Constructive interference enables unidirectional low-loss transmission, while destructive interference completely suppresses the reverse direction. After adiabatically eliminating the auxiliary modes, the optimal nonreciprocal frequency and the trade-off between insertion loss and nonreciprocal bandwidth can be controlled by engineering optomechanically induced mechanical dissipation. Additionally, the three-mode-based device requires less control-field power than two-mode systems under resolved-sideband conditions, demonstrating versatile potential for optical nonreciprocity applications across classical and quantum domains.

## 量子互动Oracle证明

**Original Title:** Quantum Interactive Oracle Proofs

**Authors:** Baocheng Sun, Thomas Vidick

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.13530v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.13530v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-20


### 摘要

### Abstract

This focus - together with rapid advances in quantum-computing hardware - has driven increasing applications in high-energy physics, particularly in lattice gauge theories. However, most existing experimental demonstrations remain restricted to (1+1)-dimensional and/or abelian gauge theories, such as the Schwinger model and the toric code. We successfully simulate the real-time dynamics of this model using quantum circuits that explicitly implement $F$-moves. We identify idling errors as the dominant error source, which can be effectively mitigated using dynamical decoupling combined with a parallelized implementation of $F$-moves.

## 半无限Fock态格子中的限制非厄米特集肤效应

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.13540v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.13540v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-20


### 摘要

本文中，我们研究了半无限Fock状态格子中的非厄米特集肤效应，其中固有耦合规模为\squtt {n}。通过解析求解非均匀、非互惠的SSH模型，我们证明了内在的非均匀耦合与非互惠相结合从根本上改变了传统的集肤效应。所有本征模并没有在物理边界处积累，而是在由非均匀轮廓确定的有限空间范围内被压缩和倾斜--我们将这种现象称为局限性非埃尔米特集肤效应。因此，从单个位置开始的格子上概率分布的演变受到双重限制：它在空间上被非均匀耦合限制在有限范围内，并进一步被非可逆性限制在该范围边缘的单边轨迹。此外，还提出了一种可行的基于单个俘获离子的实验方案。这项工作揭示了合成维度中的工程耦合剖面如何重塑非厄米特性质并实现量子状态操纵的新协议。


### Abstract

In this paper, we investigate the non-Hermitian skin effect in a semi-infinite Fock-state lattice, where the inherent coupling scales as \sqrt{n}. By analytically solving a non-uniform, non-reciprocal SSH model, we demonstrate that the intrinsic inhomogeneous coupling, in combination with nonreciprocity, fundamentally modifies the conventional skin effect. Instead of accumulating at the physical boundary, all eigenmodes become compressed and skewed within a finite spatial range determined by the inhomogeneous profile-a phenomenon we term the confined non-Hermitian skin effect. Consequently, the evolution of the probability distribution on the lattice starting from a single site is doubly confined: it is spatially bounded to a finite range by the inhomogeneous coupling, and further restricted to a one-sided trajectory at the edge of this range by the non-reciprocity. Moreover, a feasible experimental scheme based on a single trapped ion is also proposed. This work reveals how engineered coupling profiles in synthetic dimensions can reshape non-Hermitian properties and enable new protocols for quantum state manipulation.

## 连续高斯量子计量学的基本极限

**Original Title:** Fundamental Limits of Continuous Gaussian Quantum Metrology

**Authors:** Kazuki Yokomizo, Aashish A. Clerk, Yuto Ashida

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.13554v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.13554v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-20


### 摘要

连续量子计量学有望通过利用被发射到环境中的辐射量子逐渐带走的信息来实现高精度传感。尽管最近取得了进展，但目前还缺乏对玻色子系统连续计量的基本精度限制的全面了解。我们在连续高斯测量下开发了具有多模式自由玻色子的量子计量学的一般理论框架。我们推导出全球量子费舍尔信息（QFI）和环境QFI的渐进增长率的分析公式，它们分别量化了系统-环境联合状态中编码的总信息和从发射辐射中可访问的信息。我们推导出这些量的基本界限，表明虽然可以实现海森堡型与模式数的缩放，但精度最多与时间和有意义的能源呈线性关系。为了说明我们的发现，我们分析了几种具体设置，包括耦合腔阵列和捕获粒子阵列。虽然本地设置通过资源产生标准的线性缩放，但全局耦合设置可以在模式数方面实现最佳二次缩放。此外，我们证明，非互惠设置可以利用非埃尔米特皮肤效应来实现指数级增强的全球QFI。然而，值得注意的是，这种增强无法反映在环境QFI中，这凸显了存储在联合国家内的信息和辐射到环境中的信息之间的根本区别。这些发现建立了对连续玻色子感知中的资源权衡和缩放行为的理解。


### Abstract

Continuous quantum metrology holds promise for realizing high-precision sensing by harnessing information progressively carried away by the radiation quanta emitted into the environment. Despite recent progress, a comprehensive understanding of the fundamental precision limits of continuous metrology with bosonic systems is currently lacking. We develop a general theoretical framework for quantum metrology with multimode free bosons under continuous Gaussian measurements. We derive analytical expressions for the asymptotic growth rates of the global quantum Fisher information (QFI) and the environmental QFI, which quantify the total information encoded in the joint system-environment state and the information accessible from the emitted radiation, respectively. We derive fundamental bounds on these quantities, showing that while Heisenberg-type scaling with the number of modes is attainable, the precision scales at most linearly with time and a meaningful energy resource. To illustrate our findings, we analyze several concrete setups, including coupled cavity arrays and trapped particle arrays. While a local setup yields a standard linear scaling with resources, a globally coupled setup can achieve the optimal quadratic scaling in terms of the mode number. Furthermore, we demonstrate that a nonreciprocal setup can leverage the non-Hermitian skin effect to realize an exponentially enhanced global QFI. Notably, however, this enhancement cannot be reflected in the environmental QFI, highlighting a fundamental distinction between the information stored within the joint state and the information radiated into the environment. These findings establish an understanding of the resource trade-offs and scaling behaviors in continuous bosonic sensing.

**Authors:** Ilaria Svampa, Sonia L'Innocente, Stefano Mancini, Andreas Winter

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.13808v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.13808v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-20


### 摘要

在$p$-adic量子力学的背景下，我们研究了$p$-adic量子位和$p$-adic受控量子逻辑门的复合系统。我们建立在单个$p$-adic量子位作为紧$p$-adic特殊垂直群SO（3）$_p$的二维不可约表示的概念之上。我们表明，这些表示的分类简化为有限情况，因为它们都通过某个有限商SO（3）$_p$mo $p^k$进行因式分解。然后，我们解决$p$-adic量子位合成和纠缠问题，这是量子信息处理的$p$-adic公式的基础。我们对SO（3）$_p$mo $p$的表示进行分类，并分析从SO（3）$_p$得到的两个$p$-adic量子位表示的张量积。我们解决了此类系统的Clebsch-Gordan问题，揭示了耦合碱基分解为单重态和双重态。我们进一步研究这些稳定子系统产生的纠缠。对于$p=3$，我们从SO（3）$_p$模$的4 $维不可约表示中构造一组门，我们证明该门对于量子计算是通用的。


### Abstract

In the context of $p$-adic quantum mechanics, we investigate composite systems of $p$-adic qubits and $p$-adically controlled quantum logic gates. We build on the notion of a single $p$-adic qubit as a two-dimensional irreducible representation of the compact $p$-adic special orthogonal group SO(3)$_p$. We show that the classification of these representations reduces to the finite case, as they all factorise through some finite quotient SO(3)$_p$ mod $p^k$. Then, we tackle the problem of $p$-adic qubit composition and entanglement, fundamental for a $p$-adic formulation of quantum information processing. We classify the representations of SO(3)$_p$ mod $p$, and analyse tensor products of two $p$-adic qubit representations lifted from SO(3)$_p$ mod $p$. We solve the Clebsch-Gordan problem for such systems, revealing that the coupled bases decompose into singlet and doublet states. We further study entanglement arising from those stable subsystems. For $p=3$, we construct a set of gates from $4$-dimensional irreducible representations of SO(3)$_p$ mod $p$ that we prove to be universal for quantum computation.

## 具有违反宇称的费米子高斯态的一般旋转1/2 Hamilton的量子模拟

**Authors:** Michael Kaicher, Joseph Vovrosh, Alexandre Dauphin, Simon B. Jäger

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2601.13811v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.13811v1

**Submitted:** 2026-01-20


### 摘要

### Abstract

We introduce equations of motion for a parity-violating fermionic mean-field theory (PV-FMFT): a numerically efficient fermionic mean-field theory based on parity-violating fermionic Gaussian states (PV-FGS). This work provides explicit equations of motion for studying the real- and imaginary-time evolution of spin-1/2 Hamiltonians with arbitrary geometries and interactions. We extend previous formulations of parity-preserving fermionic mean-field theory (PP-FMFT) by including fermionic displacement operators in the variational Ansatz. Unlike PP-FMFT, PV-FMFT can be applied to general spin-1/2 Hamiltonians, describe quenches from arbitrary initial spin-1/2 product states, and compute local and non-local observables in a straight-forward manner at the same modest computational cost as PP-FMFT -- scaling as $O(N^3)$ in the worst case for a system of $N$ spins or fermionic modes. We demonstrate that PV-FMFT can exactly capture the imaginary- and real-time dynamics of non-interacting spin-1/2 Hamiltonians. We then study the post quench-dynamics of the one- and two-dimensional Ising model in presence of longitudinal and transversal fields with PV-FMFT and compute the single site magnetization and correlation functions, and compare them against results from other state-of-the-art numerical approaches. In two-dimensional spin systems, we show that the employed spin-to-fermion mapping can break rotational symmetry within the PV-FMFT description, and we discuss the resulting consequences for the calculated correlation functions. Our work introduces PV-FMFT as a benchmark for other numerical techniques and quantum simulators, and it outlines both its capabilities and its limitations.

### Abstract

We investigate dimensional constraints arising from representation theory when abstract graph edges possess internal degrees of freedom but lack geometric properties. Any geometrically consistent projection of these states maps necessarily to $\mathbb{R}^3$ via the Bloch sphere. This dimensional constraint $d=3$ emerges through self-consistency: edges without intrinsic geometry force directional encoding ($\mathbb{C}^2$), whose natural symmetry group $SU(2)$ has three-dimensional Lie algebra, yielding emergent geometry that validates the hypothesis via Bloch sphere correspondence ($S^2 \subset \mathbb{R}^3$). We establish uniqueness (SU($N>2$) yields $d>3$) and robustness (dimensional saturation under graph topology changes). The Euclidean metric emerges canonically from the Killing form on $\mathfrak{su}(2)$. A global gauge consistency axiom is justified via principal bundle trivialization for finite graphs. Numerical simulations verify theoretical predictions. This result demonstrates how dimensional structure can be derived from information-theoretic constraints, with potential relevance to quantum information theory, discrete geometry, and quantum foundations.

量子理论被广泛认为从根本上不确定性，但一旦无限信息被放弃，经典框架也可能表现出不确定性。与此同时，相对论通常被认为禁止超光速信号，但洛伦兹对称性正式承认超光速变换（SpTS）。Dragan和Ekert认为SpT具有类似于量子不确定论的不确定论。在这里，我们从自然假设中推导出一个不去定理，它可以解释为：超光速变换（SpTS）和有限信息不能共存。因此，任何适应SpT的理论都必须允许无界的信息内容，从而产生类似于在真实数字上制定的经典理论的确定性存在论。因此，任何由超光速变换产生的明显不确定性都只反映了由主观无知产生的概率，与量子理论中概率的客观本质不同，这表明超光速扩展所声称的不确定性不是量子的。


### Abstract

Quantum theory is widely regarded as fundamentally indeterministic, yet classical frameworks can also exhibit indeterminism once infinite information is abandoned. At the same time, relativity is usually taken to forbid superluminal signalling, yet Lorentz symmetry formally admits superluminal transformations (SpTs). Dragan and Ekert have argued that SpTs entail indeterminism analogous to the quantum one. Here, we derive a no-go theorem from natural assumptions, which can be interpreted as: superluminal transformations (SpTs) and finite information cannot coexist. Any theory accommodating SpTs must therefore allow unbounded information content, leading to a deterministic ontology akin to that of classical theories formulated over the real numbers. Thus, any apparent indeterminism arising from superluminal transformations reflects only probabilities arising from subjective ignorance, unlike the objective nature of probabilities in quantum theory, indicating that the claimed indeterminacy from superluminal extensions is not quantum.
