## 从可积性到混乱的纠缠：自举时间集成传播复杂性

**Original Title:** Scrambling of Entanglement from Integrability to Chaos: Bootstrapped Time-Integrated Spread Complexity

**Authors:** M. Süzen

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14224v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.14224v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-14


### 摘要

提出了一种时间积分的复杂性测度来诊断量子遍历性的程度。最大纠缠态内的酉演化的合奏的扰动力学量化的数值自举实现的一个合奏的哈密顿，探测不同的酉路径。使用Rosenzweig-Porter合奏，我们表明，综合传播的复杂性提供了一个细粒度的解决方案，在不同的遍历制度。这种方法提供了一种从早期到晚期诊断量子混沌的鲁棒方法。


### Abstract

A time-integrated measure of complexity is proposed for diagnosing the degree of quantum ergodicity. The scrambling dynamics of maximally entangled states within the ensemble of unitary evolutions are quantified by applying numerical bootstrapped realizations of an ensemble of Hamiltonians, probing different unitary paths. Using Rosenzweig-Porter ensembles, we show that the integrated spread complexity provides a fine-grained resolution across different ergodic regimes. This approach offers a robust method for diagnosing quantum chaos from early to late times.

## 你需要的就是规模吗？复杂SAR数据量子编码的重新思考阶段

**Original Title:** Magnitude Is All You Need? Rethinking Phase in Quantum Encoding of Complex SAR Data

**Authors:** Sakthi Prabhu Gunasekar, Prasanna Kumar R

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14229v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.14229v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-14


### 摘要

合成口径雷达（SAR）数据本质上是复杂的，而量子机器学习（QML）模型自然在复杂的希尔伯特空间中运行。这种明显的一致性表明，将幅度和阶段信息纳入量子编码中应该会提高SAR自动目标识别（ATR）的性能。在这项工作中，我们在MSTAR基准数据集的统一实验框架下，通过比较五种量子编码策略：纯幅度、联合复合形、基于I/Q、预处理阶段和纯量子来系统评估这一假设。   与预期相反，我们观察到一种一致的模式：在混合量子经典架构中，纯幅度编码优于所有复值策略，在3级任务中实现99.57%的准确性，在8级任务中实现71.19%的准确性，而阶段感知方法提供了可忽略不计（~0%）或负面的改进。相比之下，在只有184-224个可训练参数且没有经典分量的纯量子架构中，相信息变得至关重要，准确性提高了21.65%。   这些结果表明，阶段信息的效用并非数据固有的，而是严重取决于模型架构。混合模型依赖于补偿缺失的阶段信息的经典组件，而纯量子模型需要阶段来构建区分表示。我们的研究结果为在QML中编码复值数据提供了实用的设计指南，并强调了NISQ时代编码-架构协同设计的重要性。


### Abstract

Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) data is inherently complex-valued, while quantum machine learning (QML) models naturally operate in complex Hilbert spaces. This apparent alignment suggests that incorporating both magnitude and phase information into quantum encoding should improve performance in SAR Automatic Target Recognition (ATR). In this work, we systematically evaluate this assumption by comparing five quantum encoding strategies: magnitude-only, joint complex, I/Q-based, preprocessed phase, and pure quantum, under a unified experimental framework on the MSTAR benchmark dataset.
  Contrary to expectation, we observe a consistent pattern: in hybrid quantum-classical architectures, magnitude-only encoding outperforms all complex-valued strategies, achieving 99.57% accuracy on a 3-class task and 71.19% on an 8-class task, while phase-aware methods provide negligible (~0%) or negative improvements. In contrast, in purely quantum architectures with only 184-224 trainable parameters and no classical components, phase information becomes essential, contributing up to 21.65% improvement in accuracy.
  These results reveal that the utility of phase information is not inherent to the data, but depends critically on the model architecture. Hybrid models rely on classical components that compensate for missing phase information, whereas purely quantum models require phase to construct discriminative representations. Our findings provide practical design guidelines for encoding complex-valued data in QML and highlight the importance of encoding-architecture co-design in the NISQ era.

## 宇宙距离上的量子相关性测试

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14252v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.14252v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-15


### 摘要

人们普遍认为，在两个纠缠的子系统上同时实现的测量结果在统计上是即时相关的，无论它们之间的距离如何。根据贝尔定理，在这样的测量中，一切都发生在两个子系统之间，就好像有一个相关性以无限速度传播一样。到目前为止，这些相关性已经在1200公里的距离内得到了实验验证。我们讨论了将这一距离延长到390，000公里的兴趣和可行性，从而获得300倍的系数。这个想法是将其中一个旋光计安装在月球上，另一个安装在地球上。这样的实验将为量子物理学提供新的测试，并允许对替代理论和解释施加更高的限制。我们还讨论了在地月距离之外违反贝尔不等式的可能性。


### Abstract

It is commonly accepted that the results of measurements simultaneously realized over two entangled subsystems are statistically correlated instantaneously regardless of the distance between them. In accordance with Bell theorem, everything happens in such measurements as if there was a correlation propagating at infinite speed between the two subsystems.These correlations have been so far verified experimentally up to a distance of 1200 km. We discuss the interest and feasibility of extending this distance to 390,000 km, thus gaining a factor of 300. The idea is to install one of the polarimeters on the Moon, with the other on Earth. Such an experiment would provide a new test of Quantum Physics and allow to put higher constraints on alternative theories and interpretations. We also discuss the possibility to violate Bell inequalities beyond Earth-Moon distance.

## Grover搜索算法中一致性动力学的Tsallis相对$a $熵

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.13910v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-15


### 摘要

量子一致性在格罗弗的搜索算法中发挥着核心作用。我们研究了Grover搜索算法中进化状态的一致性动力学的Tsallis相对$a $熵。我们证明了一致性的Tsallis相对$a $信息随着成功概率的增加而减小，并推导出一致性与成功概率之间的互补关系。我们表明，第一个$H &{\otimes n}$的操作符一致性取决于数据库$N$的大小、成功概率和目标状态。此外，我们还说明了目标叠加状态的一致性和纠缠之间的关系，以及格罗弗迭代中一致性的产生和删除。


### Abstract

Quantum coherence plays a central role in Grover's search algorithm. We study the Tsallis relative $α$ entropy of coherence dynamics of the evolved state in Grover's search algorithm. We prove that the Tsallis relative $α$ entropy of coherence decreases with the increase of the success probability, and derive the complementarity relations between the coherence and the success probability. We show that the operator coherence of the first $H^{\otimes n}$ relies on the size of the database $N$, the success probability and the target states. Moreover, we illustrate the relationships between coherence and entanglement of the superposition state of targets, as well as the production and deletion of coherence in Grover iterations.

## 量子误差纠正码量子位丢失的AI使能解码

**Original Title:** AI-Enabled Decoding of Qubit Loss for Quantum Error-Correcting Codes

**Authors:** Yuqing Wang, Xiaotian Nie, Jiale Dai, Zhongyi Ni, Tao Zhang, Hui Zhai, Linghui Chen

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14269v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.14269v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-15


### 摘要

量子比特丢失是量子计算中的一个主要错误来源，因为它使量子纠错码的标准稳定子形式的代数结构失效。一方面，它使解码复杂化;另一方面，它在稳定器中引入随机闪烁模式作为量子比特丢失的标志。在这里，我们开发了一个基于时空图神经网络（STGNN）架构的人工智能解码器，从综合征历史中提取空间和时间相关性。我们的解码器执行双头任务，同时纠正标准泡利错误并识别量子位丢失的位置。我们的解码器比传统的最小权重完美匹配（MWPM）算法甚至使用最后一轮的量子位丢失信息作为输入的延迟擦除MWPM解码器实现了显着更高的逻辑准确性。我们的解码器还可以在随后十轮累积稳定器测量值后识别90%以上的丢失位置，从而促进量子位重新初始化，例如通过原子阵列平台上的连续加载技术。对于这两项任务，我们的STGNN的执行几乎与AlphaQubit的修改版本相同，但它采用并行输入结构，使其在推理时间上比修改后的AlphaQubit的循环输入结构更有优势。这项工作提供了一个强大且可扩展的框架来纠正量子位丢失错误，为更高效的容错量子计算铺平了道路。


### Abstract

Qubit loss is a major source of error in quantum computation, as it invalidates the algebraic structure of the standard stabilizer formalism for quantum error-correcting codes. On the one hand, it complicates decoding; on the other hand, it introduces stochastic flicker patterns in stabilizers as a hallmark of qubit loss. Here, we develop an artificial-intelligence-enabled decoder based on a spatiotemporal Graph Neural Network (STGNN) architecture to extract spatial and temporal correlations from syndrome histories. Our decoder performs a dual-head task, simultaneously correcting standard Pauli errors and identifying the locations of qubit loss. Our decoder achieves significantly higher logical accuracy than both the traditional minimum-weight perfect matching (MWPM) algorithm and even delayed-erasure MWPM decoders that use qubit loss information from the final round as input. Our decoder can also identify more than 90% of loss locations after accumulating stabilizer measurements over the subsequent ten rounds, thereby facilitating qubit reinitialization, for instance, via the continuous loading technique on the atom array platform. For both tasks, our STGNN performs nearly identically to a modified version of AlphaQubit, but it employs a parallel input structure, giving it an advantage in inference time over modified AlphaQubit's recurrent input structure. This work provides a robust and scalable framework for correcting qubit loss errors, paving the way for more efficient fault-tolerant quantum computation.

## 量子场论中的魔法与非克利福德·盖茨

**Original Title:** Magic and Non-Clifford Gates in Topological Quantum Field Theory

**Authors:** William Munizzi, Howard J. Schnitzer

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14271v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.14271v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-15


### 摘要

用于产生量子魔法的非克利福德门对于普适量子计算至关重要。我们表明，非克利福德门自然产生于拓扑量子场论中的路径积分，其中它们的魔生成性质是由理论的代数数据确定的。在Chern-Simons理论中，我们构造了Ising相互作用门，其生成器是通过简单三边界流上的路径积分来准备的，并表明它会产生远离离散Clifford点的非局部魔法。我们表明，Toffoli门在$SU（2）_1$中被$\mathbb{Z}_2$融合结构阻碍，而$SU（2）_3$是支持所需条件逻辑的最小理论，给定了映射类别群的密度。最后，我们证明T门作为Dijkgraaf-Witten理论中的路径积分出现，规范群为$\mathbb{Z}_4$，其中边界环上的单个Dehn扭转在没有逼近的情况下产生门。这些结果表明，拓扑路径积分在克利福德层次结构的多个级别中构建了门，并跨越不同类别的场论，这对拓扑量子计算具有影响。


### Abstract

Non-Clifford gates, used to generate quantum magic, are essential for universal quantum computation. We show that non-Clifford gates arise naturally from path integrals in topological quantum field theories, where their magic-generating properties are determined by the algebraic data of the theory. In Chern-Simons theory, we construct the Ising interaction gate, whose generator is prepared by path integration over simple three-boundary manifolds, and show that it produces non-local magic away from discrete Clifford points. We show that the Toffoli gate is obstructed in $SU(2)_1$ by the $\mathbb{Z}_2$ fusion structure, while $SU(2)_3$ is the minimal theory supporting the required conditional logic, given the density of the mapping class group in the projective unitary group on the manifold boundary. Finally, we demonstrate that the T gate arises as a path integral in Dijkgraaf-Witten theory, with gauge group $\mathbb{Z}_4$, where a single Dehn twist on the boundary torus produces the gate without approximation. These results show that topological path integrals construct gates in multiple levels of the Clifford hierarchy, and across distinct classes of field theories, with implications for topological quantum computing.

## 算符空间输运与边界时间晶体的出现

**Authors:** Dominik Nemeth, Ahsan Nazir, Robert-Jan Slager, Alessandro Principi

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14291v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.14291v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-15


### 摘要

边界时间晶体（BTCs）是受Lindbladian进化控制的集体旋转系统中连续时间晶体的突出例子。迄今为止，他们的分析主要依赖于半经典和数值方法。在这里，我们开发了一个完全量子兼容的框架来对集体旋转动力学进行分类，并表明BTC行为是由于缺乏刘维尔式的非平凡弱对称性而产生的。为此，我们引入了一种不可约张量表示，其中Lindbladian动力学映射到非Hermitian跳跃问题。在这张图中，动态对应于操作员权重在张量部门之间的传输。这种映射允许在一个统一的框架内识别不同的动力学机制，包括集体岁差、纯松弛和BTC阶段。我们表明，BTCs源于运营商空间中的非互惠输运，该输运使刘维本征模跨多个张量扇形去定域。这种非互惠传输为BTC振荡的初始条件的不敏感性提供了微观机制。更广泛地说，我们的结果确立了操作员空间运输作为理解消散性多体动力学的视角，并强调了与非埃尔米特现象的联系。


### Abstract

Boundary time crystals (BTCs) are prominent examples of continuous time crystals in collective spin systems governed by Lindbladian evolution. Within this picture, the dynamics corresponds to the transport of operator weight across tensor sectors. This non-reciprocal transport provides a microscopic mechanism for the insensitivity to initial conditions of BTC oscillations. More broadly, our results establish operator space transport as a perspective for understanding dissipative many-body dynamics and highlights connections to non-Hermitian phenomena.

**Authors:** Seok-Hyung Lee, Xanda C. Kolesnikow, Jun Zen, Evan T. Hockings, Campbell K. McLauchlan, Georgia M. Nixon, Thomas R. Scruby, Stephen D. Bartlett, Robin Harper, Benjamin J. Brown

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14296v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.14296v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-15


### 摘要

为了生产一种由不完美硬件制成的可操作量子计算机，我们必须设计和测试适合我们可以构建的设备的可扩展量子错误纠正码，并一致开发适应设备特定噪音特征的解码策略。在这里，我们引入了\{动态罗盘代码}，这是一种具有新型症状提取周期的子系统代码，它具有竞争力的阈值，同时有效利用排列在重十六进制网格上的量子位。我们使用超导量子位阵列来实现该代码的距离为5的实例，并演示详细的噪音特征如何提高解码器性能，以显着提高逻辑错误率。我们执行平均电路特征值采样（ACES），以获取有关症状提取过程所有元素的详细上下文相关错误信息。此外，我们利用测量设备产生的软信息来增强解码器，并通过后选择检测泄漏错误以排除。我们的受噪音影响的方法在实验中将动态罗盘代码的Distance-5实现的逻辑错误率提高了38.3%。


### Abstract

To produce an operable quantum computer that is made with imperfect hardware, we must design and test scalable quantum error correcting codes that are suited for the devices we can build and, in unison, develop decoding strategies that accommodate device-specific noise characteristics. Here, we introduce the \emph{dynamic compass code}, a subsystem code with a novel syndrome extraction cycle, that has a competitive threshold while making efficient use of qubits arranged on a heavy-hex lattice. We use a superconducting qubit array to implement a distance-5 instance of this code, and demonstrate how detailed noise characterisation can boost decoder performance to yield significant improvements in logical error rates. We perform averaged circuit eigenvalue sampling (ACES) to acquire detailed context-dependent error information on all elements of the syndrome extraction process. Furthermore, we leverage soft information produced from measurement devices to augment the decoder with measurement error information and detect leakage errors for exclusion through post-selection. Our noise-informed approach yields up to 38.3\% improvement in the logical error rate of a distance-5 implementation of the dynamic compass code in experiment.

## 多参数马尔科夫噪音计量的精度极限

**Original Title:** Precision Limits of Multiparameter Markovian-Noise Metrology

**Authors:** Anthony J. Brady, Yu-Xin Wang, Luis Pedro García-Pintos, Alexey V. Gorshkov

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14298v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.14298v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-15


### 摘要

测量随机信号（“噪音计量”）构成量子传感和开放量子系统特征化的核心任务。在这里，我们为通过Markovian Lindblad动力学编码的随机信号的多参数估计建立了最终精度界限，允许任意量子控制和无噪音辅助。尽管Markovian性强制执行与感知时间$T$的标准量子极限缩放，但我们的界限揭示了消散通道数量$R$的海森堡型缩放：当随机信号在$R$通道上表现出高等级相关性并且探测器纠缠时，平均方差（每个参数）的缩放并不优于$Q（1/（TR 2）$。对于集体$k$-体消散，$R= eta（N & k）$，表示系统大小$N$的超海森堡缩放。我们进一步表明，当未知参数通过消散本征率进入时，并行跟踪许多不同量子跳跃的快速准备和测量（RP）协议就能达到这些极限。在这种情况下，估计问题简化为多Poisson计数模型，为最佳量子噪音计量提供了概念上清晰的路径。我们通过网络噪音计量学、集体多体消散、学习泡利噪音和亚折射量子成像的应用来说明该框架的广度。


### Abstract

Measuring stochastic signals ("noise metrology") constitutes a central task in quantum sensing and the characterization of open quantum systems. Here we establish ultimate precision bounds for multiparameter estimation of stochastic signals encoded through Markovian Lindblad dynamics, allowing for arbitrary quantum control and noiseless ancillae. Although Markovianity enforces standard-quantum-limit scaling with sensing time $T$, our bounds reveal Heisenberg-type scaling in the number of dissipative channels, $R$: when the stochastic signal exhibits high-rank correlations across the $R$ channels and the probe is entangled, the average variance (per parameter) scales no better than $Ω(1/(TR^2))$. For collective $k$-body dissipation, $R=Θ(N^k)$, signifying super-Heisenberg scaling with the system size $N$. We further show that, when the unknown parameters enter through the dissipative eigenrates, a Rapid Prepare-and-Measure (RPM) protocol that tracks many distinct quantum jumps in parallel attains these limits. In this regime, the estimation problem reduces to a multi-Poisson counting model, providing a conceptually clean route to optimal quantum noise metrology. We illustrate the breadth of the framework with applications to networked noise metrology, collective many-body dissipation, learning Pauli noise, and subdiffraction quantum imaging.

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.14300v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-15


### 摘要

利用相变边界附近的系统的增强的灵敏度，临界量子计量迄今仍遭受间隙闭合相关的瓶颈效应，即，感测动态的临界减慢和参数感测窗口的急剧收缩。为了缓解所述瓶颈固有的任何用于感测的均匀晶格，在这里，我们建议利用固有的跳跃不均匀性所产生的玻色子梯形算子矩阵元素在福克空间晶格（FSL）。具体来说，使用一个双模式Jaynes-Cummings型模型，我们表明，传感参数可以被印到一个拓扑零能量模式的FSL。因此，关键系统参数变得依赖于细胞，有效地描绘出拓扑相图中的曲线。当该曲线穿过或接近拓扑相边界时，细胞依赖的临界性就会出现，而无需将网格全局调整为接近临界性。外部控制参数重塑了这条曲线，不断将量子费舍尔信息的缩放从标准调整到海森堡缩放，同时保持广泛的传感覆盖范围和降低的间隙成本。此外，单个腔上的局部光数测量饱和了量子费舍尔信息。这些结果将FSL确定为基于临界度的量子计量的可扩展且实用的途径。


### Abstract

An external control parameter reshapes this curve, continuously tuning the scaling of the quantum Fisher information from the standard to the Heisenberg scaling while maintaining broad sensing coverage and a reduced gap cost. Furthermore, a local photon-number measurement on a single cavity saturates the quantum Fisher information. These results identify FSLs as a scalable and practical route to criticality-based quantum metrology.

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14323v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.14323v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-15


### 摘要

玻色子采样是证明量子系统中量子优势的主要候选者。尽管实验和理论取得了重大进展，但对其产出统计数据的描述仍然不完整。在稀释区域之外尤其如此，此时的量子碰撞和聚束变得重要。相关的饱和区域的特征是模数随量子数线性增长，或者更一般地是次二次增长，正是实验最感兴趣的区域。因此，输出分布的反集中（硬度论点的关键要素）在玻色子采样中仍然知之甚少。在这项工作中，我们利用表示论工具来解决这一差距，获得通用粒子数保持玻色子可观测量的二次矩的封闭形式表达。我们用投影到操作符空间不可约分量上的Hilbert-Schmidt规范来表达这些量，并表明这些投影规范通过利用基本的对称结构来允许紧凑的分析表达。重点关注Fock状态输出概率，我们进一步建立了稀释制度之外的反集中。与最近的复杂性理论结果一起，我们的发现加强了在有趣的实验环境中玻色子采样的硬度保证。


### Abstract

Boson sampling is a leading candidate for demonstrating quantum advantage in photonic systems. Despite significant experimental and theoretical progress, a characterization of its output statistics remains incomplete. As a consequence, anti-concentration of the output distribution--a key ingredient in hardness arguments--remains poorly understood in boson sampling. In this work, we leverage representation-theoretic tools to address this gap, obtaining closed-form expressions for second moments of generic particle-number-preserving bosonic observables. We express these quantities in terms of Hilbert-Schmidt norms of projections onto irreducible components of the operator space and show that these projection norms admit compact analytical expressions by exploiting the underlying symmetry structure. Focusing on Fock state output probabilities, we further establish anti-concentration beyond the dilute regime. Together with recent complexity-theoretic results, our findings strengthen hardness guarantees for boson sampling in experimentally interesting settings.

**Authors:** Tasnim Ahmed, Alberto Marchisio, Muhammad Kashif, Nouhaila Innan, Muhammad Shafique

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15048v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.15048v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-16


### 摘要

混合量子神经网络（HQNN）将经典学习与参数化量子电路相结合，但其实际性能通常受到以下因素的限制：（i）有噪音的中间规模量子（NISQ）设备的噪音和（ii）量子电路架构的大离散设计空间。此外，HQNN通常使用固定电路和单个后台来训练，尽管部署经常针对编译和执行特征可能不同的异类后台。为了应对这些挑战，我们提出了GAT-QNN，这是一个基于遗传算法（GA）的框架，通过迭代采样微电路（子电路）、训练它们并将其学习到的参数重新集成到宏电路中来训练宏电路（搜索空间）。训练后，我们运行一个独立的GA驱动推理阶段，该阶段使用训练的宏电路权重评估候选微电路，并选择性能最佳的架构进行部署。这种两阶段方法可以实现后台感知的微电路选择，而无需重新训练每个候选架构，并且还可以通过部署从宏电路派生的较小微电路来减少计算资源（门数）。我们在MNIST分类（四个类别）上验证了该方法，并报告了跨多个后台的GA驱动推理一致的22-23%测试准确性收益。


### Abstract

Hybrid Quantum Neural Networks (HQNNs) combine classical learning with parameterized quantum circuits, but their practical performance is often limited by (i) the noise of Noisy Intermediate-Scale Quantum (NISQ) devices and (ii) the large, discrete design space of quantum circuit architectures. Moreover, HQNNs are commonly trained using a fixed circuit and a single backend, even though deployment frequently targets heterogeneous backends where compilation and execution characteristics may differ. To address these challenges, we propose GAT-QNN, a genetic algorithm (GA)-based framework that trains a macroCircuit (search space) by iteratively sampling microCircuits (subcircuits), training them, and reintegrating their learned parameters into the macroCircuit. After training, we run an independent GA-driven inference stage that evaluates candidate microCircuits using the trained macroCircuit weights and selects top-performing architectures for deployment. This two-stage approach enables backend-aware microCircuit selection without retraining each candidate architecture and can also reduce computational resources (gate count) by deploying smaller microCircuits derived from the macroCircuit. We validate the approach on MNIST classification (four classes) and report consistent 22-23% test accuracy gains for GA-driven inference across multiple backends.

## 通过模块化“Ridge”签名和高级协同信息对DAGI进行硬件验证

**Authors:** Petr Sramek

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15051v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.15051v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-16


### 摘要

我们报告了IBM Quantum硬件上的DAGI（有向无环图信息）框架的硬件验证，使用了一个小型受控实验，该实验的理想输出分布被限制在低维模块化管（“山脊”）。对于两个$n$位寄存器$（u，v）$，其中$n=4$（模16），每个关键实例$k$引发理想关系$v \equiv k \csot u \pmodo {16}$，在联合$（u，v）$分布中产生视觉上不同的山脊。在ibm\_torino上在单个Sampler V2作业中执行（8个键，1024次射击/键，$N=8192$总射击），山脊在硬件噪音下持续存在，山脊命中概率$p_{hit} = 0.1830$（统一基线$1/16$），对应于2.93美元的山脊对比度（95\% Bootstrap CI [2.80，3.06]）。关键恢复超出了机会：每次射击准确率0.1689（机会0.125，95%Wilson CI [0.1610，0.1772]），各组字典恢复率0.375（机会0.125）。为了测试核心DAGI假设--可恢复的关键信息主要是高位/协同的，而不是在低位边缘中可见的--我们通过莫比乌斯倒置管道计算$I（K;D_S）$对检测器位子集$S$的基于莫比乌斯的信息分解，并在阶数$k_{max}=3$时评估目标正协同效应$CPS_K$。在标签洗牌排列测试下，我们观察到$CPS_K（k=3）= 0.08788$具有显着性（准确性$p=0.001996$，$CPS_K$p=0.004975$）。均匀性诊断显示接近均匀的个位边缘，而相关性集中在特定的低位对中，并且自举可靠性扫描证实，在完整的1024次射击目标预算下，3阶目标协同在统计上仍然可靠。这些结果支持了DAGI检测和量化与已知全局代数约束相关的非平凡、硬件弹性、更高级信息结构的说法。


### Abstract

We report a hardware validation of the DAGI (Directed Acyclic Graph Information) framework on IBM Quantum hardware using a small, controlled experiment whose ideal output distribution is constrained to a low-dimensional modular manifold (a "ridge"). For two $n$-bit registers $(u,v)$ with $n=4$ (modulus 16), each key instance $k$ induces an ideal relation $v \equiv k \cdot u \pmod{16}$, producing a visually distinct ridge in the joint $(u, v)$ distribution. Executed on ibm\_torino in a single Sampler V2 job (8 keys, 1024 shots/key, $N=8192$ total shots), the ridge persists under hardware noise with ridge-hit probability $p_{hit} = 0.1830$ (uniform baseline $1/16$), corresponding to a ridge contrast of $2.93\times$ (95\% bootstrap CI [2.80, 3.06]). Key recovery exceeds chance: per-shot accuracy 0.1689 (chance 0.125, 95\% Wilson CI [0.1610, 0.1772]), and per-group dictionary recovery 0.375 (chance 0.125). To test the central DAGI hypothesis -- that recoverable key information is predominantly high-order/synergistic rather than visible in low-order marginals -- we compute a Möbius-based information decomposition of $I(K;D_S)$ over detector-bit subsets $S$ via a Möbius inversion pipeline and evaluate targeted positive synergy $CPS_K$ at order $k_{max}=3$. We observe $CPS_K(k=3) = 0.08788$ with significance under label-shuffle permutation tests (accuracy $p=0.001996$, $CPS_K$ $p=0.004975$). Uniformity diagnostics show near-uniform single-bit marginals while correlation concentrates in specific low-order pairs, and a bootstrap reliability sweep confirms order-3 targeted synergy remains statistically reliable at the full 1024-shot target budget. These results support the claim that DAGI detects and quantifies nontrivial, hardware-resilient, higher-order information structure associated with a known global algebraic constraint.

It suppresses logical error rates by up to an order of magnitude compared to prior compilers that focus primarily on maximizing parallelism.

### 摘要

Our classification reveals new static configurations that could be valuable for non-perturbative studies and for models where spin degrees of freedom couple to non-Abelian gauge fields.

## 自同同群界对幻影码的约束

**Authors:** Arthur S. Morris, Daniel Malz

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15111v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.15111v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-16


### 摘要

Specifically, we prove that any binary phantom code encoding $k$ logical qubits into $n$ physical qubits with distance $d\geq 2$ obeys the bound $k\leq \log_2(n+1)$ for all $k\neq 4$. In addition, we prove that this logarithmic ceiling cannot be circumvented by permitting additional local unitary gates, or by making use of subsystem codes: any subspace or subsystem code admitting a $\mathrm{SWAP}$-transversal implementation of every logical $\mathrm{CNOT}$ circuit is constrained to satisfy the same bound. These bounds follow from a general theorem relating the length of a quantum code to the structure of its automorphism group, a result which may find applications beyond phantom codes.
