## 量子干涉将弱手征性放大为巨量子非可逆性

**Original Title:** Quantum Interference Amplifies Weak Chirality into Giant Quantum Nonreciprocity

**Authors:** Jing Tang, Yuangang Deng

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27447v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.27447v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-24


### 摘要

在少光子水平的量子非互易性通常需要强对称性破缺，这带来了重大的实验挑战。在这里，我们证明了相控量子干涉可以将弱手性放大为巨量子非互易性。我们考虑两个相位可编程原子耦合到一个旋转的回音壁模式谐振器，干涉显着放大弱斐索分裂的效果。这种机制产生了明显的方向不对称的光子统计，具有明亮的反聚束发射在一个方向和强烈的聚束发射在相反的方向。值得注意的是，相关性和亮度隔离都遵循具有Fizeau分裂的相控功率律缩放，分别达到65~分贝和17.3~分贝。我们的结果确立了干扰增强的弱手征性是通往定向非经典光源的有力途径。


### Abstract

Quantum nonreciprocity at few-photon level typically requires strong symmetry breaking, posing significant experimental challenges. Here we demonstrate that phase-controlled quantum interference can amplify weak chirality into giant quantum nonreciprocity. We consider two phase-programmable atoms coupled to a spinning whispering-gallery-mode resonator, where interference dramatically amplifies the effect of weak Fizeau splitting. This mechanism generates pronounced directional asymmetry in photon statistics, featuring bright antibunched emission in one direction and strongly bunched emission in the opposite direction. Remarkably, both correlation and brightness isolations obey phase-controlled power-law scaling with Fizeau splitting, reaching up to 65~dB and 17.3~dB, respectively. Our results establish interference-enhanced weak chirality as a powerful route toward directional nonclassical light sources.

## 通过调节非线性1旋转系统中的混乱来实现有效的系综随机化

**Original Title:** Efficient ensemble randomization by tuning chaos in a nonlinear spin-1 system

**Authors:** Jongmin Kim, Minsung Jeong, Jongyoon Han, Y. Shin

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27448v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.27448v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-24


### 摘要

我们提出了一种有效的方案，通过用外部周期驱动调节混乱来随机化非线性1旋转系统中的旋转状态系综。在没有调制的情况下，该系统呈现出混合相空间，其特征是嵌入混乱海洋中的规则岛屿，其中全球混合受到能量节约的抑制。使用数值模拟，我们证明线性塞曼场的弱调制不仅促进了不同能量壳层之间的传输，而且还驱动系综在旋转状态上朝着哈尔随机分布。在优化条件下，在逆非线性相互作用能设置的时间尺度上实现完全随机化。在过度驱动机制中，随机化在特定调制幅度下意外地受到抑制，并伴随着相空间中粘性区域的形成。我们将这种行为归因于周期驱动的主要低次调和分量的动态抵消。这些结果说明了如何使用时间周期驱动来设计混乱系统并在非线性旋转系统中实现可控随机化。


### Abstract

We present an efficient scheme to randomize a spin-state ensemble in a nonlinear spin-1 system by tuning chaos with an external periodic drive. Without modulation, the system exhibits a mixed phase space featuring regular islands embedded in a chaotic sea, where global mixing is inhibited by energy conservation. Using numerical simulations, we demonstrate that weak modulation of a linear Zeeman field not only facilitates transport between different energy shells but also drives ensembles toward a Haar-random distribution over spin states. Under optimized conditions, complete randomization is achieved on a timescale set by the inverse nonlinear interaction energy. In the overdriven regime, randomization is unexpectedly suppressed at specific modulation amplitudes, accompanied by the formation of sticky regions in phase space. We attribute this behavior to the dynamical cancellation of the leading low-order harmonic component of the periodic drive. These results illustrate how time-periodic driving can be used to engineer chaotic systems and achieve controllable randomization in nonlinear spin systems.

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.27453v1

### 摘要

II型韦尔费米子可能会出现在黑洞事件视界后面。我们采用Painlevé-Gullstrand度规度来研究视界处Lifshitz转变的表面，相当于分离I型和II型Weyl状态的界面。我们在黑洞视界与通过狄拉克线从I型到II型韦尔费米子的转变之间找到了几个类比。我们分析了起源于半金属的Lifshitz转变处的硅保护的拓扑序。讨论了外尔半金属中Hawking辐射的出现。我们证明了从I型到II型狄拉克费米子的过渡态可以看作是一个黑洞视界，它具有独特的特征，包括一个具有非平凡拓扑不变量和临界手征反常效应的狄拉克线费米面.


### Abstract

We analyze the symmetry-protected topological order at the Lifshitz transition originating in semimetals. The emergence of Hawking radiation in Weyl semimetals is discussed. We show that the transition state from type-I to type-II Dirac fermions can be viewed as a black-hole horizon, which exhibits unique characteristics, including a Dirac-line Fermi surface with a nontrivial topological invariant and a critical chiral anomaly effect.

## 铁磁迪克-伊辛模型的量子临界性

**Original Title:** Quantum criticality of the ferromagnetic Dicke-Ising model

**Authors:** Jan Alexander Koziol

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27484v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.27484v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-26


### 摘要

我们使用朗道理论方法描述了铁磁性Dicke-Ising模型中的量子相转变。该理论定量地捕捉了正常相和超辐射相之间通过三临界点从二级到一级转变的变化。我们确定虚拟相关最近邻双旋转翻转过程是造成这种行为的关键机制。三临界点构成了上临界维度之上的量子相转变。我们讨论了在三临界点正确解释数值数据所需的有限尺寸缩放的修改。我们的结果强调了全对全相互作用量子系统中需要适应的有限尺寸缩放形式，并建立了铁磁性迪克-伊辛模型作为上临界维度以上量子相转变的范式平台，涵盖标准$''''临界度及以上。


### Abstract

We describe the quantum phase transitions in the ferromagnetic Dicke-Ising model using a Landau theory approach. The theory quantitatively captures the change from a second- to a first-order transition between the normal and superradiant phases through a tricritical point. We identify virtual correlated nearest-neighbor double spin-flip processes as the crucial mechanism responsible for this behavior. The tricritical point constitutes a quantum phase transition above the upper critical dimension. We discuss the modifications to finite-size scaling required for the correct interpretation of numerical data at the tricritical point. Our results emphasize the need for adapted finite-size scaling forms in all-to-all interacting quantum systems and establish the ferromagnetic Dicke-Ising model as a paradigmatic platform for quantum phase transitions above the upper critical dimension, encompassing both standard $φ^4$ criticality and beyond.

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.27497v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-26


### 摘要

量子密钥分发（QKD）承诺信息理论安全，但离散变量（DV）和连续变量（CV）设置中的实际部署仍然暴露于设备缺陷、通道操纵、有限密钥效应和用于适应和监控的机器学习（ML）组件中的漏洞。本调查采用问题驱动的视角，基于跨越设备、通道、协议、ML和网络层的九个实际问题类（P1-P9）。对于每个类别，我们将经典防御与支持ML的解决方案进行比较，包括异常检测、参数预测、噪音估计、对抗净化和资源分配。报告的结果包括基于DBSCAN的CV攻击检测P= 99.7%、R= 99.8%、F1=0.998，对抗鲁棒性恢复高达79.5%，低/高噪音条件下的通道放大检测为100%/91.26%，基于LightGBM的噪音预测将评估时间缩短高达98.8%。该调查进一步提出了一个将数据集、压力协议和统一评估指标（包括SKR影响、最大距离、延迟和稳健性）结合起来的基准框架。最后，我们提供了深度防御部署指南，并概述了安全实用的QKD系统的未来研究方向。


### Abstract

This survey adopts a problem-driven perspective based on nine practical problem classes (P1-P9) spanning device, channel, protocol, ML, and network layers. The survey further proposes a benchmarking framework combining datasets, stress protocols, and unified evaluation metrics including SKR impact, maximum distance, latency, and robustness. Finally, we provide defense-in-depth deployment guidelines and outline future research directions for secure and practical QKD systems.

## 全时间尺度上的多体量子混乱

**Original Title:** Many-Body Quantum Chaos At All Time Scales

**Authors:** Antonio M. García-García, Lucas Sá, Jacobus J. M. Verbaarschot, Jie-Ping Zheng

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27512v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.27512v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-26


### 摘要

我们通过研究四体$N$-Majorana Sachev-Ye-Kitaev模型的格林和超时序相关（OTBC）函数来描述多体量子混乱系统在所有时间尺度上的动力学。通过结合混乱形式主义和随机矩阵理论技术，我们始终获得这些函数的解析表达。OTEC的早期指数增长之后是指数衰减，其速率由格林函数（主要复合体鲁尔-波利科特共振的实部）控制。对于以$N$呈指数级扩展的后期，这两个函数都具有$N \mathrm 8 = 2，6$的倾斜-斜坡-平台模式，由于矩阵元素和特征值的局部能量相关性，即使在海森堡时间之后，也与各历经预测存在很大偏差。


### Abstract

We describe the dynamics of many-body quantum chaotic systems at all time scales by studying the Green's and out-of-time order correlation (OTOC) functions of the four-body, $N$-Majorana Sachdev-Ye-Kitaev model. By combining the scramblon formalism and random-matrix-theory techniques, we obtain analytical expressions for these functions at all times. The early exponential growth of the OTOC is followed by an exponential decay at a rate governed by that of the Green's function (the real part of the leading complex Ruelle-Pollicott resonances). For late times that scale exponentially with $N$, both functions have a dip-ramp-plateau pattern for $N \mathrm{mod}8 = 2, 6$ that deviates substantially from the ergodic prediction due to local-in-energy correlations of matrix elements and eigenvalues, even after the Heisenberg time.

## 模拟超导电路Lindblad动力学的多级张量网络压缩技术

**Original Title:** A multilevel tensor network compression technique for simulating Lindblad dynamics in superconducting circuits

**Authors:** Adrien Moulinas, Xavier Waintal

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27515v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.27515v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-26


### 摘要

设计超导量子硬件需要模拟工具，这些工具可以解释与理想场景的各种偏差。反过来，这需要自动检测某些结构并利用它们来使计算负担得起的方法。在这里，我们开发了一种基于张量网络的技术来模拟几种相互作用玻色子模的Lindblad动力学，重点关注了超导量子电路。该技术检测并利用两种非常常见的情况：（i）密度矩阵是纯的或离纯的不远;（ii）不同模式之间的纠缠是适度的（通常类似量子比特）。然而，（iii）模式的占用率可以任意高（使得天真的截断效率低下）。为了利用这些特征，我们使用三种不同的嵌套级别的张量网络压缩：（i）我们对密度矩阵进行全局纯化，（ii）我们压缩不同模式之间的连接以解释中度纠缠，（iii）我们使用Fock占据数的量子表示。我们展示了模拟大型猫量子位以及电离转换子量子位的技术，展示了相对于强力方法的数量级加速。在后一个例子中，它将之前在大型超级计算基础设施上报道的模拟带到了笔记本电脑级别。随着系统规模的有利扩展应该使这些系统的真正计算机辅助设计在范围内。


### Abstract

Designing superconducting quantum hardware requires simulation tools that can account for various deviations from ideal scenarios. This, in turn, requires approaches that automatically detect certain structures and leverage them to make the computation affordable. Here, we develop a tensor network based technique to simulate the Lindblad dynamics of a few interacting bosonic modes with a focus on superconducting quantum circuits. The technique detects and takes advantage of two very common situations: (i) the density matrix being pure or not far from pure and (ii) the entanglement between different modes being moderate (typically qubit-like). However, (iii) the occupation of the modes can be arbitrarily high (making naïve truncations inefficient). To leverage these features, we use three different nested levels of tensor network compression: (i) we work with a global purification of the density matrix, (ii) we compress the connection between different modes to account for the moderate entanglement and (iii) we use a quantics representation of the Fock occupation number. We showcase the technique for the simulation of large cat qubits as well as for the ionization of transmon qubits, demonstrating orders-of-magnitude speed-up with respect to brute force approaches. In the latter example, it brings the simulation, previously reported on a large supercomputing infrastructure, to laptop level. The favorable scaling with system size should bring genuine computer assisted design of these systems within scope.

## 用于增强最大团搜索的位移高斯玻色子采样

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.27522v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-26


### 摘要

高斯玻色子采样（GBS）能够解决某些类型的图形问题，因为这种设备产生的样本与高斯矩阵函数有联系。   特别是，GBS设备已被证明提供了一个增强的搜索团-或完整的子图-在无向加权图在经典算法。   通过配置输入态的压缩参数和对线性光网络进行编程，可以将图映射到GBS实验。   在实践中，有限的压缩和光子损失降低了GBS设备的最大团搜索的性能。   相比之下，相干状态--由于其Poissonian统计数据而通常被认为是经典资源--可以使用衰减激光轻松地在许多模式中准备。   在本文中，我们报告了通过在有损条件下或在有限的压缩量可用时添加位移来提高GBS找到最大加权派系的成功率。   此外，我们报告称，这种增强可以在有限的资源管理费用的情况下扩展到大型图表。


### Abstract

  In comparison, coherent states -- often considered a classical resource due to their Poissonian statistics -- can be readily prepared across many modes using an attenuated laser.
  In this paper, we report an enhancement of the success rate of GBS in finding maximum weighted cliques by adding displacements under lossy conditions or when a limited amount of squeezing is available.
  Moreover, we report that this enhancement can be scaled up to large graphs with limited resource overheads.

## 在数字量子处理器上观察共形Floquet动力学

**Original Title:** Observing conformal Floquet dynamics on a digital quantum processor

**Authors:** Liang-Hong Mo, Bastien Lapierre, Qiang Miao

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27530v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.27530v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-26


### 摘要

由于协议设计、硬件性能和经典验证中存在严重瓶颈，量子模拟传统上仅限于探索从非纠缠或低纠缠状态开始的动态学。在这里，我们报告了直接从多体临界状态开始的非平衡动力学的第一个实验观察。使用全连接的俘获离子处理器，我们通过基于多尺度纠缠重正化的硬件定制的微型深度量子电路准备了横场伊辛模型的临界底薪。初始化后，我们应用深度Floquet驱动，以保持新兴共形对称性，使我们能够根据连续体理论的分析结果来基准测试格子动力学。在由此产生的保形加热阶段，我们从洛施密特回声的普适衰变中提取与伊辛普适性类（$c=1/2$）一致的中心电荷，并观察场论预测的空间能量局部化。相反，非加热阶段表现出全球有限时间复兴。这项工作为探索关键量子动力学建立了一个可扩展且通用的框架。


### Abstract

Quantum simulations are traditionally confined to exploring dynamics starting from unentangled or low-entanglement states due to severe bottlenecks in protocol design, hardware performance, and classical verification. Here, we report the first experimental observation of non-equilibrium dynamics initiated directly from a many-body critical state. Using a fully-connected trapped-ion processor, we prepare the critical ground state of a transverse-field Ising model via a hardware-tailored, logarithmic-depth quantum circuit based on multi-scale entanglement renormalization. Following this initialization, we apply a deep Floquet drive that maintains emergent conformal symmetry, enabling us to benchmark the lattice dynamics against analytical results from continuum theory. In the resulting conformal heating phase, we extract a central charge consistent with the Ising universality class ($c=1/2$) from the universal decay of the Loschmidt echo and observe spatial energy localization predicted by field theory. Conversely, the non-heating phase exhibits global finite-time revivals. This work establishes a scalable and versatile framework for exploring critical quantum dynamics.

## 金刚石光学力学中的例外点

**Original Title:** Exceptional points in diamond optomechanics

**Authors:** Waleed El-Sayed, Elham Zohari, Joe Itoi, Peyman Parsa, Gustavo de Oliveira Luiz, Joseph E. Losby, Misa Hayashida, Marek Malac, Paul E. Barclay

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.27536v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.27536v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-26


### 摘要

多模式腔光学机械系统允许光耦合否则非相互作用的机械共振器，从而实现非厄米特现象，例如异常点，其中耦合模式的本征频率和本征载体合并。定义一个例外点及其附近的参数空间是迈向手征模式动力学和拓扑状态转移的第一步。金刚石光机设备支持将共振调谐到特殊点所需的强一致光机耦合，以及混合量子技术与旋转缺陷的应变耦合，但尚未用于多模式非埃尔米特物理学。在这里，我们调谐到钻石光机晶体中的一个特殊点，该晶体利用结构对称性破缺来产生两个耦合到光腔的高频机械共振。异常点是在低于音素激光阈值的稳定操作窗口内达到的，并且我们观察到混合模式之间光学机械衰减和反衰减的不对称重新分布。这些结果将金刚石光机晶体确立为非埃尔米特光学力学的平台，为混合旋转-音素界面中的拓扑机械动力学开辟了道路。


### Abstract

Multimode cavity optomechanical systems allow light to couple otherwise non-interacting mechanical resonators, enabling non-Hermitian phenomena such as exceptional points, where eigenfrequencies and eigenvectors of coupled modes coalesce. Accessing an exceptional point and its nearby parameter space is a first step towards chiral mode dynamics and topological state transfer. Diamond optomechanical devices support strong coherent optomechanical coupling required to tune resonances to an exceptional point, as well as strain-coupling to spin-defects for hybrid quantum technologies, but have not yet been used for multimode non-Hermitian physics. Here we tune to an exceptional point in a diamond optomechanical crystal, which uses structural symmetry breaking to produce two high-frequency mechanical resonances coupled to an optical cavity. The exceptional point is reached within a stable operating window below the phonon-lasing threshold, and we observe asymmetric redistribution of optomechanical damping and anti-damping between hybridized modes. ### 摘要

多个用户之间共享纠缠仍然是可扩展量子网络的核心挑战。最近的工作提出了一种按需纠缠分组架构，其中控制器使用时分多址（CDMA）方法来分配网络资源。量子节点被分配一个周期性的时间表，该时间表概率地满足端到端纠缠的应用程序请求。使用知名算法（例如最早截止日期优先（EDF））定期重新计算时间表。然而，当结果是随机的且到达是同步的时，静态时间表提供的灵活性有限。为了克服这一限制，我们提出了一种在线调度器，可以动态调度、推迟、再试或放弃纠缠分发保留。在我们的模拟中，动态调度器比静态基线实现了更短的完成时间、更高的完成率和更高的吞吐量。此外，当网络过载时，动态调度器继续构建截止日期可行的调度并优雅降级。


### Abstract

Sharing entanglement among multiple users remains a central challenge for scalable quantum networks. Recent work proposed an on-demand entanglement packet architecture in which a controller uses a Time Division Multiple Access (TDMA) approach to allocate network resources. However, a static schedule offers limited flexibility when outcomes are stochastic and arrivals are asynchronous. To overcome this limitation, we propose an online scheduler that dynamically schedules, defers, retries, or drops entanglement distribution reservations. Furthermore, when the network is overloaded, the dynamic scheduler continues to construct deadline-feasible schedules and degrades gracefully.
