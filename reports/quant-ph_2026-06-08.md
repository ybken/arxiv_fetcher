## 带反馈环的钍-229光学核钟

**Original Title:** A thorium-229 optical nuclear clock with feedback loop

**Authors:** L. Toscani De Col, T. Riebner, I. Morawetz, F. Schneider, N. Sempelmann, J. Schlachet-Lépinay, F. Schaden, M. Bartokos, G. A. Kazakov, K. Beeks, B. Gerstenecker, M. Pimon, S. Lahs, A. Hellerschmied, T. Lercher, J. Premper, A. Niessner, M. Matus, H. Denker, M. Cizek, O. Cip, V. Lal, G. Zitzer, V. Petrov, J. Tiedau, M. V. Okhapkin, E. Peik, T. Schumm

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.04997v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.04997v2

**Submitted:** 2026-06-03


### 摘要

钍-229同位素的激光可访问核转变已被确定为实现光学核钟的有希望的候选者。这样的核时钟可能会与基于原子或离子电子壳层转变的当前光学时钟相媲美或优于基于原子或离子电子壳层转变的光学时钟，预计对外部扰动更加稳健，并在物理学基本原理的基于时钟的测试中提供更高的灵敏度。在这里，我们通过基于连续吸收光谱的快速反馈将连续波激光稳定到148纳米核转变，来实现钍-229核时钟。钍-229原子核嵌入毫米大小的室温CGM晶体中。将148纳米辐射的亚调和频连续与ZB+单离子时钟进行比较。核钟显示了$3 ' cdot 10 ' s的分数频率不稳定性的简单散粒噪音限制缩放，其中$T $是平均时间，连续运行1天内接近$10 ' s $不稳定性。预计未来的固体核钟的不稳定性将改善几个数量级。我们使用核钟通过在20秒到1天之间的时间尺度上寻找核过渡能的周期性波动和缓慢漂移来约束超轻暗物质的模型。得益于钍-229跃迁增强的灵敏度，这些约束与暗物质与光子耦合的最佳原子钟竞争，并超越了先前关于与强力和夸克耦合的测量。


### Abstract

The laser-accessible nuclear transition in the thorium-229 isotope has been identified as a promising candidate for the realization of an optical nuclear clock. Such a nuclear clock might rival or outperform current optical clocks based on electron-shell transitions in atoms or ions, is expected to be more robust against external perturbations, and provides enhanced sensitivity in clock-based tests of fundamental principles of physics. Here, we implement a thorium-229 nuclear clock by stabilizing a continuous-wave laser to the 148 nm nuclear transition with rapid feedback based on continuous absorption spectroscopy. The thorium-229 nuclei are embedded into a millimeter-sized, room temperature calcium fluoride crystal. A subharmonic of the 148 nm radiation is continuously compared to a Yb+ single-ion clock. The nuclear clock shows a simple shot-noise limited scaling of the fractional frequency instability of $3\cdot 10^{-12} \sqrt{τ/\text{s}}$ where $τ$ is the averaging time, approaching $10^{-15}$ instabilities over 1 day of continuous operation. Improvements of the instability by several orders of magnitude can be projected for future solid-state nuclear clocks. We use the nuclear clock to constrain models of ultralight dark matter by searching for periodic fluctuations and slow drifts in the nuclear transition energy, on time scales between 20 s and 1 day. Drawing benefit from the enhanced sensitivity of the thorium-229 transition, these constraints compete with the best atomic clocks concerning dark matter coupling to photons and go beyond previous measurements regarding coupling to the strong force and quarks.

## 基于qubo和混合量子算法的铁路短期集中发车场景下发车排序和区间轨道分配协调优化

**Original Title:** Coordinated optimization of departure sequencing and section-track allocation in railway short-term concentrated departure scenarios based on qubo and hybrid quantum algorithms

**Authors:** Xiaobin Li, Yanbin Gao, Weiguang Wang, Xuechen Liang

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06543v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.06543v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-04


### 摘要

本研究探讨了铁路短期集中发车场景下发车顺序和区间轨道分配的协调优化问题。提出了一种二次无约束二元优化（QUBO）模型，以在统一的二元框架内表示出发位置分配和路段路径选择。由于调度方案的质量取决于静态组合模型无法完全捕捉的时间相关运营相互作用，因此引入了基于模拟的评估层来评估路段占用、中间站等待、平台容量压力、运行时间波动和延迟传播。在这个分层框架中，在相同的决策结构上比较了传统启发式算法、量子启发算法和混合算法。结果表明，QUBO模型在解码后能产生可行的候选方案，而仿真层则能清晰地区分出竞争算法在正常和干扰条件下的运行性能。在测试场景中，QPSO-QAOA在正常情况下性能最好，而量子增强方法在动态情况下相对于传统方法平均降低了4.28%~ 26.26%的综合代价和4.37%~ 24.25%的总时延.这些研究结果表明，基于QUBO的建模和基于仿真的评估的集成提供了一个有用的方法框架，铁路短期集中发车调度，虽然与实际运营数据的验证仍然是必要的。


### Abstract

This study examines the coordinated optimization of departure sequencing and section-track allocation in railway short-term concentrated departure scenarios. A quadratic unconstrained binary optimization (QUBO) model is formulated to represent departure-position assignment and section-track selection within a unified binary framework. Because the quality of a dispatching scheme depends on time-dependent operational interactions that cannot be fully captured by a static combinatorial model, a simulation-based evaluation layer is introduced to assess section occupation, intermediate-station waiting, platform-capacity pressure, running-time fluctuations, and delay propagation. Within this layered framework, conventional heuristics, quantum-inspired algorithms, and hybrid algorithms are compared on the same decision structure. The results show that the QUBO model can generate feasible candidate schemes after decoding, while the simulation layer clearly differentiates the operational performance of the competing algorithms under both normal and disturbed conditions. In the tested scenarios, QPSO-QAOA performs best under normal conditions, and the quantum-enhanced methods reduce comprehensive cost by 4.28\%--26.26\% and total delay by 4.37\%--24.25\% on average under dynamic conditions relative to their conventional counterparts. These findings suggest that the integration of QUBO-based modeling and simulation-based evaluation provides a useful methodological framework for railway short-term concentrated departure scheduling, although validation with real operational data remains necessary.

## 超越规范协议：从秘密共享访问结构进行量子加密克隆

**Original Title:** Beyond the Canonical Protocol: Quantum Encrypted Cloning from Secret-Sharing Access Structures

**Authors:** Gabriele Gianini, Stelvio Cimato, Jianyi Lin, Omar Hasan, Corrado Mio, Ernesto Damiani

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06552v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.06552v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-04


### 摘要

量子加密克隆表明，未知的量子状态可以分布到多个加密副本中，而不会与不可克隆定理相矛盾：每个副本单独不可用，但可以与合适的量子密钥一起兑换。最近的工作将规范的克隆协议与特定形式的量子秘密共享联系起来。在这里，我们采取相反的观点：我们没有将给定的预定克隆协议映射到QSS，而是使用QSS访问结构作为可以从中提取预定克隆方案的设计库。标准是访问结构性的。只要量子授权克隆结构包含具有非合格公共交集的合格集族，QSS方案就支持量子授权克隆结构。公共子系统被解释为密钥，而非公共部分被解释为相对于该密钥的加密克隆。因此，量子加密克隆并不需要QSS之外的可恢复性新概念;变化是QSS成分的操作解读，作为延迟和替代赎回机会的机制。这种观点将救赎与完全保密区分开来。完美的QSS产生具有禁止的非合格子系统的授权克隆方案，而斜坡QSS自然允许中间的、部分信息的非赎回子系统。由此产生的框架将量子加密克隆从特定协议扩展到通用访问结构基元。我们通过类阈值、斜坡、分层和分隔架构来说明提取原理，展示加密克隆如何对称或不对称、单独或复合、完全隐藏或泄露。同样，这些结构可以被视为等距量子码的重叠擦除恢复区域。这将秘密共享确立为加密量子冗余的系统设计语言。


### Abstract

Quantum encrypted cloning shows that an unknown quantum state can be distributed into multiple encrypted copies without contradicting the no-cloning theorem: each copy is unusable on its own, but can be redeemed together with a suitable quantum key. Recent work has related canonical encrypted-cloning protocols to particular forms of quantum secret sharing. Here we take the converse perspective: instead of mapping a given encrypted-cloning protocol into QSS, we use QSS access structures as a design library from which encrypted-cloning schemes can be extracted. The criterion is access-structural. A QSS scheme supports a quantum encrypted-cloning structure whenever it contains a family of qualified sets with a non-qualified common intersection. The common subsystem is interpreted as the key, while the non-common parts are interpreted as encrypted clones relative to that key. Thus quantum encrypted cloning does not require a new notion of recoverability beyond QSS; what changes is the operational reading of QSS constituents as a mechanism for delayed and alternative redemption opportunities. This viewpoint separates redemption from perfect secrecy. Perfect QSS yields encrypted-cloning schemes with forbidden non-qualified subsystems, whereas ramp QSS naturally allows intermediate, partially informative non-redeeming subsystems. The resulting framework broadens quantum encrypted cloning from a specific protocol to a general access-structure primitive. We illustrate the extraction principle with threshold-like, ramp, hierarchical, and compartmented architectures, showing how encrypted clones may be symmetric or asymmetric, individual or composite, perfectly hidden or leaky. Equivalently, these constructions can be viewed as overlapping erasure-recovery regions of an isometric quantum code. This establishes secret sharing as a systematic design language for encrypted quantum redundancy.

## 来自\（\coth\）-Madelung anassis的幅度相关量子流体动力学

**Original Title:** Amplitude-dependent quantum hydrodynamics from a \(\coth\)-Madelung ansatz

**Authors:** C. Dedes

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06561v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.06561v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-04


### 摘要

我们研究了马德隆变换的非线性扩展，该扩展基于形式为\[ð = Re '{\imath Thou\coth R}，\]的双曲相-幅度耦合，其中\（R\）是实幅度场，\（Thou\）是由薛定格方程控制的辅助相坐标。与传统的极分解相反，这种结构在幅度和相之间强加了奇异的双曲关系，从而赋予了波西米亚或流体动力描述固有的几何结构。我们表明，相关的速度场获得了密度梯度的贡献，从而产生了广义连续性方程和修改的量子力项。当被解释为复杂的宏观序参数时，这种广义相结构会导致修改的超电动力学;特别是，伦敦方程获得了额外的贡献，使迈斯纳响应对空间密度梯度敏感。提出的框架是受到涉及量子系统中复杂群速、消散波传播和幅度敏感输运的更广泛发展的推动。


### Abstract

We investigate a nonlinear extension of the Madelung transformation based on a hyperbolic phase--amplitude coupling of the form \[ Ψ= R e^{\imathθ\coth R}, \] where \(R\) is a real amplitude field and \(θ\) is an auxiliary phase coordinate governed by Schrödinger's equation. In contrast to the conventional polar decomposition, this construction imposes a singular hyperbolic relation between amplitude and phase, thereby endowing the Bohmian or hydrodynamic description with an intrinsically geometric structure. We show that the associated velocity field acquires a density-gradient contribution, producing generalized continuity equations and modified quantum force terms. When interpreted as a complex macroscopic order parameter, this generalized phase structure leads to modified superconducting electrodynamics; in particular, the London equations acquire additional contributions that render the Meissner response sensitive to spatial density gradients. The proposed framework is motivated by broader developments involving complex group velocities, dissipative wave propagation, and amplitude-sensitive transport in quantum systems.

## 三角切割稀疏化的量子算法

**Original Title:** Quantum Algorithms for Triangle Cut Sparsification

**Authors:** Shan Jiang, Pan Peng

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06287v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.06287v2

**Submitted:** 2026-06-04


### 摘要

Triangles capture higher-order structures in graphs and are fundamental to applications such as clustering and network analysis. To enable efficient use of such structures at scale, we study the problem of triangle cut sparsification, which aims to reduce the graph size while approximately preserving triangle counts across every cut. We investigate quantum algorithms for this problem, using triangle listing as our main technical ingredient. In particular, we present a quantum algorithm for triangle listing that, for a graph with $n$ vertices, $m$ edges, and $t$ triangles, runs in time $T_{\mathrm{q\text{-}list}} =$ $\widetilde{O}\bigl(\min(n^{5/4}t^{7/12} + n^{7/6}t^{7/9}, m + m^{3/4}t^{1/2},$ $n^{3/2}t^{1/2})\bigr)$, improving upon the best known classical bounds over a broad range of parameters. Our algorithm is based on a heavy-light vertex partition and an extension of triangle detection via quantum walks and Grover search. Leveraging this result, we design a quantum algorithm for constructing $\varepsilon$-triangle cut sparsifiers of size $\widetilde{O}(n/\varepsilon^2)$ in time $\widetilde{O}(T_{\mathrm{q\text{-}list}} + \sqrt{mn}/\varepsilon)$. Finally, we demonstrate applications to clustering algorithms based on triangle-related measures and prove a lower bound of $Ω(n/\varepsilon^2)$ on the size of any $\varepsilon$-triangle cut sparsifiers.

## 图形状态的乐趣：非本地贝尔对和Arf不变量

**Original Title:** Fun with Graph States: Nonlocal Bell Pairs and the Arf Invariant

**Authors:** Bartlomiej Czech, Yichen Feng, Xianlai Wu, Minjun Xie

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06582v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.06582v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-04


### 摘要

我们研究图状态的内能积和部分幅度--这是一种常用的量子状态，由图指定。我们发现这些量的大小与F_2上图的邻矩阵的阶有关，而相则由其二次细化的Arf不变量决定。这些事实激发了希尔伯特空间的非局部张量因式分解，对于该空间，所有图状态都是贝尔对与未纠缠的附属物的产物。这些结果可能说明了基于测量的量子计算框架中的量子优势，并表明可以用代数布局语言有效地可视化图状态。此外，我们开发了一种专门的技术来计算图状态中量子位排列的期望值，这对于计算多重不变量很有用。


### Abstract

We study inner products and partial amplitudes of graph states--a commonly employed class of quantum states, which are specified by graphs. We find that the magnitudes of these quantities are simply related to the rank of the adjacency matrix of the graph over F_2 while the phase is determined by the Arf invariant of its quadratic refinement. These facts motivate a nonlocal tensor factorization of the Hilbert space, with respect to which all graph states are products of Bell pairs with unentangled ancillae. These results may illuminate the quantum advantage in the framework of Measurement-Based Quantum Computation and suggest that graph states can be usefully visualized in the language of algebraic topology. In addition, we develop a specialized technique for computing expectation values of qubit-wise permutations in graph states, which is useful for calculating multi-invariants.

## 用运算子代数量子错误纠正揭开客观性的神秘面纱

**Original Title:** Demystifying Objectivity with Operator Algebra Quantum Error Correction

**Authors:** Marin Girard, Gong Cheng, ChunJun Cao

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06588v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.06588v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-04


### 摘要

量子达尔文主义扩展了退相干形式主义，以解释经典性和客观性如何从量子力学中产生。然而，现有的方法往往只捕捉客观性的部分方面，导致其被错误描述，并使其难以精确确定。通过将量子达尔文主义与操作员代数量子错误纠正联系起来，我们表明客观性的出现可以与量子码的代数局部可恢复性相一致。将这个代数框架应用于稳定器代码，我们表明它可以产生更精确的经典性和冗余性的描述，统一了传统的客观性测量，通过编码理论工具实现高效分类，并支持去相关动态的大规模Clifford模拟。


### Abstract

Quantum Darwinism extends the decoherence formalism to explain how classicality and objectivity emerge from quantum mechanics. However, existing approaches often capture only partial aspects of objectivity, leading to its mischaracterization and making it difficult to pin down precisely. By connecting quantum Darwinism to operator algebra quantum error correction, we show that the emergence of objectivity can be identified with the algebraic local recoverability of quantum codes. Applying this algebraic framework to stabilizer codes, we show that it yields a far more precise characterization of classicality and redundancy, unifies the traditional measures of objectivity, enables efficient classification via coding-theoretic tools, and supports large-scale Clifford simulations of decoherence dynamics.

## 载体Hopfield网络中的量子稳定模式

**Original Title:** Quantum-stabilized patterns in a vector Hopfield network

**Authors:** Richard D. Barney, Sharba Bhattacharjee, Victor Galitski, Kartiek Agarwal, Ivar Martin

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06597v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.06597v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-04


### 摘要

我们引入了量子载体Hopfield网络，其中图案由量子载体旋转的方向形成;量子动力学本质上源于旋转操作员的非交换性。我们推导出了这个网络及其经典网络的状态方程和相图。令人惊讶的是，我们发现量子涨落稳定了存储的模式。相对于经典网络，临界检索温度和目标模式重叠都得到了增强。此外，我们发现这种增强随着模式加载到网络容量而增长。我们将这种效应解释为量子有序有序的模拟，这是量子涨落促进有序相形成的机制。这些发现为量子增强联想记忆提供了一条新途径。


### Abstract

We introduce the quantum vector Hopfield network, in which patterns are formed by orientations of quantum vector spins; quantum dynamics arise intrinsically from the non-commutativity of the spin operators. Both the critical retrieval temperature and the target pattern overlap are enhanced relative to the classical network. Additionally, we find that this enhancement grows with pattern loading up to network capacity. We interpret this effect as an analog of quantum order-by-disorder, a mechanism by which quantum fluctuations promote the formation of ordered phases. These findings offer a new route to quantum-enhanced associative memory.

## 具有格子外科逻辑运算的超导表面代码处理器

### 摘要

容错逻辑操作是可扩展量子计算的基础。在这里，我们报告了在平面超导体处理器上一对距离三个表面代码逻辑量子比特之间的格子外科手术操作的实验实现。在重复的综合症提取周期中，在泄露事件被拒绝后，逻辑量子位的每周期错误率分别为$0.0365（2）$和$0.0282（1）$。通过利用联合初始化和格子分裂，我们确定性地准备逻辑贝尔状态，通过错误纠正的逻辑状态保真度确认真正的双方纠缠。我们进一步在逻辑层面执行两量子位Deutsch-Jozsa算法，以展示算法在容差框架中的实用性。最后，为了实现普适控制，我们实现了魔状态注入和门传送，以实现围绕逻辑$X$轴的连续非克利福德旋转。对于逻辑$R_{X}（pi/4）$门，在未检测到错误的情况下，我们实现了$0.943_{-9}^{+10}$的逻辑门保真度。这些结果将格子手术确立为近期表面代码架构中逻辑计算的实用且通用的范式，代表着在高温电路中实现可扩展的容错量子优势的关键里程碑。


### Abstract

Fault-tolerant logical operations are fundamental for scalable quantum computation. Here, we report the experimental realization of lattice-surgery operations between a pair of distance-three surface-code logical qubits on a planar superconducting processor. We further execute a two-qubit Deutsch-Jozsa algorithm at the logical level to demonstrate algorithmic utility in a fault-tolerant framework. Finally, to achieve universal control, we implement magic-state injection and gate teleportation to realize continuous non-Clifford rotations about the logical $X$ axis. For the logical $R_{X}(π/4)$ gate, we achieve a logical gate fidelity of $0.943_{-9}^{+10}$ conditioned on the absence of detected errors. These results establish lattice surgery as a practical and versatile paradigm for logical computation in near-term surface-code architectures, representing a critical milestone toward scalable fault-tolerant quantum advantage in superconducting circuits.

## QBism重建计划中的量子相关性

**Original Title:** Quantum correlations in QBism's reconstruction program

**Authors:** Sachin Gupta, Jacques Pienaar

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07485v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.07485v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-05


### 摘要

QBism将量子理论重新定义为一个主体概率分配的规范性框架，玻恩规则采用了被称为Urgleichung的一致性条件的形式。受这种观点的启发，qplex理论提供了更广泛的概率模型，其中有效状态和测量的集合受到QBist启发的几何条件的约束。虽然qplexes已被广泛研究的单一系统，其影响的二分相关性仍然在很大程度上未被探索。在这项工作中，我们调查二分相关的qplex理论表示联合期望值适当定义的$C$-向量之间的内积。这种几何公式允许Bell型不等式作为qplex兼容概率分配上的优化问题来研究。我们首先分析CHSH场景，并表明$C$-vector的共享内能积结构将最大值限制为Tsirelson界$2\SQRT{2}$。然后，我们转向三结果CGLMP不等式$I_{2233}$，发现相同的qplex衍生的规范和内积约束允许代数最大值4，从而表现出超量子相关性。这些结果表明，qplex几何捕获了足够的结构来重现双结果情况下的重要量子束缚，但不足以恢复全套量子相关约束。因此，分析表明，需要额外的原则来完成量子理论的QBist重建。


### Abstract

QBism recasts quantum theory as a normative framework for an agent's probability assignments, with the Born rule taking the form of a consistency condition known as the Urgleichung. Motivated by this perspective, qplex theories provide a broader class of probabilistic models in which the sets of valid states and measurements are constrained by QBist-inspired geometric conditions. While qplexes have been extensively studied for single systems, their implications for bipartite correlations remain largely unexplored. In this work, we investigate bipartite correlations in qplex theories by expressing joint expectation values as inner products between suitably defined $C$-vectors. This geometric formulation allows Bell-type inequalities to be studied as optimization problems over qplex-compatible probability assignments. We first analyze the CHSH scenario and show that the shared inner-product structure of the $C$-vectors restricts the maximal value to the Tsirelson bound $2\sqrt{2}$. We then turn to the three-outcome CGLMP inequality $I_{2233}$ and find that the same qplex-derived norm and inner-product constraints allow the algebraic maximum of 4, thereby exhibiting superquantum correlations. These results show that qplex geometry captures enough structure to reproduce an important quantum bound in the two-outcome case, but not enough to recover the full set of quantum correlation constraints. The analysis therefore suggests that additional principles are needed to complete the QBist reconstruction of quantum theory.
