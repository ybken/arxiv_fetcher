## 生成有限次零群的元素数的概率界及其应用

**Original Title:** Probabilistic Bounds on the Number of Elements to Generate Finite Nilpotent Groups and Their Applications

**Authors:** Ziyuan Dong, Xiang Fan, Tengxun Zhong, Daowen Qiu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.19494v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.19494v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-23


### 摘要

这项工作建立了生成有限次零群的元素数量的新概率界。令$\varphy_k（G）$表示$k$随机元素生成有限次零群$G$的概率。对于任何$0 <e < 1$，我们证明$\varphy_k（G）\ge 1 -e $ if $k \ge \operatorName{rank}（G）+ \lceil\log_2（2/e）\rceil$（基于群排名的界限）或if $k \ge \operatorName{len}（G）+ \lceil\log_2（1/e）\rceil$（基于群链长度的界限）。此外，这些界限几乎很紧。这两个界限都提高了之前已知的$k \ge \lceil\log_2的要求|G| +\log_2（1/e）\rceil + 2$。我们的结果为分析概率算法提供了一个基础工具，能够更好地估计有限阿贝尔隐藏子群问题（AHSP）标准量子算法的迭代次数，并减少雷格夫因子分解算法所需的电路重复。


### Abstract

This work establishes a new probabilistic bound on the number of elements to generate finite nilpotent groups. Let $\varphi_k(G)$ denote the probability that $k$ random elements generate a finite nilpotent group $G$. For any $0 < ε< 1$, we prove that $\varphi_k(G) \ge 1 - ε$ if $k \ge \operatorname{rank}(G) + \lceil \log_2(2/ε) \rceil$ (a bound based on the group rank) or if $k \ge \operatorname{len}(G) + \lceil \log_2(1/ε) \rceil$ (a bound based on the group chain length). Moreover, these bounds are shown to be nearly tight. Both bounds sharpen the previously known requirement of $k \ge \lceil \log_2 |G| + \log_2(1/ε) \rceil + 2$. Our results provide a foundational tool for analyzing probabilistic algorithms, enabling a better estimation of the iteration count for the finite Abelian hidden subgroup problem (AHSP) standard quantum algorithm and a reduction in the circuit repetitions required by Regev's factoring algorithm.

## 正规化密度函数的神经网络逼近

**Original Title:** Neural network approximation of regularized density functionals

**Authors:** Mihály A. Csirik, Andre Laestadius, Mathias Oster

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.18512v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.18512v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-23


### 摘要

密度函数理论是量子力学中最有效、最广泛使用的计算方法之一，尤其是在固体物理和量子化学等领域。从理论上看，它的中心对象是普适密度函数，它包含有关量子系统的所有固有信息。一旦提供了外部势，原则上可以通过简单的最小化来获得确切的接地能量。然而，普适密度函数是一个非常复杂的数学对象，几乎总是被其近似变体所取代。到目前为止，还没有设计出具有普遍适用性的“第一原则”、数学上一致的收敛逼近程序。在本文中，我们提出了这样一个过程，首先应用Moreau-Yosida正规化来使精确函式连续（甚至可微），然后通过神经网络逼近正规函式。由此产生的神经网络保留了精确函数的正性和凸性。更重要的是，它是可微的，因此可以直接用于Kohn-Sham计算。


### Abstract

Density functional theory is one of the most efficient and widely used computational methods of quantum mechanics, especially in fields such as solid state physics and quantum chemistry. From the theoretical perspecive, its central object is the universal density functional which contains all intrinsic information about the quantum system in question. Once the external potential is provided, in principle one can obtain the exact ground-state energy via a simple minimization. However, the universal density functional is a very complicated mathematical object and almost always it is replaced with its approximate variants. So far, no ``first principles'', mathematically consistent and convergent approximation procedure has been devised that has general applicability. In this paper, we propose such a procedure by first applying Moreau--Yosida regularization to make the exact functionals continuous (even differentiable) and then approximate the regularized functional by a neural network. The resulting neural network preserves the positivity and convexity of the exact functionals. More importantly, it is differentiable, so it can be directly used in a Kohn--Sham calculation.

## 量子机器学习用于湍流的高效降阶建模

**Original Title:** Quantum machine learning for efficient reduced order modelling of turbulent flows

**Authors:** Han Li, Yutong Lou, Dunhui Xiao

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.18552v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.18552v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-23


### 摘要

由于湍流的高维度和固有的非线性，准确预测湍流仍然是流体动力学领域的核心挑战。量子算法和机器学习的最新发展为克服湍流建模固有的计算障碍提供了新的机会。在这里，我们提出了一种新的混合量子-经典框架，该框架通过集成机器学习、量子计算和流体动力学建模（特别是降阶建模）来实现比实时更快的湍流预测。该新框架将量子固有正交分解（QPod）与量子增强深度核学习（QDKL）方法相结合。QPod利用量子电路执行高效的特征值分解来进行低阶流重建，而QDKL利用量子纠缠和非线性映射来增强核表达能力和动态预测准确性。新方法在三种基准湍流上进行了演示，我们的架构在降低模型等级的情况下实现了显着提高的预测准确性，与经典同行相比，训练速度快了10倍，参数计数减少了1/N倍，其中N是输入维度。尽管受到当前有噪的中等规模量子（NISQ）硬件的限制，但我们的结果证明了量子机器学习在改变湍流模拟方面的潜力，并为未来量子计算机中的可扩展、实时量子流体建模奠定坚实的基础。


### Abstract

Accurately predicting turbulent flows remains a central challenge in fluid dynamics due to their high dimensionality and intrinsic nonlinearity. Recent developments in quantum algorithms and machine learning offer new opportunities for overcoming the computational barriers inherent in turbulence modeling. Here we present a new hybrid quantum-classical framework that enables faster-than-real-time turbulence prediction by integrating machine learning, quantum computation, and fluid dynamics modeling, in particular, the reduced-order modeling. The novel framework combines quantum proper orthogonal decomposition (QPOD) with a quantum-enhanced deep kernel learning (QDKL) approach. QPOD employs quantum circuits to perform efficient eigenvalue decomposition for low-rank flow reconstruction, while QDKL exploits quantum entanglement and nonlinear mappings to enhance kernel expressivity and dynamic prediction accuracy. The new method is demonstrated on three benchmark turbulent flows, our architecture achieves significantly improved predictive accuracy at reduced model ranks, with training speeds up to 10 times faster and parameter counts reduced by a factor of 1/N compared to classical counterparts, where N is the input dimensionality. Although constrained by current noisy intermediate-scale quantum (NISQ) hardware, our results demonstrate the potential of quantum machine learning to transform turbulence simulation and lay a solid foundation for scalable, real-time quantum fluid modeling in future quantum computers.

### 摘要

### Abstract

We propose a complete quantum-classical hybrid branch-and-bound algorithm (QCBB) to solve binary linear programs with equality constraints. That includes bound calculation, convergence metrics and optimality guarantee to the quantum optimization based algorithm, which makes our method directly comparable to classical methods. Key aspects of the proposed algorithm are (i) encapsulation of the quantum optimization method, (ii) utilization of noisy samples for problem reduction, (iii) classical approximation based bound calculation, (iv) branch and bound traits like gap-based stopping criterion and monotonic increase in solution quality, (v) integrated composition of many different solutions that can be improved individually. We show numerical results on set partitioning problem instances and provide many details about the characteristics of the different steps of the algorithm.

## 一维分数量子媒体中的可调谐能带

**Original Title:** Tunable Bands in 1D Fractional Quantum Media

**Authors:** Brenden R. Guyette, Joshua M. Lewis, Lincoln D. Carr

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.18574v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.18574v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-23


### 摘要

分数微积分已成为地球物理学、光学和生物系统中捕捉远程相关性和异常输运的重要框架。在本文中，我们扩展分数阶微积分来探索周期势中的粒子，其中薛定汉方程被推广到其分数阶形式。该框架使我们能够研究莱维指数$q$如何支配能带的形成和倒置，从而提供了一种通过调整周期性量子系统中的$q$来设计新的物理行为和设备功能的途径。我们使用辅助时间进化算法求解不同高度$V_0$、阻挡层厚度$L$和井宽$W$的周期矩形势的分数薛定汉方程，并通过高斯过程回归补充离散能量分散。我们的分析揭示了$q=2$时的能带结构发生了质的转变，分为$q>2$和$q<2$的状态。对于$q > 2$，随着对称极小值出现在第一布里渊区内，能带会发生倒置变换，随着$q$的增加，从$k=0$向$k=\pi/a$移动。这些简并极小值定义了布洛赫动量量子位，表明类似于谷电子学中使用的谷自由度。接地带的响应按分数阶缩放为$V_0 '{-0.28\pm0.05}L ' p m0.08}W ' s ' s ' p m0.06}$，表明对几何形状的灵敏度可调。对于$q < 2$，$k = 0$附近的有效质量随$q$呈指数下降，当$q \到1$时，产生的通用有效质量为0.15\pm0.01 $，表明Lévy指数作为一个可调的自由度，能够驱动带反转，调制带隙，重塑载流子动力学。


### Abstract

Fractional calculus has become an essential framework in geophysics, optics, and biological systems to capture long-range correlations and anomalous transport. In this article, we extend fractional calculus to explore a particle in a periodic potential, where the Schrödinger equation is generalized to its fractional form. This framework allows us to study how the Lévy index $q$ governs the formation and inversion of energy bands, offering a pathway to engineer new physical behaviors and device functionalities by tuning $q$ in periodic quantum systems. We solve the fractional Schrödinger equation for periodic rectangular potentials of varying height $V_0$, barrier thickness $L$, and well width $W$ using an imaginary-time evolution algorithm, and supplement the discrete energy dispersion through Gaussian process regression. Our analysis reveals a qualitative shift in the band structure at $q=2$, separating into regimes for $q>2$ and $q<2$. For $q > 2$, energy bands undergo an inverting transformation as symmetric minima emerge within the first Brillouin zone, shifting from $k=0$ toward $k=\pm π/a$ with increasing $q$. These degenerate minima define a Bloch-momentum qubit, suggesting an analog to valley degrees of freedom used in valleytronics. The response of the ground band scales with fractional order as $V_0^{-0.28\pm0.05}L^{-0.34\pm0.08}W^{-0.49\pm0.06}$, indicating tunable sensitivity to geometry. For $q < 2$, the effective mass near $k = 0$ decreases exponentially with $q$, yielding a universal effective mass of $0.15\pm0.01$ as $q \to 1$, demonstrating that the Lévy index serves as a tunable degree of freedom capable of driving band inversion, modulating the band gap, and reshaping carrier dynamics.

## HOPPS：量子电路的硬件感知最佳阶段多项合成和边界优化

**Authors:** Xinpeng Li, Ji Liu, Shuai Xu, Paul Hovland, Vipin Chaudhary

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.18770v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.18770v1

**Submitted:** 2025-11-24


### 摘要

由{C诺特，Rz}组成的块在现代量子应用中无处不在，特别是在QAOA分析和量子加法器等电路中。编译后，其中许多都表现出很大的C诺特计数或深度，这降低了保真度。因此，我们引入HOPPS：一种基于SAT的硬件感知的最佳阶段多项合成算法，可以生成具有C诺特计数或深度最优性的{C诺特，Rz}块。   有时，{C诺特，Rz}块很大，例如在QAOA分析中，HOPPS对最优性的追求限制了其可扩展性。为了解决这个问题，我们引入了一种迭代分块优化策略：将大电路划分为较小的块，对每个块进行最佳细化，并重复该过程多次迭代。   实证结果表明，与现有的接近最优的合成工具相比，HOPPS更有效。HOPPS用作窥视孔优化器，在OLPQ下将COT计数减少高达50.0%，并将COT深度减少高达57.1%。对于大型QAOA电路，经过QisKit映射后，通过我们的迭代分块优化，电路的C诺特计数和深度可以减少高达44.4%和42.4%。指数项-相多项、量子电路合成、量子电路优化。


### Abstract

Blocks composed of {CNOT, Rz} are ubiquitous in modern quantum applications, notably in circuits such as QAOA ansatzes and quantum adders. After compilation, many of them exhibit large CNOT counts or depths, which lowers fidelity. Therefore, we introduce HOPPS: a SAT-based hardware-aware optimal phase polynomial synthesis algorithm that could generate {CNOT, Rz} blocks with CNOT count or depth optimality.
  Sometime {CNOT, Rz} blocks are large, such as in QAOA ansatzes, HOPPS's pursuit of optimality limits its scalability. To address this issue, we introduce an iterative blockwise optimization strategy: large circuits are partitioned into smaller blocks, each block is optimally refined, and the process is repeated for several iterations.
  Empirical results show that HOPPS is more efficient comparing with existing near optimal synthesis tools. Used as a peephole optimizer, HOPPS reduces the CNOT count by up to 50.0% and the CNOT depth by up to 57.1% under OLSQ. For large QAOA circuit, after mapping by Qiskit, circuit can be reduced CNOT count and depth by up to 44.4% and 42.4% by our iterative blockwise optimization. Index Terms-Phase Polynomial, Quantum Circuit Synthesis, Quantum Circuit Optimization.

## 快速扭曲湍流的端到端量子模拟

### 摘要

### Abstract

We evaluate the quantum resource requirements for simulating a minimal instance of non-trivial turbulence with classical validation. The numerical results show excellent agreement with ground-truth solutions, capturing both the qualitative evolution of turbulent fields and the quantitative behavior of statistics, including the Reynolds stresses and the fluctuating velocity spectrum. Despite its linearity, rapidly distorted turbulence captures essential turbulence mechanisms and may inform the development of quantum algorithms for the Navier-Stokes equations. Our work establishes a foundation for addressing more complex turbulent phenomena on future fault-tolerant quantum computers.

## 将纠缠链接处理为安全加密密钥
