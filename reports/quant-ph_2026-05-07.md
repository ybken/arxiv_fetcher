## 海森堡和哈伯德模型基于样本的量子对角化的批判性评估

**Original Title:** A Critical Assessment of the Sample-Based Quantum Diagonalization for Heisenberg and Hubbard Models

**Authors:** Cedric Gaberle, Manpreet Singh Jattana

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.02494v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.02494v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-04


### 摘要

基于样本的量子对角化（SQD）从通过量子状态测量获得的计算基配置中构建子空间，目标是逼近多体汉密尔顿的低能量本征空间。这种方法的有效性依赖于这样的假设：物理相关状态在计算基础上允许紧凑的表示。我们通过分析直接从海森堡和哈伯德模型格子的精确基态配置构建的SQD子空间来研究这一假设。通过消除状态准备和测量效率低下，我们隔离了波函数的固有配置空间结构。我们确定了在固定准确度阈值内重现接地能量所需的最小配置数量，并发现这个数量随着系统大小呈指数级增长。值得注意的是，即使在按概率递减顺序最佳包含配置的情况下，这种缩放也会持续存在，这表明它源于波函数的内在离域，而不是采样效率低下。我们的结果表明，SQD有效地探索了配置空间的熵，但这些模型面临着根本的可扩展性限制。


### Abstract

Sample-based quantum diagonalization (SQD) constructs subspaces from computational-basis configurations obtained via measurements of a quantum state, with the goal of approximating low-energy eigenspaces of many-body Hamiltonians. The effectiveness of this approach relies on the assumption that physically relevant states admit a compact representation in the computational basis. We investigate this assumption by analyzing SQD subspaces constructed directly from configurations of exact ground states of Heisenberg and Hubbard model lattices. By eliminating state-preparation and measurement inefficiencies, we isolate the intrinsic configuration-space structure of the wavefunction. We determine the minimal number of configurations required to reproduce the ground-state energy within fixed accuracy thresholds and find that this number grows exponentially with the system size. Notably, this scaling persists even under optimal inclusion of configurations in order of decreasing probability, demonstrating that it originates from intrinsic delocalization of the wavefunction rather than sampling inefficiencies. Our results indicate that SQD effectively probes the configuration-space entropy but faces fundamental scalability limitations for these models.

## 完全积极的非信号非马尔科夫动力学

**Original Title:** Completely-positive non-signalling non-Markovian dynamics

**Authors:** Serhii Kryhin, Vivishek Sudhir

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03197v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.03197v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-04


### 摘要

我们将非马尔科夫量子动力学定义为当前状态依赖于所有过去状态的进化，并在完全正性和非信号传递的假设下完全描述其结构。由此产生的连续时间动力学是一个积分-微方程，它用记忆积分增强了Gorini-Kossakowski-Sudarshan-Lindblad方程，并且能够描述暴露于具有任何可积分功率谱密度的噪音的系统的量子状态，无需进一步的逼近。然后，我们建立了一种形式主义来评估这种一般环境下测量结果的多次相关性，从而消除了对回归定理的需要。作为一个应用，我们推导出与非马尔科夫浴相耦合的驱动两级系统的发射光谱：熟悉的Mollow三重体获得了编码浴记忆的频率相关的谱宽。我们的工作为非马尔科夫系统的量子状态提供了严格而透明的描述，为超越马尔科夫政权的状态估计和基于状态的量子控制打开了大门。


### Abstract

We define non-Markovian quantum dynamics as evolution in which the current state depends on all past states, and completely characterize its structure under the assumptions of complete positivity and non-signalling. The resulting continuous-time dynamics is an integro-differential equation that augments the Gorini-Kossakowski-Sudarshan-Lindblad equation with a memory integral, and is capable of describing the quantum state of systems exposed to noise with any integrable power spectral density with no further approximations. We then establish a formalism to evaluate multi-time correlations of measurement outcomes in this general setting, obviating the need for a regression theorem. As an application, we derive the emission spectrum of a driven two-level system coupled to a non-Markovian bath: the familiar Mollow triplet acquires a frequency-dependent linewidth that encodes the memory of the bath. Our work provides a rigorous yet transparent description of the quantum state of non-Markovian systems, opening the door for state estimation and state-based quantum control beyond the Markovian regime.

## 作为Bayesian推理问题的涌现量子动力学：批判性分析

**Original Title:** Emergent Quantum Dynamics as a Bayesian Inference Problem: A Critical Analysis

**Authors:** Thales B. S. F. Rodrigues, Lucas L. Brugger, Vinicius G. Valle, Bruno F. Rizzuti, Cristhiano Duarte

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04112v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.04112v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-05


### 摘要

粗粒度描述可用于解释信息丢失或无法完全访问的物理过程。在本文中，我们首先提出量子动力学的有效、粗粒度描述与量子条件状态形式主义之间的联系。在此过程中，我们从主观的Bayesian的角度解决了出现动态存在的必要和充分条件。尽管我们的解决方案是（准）最优的，但它所确定的动态在分析上被证明是有限的--它在逐个州的情况下解决了问题。由于这个限制，我们然后实现半定规划技术来研究四种范式场景中有效动态的存在性。这种有效动态的存在促使人们引入一种新的鲁棒性指标，该指标量化可以向微观动态添加多少噪音，而不会损害其与给定粗粒度描述的兼容性。最后，我们还展示了如何在几个例子中分析确定有效的紧急描述。


### Abstract

Coarse-grained descriptions can be used to account for physical processes in which information is lost or not entirely accessible. In this paper, we start by proposing a connection between effective, coarse-grained descriptions of quantum dynamics and the quantum conditional states formalism. In doing so, we address necessary and sufficient conditions for the existence of emergent dynamics from a subjective Bayesian point of view. Although our solution is (quasi-)optimal, the dynamics it determines are shown to be analytically limited -- it solves the problem in a state-by-state case. Due to this limitation, we then implement semidefinite programming techniques to investigate the existence of effective dynamics in four paradigmatic scenarios. The existence of such an effective dynamics motivates the introduction of a new robustness measure that quantifies how much noise can be added to a microscopic dynamics without compromising its compatibility with a given coarse-grained description. Finally, we also show how one can analytically determine a valid emergent description in several examples.

## 集体开放量子电池中通过腔失调实现功变性保护

**Original Title:** Ergotropy Protection via Cavity Detuning in Collective Open Quantum Batteries

**Authors:** Tariq Zeyad Jawad

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04042v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.04042v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-05


### 摘要

本研究调查了开放集体量子电池遭受超辐射衰变的性能和各向异性保护。通过在中间腔内采用被动光谱失调策略，分析推导并数值验证了最佳失调值（$A &*$），以光谱隔离系统并保护量子相干性，实现了单量子位高达1088%的各向异性改进，并实现了超广泛的集体优势$N \ge 3$。我们的分析解决了一个“非马尔科夫悖论”，揭示了最大化各适形并不严格要求非马尔科夫记忆;相反，通过失调来抑制环境记忆可以最佳地保留作为基本资源的一致性。不同环境的生存地图表明，热噪音比电报噪音更严重地消散了一致性。最后，我们确定有效耦合的集体放大（$g_{\rm eff} = g\SQRT{N}）$不可避免地将大型量子位阵列驱动到超强耦合区域，从而为Tavis-Cummings描述和当前的各向异性保护协议的有效性提供了量化上限$N_{\rm max}$。


### Abstract

This study investigates the performance and ergotropy protection of open collective quantum batteries subject to superradiant decay. By employing a passive spectral detuning strategy within an intermediate cavity, an optimal detuning value ($Δ^*$) is analytically derived and numerically verified to spectrally isolate the system and protect quantum coherence, achieving up to 1088% ergotropy improvement for single qubits and superextensive collective advantage for $N \ge 3$. Our analysis resolves a "non-Markovian paradox," revealing that maximizing ergotropy does not strictly require non-Markovian memory; rather, suppressing environmental memory via detuning optimally preserves coherence, which serves as the fundamental resource. Survival maps across different environments demonstrate that thermal noise dissipates coherence more severely than telegraph noise. Finally, we establish that collective amplification of the effective coupling ($g_{\rm eff} = g\sqrt{N})$ inevitably drives large qubit arrays into the ultra-strong coupling regime, providing a quantitative ceiling $N_{\rm max}$ on the validity of the Tavis-Cummings description and the current ergotropy protection protocol.

## FTPrimitiveBench：硬件驱动和偏置噪音模型下逻辑计算的基准套件

**Original Title:** FTPrimitiveBench: A Benchmark Suite For Logical Computation Under Hardware-Motivated and Biased Noise Models

**Authors:** Shuwen Kan, Adrian Harkness, Zefan Du, Rod Rofougaran, Sean Garner, Chenxu Liu, Ying Mao, Samuel Stein

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04049v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.04049v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-05


### 摘要

容错量子计算需要了解错误纠正码如何在不同物理硬件上执行。这通常是通过对高性能计算规模的逻辑电路进行噪音稳定器模拟来评估的，并结合产生相关代码距离和深度的逻辑错误率的噪音模型。均匀去极化模型是标准基线，但其均匀假设未能捕捉到真实设备的均匀性、不对称性和相关性，而泡利、测量和时空误差并不是弱耦合的。然而，这些相同的结构化特征为联合代码-硬件协同设计创造了机会，激励噪音模型更忠实地反映目标硬件，同时保持易于模拟。   我们引入FTPrimitiveBench，这是一种系统性基准测试方法，用于研究逻辑基元如何与硬件驱动的噪音交互。它支持自定义规范和代表性的结构化噪音家族：泡利偏差、测量偏差以及空间或时空非均匀性--以及核心表面代码Clifford基元的生成器：逻辑记忆、格子手术、横向逻辑Hadamard和通过格子手术的逻辑相门。我们发现结构化噪音以定性不同的方式影响这些基元，其结果由噪音模型、基元和解码器选择之间的相互作用决定。这些结果将内存基准扩展到主动逻辑计算，其中噪音结构和原始实现之间的交互很重要。通过标准化噪音模型规范和原始结构之间的联系，FTPrimitiveBench实现了QEC协议和解码器的可重复比较研究，支持硬件感知的硬件协同设计。代码：https://github.com/ShuwenKan/FTPrimitiveBench。


### Abstract

Fault-tolerant quantum computing requires understanding how error-correcting codes perform on diverse physical hardware. This is typically assessed via noisy stabilizer simulation of logical circuits at HPC scale, combined with a noise model that yields a logical error rate for the relevant code distances and depths. The uniform depolarizing model is the standard baseline, but its homogeneous assumptions fail to capture the heterogeneity, asymmetries, and correlations of real devices, where Pauli, measurement, and spatio-temporal errors are not weakly coupled. Yet these same structured features create opportunities for joint code-hardware co-design, motivating noise models that more faithfully reflect target hardware while remaining tractable to simulate.
  We introduce FTPrimitiveBench, a systematic benchmarking approach for studying how logical primitives interact with hardware-motivated noise. It supports both custom specifications and representative structured noise families: Pauli bias, measurement bias, and spatial or spatio-temporal non-uniformity -- together with generators for core surface-code Clifford primitives: logical memory, lattice surgery, transversal logical Hadamard, and the logical phase gate via lattice surgery. We find that structured noise affects these primitives in qualitatively distinct ways, with outcomes shaped by the interplay between noise model, primitive, and decoder choice. These results extend memory benchmarks to active logical computation, where the interaction between noise structure and primitive implementation matters. By standardizing the link between noise-model specification and primitive construction, FTPrimitiveBench enables reproducible comparative studies of QEC protocols and decoders, supporting hardware-aware co-design of fault-tolerant architectures. Code: https://github.com/ShuwenKan/FTPrimitiveBench.

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.04139v1

### 摘要

我们研究量子力学中亚稳势井中一般初始状态的衰变。我们根据初始状态可以分解成的共振状态，为隧道穿过屏障的概率电流提供了一个封闭形式的表达。方程中的所有成分都在半经典极限下解析计算到第一次引导阶。专门研究同调振荡的初始状态，我们推导出了随时间变化的衰变率的逼近，并通过将其与薛定格方程的数值解进行比较来证明其准确性。


### Abstract

Specializing to a coherently-oscillating initial state, we derive an approximation to the time-dependent decay rate and demonstrate its accuracy by comparing it to a numerical solution of the Schrödinger equation.

## 一般Hamilton体的多参数函数估计

**Original Title:** Multiparameter function estimation for general Hamiltonians

**Authors:** Erfan Abbasgholinejad, Sean R. Muleady, Jacob Bringewatt, Lorcan O. Conlon, Alexey V. Gorshkov

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04136v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.04136v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-05


### 摘要

用汉密尔顿量编码的物理参数的估计是量子传感和学习的核心任务。虽然估计与单个发电机耦合的单个参数的最终精度限制已经很好地确定，但估计多个参数（每个参数都与不同的且可能非交换发电机耦合）的函数的相应界限总体上仍然未知。在这里，我们推导出最终量子极限，并针对达到这一界限的一般Hamilton中的任何参数函数提出了估计协议。我们表明，尽管该任务从根本上来说是一个多参数问题，但我们的紧界简化为优化的单参数量子Cramér-Rao界，即使对于任意生成集也是如此。我们的结果统一并扩展了之前的工作，为量子系统中的最优函数估计提供了一般框架。


### Abstract

Estimation of physical parameters encoded in a Hamiltonian is a central task in quantum sensing and learning. While the ultimate precision limit for estimating a single parameter coupled to a single generator is well established, the corresponding bound for estimating a function of multiple parameters-each coupled to distinct and possibly non-commuting generators-remains unknown in general. Here, we derive the ultimate quantum limit and present an estimation protocol for any function of parameters in a general Hamiltonian that attains this bound. We show that, although the task is fundamentally a multiparameter problem, our tight bound reduces to an optimized single-parameter quantum Cramér-Rao bound, even for arbitrary generator sets. Our result unifies and extends previous works, providing a general framework for optimal function estimation in quantum systems.

## 具有四量子位检查操作符的布局子系统二元自行车代码

**Original Title:** Topological subsystem bivariate bicycle codes with four-qubit check operators

**Authors:** Zijian Liang, Yu-An Chen

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04151v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.04151v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-05


### 摘要

高速率二元自行车（BB）码是有前途的低开销量子存储器，但它们的稳定器检查的权重通常为6美元或更高，这使得综合症提取具有挑战性。我们引入子系统二元自行车（SBB）代码，这是一种描述不变的CSS子系统结构，使用本地权重（4美元的测量值）来实现BB代码逻辑结构。通过乘以相应的指标结果来推断他们的稳定综合症。我们进一步表明，可以使用基于规范操作符换能矩阵的确定性理想准则来检测描述不变CSS子系统代码中的非局部稳定器。当该标准排除非局部稳定器时，有限深度Clifford电路将规范量子位进行分离，并使用相应的BB稳定器代码识别受保护的子系统。SBB码是拓扑的，这意味着它没有非平凡的局部逻辑运算符，当且仅当相应的BB码是拓扑的。有限搜索会产生低负担的示例，包括$[[27，6，3]]$、$[[75，10，5]]$和$[[108，12，6]$;后者编码的逻辑量子位是相同块长度和距离的子系统表面代码的六倍。这些结果显示了衡量自由度如何使高速率BB逻辑结构与本地权重兼容-4美元的综合症提取。


### Abstract

High-rate bivariate bicycle (BB) codes are promising low-overhead quantum memories, but their stabilizer checks typically have weight $6$ or higher, making syndrome extraction challenging. We introduce subsystem bivariate bicycle (SBB) codes, a translation-invariant CSS subsystem construction that realizes BB-code logical structure using local weight-$4$ gauge measurements. Their stabilizer syndromes are inferred by multiplying the corresponding gauge outcomes. We further show that nonlocal stabilizers in translation-invariant CSS subsystem codes can be detected using a determinantal-ideal criterion based on the gauge-operator commutation matrix. When this criterion excludes nonlocal stabilizers, a finite-depth Clifford circuit decouples gauge qubits and identifies the protected subsystem with a corresponding BB stabilizer code. An SBB code is topological, meaning that it has no nontrivial local logical operators, if and only if the corresponding BB code is topological. A finite search yields low-overhead examples including $[[27,6,3]]$, $[[75,10,5]]$, and $[[108,12,6]]$; the latter encodes six times more logical qubits than a subsystem surface code at the same block length and distance. These results show how gauge degrees of freedom can make high-rate BB logical structure compatible with local weight-$4$ syndrome extraction.

## 超越热力学稳定性的量子临界性

**Original Title:** Quantum criticality beyond thermodynamic stability

**Authors:** Mariam Ughrelidze, Vincent P. Flynn, Emilio Cobanera, Lorenza Viola

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04153v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.04153v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-05


### 摘要

对于由热力学稳定的Hamilton描述的处于平衡状态的多体系统来说，量子临界性与多体基态的结构变化相关。然而，存在物理相关的模型，特别是某些二次玻色子汉密尔顿模型（QBH），其不具有基状态。QBH可以是动态稳定的，也可以是不稳定的。我们表明，临界性的概念对于整个一类动态稳定或处于不稳定边界的QBH来说都有意义，无论热力学稳定性如何，并且此类QBH的关键状态是自然且明确定义的准粒子真空（QPV）。该状态是高斯状态，并且如果QBH热力稳定，则与底价重合。我们确定了一个相关的光谱间隙，即克雷恩间隙，与产生和湮没操作符之间的最小光谱分离相关，并表明当克雷恩间隙为正值时，QPV是唯一的。我们证明，对于具有有限范围耦合的动态稳定QBH，相关性是指数有界的，除非Krein间隙关闭，这与两种谱简并之一相关：异常点或Krein碰撞。因此，长期QPV相关性可能随之而来。因此，克雷恩间隙扮演着动态稳定QBH的谱间隙的角色，并且动态稳定性和临界性（与例外点相关）或多临界性（与克雷恩碰撞相关）的边界是相同的。我们还发现，纠缠量和信息几何平衡临界度的其他指标的缩放见证了超越热力学稳定性的玻色子临界行为。我们的框架为通过临界现象的视角研究所有动态稳定的QBH打开了大门，包括来自光电子学、腔量子动力学和磁元学的热力不稳定的QBH。


### Abstract

For a many-body system in equilibrium, described by a thermodynamically stable Hamiltonian, quantum criticality is associated with structural changes of the many-body ground state. However, there exist physically relevant models, notably, certain quadratic bosonic Hamiltonians (QBHs), which fail to have a ground state. QBHs can be dynamically stable or unstable. We show the notion of criticality is meaningful for the entire class of QBHs that are dynamically stable or at the boundary of instability, regardless of thermodynamic stability, and that the key state for such QBHs is a naturally and unambiguously defined quasiparticle vacuum (QPV). This state is Gaussian, and coincides with the ground state if the QBH is thermodynamically stable. We identify a relevant spectral gap, the Krein gap, associated to the minimal spectral separation between creation and annihilation operators, and show that the QPV is unique when the Krein gap is positive. We prove that, for dynamically stable QBHs with finite-range couplings, correlations are exponentially bounded unless the Krein gap closes, which is associated with one of two spectral degeneracies: an exceptional point or a Krein collision. Consequently, long-range QPV correlations can ensue. Thus, the Krein gap takes the role of the spectral gap for dynamically stable QBHs, and the boundary of dynamical stability and criticality (associated to exceptional points) or multicriticality (associated to Krein collisions) are the same. We also find that bosonic critical behavior beyond thermodynamic stability is witnessed by the scaling of the entanglement entropy and other indicators of equilibrium criticality from information geometry. Our framework opens the door to investigating all dynamically stable QBHs through the lens of critical phenomena, including thermodynamically unstable ones from photonics, cavity-QED, and magnonics.

## 不稳定性Mpemba效应

**Original Title:** Nonstabilizerness Mpemba Effects

**Authors:** Zhenyu Xiao, Hao-Kai Zhang, Shuo Liu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.04155v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.04155v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-05


### 摘要

量子状态准备可能明显违反直觉：到达目标状态的最快路径不需要从显然最近的初始条件开始。我们在从倾斜积状态初始化的$\mathrm{U（1）}$-对称随机电路中，在量子魔法（非稳定性）的动态生成中发现了这样的量子Mpemba效应，该效应由稳定器Rényi熵量化。初始魔法值较低的状态比初始魔法值较高的状态更快地产生魔法。加速度不仅仅由保守电荷分布决定。具有相同初始魔力和相同电荷分布的两个初始状态家族表现出本质上不同的魔力增长动力学，这还取决于每个电荷区内初始状态的空间结构。$\mathrm{SU（2）}$-对称电路中和不可积汉密尔顿动力学下的类似魔法Mpemba效应进一步表明，该现象既不与阿贝尔对称性也不与随机电路动力学有关，从而将量子魔法确立为Mpemba物理学的独特舞台。
