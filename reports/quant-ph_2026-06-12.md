## 异常点作为' t Hooft模型中分析性崩溃的表现

**Original Title:** Exceptional Points as Manifestations of Analyticity Breakdown in the 't Hooft Model

**Authors:** Kejun Liu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.10141v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.10141v2

**Submitted:** 2026-06-08


### 摘要

我们使用1+1D大N_c QCD的精确可解t Hooft模型作为分解因果响应函数（强子两点函数）分析性的严格实验室。光锥强子操作员的PT对称变形i gamma（x-1/2）是虚化学势的模拟物，将最低的两个强子驱动到gamma_c处的异常点（EP）。将预解式重铸为Jacobi连分数，会产生封闭形式的gamma_c：两极水平上2 pi g ' 2 N_c，深度5时收敛到7.966 g ' 2 N_c--一个分析阈值，而不是数字阈值。平方根指数nu=1/2由2x 2 Jordan形式固定，并通过有限尺寸缩放到N=1999来确认。分解具有明确的时间域签名：传播子规范对于gamma < gamma_c是有界的，在gamma_c（乔丹世俗定律）线性增长，并以指数方式超越--可观察的，因为变形的操作符是一个非埃尔米特式的Wannier-Stark阶梯，在光和地形学类似物中。阈值被锁定为约束，gamma_c propto g ' 2 N_c，并作为均匀的EP级联再现;第二次非互惠变形产生精确指数的非埃尔米特集肤效应。这是限制规范理论中异常点分析性崩溃的第一个分析控制实例。


### Abstract

We use the exactly-solvable t Hooft model of 1+1D large-N_c QCD as a rigorous laboratory for the breakdown of analyticity of a causal response function, the meson two-point function. A PT-symmetric deformation i gamma(x-1/2) of the light-cone meson operator, the analogue of an imaginary chemical potential, drives the lowest two mesons to an exceptional point (EP) at gamma_c. Recasting the resolvent as a Jacobi continued fraction yields gamma_c in closed form: 2 pi g^2 N_c at the two-pole level, converging to 7.966 g^2 N_c by depth five -- an analytic, not numerical, threshold. The square-root exponent nu=1/2 is fixed by the 2x2 Jordan form and confirmed by finite-size scaling to N=1999. The breakdown has an unambiguous time-domain signature: the propagator norm is bounded for gamma < gamma_c, grows linearly at gamma_c (the Jordan secular law), and exponentially beyond -- observable, since the deformed operator is a non-Hermitian Wannier-Stark ladder, in photonic and topolectrical analogues. The threshold is locked to confinement, gamma_c propto g^2 N_c, and recurs as a uniform EP cascade; a second, non-reciprocal deformation yields an exactly-exponential non-Hermitian skin effect. This is the first analytically-controlled instance of exceptional-point analyticity breakdown in a confining gauge theory.

## 利用单光子压缩真空产生光学GKP态的理论研究

**Original Title:** Theoretical Study for Generating Optical GKP State via a Single-Photon-Added Squeezed Vacuum

**Authors:** Deriyan Senjaya

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12467v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.12467v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-09


### 摘要

建立了一个理论框架来分析使用单量子添加压缩真空产生光学GKP状态。该状态由压缩参数$r$定义，注入50：50分束器，通过一个输出端口的条件测量获得光学GKP状态。添加单量子的压缩真空在这种情况下尤其突出，因为它提供了比薛定谔猫状态更简单、在实验上更容易获得的成分，而条件测量则确保投影到非常接近有限能GKP形式的状态上。使用Fidelity来量化这种接近度，分析表明该方案在3.76美元的挤压水平下实现了85%的最大保真度。这一性能超越了基于压缩光学奇薛丁格猫态的方法，强调了添加单量子的压缩真空是实现故障容忍量子计算的实用有效途径。


### Abstract

A theoretical framework is developed to analyze the generation of the optical GKP state using a single-photon-added squeezed vacuum. This state, defined by the squeezing parameter $r$, is injected into a 50:50 beam splitter, and the optical GKP state is obtained through conditional measurement at one output port. The single-photon-added squeezed vacuum is especially prominent in this context because it provides a simpler and more experimentally accessible ingredient than Schrodinger cat states, while conditional measurement ensures projection onto a state that closely approximates the finite-energy GKP form. Fidelity is employed to quantify this closeness, and the analysis demonstrates that the scheme achieves a maximum fidelity of 85% at a squeezing level of $3.76 \ \text{dB}$. This performance surpasses approaches based on squeezed optical odd Schrodinger cat states, underscoring the single-photon-added squeezed vacuum as a practical and effective pathway toward fault-tolerant photonic quantum computing.

## Boltzmann注意力：可学习的伊辛合作注意力耦合

**Original Title:** Boltzmann Attention: Learnable Ising Couplings for Cooperative Attention

**Authors:** Gilhan Kim, Daniel K. Park

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12478v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.12478v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-10


### 摘要

注意力机制是现代序列模型的核心，但标准注意力主要通过个人查询（关键相似性）来计算相关性。尽管softmax规范化引入了位置之间的竞争，但标准注意力层并没有明确参数化注意力决策之间的可学习交互。这限制了它直接建模注意机制本身内合作或对抗性共同注意结构的能力。我们提出了Boltzmann注意力，这是一种基于能量的概括，其中注意力模式由相互作用的伊辛模型支配。该方法通过可学习的成对耦合来增强通常的数据相关局部场，使模型能够表示超出softmax或Sigmoid注意力捕获的位置间相关性。字符级语言建模和合成括号匹配的实验表明，玻尔兹曼注意力在标准Transformer架构中始终优于标准softmax注意力，随着序列长度的增加，优势变得更加明显。一个四向消融证实，改进来自可学习的成对耦合。这些结果表明，明确的位置间的相互作用提供了一个原则性的增强注意力为基础的序列建模。此外，伊辛公式开辟了一条通往基于量子计算的采样策略的自然道路：我们证明了非绝热量子退火提供了一种实用的训练方法，同时保持了与精确玻尔兹曼计算的竞争性能。


### Abstract

Attention mechanisms are central to modern sequence models, yet standard attention computes relevance primarily through individual query--key similarities. Although softmax normalization introduces competition among positions, a standard attention layer does not explicitly parameterize learnable interactions between attention decisions. This limits its ability to directly model cooperative or antagonistic co-attention structure within the attention mechanism itself. We propose Boltzmann attention, an energy-based generalization in which attention patterns are governed by an interacting Ising model. The method augments the usual data-dependent local fields with learnable pairwise couplings, allowing the model to represent inter-position correlations beyond those captured by softmax or sigmoid attention. Experiments on character-level language modeling and synthetic bracket matching show that Boltzmann attention consistently improves over standard softmax attention within a standard Transformer architecture, with the advantage becoming more pronounced as sequence length increases. A four-way ablation confirms that the improvement arises from the learnable pairwise couplings. These results suggest that explicit inter-position interactions provide a principled enhancement for attention-based sequence modeling. Moreover, the Ising formulation opens a natural path toward quantum-computing-based sampling strategies: we demonstrate that diabatic quantum annealing provides a practical training method while maintaining competitive performance with exact Boltzmann computation.

## 几何代数量子门分解

**Original Title:** Geometric Algebra Quantum Gate Decomposition

**Authors:** Youssef Amraoui, Zeno Toffano

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12480v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.12480v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-10


### 摘要

量子门通常通过矩阵和张量积形式主义来描述，这些形式主义常常模糊其几何结构。在这项工作中，我们在复杂的几何代数（GA）框架内制定了泡利群和克利福德群。我们表明，泡利群自然地与直到全局相的叶片群相同，从而提供了泡利运算符及其在定向子空间方面的对易关系的几何解释。我们进一步证明了Clifford运算符是由pi/4-Pauli旋转子的积生成的，并引入了一种贪婪的Pauli旋转子分解算法，其经验行为表明Clifford运算符的紧凑分解令人惊讶。最后，我们表明Clifford+T的普适性允许通过该框架内的pi/8-旋转子进行自然的几何解释。


### Abstract

Quantum gates are usually described through matrix and tensor-product formalisms that often obscure their geometric structure. In this work, we formulate the Pauli and Clifford groups within the complex Geometric Algebra (GA) framework. We show that the Pauli group is naturally identified with the group of blades up to a global phase, thereby providing a geometric interpretation of Pauli operators and their commutation relations in terms of oriented subspaces. We further prove that Clifford operators are generated by products of π/4-Pauli rotors and introduce a greedy Pauli rotor decomposition algorithm whose empirical behavior suggests unexpectedly compact decompositions for Clifford operators. Finally, we show that Clifford+T universality admits a natural geometric interpretation through π/8-rotors within this framework.

## 量子噪音下不对称W级态的超链脆性

**Original Title:** Super-Link Fragility in Asymmetric W-Class States under Quantum Noise

**Authors:** Sougata Bhattacharyya, Fatih Ozaydin, Sovik Roy

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12307v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.12307v2

**Submitted:** 2026-06-10


### 摘要

非对称三量子位W级状态$|\overline{W_3 ' L}\rangle$定义了一个六边形纠缠网络几何形状，（a）两个点-基（VB）链接形成更强的二分连接，（b）而基-基（BB）链接较弱。这表明将纠缠集中到超级链路中可能有利于量子网络任务。在这里，我们表明这种直觉是不完整的。我们分析比较了对称的双方并发动力学|W>状态和不对称$|\overline{W_3 ' L}\rangle$ state，在标准噪音模型下，其在纠缠网络几何形状和激励扇形方面都有所不同。在无噪声时，并发度等级为C_{VB} > C_W > C_{BB}。在相衰减下，对于所有噪音强度，该层次结构都被保留，并且不会发生纠缠突然消亡。然而，在幅度衰减下，层次结构会重新排序。对称|W &状态变得最稳健，而$的基本一致性|\overline{W_3 ' L}\rangle$在参数$γ$的有限阈值处消失。我们将这种重新排序称为\textit{Super-Link脆性效应}。产生更强顶基链接的相同结构不对称性也使其在与多激励幅度相结合时更容易受到能量消耗的影响。在去极化下，不对称优势被消除，对于参数p的某个值，$C_W$和$C_{VB}$共享相同的突然死亡阈值，而$C_{BB}$在参数p的某个其他值处更早消失。广义幅度衰减通道连续地将衰减主导区连接到纯激励极限，在那里恢复初始层次。这些结果表明，在$W$类资源的纠缠鲁棒性是由初始并发控制，但由纠缠网络的几何形状，激发扇区，和噪声对称性的联合结构。


### Abstract

The asymmetric three-qubit W-class state $|\overline{W_3^L}\rangle$ defines an isosceles entanglement-network geometry, (a) two vertex-base (VB) links form stronger bipartite connections, (b) while the base-base (BB) link is weaker. This suggests that concentrating entanglement into a super-link may be advantageous for quantum-network tasks. Here, we show that this intuition is incomplete. We analytically compare the bipartite concurrence dynamics of the symmetric |W> state and the asymmetric $|\overline{W_3^L}\rangle$ state, which differ both in entanglement-network geometry and excitation sector under standard noise models. In the absence of noise, the concurrence hierarchy is $C_{VB} > C_W > C_{BB}$. Under phase damping, this hierarchy is preserved for all noise strengths and no entanglement sudden death occurs. Under amplitude damping, however, the hierarchy is reordered. The symmetric |W> state becomes the most robust, while the base-base concurrence of $|\overline{W_3^L}\rangle$ vanishes at the finite threshold of parameter $γ$. We term this reordering as the \textit{Super-Link Fragility Effect}. The same structural asymmetry that produces a stronger vertex-base link also makes it more vulnerable to energy dissipation when coupled with multi-excitation amplitudes. Under depolarization, the asymmetry advantage is erased, with $C_W$ and $C_{VB}$ sharing the same sudden-death threshold for some value of the parameter p, while $C_{BB}$ disappears earlier at some other value of the parameter p. The generalized amplitude damping channel continuously connects the damping-dominated regime to the pure-excitation limit, where the initial hierarchy is restored. These results show that entanglement robustness in $W$-class resources is controlled not by initial concurrence alone, but by the joint structure of entanglement-network geometry, excitation sector, and noise symmetry.

## 高梅受激磁铁中的旋转相关性、低能量尺度和各向异性标度

**Original Title:** Spin correlations, low-energy scales, and anisotropy scaling in kagome frustrated magnets

**Authors:** Phillip Popp, Stephan Rosenkranz, Arthur P. Ramirez, Sergey Syzranov

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12512v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.12512v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-10


### 摘要

中子散射是识别磁性材料量子状态的核心。在寻找量子旋转液体的过程中，非弹性光谱的宽光谱特征已被引用作为旋子激发的证据，但也可以由磁振子激发产生，在存在猝灭无序和强磁振子相互作用的情况下。我们开发了一种解决这个问题的新方法，基于$XXZ$海森堡模型中的隔热连续性，该模型的各向异性是几何令人沮丧（GF）格子的函数。使用这种方法，我们确定了有限温度旋转相关器的普遍特征和能量。聚焦于kagome格子，我们表明低能量旋转谱函数包含稳健的、与动量无关的峰值，其频率为：$ω_1 \约3.4 T #*$和$ω_2 \约6.3 T #*$，其中“隐藏能量标度”$T #*$是热容量低温峰的特征标度，许多GF磁铁也显示出旋转玻璃冻结。我们表明，低能量下的光谱特征$ω\lesssim T^*$源于单磁振子散射，并识别了各自激发的磁性。我们探索光谱特征随温度的演变，并讨论其他GF格子的扩展。我们的结果为解释kagome和其他GF量子磁铁中的中子散射提供了尖锐的光谱标准。


### Abstract

Neutron scattering is central to identifying quantum states of magnetic materials. In the search for quantum spin liquids, broad spectral features of inelastic spectra have been cited as evidence for spinon excitations, but can also arise from magnon excitations excitations in the presence of quenched disorder and strong magnon interactions. We develop a new approach to this problem, based on the adiabatic continuity in the $XXZ$ Heisenberg model on geometrically frustrating (GF) lattices as a function of the model's anisotropy. Using this approach, we identify universal features and energies of finite-temperature spin correlators. Focusing on the kagome lattice, we show that the low-energy spin spectral function contains robust, momentum-independent peaks with frequencies: $ω_1 \approx 3.4 T^*$ and $ω_2 \approx 6.3 T^*$, where the ``hidden energy scale'' $T^*$ is the characteristic scale of a low-temperature peak in the heat capacity, at which many GF magnets also display spin-glass freezing. We show that the spectral features at low energies $ω\lesssim T^*$ arise from single-magnon scattering and identify the magnetizations of the respective excitations. We explore the evolution of the spectral features with temperature and discuss extensions to other GF lattices. Our results provide a sharp spectroscopic criterion for interpreting neutron scattering in kagome and other GF quantum magnets.

## 局部强激冷中的多信息量

**Original Title:** Multi-entropy in heavy local quenches

**Authors:** Kosei Fujiki, Kenya Tasuki

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12526v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.12526v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-10


### 摘要

在二维全息共形场论中研究了重局域猝灭中三方纠缠的时间演化。我们的诊断是真正的多熵相邻的间隔，从体积和边界的角度计算。微扰体分析表明，真空测地线网络周围的一阶小质量微扰在失超后的任何时刻都相同地抵消。在完全反作用的几何中，真空减去的真正的多熵产生于由三价测地线网络选择的绕组和由成对测地线独立选择的绕组之间的不匹配。在尖锐的熄灭极限下，真正的多信息量的时间依赖性在运动学上固定到时间的有理函数的分母，并且独立于重操作符维度。CFT计算在重-轻真空块逼近中重现了相同的公式，其中重-背景均匀化图中的分支选择对应于批量中的缠绕选择。这些结果表明，在这种设置中，真正的多熵是由全局鞍选择控制的，而不是由局部能量响应或准粒子传播控制的。


### Abstract

We study the time evolution of tripartite entanglement in heavy local quenches in two-dimensional holographic conformal field theories. Our diagnostic is the genuine multi-entropy of adjacent intervals, computed from both bulk and boundary perspectives. A perturbative bulk analysis shows that the first-order small-mass perturbation around the vacuum geodesic network cancels identically at any time after the quench. In the fully back-reacted geometry, a vacuum-subtracted genuine multi-entropy arises from a mismatch between the winding selected by the trivalent geodesic network and the windings selected independently by the pairwise geodesics. In the sharp quench limit, the time dependence of genuine multi-entropy is kinematically fixed to logarithms of rational functions of time and is independent of the heavy operator dimension. The CFT calculation reproduces the same formula within the heavy-light vacuum block approximation, where the branch choice in the heavy-background uniformization map corresponds to the winding selection in the bulk. These results indicate that, in this setup, the genuine multi-entropy is controlled by global saddle selection, rather than by a local energy response or quasiparticle propagation.

## 非阿贝尔任意子扩散的统计力学和对称性：从变形到去相关

**Authors:** Avi Vadali, Robijn Vanhove, Ruben Verresen, Jason Alicea, Pablo Sala

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12527v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.12527v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-10


### 摘要

拓扑量子计算依赖于编织非阿贝尔任意子，但需要底层的拓扑秩序来承受不完美的状态准备和环境噪音。我们表明，波函数变形和去相干的不稳定性（后者通过证状分布来探测）通常被静态机械模型捕捉到，这些模型的对称性自然暴露了破坏性的任意元激励。作为一个例子，我们将这个框架与蒙特卡洛模拟相结合，以解决变形和量子通道下$D_4$拓扑序的稳定性，这些通道繁殖了多个单独无法凝聚的非阿贝尔任意子物种。我们表明，超过有限阈值，两个非阿贝尔任意子物种的扩散寄生浓缩了共同的阿贝尔-任意子融合结果，破坏了拓扑顺序。我们基于虚拟的方法将所得的平凡相与通过凝聚所有阿贝尔电荷获得的平凡相显着区分开来;换句话说，平凡相“记住”了哪些任意子凝聚。该框架提供了识别非阿贝尔拓扑序的最佳解码器的相关对称性的第一步，该解码器以综合症测量为条件。


### Abstract

Topological quantum computation relies on braiding non-Abelian anyons, but requires the underlying topological order to survive imperfect state preparation and environmental noise. We show that the instability of topological order to wavefunction deformations and to decoherence, with the latter probed by syndrome distributions, are generically captured by stat-mech models whose symmetries naturally expose the corrupting anyonic excitations. As an example, we combine this framework with Monte-Carlo simulations to resolve the stability of $D_4$ topological order under deformations and quantum channels that proliferate multiple non-Abelian anyon species that individually are unable to condense. We show that beyond a finite threshold, proliferation of two non-Abelian anyon species parasitically condenses a shared Abelian-anyon fusion outcome, destroying the topological order. Our symmetry-based approach sharply differentiates the resulting trivial phase from that obtained by condensing all Abelian charges; in other words, the trivial phase "remembers" which anyons condensed. This framework provides a first step into identifying the relevant symmetry for optimal decoders, conditioned on syndrome measurements, of non-Abelian topological order.

## 影响可解性：$（1+1）D$可解性的系统理论及其在砖砌电路中的应用

**Original Title:** Influence-solvability: a systematic theory of $(1+1)D$ solvability and its application to brickwork circuits

**Authors:** Friedrich Hübner, Sun Woo P. Kim

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.12538v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.12538v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-10


### 摘要

“可解”电路，例如二元么正及其概括，已作为经典和量子系统中易于处理的混乱非平衡动力学的典范例子而出现。然而，虽然提出了越来越复杂的充分条件，但缺乏分类和理解可解电路一般特征的系统理论。我们通过引入影响可解电路来发展这样的理论，这是一类$（1+1）D$电路，其影响矩阵代表由其自身进化产生的“浴”，由具有有限键维的均匀MPS给出。该属性允许子系统动态的有效计算，并且本质上包含可解电路的所有已知示例。我们通过使用开放边界条件MPS基本定理的一个版本来推导出一组必要且充分的局部条件。接下来，我们将我们的理论应用于具有$x =1$影响可解性的砖砌电路，并对局部维度高达$d=3$的经典砖砌电路和$d=2$的量子砖砌电路进行系统分类。我们的搜索揭示了已知可解性条件无法捕捉的新的可解电路。


### Abstract

`Solvable' circuits, such as dual unitaries and its generalisations, have arisen as paradigmatic examples of tractable chaotic non-equilibrium dynamics, both in classical and quantum systems. However, while increasingly more complicated sufficient conditions have been proposed, a systematic theory classifying and understanding general features of solvable circuits is missing. We develop such a theory by introducing influence-solvable circuits, a class of $(1+1)D$ circuits whose influence matrix, which represents the `bath' generated by its own evolution, is given by a uniform MPS with finite bond-dimension $χ$. This property allows for efficient computation of subsystem dynamics and essentially contains all known examples of solvable circuits. We derive a set of necessary and sufficient local conditions by using a version of the fundamental theorem of MPS for open boundary conditions. Next we apply our theory to brickwork circuits with $χ=1$ influence-solvability and perform a systematic classification of classical brickwork circuits with local dimension up to $d=3$ and quantum brickwork circuits with $d=2$. Our search reveals new solvable circuits that are not captured by known solvability conditions.

## 任意维度共形场论的纠缠引导

Our results establish hot-state engineering as a route to quantum-enhanced bosonic displacement sensing without mandatory ground-state initialization.
