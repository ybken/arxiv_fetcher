## 使用72个超导量子位对2D费米-哈伯德动力学进行可编程数字量子模拟

**Original Title:** Programmable digital quantum simulation of 2D Fermi-Hubbard dynamics using 72 superconducting qubits

**Authors:** Faisal Alam, Jan Lukas Bosse, Ieva Čepaitė, Adrian Chapman, Laura Clinton, Marcos Crichigno, Elizabeth Crosson, Toby Cubitt, Charles Derby, Oliver Dowinton, Paul K. Faehrmann, Steve Flammia, Brian Flynn, Filippo Maria Gambetta, Raúl García-Patrón, Max Hunter-Gordon, Glenn Jones, Abhishek Khedkar, Joel Klassen, Michael Kreshchuk, Edward Harry McMullan, Lana Mineh, Ashley Montanaro, Caterina Mora, John J. L. Morton, Dhrumil Patel, Pete Rolph, Raul A. Santos, James R. Seddon, Evan Sheridan, Wilfrid Somogyi, Marika Svensson, Niam Vaishnav, Sabrina Yue Wang, Gethin Wright

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26845v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.26845v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-30


### 摘要

模拟量子多体系统的时间动力学是费曼提出的量子计算机的最初用途，其动机是电子之间的量子相互作用在材料和分子性质中的关键作用。准确模拟此类系统仍然是通用数字量子计算机最有前途的应用之一，其中模型的所有参数都可以编程并输出任何所需的物理量。然而，在当今的量子计算机上以超越经典方法的规模执行此类模拟需要提高模拟算法和错误缓解技术的效率。在这里，我们展示了对2D费米-哈伯德模型（晶体固体中电子最著名的简化模型之一）动力学的可编程数字量子模拟，其规模超出了精确的经典模拟。我们在谷歌的Willow量子处理器上使用72个量子位，在一系列物理参数（包括现场电子-电子相互作用强度和通量）上对这个模型进行了模拟，并研究了包括磁极化子（即被局部极化包围的电荷子）的形成在内的现象，条有序状态中的动态对称性破缺，电荷子对被称为价键固体的纠缠态的吸引，以及通过热化达到平衡的方法。我们在可行的参数区域中对照精确计算来验证我们的结果，并将其与使用张量网络和运营商传播方法执行的近似经典模拟进行比较。我们的结果表明，多体相互作用电子模型的可编程数字量子模拟现在在最先进的量子硬件上具有竞争力。


### Abstract

Simulating the time-dynamics of quantum many-body systems was the original
use of quantum computers proposed by Feynman, motivated by the critical role of
quantum interactions between electrons in the properties of materials and
molecules. Accurately simulating such systems remains one of the most promising
applications of general-purpose digital quantum computers, in which all the
parameters of the model can be programmed and any desired physical quantity
output. However, performing such simulations on today's quantum computers at a
scale beyond the reach of classical methods requires advances in the efficiency
of simulation algorithms and error mitigation techniques. Here we demonstrate
programmable digital quantum simulation of the dynamics of the 2D Fermi-Hubbard
model -- one of the best-known simplified models of electrons in crystalline
solids -- at a scale beyond exact classical simulation. We implement
simulations of this model on lattice sizes up to $6\times 6$ using 72 qubits on
Google's Willow quantum processor, across a range of physical parameters,
including on-site electron-electron interaction strength and magnetic flux, and
study phenomena including formation of magnetic polarons, i.e. charge carriers
surrounded by local magnetic polarisation, dynamical symmetry breaking in
stripe-ordered states, attraction of charge carriers on an entangled state
known as a valence bond solid, and the approach to equilibrium through
thermalisation. We validate our results against exact calculations in parameter
regimes where these are feasible, and compare them to approximate classical
simulations performed using tensor network and operator propagation methods.
Our results demonstrate that programmable digital quantum simulation of
many-body interacting electron models is now competitive on state-of-the-art
quantum hardware.

## 库普曼中的盒子里的粒子--冯·诺伊曼力学：经典力学的赫伯特空间表示

**Original Title:** The Particle in a Box in Koopman--von Neumann Mechanics: A Hilbert Space representation of Classical Mechanics

**Authors:** Abhijit Sen, Lev Kaplan

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26856v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.26856v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-30


### 摘要

本文从Koopman-von Neumann（KvN）力学的观点出发，重新讨论了教科书“盒子中的粒子”。KvN力学是一种描述希尔伯特空间中经典动力学的方法。这个简单的事实改变了通常的预期：硬墙在这里不强迫能量量子化。我们显示，在一个明确的和物理的方式，为什么一个KvN粒子之间的两个理想的墙壁仍然有一个连续的能量范围。在正确的壁条件下，即捕获普通弹性反射而不是“在边界消失”的条件下，KvN描述自然地产生了没有离散能量级的空间限制。除了建立这一结果之外，我们还消除了常见的误解：例如，将KvN波函数视为位置上的量子概率幅度，会导致错误的边界图片，并由此导致关于量子化的错误结论。


### Abstract

This paper revisits the textbook 'particle in a box', but from the point of
view of Koopman-von Neumann (KvN) mechanics. KvN mechanics is a way to describe
\emph{classical} dynamics in a Hilbert space. That simple fact changes the
usual expectation: hard walls do \emph{not} force energy quantization here. We
show, in a clear and physical way, why a KvN particle confined between two
ideal walls still has a continuous range of energies. With the correct wall
condition, one that captures ordinary elastic reflection rather than 'vanishing
at the boundary,' the KvN description naturally produces spatial confinement
without discrete energy levels. Beyond establishing this result, we also clean
up common misunderstandings: for example, treating the KvN wavefunction like a
quantum probability amplitude in position alone leads to the wrong boundary
picture and, with it, the wrong conclusion about quantization.

## 车间调度问题的非变分量子方法

**Original Title:** A Non-Variational Quantum Approach to the Job Shop Scheduling Problem

**Authors:** Miguel Angel Lopez-Ruiz, Emily L. Tucker, Emma M. Arnold, Evgeny Epifanovsky, Ananth Kaushik, Martin Roetteler

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26859v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.26859v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-30


### 摘要

量子化学为组合优化提供了潜在的优势，但受到近期硬件限制的限制。我们介绍迭代QAOA，QAOA的一个变种，旨在减轻这些限制。该算法结合了非变分，浅深度电路的方法，使用固定参数的时间表与迭代的热启动过程。我们在IonQ Forte Generation QPU上的准时作业车间调度问题（JIT-JSSP）实例上对算法进行了基准测试，代表了量子硬件上执行过的一些最大的此类问题。我们比较了变分量子虚时间演化（VarQITE）算法和非变分线性斜坡（LR）QAOA算法的性能。我们发现，迭代QAOA鲁棒收敛，找到最佳的解决方案，以及高品质，接近最佳的解决方案，为所有的问题实例评估。我们使用张量网络模拟在高达97个量子比特的较大问题实例上评估该算法。该算法的标度行为表明了在容错量子计算机上解决工业规模问题的潜力。


### Abstract

Quantum heuristics offer a potential advantage for combinatorial optimization
but are constrained by near-term hardware limitations. We introduce
Iterative-QAOA, a variant of QAOA designed to mitigate these constraints. The
algorithm combines a non-variational, shallow-depth circuit approach using
fixed-parameter schedules with an iterative warm-starting process. We benchmark
the algorithm on Just-in-Time Job Shop Scheduling Problem (JIT-JSSP) instances
on IonQ Forte Generation QPUs, representing some of the largest such problems
ever executed on quantum hardware. We compare the performance of the algorithm
against both the Variational Quantum Imaginary Time Evolution (VarQITE)
algorithm and the non-variational Linear Ramp (LR) QAOA algorithm. We find that
Iterative-QAOA robustly converges to find optimal solutions as well as
high-quality, near-optimal solutions for all problem instances evaluated. We
evaluate the algorithm on larger problem instances up to 97 qubits using tensor
network simulations. The scaling behavior of the algorithm indicates potential
for solving industrial-scale problems on fault-tolerant quantum computers.

## 远离平衡态的封闭量子多体系统的熵输运

**Original Title:** Entropy transport in closed quantum many-body systems far from equilibrium

**Authors:** J. Marijan, H. Strobel, M. K. Oberthaler, J. Berges

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26873v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.26873v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-30


### 摘要

我们研究远离平衡的封闭量子多体系统中普适标度现象的熵输运。根据旋量玻色气体的空间分辨率实验数据，我们证明了，在长距离尺度上，信息量减小，而在短距离上，信息量增大。尺度的动态分离导致宏观物理学具有长期有序性，而该宏观物理学对高度熵的微物理过程不敏感。由于总冯·诺伊曼信息在量子系统的基本水平上是保守的，因此我们的分析揭示了宏观结构的出现和微观无序之间的相互联系。为了说明这种联系的范围，我们还在根据第一性原理进行的相对论量子场论计算中阐述了普遍现象，这与粒子物理学和早期宇宙宇宙学相关。


### Abstract

We investigate entropy transport for universal scaling phenomena in closed
quantum many-body systems far from equilibrium. From spatially resolved
experimental data of a spinor Bose gas, we demonstrate that entropy decreases
on long-distance scales while it increases at short distances. A dynamical
separation of scales leads to macrophysics with long-range order, which is
insensitive to the highly entropic microphysical processes. Since the total von
Neumann entropy is conserved on a fundamental level for the quantum system, our
analysis reveals a reciprocal connection between the emergence of macroscopic
structure and microscopic disorder. To illustrate the scope of this connection,
we exemplify the universal phenomenon also in a relativistic quantum field
theory calculation from first principles, which is relevant for particle
physics and early-universe cosmology.

## 室温碱蒸气中单光级原子频梳存储

**Original Title:** Single-Photon-Level Atomic Frequency Comb Storage in Room Temperature Alkali Vapour

**Authors:** Zakary Schofield, Vanderli Laurindo Jr, Ori Ezrah Mor, Patrick M. Ledingham

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26870v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.26870v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-30


### 摘要

我们已经证明了使用原子频梳协议在室温镭蒸气中对单量子级光进行的连贯存储和检索。使用速度选择性光抽运来在镭的$F=2$超精细基态内制备梳，峰之间的间隔与$5 ' 2$P $__{3/2}$激发态的$F = 2 - F =3$超精细分裂的一半重合。平均量子数$\mo_\mathrm{in} = 0.083（5）$的弱相干状态以预编程的召回时间为$7.5，$ns的效率为$\eta_{\textrm{AFC}} = 6.59（5）\，\%$，而两个时间上不同的模式已被存储和召回，$\eta_{\textrm{AFC} = 2.6（1）\，\%$，允许时间箱量子位存储。最后，观察到效率与输入脉冲极化无关，为极化量子位存储铺平了道路。


### Abstract

We have demonstrated the coherent storage and retrieval of
single-photon-level light using the atomic frequency comb protocol in a room
temperature rubidium vapour. Velocity-selective optical pumping is used to
prepare the comb within the $F=2$ hyperfine ground state of rubidium, with the
spacing between peaks coinciding with half the $F = 2 - F =3$ hyperfine
splitting of the $5^2$P$_{3/2}$ excited state. Weak coherent states of average
photon number $\mu_\mathrm{in} = 0.083(5)$ are stored with pre-programmed
recall time of $7.5\,$ns with an efficiency of $\eta_{\textrm{AFC}} =
6.59(5)\,\%$, while two temporally distinct modes have been stored and recalled
with $\eta_{\textrm{AFC}} = 2.6(1)\,\%$, allowing for time-bin qubit storage.
Finally, the efficiency is observed to be independent of the input pulse
polarisation, paving the way for polarisation qubit storage.

## 桌面可逆性的精确和大致条件：Petz恢复何时是免费的？

**Original Title:** Exact and approximate conditions of tabletop reversibility: when is Petz recovery cost-free?

**Authors:** Minjeong Song, Hyukjoon Kwon, Valerio Scarani

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26895v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.26895v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-30


### 摘要

描述开放量子动力学的通道$\mathCal{N}$本质上是不可逆转的：不可能完全消除它们的影响，但可以研究信息的部分恢复。Petz恢复映射$\hat{\mathcal{N}_{\gamma}^{（\textttt {P}）}$是一个系统结构，仅取决于$\mathcalm {N}$和参考状态$\gamma$，该状态将被准确恢复。如果实际输入状态与$\gamma$不同，则恢复是部分的，并保证接近最优。一般来说，Petz恢复地图的实现看起来与通道的实现非常不同。研究在哪些条件下两个地图需要相似甚至相同的资源是很自然的。当相应的Petz恢复图可以以这种方式实现时，对于给定的$\gamma$，有噪的前向通道$\mathCal{N}$被称为“桌面时间可逆”。首先，我们研究确切的桌面可逆性（TTR）条件。我们特别表明，需要对辅助系统进行时间敏感的控制。其次，我们提出了近似的TTR条件，不需要这样的时间敏感控制。第三，我们推导了Lindbladian TTR条件下的随机时间碰撞模型。


### Abstract

Channels $\mathcal{N}$ that describe open quantum dynamics are inherently
irreversible: it is impossible to undo their effect completely, but one can
study partial recovery of the information. The Petz recovery map
$\hat{\mathcal{N}}_{\gamma}^{(\texttt{P})}$ is a systematic construction that
depends only on $\mathcal{N}$ and on a reference state $\gamma$, which will be
recovered exactly. If the real input state was different from $\gamma$, the
recovery is partial, with a guarantee of near-optimality. Generically, an
implementation of the Petz recovery map would look very different from the
implementation of the channel. It is natural to study under which conditions
the two maps require similar or even identical resources. The noisy forward
channel $\mathcal{N}$ is called ``tabletop time-reversible'' for a given
$\gamma$ when the corresponding Petz recovery map is realizable in such a way.
First, we study the exact tabletop reversibility (TTR) conditions. We show in
particular that a time-sensitive control of an ancilla system is needed.
Second, we present the approximate TTR conditions, which do not require such a
time-sensitive control. Third, we derive Lindbladian TTR conditions under a
random-time collision model.

## 手征可积动力学中的超扩散和反常涨落

**Original Title:** Superdiffusion and anomalous fluctuations in chiral integrable dynamics

**Authors:** Cristiano Muzzi, Devendra Singh Bhakuni, Marcello Dalmonte, Lenart Zadnik, Hernan B. Xavier

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26897v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.26897v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-30


### 摘要

对称性强烈影响量子多体系统的输运性质，并可能导致偏离扩散的一般情况。在这项工作中，我们研究了时间逆转对称性破缺对可积手征旋转梯中输运及其普遍方面的影响。我们观察到无限温度的旋转输运是超扩散的，动力学临界指数z = 3/2，与Kardar-Parisi-Zhang（KPZ）普适性类相匹配，后者也缺乏时间逆转对称性。然而，我们发现净磁性转移的波动与KPZ的预测存在偏差。此外，尽管时间逆转和空间反射对称性被打破，相关的旋转电流的全概率分布仍服从涨落对称性。为了进一步研究保守量的作用，我们引入了一种可积量子电路，该电路与手征梯具有基本对称性，并且在没有能量保守的情况下表现出类似的动力学行为。我们的工作表明，时间反转对称性破缺与超扩散兼容，但不足以稳定可积系统中的KPZ普适性。这表明，为了识别量子物质中这种动力学的出现，缺少了额外的基本特征。


### Abstract

Symmetries strongly influence transport properties of quantum many-body
systems, and can lead to deviations from the generic case of diffusion. In this
work, we study the impact of time-reversal symmetry breaking on the transport
and its universal aspects in integrable chiral spin ladders. We observe that
the infinite-temperature spin transport is superdiffusive with a dynamical
critical exponent z = 3/2 matching the one of the Kardar-Parisi-Zhang (KPZ)
universality class, which also lacks the time reversal symmetry. However, we
find that fluctuations of the net magnetization transfer deviate from the KPZ
predictions. Moreover, the full probability distribution of the associated spin
current obeys fluctuation symmetry despite broken time-reversal and
space-reflection symmetries. To further investigate the role of conserved
quantities, we introduce an integrable quantum circuit that shares the
essential symmetries with the chiral ladder, and which exhibits analogous
dynamical behaviour in the absence of energy conservation. Our work shows that
time-reversal symmetry breaking is compatible with superdiffusion, but
insufficient to stabilize the KPZ universality in integrable systems. This
suggests that additional fundamental features are missing in order to identify
the emergence of such dynamics in quantum matter.

## 安德森转型--克雷洛夫空间的视角

**Original Title:** The Anderson transition -- a view from Krylov space

**Authors:** J. Clayton Peacock, Vadim Oganesyan, Dries Sels

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26920v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.26920v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-30


### 摘要

Krylov子空间展开是稀疏数值方法的主力方法，近年来，它越来越多地被探索为对多体动力学的物理见解的来源。我们重新审视备受推崇的安德森维定位模型$d=1、2、3、4$，以在Krylov空间中构建局部运动积分（LIOM）。这些表现为Krylov超运算子空间中有效跳变问题的零特征值边缘状态，并且可以在给定Lanczos系数的情况下解析地构造。我们利用这个想法，重点关注$d=3$，研究LIOM解剖中疾病驱动的安德森转变的表现。我们发现Krylov运算符复杂性的增加导致Lanczos系数波动的抑制。因此，人们可以研究无序平均Krylov链中运动积分的现象学。我们发现边缘状态在定域阶段和扩展阶段都局限在Krylov空间的消失分数上（对于体积为$V$的立方体，维度为$D_K= V ' 2 $）。重要的是，在局部阶段，无序会在（Krylov）跳跃问题中引发乘势定律衰变二聚化，产生LIOM（Krylov空间中）的伸展指数衰变，其伸展指数为1/2d$。金属LIOM完全非本地化，尽管仅跨越$\propto \SQRT{D_K}$状态）。临界LIOM表现出指数与预期的分数指数匹配的指数的指数。


### Abstract

The Krylov subspace expansion is a workhorse method for sparse numerical
methods that has been increasingly explored as source of physical insight into
many-body dynamics in recent years. We revisit the venerable Anderson model of
localization in dimensions $d=1, 2, 3, 4$ to construct local integrals of
motion (LIOM) in Krylov space. These appear as zero eigenvalue edge states of
an effective hopping problem in Krylov superoperator subspace, and can be
analytically constructed, given the Lanczos coefficients. We exploit this idea,
focusing on $d=3$, to study the manifestation of the disorder driven Anderson
transition in the anatomy of LIOMs. We find that the increasing complexity of
the Krylov operators results in a suppression of the fluctuations of the
Lanczos coefficients. As such, one can study the phenomenology of the integrals
of motion in the disorder averaged Krylov chain. We find edge states localized
on vanishing fraction of Krylov space (of dimension $D_K=V^2$ for cubes of
volume $V$), both in localized and extended phases. Importantly, in the
localized phase, disorder induces powerlaw decaying dimerization in the
(Krylov) hopping problem, producing stretched exponential decay of the LIOMs
(in Krylov space) with a stretching exponent $1/2d$. Metallic LIOMs are
completely delocalized albeit across only $\propto \sqrt{D_K}$ states).
Critical LIOMs exhibit powerlaw decay with an exponent matching the expected
fractal exponent.

## 利用Floquet动力学在少量子比特系统中进行选择性计量

**Original Title:** Harnessing Floquet dynamics for selective metrology in few-qubit systems

**Authors:** Asghar Ullah, Hasan Mermer, Melih Özkurt, Igor Lesanovsky, Özgür E. Müstecaplıoğlu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.26942v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.26942v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-30


### 摘要

周期性驱动的量子系统可以作为高度选择性的参数过滤器。我们证明了这种能力，在一个有限大小的，三量子位系统所描述的横向场Floquet伊辛模型。在这个系统中，我们确定了一个周期加倍（PD）的动力学相位，表现出明显的不对称性的磁场上施加的量子位和量子位之间的耦合强度的灵敏度。PD阶段起源于$\pi$配对，其中的初始状态表现出强烈的重叠与$\pi$配对Floquet本征态，导致强大的周期加倍的动力学和增强的灵敏度。量子费舍尔信息的分析表明，PD机制显着提高了估计伊辛相互作用强度的精确度，同时抑制了对横向磁场的敏感性。相反，非PD方案对于感测横向场是最佳的。这种过滤效应对于更大的系统大小来说是稳健的，并且可以通过经典费舍尔信息使用实验上可访问的可观测量（例如磁性和两量子位相关性）进行量化。我们的工作表明，有限大小Floquet系统中不同的动力学机制可以用于有针对性的量子传感。


### Abstract

Periodically driven quantum systems can function as highly selective
parameter filters. We demonstrate this capability in a finite-size, three-qubit
system described by the transverse-field Floquet Ising model. In this system,
we identify a period-doubling (PD) dynamical phase that exhibits a stark
asymmetry in metrological sensitivity to the magnetic field applied on the
qubits and to the coupling strength between the qubits. The PD phase originates
from $\pi$-pairing, where the initial state exhibits strong overlap with
$\pi$-paired Floquet eigenstates, leading to robust period-doubled dynamics and
enhanced metrological sensitivity. The analysis of quantum Fisher information
reveals that the PD regime significantly enhances precision for estimating the
Ising interaction strength while simultaneously suppressing sensitivity to the

task when relying solely on local observables. We exhibit that the
experimentally accessible two-body Bell operator, originally designed to detect
nonlocal correlations in quantum states, serves as an effective witness of
DQPTs in a long-range (LR) XY spin chain subjected to a magnetic field, where
the interaction strength decays as a power law. Following a sudden quench of
the system parameters, the Bell operator between nearest-neighbor spins
exhibits a distinct drop at the critical boundaries. In this study, we consider
two quenching protocols, namely sudden quenches of the magnetic field strength
and the interaction fall-off rate. This pronounced behavior defines a
threshold, distinguishing intra-phase from inter-phase quenches, remaining
valid regardless of the strength of long-range interactions, anisotropy, and
system sizes. Comparative analyses further demonstrate that conventional
classical and quantum correlators, including entanglement, fail to capture this
transition during dynamics.

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.27661v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.27661v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-31


### 摘要

利用光玻色子模的单向量子计算提供了无与伦比的光模可扩展性，并且最近实验发展迅速。可扩展性需要稳健且低错误的门和测量。挤压门是必要的高斯操作之一。我们找到了团簇状态架构中的最佳压缩门。我们的方法新使用不平衡分束器的幅度传输系数和零差检测，以及随后的单位收益反馈来压缩输入状态。该方法优于基于最佳旋转零差检测但使用固定平衡分束器的当前方法。针对高斯和非高斯输入状态评估了两个集群状态压缩器的性能。我们使用不同的指标来基准测试压缩输出状态的质量。这一结果为在实验上可实现的团簇态中实现低噪声压缩门开辟了道路。


### Abstract

unmatched scalability of light modes, and it has seen rapid experimental

states. The result opens a road to low-noise squeezing gates in experimentally

## 超越一维空间的量子电动力学的可扩展量子计算

**Original Title:** Scalable quantum computation of Quantum Electrodynamics beyond one spatial dimension

**Authors:** Zong-Gang Mou, Bipasha Chakraborty

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.27668v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.27668v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-31


### 摘要

在汉密尔顿公式中，量子场论计算随着空间体积呈指数级扩展，使得实时模拟在经典计算机上变得棘手，并激励量子计算方法。在汉密尔顿量子化中，玻色子场引入了无限维希尔伯特空间的额外挑战。我们为量子电动力学（QED）提出了一种可扩展的量子算法，这是一种高于一维空间的阿贝尔规范场理论，旨在解决这一限制，同时保持规范不变性。在我们的公式中，当实现保持完全规范不变时，高斯定律就会自动满足。我们演示了如何在整个网格离散化、数字化和量化化过程中保持规范不变性，并确定了扩展到大希尔伯特空间维度的最有效表示。在此框架内，我们对几种量子误差缓解技术进行了基准测试，并找到了执行最有效的校准方法。该方法自然扩展到更大的网格，我们在当前的量子硬件上实现并测试了2+1和3+1维设置。我们的结果表明，下一代量子平台可以对大规模QED动力学进行可靠的、完全量子模拟。


### Abstract

In the Hamiltonian formulation, Quantum Field Theory calculations scale
exponentially with spatial volume, making real-time simulations intractable on
classical computers and motivating quantum computation approaches. In
Hamiltonian quantisation, bosonic fields introduce the additional challenge of
an infinite-dimensional Hilbert space. We present a scalable quantum algorithm
for Quantum Electrodynamics (QED), an Abelian gauge field theory in higher than
one spatial dimensions, designed to address this limit while preserving gauge
invariance. In our formulation, Gauss's law is automatically satisfied when the
implementation remains fully gauge invariant. We demonstrate how gauge
invariance is maintained throughout the lattice discretisation, digitisation,
and qubitisation procedures, and identify the most efficient representation for
extending to large Hilbert space dimensions. Within this framework, we
benchmark several quantum error mitigation techniques and find the calibration
method to perform most effectively. The approach scales naturally to larger
lattices, and we implement and test the 2+1 and 3+1 dimensional setups on
current quantum hardware. Our results indicate that next-generation quantum
platforms could enable reliable, fully quantum simulations of large-scale QED
dynamics.

## 量子废物管理：在量子信息处理中利用剩余状态

**Original Title:** Quantum waste management: Utilizing residual states in quantum information processing

**Authors:** Karol Horodecki, Chirag Srivastava, Leonard Sikorski, Siddhartha Das

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.27687v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.27687v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-31


### 摘要

我们提出了一个量子剩余管理框架，其中资源蒸馏过程后丢弃的状态被重新利用作为后续量子信息任务的输入。这种方法通过结合从剩余状态提取二次资源来扩展了传统的量子资源理论，从而提高了总体资源利用率。作为一个具体例子，我们研究了量子密钥分发（QKD）后剩余剩余状态中私人随机性的提炼。更具体地说，我们定量地表明，在执行众所周知的一致Devetak-Winter协议后，可以从其剩余部分中本地提取私人随机性。我们进一步考虑Gottesman-Lo QKD协议，并从其执行后留下的丢弃状态中提供可实现的私有随机性率。我们还提供了一个正式框架，强调了提高顺序信息处理任务量子资源利用率的一般原则。


### Abstract

We propose a framework for quantum residual management, in which states
discarded after a resource distillation process are repurposed as inputs for
subsequent quantum information tasks. This approach extends conventional
quantum resource theories by incorporating secondary resource extraction from
residual states, thereby enhancing overall resource utility. As a concrete
example, we investigate the distillation of private randomness from the
residual states remaining after quantum key distribution (QKD). More
specifically, we quantitatively show that after performing a well-known
coherent Devetak-Winter protocol one can locally extract private randomness
from its residual. We further consider the Gottesman-Lo QKD protocol, and
provide the achievable rate of private randomness from the discarded states
that are left after its performance. We also provide a formal framework that
highlights a general principle for improving quantum resource utilization
across sequential information processing tasks.
