## QSMOTE-CGM/kCGM：基于QSMOTE的CGM和kCGM用于不平衡数据集分类

**Original Title:** QSMOTE-PGM/kPGM: QSMOTE Based PGM and kPGM for Imbalanced Dataset Classification

**Authors:** Bikash K. Behera, Giuseppe Sergioli, Robert Giuntini

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.16960v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.16960v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-18


### 摘要

量子启发的机器学习（QiML）利用量子理论的数学框架来增强经典算法，特别强调多维特征空间中的内部积结构。在主要的方法中，广泛用于支持向量机的核心技巧提供了高效的相似性计算，而源自量子状态辨别的相当好的测量（CGM）则实现了基于希尔伯特空间几何的分类。本工作以核化CGM（KCGM）和直接基于CGM的分类器的最新发展为基础，对这些范式进行了统一的理论和实证比较。我们使用Quantum SMOTE（QSMOTE）变体分析了它们在合成过采样场景中的性能。实验结果表明，CGM和KCGM分类器的性能始终优于经典的随机森林基线，特别是当使用多个量子副本时。值得注意的是，具有立体声编码和n_copies=2的CGM实现了最高的总体准确性（0.8512）和F1评分（0.8234），而KCGM在QSMOTE变体中表现出竞争力和更稳定的行为，最高评分为0.8511（立体声）和0.8483（幅度）。这些发现强调，量子启发的分类器不仅在召回率和平衡性能方面提供了切实的提高，而且还提供了互补优势：CGM受益于特定编码的增强，而KCGM则确保了采样策略的稳健性。我们的结果促进了对基于核和基于测量的QiML方法的理解，为其在不同数据特征和计算约束下的适用性提供了实用指导。


### Abstract

Quantum-inspired machine learning (QiML) leverages mathematical frameworks from quantum theory to enhance classical algorithms, with particular emphasis on inner product structures in high-dimensional feature spaces. Among the prominent approaches, the Kernel Trick, widely used in support vector machines, provides efficient similarity computation, while the Pretty Good Measurement (PGM), originating from quantum state discrimination, enables classification grounded in Hilbert space geometry. Building on recent developments in kernelized PGM (KPGM) and direct PGM-based classifiers, this work presents a unified theoretical and empirical comparison of these paradigms. We analyze their performance across synthetic oversampling scenarios using Quantum SMOTE (QSMOTE) variants. Experimental results show that both PGM and KPGM classifiers consistently outperform a classical random forest baseline, particularly when multiple quantum copies are employed. Notably, PGM with stereo encoding and n_copies=2 achieves the highest overall accuracy (0.8512) and F1-score (0.8234), while KPGM demonstrates competitive and more stable behavior across QSMOTE variants, with top scores of 0.8511 (stereo) and 0.8483 (amplitude). These findings highlight that quantum-inspired classifiers not only provide tangible gains in recall and balanced performance but also offer complementary strengths: PGM benefits from encoding-specific enhancements, whereas KPGM ensures robustness across sampling strategies. Our results advance the understanding of kernel-based and measurement-based QiML methods, offering practical guidance on their applicability under varying data characteristics and computational constraints.

## 消逝区测量的速度反映了波函数的空间衰减，而不是粒子运动

**Original Title:** The measured speed in the evanescent regime reflects the spatial decay of the wavefunction, not particle motion

**Authors:** Weixiang Ye

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.16580v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.16580v2

**Submitted:** 2025-12-18


### 摘要

Sharoglazova等人最近的论文报告了从耦合光路实验中的量子空间分布中提取的能量相关参数$v $。作者将$v $解释为量子粒子的速度，即使在经典禁止的区域中也是如此，并声称其有限值与波西米亚力学对零粒子速度的预测相矛盾。这一挑战源于对波西米亚存在论框架内v的操作含义的根本误解。我们证明，v量化了波函数幅度的空间梯度，这是引导场的几何属性，而不是点状粒子的运动学速度。因此，该实验并没有提出挑战，而是说明了博米安力学固有的波和粒子方面之间清晰的存在论分离。


### Abstract

The recent paper by Sharoglazova et al. reports an energy-dependent parameter $ν$ extracted from the spatial distribution of photons in a coupled-waveguide experiment. The authors interpret $ν$ as the speed of quantum particles, even in the classically forbidden regime, and claim that its finite value contradicts the Bohmian mechanics prediction of zero particle velocity. This challenge arises from a fundamental misunderstanding of the operational meaning of v within the Bohmian ontological framework. We demonstrate that v quantifies the spatial gradient of the wavefunctions amplitude, a geometric property of the guiding field, not the kinematical velocity of point-like particles. The experiment therefore does not challenge but rather illustrates the clean ontological separation between the wave and particle aspects inherent to Bohmian mechanics.

## 无关紧要的胡萝卜和不存在的大棒：向量子安全系统过渡的信任、治理和安全

**Original Title:** Irrelevant carrots and non-existent sticks: trust, governance, and security in the transition to quantum-safe systems

**Authors:** Ailsa Robertson, Siân Brooke, Sebastian De Haro, Christian Schaffner

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.16974v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.16974v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-18


### 摘要

量子计算对全球网络安全构成了紧迫且广泛认识的威胁，使今天收集的加密政府、金融和医疗保健数据能够在不久的将来解密。因此，向量子安全加密技术过渡至关重要，需要在复杂的多参与者创新系统中采取协调行动。这项研究借鉴了阿姆斯特丹专家研讨会的见解，为量子安全的未来制定了一个具有社会知识的愿景，并分析了当前的创新格局，以找出关键差距以及解决这些差距所需的行动。我们绘制了参与移民过程的十二个关键参与者群体，发现监管机构发挥着最强的直接影响力，而标准化机构则发挥着至关重要的间接作用。这项研究提供了第一个系统级的参与者映射，影响路径和治理责任塑造量子安全过渡，揭示了几个责任不明确的所有权。虽然以荷兰为中心，但我们的研究结果适用于其他国家导航量子安全过渡的情况。


### Abstract

Quantum computing poses an urgent and widely recognised threat to global cybersecurity, enabling encrypted government, financial, and healthcare data harvested today to be decrypted in the near future. Transitioning to quantum-safe cryptography is therefore essential, demanding coordinated action across a complex, multi-actor innovation system. Drawing on insights from an expert workshop in Amsterdam, this study develops a socially informed vision for a quantum-safe future and analyses the current innovation landscape to identify critical gaps and the actions needed to address them. We map twelve key actor groups involved in the migration process, finding that regulators exert the strongest direct influence, while standardisation bodies play a crucial indirect role. This research provides one of the first system-level mappings of actors, influence pathways and governance responsibilities shaping the quantum-safe transition, revealing several responsibilities with unclear ownership. Although centred on the Netherlands, our findings are applicable to other national contexts navigating quantum-safe transitions.

## 量子电路的符号泡利传播预训练

**Original Title:** Symbolic Pauli Propagation for Gradient-Enabled Pre-Training of Quantum Circuits

**Authors:** Saverio Monaco, Jamal Slim, Florian Rehm, Dirk Krücker, Kerstin Borras

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.16674v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.16674v2

**Submitted:** 2025-12-18


### 摘要

量子机器学习模型通常需要昂贵的片上训练程序，并且通常缺乏有效的梯度估计方法。通过使用泡利传播，可以将可观测量的符号表示作为电路参数的分析函数。尽管此类功能表示中的项数量随着电路深度而迅速增长，但对泡利权重和频率分量的类比和受控截断的适当选择可以产生目标可观测量的准确但易于处理的估计器。通过正确的模拟器设计，这种方法可以扩展到经典模拟无法达到的系统大小，从而能够对更大的量子系统进行可扩展的训练。这还可以在部署到量子硬件上之前通过基于梯度的优化进行一种形式的经典预训练。在变分量子本征解算器上演示了所提出的方法，用于获得旋转模型的底薪，表明可以通过可扩展且计算高效的程序获得准确的结果。


### Abstract

Quantum Machine Learning models typically require expensive on-chip training procedures and often lack efficient gradient estimation methods. By employing Pauli propagation, it is possible to derive a symbolic representation of observables as analytic functions of a circuit's parameters. Although the number of terms in such functional representations grows rapidly with circuit depth, suitable choices of ansatz and controlled truncations on Pauli weights and frequency components yield accurate yet tractable estimators of the target observables. With the right ansatz design, this approach can be extended to system sizes beyond the reach of classical simulation, enabling scalable training for larger quantum systems. This also enables a form of classical pre-training through gradient-based optimization prior to deployment on quantum hardware. The proposed approach is demonstrated on the Variational Quantum Eigensolver for obtaining the ground state of a spin model, showing that accurate results can be achieved with a scalable and computationally efficient procedure.

## 双方纠缠的捆绑

**Original Title:** Bundling of bipartite entanglement

**Authors:** Maike Drieb-Schoen, Florian Dreier, Wolfgang Lechner

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.16979v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.16979v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-18


### 摘要

我们研究了二分纠缠，并证明了在约束能量子空间中，多个二分的纠缠谱在整个子空间上是相同的。我们表明，在量子多体系统中，二体纠缠论的影响方式是，它在正时间演化下形成“束”。利用子空间的结构，我们提出了验证两个二分区的纠缠谱在整个子空间中是否相同的方法。对于由宇称嵌入定义的子空间，我们进一步提供了一种可以在多项时间内确定这一点的算法。


### Abstract

We investigate bipartite entanglement and prove that in constrained energy subspaces, the entanglement spectra of multiple bipartitions are the same across the whole subspace. We show that in quantum many-body systems the bipartite entanglement entropy is affected in such a way that it forms "bundles" under unitary time evolution. Leveraging the structure of the subspace, we present methods to verify whether the entanglement spectrum of two bipartitions is identical throughout the entire subspace. For the subspace defined by the parity embedding, we further provide an algorithm that can determine this in polynomial time.

## 连续体经典净化

**Original Title:** Continuum canonical purifications

**Authors:** Jonathan Sorce

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.17014v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.17014v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-18


### 摘要

我们构建并描述了一般代数状态的典型净化，将Woronowicz和Dutta/Faulkner的先前构建扩展到一般量子场论。给定 *-代数上的量子状态，典型净化是“双重”代数上的状态，该代数允许用阴极射线管反射进行解释。我们研究这些放大状态在技术意义上是“纯粹的”的条件，计算它们的模共聚，并将它们与某些环境下的GNS和自然视锥纯化联系起来。在卡米尼蒂和卡佩西亚即将发表的一篇论文中，我们将这一一般理论应用于量子场论中的激发性问题。


### Abstract

We construct and characterize canonical purifications for general algebraic states, extending prior constructions by Woronowicz and by Dutta/Faulkner to general quantum field theories. Given a quantum state on a *-algebra, the canonical purification is a state on a "doubled" algebra that admits an interpretation in terms of CRT reflection. We study the conditions under which these enlarged states are "pure" in the technical sense, compute their modular conjugations, and relate them to GNS and natural-cone purifications in certain settings. In a forthcoming paper with Caminiti and Capeccia, we provide an application of this general theory to the problem of excitability in quantum field theory.

## GIE提案中的子系统（依赖）

**Original Title:** Subsystems (in)dependence in GIE proposals

**Authors:** Nicolas Boulle, Guilherme Franzmann

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.17024v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.17024v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-18


### 摘要

最近的提议表明，检测两个空间叠加的质量之间的纠缠将建立引力的量子性质。然而，这些引力诱导纠缠（GIE）实验依赖于子系统独立性的假设。我们通过代数量子场论（AQFT）的镜头来研究这些建议的理论基础，区分不同的操作和代数概念的独立性。我们认为，状态和测量的子系统的独立性，必不可少的实验逻辑，是不平凡的规范约束和引力敷料的存在。使用引力修饰场，我们记得类空分离的可观测量之间的对易关系是不平凡的，从而破坏了严格的赫伯特空间因式分解。我们进一步探讨了对纠缠证人的影响，研究了子系统代数无法交换时的Tsirelson界，并表明Tsirelson界对于适当对称化的CHSH观测值仍然存在，即使这种“联合”观测值的操作状态在可交换性失败时变得微妙。我们的分析强调了即使在线性化协变量子引力内，微观因果关系的违反也可能会影响拟议的量子引力实验室测试的解释、建模和设计，尽管对于当前的实验制度来说仍然可以忽略不计。尽管我们将GIE风格的协议视为具体的案例研究，但我们强调的子系统独立性问题与低能量（微扰）量子引力是通用的。最后，我们得出了对着装引起的微观因果关系违反的估计，这为当前提案提出了一种补充途径：原则上，将着装引起的微观因果关系违反本身作为对引力量子性质的探测。


### Abstract

Recent proposals suggest that detecting entanglement between two spatially superposed masses would establish the quantum nature of gravity. However, these gravitationally induced entanglement (GIE) experiments rely on assumptions about subsystem independence. We sharpen the theoretical underpinnings of such proposals by examining them through the lens of algebraic quantum field theory (AQFT), distinguishing distinct operational and algebraic notions of independence. We argue that state and measurement independence of subsystems, essential to the experimental logic, is nontrivial in the presence of gauge constraints and gravitational dressing. Using gravitationally dressed fields, we recall that commutation relations between spacelike separated observables are nontrivial, undermining strict Hilbert space factorization. We further explore the implications for entanglement witnesses, investigating the Tsirelson bound when subsystem algebras fail to commute, and showing that the Tsirelson bound persists for a suitably symmetrized CHSH observable even though the operational status of such "joint" observables becomes delicate when commensurability fails. Our analysis highlights how even within linearized covariant quantum gravity, violations of microcausality may affect both the interpretation, modelling, and design of proposed laboratory tests of quantum gravity, despite remaining negligible for current experimental regimes. Although we consider GIE-style protocols as a concrete case study, the subsystem-independence issues we highlight are generic to low-energy (perturbative) quantum gravity. Finally, we derive estimates for dressing-induced microcausality violations, which suggest a complementary avenue to current proposals: in principle, bounding dressing-induced microcausality violations themselves as a probe of the quantum nature of gravity.

## 比较光量子态的零差断层扫描术

**Original Title:** Comparing Homodyne and Heterodyne Tomography of Quantum States of Light

**Authors:** Rhea P. Fernandes, Andrew J. Pizzimenti, Christos N. Gagatsos, Joseph M. Lukens

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.17031v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.17031v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-18


### 摘要

非高斯量子态是光子量子信息处理中的关键资源，其产生和表征在量子光学中的重要性日益增加。在这项工作中，我们从理论上和数值分析的相对效率的零差与外差测量重建非高斯状态，连续变量层析成像中的一个主要的突出问题。结合Fisher信息为基础的形式主义与模拟实验，我们发现零差层析成像优于外差测量的所有非高斯状态测试，虽然两种模式之间的分离证明显着窄于所建议的渐近克拉美-拉奥下限。我们的研究结果应该找到用于优化实际连续变量量子系统的测量策略。


### Abstract

Non-Gaussian quantum states are critical resources in photonic quantum information processing, rendering their generation and characterization of increasing importance in quantum optics. In this work, we theoretically and numerically analyze the relative efficiency of homodyne versus heterodyne measurements for reconstructing non-Gaussian states, a major outstanding question in continuous-variable tomography. Combining a Fisher information-based formalism with simulated experiments, we find homodyne tomography to outperform heterodyne measurements for all non-Gaussian states tested, although the separation between the two modalities proves significantly narrower than suggested by the asymptotic Cramer-Rao lower bound. Our results should find use for optimizing measurement strategies in practical continuous-variable quantum systems.

## 挤压光的阿秒控制

**Original Title:** Attosecond Control of Squeezed Light

**Authors:** Russell Zimmerman, Shashank Kumar, Shiva Kant Tiwari, Eric Liu, Francis Walz, Siddhant Pandey, George J. Economou, Hadiseh Alaeian, Chen-Ting Liao, Valentin Walther, Niranjan Shivaram

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.17046v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.17046v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-18


### 摘要

压缩光通过增强引力波检测等敏感应用的干涉测量，彻底改变了量子计量学。压缩光在量子信息科学中也发挥了关键作用，在量子计算和通信中有许多应用。此前，压缩光主要是使用非线性光学相互作用产生的，其中通过使用合适的材料工程调节产生媒体的非线性来控制压缩程度。在这里，我们用强超快激光场调制拉曼中的三阶非线性响应，以控制阿秒时间尺度上的压缩程度。我们证明了通过输入的费米激光脉冲之间的子周期相延迟控制材料的强场驱动非线性响应，将非线性过程中产生的超快压缩光从幅度压缩改变为相压缩的能力。量子噪音的压缩是使用频率分辨平衡零差检测方案来测量的，该方案能够同时提取不同频率模式下的场四象限。使用这种频率分辨测量，我们提取了完整的相关性矩阵，其中包含了费米压缩光脉冲不同频率模式的场四象限之间的量子相关性。这些结果对于开发具有前所未有的对正交压缩控制水平的量子光源、在多模式量子信息处理中的应用以及通过超快光-物质相互作用中量子场相关性的转换来测量瞬时量子物质相关性具有重大影响。


### Abstract

Squeezed light has revolutionized quantum metrology by enhancing interferometry for sensitive applications such as the detection of gravitational waves. Squeezed light has also played a pivotal role in quantum information science with numerous applications in quantum computing and communication. Previously, squeezed light has been primarily generated using nonlinear optical interactions, where control of the degree of squeezing was possible by tuning the nonlinearity of the generating medium using suitable material engineering. Here, we modulate the third-order nonlinear response in dielectrics with strong ultrafast laser fields to control the degree of squeezing on attosecond time scales. We demonstrate the ability to change the ultrafast squeezed light generated in the nonlinear process from amplitude-squeezed to phase-squeezed by controlling the strong-field-driven nonlinear response of the material through a sub-cycle phase delay between the input femtosecond laser pulses. The squeezing of quantum noise is measured using a frequency-resolved balanced homodyne detection scheme capable of extracting the field quadratures in different frequency modes simultaneously. Using this frequency-resolved measurement we extract the complete coherency matrix containing the quantum correlations between field quadratures across different frequency modes of the femtosecond squeezed light pulse. These results have major implications for the development of quantum light sources with unprecedented levels of control over quadrature squeezing, for applications in multimode quantum information processing, and for measuring transient quantum matter correlations via transduction to quantum field correlations in an ultrafast light-matter interaction.

**Authors:** Amir Targholizadeh, Grigoriy Y. Nikulin, Pankaj K. Jha

我们引入了一个纠缠证人，它在包含费米粒子和玻色子粒子的系统中识别出非对角远程有序（ODLRO）--一种独特的纠缠形式。通过分析每个子系统的粒子孔简化密度矩阵，该方法在费米和玻色子区域独立检测ODLRO，并识别整个混合粒子系统何时出现远程有序。目击者还量化了每种粒子类型内ODLRO的大小，揭示了费米和玻色子相关如何结合形成系统的总纠缠，包括由多体相关而不是粒子统计驱动的粒子-洞对的玻色子凝聚。使用Lipkin-Meshkov-Glick旋转模型，我们展示了从局部化到一种粒子类型的ODLRO到两种粒子类型共享的ODLRO的转变如何捕捉混合粒子环境中集体纠缠的开始，为费米子和玻色子相关性共存的系统提供了新的见解。


### Abstract

We introduce an entanglement witness that identifies off-diagonal long-range order (ODLRO) -- a distinctive form of entanglement -- in systems containing both fermionic and bosonic particles. By analyzing the particle-hole reduced density matrices of each subsystem, the approach detects ODLRO independently in both fermionic and bosonic sectors and identifies when long-range order develops across the entire mixed-particle system. The witness also quantifies the magnitude of ODLRO within each particle type, revealing how fermionic and bosonic correlations combine to form the total entanglement of the system, including a bosonic condensation of particle-hole pairs driven by many-body correlations rather than particle statistics. Using the Lipkin-Meshkov-Glick spin model, we show how the transition from ODLRO localized to one particle type to ODLRO shared by both particle types captures the onset of collective entanglement in a mixed-particle environment, providing new insight into systems where fermionic and bosonic correlations coexist.

## 单光两点传播场的概念：皮米X射线位移传感和纳米分辨率3D X射线显微断层摄影的一种方法

**Original Title:** A Concept of Two-Point Propagation Field of a Single Photon: A Way to Picometer X-ray Displacement Sensing and Nanometer Resolution 3D X-ray Micro-Tomography

**Authors:** Li Hua Yu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.17863v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.17863v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-19


### 摘要

我们引入了两点传播场（TPPF），这是一种实值、相敏量，定义为单量子检测概率相对于源和检测缝隙之间的无限小不透明扰动的函数求导。TBPF是通过分析推导出来的，并被证明在检测缝隙附近表现出稳定的高频弦结构（6.7纳米周期）。这种结构可以使用常规可用的同步加速器通量和实用的纳米制造缝隙/梳状几何形状，以约15 PM的精度进行散粒噪音限制的位移传感，仅要求最后0.5 mm的机械稳定性。相同的原理为未来的纳米分辨率3D X射线显微断层扫描块体样本提供了基础，可能会导致辐射剂量降低。据估计，中央阻挡器和离轴多缝阵列这两种概念策略可将所需的射入注量降低一个数量级以上，随着近期探测器的开发，综合减少两到三个数量级。tpPF概念最初是在单粒子传播的微扰研究中开发的，因此将基本的量子测量问题与实际的高分辨率X射线计量和成像联系起来。


### Abstract

We introduce the two-point propagation field (TPPF), a real-valued, phase-sensitive quantity defined as the functional derivative of the single-photon detection probability with respect to an infinitesimal opaque perturbation placed between source and detection slits. The TPPF is analytically derived and shown to exhibit a stable, high-frequency sinusoidal structure (6.7 nm period) near the detection slit. This structure enables shot-noise-limited displacement sensing at ~15 pm precision using routinely available synchrotron fluxes and practical nanofabricated slit/comb geometries, requiring mechanical stability only over the final 0.5 mm. The same principle provides a foundation for future nanometer-resolution 3D X-ray microtomography of bulk samples, potentially resulting in a reduced radiation dose. Two conceptual strategies, a central blocker and off-axis multi-slit arrays, are estimated to lower the required incident fluence by more than one order of magnitude each, yielding combined reductions of two to three orders of magnitude with near-term detector development. The TPPF concept, originally developed in a perturbative study of single-particle propagation, thus bridges fundamental quantum measurement questions with practical high-resolution X-ray metrology and imaging.

## 利用光介导相互作用模拟拓扑超导体及其竞争有序

**Original Title:** Simulation of topological superconductors and their competing orders using photon-mediated interactions

**Authors:** Anjun Chu, Joyce Kwan, Eric Yilun Song, Seth Hew Peng Chew, James K. Thompson, Ana Maria Rey

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.17889v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.17889v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-19


### 摘要

实现和控制以拓扑超导体为特征的非常规配对仍然是一个核心挑战。我们引入了一个腔QED量子模拟器，它通过定制模拟动量相关配对通道的原子伪旋转之间的腔介导耦合，设计出竞争的手征$p_x+ip_y$和$d_{x#2-y#2}+id_{yx}$阶。可以使用腔QED系统中自然出现的不公度腔-格波波在2D光学格子中设计所需的空间不均匀腔介导的耦合。这个最小且完全可调的平台能够实现受控状态准备和连续测量超导有序参数，揭示具有单一主导配对通道的平衡和突然失超设置中的相，以及具有竞争配对通道的共存状态。至关重要的是，我们的实施允许直接观察平衡中和平衡之外的拓扑转变，为竞争的拓扑相的量子模拟提供了一条强有力的途径，而这些相在固态和超冷原子系统中仍然难以捉摸。


### Abstract

Realizing and controlling the unconventional pairing featured by topological superconductors remains a central challenge. We introduce a cavity QED quantum simulator that engineers competing chiral $p_x+ip_y$ and $d_{x^2-y^2}+id_{xy}$ orders by tailoring cavity-mediated couplings between atomic pseudospins that emulate momentum-dependent pairing channels. The desired spatially inhomogeneous cavity-mediated couplings can be engineered in a 2D optical lattice using incommensurate cavity-lattice wavelengths naturally occurring in cavity QED systems. This minimal and fully tunable platform enables controlled state preparation and continuous measurement of superconducting order parameters, revealing phases in both equilibrium and sudden-quench settings with a single dominant pairing channel, as well as coexistence regimes with competing pairing channels. Crucially, our implementation allows direct observation of topological transitions in and out of equilibrium, providing a powerful route to the quantum simulation of competing topological superconducting phases that remain elusive in solid-state and ultracold-atom systems.

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.17893v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.17893v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-19


### 摘要

神经量子状态（NQS）使用神经网络来表示量子多体系统的波函数，但它们的性能取决于基的选择，但其潜在机制仍然知之甚少。我们使用完全可解的一维伊辛模型来表明，局部基旋转使损失景观保持不变，同时在参数空间中重新定位精确的波函数，有效地增加了其与典型初始化的几何距离。通过扫描旋转角，我们计算量子Fisher信息和Fubini-Study距离，以量化旋转波函数如何在损失景观中移动。用量子自然梯度训练的浅层架构（重点是限制性Boltzmann机（RBM））更有可能根据旋转角度落入鞍点区域：它们实现低能量误差，但无法重现正确的系数分布。在铁磁情况下，近简并本征态会产生高弯曲势垒，从而在中等密度下陷阱优化。我们引入了一个基于可解析解的旋转伊辛模型的框架，以研究在固定损失景观中重新定位目标波函数如何暴露信息几何障碍，例如鞍点和高弯曲区域，从而阻碍浅层NQS优化，强调了变分训练中对队列感知模型设计的必要性。


### Abstract

Neural Quantum States (NQS) use neural networks to represent wavefunctions of quantum many-body systems, but their performance depends on the choice of basis, yet the underlying mechanism remains poorly understood. We use a fully solvable one-dimensional Ising model to show that local basis rotations leave the loss landscape unchanged while relocating the exact wavefunction in parameter space, effectively increasing its geometric distance from typical initializations. By sweeping a rotation angle, we compute quantum Fisher information and Fubini-Study distances to quantify how the rotated wavefunction moves within the loss landscape. Shallow architectures (with focus on Restricted Boltzmann Machines (RBMs)) trained with quantum natural gradient are more likely to fall into saddle-point regions depending on the rotation angle: they achieve low energy error but fail to reproduce correct coefficient distributions. In the ferromagnetic case, near-degenerate eigenstates create high-curvature barriers that trap optimization at intermediate fidelities. We introduce a framework based on an analytically solvable rotated Ising model to investigate how relocating the target wavefunction within a fixed loss landscape exposes information-geometric barriers,such as saddle points and high-curvature regions,that hinder shallow NQS optimization, underscoring the need for landscape-aware model design in variational training.

## 光机散射中的可视化检测效率

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.17894v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.17894v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-19


### 摘要

许多光学测量技术，例如来自波长级颗粒的光散射或用光学杠杆检测表面的运动，将信息编码为复杂的辐射图案。提取所有可用信息对于许多量子增强传感协议至关重要，但通常是不切实际的，因为它需要许多通道来空间解析散射信号。我们提出了一种新方法，通过绘制出局部对检测器表面检测效率的贡献，来可视化实际测量方案如何有效地捕获散射光中可用的信息。我们使用该工具通过实验优化具有象限光电二极管的光机共振器运动幅度的自由空间测量。我们表明，阻挡光电探测器的部分可以提高灵敏度，这与直觉相反，在检测系统中的高级机械模式方面产生了显着的改进。我们还展示了如何将我们的方法应用于小颗粒的光散射测量。


### Abstract

Many optical measurement techniques, such as light scattering from wavelength-scale particles or detecting motion from a surface with an optical lever, encode information in a complex radiation pattern. Extracting all available information is essential for many quantum-enhanced sensing protocols but is often impractical, as it requires many channels to spatially resolve the scattered signal. We present a new method to visualize how efficiently a practical measurement scheme captures the information available in the scattered light by mapping out the local contribution to the detection efficiency on the detector surface. We show that blocking sections of the photodetector enhances sensitivity, counterintuitively yielding a significant improvement in detecting higher-order mechanical modes in the system. We also show how our method can be applied to light scattering measurements of small particles.
