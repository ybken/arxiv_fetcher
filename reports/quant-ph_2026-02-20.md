## 量子启发的张量网络用于逼近PDL流图

**Original Title:** Quantum-Inspired Tensor Networks for Approximating PDE Flow Maps

**Authors:** Nahid Binandeh Dehaghani, Ban Q. Tran, Rafal Wisniewski, Susan Mengel, A. Pedro Aguiar

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.15906v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.15906v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-16


### 摘要

我们研究了量子启发的张量网络（QTN）来逼近流体动力偏微方程（PDEs）的流图。受离散传输和扩散动力学张量化后出现的有效低阶结构的激励，我们将PCE状态编码为矩阵积状态（MPS），并将进化操作符表示为张量串形式的结构化低阶矩阵积操作符（MPO）（例如，由以MPO形式组装的有限差离散化产生）。MPO直接以MPS形式应用，每一步后通过规范化和基于ASD的截断来控制等级增长。我们通过标准矩阵积属性提供理论背景，包括精确的MPS可代表性界限、奇异值截断的局部最优性和利普希茨型多步误差传播估计。一维和二维线性平流扩散和非线性粘性伯格斯方程的实验证明了准确的短期预测、光滑扩散区域中的有利缩放以及非线性多步预测中的误差增长。


### Abstract

We investigate quantum-inspired tensor networks (QTNs) for approximating flow maps of hydrodynamic partial differential equations (PDEs). Motivated by the effective low-rank structure that emerges after tensorization of discretized transport and diffusion dynamics, we encode PDE states as matrix product states (MPS) and represent the evolution operator as a structured low-rank matrix product operator (MPO) in tensor-train form (e.g., arising from finite-difference discretizations assembled in MPO form). The MPO is applied directly in MPS form, and rank growth is controlled via canonicalization and SVD-based truncation after each step. We provide theoretical context through standard matrix product properties, including exact MPS representability bounds, local optimality of SVD truncation, and a Lipschitz-type multi-step error propagation estimate. Experiments on one- and two-dimensional linear advection-diffusion and nonlinear viscous Burgers equations demonstrate accurate short-horizon prediction, favorable scaling in smooth diffusive regimes, and error growth in nonlinear multi-step predictions.

## Maxwell-Dirac电动力学中旋量场微分代数消去的可能性

**Original Title:** On the possibility of differential algebraic elimination of the spinor field from the Maxwell-Dirac electrodynamics

**Authors:** Andrey Akhmeteli

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.15907v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.15907v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-16


### 摘要

研究了Maxwell-Dirac方程中旋量场能否用微分代数方法消去。构造了一个一般的截断幂级数解，将Maxwell-Dirac电动力学的延拓系统关于该解线性化，并计算了相关系数矩阵的秩.结果表明，一般情况下，旋量分量是唯一确定的电磁场及其导数。这有力地表明，微分代数消除旋量场是可能的。


### Abstract

We investigate whether the spinor field can be eliminated from the Maxwell-Dirac equations by differential algebraic methods. A generic truncated power series solution is constructed, the prolonged system of the Maxwell-Dirac electrodynamics is linearized about the solution, and the ranks of the associated coefficient matrices are computed. The results indicate that, generically, the spinor components are uniquely determined by the electromagnetic field and its derivatives. This strongly suggests that differential-algebraic elimination of the spinor field is possible.

## 受GOP启发的具有能量-时间纠缠的多维超密集编码

**Original Title:** GKP-inspired high-dimensional superdense coding with energy-time entanglement

**Authors:** Kai-Chi Chang, Arjun Mirani, Murat Can Sarihan, Xiang Cheng, Michelle Harasimowicz, Patrick Hayden, Chee Wei Wong

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.15125v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.15125v2

**Submitted:** 2026-02-16


### 摘要

超密集编码是利用纠缠来提高经典通信容量的应用，是量子通信的基石。本文提出了一种利用能量-时间纠缠态的多维超密集编码协议。这些状态是双子频率梳，是纠缠时间-频率Gottesman-Kitaev-Preskill（TFGKP）状态或时间-频率网格状态的一个例子。受GKP代码的启发，我们的协议涉及离散化连续时间和频率自由度，并通过时频位移对信息进行编码。这种方法利用了量子频率梳中固有的较大的Hilbert空间，具有对时间和频谱误差的弹性。除了描述该协议的理论结构外，我们还提出了一种使用标准电信组件、时间分辨单量子探测器和频率分束器的实验实现。我们还分析了实验噪音和错误对协议通道容量的影响。我们证明，对于现实的实验参数，当代技术满足了使用双量子频率梳进行超密集编码的先决条件，实现了每个发射光约8.91比特的传输率（相当于481个可区分的消息，误差逐渐消失）。这是Kwiat-Weinfurter方案之前实现的最高4位传输速率的两倍多，同时还具有竞争力的光损失。此外，我们的结果比使用相同参数的单量子频率梳可实现的速率高出4.6倍。因此，我们的协议代表了时间-频率网格状态在纠缠辅助通信中的实验可行应用，为连续变量和多维量子信息的活跃场做出贡献。


### Abstract

Superdense coding, the application of entanglement to boost classical communication capacity, is a cornerstone of quantum communication. In this paper, we propose a high-dimensional superdense coding protocol using energy-time entangled states. These states are biphoton frequency combs, an example of entangled time-frequency Gottesman-Kitaev-Preskill (TFGKP) states or time-frequency grid states. Inspired by GKP codes, our protocol involves discretizing the continuous time and frequency degrees of freedom and encoding information by time-frequency displacements. This approach leverages the inherently large Hilbert space found in quantum frequency combs, with resilience against both temporal and spectral errors. In addition to describing the theoretical structure of the protocol, we propose an experimental implementation using standard telecommunication components, time-resolving single-photon detectors and a frequency beamsplitter. We also analyze the effect of experimental noise and errors on the channel capacity of the protocol. We demonstrate that for realistic experimental parameters, contemporary technologies satisfy the prerequisites for superdense coding with biphoton frequency combs, achieving a transmission rate of approximately 8.91 bits per transmitted photon (equivalent to 481 distinguishable messages with asymptotically vanishing errors). This more than doubles the previously highest transmission rate of 4 bits achieved by the Kwiat-Weinfurter scheme, while also having competitive optical loss. Furthermore, our results beat the rate achievable using a single-photon frequency comb with identical parameters by 4.6 times. Our protocol thus represents an experimentally feasible application of time-frequency grid states to entanglement-assisted communication, contributing to the active fields of continuous-variable and high-dimensional quantum information.

## 超越强化学习：快速且可扩展的量子电路合成

**Original Title:** Beyond Reinforcement Learning: Fast and Scalable Quantum Circuit Synthesis

**Authors:** Lukas Theißinger, Thore Gerlach, David Berghaus, Christian Bauckhage

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.15146v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.15146v2

**Submitted:** 2026-02-16


### 摘要

量子么正合成解决了将抽象量子算法转化为硬件可执行量子门序列的问题。由于底层组合搜索空间的指数级增长，准确解决这一任务通常是不可行的。现有方法存在优化目标不一致、训练成本高昂以及不同量子位计数的概括有限的问题。我们通过使用监督学习来逼近剩余个位数的最小描述长度，并将此估计与随机束搜索相结合以识别接近最优的门序列来减轻这些限制。我们的方法依赖于具有零激发概括的轻量级模型，与之前的基线相比，大幅减少了训练费用。在多个基准测试中，我们实现了更快的时钟合成时间，同时在复杂电路的成功率方面超越了最先进的方法。


### Abstract

Quantum unitary synthesis addresses the problem of translating abstract quantum algorithms into sequences of hardware-executable quantum gates. Solving this task exactly is infeasible in general due to the exponential growth of the underlying combinatorial search space. Existing approaches suffer from misaligned optimization objectives, substantial training costs and limited generalization across different qubit counts. We mitigate these limitations by using supervised learning to approximate the minimum description length of residual unitaries and combining this estimate with stochastic beam search to identify near optimal gate sequences. Our method relies on a lightweight model with zero-shot generalization, substantially reducing training overhead compared to prior baselines. Across multiple benchmarks, we achieve faster wall-clock synthesis times while exceeding state-of-the-art methods in terms of success rate for complex circuits.

## 使用可编程量子处理器观察新兴弯曲时空中的量子多体动力学

**Original Title:** Observing quantum many-body dynamics in emergent curved spacetime using programmable quantum processors

**Authors:** Brendan Rhyno, Bastien Lapierre, Smitha Vishveshwara, Khadijeh Najafi, Ramasubramanian Chitra

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.15524v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.15524v2

**Submitted:** 2026-02-17


### 摘要

我们使用IBM Heron处理器上的80个超导量子位在新兴弯曲背景中数字模拟了量子多体动力学。通过工程空间变化的耦合在自旋-XXZ链，符合低能量描述的模型中的一个不均匀的Tomonaga-Luttinger液体，我们实现了激发，遵循测地线的一个有效的度量继承从底层的空间变形。在Néel和少数旋转翻转状态的激冷之后，我们观察到弯曲的光锥传播、局部磁性中的水平诱导冻结以及由工程空间变形设置的位置相关振荡频率。尽管空间不均匀性很强，但不等时闪烁子揭示了旋转链中的弹道准粒子传播。这些结果将大规模数字量子处理器建立为一个灵活的平台，用于在可调谐和合成弯曲时空中详细和受控地探索多体动力学。


### Abstract

We digitally simulate quantum many-body dynamics in emergent curved backgrounds using 80 superconducting qubits on IBM Heron processors. By engineering spatially varying couplings in the spin-$\frac12$ XXZ chain, consistent with the low-energy description of the model in terms of an inhomogeneous Tomonaga-Luttinger liquid, we realize excitations that follow geodesics of an effective metric inherited from the underlying spatial deformation. Following quenches from Néel and few-spin-flip states, we observe curved light-cone propagation, horizon-induced freezing in the local magnetization, and position-dependent oscillation frequencies set by the engineered spatial deformation. Despite strong spatial inhomogeneity, unequal-time correlators reveal ballistic quasiparticle propagation in the spin chain. These results establish large-scale digital quantum processors as a flexible platform for detailed and controlled exploration of many-body dynamics in tunable and synthetic curved spacetimes.

## 量子位的稳态一致性与量子地图不相容

**Original Title:** Steady state coherence in a qubit is incompatible with a quantum map

**Authors:** Hans C. Fogedby

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.15790v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.15790v2

**Submitted:** 2026-02-17


### 摘要

在复合系统与浴相互作用的情况下，我们考虑了最近关于单个量子位中稳态内聚性的提议。基于场理论方法，我们在雷德菲尔德描述中重新分析了这个问题。我们发现，根据最近提案的雷德菲尔德方法产生了稳态内聚性，但也违反了产生负居数的量子地图的性质。这个问题可以通过应用符合适当量子地图的林德布拉德方程来解决。然而，林德布拉德方程也意味着稳态一致性的缺乏。我们得出的结论是，量子位的稳态一致性与量子地图不相容。


### Abstract

We consider the recent proposal of steady state coherences in a single qubit in the case of a composite system-bath interaction. Based on a field theoretical approach we reanalyse the issue within a Redfield description. We find that the Redfield approach in accordance with a recent proposal yields steady state coherences but also violates the properties of a quantum map yielding negative populations. The issue is resolved by applying the Lindblad equation which is in accordance with a proper quantum map. The Lindblad equation, however, also implies the absence of steady state coherence. We conclude that steady state coherence in a a qubit is incompatible with a quantum map.

### 摘要

## 接近可积性的远程相互作用旋转链中的类KPZ输运

**Original Title:** KPZ-like transport in long-range interacting spin chains proximate to integrability

**Authors:** Sajant Anand, Jack Kemp, Julia Wei, Christopher David White, Michael P. Zaletel, Norman Y. Yao

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.15933v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.15933v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-17


### 摘要

海森堡模型等各向同性可积旋转链的特征是超扩散旋转输运，属于一个迄今尚未确定的动力学普适性类别，该类别与Kardar、Parisi和Zhang（KPZ）的类别密切相关。为了确定这些结果是否扩展到更通用的一维模型，特别是那些可在量子模拟器中实现的模型，我们使用最先进的张量网络方法研究了不可积的远程海森堡模型中的旋转和能量输运。尽管缺乏可积性和扩散的渐进预期，但对于指数律模型（指数为2 & a &\infty$），我们观察到长寿命的$z=3/2$超扩散旋转输运和与KPZ标度函数一致的两点相关子，最多可达倍t \sim 10#3/J$。我们猜测这种类似KPZ的输运是由于这种乘势定律相互作用模型与Inozemtsev模型的可积家族的接近性，我们表明该模型也在所有相互作用范围内表现出类似KPZ的旋转输运。最后，我们考虑了在里德伯原子阵列和超冷极分子中自然实现的各向异性旋转模型，证明在实验环境中可以观察到广泛的长寿命、非扩散输运。


### Abstract

Isotropic integrable spin chains such as the Heisenberg model feature superdiffusive spin transport belonging to an as-yet-unidentified dynamical universality class closely related to that of Kardar, Parisi, and Zhang (KPZ). To determine whether these results extend to more generic one-dimensional models, particularly those realizable in quantum simulators, we investigate spin and energy transport in non-integrable, long-range Heisenberg models using state-of-the-art tensor network methods. Despite the lack of integrability and the asymptotic expectation of diffusion, for power-law models (with exponent $2 < α< \infty$) we observe long-lived $z=3/2$ superdiffusive spin transport and two-point correlators consistent with KPZ scaling functions, up to times $t \sim 10^3/J$. We conjecture that this KPZ-like transport is due to the proximity of such power-law-interacting models to the integrable family of Inozemtsev models, which we show to also exhibit KPZ-like spin transport across all interaction ranges. Finally, we consider anisotropic spin models naturally realized in Rydberg atom arrays and ultracold polar molecules, demonstrating that a wide range of long-lived, non-diffusive transport can be observed in experimental settings.

## 张量网络状态下Clifford解开的局限性

**Original Title:** Limits of Clifford Disentangling in Tensor Network States

**Authors:** Sergi Masot-Llima, Piotr Sierant, Paolo Stornati, Artur Garcia-Saez

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.15942v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.15942v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-17


### 摘要

张量网络方法利用量子状态的有限纠缠来有效地模拟多体系统。或者，Clifford电路提供了一个用于处理高度纠缠稳定器状态的框架，这些稳定器状态具有低魔力，因此也是典型的易于处理的。Clifford张量网络结合了两种方法的优点，利用Clifford电路来降低状态张量网络描述的经典复杂性，对模拟方法产生了良好的影响。我们研究克利福德变换作用于张量网络的解开能力，特别强调纠缠冷却策略。我们确定了精确或启发式克利福德理清器有效的制度，解释了两种方法之间的联系，并随着非克利福德资源的积累来描述它们的崩溃。此外，我们证明，除了稳定器设置之外，任何Clifford操作都无法普遍将单个量子位从任意非Clifford旋转中解脱出来。我们的结果澄清了基于Clifford的模拟方法的功能和基本局限性。


### Abstract

Tensor network methods leverage the limited entanglement of quantum states to efficiently simulate many-body systems. Alternatively, Clifford circuits provide a framework for handling highly entangled stabilizer states, which have low magic and are thus also classically tractable. Clifford tensor networks combine the benefits of both approaches, exploiting Clifford circuits to reduce the classical complexity of the tensor network description of states, with promising effects on simulation approaches. We study the disentangling power of Clifford transformations acting on tensor networks, with a particular emphasis on entanglement cooling strategies. We identify regimes where exact or heuristic Clifford disentanglers are effective, explain the link between the two approaches, and characterize their breakdown as non-Clifford resources accumulate. Additionally, we prove that, beyond stabilizer settings, no Clifford operation can universally disentangle even a single qubit from an arbitrary non-Clifford rotation. Our results clarify both the capabilities and fundamental limitations of Clifford-based simulation methods.

## 使用硅封盖增强的导电纳米线单量子探测器性能

**Original Title:** Enhanced Superconducting Nanowire Single Photon Detector Performances using Silicon Capping

**Authors:** C. Klein, S. Cohen, T. Descamps, A. Iovan, P. Zolotov, P. Vennéguès, I. Florea, F. Semond, V. Zwiller

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.15948v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.15948v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-17


### 摘要

基于钼钛氮（NdTIN）的超纳米线单量子探测器（SNSPD）因其在从X射线到中红外的宽光谱范围内的高性能而闻名。尽管如此，由于表面氧化和厚度小于5纳米的薄膜缺乏均匀性而造成的制造挑战和性能下降仍然是实现高质量检测器的重大障碍。在这项工作中，我们研究了硅覆盖层对薄膜性质和SNSPD性能的影响。硅覆盖层有效地抑制氧化并提高超临界转变温度。这使得在3 K下薄至3纳米的薄膜具有超导电性，增加了图案化纳米线的临界电流，并显着将饱和平台从可见光扩展到近红外（高达2050纳米）：这些检测器保持低于50 ps的定时抖动，即使对于宽至250纳米的纳米线和检测面积为20 x 20 μm2的纳米线也是如此。我们的结果确定，受覆盖层保护的较薄薄膜可以制造更宽的线，减少纳米织物的挑战并扩大有效单量子检测的工作温度范围。


### Abstract

Niobium Titanium nitride (NbTiN) based superconducting nanowire single photon detectors (SNSPDs) are known for their high performance across a wide spectral range, from the X-ray to the mid-infrared. Nonetheless, fabrication challenges and performance degradation attributable to surface oxidation and lack of uniformity in films thinner than 5 nm remain a significant barrier for achieving high-quality detectors. In this work, we study the influence of a Silicon capping layer on film properties and on the performance of SNSPDs. A Silicon capping layer effectively suppresses oxidation and increases the superconducting transition temperature. This enables superconductivity in films as thin as 3 nm at 3 K, increases critical current in patterned nanowires and significantly extends the saturation plateau from the visible to the near infrared (up to 2050 nm): These detectors maintain sub-50 ps timing jitter, even for nanowires as wide as 250 nm and with detection areas of 20x20μm2. Our results establish that thinner films protected by a capping layer allow for the fabrication of wider wires, decreasing nanofabrication challenges and extending the operating temperature range for efficient single photon detection.

## 通过条件动力学和全相关数据学习对量子侧通道泄漏进行硬件不可知建模

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.15966v1
