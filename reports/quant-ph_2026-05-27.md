## 类似于运营商增长的量子猝灭

**Original Title:** Quantum Quenches that Resemble Operator Growth

**Authors:** Xiangyu Cao

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.23874v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.23874v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-22


### 摘要

我们研究生长猝灭，这是局部猝灭，可能会逐渐破坏某些动力学约束量子格子模型（例如东西方模型）中的假真空的稳定。我们指出了与海森堡图像中局部操作员动力学的正式类比。利用这个类比，我们通过调整操作者动力学概念和方法获得了有关生长抑制的几个结果。首先，应用Krylov方法（递代方法），我们推测了通用猝灭中Lanzcos系数的线性增长，$a_m \sim ¥ m$（对角线）和$b_m \sim a m$（非对角线），扩展了运营商增长假设。我们表明，当$时，生长抑制动力学都局限于Krylov和Fock空间|ν|> 2 a $，并当$时，推导出李雅普诺夫指数的增长抑制类似物$_L \le \SQRT{4 a #2-v #2}$的界|ν| < 2阿尔法$。其次，我们在Sachdev-Ye-Kitaev（SYK）模型的启发下在大型$N$可解增长抑制中实现了Fock本地化。在大-$q$ SYK增长激冷中，李亚普诺夫指数的界限饱和。相比之下，生长抑制几乎总是Fock局限于非随机的全对全生长抑制，服从于半经典。最后，在一维East-West模型中，我们将Fock空间笼态解释为守恒电荷的存在。我们表明，后者具有弹道输运由于目前的保护。此外，添加具有微调幅度的跳频会由于平坦频带而引起部分局部化。我们的工作表明，生长猝灭作为一个有前途的方法来实现非平衡相干现象在多体系统。


### Abstract

We study growth quenches, which are local quenches that may gradually destabilize a false vacuum in certain kinetic constrained quantum lattice models, such as the East-West model. We point out a formal analogy with the dynamics of a local operator in the Heisenberg picture. Exploiting this analogy, we obtain several results on growth quenches by adapting operator-dynamics concepts and methods. First, applying the Krylov approach (recursion method), we conjecture the linear growth of Lanzcos coefficients in generic quenches, $a_m \sim νm$ (diagonal), and $b_m \sim αm$ (off-diagonal), extending an operator growth hypothesis. We show that the growth quench dynamics is localized in both Krylov and Fock spaces when $|ν| > 2 α$, and derive a bound for the growth quench analogue of Lyapunov exponent $λ_L \le \sqrt{4 α^2 - ν^2}$ when $|ν| < 2 α$. Second, we realize the Fock localization in large $N$ solvable growth quenches inspired by Sachdev-Ye-Kitaev (SYK) models. The bound on Lyapunov exponent is saturated in large-$q$ SYK grow quench. By contrast, the growth quench is almost always Fock localized in a nonrandom all-to-all growth quench amenable to semiclassics. Finally, in the 1D East-West model, we interpret Fock space cage states as the existence of a conserved charge. We show that the latter has ballistic transport due to current conservation. Moreover, adding hopping with a fine-tuned amplitude induces a partial localization due to a flat band. Our work suggest growth quenches as a promising approach to realize non-equilibrium coherent phenomena in many-body systems.

## 区分波西米亚语境与科兴-斯佩克语境

**Original Title:** Distinguishing Bohmian contextuality from Kochen-Specker contextuality

**Authors:** Anton Skott, Jan-Åke Larsson

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.24446v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.24446v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-23


### 摘要

量子上下文性是一个用于描述隐藏变量理论性质的概念，即隐藏变量预先确定的测量结果取决于测量上下文。测量上下文一词可能有不同的含义，从而产生不同口味的量子上下文。第一个发现的味道是Kochen-Specker（KS）上下文，其中测量结果将取决于与所选测量联合执行的兼容测量。这里要与KS上下文进行比较的另一种风格是Bohmian力学中提到的，其中一些特定测量的结果并不完全由模型状态指定，而是还取决于所使用测量设备的具体细节。有人声称，这种类型的波西米亚语境对于在隐藏变量模型中实现KS语境是必要的。在本文中，我们表明事实并非如此。最近提出的上下文实体模型（COM）[Hindlycke和Larsson，Phys. Rev. Lett. 2022]产生KS上下文预测，但不具有波西米亚上下文; COM允许的每个测量的结果都可以从模型状态本身预测。这将波西米亚语境与KS语境区分开来，并使得对这两个概念进行单独研究。


### Abstract

Quantum contextuality is a concept used to describe the property of hidden-variable theory that measurement outcomes predetermined by the hidden variables depend on the measurement context. The term measurement context can have different meanings, giving rise to different flavours of quantum contextuality. The first discovered flavour is Kochen-Specker (KS) contextuality where measurement outcomes will depend on what compatible measurements are jointly performed with the selected measurement. Another flavour, here to be compared with KS contextuality, is that referred to in Bohmian mechanics where outcomes of some specific measurements are not completely specified by the model state, but depend also on specifics of the measurement device used. It has been claimed that this type of Bohmian contextuality is necessary to enable KS contextuality in a hidden variable model. In this paper we show that this is not the case. The recently proposed Contextual Ontological Model (COM) [Hindlycke and Larsson, Phys. Rev. Lett. 2022] produces KS contextual predictions but does not have the Bohmian contextuality; the outcome of every measurement allowed by COM can be predicted from the model state itself. This distinguishes Bohmian contextuality from KS contextuality, and enables individual study of the two concepts.

## 锥形保罗陷阱中从集体向局部放射运动模式的转变

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.25125v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.25125v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-24


### 摘要

对于耦合的失调振荡器，可以观察到单独或集体振荡。后者用于线性保罗陷阱中的量子信息处理。在这里，我们研究了锥形保罗陷阱中从较强轴向约束下的集体辐射模式到较低轴向约束下的单个辐射振荡的转变。在过渡区域中对本征模进行了详细的实验研究，并与理论预测进行了比较。所研究的特征将使研究改进的热传输现象和俘获离子中的缺陷形成成为可能。


### Abstract

With coupled detuned oscillators, either individual or collective oscillations are observable. The latter is used in quantum information processing in linear Paul traps. Here, we study the transition from collective radial modes at stronger axial confinements into individual radial oscillations at low axial confinements in a tapered Paul trap. The eigenmodes are experimentally studied in detail in the transition regime and compared with theoretical predictions. The features studied will enable investigation of modified heat transport phenomena and defect formation in trapped ions.

## 来自多水平干涉的消散时间准晶体

**Original Title:** Dissipative Time Quasicrystals from Multilevel Interference

**Authors:** Kang Shen, Xiangming Hu, Fei Wang

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.25667v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.25667v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-25


### 摘要

边界时间晶体在驱动-消散多体系统中通过持续的周期性振荡表现出连续时间转换对称性的自发破坏。在这里，我们表明多层干扰提供了超越周期顺序的自然路径，在无需外部施加准周期驱动的情况下实现了消散时间准晶体。我们考虑了具有两个简并激发态和两个简并激发态的集体驱动消散四层系综。在热力学极限下，精确的平均场动力学简化为二维环面上的无理流，产生具有由两个不公度的基本频率产生的离散谱的准周期序参数。最大李雅普诺夫指数的消失表明非线性自相容动力学保持非混乱。我们的结果为时间准周期序建立了一个最小干扰诱导机制，并为多层系统中的更高维度准周期动力学开辟了一条道路。


### Abstract

Boundary time crystals exhibit spontaneous breaking of continuous time-translation symmetry through persistent periodic oscillations in driven-dissipative many-body systems. Here, we show that multilevel interference provides a natural route beyond periodic order, enabling dissipative time quasicrystals without externally imposed quasiperiodic driving. We consider a collectively driven-dissipative four-level ensemble with two degenerate excited states and two degenerate ground states. In the thermodynamic limit, the exact mean-field dynamics reduces to an irrational flow on a two-dimensional torus, yielding quasiperiodic order parameters with discrete spectra generated by two incommensurate fundamental frequencies. Vanishing maximal Lyapunov exponents demonstrate that the nonlinear self-consistent dynamics remains nonchaotic. Our results establish a minimal interference-induced mechanism for time-quasiperiodic order and open a route toward higher-dimensional quasiperiodic dynamics in multilevel systems.

## 精确的强零模在具有大各向异性的可积旋转系统中是通用的

**Original Title:** Exact strong zero modes are generic in integrable spin systems with large anisotropy

**Authors:** Sascha Gehrmann

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.26205v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.26205v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-25


### 摘要

强零模（SZR）是边缘局部化的操作员，它与Hamilton量进行交换，直至系统大小呈指数级小的修正，从而产生长得惊人的边缘相干时间。在某些设置中，特别是某些可积模型，可以使这个交换子在有限大小下完全消失，从而定义精确的SZR（ESZR）。然而，可集成环境中现有的ESZR构造是逐个模型进行的，尚未统一到通用框架中。在这里，我表明ESZR通常出现在具有各向异性相互作用的广泛可积旋转模型家族中。它们的存在源于基础R和K矩阵的两个代数性质--分别是谱参数的准周期性和无踪迹性--提供了统一的、与模型无关的机制。该框架将XXZ链中已知的ESZR及其更高旋转的概括恢复为特殊情况，并预测之前未识别的模型中的ESZR。


### Abstract

Strong zero modes (SZMs) are edge-localized operators that commute with the Hamiltonian up to corrections exponentially small in system size, yielding anomalously long edge coherence times. In some settings, notably certain integrable models, this commutator can be made to vanish exactly at finite size, defining an exact SZM (ESZM). Existing ESZM constructions in the integrable setting, however, have proceeded model by model and have not been unified into a common framework. Here, I show that ESZMs arise generically in a broad family of integrable spin models with anisotropic interactions. Their existence follows from two algebraic properties of the underlying R- and K-matrices -- quasi-periodicity in the spectral parameter and tracelessness, respectively -- providing a uniform, model-independent mechanism. The framework recovers the known ESZM in XXZ chain and its higher-spin generalizations as special cases and predicts ESZMs in previously unrecognized models.

## 黑洞熵波动的权衡关系

**Original Title:** Trade-off Relation for Black Hole Entropy Fluctuations

**Authors:** Kensuke Gallock-Yoshimura, Yoshihiko Hasegawa

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.26214v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.26214v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-25


### 摘要

黑洞通过改变量子物质场的熵来对坍缩做出反应。由于此类物质本质上是量子的，因此熵响应应该对其量子涨落敏感。我们表明，在随机半经典引力中，视界无法记录具有任意小的熵波动的相关量子信息。对于Danielson-Satishchandran-Wald退相关实验中编码哪条路径信息的落体光，我们推导出黑洞熵变的随机方差与量子数之间的权衡关系。


### Abstract

Black holes respond to infalling quantum matter fields by changing their entropy. Since such matter is quantum in nature, the entropy response should be sensitive to its quantum fluctuations. We show, within stochastic semiclassical gravity, that a horizon cannot record relevant quantum information with arbitrarily small entropy fluctuations. For the infalling photons encoding which-path information in the Danielson-Satishchandran-Wald decoherence experiment, we derive a trade-off relation between the stochastic variance of the black hole entropy change and the photon number.

## 纠缠生成的普遍界限

**Original Title:** Universal Bound for Entanglement Generation

**Authors:** Alfred Li, Daisuke Miki, Yanbei Chen

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.26215v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.26215v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-25


### 摘要

我们推导出了在白热噪音存在下一般双线性相互作用下纠缠产生的普遍条件。虽然已经提出了各种协议来增加产生的纠缠量，但目前尚不清楚它们是否也可以放宽纠缠产生本身的阈值。使用Gorini-Kossakowski-Sudarshan-Lindblad描述，我们分析了一般的多模式系统，并推导出白热噪音下双线性相互作用的分离性保持条件。作为重力诱导纠缠的应用，我们表明引力相互作用必须主导热噪音才能产生纠缠。特别是，这种束缚不能通过改变初始状态或引入介体系统来放松，尽管这些成分一旦产生就可能会增强纠缠的量。这些结果建立了热环境中纠缠生成协议的一般限制。


### Abstract

We derive a universal condition for entanglement generation under general bilinear interactions in the presence of white thermal noise. While various protocols have been proposed to enhance the amount of generated entanglement, it remains unclear whether they can also relax the threshold for entanglement generation itself. Using a Gorini-Kossakowski-Sudarshan-Lindblad description, we analyze general multimode systems and derive a separability-preserving condition for bilinear interactions under white thermal noise. As an application to gravity-induced entanglement, we show that the gravitational interaction must dominate over thermal noise for entanglement to arise. In particular, this bound cannot be relaxed by changing the initial state or by introducing mediator systems, although such ingredients may enhance the amount of entanglement once it is generated. These results establish a general limitation on entanglement-generation protocols in thermal environments.

## 基于张量网络的扭场到局部算子的映射

**Original Title:** Mapping twist fields to local operators via tensor networks

**Authors:** Andrea Bulgarelli, Marco Panero, Paolo Stornati, Luca Tagliacozzo

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.26208v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.26208v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-25


### 摘要

扭场是计算量子多体系统中雷尼信息量的强大形式工具，但它们在张量网络状态中的传统公式涉及作用于虚拟自由度的操作，而这些操作在实验中无法直接访问。在这项工作中，我们构造了作用于物理希尔伯特空间的显式局部操作符，其期望值再现矩阵积状态中扭曲场的作用。我们的构造在内射性极限和张量选择在垂直性中心时是精确的，并提供了一种直接的操作方法来评估雷尼（Rényi）信息，而无需访问辅助张量指数。我们在横场伊辛模型中对我们的公式进行了数值测试，证明了随着达到注入尺度，快速收敛到精确的纠缠量。此外，我们表明，一旦参考大小超过由相关长度设置的特征标度，从相对较小的参考系统确定的扭曲操作符就可以可靠地传输到较大的系统。由于由此产生的操作符承认按照有限数量的局部可观测量进行分解，因此我们的结果提供了一个可扩展且通过实验可访问的框架来探测量子模拟器中的纠缠。


### Abstract

Twist fields are a powerful formal tool to compute Rényi entropies in quantum many-body systems, but their conventional formulation in tensor network states involves operations acting on virtual degrees of freedom, which are not directly accessible in experiments. In this work, we construct explicit local operators acting on the physical Hilbert space whose expectation values reproduce the action of twist fields in matrix product states. Our construction is exact in the injectivity limit and when the tensor is chosen at the center of orthogonality, and provides a direct operational method to evaluate Rényi entropies without accessing auxiliary tensor indices. We test our formulation numerically in the transverse-field Ising model, demonstrating rapid convergence to the exact entanglement entropy as the injectivity scale is reached. Furthermore, we show that twist operators determined from relatively small reference systems can be reliably transferred to larger systems, once the reference size exceeds a characteristic scale set by the correlation length. Since the resulting operators admit a decomposition in terms of a finite number of local observables, our results provide a scalable and experimentally accessible framework to probe entanglement in quantum simulators.

## 费米子非高斯性的实践检验和见证

**Original Title:** Practical Tests and Witnesses of Fermionic non-Gaussianity

**Authors:** Tobias Haug, Xhek Turkeshi, Piotr Sierant

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.26218v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.26218v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-25


### 摘要

检测量子状态何时离开可有效模拟的费米高斯区是对量子设备进行基准测试和认证费米魔法资源的核心任务。我们基于费米子反平坦度（FAF）开发实际测试和证人，FAF是一种基于协方差矩阵的非高斯性测量。对于$n$-量子位状态，我们使用两个补充协议来估计FAF：两个副本Bell测量和基于Majorana双线性交换匹配的单副本方案。这些产率测试器将纯高斯状态与远离高斯集的状态$e $区分开来，使用$O（n#2/e #2）$两副本贝尔测量或$O（n#3/e #4）$单副本测量，提高了对$n$和$的依赖性的最新技术水平。对于混合状态，我们引入了经过纯度纠正的FAF见证，它证明非高斯性并且对噪音具有高度稳健性。通过我们的见证，我们在IQM量子计算机上证明了噪音可以减少和增强非高斯性。最后，通过检查伪非高斯性，我们表明伪随机状态生成的密码任务需要广泛的费米子非高斯性。总而言之，这些结果为检测、见证和量化非高斯费米子资源提供了实验上可用的工具。


### Abstract

Detecting when a quantum state leaves the efficiently simulable fermionic Gaussian regime is a central task for benchmarking quantum devices and certifying fermionic magic resources. We develop practical tests and witnesses based on fermionic antiflatness (FAF), a covariance-matrix-based measure of non-Gaussianity. For $n$-qubit states, we estimate FAF using two complementary protocols: two-copy Bell measurements and a single-copy scheme based on commuting matchings of Majorana bilinears. These yield testers that distinguish pure Gaussian states from states $ε$-far from the Gaussian set, using $O(n^2/ε^2)$ two-copy Bell measurements or $O(n^3/ε^4)$ single-copy measurements, improving the state of the art in the dependence on both $n$ and $ε$. For mixed states, we introduce a purity-corrected FAF witness that certifies non-Gaussianity and is highly robust to noise. With our witness, we demonstrate on the IQM quantum computer that noise can both reduce and enhance non-Gaussianity. Finally, by examining pseudo non-Gaussianity, we show that the cryptographic task of pseudorandom-state generation requires extensive fermionic non-Gaussianity. Together, these results provide experimentally accessible tools for detecting, witnessing, and quantifying non-Gaussian fermionic resources.

## 定向渗透量子态的纠缠模式转变

**Original Title:** Entanglement Pattern Transition of Quantum States from Directed Percolation

**Authors:** Julian Boesl, Frank Pollmann, Michael Knap

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.26219v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.26219v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-25


### 摘要

纠缠结构和临界现象的变化是量子相转变的标志。在这里，我们讨论它们如何出现在具有不同纠缠模式的状态类别之间的转变中，超出了物质稳定平衡相的范式。使用随机自动机和等距张量网络状态（isoTNS）之间的映射，我们从Domany-Kinzel自动机构建了一个二维量子状态，这是一个具有有向渗透类吸收相转变的（1+1）D过程。在自动机的临界点，相应的isoTNS托管所有空间方向上的代数相关性。该状态的连续父Hamilton具有简并的底态管。它由一个产物状态（吸收状态）和一个第二状态组成，第二状态经历从远处区域之间的成对纠缠（类似于W状态）转变为具有平凡纠缠的状态。我们的结果证明了isoTNS和经典随机进化之间的对应关系如何可用于探索稳定的接地状态流之外的希尔伯特空间结构。


### Abstract

Changes in the entanglement structure and critical phenomena are hallmarks of quantum phase transitions. Here, we discuss how they appear in transitions between classes of states with distinct entanglement patterns beyond the paradigm of stable equilibrium phases of matter. Using a mapping between stochastic automata and isometric Tensor Network States (isoTNS), we construct a two-dimensional quantum state from the Domany-Kinzel automaton, which is a (1+1)D process with an absorbing phase transition in the directed percolation class. At the critical point of the automaton, the corresponding isoTNS hosts algebraic correlations in all spatial directions. The continuous parent Hamiltonian of this state has a degenerate ground state manifold. It consists of a product state (the absorbing state) and a second state that undergoes a transition from pairwise entanglement between distant regions, similarly to the W state, to a state with trivial entanglement. Our results demonstrate how the correspondence between isoTNS and classical stochastic evolution can be used to probe the Hilbert space structure beyond stable ground state manifolds.

## 参数驱动耦合量子振荡器中的模式选择性激发

**Original Title:** Mode-selective excitation in parametrically driven coupled quantum oscillators

**Authors:** Ranjani Seshadri

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.26227v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.26227v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-25


### 摘要

当以其自然频率的两倍驱动时，参数驱动的经典谐振子表现出共振不稳定性，最低能量配置保持不受驱动的影响。相比之下，由于波函数的空间离域化，量子力学对应物的底薪表现出对这种驱动的非平凡响应。PR的标准实现涉及调制振荡器的自然频率。在这里，我们研究了一种不同的驱动协议，其中两个这样的量子谐振子之间的耦合被参数化调制。我们表明，原则上可以调整驱动频率，以选择性地激发任何所需的正常模式，同时使另一个模式接近其接地状态。只有每个正常模式中量子数为偶数的状态才会被填充。在参数共振窗口内，激发相对于占有数遵循乘势律衰减，与非共振观察到的指数衰减相反。我们还简要讨论如何将这个框架扩展到N个耦合振荡器的系统


### Abstract

A parametrically driven classical harmonic oscillator exhibits resonant instability when driven at twice its natural frequency, with the lowest energy configuration remaining unaffected by the drive. In contrast, the ground state of the quantum mechanical counterpart shows a non-trivial response to such a drive due to the spatial delocalization of the wavefunction.
