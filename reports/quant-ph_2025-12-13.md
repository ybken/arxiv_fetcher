## 经过认证的私人量子时间比任何经典时钟都快

**Original Title:** Certified Private Quantum Time Ticks Away Faster than Any Classical Clock

**Authors:** Karl Svozil

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.09100v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.09100v2

**Submitted:** 2025-12-09


### 摘要

我们引入了纠缠时钟的概念，其中时间流在操作上由单重状态上测量结果的离散登记来定义。将两个这样的时钟与经典模型的同步率进行比较，我们发现，在相对角度较钝时，量子时钟的滴答声严格比这个经典基准更快。我们进一步提出了一种认证私人时间的协议，将与设备无关的随机性认证适应于时间域;这保证了主权时间轴的运行速度比任何本地现实机制允许的都快。


### Abstract

We introduce the concept of an entangled clock, where the flow of time is operationally defined by the discrete registration of measurement outcomes on a singlet state. Comparing the synchronization rate of two such clocks against classical models, we find that at obtuse relative angles, the quantum clock ticks strictly faster than this classical benchmark. We further propose a protocol for Certified Private Time, adapting device-independent randomness certification to the temporal domain; this guarantees a sovereign timeline that ticks away faster than any local realistic mechanism allows.

## 通过Dropout优化Surface代码的测量计划

**Original Title:** Optimized Measurement Schedules for the Surface Code with Dropout

**Authors:** Benjamin Anker, Dripto M. Debroy

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10871v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.10871v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-11


### 摘要

最近的工作表明，制造缺陷可以很好地处理，使用依赖于中间错误校正周期状态的策略。在这项工作中，我们提出了两个改进的原始处方。首先，我们量化了最初为标准正方形网格表面代码提出的一组更完整的规范算子的选择对标准正方形网格表面代码的影响，以及有效切除未使用量子位的新方法。其次，我们利用LUCI框架的表达能力作为中间表示，使用整数线性规划从有效的LUCI电路的大空间中找到高性能的物理电路。我们表明，在$d = 11$表面代码上，量子位和耦合器的数据丢失率为$1%（3%）$，这些优化允许使用SI 1000噪音模型在$0.1%$ noise下进行的综合征提取比$4d$提高14.5%（23.6%）$。


### Abstract

Recent work has shown that fabrication defects can be well-handled using a strategy relying on the mid-error-correction-cycle state. In this work we present two improvements to the original prescription. First, we quantify the impact of the choice of a more complete set of gauge operators originally proposed for the hex-grid surface code on the standard square-grid surface code, as well as a new method for excising effectively unused qubits. Second, we leverage the expressivity of the LUCI framework as an intermediate representation, using integer linear programming to find performant physical circuits from the large space of valid LUCI circuits. We show that on the $d = 11$ surface code at $1\%(3\%)$ dropout rate for qubits and couplers, these optimizations allow for a total improvement of $14.5\%(23.6\%)$ over $4d$ round of syndrome extraction using the SI1000 noise model at $0.1\%$ noise.

## 多次量子虚时间进化

**Original Title:** Multiple-time Quantum Imaginary Time Evolution

**Authors:** Julio Del Castillo, Mats Granath, Evert van Nieuwenburg

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10875v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.10875v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-11


### 摘要

量子虚时进化（QITE）是在量子硬件上准备接地状态的一种强大方法。然而，对于普通汉密尔顿人来说，执行QITE的测量预算很昂贵。保真度和计算成本都很大程度上取决于合适的局部区域和Hamilton分区的定义。在这项工作中，我们介绍了多时间QITE算法（MT-QITE）。我们展示了使用多个虚时间如何大幅提高所得基态的保真度以及相对于之前发布的QITE算法的测量费用，同时保留其确定性特征及其与临时分析的独立性。此外，与QITE和其他基于QITE的算法不同，MT-QITE是可并行的，并且我们表明，即使在具有非局部相互作用的Hamilton中，分区也可能具有计算优势。


### Abstract

Quantum Imaginary-Time Evolution (QITE) is a powerful method for preparing ground states on quantum hardware. However, executing QITE has costly measurement budgets for general Hamiltonians. Both fidelity and computational cost are strongly dependent on the definition of suitable local domains and Hamiltonian partitions. In this work, we introduce the Multiple-Time QITE algorithm (MT-QITE). We show how using more than one imaginary time substantially improves the fidelity of the resulting ground state as well as the measurement overhead with respect to the previously published QITE algorithm, while preserving its deterministic character and its independence from ad hoc ansatze. Moreover, unlike QITE and other QITE-based algorithms, MT-QITE is parallelizable, and we show that even in Hamiltonians with non-local interactions, partitioning may entail a computational advantage.

## INTECALC：计算多方量子系统中几何纠缠的工具包

**Original Title:** ENTCALC: Toolkit for calculating geometric entanglement in multipartite quantum systems

**Authors:** Piotr Masajada, Aby Philip, Alexander Streltsov

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10884v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.10884v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-11


### 摘要

我们介绍了entcalc，这是一个用于估计多方量子态几何纠缠的Python和LAB包。该包的运作方式如下：给定一个多方量子状态作为输入，它输出其几何纠缠度的估计。对于纯状态，它计算几何纠缠以及估计误差。对于混合状态，它提供了几何纠缠的下限和上限，从而识别出真实值所在的区间。我们提供了多种计算下限的方法，使用户能够平衡准确性与计算成本。我们将entcalc应用于几个代表性示例，包括$3.0times3 $ PPT纠缠态、GZZ和W态的混合物、选定的三量子位旋转链的热态以及有噪音的GZZ和W态。我们通过量化旋转链中的纠缠来观察量子相转变的特征。我们还证明，非相邻位点之间的纠缠可以通过调节外部磁场来激活。在所有测试的情况下，发现下限和上限之间的差距非常小，这表明entcalc提供了这些状态的几何纠缠的高度准确估计。


### Abstract

We present entcalc, a Python and MATLAB package for estimating the geometric entanglement of multipartite quantum states. The package operates as follows: given a multipartite quantum state as input, it outputs an estimate of its geometric entanglement. For pure states, it computes the geometric entanglement together with an estimation error. For mixed states, it provides both lower and upper bounds on the geometric entanglement, thereby identifying an interval in which the true value lies. We provide several methods to compute the lower bound, enabling users to balance accuracy against computational cost. We apply entcalc to several representative examples, including for $3\otimes3$ PPT entangled states, mixtures of GHZ and W states, thermal states of selected three-qubit spin chains, and noisy GHZ and W states. We observe signatures of quantum phase transitions by quantifying entanglement in spin chains. We also demonstrate that entanglement between non-neighbouring sites can be activated by tuning the external magnetic field. In all tested cases, the gap between the lower and upper bounds is found to be very small, indicating that entcalc provides highly accurate estimates of the geometric entanglement for these states.

## 量化经典和量子界限以解析光学显微镜中紧密间隔、非相互作用、同时发射的偶极源

**Original Title:** Quantifying classical and quantum bounds for resolving closely spaced, non-interacting, simultaneously emitting dipole sources in optical microscopy

**Authors:** Armine I. Dingilian, Aarnah Kurella, Cheyenne S. Mitchell, Dhananjay Dhruva, David J. Durden, Mikael P. Backlund

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10889v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.10889v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-11


### 摘要

最近的理论和实验工作表明，与估计两个光学点源之间的间隔相关的量子Fisher信息在小间隔处仍然是有限的，有效地开辟了同时发射源的超分辨率成像的新途径。然而，大多数研究迄今为止，隐含调用标量近似，这是不合适的高数值孔径显微镜的上下文中。利用参数估计理论，在这里，我们考虑估计两个紧密间隔的偶极子发射器之间的分离，一个常用的单分子光学信标模型。我们考虑两种极限情况：一种是发射器的方向是固定的和相等的，另一种是两个偶极子在测量过程中自由地对所有的方向空间进行采样。我们使用Fisher信息和Cramér-Rao界的量子和经典变体来量化精度限制。在所有情况下，发射的垂直性质使分析变得复杂，但通过在方位角-辐射极化基础上对收集的光进行适当过滤，可以挽救之前提出的通过图像倒置干涉测量饱和量子费舍尔信息的方案。


### Abstract

Recent theoretical and experimental work has shown that the quantum Fisher information associated with estimating the separation between two optical point sources remains finite at small separations, effectively opening new routes to super-resolution imaging of simultaneously emitting sources. Most studies to date, however, implicitly invoke the scalar approximation, which is not appropriate in the context of high-numerical-aperture microscopy. Utilizing parameter estimation theory, here we consider the estimation of separation between two closely spaced dipole emitters, a commonly employed model for single-molecule optical beacons. We consider two limiting cases: one in which the orientations of the emitters are fixed and equal, and another in which both dipoles freely sample all of orientation space over the course of the measurement. We quantify precision limits using quantum and classical variants of the Fisher information and Cramér-Rao bound. In all cases, the vectorial nature of the emission complicates the analyses, but with appropriate filtering of the collected light in the azimuthal-radial polarization basis, a previously proposed scheme to saturate the quantum Fisher information via image inversion interferometry can be salvaged.

## 具有强电子相关性的电声系统的混合量子经典矩阵积状态和Lanczos方法：应用于与爱因斯坦音素耦合的无序系统

**Original Title:** Hybrid quantum-classical matrix-product state and Lanczos methods for electron-phonon systems with strong electronic correlations: Application to disordered systems coupled to Einstein phonons

**Authors:** Heiko Georg Menzler, Suman Mondal, Fabian Heidrich-Meisner

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10899v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.10899v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-11


### 摘要

我们提出了两种量子经典混合方法来模拟电子-音素系统的时间依赖性，这些方法准确地用数字方式处理电子相关性，经典地处理光学-音素自由度。它们是一种依赖于时间的Lanczos方法和一种矩阵积状态方法，每种方法都与多轨迹Eurfest方法相结合。由于这种逼近，对于小音素频率的隔热区，预计会得到可靠的结果。我们讨论了一维相互作用的无旋费米子系统的两种方法的收敛性质，并为荷尔斯泰因链提供了基准。作为第一个应用，我们研究了与爱因斯坦振子耦合的相互作用无纺费米子系统中以及存在猝灭无序的情况下电荷密度波序的衰变。我们研究了松弛动力学对电子-音素耦合强度的依赖性，并提供了数字证据，表明强无序系统与经典振子的耦合导致离域，从而破坏了该系统中（有限大小）多体局部化的稳定性。


### Abstract

We present two quantum-classical hybrid methods for simulating the time-dependence of electron-phonon systems that treat electronic correlations numerically exactly and optical-phonon degrees of freedom classically. These are a time-dependent Lanczos and a matrix-product state method, each combined with the multi-trajectory Ehrenfest approach. Due to the approximations, reliable results are expected for the adiabatic regime of small phonon frequencies. We discuss the convergence properties of both methods for a system of interacting spinless fermions in one dimension and provide a benchmark for the Holstein chain. As a first application, we study the decay of charge density wave order in a system of interacting spinless fermions coupled to Einstein oscillators and in the presence of quenched disorder. We investigate the dependence of the relaxation dynamics on the electron-phonon coupling strength and provide numerical evidence that the coupling of strongly disordered systems to classical oscillators leads to delocalization, thus destabilizing the (finite-size) many-body localization in this system.

## 相稳定长波中的快速多模式俘获离子激光冷却

**Original Title:** Rapid multi-mode trapped-ion laser cooling in a phase-stable standing wave

**Authors:** Zhenzhong Xing, Hamim Mahmud Rivy, Vighnesh Natarajan, Aditya Milind Kolhatkar, Gillenhaal Beck, Karan K. Mehta

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10900v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.10900v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-11


### 摘要

激光冷却是精确控制和询问原子量子系统的基础。在具有俘获离子的量子计算和计量学的背景下，用于缩放的感兴趣的集成光学控制还可以额外提高相干和非相干操作的性能。在这里，我们利用钙离子控制所需的紫外至红外波长的多通道集成传输，包括被动相稳定的紫外长波来演示快速、宽带激光冷却。我们通过实验验证了一项长期存在的预测，实现了驻波（SW）节点处的Doppler冷却低于传统的Doppler极限。利用电磁感应透明度（EIT），我们在实验上在150 $μ$s内冷却了跨越约5 MHz带宽的运动模式，从Doppler温度到接近接地状态，达到$\bar n \约0.05$音素数目标模式。针对可比的运行波（RW）方案的直接评估表明，SW实施在冷却率、运动模式带宽和最终音素数方面同时具有优势，正如之前理论上预测的那样。我们的结果证明了结构光具有强大的接地激光冷却的能力，以及可扩展的光学控制方法在基本功能上的明显优势。


### Abstract

Laser cooling is fundamental to precise control and interrogation of atomic quantum systems. In the context of quantum computing and metrology with trapped ions, the integrated optical control of interest for scaling may additionally enable increased performance of coherent and incoherent operations. Here we utilize multi-channel integrated delivery of ultraviolet to infrared wavelengths required for calcium ion control including in passively phase-stable ultraviolet standing waves to demonstrate rapid, broadband laser cooling. We experimentally verify a long-standing prediction, realizing Doppler cooling to below the conventional Doppler limit at a standing-wave (SW) node. Utilizing electromagnetically induced transparency (EIT), we experimentally cool motional modes spanning an approximately 5 MHz bandwidth from the Doppler temperature to near the ground state within 150 $μ$s, reaching $\bar n \approx 0.05$ phonon number occupancies for the target mode. Direct evaluation against the comparable running-wave (RW) scheme shows the SW implementation's simultaneous advantage in cooling rate, motional mode bandwidth, and final phonon number as previously theoretically predicted. Our results demonstrate structured light's capability for robust ground-state laser cooling, and a clear advantage in a fundamental functionality enabled by scalable approaches to optical control.

## 近似张量网络收缩的分区展开

**Original Title:** Partitioned Expansions for Approximate Tensor Network Contractions

**Authors:** Glen Evenbly, Johnnie Gray, Garnet Kin-Lic Chan

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10910v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.10910v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-11


### 摘要

我们提出了一种通过将网络划分为计算上更便宜的网络的总和来逼近张量网络的收缩的方法。这种方法我们称之为分区网络扩展（PNE），它建立在最近的工作的基础上，该工作使用循环修正系统地改进了信念传播（BP）逼近。然而，与以前的方法相比，我们的扩展不需要实施已知的BP固定点，即使在BP完全失败的情况下仍然可以产生准确的结果。我们方法的灵活性通过应用于各种示例网络，包括有限2D和3D网络、无限网络、具有开放索引的网络以及具有简并BP不定点的网络。由Ising，AKLT和随机张量组成的网络的基准数值结果通常显示出比BP的精度提高了几个数量级（当BP解决方案可获得时），并且还显示出比基于奇异值分解（SVD）的传统网络近似在某些任务上的性能有所改善。


### Abstract

We propose a method for approximating the contraction of a tensor network by partitioning the network into a sum of computationally cheaper networks. This method, which we call a partitioned network expansion (PNE), builds upon recent work that systematically improves belief propagation (BP) approximations using loop corrections. However, in contrast to previous approaches, our expansion does not require a known BP fixed point to be implemented and can still yield accurate results even in cases where BP fails entirely. The flexibility of our approach is demonstrated through applications to a variety of example networks, including finite 2D and 3D networks, infinite networks, networks with open indices, and networks with degenerate BP fixed points. Benchmark numerical results for networks composed of Ising, AKLT, and random tensors typically show an improvement in accuracy over BP by several orders of magnitude (when BP solutions are obtainable) and also demonstrate improved performance over traditional network approximations based on singular value decomposition (SVD) for certain tasks.

## 耗散双光子谐振腔中的量子比特退相干：实时瞬子和Wigner函数

**Original Title:** Qubit decoherence in dissipative two-photon resonator: real-time instantons and Wigner function

**Authors:** V. Yu. Mylnikov, S. O. Potashin, Alex Kamenev

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10921v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.10921v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-11


### 摘要

研究了在有限失谐量作用下双光子驱动和双光子耗散的玻色单腔的量子动力学。利用一个隐藏的时间反演对称性，Wigner表示和WKB方法，我们引入了一个有效的相空间势描述的稳态。它揭示了两个吸引点，这是由于量子涨落的亚稳态。通过采用Keldysh实时路径积分形式主义，我们计算这些吸引子之间的量子激活过程的瞬子轨迹，并建立与维格纳表示的基本连接。这种关系将稳态相空间描述与动态量子激活过程统一起来。我们还推导出了系统退散率的解析式。我们的工作为分析驱动消耗非线性共振器中的量子双稳、亚稳和去相干提供了一个连贯的理论框架，对玻色子量子位和量子信息处理的设计有直接影响。


### Abstract

We study the quantum dynamics of a single bosonic cavity subject to two-photon driving and two-photon dissipation in the presence of finite detuning. Exploiting a hidden time-reversal symmetry, the Wigner representation and the WKB method, we introduce an effective phase-space potential for description of the steady state. It reveals two attracting points, which are metastable due to quantum fluctuations. By employing the Keldysh real-time path integral formalism, we compute the instanton trajectory governing the quantum activation process between these attractors and establish a fundamental connection with the Wigner representation. This relation unifies the steady-state phase-space description with dynamical quantum activation processes. We also derive an analytical expression for the decoherence rate of the system. Our work provides a coherent theoretical framework for analyzing quantum bistability, metastability, and decoherence in driven-dissipative nonlinear resonators, with direct implications for the design of bosonic qubits and quantum information processing.

**Submitted:** 2025-12-11


### 摘要

我们开发了一个从有噪的量子实验中学习的框架，重点关注通过有噪的耦合访问未特征化的系统的故障容忍设备。我们的起点是复杂性类$\textsf{NBQP}$（“有噪音的BQP”），它对有噪音的容错量子计算机进行建模，这些计算机通常无法对其查询的Oracle系统进行错误纠正。使用这个类，我们表明，对于自然预言问题，噪音可以消除理想无噪音学习器的指数量子学习优势，同时保留NISQ和耐故障设备之间的超方程差距。除了Oracle分离之外，我们还研究具体的嘈杂学习任务。对于纯度测试，指数级两拷贝优势在局部去极化噪音的单一应用下就会崩溃。尽管如此，我们发现了一种由AdS/CFT驱动的环境，其中抗噪结构可以在有噪的情况下恢复量子学习优势。然后，我们分析有噪音的泡利阴影断层扫描，推导出描述实例大小、量子存储和噪音如何控制样本复杂性的下限，并设计具有参数相似缩放的算法。总而言之，我们的结果表明，大多数指数量子学习优势背后的Bell基和SWAP测试基元从根本上对噪音很脆弱，除非实验系统具有潜在的噪音稳健结构。因此，在未来的实验中实现有意义的量子优势需要了解抗噪物理性质如何与可用的算法技术相结合。


### Abstract

We develop a framework for learning from noisy quantum experiments, focusing on fault-tolerant devices accessing uncharacterized systems through noisy couplings. Our starting point is the complexity class $\textsf{NBQP}$ ("noisy BQP"), modeling noisy fault-tolerant quantum computers that cannot, in general, error-correct the oracle systems they query. Using this class, we show that for natural oracle problems, noise can eliminate exponential quantum learning advantages of ideal noiseless learners while preserving a superpolynomial gap between NISQ and fault-tolerant devices. Beyond oracle separations, we study concrete noisy learning tasks. For purity testing, the exponential two-copy advantage collapses under a single application of local depolarizing noise. Nevertheless, we identify a setting motivated by AdS/CFT in which noise-resilient structure restores a quantum learning advantage in a noisy regime. We then analyze noisy Pauli shadow tomography, deriving lower bounds that characterize how instance size, quantum memory, and noise control sample complexity, and design algorithms with parametrically similar scalings. Together, our results show that the Bell-basis and SWAP-test primitives underlying most exponential quantum learning advantages are fundamentally fragile to noise unless the experimental system has latent noise-robust structure. Thus, realizing meaningful quantum advantages in future experiments will require understanding how noise-robust physical properties interface with available algorithmic techniques.

## 关于决策主体和更高级因果过程

**Original Title:** On Decision-Making Agents and Higher-Order Causal Processes

**Authors:** Matt Wilson

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10937v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.10937v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-11


### 摘要

我们建立了部分可观察Markov决策过程（POMDPs）中的决策主体和单输入过程函数（高级量子运算的经典极限）之间的精确对应关系。在此标识中，代理的策略和内存更新结合到流程函数w中，该函数通过链接产品与POMDP环境交互。这表明了一种双重解释：在物理学观点中，过程功能充当插入本地操作（代理干预）的环境，而在人工智能观点中，它对代理进行编码，而插入的功能代表环境。我们通过将独立于观察的去中心化POMDPs识别为多输入过程功能的自然域，将这一观点扩展到多代理系统。


### Abstract

We establish a precise correspondence between decision-making agents in partially observable Markov decision processes (POMDPs) and one-input process functions, the classical limit of higher-order quantum operations. In this identification an agent's policy and memory update combine into a process function w that interacts with a POMDP environment via the link product. This suggests a dual interpretation: in the physics view, the process function acts as the environment into which local operations (agent interventions) are inserted, whereas in the AI view it encodes the agent and the inserted functions represent environments. We extend this perspective to multi-agent systems by identifying observation-independent decentralized POMDPs as natural domains for multi-input process functions.
