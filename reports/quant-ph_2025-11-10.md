## 在欧洲首台利用其异类CPU-图形处理器架构的兆级超级计算机上进行50个量子比特的通用量子模拟

**Original Title:** Universal Quantum Simulation of 50 Qubits on Europe`s First Exascale Supercomputer Harnessing Its Heterogeneous CPU-GPU Architecture

**Authors:** Hans De Raedt, Jiri Kraus, Andreas Herten, Vrinda Mehta, Mathis Bode, Markus Hrywniak, Kristel Michielsen, Thomas Lippert

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.03359v2

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.03359v2

**Submitted:** 2025-11-05


### 摘要

我们开发了一个新版本的高性能J ulich通用量子计算机模拟器（JUQCS-50），它利用了JUPITER超级计算机中使用的GH 200超级芯片的关键功能，首次实现了50量子位通用量子计算机的模拟。JUQCS-50通过三项关键创新实现了这一目标：（1）通过高带宽CPU-GPU互连和LPDDR 5内存将可用内存扩展到GPU限制之外;（2）自适应数据编码，以减少内存占用，同时在精度和计算工作量方面进行可接受的权衡;以及（3）动态网络流量优化器。这些进步导致K计算机上先前48量子位记录的11.4倍加速。


### Abstract

We have developed a new version of the high-performance J\"ulich universal
quantum computer simulator (JUQCS-50) that leverages key features of the GH200
superchips as used in the JUPITER supercomputer, enabling simulations of a
50-qubit universal quantum computer for the first time. JUQCS-50 achieves this
through three key innovations: (1) extending usable memory beyond GPU limits
via high-bandwidth CPU-GPU interconnects and LPDDR5 memory; (2) adaptive data
encoding to reduce memory footprint with acceptable trade-offs in precision and
compute effort; and (3) an on-the-fly network traffic optimizer. These advances
result in an 11.4-fold speedup over the previous 48-qubit record on the K
computer.

## 因果顺序不确定的异常热力流动和量子奥托循环

**Original Title:** Anomalous heat flow and quantum Otto cycle with indefinite causal order

**Authors:** Qing-Feng Xue, Qi Zhang, Xu-Cai Zhuang, Yun-Jie Xia, Enrico Russo, Giulio Chiribella, Rosario Lo Franco, Zhong-Xiao Man

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.04028v2

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.04028v2

**Submitted:** 2025-11-06


### 摘要

热量从较高的温度自发流动到较低的温度的原理是经典热力学的基石，通常被认为与相互作用的顺序无关。虽然这对于处于平衡状态的宏观系统来说也是如此，但我们在这里表明，当两个相同热化通道之间的相互作用顺序不确定时，异常的热量就会出现，从而热量有时可以从较冷的实体流向较热的实体。利用这种反常的热量流动，我们设计了一个具有不确定因果顺序的量子奥托循环，它不仅实现了制冷，还能产生功。在量子量子设置中对异常热量流动和量子奥托循环进行了实验模拟，这为该理论提供了原理证明。


### Abstract

The principle that heat spontaneously flows from higher temperature to lower
temperature is a cornerstone of classical thermodynamics, often assumed to be
independent of the sequence of interactions. While this holds true for
macroscopic systems at equilibrium, here we show that, when the order of
interactions between two identical thermalization channels is indefinite, an
anomalous heat flow emerges, whereby heat can sometime flow from a colder
entity to a hotter one. Taking advantage of this anomalous heat flow, we design
a quantum Otto cycle with indefinite causal order, which not only achieves
refrigeration but also generates work. The anomalous heat flow and the quantum
Otto cycle are experimentally simulated in a photonic quantum setup, which
provides a proof-of-principle demonstration of the theory.

## 量子场的连续矩阵积运算符

**Original Title:** Continuous matrix product operators for quantum fields

**Authors:** Erickson Tjoa, J. Ignacio Cirac

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2511.04545v2

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.04545v2

**Submitted:** 2025-11-06


### 摘要

在这篇工作中，我们介绍了量子场论中连续矩阵乘积算子的一个例子。我们表明：（i）他们承认一个封闭形式的表达，在有限数量的矩阵值的功能，而不参考任何晶格参数;（ii）他们得到一个合适的连续极限矩阵乘积算子;（iii）他们保持纠缠面积法直接在连续，特别是他们映射连续矩阵乘积态（cMPS）到另一个cMPS。作为一个应用，我们利用这个矩阵不等式构造了几个超越量子元胞自动机的连续矩阵积酉群。


### Abstract

In this work we introduce an ansatz for continuous matrix product operators
for quantum field theory. We show that (i) they admit a closed-form expression
in terms of finite number of matrix-valued functions without reference to any
lattice parameter; (ii) they are obtained as a suitable continuum limit of
matrix product operators; (iii) they preserve the entanglement area law
directly in the continuum, and in particular they map continuous matrix product
states (cMPS) to another cMPS. As an application, we use this ansatz to
construct several families of continuous matrix product unitaries beyond
quantum cellular automata.
