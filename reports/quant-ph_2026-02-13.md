## 量子多世界解释，简单地说

**Original Title:** The Quantum Many-Worlds Interpretation, Simply Told

**Authors:** Brian C. Odom

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.09272v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.09272v2

**Submitted:** 2026-02-09


### 摘要

量子力学的多世界解释（MWI）提出了一个简单的问题。如果一切都按照相同的量子方程随时间进化，现实会是什么样子？以同样的立场对待微观物体、测量设备和观察者有一种有吸引力的一致性，但预测是否与我们的观察相匹配？在这里，我们为测辐射热计探测器建立了一个模型，在原子干涉仪中进行哪条路径测量。我们讨论了MWI的主张，即虽然两种测量结果都发生在每次实验迭代中，但观察者将只经历一种或另一种结果，其概率与实验一致。最后，我们讨论MWI如何没有远距离动作。本文的撰写目的是供任何拥有量子力学本科课程的人阅读。


### Abstract

The many-worlds interpretation (MWI) of quantum mechanics poses a simple question. What would reality look like if everything evolved in time according to the same quantum equations? There is an attractive consistency to treating microscopic objects, measuring devices, and observers all on the same footing, but do the predictions match our observations? Here, we build a model for a bolometer detector making a which-path measurement in an atom interferometer. We discuss the MWI claim that, while both measurement outcomes occur in each experimental iteration, an observer will experience only one outcome or the other, with a probability consistent with experiment. Finally, we discuss how MWI does not have action at a distance. This article is written to be accessible to anyone with an undergraduate course in quantum mechanics.

## 顺磁性吸附物之间特定材料无序交换相互作用模拟的场相关量子位通量噪音

**Original Title:** Field-Dependent Qubit Flux Noise Simulated from Materials-Specific Disordered Exchange Interactions Between Paramagnetic Adsorbates

**Authors:** Keith G. Ray, Yaniv Rosen, Jonathan L Dubois, Vincenzo Lordi

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.09471v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.09471v2

**Submitted:** 2026-02-10


### 摘要

从量子位和磁力计到暗物质探测器的超导量子设备都受到顺磁性表面缺陷和杂质产生的磁通量噪音的影响。这些旋转系统可以具有复杂的动力学特征，包括Berezinskiii-Kosterlitz-无轴转变，其取决于格子、相互作用、外场和无序。然而，典型模型中包含的无序并不是特定于材料的，从而降低了准确捕获测量的通量噪音现象的能力。我们对由Al$_2$O$_3$表面上顺磁性O$_2$分子组成的旋转格子进行了基于第一原理的模拟，该表面可能是超导量子位中的通量噪音源，以阐明减轻通量噪音的机会。我们模拟了具有无序取向的表面吸收物的集合，并使用密度函数理论计算了取向依赖性的交换耦合。因此，我们的旋转模拟没有自由参数或假设的无序功能形式，而是捕捉到了真实系统中出现的缺陷景观中的相关性。我们计算了电子对之间的交换相互作用范围，最小值为0.016 meV和-0.023 meV，处于作为二级系统并耦合到GHz共振器所需的范围内。我们计算了通量噪音频率、温度和外加磁场的相关性，以及极化率-通量噪音互相关。计算的趋势与实验相符，证明具有顺磁性的表面排列有材料特定的无序和相互作用，捕捉到了在超导电路中观察到的磁通量噪音的各种性质。此外，我们发现外部电场可以调节旋转-旋转相互作用强度并降低通量噪音。


### Abstract

Superconducting quantum devices, from qubits and magnetometers to dark matter detectors, are influenced by magnetic flux noise originating from paramagnetic surface defects and impurities. These spin systems can feature complex dynamics, including a Berezinskii-Kosterlitz-Thouless transition, that depend on the lattice, interactions, external fields, and disorder. However, the disorder included in typical models is not materials-specific, diminishing the ability to accurately capture measured flux noise phenomena. We present a first principles-based simulation of a spin lattice consisting of paramagnetic O$_2$ molecules on an Al$_2$O$_3$ surface, a likely flux noise source in superconducting qubits, to elucidate opportunities to mitigate flux noise. We simulate an ensemble of surface adsorbates with disordered orientations and calculate the orientation-dependent exchange couplings using density functional theory. Thus, our spin simulation has no free parameters or assumed functional form of the disorder, and captures correlation in the defect landscape that would appear in real systems. We calculate a range of exchange interactions between electron pairs, with the smallest values, 0.016 meV and -0.023 meV, being in the range required to act as a two-level system and couple to GHz resonators. We calculate the flux noise frequency, temperature, and applied external magnetic field dependence, as well as the susceptibility-flux noise cross-correlation. Calculated trends agree with experiment, demonstrating that a surface harboring paramagnetic adsorbates arranged with materials-specific disorder and interactions captures the various properties of magnetic flux noise observed in superconducting circuits. In addition, we find that an external electric field can tune the spin-spin interaction strength and reduce magnetic flux noise.

## 具有互磁性的玻色-爱因斯坦凝聚体的性质

**Original Title:** Properties of Bose-Einstein condensates with altermagnetism

**Authors:** Jia Wang, Zhao Liu, Xia-Ji Liu, Hui Hu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.10362v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.10362v2

**Submitted:** 2026-02-10


### 摘要

我们研究了在存在\{互磁性}的情况下混相区中弱相互作用的双成分玻色-爱因斯坦凝聚体，即共线且全局补偿的磁序，打破了旋转对称性，同时保持零净磁性。在博戈柳博夫理论中，我们推导出准粒子谱和相干因子，并表明交流磁序一般会引起低能量激发的角度依赖性。结果，音速、量子耗尽中的动量分辨磁性以及密度-旋转响应函数获得各向异性分量。我们表明，这些各向异性贡献在角度平均后消失，这与互磁性的定义特征一致：非平凡的局部旋转极化而没有整体磁性。最后，我们评估了地磁相对接地能量的Lee-Huang-Yang修正。在可预见的未来，我们的结果应该可以用超冷原子实验来验证。


### Abstract

We investigate a weakly interacting two-component Bose--Einstein condensate in the miscible regime in the presence of \emph{altermagnetism}, i.e., a collinear and globally compensated magnetic order that breaks spin-rotation symmetry while maintaining zero net magnetization. Within Bogoliubov theory, we derive the quasiparticle spectrum and coherence factors and show that altermagnetic order generically induces an angular dependence of the low-energy excitations. As a result, the sound velocity, momentum-resolved magnetization in the quantum depletion, and density--spin response functions acquire anisotropic components. We show that these anisotropic contributions vanish after angular averaging, consistent with the defining feature of altermagnetism: nontrivial local spin polarization without a global magnetization. Finally, we evaluate the Lee--Huang--Yang correction to the ground-state energy in the altermagnetic phase. Our results should be testable with ultracold-atom experiments in the foreseeable future.

## 多量子位纠缠的最大剩余强一夫一妻制不等式

**Original Title:** Maximum residual strong monogamy inequality for multiqubit entanglement

**Authors:** Dong-Dong Dong, Xue-Ke Song, Liu Ye, Dong Wang, Gerardo Adesso

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.10668v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.10668v2

**Submitted:** 2026-02-11


### 摘要

我们建立了两个新的不等式：加权强一夫一妻制（WSM）和最大剩余强一夫一妻制（MRSM），这加剧了多量子位状态的广义Coffman-Kundu-Wootters不平等。WSM不平等与强一夫一妻制（SM）猜想区分开来[Phys. Rev. Lett. 113，110501（2014）]通过使用系数而不是指数来调制分配给各种m部贡献的权重。相比之下，MRSM不等式仅使用最大m部纠缠来公式化。我们发现MRSM不等式的剩余纠缠可以有效地区分可分状态。我们还比较了各种SM不等式的紧密性，并使用四量子位混合状态和五量子位纯状态提供示例来说明MRSM不等式。这些例子描述了涉及不同数量量子位的纠缠分量之间的权衡关系。我们的结果提供了一个严格的框架来描述和量化多方纠缠的一夫一妻制。


### Abstract

We establish two new inequalities, the weighted strong monogamy (WSM) and the maximum residual strong monogamy (MRSM), which sharpen the generalized Coffman-Kundu-Wootters inequity for multiqubit states. The WSM inequality distinguishes itself from the strong monogamy (SM) conjecture [Phys. Rev. Lett. 113, 110501 (2014)] by using coefficients rather than exponents to modulate the weight allocated to various m-partite contributions. In contrast, the MRSM inequality is formulated using only the maximum m-partite entanglement. We find that the residual entanglement of the MRSM inequality can effectively distinguish the separable states. We also compare the tightness of various SM inequalities and provide examples using a four-qubit mixed state and a five-qubit pure state to illustrate the MRSM inequality. These examples characterize the trade-off relations among entanglement components involving varying numbers of qubits. Our results provide a rigorous framework to characterize and quantify the monogamy of multipartite entanglement.

## Bell态与量子隐形传态的注记

**Original Title:** Notes on Bell states and quantum teleportation

**Authors:** Yong Zhang, Wei Zeng, Ming Lian

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11218v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.11218v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-11


### 摘要

贝尔态和量子隐形传输在量子信息和计算的研究中发挥着重要作用。但对两者的全面理论研究仍有待于进行。这项工作的目的是研究广义贝尔状态的重要代数性质，并探索量子隐形传态的拓扑特征。首先，引入基定理和基群来解释广义贝尔基通过单位矩阵的扩展仍然是标准垂直基。然后定义扭转操作符，以建立广义多量子比特贝尔状态与两个量子比特贝尔状态的张量积之间的联系。除此之外，Temperley-Lieb代数、辫子群关系和Yang-Baxter方程也被用来提供广义贝尔状态和量子隐形传输的拓扑图解描述。事实证明，我们的方法能够清晰地说明相关的量子信息协议，并展示出量子纠缠和量子隐形传输的拓扑性质。


### Abstract

Bell states and quantum teleportation play important roles in the study of quantum information and computation. But a comprehensive theoretical research on both of them remains to be performed. This work aims to investigate important algebraic properties of generalized Bell states as well as explore topological features of quantum teleportation. First, the basis theorem and basis group are introduced to explain that the extension of a generalized Bell basis by a unitary matrix is still an orthonormal basis. Then a twist operator is defined to make a connection between a generalized multiple qubit Bell state and a tensor product of two qubit Bell state. Besides them, the Temperley--Lieb algebra, the braid group relation and the Yang--Baxter equation are used to provide a topological diagrammatic description of generalized Bell states and quantum teleportation. It turns out that our approach is able to present a clear illustration of relevant quantum information protocols and exhibit a topological nature of quantum entanglement and quantum teleportation.

## 变分量子本征解器中激励的有效操作符选择和热启动策略

**Original Title:** Efficient Operator Selection and Warm-Start Strategy for Excitations in Variational Quantum Eigensolvers

**Authors:** Max Haas, Thierry N. Kaldenbach, Thomas Hammerschmidt, Daniel Barragan-Yani

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.10776v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.10776v2

**Submitted:** 2026-02-11


### 摘要

我们提出了一种利用优化器ExcitationSolve来高效准备电子基态的新颖方法[Jäger等人，Comm. Phys.（2025）]并建立了基于变分量子本征解的操作员选择方法，例如能量排序。通过结合这些工具，我们展示了一种计算高效的协议，该协议能够通过对运营商池的单次扫描从正耦合集群类比构建大约的底状态。该方法利用高效的经典预处理来选择大多数相关操作符，降低了与传统优化方法相关的计算复杂性。此外，我们表明该方法可以与单变分参数耦合交换操作符无缝集成，从而进一步减少所需的COT操作的数量。总体而言，我们根据经验观察到超越最先进方法的二次收敛加速，促进了量子化学中量子优势的实现。


### Abstract

We present a novel approach for efficient preparation of electronic ground states, leveraging the optimizer ExcitationSolve [Jäger et al., Comm. Phys. (2025)] and established variational quantum eigensolver-based operator selection methods, such as Energy Sorting. By combining these tools, we demonstrate a computationally efficient protocol that enables the construction of an approximate ground state from a unitary coupled cluster ansatz via a single sweep over the operator pool. Utilizing efficient classical pre-processing to select the majority of relevant operators, this approach reduces the computational complexity associated with traditional optimization methods. Furthermore, we show that this method can be seamlessly integrated with one-variational-parameter couple exchange operators, thereby further reducing the number of required CNOT operations. Overall, we empirically observe a quadratic convergence speedup beyond state-of-the-art methods, advancing the realization of quantum advantage in quantum chemistry.

## 准概率分解的分层抽样

**Original Title:** Stratified Sampling for Quasi-Probability Decompositions

**Authors:** Joshua W. Dai, Bálint Koczor

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11245v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.11245v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-11


### 摘要

准概率分解（QPD）已被证明在许多量子算法和协议中至关重要--用“更容易”的电路变体的集合取代“困难”的量子电路，这些变体的加权结果再现任何可观察目标。然而，这不可避免地会导致超出博恩规则散粒噪音的配置差异增加。我们开发了一个广泛的框架来解释和减少这种方差，并证明分层抽样--在理想比例分配下--会产生一个无偏估计量，其方差永远不会比天真抽样更差（只有在退化情况下才具有平等性）。   此外，我们还提供了一个经典的动态程序来实现对任意产品形式的QPD的分层。典型QPD的数值模拟，例如概率误差消除（AEC）和概率角度插值（PRI），证明了总体方差的恒定因子降低（在Oracle模型中高达$\sim 60$--$80\%$）和在悲观的单次触发机制中稳健的$\sim 10\%$节省。我们的结果可以立即应用于降低实际相关QPD的净采样成本，这些QPD通常用于短期和早期的容差算法，而不需要额外的量子资源。


### Abstract

Quasi-probability decompositions (QPDs) have proven essential in many quantum algorithms and protocols -- one replaces a ``difficult'' quantum circuit with an ensemble of ``easier'' circuit variants whose weighted outcomes reproduce any target observable. This, however, inevitably yields an increased configuration variance beyond Born-rule shot noise. We develop a broad framework for accounting for and reducing this variance and prove that stratified sampling -- under ideal proportional allocation -- results in an unbiased estimator with a variance that is never worse than naïve sampling (with equality only in degenerate cases).
  Furthermore, we provide a classical dynamic programme to enable stratification on arbitrary product-form QPDs. Numerical simulations of typical QPDs, such as Probabilistic Error Cancellation (PEC) and Probabilistic Angle Interpolation (PAI), demonstrate constant-factor reductions in overall variance (up to $\sim 60$--$80\%$ in an oracle model) and robust $\sim 10\%$ savings in the pessimistic single-shot regime. Our results can be applied immediately to reduce the net sampling cost of practically relevant QPDs that are commonly used in near term and early fault-tolerant algorithms without requiring additional quantum resources.

## 不可学习的物质阶段

**Original Title:** Unlearnable phases of matter

**Authors:** Tarun Advaith Kumar, Yijian Zou, Amir-Reza Negari, Roger G. Melko, Timothy H. Hsieh

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11262v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.11262v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-11


### 摘要

我们通过证明物质的非平凡混合状态阶段在计算上很难学习来识别机器学习的根本局限性。我们专注于分布的无监督学习，表明自回归神经网络无法学习以局部不可区分（LI）状态为特征的分布的全局属性。我们证明了条件互信息（RCM）是LI的有用诊断：我们证明，对于经典分布，状态的远程RCM意味着空间LI伙伴。通过引入一个受限制的统计查询模型，我们证明了具有长范围CMA的非平凡阶段，例如强到弱自发对称性破缺阶段，很难学习。我们通过使用循环、卷积和Transformer神经网络来学习位翻转噪音下复曲面/表面代码的综合症和物理分布来验证我们的主张。我们的研究结果表明，学习的难度作为检测混合状态阶段、转变和错误纠正阈值的诊断工具，并且他们建议将RCM和更一般的“非本地Gibbsness”作为衡量分布学习难度的指标。


### Abstract

We identify fundamental limitations in machine learning by demonstrating that non-trivial mixed-state phases of matter are computationally hard to learn. Focusing on unsupervised learning of distributions, we show that autoregressive neural networks fail to learn global properties of distributions characterized by locally indistinguishable (LI) states. We demonstrate that conditional mutual information (CMI) is a useful diagnostic for LI: we show that for classical distributions, long-range CMI of a state implies a spatially LI partner. By introducing a restricted statistical query model, we prove that nontrivial phases with long-range CMI, such as strong-to-weak spontaneous symmetry breaking phases, are hard to learn. We validate our claims by using recurrent, convolutional, and Transformer neural networks to learn the syndrome and physical distributions of toric/surface code under bit flip noise. Our findings suggest hardness of learning as a diagnostic tool for detecting mixed-state phases and transitions and error-correction thresholds, and they suggest CMI and more generally ``non-local Gibbsness'' as metrics for how hard a distribution is to learn.

## 具有任意子的量子计算是可容忍的

**Original Title:** Quantum computing with anyons is fault tolerant

**Authors:** Anasuya Lyons, Benjamin J. Brown

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11258v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.11258v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-11


### 摘要

在开创性的工作（arxiv：quant-ph/9707021）中，阿列克谢·基塔耶夫提出了拓扑量子计算（arXiv：cond-mat/0010440，arxiv：quant-ph/9707021，arXiv：quant-ph/0001108，arXiv：0707.1889），量子计算机的逻辑门是通过在二维平面上创建、编织和融合任意子粒子来进行的。此外，他还表明，当任何子被创建为零温度下准备的Top-order格子模型的准粒子激发时，该提案本质上对局部扰动（arXiv：cond-mat/0010440，arxiv：Quant-ph/9707021，arXiv：1001.0344，arXiv：1001.4363）具有鲁棒性。在这一提议之后的几十年里，在构建耐故障量子计算机方面取得了相当大的技术发展。现代方法不是在零温度下保持一些目标接地状态，而是主动纠正目标状态经历的错误，其中我们使用有噪的量子电路元件来识别并随后纠正与理想状态的偏差。我们提出了一种错误纠正方案，使我们能够通过编织任意子来执行稳健的普适量子计算。我们表明，假设电路元件低于局部噪音的某个阈值水平，我们的方案可以在适当大的设备上执行，故障率任意小。因此，我们开发的错误纠正方案使我们能够使用目前正在开发的现代量子硬件来执行耐故障的拓扑量子计算。


### Abstract

In seminal work (arxiv:quant-ph/9707021) Alexei Kitaev proposed topological quantum computing (arXiv:cond-mat/0010440, arxiv:quant-ph/9707021, arXiv:quant-ph/0001108, arXiv:0707.1889), whereby logic gates of a quantum computer are conducted by creating, braiding and fusing anyonic particles on a two-dimensional plane. Furthermore, he showed the proposal is inherently robust to local perturbations (arXiv:cond-mat/0010440, arxiv:quant-ph/9707021, arXiv:1001.0344, arXiv:1001.4363) when anyons are created as quasiparticle excitations of a topologically ordered lattice model prepared at zero temperature. Over the decades following this proposal there have been considerable technological developments towards the construction of a fault-tolerant quantum computer. Rather than maintaining some target ground state at zero temperature, a modern approach is to actively correct the errors a target state experiences, where we use noisy quantum circuit elements to identify and subsequently correct for deviations from the ideal state. We present an error-correction scheme that enables us to carry out robust universal quantum computation by braiding anyons. We show that our scheme can be carried out on a suitably large device with an arbitrarily small failure rate assuming circuit elements are below some threshold level of local noise. The error-corrected scheme we have developed therefore enables us to carry out fault-tolerant topological quantum computation using modern quantum hardware that is now under development.

## 量子计算机上预博恩-奥本海默动力学的有效模拟

**Original Title:** Efficient Simulation of Pre-Born-Oppenheimer Dynamics on a Quantum Computer

**Authors:** Matthew Pocrnic, Ignacio Loaiza, Juan Miguel Arrazola, Nathan Wiebe, Danial Motlagh

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11272v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.11272v1

**Submitted:** 2026-02-11


### 摘要

在这项工作中，我们提出了一种量子算法，用于在第一量子化的真实空间网格上直接模拟电子-核动力学。我们的算法通过利用交换网络的线性缩放来实现粒子相互作用的二次数，同时使用利用高度优化的算术例程的库伦相互作用的新型交替符号实现，对前Born-Oppenheimer分子Hamilton进行块编码，实现了同类最佳效率。我们对一系列科学和工业相关化学反应的方法进行基准测试。我们证明，与之前最先进的NH_3+ BF_3 $反应相比，成本大幅降低，使用1362美元的逻辑量子位（系统+辅助）实现了每毫微微秒8.7美元的Toffoli成本。我们的结果显着降低了光化学反应的故障模拟所需的资源，同时提供了一套算法基元，预计将作为更广泛类别量子算法的基础构建模块。


### Abstract

In this work, we present a quantum algorithm for direct first-principles simulation of electron-nuclear dynamics on a first-quantized real-space grid. Our algorithm achieves best-in-class efficiency for block-encoding the pre-Born-Oppenheimer molecular Hamiltonian by harnessing the linear scaling of swap networks for implementing the quadratic number of particle interactions, while using a novel alternating sign implementation of the Coulomb interaction that exploits highly optimized arithmetic routines. We benchmark our approach for a series of scientifically and industrially relevant chemical reactions. We demonstrate over an order-of-magnitude reduction in costs compared to previous state-of-the-art for the $\rm NH_3+BF_3$ reaction, achieving a Toffoli cost of $8.7\times10^{9}$ per femtosecond using $1362$ logical qubits (system + ancillas). Our results significantly lower the resources required for fault-tolerant simulations of photochemical reactions, while providing a suite of algorithmic primitives that are expected to serve as foundational building blocks for a broader class of quantum algorithms.

## 通过过滤器设计实现量子噪音光谱的超分辨率

**Original Title:** Superresolution in Quantum Noise Spectroscopy via Filter Design

**Authors:** Joseph T. Iosue, Paraj Titum, Taohan Lin, Clare Lau, Leigh M. Norris

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11275v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.11275v1

### 摘要

### Abstract
