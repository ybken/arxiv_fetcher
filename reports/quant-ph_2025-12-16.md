## 通过自适应时间表改善隔热状态准备中的间隙依赖性

**Original Title:** Improved gap dependence in adiabatic state preparation by adaptive schedule

**Authors:** Xi Guo, Dong An

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10329v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.10329v2

**Submitted:** 2025-12-11


### 摘要

隔热量子计算是状态准备的强大框架，而其进化时间通常在逆Hamilton谱间隙中进行二次扩展，导致计算复杂性次优。在这项工作中，我们引入了一种非线性自适应策略来寻找时间调度函数，并表明在温和的间隙测量条件下，对于广泛的系统，间隙依赖性可以二次改进为逆线性。通过变分分析，我们进一步证明了我们的调度对于具有线性间隙的系统的最优性和对于一般系统的部分最优性，同时我们也严格证明了常用的线性调度从来都不是最优的。


### Abstract

Adiabatic quantum computing is a powerful framework for state preparation, while its evolution time often scales quadratically in the inverse Hamiltonian spectral gap, leading to sub-optimal computational complexity. In this work, we introduce a nonlinear adaptive strategy for finding the time scheduling function, and show that the gap dependence can be quadratically improved to be inverse linear for a wide range of systems under a mild gap measure condition. Through variational analysis, we further demonstrate the optimality of our schedule for systems with linear gap and the partial optimality for general systems, while we also rigorously show that the commonly used linear schedule is never optimal.

## C$#*$-代数中的纠缠：状态空间的张量积

**Original Title:** Entanglement in C$^*$-algebras: tensor products of state spaces

**Authors:** Magdalena Musat, Mikael Rørdam

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10410v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.10410v2

**Submitted:** 2025-12-11


### 摘要

我们分析了作为C$^*$-代数的状态空间产生的紧凸集的Namioka-Phelps最小和最大张量积，并相关地研究了（无限维）C$^*$-代数中的纠缠。两个C$#*$-代数的状态空间的最小Namioka-Phelps张量积被证明对应于C$#*$-代数张量积上的可分离（=非纠缠）状态集。我们表明，当两个C$#*$-代数之一是交换的时，状态空间的这些最大张量积和最小张量积精确一致。这证实了巴克的一个旧猜想，即紧凸集是C$#*$-代数的状态空间。   两个C$#*$-代数的迹单形的Namioka-Phelps张量积始终是C$#*$-代数的张量积的迹单形。例如，这可以用来表明C$#*$-代数的（任何）张量积的迹单形是Poulsen单形当且仅当每个C$#*$-代数的迹单形是Poulsen单形或平凡（而不是全部平凡）。


### Abstract

We analyze the Namioka-Phelps minimal and maximal tensor products of compact convex sets arising as state spaces of C$^*$-algebras, and, relatedly, study entanglement in (infinite dimensional) C$^*$-algebras. The minimal Namioka-Phelps tensor product of the state spaces of two C$^*$-algebras is shown to correspond to the set of separable (= un-entangled) states on the tensor product of the C$^*$-algebras. We show that these maximal and minimal tensor product of the state spaces agree precisely when one of the two C$^*$-algebras is commutative. This confirms an old conjecture by Barker in the case where the compact convex sets are state spaces of C$^*$-algebras.
  The Namioka-Phelps tensor product of the trace simplexes of two C$^*$-algebras is shown always to be the trace simplex of the tensor product of the C$^*$-algebras. This can be used, for example, to show that the trace simplex of (any) tensor product of C$^*$-algebras is the Poulsen simplex if and only if the trace simplex of each of the C$^*$-algebras is the Poulsen simplex or trivial (and not all trivial).

## 噪音量子系统的纠缠进化：主方程-TFD解决方案

**Original Title:** Entanglement Evolution of Noisy Quantum Systems: Master Equation-TFD Solutions

**Authors:** Urjjarani Patel, KVS Shiv Chaitanya

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.11932v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.11932v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-12


### 摘要

本文应用热场动力学（TFD）将量子光学非线性主方程映射为任意初始条件下的类薛定谔方程。这种形式为解决开放量子系统问题提供了一种更有效的方法。然后，我们使用Hartree-Fock近似解析地求解两个单独的噪声量子系统的主方程，这使得我们能够使用协方差矩阵的本征值来分析每种情况下的纠缠和量子互信息，然后是双模和单模压缩态。


### Abstract

In this paper, Thermofield Dynamics (TFD) is applied to map a quantum optics nonlinear master equation into a Schrodinger-like equation for any arbitrary initial condition. This formalism provides a more efficient way for solving open quantum system problems. Then we use the Hartree-Fock approximation to solve the master equations of two separate noisy quantum systems analytically, which allows us to analyze the entanglement and quantum mutual information in each case using the eigenvalues of a covariance matrix, followed by two-mode and single-mode squeezed states.

## 置换矩阵的量子电路

**Original Title:** Quantum circuits for permutation matrices

**Authors:** Jason Hanson

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.11938v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.11938v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-12


### 摘要

提出了两种不同的算法，用于生成代表$2 ' n $字母上排列的矩阵的量子电路实现。所有电路都涉及$n$量子位，并且仅使用多控制Toffoli门。第一种算法从排列的任何分解到转置的积来构造一个回路，但使用一条辅助线。第二种方法不使用辅助，从分解到海明距离为1的转置产物来构建电路。我们表明任何排列都允许这样的分解，并给出了减少所涉及的转置数量的策略。


### Abstract

Two different algorithms are presented for generating a quantum circuit realization of a matrix representing a permutation on $2^n$ letters. All circuits involve $n$ qubits and only use multi--controlled Toffoli gates. The first algorithm constructs a circuit from any decomposition of the permutation into a product of transpositions, but uses one ancilla line. The second, which uses no ancillae, constructs a circuit from a decomposition into a product of transpositions that have a Hamming distance of one. We show that any permutation admits such a decomposition, and we give a strategy for reducing the number of transpositions involved.

## 多体定位中的一致同步振荡

**Original Title:** Coherently synchronized oscillations in many-body localization

**Authors:** Zi-Jian Li, Yi-Ting Tu, Sankar Das Sarma

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.11953v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.11953v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-12


### 摘要

我们在镜像对称多体定域系统中发现了一种意想不到的相干同步振荡现象。通过改变旋转-旋转相互作用，发现了旋转振荡的同步转变。为了理解这一现象，提出了一种有效的基于运动局部积分的伊辛模型。我们发现同步转变可以理解为顺磁性到铁磁性伊辛转变。基于伊辛模型，我们从理论上估计了同步频率和同步转变点，与数值结果吻合良好。


### Abstract

We find an unexpected phenomenon of coherently synchronized oscillations in a mirror-symmetric many-body localized system. A synchronization transition of the spin oscillations is found by changing the spin-spin interactions. To understand this phenomenon, an effective Ising model based on local integrals of motion is proposed. We find that the synchronization transition can be understood as a paramagnetic-to-ferromagnetic Ising transition. Based on the Ising model, we theoretically estimate the synchronized frequencies and the synchronization transition points, which agree well with numerical results.

## 一维多体量子态的等距张量网络全息表示

**Original Title:** Holographic Representation of One-Dimensional Many-Body Quantum States via Isometric Tensor Networks

**Authors:** Kaito Kobayashi, Benjamin Sappler, Frank Pollmann

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.11967v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.11967v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-12


### 摘要

等距张量网络状态（isoTNS）通过实施等距结构，可以高效、准确地模拟更高维度的量子系统。我们通过引入全息isoTNS类比将这个想法带回到一维：等距张量的（1+1）维网格，其中水平轴编码物理空间，辅助“全息”轴增强了表现力。尽管扩大了几何形状，但由于等距约束，收缩和局部更新仍然具有计算效率。我们调查了这个类比，并将其与矩阵产品状态（MPS）进行比较进行基准测试。首先，我们表明，随机初始化的全息isoTNS即使在中等键维度下也通常显示出体积定律纠缠，超过了MPS和相关反相的代表性限制。其次，通过分析构造和变分优化，我们证明全息isoTNS可以忠实地表示任意费米子高斯态、克利福德态和局部进化下的某些短期进化状态--一个高度纠缠但复杂性较低的状态族。第三，为了在广泛的情况下利用这种表现力，我们在全息isoTNS上实现了时间演变的块抽取（TEBD）算法。虽然该方法保持高效和可扩展性，但TEBD扫描上的误差累积会抑制纠缠并导致与精确动态的快速偏离。总体而言，全息isoTNS拓宽了张量网络方法的范围，为研究体积定律范围内的物理学开辟了新途径。


### Abstract

Isometric tensor network states (isoTNS) allow for efficient and accurate simulations of higher-dimensional quantum systems by enforcing an isometric structure. We bring this idea back to one dimension by introducing a holographic isoTNS ansatz: a (1+1)-dimensional lattice of isometric tensors where the horizontal axis encodes physical space and an auxiliary "holographic" axis boosts expressivity. Despite the enlarged geometry, contractions and local updates remain computationally efficient due to isometric constraints. We investigate this ansatz and benchmark it in comparison to matrix product states (MPS). First, we show that randomly initialized holographic isoTNS typically display volume-law entanglement even at modest bond dimension, surpassing the representational limits of MPS and related ansätze. Second, through analytic constructions and variational optimization, we demonstrate that holographic isoTNS can faithfully represent arbitrary fermionic Gaussian states, Clifford states, and certain short-time-evolved states under local evolution -- a family of states that is highly entangled but low in complexity. Third, to exploit this expressivity in broad situations, we implement a time-evolving block decimation (TEBD) algorithm on holographic isoTNS. While the method remains efficient and scalable, error accumulation over TEBD sweeps suppresses entanglement and leads to rapid deviations from exact dynamics. Overall, holographic isoTNS broaden the reach of tensor-network methods, opening new avenues to study physics in the volume-law regime.

## 具有边界的均匀矩阵积状态

**Original Title:** Uniform matrix product states with a boundary

**Authors:** Marta Florido-Llinàs, Álvaro M. Alhambra, David Pérez-García, J. Ignacio Cirac

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.11968v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.11968v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-12


### 摘要

典型形式是张量网络状态分析理解的核心，支撑着关键结果，例如矩阵积状态（MPS）框架内一维对称保护的拓扑相的完整分类。然而，所建立的理论仅适用于具有周期性边界条件的均匀MPS，从而使许多物理相关状态超出了其范围。在这里，我们引入了具有边界矩阵的均匀MPS的广义规范形式，从而将分析MPS框架扩展到具有更广泛物理意义的更一般设置。这种规范形式揭示了任何此类MPS都可以被表示为作用于代数正规语言状态的结构化类的块可逆矩阵积运算符，这些状态捕获了其基本的长期和规模不变特征。我们的构造建立在独立感兴趣的新代数结果之上，这些结果描述了由非半单矩阵集生成的跨度和代数，包括广义量子Wielandt不等式，该不等式给出了固定长度的块长度的明确上限。长度跨度稳定到代数。这些结果共同为具有边界的统一MPS奠定了统一的理论基础，弥合了周期性和任意边界设置之间的差距，并为将矩阵积状态的关键分析和分类结果扩展到更广泛的状态和操作员类别提供了基础。


### Abstract

Canonical forms are central to the analytical understanding of tensor network states, underpinning key results such as the complete classification of one-dimensional symmetry-protected topological phases within the matrix product state (MPS) framework. Yet, the established theory applies only to uniform MPS with periodic boundary conditions, leaving many physically relevant states beyond its reach. Here we introduce a generalized canonical form for uniform MPS with a boundary matrix, thus extending the analytical MPS framework to a more general setting of wider physical significance. This canonical form reveals that any such MPS can be represented as a block-invertible matrix product operator acting on a structured class of algebraic regular language states that capture its essential long-range and scale-invariant features. Our construction builds on new algebraic results of independent interest that characterize the span and algebra generated by non-semisimple sets of matrices, including a generalized quantum Wielandt's inequality that gives an explicit upper bound on the blocking length at which the fixed-length span stabilizes to an algebra. Together, these results establish a unified theoretical foundation for uniform MPS with boundaries, bridging the gap between periodic and arbitrary-boundary settings, and providing the basis for extending key analytical and classification results of matrix product states to a much broader class of states and operators.

## 弱相互作用费米子累积量展开和多项时间算法的收敛性

**Original Title:** Convergence of the Cumulant Expansion and Polynomial-Time Algorithm for Weakly Interacting Fermions

**Authors:** Hongrui Chen, Cambyse Rouzé, Jielun Chen, Jiaqing Jiang, Samuel O. Scalet, Yongtao Zhan, Garnet Kin-Lic Chan, Lexing Ying, Yu Tong

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.12010v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.12010v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-12


### 摘要

我们提出了一种随机算法来计算弱相互作用费米子的log分配函数，具有多项运行时间，无论是系统规模还是精度。尽管弱相互作用费米子系统对于许多计算方法（例如图形量子蒙特卡罗）来说被认为是易于处理的，但一直缺乏对多项运行时间的数学严格证明。在这项工作中，我们首先扩展了之前工作中开发的证明技术，用于证明周期系统中累积量展开的收敛性到非周期情况。用于分析连通费曼图之和的关键方程（我们称之为树决定性展开）揭示了总和中的潜在树结构。这使我们能够设计一种新的随机算法，通过信念传播增强的重要性抽样来计算日志划分函数。这种方法与基于马尔科夫链蒙特卡罗的传统方法不同，其效率难以保证，使我们能够获得具有可证明的多项运行时间的算法。


### Abstract

We propose a randomized algorithm to compute the log-partition function of weakly interacting fermions with polynomial runtime in both the system size and precision. Although weakly interacting fermionic systems are considered tractable for many computational methods such as the diagrammatic quantum Monte Carlo, a mathematically rigorous proof of polynomial runtime has been lacking. In this work we first extend the proof techniques developed in previous works for proving the convergence of the cumulant expansion in periodic systems to the non-periodic case. A key equation used to analyze the sum of connected Feynman diagrams, which we call the tree-determinant expansion, reveals an underlying tree structure in the summation. This enables us to design a new randomized algorithm to compute the log-partition function through importance sampling augmented by belief propagation. This approach differs from the traditional method based on Markov chain Monte Carlo, whose efficiency is hard to guarantee, and enables us to obtain a algorithm with provable polynomial runtime.

## 腔介导双量子位门：通过NISQ时代量子模拟调整至最佳性能

**Original Title:** Cavity Mediated Two-Qubit Gate: Tuning to Optimal Performance with NISQ Era Quantum Simulations

**Authors:** Shreekanth S. Yuvarajan, Vincent Iglesias-Cardinale, David Hucul, Herbert F. Fotso

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.12030v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.12030v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-12


### 摘要

各种光介导的操作对于实现可扩展的量子信息处理平台至关重要，它们的准确描述对于识别最佳机制及其实验实现至关重要。这种光与物质的相互作用通常通过各种分析和计算方法来研究，这些方法受到逼近技术或计算缩放的限制。量子处理器为应对这些挑战提供了一条新途径。我们考虑腔介导的两量子位门的情况。为了研究量子位之间的量子状态转移，我们使用能够可靠跟踪系统动态的量子电路实施模拟。我们的量子算法与NISQ（Noisy Scale Intermediate Quantum）时代的系统兼容，使我们能够将量子位之间状态转移操作的保真度绘制为广泛的系统参数的函数，包括量子位和腔之间的各自失调、腔的衰减因子以及量子位和腔之间的各自耦合。该算法提供了稳健且直观的解决方案，并与分析解决方案或经典模拟算法在各自的有效性范围内取得了令人满意的一致性。它使我们能够识别与异类量子平台相关的未充分探索的最佳性能机制，其中两量子位门在既不相互共振也不与腔共振的远失调量子位之间相当有效。除了其当前的应用之外，本文中介绍的方法还可以有效地用于模型的难以控制的变体，以及模拟和优化量子信息处理中的量子介导两量子位门和其他相关操作的各种努力。


### Abstract

A variety of photon-mediated operations are critical to the realization of scalable quantum information processing platforms and their accurate characterization is essential for the identification of optimal regimes and their experimental realizations. Such light-matter interactions are often studied with a broad variety of analytical and computational methods that are constrained by approximation techniques or by computational scaling. Quantum processors present a new avenue to address these challenges. We consider the case of cavity mediated two-qubit gates. To investigate quantum state transfer between the qubits, we implement simulations with quantum circuits that are able to reliably track the dynamics of the system. Our quantum algorithm, compatible with NISQ (Noisy Intermediate Scale Quantum) era systems, allows us to map out the fidelity of the state transfer operation between qubits as a function of a broad range of system parameters including the respective detunings between the qubits and the cavity, the damping factor of the cavity, and the respective couplings between the qubits and the cavity. The algorithm provides a robust and intuitive solution, alongside a satisfactory agreement with analytical solutions or classical simulation algorithms in their respective regimes of validity. It allows us to identify under-explored regimes of optimal performance, relevant for heterogeneous quantum platforms, where the two-qubit gate can be rather effective between far-detuned qubits that are neither resonant with each other nor with the cavity. Besides its present application, the method introduced in the current paper can be efficiently used in otherwise untractable variations of the model and in various efforts to simulate and optimize photon-mediated two-qubit gates and other relevant operations in quantum information processing.

## 量子增强生物传感能够早期检测细菌生长

**Original Title:** Quantum-enhanced biosensing enables earlier detection of bacterial growth

**Authors:** Rayssa B. de Andrade, Anne Egholm Høgh, Gaetana Spedalieri, Stefano Pirandola, Kirstine Berg-Sørensen, Tobias Gehring, Ulrik L. Andersen

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.12057v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.12057v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-12


### 摘要

快速检测细菌生长在临床、食品安全和环境环境中至关重要，但传统的光学方法受到噪音的限制，需要数小时的培养。在这里，我们首次展示了使用压缩光进行早期细菌检测的量子增强光学测量的实验演示。通过用量子探针监测大肠杆菌培养物的吸光率，我们实现了超过散粒噪音极限的灵敏度，能够比经典传感器提前30分钟识别生长开始。通过具有截断高斯分布的统计建模和假设测试来验证降噪效果，以低误报率确认早期检测。这项工作说明了量子资源如何改进实时、非侵入性诊断。我们的结果为量子增强生物传感器铺平了道路，该传感器可以加速检测微生物生长和其他生物过程，而不会增加光损伤。


### Abstract

Rapid detection of bacterial growth is crucial in clinical, food safety, and environmental contexts, yet conventional optical methods are limited by noise and require hours of incubation. Here, we present the first experimental demonstration of a quantum-enhanced photometric measurement for early bacterial detection using squeezed light. By monitoring the optical absorbance of an Escherichia coli culture with a quantum probe, we achieve a sensitivity beyond the shot-noise limit, enabling identification of growth onset up to 30 minutes earlier than with a classical sensor. The noise reduction is validated through statistical modeling with a truncated Gaussian distribution and hypothesis testing, confirming earlier detection with low false-alarm rates. This work illustrates how quantum resources can improve real-time, non-invasive diagnostics. Our results pave the way for quantum-enhanced biosensors that accelerate detection of microbial growth and other biological processes without increasing photodamage.

## TreeVQA：一个用于变分量子算法中镜头缩减的树结构执行框架

**Original Title:** TreeVQA: A Tree-Structured Execution Framework for Shot Reduction in Variational Quantum Algorithms

**Authors:** Yuewen Hou, Dhanvi Bharadwaj, Gokul Subramanian Ravi

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.12068v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.12068v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-12


### 摘要

变分量子算法（VQA）在近期和中期量子计算中很有前途，但它们的执行成本很高。每个任务都需要多次迭代和每次迭代大量电路，而现实世界的应用程序通常涉及多个任务，并按照探索应用程序能源格局所需的精确度进行扩展。这需要大量的处决镜头，使得实际使用成本过高。我们观察到，通过利用应用程序任务的执行相似性可以显着降低VQA成本。基于这一见解，我们提出了TreeVQA，这是一个基于树的执行框架，它从联合执行任务开始，并仅在其量子执行出现分歧时逐步分支。TreeVQA作为VQA包装器实现，与典型的VQA应用程序集成。对科学和组合基准的评估显示，在相同目标准确性下，射击次数平均减少25.9美元，对于大规模问题，射击次数减少超过100美元。随着问题规模和精度要求的增加，好处进一步增加。


### Abstract

Variational Quantum Algorithms (VQAs) are promising for near- and intermediate-term quantum computing, but their execution cost is substantial. Each task requires many iterations and numerous circuits per iteration, and real-world applications often involve multiple tasks, scaling with the precision needed to explore the application's energy landscape. This demands an enormous number of execution shots, making practical use prohibitively expensive. We observe that VQA costs can be significantly reduced by exploiting execution similarities across an application's tasks. Based on this insight, we propose TreeVQA, a tree-based execution framework that begins by executing tasks jointly and progressively branches only as their quantum executions diverge. Implemented as a VQA wrapper, TreeVQA integrates with typical VQA applications. Evaluations on scientific and combinatorial benchmarks show shot count reductions of $25.9\times$ on average and over $100\times$ for large-scale problems at the same target accuracy. The benefits grow further with increasing problem size and precision requirements.

## 全同粒子量子力学中自旋统计定理的证明

**Original Title:** Proof of Spin-Statistics Theorem in Quantum Mechanics of Identical Particles

**Authors:** Takafumi Kita

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.12071v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.12071v1
