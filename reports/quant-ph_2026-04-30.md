## 超时空序列决策问题的一致推出Oracle

**Original Title:** Coherent Rollout Oracles for Finite-Horizon Sequential Decision Problems

**Authors:** Nishant Shukla

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25962v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.25962v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-28


### 摘要

序列决策问题的一致量子推出需要一个单一模拟器：随机性必须存在于显式量子寄存器中，并且基本状态选择器必须可逆地映射到动作。对于依赖于分支的有效动作，该映射是在纠缠的$N$位有效性屏蔽上进行的总体一致排序选择：返回$r$第一个有效位的位置，或者如果$r$超出范围，则返回哨兵。   我们对这个基元进行了第一次可逆电路复杂性分析。对于选择器宽度$w = \lceil\log_2（N+1）\rceil$，rank-select允许$O（Nw）$-gate低辅助有界跨度扫描，在其模型中被证明是门最优的，并且当远程门可用时，允许$O（N\log w）$-gate低辅助阻塞结构;在所有有界扇入布局中，无条件门下限为$Q（N）$。将排名选择与可逆转移和预测评估电路组成，为满足这些原始假设的有限视野规划问题提供了一个显式的多项一致展开预言。   由此产生的Oracle满足Wang等人的最佳臂管道的访问模型，产生$\widetilde{O}（\SQRT{k}/\varepsimmon）$coherencial Oracle针对标准经典$«（k/\varepsimmon ' 2）$手臂拉力下限进行调用。我们给出了一个有界影响提升定理，将这个下界结构从基本配置扩展到指数配置族。我们通过随机安置游戏健全性检查来实例化Sir流行病干预的构建，并对Lean 4中的主要结果进行机器检查。代码和证明：https://github.com/BinRoot/b01t/tree/main/demos/rollout。


### Abstract

Coherent quantum rollout for sequential decision problems requires a unitary simulator: randomness must live in explicit quantum registers, and basis-state selectors must be mapped to actions reversibly. With branch-dependent valid actions, this mapping is totalized coherent rank-select over an entangled $N$-bit validity mask: return the position of the $r$-th valid bit, or a sentinel if $r$ is out of range.
  We give the first reversible-circuit complexity analysis of this primitive. For selector width $w = \lceil \log_2(N+1) \rceil$, rank-select admits an $O(Nw)$-gate low-ancilla bounded-span scan, proved gate-optimal in its model, and an $O(N\log w)$-gate low-ancilla blocked construction when long-range gates are available; across all bounded-fan-in layouts, the unconditional gate lower bound is $Ω(N)$. Composing rank-select with reversible transition and predicate-evaluation circuits gives an explicit polynomial-size coherent rollout oracle for finite-horizon planning problems satisfying these primitive assumptions.
  The resulting oracle satisfies the access model of the best-arm pipeline of Wang et al., yielding $\widetilde{O}(\sqrt{k}/\varepsilon)$ coherent oracle calls against the standard classical $Ω(k/\varepsilon^2)$ arm-pull lower bound. We give a bounded-influence lifting theorem that extends this lower-bound construction from a base configuration to an exponential family of configurations. We instantiate the construction on SIR epidemic intervention, with a stochastic placement-game sanity check, and machine-check the main results in Lean 4. Code and proofs: https://github.com/BinRoot/b01t/tree/main/demos/rollout.

## 制作约瑟夫森结的简单方法

**Original Title:** A simple method to fabricate Josephson junctions

**Authors:** Imran Mahboob, Satoshi Sasaki, Takaaki Takenaka

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25195v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.25195v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-28


### 摘要

本文介绍了一种在金属化和氧化之前，用光刻和氩腐蚀制作Al/AlOx $/Al约瑟夫森结（JJs）的最小方法。可以制造出面积为1 ~ 6 μ m^2 $的JJ，在适当的氧化条件下，结电阻可以变化2个数量级。透射电子显微镜揭示了成功制造的JJ与几个晶界表明减少能量损失从两个级别的系统。与电子束印刷术相比，用这种方法制造的超导量子干涉器件（SQUID）在多个芯片上表现出更小的电阻变化，并且这些器件可以维持10 mK的重复热循环，而出色的通量响应保持不变。该技术的量子应用通过将SQUID共振器嵌入3D腔中并参数化放大低量子数并获得40分贝的收益来演示。这项工作建立了迄今为止制造JJ的最简单方法，并且可能证明它对于广泛利用基于超导电路的量子技术至关重要。


### Abstract

A minimal method to fabricate Al/AlO$_x$/Al Josephson junctions (JJs) using photolithography and argon etching, before metallization and oxidation, is demonstrated. JJs with areas ranging from 1 to 6 $μ$m$^2$ can be fabricated and, with the appropriate oxidation conditions, the junction resistance can be varied by $\sim$2 orders of magnitude. Transmission electron microscopy reveals the successful fabrication of JJs with few grain boundaries suggesting reduced energy loss from two-level-systems. Superconducting QUantum Interference Devices (SQUIDs) fabricated from this methodology exhibit reduced resistance variation over multiple chips, compared with electron beam lithography, and the devices can sustain repeated thermal cycles to 10 mK with the excellent flux response remaining unchanged. The quantum applications of this technology are demonstrated by embedding a SQUID resonator into a 3D cavity and parametrically amplifying low photon numbers with gains of $\sim$40 dB. This work establishes the simplest approach to fabricating JJs to date, and it could prove pivotal to the widespread utilization of superconducting circuit-based quantum technologies.

## 半发射机设备独立量子密钥分发

**Original Title:** Semi-transmitter-device-independent quantum key distribution

**Authors:** Qiang Zeng, Abhishek Mishra, Haoyang Wang, Zhiliang Yuan

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25225v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.25225v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-28


### 摘要

与测量设备相关性相比，发射机设备相关性是量子密钥分发（QKD）中一个长期存在但往往隐含的问题。单边设备无关（1 sDI）场景放宽了DI框架的安全条件，并为发射机侧依赖性提供了直观的解决方案。在这里，我们表明，通过以1 sDI配置构建可组合发射器，可以在很大程度上消除发射器与设备的依赖性。我们的方案将纠缠源作为一个独立部分集成到发射机中，而检测模块是另一个被视为黑匣子的独立部分。在原理验证实验中，我们在20~km的等效传输光纤距离下获得了1~ kMbps的安全密钥速率，展示了第一个离散变量1 sDI-QKD。通过实现半发射机设备独立的安全性，同时保持强大的丢失容忍度，我们的方法为现实世界的1 sDI-QKD部署建立了安全性和实用性的桥梁。


### Abstract

Transmitter-device-dependence is a longstanding but often implicit problem in quantum key distribution (QKD), as compared to measurement-device-dependence. One-sided device-independent (1sDI) scenario relaxes the security conditions of DI framework and offers an intuitive solution to transmitter-sided dependence. Here we show that, by constructing a composable transmitter in a 1sDI configuration, the transmitter-device-dependence can be largely eliminated. Our scheme integrates the entanglement source into the transmitter as an individual part, and the detection module is another individual part treated as black box. In a proof-of-principle experiment we obtain a secure key-rate of 1~kbps at an equivalent transmission fiber distance of 20~km, demonstrating the first discrete-variable 1sDI-QKD. By implementing semi-transmitter-device-independent security while maintaining strong loss tolerance, our approach bridges security and practicality for real-world 1sDI-QKD deployments.

## 指数量子门普适性的李代数判据

**Original Title:** A Lie-algebraic Criterion for the Universality of Exponentiated Quantum Gates

**Authors:** Yinuo Xue, Qian Chen, Jing-Song Huang

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25971v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.25971v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-28


### 摘要

我们提出了一个标准，作为一个多项式时间算法的基础，以决定是否由一些哈密顿指数的qudit门的有限集是普遍的。我们的方法制定的普遍性在李代数方面，并适用于Borel-德Siebenthal理论与对角发电机具有无公度谱。在这个框架中，非普适性检测不变的子空间，相当于一个图形连接障碍，而普遍性是通过添加发电机，耦合断开组件修复。我们进一步证明了两个发电机是足够的通用控制。我们的工作揭示了李代数表示的普遍性和不可约性之间的深刻联系。


### Abstract

We present a criterion that serves as the basis for a polynomial-time algorithm to decide whether a finite set of qudit gates exponentiated by some Hamiltonians is universal. Our approach formulates universality in Lie algebraic terms and applies Borel--de Siebenthal theory with a diagonal generator having incommensurate spectrum. In this framework, nonuniversality is detected by invariant subspaces, equivalently by a graph-connectivity obstruction, while universality is repaired by adding generators that couple disconnected components. We further prove that two generators are sufficient for universal control. Our work reveals a profound link between qudit universality and irreducibility of Lie algebra representations.

## 不留瓷砖：表面代码架构的多路编程

**Original Title:** No Tile Left Behind: Multiprogramming for Surface-Code Architectures

**Authors:** Archisman Ghosh, Avimita Chatterjee, Swaroop Ghosh

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25976v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.25976v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-28


### 摘要

容错量子计算（FTQC）正在成为执行实际大规模量子工作负载的架构体系。然而，在这种情况下，多路编程不再是划分扁平量子位池的问题。量子错误纠正暴露了数据切片、辅助切片和魔法状态服务资源的结构化布局，因此并发执行必须考虑紧凑的放置、连接性、路由余量和共享支持基础设施。这使得FTQC多路编程从根本上比NISQ多路编程更困难：准入决策可能会分散剩余的布图规划，保守的保留可能会浪费辅助，数据、辅助和魔法状态资源之间的动态竞争可能会降低吞吐量和服务质量。在这项工作中，我们开发了FTQC多道编程的正式框架，该框架捕捉这些结构约束及其运行时含义。我们制定了基线静态分配问题，通过分层感知的调度策略将其扩展到有限资源和在线设置，并进一步将其推广到具有动态魔法状态生成的支持培育的架构。通过对合成Clifford+T工作负载的模拟，提出的调度器实现了3.1倍的标准化系统加速，比之前的FTQC多道编程基线提高了约29%，同时保持较低的平均减速。


### Abstract

Fault-tolerant quantum computing (FTQC) is emerging as the architectural regime in which practical large-scale quantum workloads will execute. In this setting, however, multiprogramming is no longer a matter of partitioning a flat pool of qubits. Quantum error correction exposes a structured floorplan of data tiles, ancilla tiles, and magic-state service resources, so concurrent execution must account for compact placement, connectivity, routing headroom, and shared support infrastructure. This makes FTQC multiprogramming fundamentally harder than its NISQ counterpart: admission decisions can fragment the remaining floorplan, conservative reservations can waste ancilla, and dynamic contention across data, ancilla, and magic-state resources can degrade both throughput and quality of service. In this work, we develop a formal framework for FTQC multiprogramming that captures these structural constraints and their runtime implications. We formulate the baseline static allocation problem, extend it to limited-resource and online settings through hierarchy-aware scheduling policies, and further generalize it to cultivation-enabled architectures with dynamic magic-state generation. Through simulation on synthetic Clifford+T workloads, the proposed scheduler achieves a normalized system speedup of 3.1x, improving over prior FTQC multiprogramming baselines by ~29% while maintaining low mean slowdown.

## 高保真纠缠门和中性原子非局部电路

## 建立电子量子电荷液体的微观模型

**Original Title:** Towards a microscopic model for an electronic quantum charge liquid

**Authors:** Jacob R. Taylor, Sankar Das Sarma, Seth Musser

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25992v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.25992v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-28


### 摘要

我们提供了一种构建电子量子电荷液体（QCL）的方法，这是一种由费米子在不破坏平移的网格的分数填充处组成的状态。从填充$v =3/2$的无纺费米子开始，我们将它们配对，以获得填充$v =3/4$每个单位胞的玻色子。正方形网格上的四聚体模型（二聚体模型的推广）在填充$v = 3/4$时被评估为候选玻色QCL。研究表明，这些模型表现出局部$\mathbb{Z}_4$对称性。通过对一类四聚体波函数的数值研究，发现虽然一个波函数由于$\mathrm{U}（1）' 3 $对称性而无间隙，但至少另一个波函数可以明确地证明是有间隙的。这种状态的间隙性质，以及它的$\mathbb{Z}_4$对称性，使我们认为它是难以捉摸的玻色QCL的一个例子，显示最小的$\mathbb{Z}_4$topical order。我们通过讨论其他网格几何结构、电子QCL和里德伯原子的可能扩展来得出结论。


### Abstract

We provide a route to constructing an electronic quantum charge liquid (QCL), a state made up of fermions at fractional filling of a lattice that does not break translation. Starting with spinless fermions at filling $ν=3/2$ we pair them to get bosons at filling $ν=3/4$ per unit cell. The tetramer model, a generalization of the dimer model, on the square lattice is evaluated as a candidate bosonic QCL at filling $ν= 3/4$. It is shown that these models exhibit a local $\mathbb{Z}_4$ symmetry. Upon numerical study of a family of tetramer wavefunctions it is found that while one is gapless due to $\mathrm{U}(1)^3$ symmetry at least one other can be definitively shown to be gapped. The gapped nature of this state, along with its $\mathbb{Z}_4$ symmetry, leads us to propose that it is an example of the elusive bosonic QCL displaying the minimal $\mathbb{Z}_4$ topological order. We conclude by discussing possible extensions to other lattice geometries, electronic QCLs, and to Rydberg atoms.

## 非单位共形场论中的格点布局缺陷

## 多拷贝机制中最易分辨的量子态

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.26927v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.26927v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-29


### 摘要

Curiously, we also show that this quantum advantage is strongly reduced when one is restricted to real quantum states.
