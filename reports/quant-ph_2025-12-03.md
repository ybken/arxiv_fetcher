## 见证COW协议中的有效纠缠

**Original Title:** Witnessing the Effective Entanglement in the COW Protocol

**Authors:** F. Rezazadeh, A. Mani, M. Khodabandeh, M. Jaberi, S. A. Madani

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.01359v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.01359v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-01


### 摘要

我们提出了一个严格的数学框架，用于验证一致单向（COW）量子密钥分发设置中的有效纠缠。特别是，我们引入了一个双参数纠缠证人家族，确定它们构成有效证人的参数范围，并证明它们在COW协议中揭示有效纠缠的能力。此外，我们还分析了之前从COW实现中获得的实验数据，并报告了有效纠缠的清晰签名。


### Abstract

We present a rigorous mathematical framework for verifying effective entanglement in a Coherent One-Way (COW) quantum key distribution setup. In particular, we introduce a two-parameter family of entanglement witnesses, identify the parameter ranges where they constitute valid witnesses, and demonstrate their ability to reveal effective entanglement in the COW protocol. Additionally, we analyze previously obtained experimental data from a COW implementation and report clear signatures of effective entanglement.

## 网络入侵检测的子波变换量子支持量建模

**Original Title:** Modeling Wavelet Transformed Quantum Support Vector for Network Intrusion Detection

**Authors:** Swati Kumari, Shiva Raj Pokhrel, Swathi Chandrasekhar, Navneet Singh, Hridoy Sankar Dutta, Adnan Anwar, Sutharshan Rajasegarar, Robin Doss

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.01365v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.01365v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-01


### 摘要

网络流量异常检测是一项关键的网络安全挑战，需要针对复杂的物联网（IoT）环境提供强大的解决方案。我们提出了一种新型的混合量子-经典框架，将增强的量子支持载体机（QSV）与量子哈尔子波包变换（QWPT）集成在一起，用于在现实的有噪的中等规模量子条件下进行卓越的异常分类。我们的方法采用幅度编码量子状态准备、多级别QWPT特征提取以及通过Shannon Entropy分析和卡方测试进行的行为分析。使用QSV对特征进行分类，该量子核通过与同时扰动随机逼近（SPSA）优化器的混合训练进行优化。在无噪音和去极化噪音条件下的评估显示了出色的性能：BoT-IoT的准确性为96.67%，在物联网-23数据集的准确性为89.67%，超过量子自动编码器方法7个百分点以上。


### Abstract

Network traffic anomaly detection is a critical cy- bersecurity challenge requiring robust solutions for complex Internet of Things (IoT) environments. We present a novel hybrid quantum-classical framework integrating an enhanced Quantum Support Vector Machine (QSVM) with the Quantum Haar Wavelet Packet Transform (QWPT) for superior anomaly classification under realistic noisy intermediate-scale Quantum conditions. Our methodology employs amplitude-encoded quan- tum state preparation, multi-level QWPT feature extraction, and behavioral analysis via Shannon Entropy profiling and Chi-square testing. Features are classified using QSVM with fidelity-based quantum kernels optimized through hybrid train- ing with simultaneous perturbation stochastic approximation (SPSA) optimizer. Evaluation under noiseless and depolarizing noise conditions demonstrates exceptional performance: 96.67% accuracy on BoT-IoT and 89.67% on IoT-23 datasets, surpassing quantum autoencoder approaches by over 7 percentage points.

## 一维超导体中的无场二极管效应：Fulde-Ferrell配对和互磁性之间的复杂相互作用

**Original Title:** Field-free diode effects in one-dimensional superconductor: a complex interplay between Fulde-Ferrell pairing and altermagnetism

**Authors:** SVS Sai Ruthvik, Tanay Nag

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.01415v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.01415v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-01


### 摘要

我们研究了在没有任何外部磁场的情况下，一维非互惠无损耗超流的出现，该超流通过超导体效应（RST）和约瑟夫森二极管效应（JDE）表现出来，其中反转对称性（IS）和时间反转对称性（TRS）可以分别通过旋转轨道耦合（SOC）和互磁性（AM）本质上打破。我们在两个模型中分别研究了Ising和Rashba SOC，其中用晶体学角组装的双成分AM可以在Fulde-Ferrell（FF）超导体中导致定性相似的间接带隙闭合和非互惠超电流。有趣的是，在没有上述SOC的情况下，超速仍然存在，并且效率的标志可以仅通过调整角度来改变。具有互补动量功能的平行旋转分量确保IS和TRS的破坏，可以在FF配对存在的情况下引发超速。在JDE背景下继续分析，我们在三种不同的设置中探索SOC和AM与p波和Fulde-Ferrell超导电性之间的相互作用。在p波超导电性的情况下，体带有助于非互惠性，而对于FF超导电性，JDE由Andreev束缚态主导。重要的是，与p波超导电性不同，即使没有AM和SOC，JDE也会由于有限动量Cooper对而继续存在。JD效率的符号可以随着p波超导电性的角度进行调整，而没有这种符号反转是FF超导电性的标志性特征。与RST类似，平行旋转分量与p波超导电性结合可以导致JDE，在不存在SOC和AM的情况下，JDE也可以仅通过FF配对来介导。


### Abstract

We investigate the emergence of nonreciprocal dissipationless supercurrents in one-dimension manifested through the superconducting diode effect (SDE) and the Josephson diode effect (JDE) in the absence of any external magnetic field, where inversion symmetry (IS) and time-reversal symmetry (TRS) can be intrinsically broken by spin-orbit coupling (SOC), and altermagnetism (AM), respectively. We investigate Ising and Rashba SOC separately in two models where two-component AM, assembled with crystallographic angle, can lead to qualitatively similar indirect band-gap closing and non-reciprocal supercurrent in a Fulde-Ferrell (FF) superconductor. Interestingly, in the absence of the above SOCs, SDE persists and the sign of efficiency can be altered by tuning the angle only. Parallel spin components with complementary momentum functions, ensuring the breaking of IS and TRS, can induce SDE in the presence of FF pairing. Continuing the analysis in the context of JDE, we explore the interplay between SOCs and AM with the p-wave and Fulde-Ferrell superconductivity in three different setups. The bulk bands contributes to the non-reciprocity in the case of p-wave superconductivity while JDE is dominated by Andreev bound states for FF superconductivity. Importantly, JDE continues to exist due to finite momentum Cooper pair even without AM and SOC unlike the p-wave superconductivity. The sign of JD efficiency can be tuned with angle for p-wave superconductivity while absence of such sign reversal is a hallmark signature of FF superconductivity. Similar to SDE, parallel spin components in conjunction with p-wave superconductivity can lead to JDE that can also be mediated by only FF pairing in the absence of SOC and AM.

## 有噪量子强化学习策略的形式化验证

**Original Title:** Formal Verification of Noisy Quantum Reinforcement Learning Policies

**Authors:** Dennis Gross

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.01502v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.01502v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-01


### 摘要

量子强化学习（QRL）旨在利用量子效应来创建顺序决策策略，这些策略比经典策略更有效地完成任务。然而，QRL政策面临来自量子测量和硬件噪音的不确定性，例如位翻转、相翻转和去极化错误，这可能会导致不安全的行为。现有的工作没有提供系统性的方法来验证经过培训的QRL政策是否满足特定噪音条件下的安全要求。   我们引入了QVerify，这是一种形式化验证方法，它应用概率模型检查来分析有和没有建模量子噪音的训练过的QRL策略。QVerify构建了政策与环境相互作用的完整模型，将量子不确定性直接纳入转移概率，然后使用Storm模型检查器检查安全属性。   跨多个QRL环境的实验表明，QVerify可以精确测量不同的噪音模型如何影响安全性，揭示性能下降和噪音可以提供帮助的情况。通过在部署前实现严格的安全验证，QVerify解决了一个关键需求：由于访问量子硬件的费用很高，因此部署前验证对于任何对安全至关重要的QRL使用都至关重要。QVerify瞄准了一个潜在的经典量子最佳点：经过训练的QRL策略，可以在量子硬件上高效执行，但对于经典概率模型检查来说仍然易于操作，尽管对于实时经典部署来说太慢了。


### Abstract

Quantum reinforcement learning (QRL) aims to use quantum effects to create sequential decision-making policies that achieve tasks more effectively than their classical counterparts. However, QRL policies face uncertainty from quantum measurements and hardware noise, such as bit-flip, phase-flip, and depolarizing errors, which can lead to unsafe behavior. Existing work offers no systematic way to verify whether trained QRL policies meet safety requirements under specific noise conditions.
  We introduce QVerifier, a formal verification method that applies probabilistic model checking to analyze trained QRL policies with and without modeled quantum noise. QVerifier builds a complete model of the policy-environment interaction, incorporates quantum uncertainty directly into the transition probabilities, and then checks safety properties using the Storm model checker.
  Experiments across multiple QRL environments show that QVerifier precisely measures how different noise models influence safety, revealing both performance degradation and cases where noise can help. By enabling rigorous safety verification before deployment, QVerifier addresses a critical need: because access to quantum hardware is expensive, pre-deployment verification is essential for any safety-critical use of QRL. QVerifier targets a potential classical-quantum sweet spot: trained QRL policies that execute efficiently on quantum hardware, yet remain tractable for classical probabilistic model checking despite being too slow for real-time classical deployment.

## 学习量子分类器的简化表示

**Original Title:** Learning Reduced Representations for Quantum Classifiers

**Authors:** Patrick Odagiu, Vasilis Belis, Lennart Schulze, Panagiotis Barkoutsos, Michele Grossi, Florentin Reiter, Günther Dissertori, Ivano Tavernelli, Sofia Vallecorsa

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.01509v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.01509v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-01


### 摘要

由大量特征指定的数据集目前不在量子机器学习算法的适用范围之外。这一僵局的一个直接解决方案是在将数据传递给量子算法之前应用降维方法。我们研究了具有67个特征的粒子物理数据集的六种传统特征提取算法和五种基于自动编码器的降维模型。然后，这些模型生成的简化表示被用来训练量子支持载体机，以解决二元分类问题：希格斯玻色子是否是在LHC的质子碰撞中产生的。我们表明，通过我们设计的方法SinkClass自动编码器，自动编码器方法学习了更好的数据低维表示，其性能比基线好40%。这里开发的方法开放了量子机器学习对更大数据集的适用性。此外，我们还提供了在此背景下有效降低维度的方法。


### Abstract

Data sets that are specified by a large number of features are currently outside the area of applicability for quantum machine learning algorithms. An immediate solution to this impasse is the application of dimensionality reduction methods before passing the data to the quantum algorithm. We investigate six conventional feature extraction algorithms and five autoencoder-based dimensionality reduction models to a particle physics data set with 67 features. The reduced representations generated by these models are then used to train a quantum support vector machine for solving a binary classification problem: whether a Higgs boson is produced in proton collisions at the LHC. We show that the autoencoder methods learn a better lower-dimensional representation of the data, with the method we design, the Sinkclass autoencoder, performing 40% better than the baseline. The methods developed here open up the applicability of quantum machine learning to a larger array of data sets. Moreover, we provide a recipe for effective dimensionality reduction in this context.

## QuantumCanvas：原子相互作用视觉学习的多模式基准

**Original Title:** QuantumCanvas: A Multimodal Benchmark for Visual Learning of Atomic Interactions

**Authors:** Can Polat, Erchin Serpedin, Mustafa Kurban, Hasan Kurban

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.01519v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.01519v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-01


### 摘要

尽管分子和材料机器学习取得了快速进展，但大多数模型仍然缺乏物理可移植性：它们适合整个分子或晶体之间的相关性，而不是学习原子对之间的量子相互作用。然而，成键、电荷重新分布、轨道杂交和电子耦合都是从这些定义多体系统中局部量子场的两体相互作用中产生的。我们引入QuantumCanvas，这是一个大规模多模式基准，将两体量子系统视为物质的基本单位。该数据集跨越2，850个元素-元素对，每个元素对都注释了18个电子、热力学和几何属性，并与从l和m分辨轨道密度、角场变换、共占据图和电荷密度投影中获得的十通道图像表示配对。这些物理基础图像编码空间、角度和静电对称性，而没有明确的坐标，为量子学习提供了可解释的视觉模式。我们对18个目标的8种架构进行了基准测试，报告使用GATv 2时能隙的平均绝对误差为0.201 eV，使用EGNN时能隙的平均绝对误差为0.265 eV，使用HOMO时能隙的平均绝对误差为0.274 eV。对于与能量相关的量，DimeNet获得了2.27 eV的总能量MAE和0.132 eV的排斥能MAE，而多峰聚变模型获得了2.15 eV的Mermin自由能MAE。在QM 9、MD 17和CrysMTM等较大数据集上进行微调时，QuantumCanvas上的预训练可以进一步提高收敛稳定性和概括性。通过将轨道物理学与基于视觉的表示学习统一起来，QuantumCanvas为通过耦合的视觉和数字模式学习可转移量子相互作用提供了原则性且可解释的基础。数据集和模型实现可在https://github.com/KurbanIntelligenceLab/QuantumCanvas上获取。


### Abstract

Despite rapid advances in molecular and materials machine learning, most models still lack physical transferability: they fit correlations across whole molecules or crystals rather than learning the quantum interactions between atomic pairs. Yet bonding, charge redistribution, orbital hybridization, and electronic coupling all emerge from these two-body interactions that define local quantum fields in many-body systems. We introduce QuantumCanvas, a large-scale multimodal benchmark that treats two-body quantum systems as foundational units of matter. The dataset spans 2,850 element-element pairs, each annotated with 18 electronic, thermodynamic, and geometric properties and paired with ten-channel image representations derived from l- and m-resolved orbital densities, angular field transforms, co-occupancy maps, and charge-density projections. These physically grounded images encode spatial, angular, and electrostatic symmetries without explicit coordinates, providing an interpretable visual modality for quantum learning. Benchmarking eight architectures across 18 targets, we report mean absolute errors of 0.201 eV on energy gap using GATv2, 0.265 eV on HOMO and 0.274 eV on LUMO using EGNN. For energy-related quantities, DimeNet attains 2.27 eV total-energy MAE and 0.132 eV repulsive-energy MAE, while a multimodal fusion model achieves a 2.15 eV Mermin free-energy MAE. Pretraining on QuantumCanvas further improves convergence stability and generalization when fine-tuned on larger datasets such as QM9, MD17, and CrysMTM. By unifying orbital physics with vision-based representation learning, QuantumCanvas provides a principled and interpretable basis for learning transferable quantum interactions through coupled visual and numerical modalities. Dataset and model implementations are available at https://github.com/KurbanIntelligenceLab/QuantumCanvas.

## 量子度量空间中的不间断点：相对于模糊框架的结构优势

**Original Title:** Fixed Points in Quantum Metric Spaces: A Structural Advantage over Fuzzy Frameworks

**Authors:** Nicola Fabiano

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.01583v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.01583v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-01


### 摘要

我们在量子度量空间框架下证明了压缩映射不动点的存在唯一性定理，其中可互换性由$L ' 2 $模定义：$d_Q（_1，_2）= \|_1-_2\|$。该结果适用于保持函数形式的连续压缩进化下的正规化实值高斯波函数。相比之下，虽然模糊度量空间承认类似的不动点定理，但它们缺乏干扰、相敏感性和拓扑保护。这种比较揭示了量子框架中更深层次的结构一致性--不仅是技术优势，而且与赫伯特空间的几何丰富性的兼容性。我们的工作将模糊逻辑的批评扩展到内在不确定性下的动态推理。


### Abstract

We prove an existence and uniqueness theorem for fixed points of contraction maps in the framework of quantum metric spaces, where distinguishability is defined by the $L^2$ norm: $d_Q(ψ_1,ψ_2) = \|ψ_1 - ψ_2\|$. The result applies to normalized real-valued Gaussian wavefunctions under continuous contractive evolution preserving the functional form. In contrast, while fuzzy metric spaces admit analogous fixed point theorems, they lack interference, phase sensitivity, and topological protection. This comparison reveals a deeper structural coherence in the quantum framework -- not merely technical superiority, but compatibility with the geometric richness of Hilbert space. Our work extends the critique of fuzzy logic into dynamical reasoning under intrinsic uncertainty.

## 开放量子系统Wigner函数的直接计算

**Original Title:** Directly computing Wigner functions for open quantum systems

**Authors:** Nick Huggett, Christian Käding, Mario Pitschmann, James Read

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.01590v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.01590v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-01


### 摘要

维格纳函数是一种众所周知的相空间分布函数，在量子力学中有许多应用。在本文中，我们考虑了一个开放量子系统，该系统由一个非相对论单粒子与一般的、可能是相对论的环境相互作用组成。对于这个系统，我们推导出从其初始值直接计算时变维格纳函数的公式。这一结果使得与时间相关的维格纳函数更适用，而不必进行求解相应运动方程所需的额外逼近。作为我们的发现的说明，我们讨论了非相对论性单纯量粒子通过汤川相互作用与包含另一种类型的纯量场的环境相互作用的例子，该环境被相对论处理。


### Abstract

The Wigner function is a well-known phase space distribution function with many applications in quantum mechanics. In this article, we consider an open quantum system consisting of a non-relativistic single particle interacting with a general, possibly relativistic environment. For this system, we derive an expression for directly computing the time-dependent Wigner function from its initial values. This result renders time-dependent Wigner functions more applicable without having to make additional approximations that would otherwise be required in order to solve the corresponding equation of motion. As an illustration of our findings, we discuss the example of a non-relativistic single scalar particle interacting via a Yukawa interaction with an environment comprising another type of scalar field that is treated relativistically.

## 量子计算在高能物理中的应用：集群、集成和生成模型

**Original Title:** Quantum computing applications in High Energy Physics: clustering, integration and generative models

**Authors:** Jorge J. Martínez de Lejarza

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.01597v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.01597v1

**Submitted:** 2025-12-01


### 摘要

这篇博士论文探讨了量子计算解决高能物理学（HEP）中计算挑战的潜力。由于标准模型（SM）没有解决关键问题，而且自希格斯玻色子发现以来也没有出现任何新物理学的迹象，因此先进的实验和计算工具变得必要。量子计算为经典方法提供了一个有希望的替代方案，这项工作研究了量子算法在HEP研究中有用的三个主要途径。首先，我们开发量子子程序，用于计算Minkowski距离并识别未排序数据中的最大值，并将它们插入到$k$-means、Affinity Propagation和$k_T$-jet集群等集群算法中。这些量子算法与经典性能相匹配，同时在具有qRAM的量子硬件上实施时提供理论优势。然后，我们介绍了一种新型量子蒙特卡罗积分器--量子傅里叶迭代幅度估计（QFIAE），它将量子神经网络与幅度估计相结合来集成多元函数。QFIAE在模拟器和真实量子计算机上执行，通过环树二元性评估费曼环路积分，并扩展到计算（部分在硬件上）微扰量子场论中NLO的物理观测量。最后，我们提出了用于建模多元分布的量子切比雪夫概率模型（QCPM），将它们应用于分裂成kaons和pion的部分子的分裂函数。这些模型展示了准确的生成和插值能力。我们还展示了变量之间的纠缠如何在训练中发挥关键作用，提高模型准确性。总体而言，这些结果表明量子算法如何已经可以在当前硬件上解决相关的HEP问题，同时为未来充分利用量子计算优势的耐故障应用铺平道路。


### Abstract

This PhD thesis explores the potential of quantum computing to address computational challenges in high-energy physics (HEP). As the Standard Model (SM) leaves key questions unanswered and no signs of new physics have emerged since the Higgs boson discovery, advanced experimental and computational tools become necessary. Quantum computing offers a promising alternative to classical methods, and this work investigates three main avenues where quantum algorithms can be useful in HEP research. First, we develop quantum subroutines for computing Minkowski distances and identifying maximum values in unsorted data, inserting them into clustering algorithms such as $k$-means, Affinity Propagation, and $k_T$-jet clustering. These quantum algorithms match classical performance while offering theoretical advantages when implemented on quantum hardware with qRAM. Then, we introduce a novel quantum Monte Carlo integrator, Quantum Fourier Iterative Amplitude Estimation (QFIAE), which combines a quantum neural network with amplitude estimation to integrate multivariate functions. QFIAE is executed on simulators and real quantum computers to evaluate Feynman loop integrals via Loop-Tree Duality, and is extended to compute (partially on hardware) a physical observable at NLO in perturbative quantum field theory. Finally, we present Quantum Chebyshev Probabilistic Models (QCPMs) for modeling multivariate distributions, applying them to fragmentation functions of partons fragmenting into kaons and pions. These models demonstrate accurate generative and interpolation capabilities. We also show how entanglement between variables plays a key role in training, enhancing model accuracy. Overall, these results show how quantum algorithms can already tackle relevant HEP problems on current hardware, while paving the way for future fault-tolerant applications that fully exploit quantum computational advantages.

## 小分子VQE的资源估计：费米子映射和汉密尔顿约化的影响

**Original Title:** Resource Estimation for VQE on Small Molecules: Impact of Fermion Mappings and Hamiltonian Reductions

**Authors:** Anurag K. S. V., Ashish Kumar Patra, Vikas Dattatraya Ghevade, Sai Shankar P., Ruchika Bhat, Raghavendra V., Rahul Maitra, Jaiganesh G

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2512.01605v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.01605v1
