## 模拟湍流边界层中拉格朗日示踪剂弥散的量子增强马尔可夫链蒙特卡罗抽样

**Original Title:** Quantum-enhanced Markov chain Monte Carlo sampling to model Lagrangian tracer dispersion in turbulent boundary layer

**Authors:** Fabian Schindler, Jörg Schumacher

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.15985v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.15985v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-14


### 摘要

我们提出了一种量子增强马尔科夫链蒙特卡罗（QE-MCMC）方法，从取决于所有三个分量和高度的联合目标分布中采样湍流加速度，以模拟无质量拉格朗日追踪粒子在两种湍流剪切流中的传输和扩散。以均匀剪切率S为特征的均匀剪切流被视为起点。其次，考虑摩擦雷诺数Re_tau = 1000时在平面湍流通道流的两半中形成的湍流边界层，其中平均剪切率S（y）随着距壁面y的距离而变化。在这种混合量子经典方法中，两个Metropolis-Hastings采样子步骤中的第一个的提议分布Q由参数量子电路构建。该算法生成合成的追踪粒子轨迹。由此产生的踪迹-粒子对扩散的标度定律是从拉格朗日角度探索湍流混合的核心量，与由耦合朗之万方程组成的随机输运模型和经典MCMC对应物一致。与经典的抽样方法不同，QE-MCMC使用经过调整的目标分布。由于湍流槽道流动中追踪动力学的高度依赖性，在马尔科夫链转移矩阵的第一和第二特征值之间引入了有效的高度加权谱间隙。当从在最高量子位数和分辨率处具有互相关性的多元分布进行抽样时，发现后者显着超过经典MCMC。因此，我们的结果支持这种一次性算法作为生成式拉格朗日量子计算模块的适用性，可能嵌入复杂的流体流动问题中。我们的模块被发现对于每个空间维度Nq <= 6的相对较少的量子位数量可靠地工作。


### Abstract

We present a quantum-enhanced Markov chain Monte Carlo (QE-MCMC) method to sample turbulent acceleration vectors from a joint target distribution that depends on all three components and height to model the transport and dispersion of massless Lagrangian tracer particles in two turbulent shear flows. A homogeneous shear flow, characterized by a uniform shear rate S, is considered as the starting point. Secondly, a turbulent boundary layer, which forms in both halves of a plane turbulent channel flow at friction Reynolds number Re_tau = 1000, is considered, where the mean shear rate S(y) varies with distance from the wall y. In this hybrid quantum-classical method, the proposal distribution Q for the first of two Metropolis-Hastings sampling substeps is constructed by a parametric quantum circuit. The algorithm generates synthetic tracer particle tracks. The resulting scaling laws for tracer-particle pair dispersion, a central quantity to probe turbulent mixing from a Lagrangian perspective, agree with a stochastic transport model consisting of coupled Langevin equations and with the classical MCMC counterpart. Differently from the classical sampling method, QE-MCMC uses a tempered target distribution. Due to the height dependence of the tracer dynamics in turbulent channel flow, an effective height-weighted spectral gap between the first and second eigenvalue of the Markov-chain transition matrix is introduced. The latter is found to significantly exceed the one of classical MCMC when sampling from a multivariate distribution with cross-correlations at the highest qubit numbers and thus resolutions. Consequently, our results support the applicability of this one-shot algorithm as a generative Lagrangian quantum-computing module, possibly embedded in a complex fluid-flow problem. Our module is found to work reliably for a relatively small number of qubits per spatial dimension of Nq <= 6.

## 量子误差修正的检测器误差模型重建

**Original Title:** Reconstruction of detector error model for quantum error correction

**Authors:** Cheng Ye, Pan Zhang

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.16288v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.16288v2

**Submitted:** 2026-06-15


### 摘要

容错量子计算从根本上依赖于电路级噪音的准确描述来优化解码算法。然而，提取复杂的多体误差相关性仍然具有挑战性。当代的贪婪推理算法可能会遭受统计失真，丢弃真正的物理机制，同时引入许多非物理的误报。在这里，我们引入了基于相关分析的超图重建（CAHR）算法，这是一个全球一致的框架，可以将实验症状统计数据直接转化为离散物理超图。通过将精确的代数相关方程与自上而下的并发修剪策略相结合，CAHR可以在我们的基准设置中恢复$d=5$旋转表面代码和密集8体2D颜色代码的故障布局，而不会出现误报。此外，我们表明密集码中的精确连续参数提取受到\textit{方差级联}的限制，其中绝对统计方差从高到低程度机制线性累积。这激发了两阶段推理范式：利用CAHR提取故障布局，然后进行连续概率优化。这提供了一种在现实量子硬件中描述和解码高度相关的噪音的实用方法。


### Abstract

Fault-tolerant quantum computing fundamentally relies on the accurate characterization of circuit-level noise to optimize decoding algorithms. However, extracting complex multi-body error correlations remains challenging. Contemporary greedy inference algorithms can suffer from statistical distortion, discarding true physical mechanisms while introducing many unphysical false positives. Here, we introduce the Correlation-Analysis-based Hypergraph Reconstruction (CAHR) algorithm, a globally consistent framework to invert experimental syndrome statistics directly into discrete physical hypergraphs. By coupling exact algebraic correlation equations with a top-down concurrent-pruning strategy, CAHR recovers the fault topology without false positives for both $d=5$ rotated surface codes and dense 8-body 2D color codes in our benchmark settings. Furthermore, we show that exact continuous parameter extraction in dense codes is limited by a \textit{variance cascade}, where absolute statistical variance accumulates linearly from high- to low-degree mechanisms. This motivates a two-stage inference paradigm: utilizing CAHR to extract the fault topology, followed by continuous probability optimization. This provides a practical approach for characterizing and decoding highly correlated noise in realistic quantum hardware.

## 用于模拟拓扑相的超冷原子格子系统：综述

**Authors:** Bei-Bei Wang, Xiao-Dong Lin, Jinyi Zhang, Long Zhang

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.16598v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.16598v2

**Submitted:** 2026-06-15


### 摘要

由于最近的快速进展，用于模拟拓扑相的超冷原子晶格系统现在正处于关键阶段，从既定的范式发展成为越来越多功能和可编程的量子模拟器。在这篇综述中，我们调查了最近的实验进展在四个主要类别的平台：光学晶格，包括光学晶格与激光辅助隧道和光学拉曼晶格;合成晶格在动量或内部状态空间; Floquet工程晶格;和光镊阵列，所有这些都提供了不同的能力，实现和探测拓扑物质。对于每一个类，我们突出了代表性的实验突破，已经实现的拓扑模型，以及先进的检测和表征技术，强调这些互补的方法如何共同扩展量子模拟的前沿。我们还讨论了强相关和非平衡拓扑阶段的新兴方向，并对未来前景进行了展望。


### Abstract

Owing to rapid recent progress, ultracold atomic lattice systems for simulating topological phases are now at a pivotal stage, evolving from established paradigms into increasingly versatile and programmable quantum simulators. In this review, we survey recent experimental advances across four major classes of platforms: optical lattices, including optical lattices with laser-assisted tunneling and optical Raman lattices; synthetic lattices in momentum or internal-state space; Floquet-engineered lattices; and optical tweezer arrays, all of which offer distinct capabilities for realizing and probing topological matter. For each class, we highlight representative experimental breakthroughs, the topological models that have been realized, and the advanced detection and characterization techniques employed, emphasizing how these complementary approaches collectively expand the frontier of quantum simulation. We also discuss emerging directions in strongly correlated and nonequilibrium topological phases, and conclude with an outlook on future prospects.

## 使用ADAPT-VQE进行随机全对全Hamilton方程的基状态准备

**Original Title:** Ground state preparation of random all-to-all Hamiltonians using ADAPT-VQE

**Authors:** Sabhyata Gupta, Bharath Sambasivam, Sophia E. Economou, Edwin Barnes, Alexander F. Kemper, Raghav G. Jha

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18339v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.18339v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-16


### 摘要

具有全对全相互作用的随机Hamilton体的底价，例如量子Sherrington-Kirkpatrick（SK）模型和Sachdev-Ye-Kitaev（SYK）模型，遵循体积定律纠缠，预计很难使用张量网络进行建模。近年来，在突破使用神经量子状态的经典方法的极限方面取得了一些进展。然而，是否存在量子算法，可以在模拟随机汉密尔顿学派方面提供比最先进的经典方法的量子优势，这仍然是一个悬而未决的问题。在这项工作中，我们证明了一种这样的算法，TETRIS-ADAPT-VQE，可以为包含高达$N=20$马约拉纳费米子的稠密和稀疏SYK模型构建准确的基状态，实现了稳定度$\geq 99.3\%$，而对于具有高达$L=18$站点的量子SK模型，实现了稳定度$\geq 99.9998\%$。我们发现，虽然对于SK模型来说，基状态的准备是有效的（就操作员池大小和电路深度而言），但对于密集或适度稀疏的SYK模型来说，它都是无效的。


### Abstract

The ground state of random Hamiltonians with all-to-all interactions such as the quantum Sherrington-Kirkpatrick (SK) model and the Sachdev-Ye-Kitaev (SYK) model follow volume-law entanglement and are expected to be hard to model using tensor networks. In recent years, some progress has been made to push the limit of classical methods using neural quantum states. However, it remains an open question whether there exist quantum algorithms that could offer a quantum advantage over the state-of-the-art classical methods in simulating random Hamiltonians. In this work, we show that one such algorithm, TETRIS-ADAPT-VQE, can construct accurate ground states for dense and sparse SYK models containing up to $N=20$ Majorana fermions achieving fidelities $\geq 99.3\%$ and for the quantum SK model with up to $L=18$ sites achieving fidelities $\geq 99.9998\%$. We find that while the preparation of ground states is efficient (in terms of operator pool size and circuit depth) for the SK model, it is not efficient for either dense or moderately sparse SYK models.

## 量子浴波动带来的混乱

**Original Title:** Chaos from quantum bath fluctuations

**Authors:** Ilan Baud, Tamoghna Ray, Mahaveer Prasad, Manas Kulkarni, Camille Aron

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18340v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.18340v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-16


### 摘要

大环境对有限大小量子力学系统的影响是双重的：它带来消散，但也带来热和量子起源的波动。虽然消散往往会稳定动态，但我们质疑环境量子涨落是否以及如何在典型的非混乱系统中产生混乱。我们建立了量子光学的一个范式模型：消散性迪克模型，其中大旋转与消散性和声模相互作用。我们通过在大但有限的旋转下的半经典体系中工作来计算经典/量子对应关系。我们证明，从超辐射区的经典规则相空间开始，量子噪音可以产生一个具有分维和正李雅普诺夫指数的奇怪吸引子。我们揭示了数学界最近发展起来的与剪切引起的混乱的深层联系。


### Abstract

The effect of a large environment on a finite-size quantum mechanical system is two-fold: It brings dissipation, but also fluctuations of thermal and quantum origin. While dissipation tends to stabilize the dynamics, we question if and how environmental quantum fluctuations can generate chaos in an otherwise classically non-chaotic system. We work out a paradigmatic model of quantum optics: the dissipative Dicke model, where a large spin interacts with a dissipative harmonic mode. We dial in the classical/quantum correspondence by working in the semiclassical regime at large but finite spin. We demonstrate that, starting from a classically regular phase space in the superradiant regime, quantum noise can generate a strange attractor with fractal dimension and a positive Lyapunov exponent. We unveil the deep connection with shear-induced chaos that was recently developed in the mathematical community.

## 稳态光谱接吻和消散相转变

**Original Title:** Steady-state spectral kissing and dissipative phase transitions

**Authors:** Devesh Karthik, Jorge Chávez-Carlos, Edson M. Signor, Victor S. Batista, Francisco Pérez-Bernal, Lea F. Santos

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18348v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.18348v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-16


### 摘要

最近在克尔参数振荡器（KPO）中实现的光谱接吻是指成对的能量级的合并，并作为激发态量子相转变（ESQPT）的表现而出现。在这里，我们表明这种现象具有编码在稳态密度矩阵的谱中的消耗对应物。以消散的KPO为代表性例子，我们证明，在弱消散区域中，稳态密度矩阵的特征值组织成准简并对，反映了相应封闭系统的光谱接吻。随着消散强度的增加，这种配对逐渐消失。通过分析系统的经典极限，我们推导出了控制稳态光谱接吻开始及其在消散相转变时消失的临界线的分析公式。


### Abstract

Spectral kissing, recently realized in a Kerr parametric oscillator (KPO), refers to the merging of pairs of energy levels and arises as a manifestation of an excited-state quantum phase transition (ESQPT). Here, we show that this phenomenon has a dissipative counterpart encoded in the spectrum of the steady-state density matrix. Using a dissipative KPO as a representative example, we demonstrate that, in the weak-dissipation regime, the eigenvalues of the steady-state density matrix organize into quasi-degenerate pairs that mirror the spectral kissing of the corresponding closed system. As the dissipation strength increases, this pairing gradually disappears. By analyzing the classical limit of the system, we derive analytical expressions for the critical lines governing both the onset of steady-state spectral kissing and its disappearance at a dissipative phase transition.

## 量子场论中的幽灵与不稳定粒子

**Original Title:** Ghosts versus Unstable Particles in Quantum Field Theory

**Authors:** Luca Buoninfante

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18349v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.18349v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-16


### 摘要

我们通过将鬼魂与量子场论中的不稳定粒子进行比较来阐明多粒子阈值以上鬼魂的物理本质。我们首先考虑渐进公式，其中普通的正规范单粒子状态可能是不稳定的和衰变的，而幽灵可以渐进地生存而不衰变，但由于与掩盖负规范单粒子状态的多粒子成分的干扰，不允许任何粒子解释。这种区别源于修饰传播子的两种不同分析结构，在幽灵或普通情况下，其复共极点分别位于第一个或第二个Riemann片中。原则上，幽灵共振在现象上与普通共振有区别，更窄，正能量峰值和负能量峰值之间的干扰较弱。然后，我们在有限的时间间隔内制定量子场论，并在修饰传播子的适当近似范围内工作，发现有限时间效应放大了共振行为的差异，并产生新的特征，例如鬼共振中的更高峰值。还确定了不同的时间范围：对于短于倒数宽度的时间，大约的自由粒子描述是有效的，而在稍后的时间，相互作用和干涉效应占主导地位，导致衰变或多粒子掩蔽。修饰传播子中的复极点仅在后期出现，并渐进地成为复-共矩对，从而决定了渐进动力学。这项研究支持在渐进极限下不存在自由传播的幽灵粒子。


### Abstract

We elucidate the physical nature of ghosts above the multi-particle threshold by contrasting them with unstable particles in quantum field theory. We first consider the asymptotic formulation, where ordinary positive-norm one-particle states can be unstable and decay, whereas ghosts survive asymptotically without decaying, yet admit no particle interpretation due to interference with the multi-particle component which masks the negative-norm one-particle state. This distinction originates from two different analytic structures of the dressed propagator, whose complex conjugate poles lie in the first or second Riemann sheet in the ghost or ordinary case, respectively. Ghost resonances are, in principle, phenomenologically distinguishable from ordinary ones, being narrower and exhibiting weaker interference between positive- and negative-energy peaks. We then formulate the quantum field theory in a finite interval of time and, working within a suitable approximation for the dressed propagator, find that finite-time effects amplify differences in the resonant behavior and give rise to new features, such as higher peaks in ghost resonances. Distinct temporal regimes are also identified: for times shorter than the inverse width, an approximate free-particle description is valid, whereas at later times interactions and interference effects dominate, leading to decay or multi-particle masking. Complex poles in the dressed propagator emerge only at late times and become complex-conjugate pairs asymptotically, determining the asymptotic dynamics. This study supports the absence of freely propagating ghost particles in the asymptotic limit.

## N=4超Yang-Mills和长程自旋链中的弱混沌

**Original Title:** Probing weak chaos in $\mathcal N=4$ super Yang-Mills and long-range spin chains

**Authors:** Pawel Caputa, Brian Creed, Rathindra Nath Das, Saskia Demulder, Tristan McLoughlin

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18351v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.18351v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-16


### 摘要

我们研究了$\mathcalN =4$ super Yang-Mills的$\mathfrak{su}（2）$扇形中平面扩张操作的有限环截断中的量子混乱特征及其$β$-变形。这些截断定义了最近邻居XXX旋转链的全息驱动的远程变形。在单循环时，模型是可积的，而全循环平面理论预计将再次是可积的。因此，伪循环截断为研究这两个可积极限之间如何出现混乱行为提供了一个自然的环境。我们使用谱统计、特征向量诊断和传播复杂性来分析这个问题。我们发现，两环和四环截断在足够大的耦合下发展出类似于GOE的水平统计，但具有弱可积性破缺的特征。四环的可积性破缺比两环的可积性破缺弱，并且发生混乱的临界耦合更大，至少对于长旋转链来说是这样。三圈截断并没有显示在研究范围内相同的混乱开始。特征量诊断表明，相应的特征状态的随机性低于GOE载体，表明弱的历经性和多重分数性。最后，我们可以识别Krylov空间数据中特征值和特征量混乱的特征。也就是说，我们证明了水平间隔统计与Krylov链上传播复杂性和无序性峰值之间的相关性。Hamilton本征基中初始状态的离域被证明强烈影响复杂性的饱和度。我们的结果表明，有限环扩张操作符并不是一般的远程旋转链Hamilton，但已经显示出与全环平面理论中可积性的恢复一致的模式。


### Abstract

We study signatures of quantum chaos in finite-loop truncations of the planar dilatation operator in the $\mathfrak{su}(2)$ sector of $\mathcal N=4$ super Yang-Mills and its $β$-deformation. These truncations define holographically motivated long-range deformations of the nearest-neighbour XXX spin chain. At one-loop the model is integrable, while the all-loop planar theory is expected to again be integrable. Finite-loop truncations therefore provide a natural setting for investigating how chaotic behaviour emerges between these two integrable limits. We analyse this question using spectral statistics, eigenvector diagnostics and spread complexity. We find that the two- and four-loop truncations develop GOE-like level statistics at sufficiently large coupling but with features characteristic of weak integrability breaking. The integrability breaking at four-loops is weaker than at two-loops and the critical coupling at which chaos occurs is larger, at least for long spin chains. The three-loop truncation does not show the same onset of chaos in the range studied. Eigenvector diagnostics show that the corresponding eigenstates remain less random than GOE vectors, indicating weak ergodicity and multifractality. Finally, we can identify signatures of the eigenvalue and eigenvector chaos in the Krylov-space data. Namely, we demonstrate a correlation of the level spacing statistics with the peak of spread complexity and disorder on the Krylov chain. The delocalisation of the initial state in the Hamiltonian eigenbasis is shown to strongly affect the saturation of complexity. Our results suggest that finite-loop dilatation operators are not generic long-range spin chain Hamiltonians, but already display patterns consistent with the restoration of integrability in the all-loop planar theory.

## 用于量子传感和通信应用的全反射场拓宽不平衡干涉仪

**Original Title:** All Reflective Field-widened Unbalanced Interferometer for Quantum Sensing and Communication Applications

**Authors:** Ramy Tannous, Dogan Sinar, Tabitha D. Arulpragasam, Thomas Jennewein

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18358v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.18358v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-16


### 摘要

由于大气湍流造成的波阵面失真，自由空间光通道上的时间点编码信号的干扰通常需要对接收器进行严格的模式过滤，通常使用自适应光学来解决。基于场拓宽干涉仪的无源多模式接收器提供了一种引人注目的替代方案，可以实现直接干涉，而无需额外的波阵面修正。我们展示了一种场宽干涉仪设计，该设计仅用反射表面实现，并实现了空间多模式束的高干涉可见度（大于0.97）。通过两个成像系统实现了多模式束的干涉，这两个成像系统由球凸面镜和平面镜之间的腔体配置组成。该配置能够实现小的形状因子，本质上是无彩色的，并且基于标准的球形镜，这降低了系统的复杂性。该干涉仪适用于空间多模式和湍流光通道，例如卫星通信，并且专为使用时间点编码量子位的量子系统设计。


### Abstract

Interference of time-bin encoded signals over free-space optical channels typically requires stringent mode filtering on receivers due to wavefront distortions from atmospheric turbulence, conventionally addressed with adaptive optics. Passive multimode receivers based on field-widened interferometers present a compelling alternative, enabling direct interference without the overhead of wavefront correction. We demonstrate a field-widened interferometer design that is implemented solely with reflective surfaces and achieves a high interference visibility (greater than 0.97) for spatially multimode beams. The interference of the multimode beams is enabled by two imaging systems that consist of a cavity configuration between a spherical concave mirror and a flat mirror. The configuration enables small form-factors, is inherently achromatic, and is based on standard spherical mirrors which reduces the complexity of the system. The interferometer is applicable for spatially multimode and turbulent optical channels, such as satellite communication, and is designed for quantum systems that use time-bin encoded qubits.

## 拓扑序和局部非正性流的普适纠缠探测

**Original Title:** Universal entanglement probes of topological order and locally-achiral manifolds

**Authors:** Yarden Sheffer

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18361v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.18361v1

Exploiting this asymmetry, we show that under a per-block covertness budget the asymptotically optimal signaling strategy is uniform across the detection segment, and we derive an explicit minimum-length condition under which a single-pass cumulative sum (CUSUM) detector crosses threshold within the same block with exponentially high probability. The resulting design law yields a covert blockwise binary codebook over a finite transmission horizon and establishes a concrete link between bosonic covert communication, sequential detection, and blockwise signaling design. More broadly, these results provide design guidance for covert quantum communication systems with physically realizable receivers, and help bridge information-theoretic covertness guarantees with implementable receiver-aware optical communication design.
