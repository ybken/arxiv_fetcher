## 稳定轨道的量子最优控制

**Original Title:** Quantum optimal control of steady orbits

**Authors:** Shebha Anandhi Jegadeesan, Yujie Zhao, Robert Hunter, Hassane El Mkami, Maximilian Keitel, Callum Musselwhite, Graham Smith, Guinevere Mathies, Ilya Kuprov

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.15383v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.15383v2

**Submitted:** 2026-06-13


### 摘要

周期性驱动的消散系统可以进入稳定的轨道--其动力学上的固定循环。在量子力学中，稳定轨道发生在冷却发动机（用于初始化量子器件）、相干振荡器（例如激光器和脉泽）、精确计量器件（原子钟、光学和旋转磁力计）和磁共振（稳态自由岁差、动态核极化）中。稳态轨道和频闪稳态是量子最优控制的一个有希望的目标，但数字复杂性令人望而却步：无限循环击败了依赖于时间域显式数字传播的梯度上升脉冲工程（GRAPE）。在这里，我们提出了一种有效的量子控制策略，用于频闪稳态和极限环，当控制序列重复无限多次时，这些稳态和极限环将渐进地逼近。该形式主义与弗洛伊德-林德布拉德状态工程和有效的汉密尔顿理论不同：它找到了驱动消散量子系统通过用户指定的航路点朝着稳定轨道的控制序列。软件实现（与GRAPE相同的数字复杂性扩展）是针对Spinach库完成的。


### Abstract

Periodically driven dissipative systems can settle into steady orbits - fixed loops on their dynamical manifolds. In quantum mechanics, steady orbits occur in cooling engines (used to initialise quantum devices), coherent oscillators (such as lasers and masers), precision metrology devices (atomic clocks, optical and spin magnetometers), and magnetic resonance (steady state free precession, dynamic nuclear polarisation). Steady orbits and stroboscopic steady states are a promising target for quantum optimal control, but the numerical complexity is prohibitive: the infinite loop defeats gradient ascent pulse engineering (GRAPE) which relies on explicit numerical propagation in the time domain. Here we propose an efficient quantum control strategy for stroboscopic steady states and limit cycles that are approached asymptotically when a control sequence is repeated infinitely many times. The formalism is different from Floquet-Lindblad state engineering and effective Hamiltonian theories: it finds control sequences that drive a dissipative quantum system towards a steady orbit passing through user-specified waypoints. The software implementation (same numerical complexity scaling as GRAPE) is done for the Spinach library.

## 基于变分量子本征解器的分子量子Bootstrap嵌入

**Original Title:** Variational Quantum Eigensolver-Based Quantum Bootstrap Embedding for Molecules

**Authors:** Derek Peng

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17095v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.17095v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-13


### 摘要

由于现代硬件有限的量子体积和中等保真度量子位，在近期量子硬件上模拟强相关分子系统仍然具有挑战性。规避这一挑战的一种潜在方法是通过引导嵌入（BE）。Bootstrap嵌入将分子分解为更小的片段，然后以迭代的方式嵌入其他片段的“池”中。Bootstrap嵌入对量子模拟很有吸引力，因为将系统分段可以降低任何给定片段的量子位需求。在这项工作中，我们开发了一个量子自举嵌入（QBE）工作流程，该工作流程使用变分量子本征解算器（VQE）片段解算器，并研究决定整体VQE-QBE算法成功的算法选择。为了提高效率，我们引入了FastAdaptVQE，这是自适应变分量子本征解算器（ADAPT-VQE）的稀疏矩阵加速形式，用直接状态载体线性代数取代符号交换子计算，以及MatrixFreeAdaptVQE，这是一种无矩阵扩展，可以消除处理较大碎片时出现的稀疏矩阵内存瓶颈。我们还修改了ADAPT-VQE操作员选择步骤，用前瞻策略取代纯粹的贪婪选择。$H_4$和$F_2$的基准达到化学准确度，使用全配置相互作用（FCI）求解器的自举嵌入结果在1 kCal/摩尔内。这些结果表明，QBE与VQE相结合可以准确计算分子系统的能量。这项研究为将能量计算扩展到近期量子硬件上的更大分子系统和量子材料奠定了基础。


### Abstract

Simulating strongly correlated molecular systems on near-term quantum hardware remains challenging due to modern hardware's limited quantum volume and moderate-fidelity qubits. One potential way to circumvent this challenge is through bootstrap embedding (BE). Bootstrap embedding breaks molecules into smaller fragments that are then embedded into the "bath" of other fragments in an iterative way. Bootstrap embedding is appealing for quantum simulation because fragmenting the system reduces the qubit requirements for any given fragment. In this work, we develop a quantum bootstrap embedding (QBE) workflow that uses variational quantum eigensolver (VQE) fragment solvers and study the algorithmic choices that determine the overall VQE-QBE algorithm's success. To improve efficiency, we introduce FastAdaptVQE, a sparse matrix-accelerated form of the adaptive variational quantum eigensolver (ADAPT-VQE) that replaces symbolic commutator evaluation with direct statevector linear algebra, and MatrixFreeAdaptVQE, a matrix-free extension that removes the sparse-matrix memory bottleneck that appears when treating larger fragments. We also modify the ADAPT-VQE operator selection step by replacing the purely greedy choice with a look-ahead strategy. Benchmarks on $H_4$ and $F_2$ reach chemical accuracy, within 1 kcal/mol of bootstrap embedding results using a full configuration interaction (FCI) solver. These results show that combining QBE with VQE can accurately calculate energies of molecular systems. This research lays the foundation for extending energy calculations to larger molecular systems and quantum materials on near-term quantum hardware.

## 量子电影：通过生成世界模型对量子计算硬件的交互式电影探索

**Original Title:** Quantum Cinema: An Interactive Cinematic Exploration of Quantum Computing Hardware via Generative World Models

**Authors:** Aoyu Zhang, Dongping Liu, Luyao Zhang

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17102v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.17102v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-14


### 摘要

量子计算有望在科学和工业领域取得变革性进展，但实现这些计算的物理硬件仍然对公众来说是不可见的：量子处理器在接近绝对零度的温度下在密封稀释冰箱内运行，使得直接观察变得不可能。量子计算日益增长的社会影响与公众可视化它的能力之间的这种“想象力差距”是量子素养和劳动力发展的重大障碍。我们介绍了Quantum Cinema，这是一个开源、基于浏览器的交互式应用程序，通过使用生成式世界模型将隐形量子硬件转化为可探索的电影体验来缩小这一差距。量子电影引导用户完成四幕叙事--从诺贝尔奖获得者量子纠缠的基础科学，到三个主要量子计算架构的精心策划的视频介绍（俘获离子、中性原子和超导体系统），进入身临其境的三维生成世界，使不可见的量子现象可见，最后是基于真实量子设备规范的交互式雷达图比较。所有三维环境都是使用WorldLabs的生成式世界模型平台生成的，并以亚马逊网络服务（AWS）Braket量子硬件精心策划的指标为科学依据。量子电影院无需安装，无需专门的硬件，也无需量子计算背景。它旨在服务于两个不同的社区：寻求复制或扩展平台的学者和开发人员，以及寻求直观工具向不同受众解释量子硬件的教育工作者、研究人员和科学传播者。本文描述了系统架构、生成式世界模型管道、两个社区的用例以及未来工作的方向。


### Abstract

Quantum computing promises transformative advances across science and industry, yet the physical hardware that enables these computations remains invisible to the public: quantum processors operate inside sealed dilution refrigerators at temperatures near absolute zero, making direct observation impossible. This "imagination gap" between quantum computing's growing societal impact and the public's ability to visualize it represents a significant barrier to quantum literacy and workforce development. We present Quantum Cinema, an open-source, browser-based interactive application that closes this gap by transforming invisible quantum hardware into explorable, cinematic experiences using generative world models. Quantum Cinema guides users through a four-act narrative -- from the foundational Nobel Prize-winning science of quantum entanglement, through curated video introductions to three major quantum computing architectures (trapped-ion, neutral-atom, and superconducting systems), into immersive three-dimensional generative worlds that make invisible quantum phenomena observable, and finally to interactive radar-chart comparisons grounded in real quantum device specifications. All three-dimensional environments are generated using WorldLabs' generative world model platform and are scientifically grounded in curated metrics from Amazon Web Services (AWS) Braket quantum hardware. Quantum Cinema requires no installation, no specialized hardware, and no quantum computing background. It is designed to serve two distinct communities: scholars and developers seeking to replicate or extend the platform, and educators, researchers, and science communicators seeking an intuitive tool for explaining quantum hardware to diverse audiences. This paper describes the system architecture, the generative world model pipeline, use cases for both communities, and directions for future work.

## MAPS：一种用于几何可视化高d值量子状态空间的新型多轴投影球

**Original Title:** MAPS: A Novel Multi-Axial Projective Sphere for Geometrically Visualizing Higher d-Valued Quantum State-Space of Qudits

**Authors:** Ali Al-Bayaty

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.15801v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.15801v2

**Submitted:** 2026-06-14


### 摘要

量子系统的d值量子状态空间的可视化是量子计算和信息科学领域科学研究和实际应用的基础支柱，其中d >= 2。量子位的2值量子状态在三维布洛赫球上被优雅地可视化。相比之下，扩展这个几何范式以可视化qudit（d >= 3）的较高d值量子状态，例如，a quotrit（d=3）、quququadit（d=4）和quintit（d=5）导致严重的结构和布局复杂性。本文引入了一种新的广义三维框架，以有效地可视化qudit的较高d值量子态，从易于说明、结构简单性和自然表示等方面为研究人员和工程师提供。我们将这个新框架称为“多轴投影球（MAPS）”，它由n个投影相交的空间轴组成，其中0 <= n <= d-1。我们还提出了一组新颖的d值相轴向门，以沿着这些n轴旋转和移动qudit的d值量子状态。我们的通用框架可用于可视化实际应用的多维数据，例如，机器学习、量子机器学习和量子化学，其中MAPS的每个轴都代表此类数据的单个特征及其相应的不同值。


### Abstract

Visualizing the d-valued quantum state-space of quantum systems serves as a foundational pillar for the scientific research and practical applications in quantum computing and information science, where d >= 2. The 2-valued quantum states of a qubit are elegantly visualized on the three-dimensional Bloch sphere. In contrast, expanding this geometrical paradigm to visualize higher d-valued quantum states of a qudit (d >= 3), e.g., a qutrit (d=3), ququadit (d=4), and quintit (d=5), leads to severe structural and topological complexities. This paper introduces a new generalized three-dimensional framework to effectively visualize higher d-valued quantum states of a qudit, in the aspects of ease of illustration, structural simplicity, and natural representation for researchers and engineers. We called this new framework the "multi-axial projective sphere (MAPS)", which consists of n projectional intersecting spatial axes, where 0 <= n <= d-1. We also propose a group of novel d-valued phase axial-based gates, to swivel and shift d-valued quantum states of a qudit along these n axes. Our generalized framework could be used for visualizing high-dimensional data for practical applications, e.g., machine learning, quantum machine learning, and quantum chemistry, where every axis of the MAPS represents a single feature of such data with its corresponding distinct values.

## 具有张量网络的三维十亿大小非埃尔米特物质的实空间谱函数

**Original Title:** Real-space spectral functions of three-dimensional billion-size topological non-Hermitian matter with tensor networks

**Authors:** Yitao Sun, Jose L. Lado, Guangze Chen

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.16424v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.16424v2

**Submitted:** 2026-06-15


### 摘要

非埃尔米特系统承载着广泛的非常规拓扑现象，而由于站点数量迅速增长，有限三维系统中的大规模模拟仍然具有挑战性。特别是，更高级的拓学角模通常仅在小格子中进行研究，其中强的有限尺寸效应可以掩盖其固有行为。在这里，我们开发了一个张量网络框架，该框架将量子张量交叉插值与核多项方法相结合，实现大型非厄米特紧束缚Hamilton的紧凑表示，并直接计算超过十亿个格点的系统的实空间谱函数。使用这种方法，我们研究了具有结构化实空间几何形状的三维非厄米特高级拓扑绝缘体。前所未有的系统尺寸使得可以直接访问宏观区域，并允许在真正的三维系统中解析角模光谱响应。通过调整损失强度，我们识别出弱和强损失区域中不同的间隙角模。我们的结果确立了张量网络算法是在超大非埃尔米特系统中执行真实空间光谱计算的强大策略。


### Abstract

Non-Hermitian systems host a wide range of unconventional topological phenomena while large-scale simulations in finite three dimensional systems remain challenging because of the rapidly growing number of sites. In particular, higher-order topological corner modes are often studied only in small lattices, where strong finite-size effects can mask their intrinsic behavior. Here, we develop a tensor-network framework that combines quantics tensor cross interpolation with the kernel polynomial method, enabling compact representations of large non-Hermitian tight-binding Hamiltonians and direct calculations of real-space spectral functions for systems exceeding one billion lattice sites. Using this approach, we investigate three-dimensional non-Hermitian higher-order topological insulators with with structured real-space geometries. The unprecedented system size enables direct access to the macroscopic regime and allows corner-mode spectral responses to be resolved in genuinely three-dimensional systems. By tuning the loss strength, we identify distinct in-gap corner modes across weak- and strong-loss regimes. Our results establish tensor-network algorithms as a powerful strategy to perform real-space spectral calculations in exceptionally large non-Hermitian systems.

## 通过量子点的随机矩阵理论在表面代码中投影逻辑系综

**Original Title:** Projected logical ensembles in surface codes via the random-matrix theory of quantum dots

**Authors:** Mircea Bejan, Jan Behrends, Max McGinley, Benjamin Béri

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17140v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.17140v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-15


### 摘要

测量是主动量子误差修正（QEC）的基础，并被认为是新型测量引发的多体现象的来源。在这里，我们研究了QEC中出现的测量后逻辑状态的统计性质，该状态取决于确定性横向么门的topical码。在症状提取和最大似然解码之后，就会出现Born加权集成，我们将其称为“投影逻辑集成”（PLE）。我们专注于受均匀单量子位Pauli-$X$旋转影响的表面代码，描述了PLE测量引起的随机性。为此，我们表明，对于具有单个逻辑量子位的代码，PLE与描述介观量子点的散射矩阵系组同质，该量子点是从具有适当边界条件的2D Majorana网络模型中获得的。我们发现了这些量子点是混乱的，因此它们的散射矩阵可以用随机矩阵理论很好地描述。在这些制度中，PLE接近一个普遍系综，该系综在对称性和解码器诱导的约束下是最大随机的。由稳定器和逻辑运算符权重设置的对称性约束实现了Altland-Zirnbauer类D或DIII，我们都说明了这一点。我们的结果建立了介观物理学中出现的普遍性概念、量子多体系统和QEC之间的基本联系。


### Abstract

Measurements underpin active quantum error correction (QEC) and have been recognized as a source of novel measurement-induced many-body phenomena. Here, we study the statistical properties of post-measurement logical states arising in QEC on topological codes subject to deterministic transversal unitary gates. Upon syndrome extraction followed by maximum-likelihood decoding, a Born-weighted ensemble arises which we dub the "projected logical ensemble" (PLE). Focusing on surface codes subject to uniform single-qubit Pauli-$X$ rotations, we characterize the measurement-induced randomness of the PLE. To this end, we show that for a code with a single logical qubit, the PLE is isomorphic to an ensemble of scattering matrices describing mesoscopic quantum dots obtained from a 2D Majorana network model with suitable boundary conditions. We uncover regimes where these quantum dots are chaotic such that their scattering matrices are well-described by random matrix theory. In these regimes, the PLE approaches a universal ensemble that is maximally random up to symmetry and decoder-induced constraints. The symmetry constraints, set by stabilizer and logical operator weights, realize Altland-Zirnbauer classes D or DIII, which we both illustrate. Our results establish a fundamental connection between emergent universality concepts in mesoscopic physics, quantum many-body systems, and QEC.

## 当重新规范化记住：紫外线/红外混合作为纠缠桥梁

**Original Title:** When Renormalisation Remembers: UV/IR Mixing as an Entanglement Bridge

**Authors:** Steven Abel

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17147v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.17147v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-15


### 摘要

传统上，重新正化被理解为威尔逊式的无记忆过程，其中紫外线（紫外线）自由度逐渐脱钩，留下自主的红外线（IR）描述。然而，情况并非如此：在紫外/红外混合理论中，相距很远的尺度之间的相关性可以持续存在。在这项工作中，我将紫外/红外混合重新描述为一种Hilbert空间现象，实现为跨再正化尺度的相关性。该公式是使用Born-Reciprocal张量网络（BRTN）来实现的，这是一种张量网络的新配置，在相空间互惠下是全球对称的。在这个网络上，我准备真空并重现预期的辐射修正。由此产生的重正化几何展现了记忆力，具有连接红外物理学互逆表示的桥梁，其跨桥纠缠为有效描述的可行性提供了精确的标准。我分析了何时满足这个标准，并表明存在一个大容量限制，基本规模保持固定，其中局部描述的障碍逐渐消失：威尔逊行为被恢复，重新规范化被遗忘。因此，BRTN为紫外/红外混合提供了一个具体且可计算的平台。


### Abstract

Renormalisation is traditionally understood to be a Wilsonian memoryless process in which ultraviolet (UV) degrees of freedom gradually decouple, leaving an autonomous infrared (IR) description. However this need not be the case: in UV/IR mixed theories correlations between widely separated scales can persist. In this work I recast UV/IR mixing as a Hilbert-space phenomenon, realised as correlations across renormalisation scales. This formulation is implemented using the Born-Reciprocal Tensor Network (BRTN), a new configuration of tensor network that is globally symmetric under phase-space reciprocity. On this network I prepare the vacuum and reproduce the expected radiative corrections. The resulting renormalisation geometry exhibits memory, with a bridge linking reciprocal representations of IR physics, whose cross-bridge entanglement provides a precise criterion for the viability of an effective description. I analyse when this criterion is met, and show that there is a large-volume limit, with the fundamental scale held fixed, in which the obstruction to a local description scales away: Wilsonian behaviour is restored and renormalisation forgets. The BRTN therefore provides a concrete and calculable platform for UV/IR mixing.

## 从有效场论看核子-核子散射中的量子资源和维格纳对称性

**Original Title:** Quantum Resources and Wigner Symmetry in Nucleon-Nucleon Scattering from Effective Field Theory

**Authors:** Ian Low, Thomas R. Richardson, Sokratis Trifinopoulos

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17148v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.17148v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-15


### 摘要

我们研究了无pi有效场论中通过次领先级的低能量核子-核子散射中的旋转自由度量子资源，例如纠缠、稳定剂幻数和非局部幻数。将每个核子旋转视为一个量子位，我们计算了在一般的质量中心动量和散射角$β $下散射操作符的相应资源生成能力。该分析保留了由两个衍生接触相互作用产生的$S$-和$P$-波通道。当微观物理学表现出维格纳的$SU（4）$旋转风味对称性时，中子-质子幅度变得与旋转空间单位子成比例，因此在散射后不会产生新的资源，扩展了之前对领先阶$S$波散射的观察。同一核子通道仍然可以产生资源，因为来自相同粒子的约束投射到了部分希尔伯特空间。这些结果表明，增强的对称性、部分波结构和资源产生如何在低能量二体散射中交织在一起。


### Abstract

We study quantum resources in the spin degrees of freedom, such as entanglement, stabilizer magic, and non-local magic, in low-energy nucleon-nucleon scattering through next-to-leading order in pionless effective field theory. Treating each nucleon spin as a qubit, we calculate the corresponding resource-generating powers of the scattering operator at generic center-of-mass momentum and scattering angle $Θ$. The analysis retains $S$- and $P$-wave channels generated by two-derivative contact interactions. When the microscopic physics exhibits Wigner's $SU(4)$ spin-flavor symmetry, the neutron-proton amplitude becomes proportional to the spin-space identity operator and therefore generates no new resources after scattering, extending an observation previously made for leading-order $S$-wave scattering. The same-nucleon channel remains resource-generating because constraints from identical particles project out part of the Hilbert space. These results show how enhanced symmetries, partial-wave structure, and resource generation are intertwined in low-energy two-body scattering.

## 具有局域控制的费米哈密顿工程

**Original Title:** Fermionic Hamiltonian engineering with local control

**Authors:** Özgün Kum, Matthias Zipper, Ludwig Mathey, Martin Kliesch

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17158v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.17158v1

**Submitted:** 2026-06-15


### 摘要

量子模拟器通过在可控量子设备上再现其动态动态，能够探索凝聚态系统中的复杂量子现象。然而，实验限制往往限制了可以在本地实现的汉密尔顿类。Hamilton工程通过从硬件定义的固定系统Hamilton扩展可访问目标Hamilton集合来解决这一限制。我们引入了一个新的费米子Hamilton工程框架，该框架基于系统Hamilton与实验上可行的局部费米子么元序列下的耦合自由进化。通过线性规划有效获得所需的序列和自由进化时间。通过将系统进化与这些局部正性交织在一起，我们的方法在广泛的目标Hamilton下实现了有效的时间进化，并且对有限脉冲时间误差具有内在的鲁棒性。特别是，我们证明可以实现任意复隧道系数，仅受基础系统Hamilton的连通性约束。我们通过在1088模格子和具有复杂隧道系数的相互作用费米-哈伯德链上设计非相互作用Harper-Hofstadter模型的动力学来说明这种能力。通过施工，我们的方法避免了Floquet工程固有的持续能量吸收。


### Abstract

Quantum simulators enable the exploration of complex quantum phenomena in condensed-matter systems by reproducing their dynamics on controllable quantum devices. However, experimental constraints often restrict the class of Hamiltonians that can be realized natively.
