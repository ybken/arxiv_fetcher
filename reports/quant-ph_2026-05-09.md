## Hamilton模拟动态电路中的错误缓解

**Original Title:** Error Mitigation in Dynamic Circuits for Hamiltonian Simulation

**Authors:** Sumeet Shirgure, Siyuan Niu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05256v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.05256v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-06


### 摘要

动态量子电路集成了中间电路测量和前向操作，以实现实时经典处理和条件量子逻辑。这些能力是量子错误纠正等关键量子协议的核心，并且最近在一系列应用中展示了减少量子资源（包括电路深度和门数）的巨大潜力。然而，在真实量子硬件上执行动态电路会带来一个关键的权衡：虽然资源需求降低，但由于中间电路测量的高错误率以及中间电路测量和前向操作引入的延长空闲期间积累的去相干误差，电路保真度会下降。在本文中，我们系统地研究了标准误差缓解技术对动态电路应用的影响，该应用涉及海森堡模型等物理相关系统的Hamilton模拟和接地状态估计。我们探讨动态去耦（DD）作为一种策略，以抑制解相干和串扰的错误，在空闲窗口期间引入的中间电路测量和前馈延迟，并通过零噪声外推（ZNE）检查错误缓解。通过在IBM量子硬件上进行的实验，我们对这些策略的有效组合进行了基准测试，这些策略可以最大限度地提高动态量子电路在这些应用中的实际效益。我们证明，DD和ZNE的组合是有效的，在减轻中间电路测量和前馈操作过程中引入的错误，以及错误的测量所产生的错误。这种方法在基态估计中产生至少60%的保真度改进，并且对于伊辛模型和海森堡模型，时间演化状态的观测误差分别降低了99%和20%。


### Abstract

Dynamic quantum circuits integrate mid-circuit measurements and feed-forward operations to enable real-time classical processing and conditional quantum logic. These capabilities are central to key quantum protocols such as quantum error correction, and have recently demonstrated significant potential for reducing quantum resources, including circuit depth and gate count, across a range of applications. However, executing dynamic circuits on real quantum hardware introduces a critical trade-off: while resource requirements decrease, circuit fidelity degrades due to high error rates of mid-circuit measurements, as well as the decoherence errors accumulated during the extended idle periods introduced by both mid-circuit measurements and feed-forward operations. In this paper, we systematically investigate the impact of standard error mitigation techniques on dynamic circuit applications pertaining to Hamiltonian simulation and ground state estimation of physically relevant systems like the Heisenberg model. We explore dynamical decoupling (DD) as a strategy to suppress decoherence and crosstalk errors during idle windows introduced by mid-circuit measurements and feed-forward delays, and also examine error mitigation via zero-noise extrapolation (ZNE). Through experiments conducted on IBM quantum hardware, we benchmark effective combinations of these strategies that maximize the practical benefits of dynamic quantum circuits in these applications. We demonstrate that a combination of DD and ZNE is effective in mitigating the errors introduced during mid-circuit measurements and feed-forward operations, as well as the errors arising from faulty measurements. This approach yields a fidelity improvement of at least 60% in ground state estimation and reduces observed error of time-evolved states by up to 99% for the Ising model and up to 20% for the Heisenberg model.

## 左手原子系统中电磁诱导透明度抑制吸收的负折射

**Original Title:** Negative refraction with absorption suppressed by electromagneticly induced transparency in a left-handed atomic system

**Authors:** Shun-Cai Zhao

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05261v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.05261v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-06


### 摘要

本文旨在在稠密四原子系统中实现电磁诱导透明度（EIT）抑制吸收的负折射。如果没有响应探测场的两个相等的过渡频率，原子系统就会显示出负折射，同时具有负介电常数和负渗透率（左手性）。探测场的响应被放大并在某些频率范围内传播透明度。因此，考虑到负折射材料的主要应用局限性是大量的消散和吸收，该方案可以实现我们寻找低损耗负折射的目标。然而，过高的信号场强度会增加我们的方案中共振附近的吸收。


### Abstract

This paper intends to realize negative refraction with absorption suppressed by the electromagneticly induced transparency(EIT) in a dense four-level atomic system. Without the two equal transition frequencies responding to the probe field, the atomic system displays a negative refraction with the simultaneously negative permittivity and negative permeability(Left-handedness). The response of the probe field is amplified and propagates transparency in some frequency extents. Therefore, our aim for searching the low-loss negative refraction can be achieved in the scheme, given the main applied limitation of the negative refractive materials is the large amount of dissipation and absorption. However, an excessive signal field intensity would increase the absorption near the resonance in our scheme.

## 量子适当评分规则：极小极大估计和资源理论优势

**Original Title:** Quantum Proper Scoring Rules: Minimax Estimation and Resource-Theoretic Advantages

**Authors:** M. W. AlMasri

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05268v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.05268v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-06


### 摘要

我们将适当的评分规则推广到量子域，用密度运算符取代概率分布。我们通过运算符凸生成元定义量子值函，并建立一个完整的二元理论，产生适当的量子评分规则。我们在麦卡锡型激励下推导出量子状态断层扫描的极小极大最优界，证明了量子Cramér-Rao-McCarthy界，该界明确地将极小极大风险与生成函数的弯曲和量子费舍尔信息联系起来。我们量化预测任务中量子资源（一致性、纠缠性、适应性）的经济价值，在经典和量子估计策略之间建立缩放分离。我们的结果指导量子传感器、激励兼容的量子数据市场和稳健的量子机器学习协议的设计。


### Abstract

We generalize proper scoring rules to the quantum domain, replacing probability distributions with density operators. We define Quantum Value Functionals via operator convex generators and establish a complete duality theory yielding proper quantum scoring rules. We derive minimax optimal bounds for quantum state tomography under McCarthy-type incentives, proving a Quantum Cramér-Rao-McCarthy Bound that explicitly links minimax risk to the curvature of the generating function and the Quantum Fisher Information. We quantify the economic value of quantum resources (coherence, entanglement, adaptivity) in forecasting tasks, establishing scaling separations between classical and quantum estimation strategies. Our results guide the design of quantum sensors, incentive-compatible quantum data markets, and robust quantum machine learning protocols.

## 网络介导的电容性耦合推动了高温导体电路中的快速OTEC饱和

**Original Title:** Network-Mediated Capacitive Coupling Drives Fast OTOC Saturation in Superconducting Circuits

**Authors:** Carla Caro Villanova, Alan C. Santos

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05035v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.05035v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-06


### 摘要

我们研究了超越有效最近邻描述的超临界转换子阵列中电容网络介导的相互作用的动力学和光谱后果。虽然有效的最近邻相互作用模型很好地捕捉到了弱耦合机制，但我们表明，增加电容连接性会导致动态可观测量中的这种逼近显着偏离。使用超时有序相关器（OTOCs），我们证明这种网络介导的耦合显着加速了操作员加扰，与最近邻限制相比导致快速饱和。这种动态交叉伴随着谱统计从Poissonian行为向水平排斥的转变，比例参数保持在Poissonian和Gaussian Orthodox Ensemble（GOE）极限之间。这表明部分历经性的出现，而不是完全发展的量子混乱。由于这种行为出现在当前的超导子设备的实验现实状态中，因此识别网络介导的耦合何时定性地改变信息动态与可扩展的超导架构直接相关。


### Abstract

We investigate the dynamical and spectral consequences of capacitance-network-mediated interactions in superconducting transmon arrays beyond effective nearest-neighbor descriptions. While weak coupling regimes are well captured by an effective nearest-neighbor interacting models, we show that increasing capacitive connectivity induces a pronounced departure from this approximation in dynamical observables. Using Out-of-Time-Ordered Correlators (OTOCs), we demonstrate that such network-mediated couplings significantly accelerate operator scrambling, leading to rapid saturation compared to the nearest-neighbor limit. This dynamical crossover is accompanied by a shift in spectral statistics away from Poissonian behavior toward level repulsion, with the ratio parameter remaining intermediate between Poisson and Gaussian Orthogonal Ensemble (GOE) limits. This indicates the emergence of partial ergodicity rather than fully developed quantum chaos. As this behavior arises within experimentally realistic regimes of current superconducting transmon devices, identifying when network-mediated couplings qualitatively alter information dynamics is directly relevant for scalable superconducting architectures.

## 强到弱自发对称性破缺中的电荷扰乱

**Original Title:** Charge Scrambling in Strong-to-Weak Spontaneous Symmetry Breaking

**Authors:** Jong Yeon Lee

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05288v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.05288v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-06


### 摘要

强到弱自发对称性破缺（SWTSB）是由非线性相关器诊断的，但它对保守电荷涨落的直接静态含义并不是自动的。我们表明，对于连续对称性，远程Rényi-1相关器，加上足够快速地逼近其非零渐进值，迫使子系统电荷不确定性：块电荷方差具有广泛的下限;等效地，截断对称性期望具有广泛的弯曲。这给出了电荷扰乱的精确静态波动足迹。我们构建例子来表明其含义是有条件的和不可逆的：当Rényi-1尾部太慢时，去相超流体保留了Rényi-1 SWTSB，具有次广泛的电荷变化，而稀疏的固定电荷投影仪具有广泛的电荷变化，但没有局部电荷转移Rényi-1阶或远程条件互信息。最后，我们引入了一个\{twist overlap}相关器，它作为适用于离散和连续对称性的电荷方差的模拟。这自然地将局部块电荷波动分解为强对称和弱对称通道。我们发现弱对称性通道隔离了一致电荷涨落，并且与维格纳--Yanase倾斜信息直接相关。总而言之，这些结果为区分非线性SWTSB序、局部电荷不确定性和一致电荷涨落提供了统一的理解。


### Abstract

Strong-to-weak spontaneous symmetry breaking (SWSSB) is diagnosed by nonlinear correlators, but its direct static implication for conserved charge fluctuations is not automatic. We show that, for continuous symmetries, long-range Rényi-1 correlator, together with a sufficiently rapid approach to its nonzero asymptotic value, forces subsystem charge indefiniteness: the block-charge variance has an extensive lower bound; equivalently, the truncated symmetry expectation has extensive curvature. This gives a precise static fluctuation footprint of charge scrambling. We construct examples to show that the implication is conditional and non-reversible: dephased superfluids retain Rényi-1 SWSSB with subextensive charge variance when the Rényi-1 tail is too slow, while sparse fixed-charge projectors have extensive charge variance but no local charge-transfer Rényi-1 order or long-range conditional mutual information. Finally, we introduce a \emph{twist overlap} correlator, which serves as an analogue of charge variance applicable to both discrete and continuous symmetries. This naturally decomposes local block-charge fluctuations into strong- and weak-symmetry channels. We found that the weak-symmetry channel isolates coherent charge fluctuations and is directly related to the Wigner--Yanase skew information. Taken together, these results give a unified understanding for distinguishing nonlinear SWSSB order, local charge indefiniteness, and coherent charge fluctuations.

## 基于李代数的含时系统中的Krylov动力学和运算符增长

**Original Title:** Krylov Dynamics and Operator Growth in Time-Dependent Systems via Lie Algebras

**Authors:** András Grabarits, E. Medina-Guerra, Adolfo del Campo

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05290v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.05290v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-06


### 摘要

我们研究Krylov空间（进化发生的最小子空间）中由时相关Hamilton产生的量子动力学。我们在与时相关的Krylov子空间中的动力学与Hamilton的基本李代数结构之间建立了直接联系。我们开发了一个一般框架，其中依赖于时间的Krylov子空间中的动力学是由相关李代数的阶梯运算符生成的。特别是，我们确定了精确的时间相关Krylov动力学由相互作用图片Hamilton自然确定并由嵌入的$\mathfrak{sl}（2，\mathbb {C}）$子代数支配的最小条件。我们进一步表明，时间进化操作符的精确单指数表示会在单位相关的基础上产生独特的时间无关Krylov动态，但仍然可以从中恢复精确的时间相关Krylov动态。我们还将该框架扩展到振子代数，作为倒数零海森堡-韦伊尔代数的最简单扩展，并提供进一步的例子，包括平移和扩张的谐振子、由闭Virasoro子代数控制的系统、旋转磁场中的旋转以及多水平系统的更高维度概括。此外，我们对依赖于时间的生成器生成的复杂性增长率引入了一个新的量子速度限制，并表明，对于由李代数控制的进化，它保留了与时间无关的情况相同的函数形式。值得注意的是，这个界限的饱和度受到时间驱动的强烈影响，并且只有当汉密尔顿在不同时间与自己通勤时才会持续存在。这些结果建立了一个统一的框架，用于描述具有基础李代数结构的时变量子系统中的操作员增长和Krylov复杂性。


### Abstract

We study quantum dynamics generated by time-dependent Hamiltonians in Krylov space, the minimal subspace in which the evolution takes place. We establish a direct link between dynamics in the time-dependent Krylov subspace and the underlying Lie-algebraic structure of the Hamiltonian. We develop a general framework in which the dynamics in the time-dependent Krylov subspace is generated by ladder operators of the associated Lie algebra. In particular, we identify the minimal conditions under which the exact time-dependent Krylov dynamics is naturally determined by the interaction-picture Hamiltonian and governed by an embedded $\mathfrak{sl}(2,\mathbb{C})$ subalgebra. We further show that an exact single-exponential representation of the time-evolution operator gives rise to a distinct time-independent Krylov dynamics in a unitarily related basis, from which the exact time-dependent Krylov dynamics can nevertheless be recovered. We also extend the framework to the oscillator algebra as the simplest extension of the nilpotent Heisenberg--Weyl algebra, and provide further examples, including the translated and dilated harmonic oscillator, systems governed by closed Virasoro subalgebras, a spin in a rotating magnetic field, and higher-dimensional generalizations for multi-level systems. In addition, we introduce a new quantum speed limit to the complexity growth rate generated by a time-dependent generator and show that, for evolutions governed by a Lie algebra, it retains the same functional form as in the time-independent case. Remarkably, saturation of this bound is strongly affected by temporal driving and persists only when the Hamiltonian commutes with itself at different times. These results establish a unified framework for characterizing operator growth and Krylov complexity in time-dependent quantum systems with underlying Lie-algebraic structures.

## 通用神经脉动器：多体量子系统中的学习时间进化

**Original Title:** Universal Neural Propagator: Learning Time Evolution in Many-Body Quantum Systems

**Authors:** Zihao Qi, Christopher Earls, Yang Peng

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05299v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.05299v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-06


### 摘要

模拟量子多体动力学的传统方法会产生单一轨迹：如果汉密尔顿量或初始状态发生变化，则必须重新执行计算。最近对基础模型的努力已经开始解决这一局限性，但现有方法要么跨汉密尔顿主义者，要么跨初始状态，但不能跨两者转移。在这项工作中，我们引入了通用神经传播器（UNP），这是一个单一的统一模型，可以学习从驱动协议到时间进化传播器的功能映射。单个UNP模型以完全自我监督的方式训练，同时预测驾驶协议的函数空间和指数级大的初始状态希尔伯特空间的动态。我们以二维驱动伊辛模型为基准，并展示了UNP在产品和纠缠初始状态以及分发内和分发外驱动协议之间的准确性和可移植性。除了精确对角化之外，UNP在系统大小上仍然保持准确，并且可以使用可观察数据在所有初始状态之间进行有效的微调。通过将学习对象从量子状态转移到操作员，这项工作开辟了一条可转移模拟驱动量子物质的途径。


### Abstract

Conventional approaches to simulating quantum many-body dynamics produce a single trajectory: if the Hamiltonian or the initial state is changed, the computation must be re-performed. Recent efforts toward foundation models have begun to address this limitation, yet existing methods transfer across either Hamiltonians or initial states, but not both. In this work, we introduce the Universal Neural Propagator (UNP), a single, unified model that learns the functional mapping from driving protocols to time-evolution propagators. Trained in an entirely self-supervised way, a single UNP model predicts dynamics across a function space of driving protocols and an exponentially large Hilbert space of initial states simultaneously. We benchmark on a two-dimensional driven Ising model and demonstrate the UNP's accuracy and transferability across product and entangled initial states, as well as for both in- and out-of-distribution driving protocols. The UNP remains accurate at system sizes beyond exact diagonalization, and can be efficiently fine-tuned across all initial states using observable data. By shifting the object of learning from quantum states to operators, this work opens a route toward transferable simulation of driven quantum matter.

## 挫败里德伯格阵列中量子多体疤痕的系统构建

**Original Title:** Systematic construction of quantum many-body scars in frustrated Rydberg arrays

**Authors:** Jean-Yves Desaules, Aron Kerschbaumer, Marko Ljubotina, Maksym Serbyn

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05297v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.05297v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-06


### 摘要

迄今为止，里德伯原子阵列中的量子多体伤痕仅在二体格子上观察到，这留下了它们是否以及如何经受住挫折以及导致非热动力学的适当初始状态是什么的问题。我们引入了一个图形理论框架来寻找适合在任意网格上留下疤痕的候选者。我们的框架预测了两种不同的机制：I型疤痕通过使用局部纠缠状态克服轻微挫败来概括二分情况，而II型疤痕利用强烈挫败来钉住网格的一部分，让其余部分自由振荡。我们用数字方式证明了这两种机制，并发现了六边形网格上的指数级伤痕轨迹家族，这些轨迹可以编码免受热化的信息。我们的结果将疤痕确定为一维以外的里德伯系统的一般特征，并提供了一种在实验上可用的途径来系统地探测量子模拟器中的非热动力学。


### Abstract

Quantum many-body scars in Rydberg atom arrays have thus far only been observed on bipartite lattices, leaving open the question of whether and how they survive frustration, and what the appropriate initial states are that lead to nonthermal dynamics. We introduce a graph-theoretic framework to find suitable candidates for scarring on arbitrary lattices. Our framework predicts two distinct mechanisms: type-I scars generalize the bipartite case by using locally entangled states to overcome mild frustration, while type-II scars exploit strong frustration to pin part of the lattice, leaving the remainder to oscillate freely. We numerically demonstrate both mechanisms and uncover an exponential family of scarred trajectories on the hexagonal lattice that can encode information protected from thermalization. Our results establish scarring as a generic feature of Rydberg systems beyond one dimension and provide an experimentally accessible route to systematically probing non-thermal dynamics in quantum simulators.

## 通过格子几何学工程量子多体疤痕

**Original Title:** Engineering Quantum Many-Body Scars through Lattice Geometry

**Authors:** Erick Parra Verde, Kevin P. Mours, Johannes Zeiher, Ana Hudomal, Jad C. Halimeh

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05296v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.05296v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-06


### 摘要

量子多体伤疤使持续的非遍历动力学在其他热化系统，但他们的稳定通常依赖于微调的初始状态或工程哈密顿扰动。在这里，我们表明，晶格几何形状本身可以作为一个强大的和实验上可访问的控制旋钮诱导和增强疤痕。通过将一维链转化为准一维三角形装饰晶格，我们发现完全极化状态-通常在PXP模型中热化-表现出明显的保真度恢复，缓慢的纠缠增长，以及与弱纠缠本征态塔的强烈重叠。我们跟踪这种行为的几何诱导重组的约束希尔伯特空间，从而邻接图分解成超立方体子图，强制相干人口转移和稳定的紧急近似$\mathrm{su}（2）$代数。我们提出在镊子捕获里德伯原子的可编程阵列中直接实现，其中三角形装饰的几何形状可以使用空间光调制器来实现，并通过激发密度的时间分辨测量来探测由此产生的疤痕动力学。我们的结果确立了格子连通性作为在约束量子系统中工程非历经动力学的设计原则。


### Abstract

Quantum many-body scars enable persistent non-ergodic dynamics in otherwise thermalizing systems, yet their stabilization typically relies on fine-tuned initial states or engineered Hamiltonian perturbations. Here we show that lattice geometry alone can serve as a powerful and experimentally accessible control knob for inducing and enhancing scarring. By transforming a one-dimensional chain into a quasi-one-dimensional triangle-decorated lattice, we find that the fully polarized state -- normally thermalizing in the PXP model -- exhibits pronounced fidelity revivals, slow entanglement growth, and strong overlap with a tower of weakly entangled eigenstates. We trace this behavior to a geometry-induced restructuring of the constrained Hilbert space, whereby the adjacency graph decomposes into hypercube subgraphs that enforce coherent population transfer and stabilize an emergent approximate $\mathrm{su}(2)$ algebra. We propose a direct implementation in programmable arrays of tweezer-trapped Rydberg atoms, where the triangle-decorated geometry can be realized using spatial light modulators and the resulting scarring dynamics probed via time-resolved measurements of excitation density. Our results establish lattice connectivity as a design principle for engineering non-ergodic dynamics in constrained quantum systems.

## 无序费米子中的测量诱导相变

**Authors:** Yunxiang Liao, Max Matheussen, Xinghai Zhang

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.05306v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.05306v1
