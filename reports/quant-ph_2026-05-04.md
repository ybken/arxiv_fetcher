## 量子芯片上的无拥堵路由

**Original Title:** Congestion-free routing on quantum chips

**Authors:** Mithilesh Kumar, Yusuf Tahir, Varun Daiya, Sanjana Mattaparthi, Aarav Shaurya

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.27015v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.27015v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-29


### 摘要

有限的连接性使得非本地量子门在近邻硬件上变得昂贵，而在近邻硬件上，编译通常依赖于SWAP传输，从而继承深度开销和路径拥堵。我们提出了一个免交换路由框架，其中更高级别的qudit充当垂直频谱巴士，在不移动计算状态的情况下传输控制信息。我们表明，最近邻体系结构中的确切拥堵缓解需要局部Hilbert空间扩展。在此模型中，与$3L$基线相比，在长度为$L$的路径上的非本地操作需要$2L+1$逻辑路由基元。重叠的路线通过编码在相同实体quudits中的公交车标签仍然可以区分。该路由代数扩展到共同目标处的布尔扇入：到达不同公交车上的多个控制触发基于公交车数字的任意布尔函数的局部么正性，产生深度为$2L + D_g + O（1）$的多控制操作，扇入大小$K$和目标合成成本$D_g$。我们证明了Cnot和布尔扇入的可解码性、可逆性和正确性，以及精确重叠路由的状态计数下限$d \geq 2 '{K+1}$。Cirq模拟证实了单控制正确性和零串烧。QFT、QAOA和镜像交互电路的乘客级基准验证了预测的拥堵规律和交通减少。有噪音的QuTiP模拟表明，架构优势取决于更高级别的一致性和速度。这些结果将频谱qudit路由识别为一种隔离-解除架构，该架构将非本地控制交付与本地目标端聚合分离，为克服量子位路由限制提供了最低限度的机制。


### Abstract

Limited connectivity makes nonlocal quantum gates expensive on near-neighbor hardware, where compilation typically relies on SWAP transport, inheriting both depth overhead and path congestion. We present a swap-free routing framework in which higher levels of a qudit act as orthogonal spectral buses that transport control information without moving the computational state. We show that exact congestion relief in nearest-neighbor architectures requires local Hilbert-space expansion. In this model, a nonlocal operation over a path of length $L$ requires $2L+1$ logical routing primitives, compared to the $3L$ baseline. Overlapping routes remain distinguishable through bus labels encoded in the same physical qudits. This routing algebra extends to Boolean fan-in at a common target: multiple controls arriving on distinct buses trigger a local unitary based on an arbitrary Boolean function of bus digits, yielding multi-control operations of depth $2L + D_g + O(1)$ for fan-in size $K$ and target-synthesis cost $D_g$. We prove decodability, reversibility, and correctness for CNOT and Boolean fan-in, along with a state-count lower bound $d \geq 2^{K+1}$ for exact overlap routing. Cirq simulations confirm single-control correctness and zero crosstalk. Compiler-level benchmarks on QFT, QAOA, and mirror-interaction circuits verify the predicted congestion law and transport reduction. Noisy QuTiP simulations show that the architectural advantage depends on higher-level coherence and speed. These results identify spectral qudit routing as a congestion-relief architecture that separates nonlocal control delivery from local target-side aggregation, providing a minimal mechanism for overcoming qubit routing limitations.

## 方程约束双线性量子控制的Tikhonov正规投影梯度流

**Original Title:** Tikhonov-regularised projected gradient flow for equality-constrained bilinear quantum control

**Authors:** Tanveer Ahmad

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.26625v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.26625v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-29


### 摘要

We study a projection-type gradient flow for equality-constrained maximisation of a smooth bilinear control objective on $\mathcal{H}=L^2(0,T;\mathbb{R})$, eliminating Lagrange multipliers through an $(M{+}1)\times(M{+}1)$ moving Gram matrix $Γ(s)_{\ell\ell'}=\int_0^T S(t)\,c_\ell(s,t)\,c_{\ell'}(s,t)\,\mathrm{d}t$. The flow generates monotonic ascent in continuous time but becomes unstable on discretisation; existing implementations rely on heuristic step-size safeguards lacking rigorous justification. We close this gap by replacing $Γ$ with $Γ_{\varepsilon}:=Γ+\varepsilon^{2}I$ and prove: (i) an exact spectral identity giving $κ(Γ_{\varepsilon})=(σ_{\max}^{2}+\varepsilon^{2})/(σ_{\min}^{2}+\varepsilon^{2})$; (ii) objective monotonicity $\mathrm{d}J/\mathrm{d}s\ge 0$ for all $\varepsilon\ge 0$; (iii) constraint drift $|h_{m}-C_{m}|=\mathcal{O}(\varepsilon^{2})$ with a computable prefactor; (iv) convergence of the regularised trajectory to the unregularised one in $L^{2}(0,T)$ at rate $\mathcal{O}(\varepsilon^{2})$ under uniform invertibility of $Γ$; and (v) a discrete CFL criterion $Δs\,G\,\|Γ_{\varepsilon}^{-1}\|\leα<2$ guaranteeing objective monotonicity of the forward-Euler scheme up to $\mathcal{O}(Δs^{2})$ local truncation error. The theory is validated on a three-level bilinear benchmark for all-optical Bell-state preparation, where $κ(Γ)\in[10^{9},10^{11}]$, the predicted $\varepsilon^{2}$ rate is confirmed over eight decades, and moderate regularisation eliminates step rejections and reduces constraint drift by more than an order of magnitude at unchanged final fidelity.

## 可及性框架下玻恩定则和量子算符形式的边界约化推导

**Original Title:** Derivation of the Born Rule and Operational Quantum Formalism in the Accessibility Framework through Boundary Reduction

**Authors:** Everett Fall, Hironori Kondo

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2604.27125v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.27125v2

**Submitted:** 2026-04-29


### 摘要

我们表明，操作量子形式主义--博恩规则、吕德斯状态更新、量子干涉、非马尔科夫有效动力学和Tsirelson界$2的贝尔不等式破坏--产生于可及性理论（AT）中，从孔隙结构以及论文中所述的显式一致性和定域性假设。AT是一个基于真实分级谱三重组和单一代数选择原则的框架。普遍可及性平衡原则要求谱三重组复杂性的三个独立测量--其代数、规范理论和几何内容--完全相等并最小化，唯一地选择代数$\mathbb{C} \oplus \mathbb{H} \oplus M_3（\mathbb{C}）$以及标准模型规范群、粒子含量、二维洛伦兹时空、三代和引力动力学。对余维一几何边界的限制将此代数简化为其交换中心$\mathbb{C} \oplus\mathbb {C}\oplus \mathbb{C}$ --孔隙--它为任何嵌入式观察者定义了永久的信息瓶颈。通过这一瓶颈进行推理的一致性条件，以及关于48维内部赫伯特空间的格里森定理，唯一地确定了博恩规则;其余的操作特征来自所述假设下的相同操作级框架。在存在论层面，该理论是确定性和状态实在论的，而操作量子形式主义则出现在观察者层面，这是由于对底层代数实在的访问在结构上受到限制。


### Abstract

We show that the operational quantum formalism -- the Born rule, Lüders state updating, quantum interference, non-Markovian effective dynamics, and Bell inequality violation at the Tsirelson bound $2\sqrt{2}$ -- arises within Accessibility Theory (AT) from the Aperture construction together with explicit coherence and locality assumptions stated in the paper. AT is a framework built on real graded spectral triples and a single algebraic selection principle. The Principle of Universal Accessibility Balance requires three independent measures of the complexity of a spectral triple -- its algebraic, gauge-theoretic, and geometric content -- to be exactly equal and minimized, uniquely selecting the algebra $\mathbb{C} \oplus \mathbb{H} \oplus M_3(\mathbb{C})$ and with it the Standard Model gauge group, particle content, four-dimensional Lorentzian spacetime, three generations, and gravitational dynamics. Restriction to a codimension-one geometric boundary reduces this algebra to its commutative center $\mathbb{C} \oplus \mathbb{C} \oplus \mathbb{C}$ -- the Aperture -- which defines a permanent information bottleneck for any embedded observer. Coherence conditions on inference through this bottleneck, together with Gleason's theorem on the 48-dimensional internal Hilbert space, uniquely determine the Born rule; the remaining operational features follow from the same observer-level framework under the stated assumptions. At the ontological level the theory is deterministic and state-realist, while the operational quantum formalism appears at the observer level as a consequence of structurally limited access to the underlying algebraic reality.

## 代数量子场论中的本质二元性和最大非信号扩展

**Original Title:** Essential Duality and Maximal Non-signalling Extensions in Algebraic Quantum Field Theory

**Authors:** Hassan Nasreddine

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00075v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.00075v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-30


### 摘要

我们表明，在可加性下，$B（\mathcal{H}）$内的最大von Neumann代数扩张，其内部自同射相对于所有空间分离区域是非信号的$\mathcal{A}（O '）'$。因此，$\mathCal{A}（O）$就该性质而言是最大的当且仅当本质二元性成立。   这个证明纯粹是代数的。当本质二元性失败时，我们构造一个适当的扩展，其所有内部自同射，以及更一般地说，在代数中接受克劳斯运算符的所有正规完全正映射，都是非信号的。在本质对偶下，任何真扩张都必然允许一个信号运算。使用荒木相对熵的熵公式提供了信号的定量诊断，尽管它没有在证明中使用。   其他的结构性结果包括楔交恒等式$\mathcal{A}（O '）' = \bigcap_{W \supset O}\mathcal{A}（W）$和本质对偶的等价刻画。这些结果确定基本的对偶性作为一个操作的极大值条件内的给定表示。


### Abstract

We show that, under additivity, the maximal von Neumann algebra extension of $\mathcal{A}(O)$ inside $B(\mathcal{H})$ whose inner automorphisms are non-signalling with respect to all spacelike-separated regions is $\mathcal{A}(O')'$. Consequently, $\mathcal{A}(O)$ is maximal with respect to this property if and only if essential duality holds.
  The proof is purely algebraic. When essential duality fails, we construct a proper extension all of whose inner automorphisms, and more generally all normal completely positive maps admitting Kraus operators in the algebra, are non-signalling. Under essential duality, any proper extension necessarily admits a signalling operation. An entropic formulation using Araki relative entropy provides a quantitative diagnostic of signalling, though it is not used in the proof.
  Additional structural results include the wedge-intersection identity $\mathcal{A}(O')' = \bigcap_{W \supset O}\mathcal{A}(W)$ and equivalent characterisations of essential duality. These results identify essential duality as an operational maximality condition within the given representation.

## 局部稳定阶段的统一框架

**Original Title:** A Unified Framework for Locally Stable Phases

**Authors:** Zhi Li, Raz Firanko, Timothy H. Hsieh

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00088v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.00088v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-30


### 摘要

我们提出了一个统一的框架来描述平衡、非平衡和亚稳状态下物质的纯态和混合态相。我们引入了局部稳定状态的概念，该概念由操作属性定义，即任何局部操作（包括后选择）都可以通过局部通道逆转。我们证明了局部稳定性相当于短程相关状态，该状态由相关性和条件互信息的衰减定义。我们证明这些性质在局部可逆通道下是不变的，从而定义了局部稳定相。此外，我们证明了局部稳定性既意味着包括保真度相关器在内的一类非线性相关器的衰变，也意味着典范净化中相关性的衰变，从而弥合了混合状态和纯状态之间的差距。一路上，我们建立了两个可能具有独立意义的结果：我们表明，对局部稳定（短程相关）状态的后选择可以通过局部通道实现，并且量子马尔科夫链可以通过非线性可观测量的局部可计算性来描述。


### Abstract

We propose a unifying framework for characterizing pure and mixed state phases of matter across equilibrium, non equilibrium, and metastable regimes. We introduce the concept of locally stable states, defined by the operational property that any local operation (including post selection) can be reversed by a local channel. We prove that local stability is equivalent to a state being short range correlated, defined by the decay of both correlations and conditional mutual information. We demonstrate that these properties are invariant under locally reversible channels, thus defining locally stable phases. Furthermore, we prove that local stability implies both the decay of a family of nonlinear correlators, including the fidelity correlator, and the decay of correlations in the canonical purification, thus bridging the gap between mixed and pure states. Along the way, we establish two results which may be of independent interest: we show that post-selection on locally stable (short range correlated) states can be implemented via local channels and that quantum Markov chains can be characterized by the local computability of nonlinear observables.

## 铁磁微粒的片上悬浮

**Original Title:** On-chip levitation of ferromagnetic microparticles

**Authors:** Martijn Janse, M. Luisa Mattana, Julian van Doorn, Eli van der Bent, Richard Wagner, Robert Smit, Bas Hensen

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00090v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.00090v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-30


### 摘要

微观物体在真空中的悬浮结合了卓越的环境隔离与对其动态的精确控制，突破了传感和宏观量子物理学的极限。特别是，磁悬浮允许大范围的颗粒尺寸，同时避免高强度光学捕获束和电场噪音的有害影响。然而，现有的反磁和迈斯纳悬浮方法通常受到低机械本征频率、由于线圈或磁铁体积大而与其他系统的集成性有限以及对于迈斯纳悬浮来说，需要低温操作。在这里，我们展示了一种能够稳定悬浮纳克（6.5微米半径）铁磁微球的室温片上磁悬浮平台。该平台具有可扩展性和可调性，并支持本征频率超过10 GHz的语音模式。进一步小型化和与固态旋转量子位的耦合可以使冷却到量子接地状态。除了量子实验之外，这种架构还实现了对隔离铁磁热力学的集成精确传感和研究。


### Abstract

Levitation of microscopic objects in vacuum combines exceptional environmental isolation with precise control of their dynamics, pushing the limits of sensing and macroscopic quantum physics. In particular, magnetic levitation allows a large range of particle sizes, while avoiding detrimental effects from high-intensity optical trapping beams and electric field noise. However, existing diamagnetic and Meissner levitation approaches are typically constrained by low mechanical eigenfrequencies, limited integrability with other systems due to bulky coils or magnets, and, for Meissner levitation, the need for cryogenic operation. Here, we demonstrate a room-temperature on-chip magnetic levitation platform capable of stably levitating a nanogram (6.5 micrometer radius) ferromagnetic microsphere. The platform is scalable and tunable, and supports librational modes with eigenfrequencies exceeding 10 kHz. Further miniaturization and coupling to solid-state spin qubits could enable cooling to the quantum ground state. Beyond quantum experiments, this architecture enables integrated precision sensing and studies of isolated ferromagnet thermodynamics.

## 图论测量捕捉大型量子系统上的弱历经破缺

**Original Title:** Graph-theory measures capture weak ergodicity breaking on large quantum systems

**Authors:** Heiko Georg Menzler, Rafał Świętek, Mari Carmen Bañuls, Fabian Heidrich-Meisner

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00094v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.00094v1

**Submitted:** 2026-04-30


### 摘要

我们研究封闭量子多体系统中弱遍性违背的发生，并重点关注它们通过模型参数控制的转变发生的情况。我们的分析基于在Fock空间中表示量子系统，并利用图形理论测量。作为主要结果，我们表明最近引入的图能量中心性通过其分布的特征变化捕捉了已知的弱历经性破坏转变。虽然大多数数值工具仅限于小系统尺寸，但我们的测量可以针对数百个地点的大型系统进行分析计算，在某些情况下，甚至在热力学极限下也是如此。我们通过证明我们的基于福克空间的测量对动力学约束量子模型的适用性来得出结论，在该模型中，我们发现了伴随玻璃动力学的弱历经破坏转变的证据。


### Abstract

We study the onset of weak ergodicity violations in closed quantum many-body systems and focus on cases in which they occur through a transition that is controlled by a model parameter. Our analysis is based on representing quantum systems in Fock space and utilizes graph-theoretical measures. As a main result, we show that the recently introduced graph-energy centrality captures known weak ergodicity-breaking transitions via characteristic changes in its distribution. While most numerical tools are limited to small system sizes, our measure can be calculated analytically for large systems of many hundreds of sites and in some cases, even in the thermodynamic limit. We conclude by demonstrating the applicability of our Fock-space based measure to a kinetically constrained quantum model, where we find evidence for a weak ergodicity-breaking transition accompanied by glassy dynamics.

## 多层偶极相互作用里德堡原子阵列中的可扩展旋转-拉曼压缩

### Abstract

Our results constitute a first theoretical step beyond the well-studied qubit setting toward scalable entanglement generation in qudit systems with dipolar interactions, directly relevant to current Rydberg tweezer experiments.

## 用于量子学习的可证明且可扩展的量子高斯过程

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.00794v1

### 摘要

微代数方程（DTE）在约束动力系统中自然产生，但它们的代数约束和隐藏的兼容性条件使它们比标准常微方程更加微妙。本文启动了约束线性ADE的量子算法研究。我们引入了一个膨胀框架，该框架将一般非厄米特约束的进化嵌入到扩大的希尔伯特空间上的投影Schrödinger型动力学中，\[ i\fRAC{d}{dt} ð（t）=P\tH P ð（t），\]其中$\tH$是厄米特，$P$是提升约束子空间上的垂直投影仪。这识别了具有量子Zeno型动力学的DTE演变，并支持使用块编码、基于QSV的投影仪构造和Hamilton模拟。   我们将该框架应用于非定常斯托克斯方程的保结构离散化，其中压力强制离散不可压缩性约束。我们推导出相应的投影Hamilton公式，识别出由解光滑性驱动的低能量光谱截止，并与经典投影型方法进行比较讨论由此产生的量子模拟成本。这些结果为了解量子算法、DTE和受约束的PCE动态的潜在交叉点迈出了第一步。


### Abstract

Differential-algebraic equations (DAEs) arise naturally in constrained dynamical systems, but their algebraic constraints and hidden compatibility conditions make them more subtle than standard ordinary differential equations. This paper initiates a quantum-algorithmic study of constrained linear DAEs. We introduce a dilation framework that embeds the generally non-Hermitian constrained evolution into a projected Schrödinger-type dynamics on an enlarged Hilbert space, \[ i\frac{d}{dt}Ψ(t)=P\tH PΨ(t), \] where $\tH$ is Hermitian and $P$ is the orthogonal projector onto the lifted constraint subspace. This identifies the DAE evolution with a quantum Zeno-type dynamics and enables the use of block encodings, QSVT-based projector construction, and Hamiltonian simulation.
  We apply the framework to structure-preserving discretizations of the unsteady Stokes equations, where the pressure enforces the discrete incompressibility constraint. We derive the corresponding projected Hamiltonian formulation, identify low-energy spectral cutoffs motivated by solution smoothness, and discuss the resulting quantum simulation cost in comparison with classical projection-type methods. The results provide a first step toward understanding the potential intersection of quantum algorithms, DAEs, and constrained PDE dynamics.

## 级联变分量子本征解算器的概率分布分析

**Original Title:** Probability Distribution Analysis of the Cascaded Variational Quantum Eigensolver

**Authors:** Yi-Hua Lai, John P. T. Stenger, Gloria Bazargan, Igor V. Schweigert, Daniel Gunlycke

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00807v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.00807v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-01


### 摘要

级联变分量子本征解算器（CVQE）避免了传统VQE算法中所需的量子和经典处理单元之间迭代通信的需要。虽然CVQE提供了选择指导状态作为输入的完全自由，但并非所有指导状态都足以满足解决方案的准确性和资源效率。我们的工作提出了一个基于准状态准备的过程，用于选择引导状态，以最少的资源消耗产生准确的多电子接地状态解决方案。通过分析CVQE计算不同阶段的状态概率分布，我们确定针对给定资源约束的最佳引导状态参数。我们使用有噪的中间尺度量子（NISQ）计算，通过比较原型双分子反应$\mathrm{H}_2 + \mathrm {H}_2^+ \rightarrow \mathrm{H}_3^+ + \mathrm{H}$的最小能量路径上的电子能量来证明这一过程。


### Abstract

The cascaded variational quantum eigensolver (CVQE) circumvents the need for iterative communication between the quantum and classical processing units that is necessary in the conventional VQE algorithm. While CVQE offers complete freedom to choose the guiding state as input, not all guiding states suffice for solution accuracy, as well as resource efficiency. Our work presents a process based on trapezoidal-state preparation for selecting guiding states that yield accurate many-electron ground-state solutions with minimal resource consumption. By analyzing the state probability distributions at different stages of the CVQE calculations, we determine the optimal guiding-state parameters for given resource constraints. We demonstrate the process by comparing electronic energies along the minimal-energy path for a prototypical bimolecular reaction, $\mathrm{H}_2 + \mathrm{H}_2^+ \rightarrow \mathrm{H}_3^+ + \mathrm{H}$, using Noisy Intermediate-Scale Quantum (NISQ) computing.
