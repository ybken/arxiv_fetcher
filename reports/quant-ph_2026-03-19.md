## 玻色子逻辑量子位的半透明通用控制

**Original Title:** Error semitransparent universal control of a bosonic logical qubit

**Authors:** Saswata Roy, Owen C. Wetherbee, Valla Fatemi

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.15356v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.15356v2

**Submitted:** 2026-03-16


### 摘要

玻色子代码为逻辑量子位构建提供了硬件高效的方法，并主持了超越逻辑量子存储器的首次演示。然而，这些成就是为了闲置信息而取得的，而实现可扩展系统中的通用量子计算仍然是一个关键瓶颈。错误透明（ET）门提供了解决这个问题的途径，但实验演示仅限于阶段门。在这里，我们引入了一个基于动态编码子空间的框架，该框架使简单的线性驱动器能够实现对振荡器量子损失误差半透明（EsT）的通用门。使用{X，H，T}的EsT逻辑门集，我们观察到以量子损失为条件的不忠实度减少了五倍，展示了通过量子误差修正延长的主动操纵寿命，并使用来自该集的八个门的序列构建复合EsT非Clifford操作。我们的方法与可检测辅助错误的方法兼容，通过标准量子控制工具包提供了一种对玻色子逻辑量子位进行误差减轻的通用控制的方法。


### Abstract

Bosonic codes offer hardware-efficient approaches to logical qubit construction and hosted the first demonstration of beyond-break even logical quantum memory. However, such accomplishments were done for idling information, and realization of fault-tolerant logical operations remains a critical bottleneck for universal quantum computation in scaled systems. Error-transparent (ET) gates offer an avenue to resolve this issue, but experimental demonstrations have been limited to phase gates. Here, we introduce a framework based on dynamic encoding subspaces that enables simple linear drives to accomplish universal gates that are error semi-transparent (EsT) to oscillator photon loss. With an EsT logical gate set of {X, H, T}, we observe a five-fold reduction in infidelity conditioned on photon loss, demonstrate extended active-manipulation lifetimes with quantum error correction, and construct a composite EsT non-Clifford operation using a sequence of eight gates from the set. Our approach is compatible with methods for detectable ancilla errors, offering an approach to error-mitigated universal control of bosonic logical qubits with the standard quantum control toolkit.

## 微波量子之间的直接控相门

**Original Title:** A direct controlled-phase gate between microwave photons

**Authors:** Adrian Copetudo, Amon M. Kasper, Tanjung Krisnanda, Gregoire Veyrac, Shushen Qin, Hui Khoon Ng, Yvonne Y. Gao

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.15587v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.15587v2

**Submitted:** 2026-03-16


### 摘要

有用的量子信息处理最终需要在大型希尔伯特空间上进行操作，其中逻辑信息可以被有效地编码并免受噪音影响。谐振子自然地提供了对此类多维空间的访问，并实现硬件高效、可纠正错误的玻色子编码。然而，振荡器之间的直接纠缠操作仍然是一个突出的挑战。现有的策略通常依赖于参数化激活相互作用，这些相互作用填充辅助非线性元素的激发状态。这会在振荡器之间引发有效的相互作用，但代价是引入额外的消散通道和编码管汇的潜在泄漏。在这里，我们在两个超导体腔中容纳的微波量子之间设计了拉曼辅助交叉克尔相互作用，而不激发非线性元件，从而抑制了耦合器引起的退散。这种方法在微波量子之间产生直接耦合，利用该耦合在两个振荡器的单量子和双量子子空间内实现受控相门，直接将它们纠缠。最后，我们利用这种动力学将存储腔的光数宇称映射到辅助振荡器而不是非线性元件上，从而实现错误检测，同时保护存储模式免受测量引起的退散。我们的工作通过实现振荡器之间保持相干的直接光-光相互作用来扩展玻色子电路量子电动力学（cQED）工具箱。这实现了完全在玻色子代码空间内操作的纠缠门，同时抑制非线性辅助激励的去相关，为容差玻色子量子计算提供了一个关键的基元。


### Abstract

Useful quantum information processing ultimately requires operations over large Hilbert spaces, where logical information can be encoded efficiently and protected against noise. Harmonic oscillators naturally provide access to such high-dimensional spaces and enable hardware-efficient, error-correctable bosonic encodings. However, direct entangling operations between oscillators remains an outstanding challenge. Existing strategies typically rely on parametrically activating interactions that populate the excited states of an ancillary nonlinear element. This induces an effective interaction between the oscillators, at the expense of introducing additional dissipation channels and potential leakage from the encoded manifold. Here, we engineer a Raman-assisted cross-Kerr interaction between microwave photons hosted in two superconducting cavities, without exciting the nonlinear element, thereby suppressing coupler-induced decoherence. This approach generates a direct coupling between microwave photons that is exploited to implement a controlled-phase gate within the single- and two-photon subspaces of two oscillators, directly entangling them. Finally, we harness this dynamics to map the photon-number parity of a storage cavity onto an auxiliary oscillator rather than a nonlinear element, enabling error detection while protecting the storage mode from measurement-induced decoherence. Our work expands the bosonic circuit quantum electrodynamics (cQED) toolbox by enabling coherence-preserving direct photon-photon interactions between oscillators. This realizes an entangling gate that operates entirely within a bosonic code space while suppressing decoherence from nonlinear ancilla excitations, providing a key primitive for fault-tolerant bosonic quantum computing.

## 物理信息神经网络用于求解具有定制场的强场物理学中的鞍点方程

**Original Title:** Physics-informed neural networks for solving saddle-point equations in strong-field physics with tailored fields

**Authors:** Jiakang Chen, Sufia Hashim, Carla Figueira de Morisson Faria

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.15786v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.15786v2

**Submitted:** 2026-03-16


### 摘要

我们开发了一个无监督的物理信息神经网络来求解在强场逼近下管理直接阈值以上电离（ATI）的鞍点方程（SPP）。这种设置提供了一个众所周知的测试平台，其中鞍点结构因定制驱动场而闻名，从而能够对所提出的求解器进行系统验证。该网络是通过最小化SPP的剩余来训练的，并且仅需要定义驱动场形状及其参数，例如强度、载体包封相、椭圆率和相对相。我们引入了一种窗口参数化策略，该策略将网络输出映射到复杂时间平面的指定区域，引导优化朝着物理相关的解决方案并提高收敛稳定性。我们针对一系列场将PINN与传统求解器进行了基准测试，展示了在宽参数范围内主要复电离时间的稳健恢复。随着激光参数的变化，该网络跟踪电离事件主导地位的变化，从而能够探索传统求解器需要重复手动初始化的状态。使用PINN推导的解，我们计算了相干ATI光电子动量分布，并表明驱动场的对称性反映在鞍点结构和所得光谱中。这些结果将PINN确立为半经典强场计算的一个有前途的框架，并为将机器学习求解器扩展到库伦修正模型或更复杂的过程（例如重新分配ATI）提供了基础，对于该过程来说，MBE是高度非线性的，并且存在多个紧密间隔的解决方案使得传统的牛顿型寻根法对初始猜测高度敏感，这阻碍了跨激光参数空间的系统研究，特别是对于定制的场。


### Abstract

We develop an unsupervised physics-informed neural network to solve saddle-point equations (SPEs) governing direct above-threshold ionization (ATI) within the strong-field approximation. This setting provides a well-understood testbed in which the saddle-point structure is known for tailored driving fields, enabling systematic validation of the proposed solver. The network is trained by minimizing the residual of the SPEs and requires only the definition of the driving-field shape and its parameters, such as intensity, carrier-envelope phase, ellipticity, and relative phase. We introduce a window parametrization strategy that maps network outputs to prescribed regions of the complex-time plane, guiding the optimization toward physically relevant solutions and improving convergence stability. We benchmark the PINN against a conventional solver for a range of fields, demonstrating robust recovery of the dominant complex ionization times over wide parameter ranges. The network tracks changes in ionization event dominance as laser parameters are varied, enabling exploration of regimes where conventional solvers require repeated manual initialization. Using the PINN-derived solutions, we compute coherent ATI photoelectron momentum distributions and show the symmetries of the driving fields are reflected in both the saddle-point structure and the resulting spectra. These results establish PINNs as a promising framework for semiclassical strong-field calculations and provide a foundation for extending machine-learning solvers to Coulomb-corrected models or to more complex processes, such as rescattered ATI for which the SPEs are highly nonlinear and the presence of multiple closely-spaced solutions makes conventional Newton-type root-finding highly sensitive to initial guesses, which hinders systematic investigations across laser-parameter spaces, particularly for tailored fields.

## 具有量子位输出的CPDNN量子通道是CPCP

**Original Title:** CPDNN quantum channels with qubit output are CPCP

**Authors:** Hyunho Cha

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16962v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.16962v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-17


### 摘要

量子幅度非负性的资源理论将完全正完全正（CPCP）量子通道与更大、更容易处理的完全正双非负（CPDNN）量子通道区分开来。是否存在一个四进制到量子位量子通道\（®：M_3\to M_2\）（即CPDNN而不是CPCP），这是开放的。我们以否定的方式回答了这个问题，并证明了更强的说法，即每个CPDNN量子通道\（®：M_n\到M_2\）对于每个\（n\in\mathbb N\）来说都是CPCP。同样，对于量子位输出量子通道来说，双非负驰豫是精确的。


### Abstract

The resource theory for nonnegativity of quantum amplitudes distinguishes completely positive completely positive (CPCP) quantum channels from the larger and more tractable class of completely positive doubly nonnegative (CPDNN) quantum channels. It was left open whether there exists a qutrit-to-qubit quantum channel \(Φ:M_3\to M_2\) that is CPDNN but not CPCP. We answer this question in the negative and prove the stronger statement that every CPDNN quantum channel \(Φ:M_n\to M_2\) is CPCP for every \(n\in\mathbb N\). Equivalently, for qubit-output quantum channels the doubly nonnegative relaxation is exact.

## Chipmunq：芯片量子架构的容错集成器

**Original Title:** Chipmunq: A Fault-Tolerant Compiler for Chiplet Quantum Architectures

**Authors:** Peter Wegmann, Aleksandra Świerkowska, Emmanouil Giortamis, Pramod Bhatotia

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16389v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.16389v2

**Submitted:** 2026-03-17


### 摘要

随着量子计算通过量子错误纠正向容差迈进，模块化小芯片架构已经出现，可以提供所需的大量量子位计数，同时克服单片芯片的制造限制。然而，这种转变引入了一个关键的编译差距：现有框架无法在管理小芯片后台的噪音、稀疏互连的同时处理容错量子电路的规模。   我们介绍了Chipmunq，这是第一个硬件感知编译器，用于将故障电路映射和路由到模块化架构上。Chipmunq采用量子错误纠正感知分区策略，该策略保留了逻辑量子位补丁的完整性，防止通用编译器中常见的过高门管理费用。我们的评估表明，与最先进的工具相比，Chipmunq的编译时间加快了13.5倍。通过结合小芯片约束和有缺陷的量子位，它可以在不同的代码距离内将电路深度减少86.4%，将SWAP门计数减少91.4%。至关重要的是，Chipmunq克服了异类小芯片间链接，将逻辑错误率提高了两个数量级。


### Abstract

As quantum computing advances toward fault-tolerance through quantum error correction, modular chiplet architectures have emerged to provide the massive qubit counts required while overcoming fabrication limits of monolithic chips. However, this transition introduces a critical compilation gap: existing frameworks cannot handle the scale of fault-tolerant quantum circuits while managing the noisy, sparse interconnects of chiplet backends.
  We present Chipmunq, the first hardware-aware compiler for mapping and routing fault-tolerant circuits onto modular architectures. Chipmunq employs a quantum-error-correction-aware partitioning strategy that preserves the integrity of logical qubit patches, preventing prohibitive gate overheads common in general-purpose compilers. Our evaluation demonstrates that Chipmunq achieves a 13.5x speedup in compilation time compared to state-of-the-art tools. By incorporating chiplet constraints and defective qubits, it reduces circuit depth by 86.4% and SWAP gate counts by 91.4% across varying code distances. Crucially, Chipmunq overcomes heterogeneous inter-chiplet links, improving logical error rates by up to two orders of magnitude.

## 具有有噪量子电路的混合经典-量子转移学习

**Submitted:** 2026-03-17


### 摘要

量子迁移学习将预训练的经典深度学习模型与量子电路相结合，以重用富有表达性的特征表示，同时限制可训练参数的数量。在这项工作中，我们引入了一系列紧凑的量子转移学习架构，这些架构将变分量子分类器附加到冻结卷积主干上以进行图像分类。我们实例化和评估了PennyLane和QisKit中实现的几个经典量子混合模型，并将它们与跨异类图像数据集的经典转移学习基线进行系统性比较。为了确保现实的评估，我们使用根据IBM量子硬件规范以及真实IBM量子硬件校准的噪音模型，在理想模拟和噪音模拟下评估所有方法。实验结果表明，提出的量子转移学习架构实现了有竞争力的准确性，在某些情况下还具有卓越的准确性，同时相对于经典基线持续减少训练时间和能源消耗。在所评估的方法中，基于PennyLane的实现在准确性和计算效率之间提供了最有利的权衡，这表明混合量子转移学习可以在现实的NISQ时代环境中提供实际好处，此时特征提取仍然是经典的。


### Abstract

Quantum transfer learning combines pretrained classical deep learning models with quantum circuits to reuse expressive feature representations while limiting the number of trainable parameters. In this work, we introduce a family of compact quantum transfer learning architectures that attach variational quantum classifiers to frozen convolutional backbones for image classification. We instantiate and evaluate several classical-quantum hybrid models implemented in PennyLane and Qiskit, and systematically compare them with a classical transfer-learning baseline across heterogeneous image datasets. To ensure a realistic assessment, we evaluate all approaches under both ideal simulation and noisy emulation using noise models calibrated from IBM quantum hardware specifications, as well as on real IBM quantum hardware. Experimental results show that the proposed quantum transfer learning architectures achieve competitive and, in several cases, superior accuracy while consistently reducing training time and energy consumption relative to the classical baseline. Among the evaluated approaches, PennyLane-based implementations provide the most favorable trade-off between accuracy and computational efficiency, suggesting that hybrid quantum transfer learning can offer practical benefits in realistic NISQ era settings when feature extraction remains classical.

## 吉布斯态的不确定性关系

**Original Title:** Uncertainty Relation for Entropy and Temperature of Gibbs States

**Authors:** Francis J. Headley

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16456v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.16456v2

**Submitted:** 2026-03-17


### 摘要

我们推导出吉布斯状态下用于信息量估计的量子Fisher信息，并证明$F_s = 1/C_v$，与温度Fisher信息$F_S = C_v/T #2 $是二元的。它们的积$F_S\csot F_T = 1/T^2$独立于汉密尔顿量，从而产生普适不确定关系$A #2 S\，A #2 T \geq T#2/n#2 $，其中所有系统特定量（例如热容量、汉密尔顿量和自由度数）相同抵消。这是$S$和$T$之间Legendre共乘性的字形表达。我们将能量测量确定为信息量估计的最佳协议，分析关键点缩放，其中$F_S \sim|不|' a\到0$，并将$F_S$连接到信息量坐标中的Ruppeiner度量。结果表明，不确定关系适用于所有标准热力学共勉对，并且我们考察了冯·诺伊曼（von Neumann）信息在伦尼家族中的杰出作用。给出了大经典和广义吉布斯合奏的概括。


### Abstract

We derive the quantum Fisher information for entropy estimation in a Gibbs state and show that $F_s = 1/C_v$, dual to the temperature Fisher information $F_S = C_v/T^2$. Their product $F_S\cdot F_T = 1/T^2$ is independent of the Hamiltonian, yielding the universal uncertainty relation $Δ^2 S\,Δ^2 T \geq T^2/n^2$ in which all system-specific quantities such as heat capacity, the Hamiltonian, and the number of degrees of freedom cancel identically. This is the metrological expression of the Legendre conjugacy between $S$ and $T$. We identify energy measurement as the optimal protocol for entropy estimation, analyse critical-point scaling where $F_S \sim |t|^α\to 0$, and connect $F_S$ to the Ruppeiner metric in entropy coordinates. The uncertainty relation is shown to hold for all standard thermodynamic conjugate pairs, and we examine the distinguished role of the von~Neumann entropy within the Rényi family. Generalisations to the grand canonical and generalised Gibbs ensembles are given.

## 对数深度量子态的制备

**Original Title:** Logarithmic-depth quantum state preparation of polynomials

**Authors:** Baptiste Claudon, Alexis Lucas, Jean-Philip Piquemal, César Feniou, Julien Zylberman

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16527v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.16527v2

**Submitted:** 2026-03-17


### 摘要

量子状态准备是许多量子算法中的核心基础，但它通常是资源密集型的，有效的构造仅适用于结构化状态族。这项工作引入了一种制备量子状态的方法，其幅度由次数$-d$的多项给出，使用具有对数$n$量子位且仅$\mathcali O（n）$ ancilla量子位的电路，改进了之前需要线性深度电路的方法。该构造首先依赖于仿射对角线运算符的块编码，该运算符基于其Pauli基分解，仅涉及$n$项。引入了一种修改的线性二进制组合（LCU）技术来实现对log深度的这种分解，并引入了一种用于EXACT-one Oracle的新颖电路，该电路标记了其中恰好有一个量子位处于状态$中的基本状态|1\rangle $。然后，它使用广义量子本征值变换（GQET）将该仿射运算符提升为任意次数的多项。报告了理论分析和数值模拟，以及在使用价值14美元的量子位和超过500美元的原始量子门的陷阱离子量子处理器上的原理验证实现。由于多元逼近在科学计算中无处不在，因此这种结构为量子状态准备提供了一种可扩展且资源高效的方法，进一步提高了量子算法在化学、物理、工程和金融等领域的潜力。


### Abstract

Quantum state preparation is a central primitive in many quantum algorithms, yet it is generally resource intensive, with efficient constructions known only for structured families of states. This work introduces a method for preparing quantum states whose amplitudes are given by a degree$-d$ polynomial, using circuits with logarithmic depth in the number $n$ of qubits and only $\mathcal O(n)$ ancilla qubits, improving previous approaches that required linear-depth circuits. The construction first relies on a block-encoding of an affine diagonal operator based on its Pauli-basis decomposition, which involves only $n$ terms. A modified linear-combination-of-unitaries (LCU) technique is introduced to implement this decomposition in logarithmic depth, together with a novel circuit for the EXACT-one oracle that flags basis states in which exactly one qubit is in the state $|1\rangle$. It then uses a generalized quantum eigenvalue transformation (GQET) to promote this affine operator to an arbitrary degree polynomial. Theoretical analysis and numerical simulations are reported along with a proof-of-principle implementation on a trapped-ion quantum processor using $14$ qubits and more than $500$ primitive quantum gates. Because polynomial approximations are ubiquitous in scientific computing, this construction provides a scalable and resource-efficient approach to quantum state preparation, further improving the potential of quantum algorithms in fields such as chemistry, physics, engineering, and finance.

## 来自光阵面Hamilton的质子的旋转纠缠特征

**Original Title:** Spin entanglement signatures of proton from a light-front Hamiltonian

**Authors:** Chen Qian, Siqi Xu, Yang-Guang Yang, Xingbo Zhao

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.16681v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.16681v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-17


### 摘要

量子纠缠为强子内部结构提供了定量探测，并为研究强子波函数中的量子相关性提供了敏感的手段。对于重子来说，三个价量子的旋转状态形成了一个三方量子位系统，其纠缠结构可以用四类三量子位状态来描述。在这项工作中，我们比较了从基本光阵面量子化（BLFQ）获得的质子旋转纠缠与从量子-双量子模型获得的质子旋转纠缠。通过分析二粒子和三粒子纠缠，我们发现，在价Fock区，与BLFQ波函数相比，在价Fock区产生了比BLFQ波函数更多的纠缠性。这种差异主要源于量子-双量子模型中较大的W型和Bell型纠缠。在BLFQ中，更大的强耦合常数和更小的量子质量将价态量子之间的旋转相关性驱动为有效的量子-双量子配置，该配置具有活跃的$d$ quark和相关的$uu$对。


### Abstract

Quantum entanglement provides a quantitative probe of the internal structure of hadrons and offers a sensitive means to study the quantum correlation in the hadron wave functions. Experimental implementation on an NMR quantum processor further verifies its feasibility in real-world noisy environments. Compared to existing quantum algorithms (e.g., VQE, QPE and their variants), WQTE exhibits superior circuit depth efficiency, resource economy, and noise resilience, making it a promising solution for eigen-energy computation on noisy intermediate-scale quantum (NISQ) devices.

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.17959v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-18


### 摘要

This work provides a robust and scalable framework for quantum spectral estimation in the NISQ era.
