## 通过量子模拟进行多任务处理

**Original Title:** Multi-tasking through quantum annealing

**Authors:** Jargalsaikhan Artag, Koki Awaya, Takumi Kanezashi, Daisuke Tsukayama, Moe Shimada, Jun-ichi Shirakashi

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.09468v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.09468v2

**Submitted:** 2026-03-10


### 摘要

量子模拟通过利用隔热量子系统的原理来大致解决组合优化问题。在这种方法中，系统的汉密尔顿量从初始一般状态演变到特定问题的状态。这项研究引入了多任务量子模拟（MTQA），这是一种通过将多个优化问题嵌入量子硬件上空间不同的区域来实现并行处理的方法。MTQA使用两个NP难问题进行评估：最小点覆盖问题（MVCP）和图分割问题（GPP）。这种并行方法通过并发利用空闲量子位来优化量子资源利用率。研究结果表明，MTQA实现了与单问题量子求解和经典模拟求解（SA）相当的解决方案质量，同时显着减少了解决时间（TTC）指标。特征谱分析在理论上进一步支持了这样的假设：并行嵌入可以保留量子一致性，并且不会通过有效利用可用的量子硬件（例如，量子位和耦合器）。MTQA在量子模拟中实现高效的多任务处理，优化硬件利用率并提高并发任务的吞吐量，并展示现实世界应用程序中多达100个节点的问题的性能。


### Abstract

Quantum annealing approximately solves combinatorial optimization problems by leveraging the principles of adiabatic quantum systems. In this approach, the system's Hamiltonian evolves from an initial general state to a problem-specific state. This study introduces multi-tasking quantum annealing (MTQA), a method that enables the parallel processing of multiple optimization problems by embedding them into spatially distinct regions on quantum hardware. MTQA is evaluated using two NP-hard problems: the minimum vertex cover problem (MVCP) and the graph partitioning problem (GPP). This parallel approach optimizes quantum resource utilization by concurrently utilizing idle qubits. The findings demonstrate that MTQA achieves a solution quality comparable to single-problem quantum annealing and classical simulated annealing (SA), while notably reducing the time-to-solution (TTS) metrics. Eigenspectrum analysis further theoretically supports the hypothesis that parallel embedding preserves quantum coherence and does not increase computational complexity by efficiently utilizing available quantum hardware (e.g., qubits and couplers). MTQA enables efficient multitasking in quantum annealing, optimizing hardware utilization and improving throughput for concurrent tasks and demonstrating performance for problems up to 100 nodes in real-world applications.

## 量子电池和时间膨胀

**Original Title:** Quantum batteries and time dilation

**Authors:** Esteban Martínez-Vargas

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.11079v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.11079v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-10


### 摘要

时空是基本的还是可以通过量子相互作用得到？我们在这里提出了一种仅从量子力学中描述时间膨胀的方法。首先，我们首先观察到，任何时间的操作概念都必须暗示某种规则运动，最重要的是，某种记忆。因此，我们这里使用的时钟模型是量子存储器的一个简单例子：充电电池。我们在这里描述了用量子开放动力学对此类电池的充电。这些电池的预期价值随着时间的推移线性增长，就像$\varphy t$一样。开放动态取决于雪茄状态$Sigma $。因此，对于不同的雪茄状态，我们有不同的$\varphy $。我们可以这样描述时间膨胀和度量。我们用黑洞度规来计算。


### Abstract

Is spacetime fundamental or can it be derived through quantum interactions? We propose here a way to describe time dilation solely from quantum mechanics. First we start by observing that any operational notion of time must imply some sort of regular motion and, crucially, some sort of memory. Thus the clock model we use here is a simple example of a quantum memory: a charging battery. We describe here the charging of such batteries with quantum open dynamics. The expected value of these batteries grow linearly in time like $\varphi t$. The open dynamics is dependent on an auxiliar state $σ$. Therefore, with a different auxiliar state we have a different $\varphi$. We can describe time dilation and thus a metric this way. We exemplify with a black hole metric.

## 卫星增强全球量子转发器网络的可行性

**Original Title:** Feasibility of satellite-augmented global quantum repeater networks

**Authors:** Manik Dawar, Clement Paillet, Nilesh Vyas, Andrew Thain, Rodrigo Henriques Guilherme, Ralf Riedinger

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.11127v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.11127v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-11


### 摘要

大规模量子网络需要高保真度端到端纠缠的分布。为了克服地面光纤固有的范围限制，出现了一种领先的架构：基于卫星的源将纠缠传输到地面的量子转发器。通过弥合抽象分析框架和计算量大的数值模拟之间的差距，本文为此类网络利用当前和近期空间技术可实现的性能问题提供了第一个定量答案，同时考虑到纠缠交换和净化。这是通过将卫星到地面链路的详细物理模型集成到量子转发器的分析纠缠资源估计框架中来实现的，从而优化端到端纠缠率。我们在领先的量子硬件平台上进行的分析表明，低地轨道卫星星座与采用中性原子或氮和硅空位量子位的量子转发器相结合，可以实现全球量子网络，将纠缠分布在长达20，000公里的距离上，足以连接地球上的任何两个点。这项工作强调了空间和量子硬件技术中需要解决的主要瓶颈，从而指导实现大规模量子网络所需的明智投资。


### Abstract

A large scale quantum network requires the distribution of high-fidelity end-to-end entanglement. To overcome the range limitations inherent to terrestrial fiber, a leading architecture has emerged: satellite-based sources transmitting entanglement to quantum repeaters on the ground. By bridging the gap between abstract analytical frameworks and computationally heavy numerical simulations, this paper provides the first quantitative answer to the question of such a network's achievable performance with current and near-term space technology, while accounting for entanglement swapping and purification. This is achieved by integrating a detailed physical model of a satellite-to-ground link into an analytical entanglement resource estimation framework for quantum repeaters, enabling an optimization of the end-to-end entanglement rate. Our analysis, performed across leading quantum hardware platforms, shows that Low Earth Orbit satellite constellations combined with quantum repeaters employing Neutral Atom or Nitrogen and Silicon Vacancy qubits, could enable a global quantum network, distributing entanglement over distances up to 20,000 km, sufficient for connecting any two points on Earth. This work highlights the major bottlenecks in space and quantum hardware technologies, which need to be addressed, thereby guiding informed investments necessary for enabling a large scale quantum network.

## 变分自适应高斯分解：可扩展的无象限时间切片融化高斯动力学

**Original Title:** Variational Adaptive Gaussian Decomposition: Scalable Quadrature-Free Time-Sliced Thawed Gaussian Dynamics

**Authors:** Rahul Sharma, Amartya Bose

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10931v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.10931v2

**Submitted:** 2026-03-11


### 摘要

时间切片已成为一种将半经典传播纳入实时路径积分公式并恢复完整量子动力学的策略。核心步骤是将时间演变的波函数分解为高斯波包（GWP）的叠加。在这里，我们引入了一个用于GWP分解的无二次变分框架，将其重新定义为一个优化问题，其中选择GWP参数以最大化与时间演变波函数的重叠。自动编码器-解码器神经网络用于此优化，并在传播期间自适应地重新优化表示。此分解中的每个波包代表底层半经典动量的局部补丁，同时保留所有自由度之间的完全相关性。这种变分自适应高斯分解（VAGD）方法产生了紧凑的高斯展开，为时间切片半经典量子动力学提供了可扩展的途径。虽然一般来说，应用VAGD来促进解冻高斯逼近（TGA）的时间切片，可以提供一种以系统性方式改进半经典处理的途径，以完整量子力学结果。


### Abstract

Time-slicing has emerged as a strategy for incorporating semiclassical propagation into real-time path integral formulation and recovering full quantum dynamics. A central step is the decomposition of a time-evolved wave function into a superposition of Gaussian wave packets (GWPs). Here we introduce a quadrature-free variational framework for GWP decomposition, reformulating it as an optimization problem in which the GWP parameters are chosen to maximize the overlap with the time-evolving wave function. An autoencoder-decoder neural network is used for this optimization, with the representation being adaptively reoptimized during propagation. Each wave packet in this decomposition represents a localized patch of the underlying semiclassical manifold, while retaining full correlations between all degrees of freedom. This variational adaptive Gaussian decomposition (VAGD) approach yields a compact Gaussian expansion, providing a scalable route to time-sliced semiclassical quantum dynamics. While general, applying VAGD to facilitate time-slicing of thawed Gaussian approximation (TGA) allows a route to improving the semiclassical treatment to the full quantum mechanical result in a systematic manner.

## 广义简化密度矩阵量子蒙特卡罗提供更多信息

**Original Title:** Generalized Reduced-Density-Matrix Quantum Monte Carlo Gives Access to More

**Authors:** Zhiyan Wang, Zhe Wang, Bin-Bin Mao, Zheng Yan

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10948v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.10948v2

**Submitted:** 2026-03-11


### 摘要

长期以来，人们一直关注如何从配分函数的量子蒙特卡罗（QMC）模拟中提取更多信息，例如非对角可观测量，但也存在着众多困难，其中许多困难是无法克服的。在本文中，我们指出所有困难都源于模拟的起点：计算配分函数。我们引入范式转变：当我们将模拟对象从配分函数转换为广义约化密度矩阵（GRDM）时，测量的困难问题就可以很容易地得到解决。通过设计GRDM，可以在QMC中轻松测量等时间和非等时间非对角可观测量，并具有多项计算复杂性。作为演示，GRDM能够直接访问动态光谱的非等时相关器以及揭示混合状态中强到弱对称性破缺的Rényi-1相关器，这些能力超出了现有方法的能力。这为QMC内的全息特征建立了统一的框架。


### Abstract

For a long time, people have been focusing on how to extract more information, such as off-diagonal observables, from the quantum Monte Carlo (QMC) simulation of the partition function, but there have been numerous difficulties, and many of them are insurmountable. In this article, we point out that all the difficulties stem from the starting point of the simulation: calculating a partition function. We introduce a paradigm shift: when we transform the simulated object from a partition function to a generalized reduced density matrix (GRDM), the difficult problem of measurement can be readily solved. By designing the GRDM, both equal-time and nonequal-time off-diagonal observables have been measured easily in QMC with a polynomial computation complexity. As a demonstration, the GRDM enables direct access to nonequal-time correlators for dynamical spectra as well as Rényi-1 correlators that reveal strong-to-weak symmetry breaking in the mixed state, capabilities that lie beyond the reach of prior methods. This establishes a unified framework for holographic characterization within QMC.

### 摘要

### Abstract

Focusing on nuclear shell model eigenstates, we first demonstrate that the Density Matrix Renormalization Group algorithm can efficiently approximate target states as Matrix Product States, capitalizing on the favourable entanglement structure of these fermionic systems. We establish concrete resource estimates by decomposing the resulting circuits into the industry-standard Clifford$+T$ gateset, exploring the benefits of specialized $U3$ synthesis techniques. This low number gives confidence these eigenstates can be prepared on early fault-tolerant quantum computers. Our work establishes a viable path toward practical ground state preparation for nuclear structure and other fermionic applications.

### Abstract

## DysonNet：神经量子状态的恒时本地更新

**Original Title:** DysonNet: Constant-Time Local Updates for Neural Quantum States

**Authors:** Lucas Winter, Andreas Nunnenkamp

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.11189v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.11189v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-11


### 摘要

神经量子态（NQS）为多体波函数提供了一个灵活的变分框架，但存在计算成本高和可解释性有限的问题。我们引入DysonNet，这是一个广泛的NQS类，它通过全局线性层严格耦合局部非线性。这种结构类似于截断的戴森级数，它给出了从静态杂质散射的局部波函数更新的直观解释。通过计算散射序列，单自旋翻转更新可以在$\mathcal{O}（1）$时间内计算，与系统大小无关，使用我们称为ABACUS的算法。通过状态空间模型S4实现DysonNet，我们在Vision-Transformers上获得了高达230美元的加速比，用于计算本地估计器。这对应于训练时间缩放的渐进$\mathCal{O}（N%2）$改进，在面积定律阶段达到$\mathCal{O}（N%2 N）$总训练复杂性。1D远程Ising模型和令人沮丧的$J_1$-$J_2$链的基准表明，DysonNet符合最先进的NQS准确性，同时消除了主要的本地更新负担。更广泛地说，我们的结果提出了一条通往可扩展NQS架构的途径，其中物理可解释性直接提高了计算效率。


### Abstract

Neural quantum states (NQS) provide a flexible variational framework for many-body wavefunctions, but suffer from high computational cost and limited interpretability. We introduce DysonNet, a broad class of NQS that couples strictly local nonlinearities through global linear layers. This structure is analogous to a truncated Dyson series which gives an intuitive interpretation of local wavefunction updates as scattering from static impurities. By resumming the scattering series, single-spin-flip updates can be computed in $\mathcal{O}(1)$ time, independent of system size, using an algorithm we call ABACUS. Implementing DysonNet with the state-space model S4, we obtain up to $230\times$ speedups over Vision-Transformers for computing the local estimator. This corresponds to an asymptotic $\mathcal{O}(N^2)$ improvement in training-time scaling, reaching $\mathcal{O}(N \log^2 N)$ total training complexity in area-law phases. Benchmarks on the 1D long-range Ising model and frustrated $J_1$-$J_2$ chains show that DysonNet matches state-of-the-art NQS accuracy while removing the dominant local-update overhead. More broadly, our results suggest a route to scalable NQS architectures where physical interpretability directly enables computational efficiency.

**Authors:** Aleksey Lunkin

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.12234v1

### 摘要

### 摘要

## 数字量子处理器上跨尺度的普遍性的发生

**Original Title:** Onset of Ergodicity Across Scales on a Digital Quantum Processor

**Authors:** Faisal Alam, Marcos Crichigno, Elizabeth Crosson, Steven T. Flammia, Filippo Maria Gambetta, Max Hunter Gordon, Michael Kreshchuk, Ashley Montanaro, Alberto Nocera, Raul A. Santos

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12236v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2603.12236v1

**Submitted:** 2026-03-12


### 摘要

了解孤立的量子多体系统如何热化仍然是现代物理学的核心问题。我们在IBM的夜鹰超级处理器上使用数字量子模拟来研究二维无序海森堡·Floquet模型中的各态分布，使系统规模达到高达10美元x 10美元量子位。我们通过将系统粗粒度划分为不同大小的空间斑块并引入基于每个斑块碰撞信息的测量来探索不同长度尺度的各历经行为，从而能够详细研究跨尺度出现各历经行为的时间。超导量子处理单元的高采样率，加上最佳样本估计器，使我们能够访问大小高达3美元的补丁。我们观察到，随着海森堡耦合$J$的增加，无噪音系统经历了从亚历经行为到历经行为的平滑交叉，较小的补丁首先接近其随机矩阵理论值，从而揭示了跨尺度的层次结构。在经典张量网络模拟可靠的参数空间区域，小补丁或小$J$值，我们发现与误差减轻的量子模拟非常一致。除了这个制度之外，体积定律纠缠和收缩复杂性的增长导致经典方法的成本急剧上升。我们的结果为量子计算机在量子热化研究中的使用开辟了新的方向。


### Abstract

Understanding how isolated quantum many-body systems thermalize remains a central question in modern physics. We study the onset of ergodicity in a two-dimensional disordered Heisenberg Floquet model using digital quantum simulation on IBM's Nighthawk superconducting processor, reaching system sizes of up to $10\times10$ qubits. We probe ergodicity across different length scales by coarse-graining the system into spatial patches of varying sizes and introducing a measure based on the collision entropy of each patch, enabling a detailed study of when ergodic behavior emerges across scales. The high sampling rate of superconducting quantum processing units, together with an optimal sample estimator, allow us to access patches of sizes up to $3\times3$. We observe that as the Heisenberg coupling $J$ increases, the noiseless system undergoes a smooth crossover from subergodic to ergodic behavior, with smaller patches approaching their random-matrix-theory values first, thereby revealing a hierarchy across scales. In the region of parameter space where classical tensor-network simulations are reliable, small patches or small values of $J$, we find excellent agreement with the error-mitigated quantum simulation. Beyond this regime, volume-law entanglement and contraction complexity growth causes the cost of classical methods to rise sharply. Our results open new directions for the use of quantum computers in the study of quantum thermalization.
