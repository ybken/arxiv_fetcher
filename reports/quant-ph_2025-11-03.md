## 消散性超量子位的非线性量子进化

**Original Title:** Nonlinear quantum evolution of a dissipative superconducting qubit

**Authors:** Orion Lee, Qian Cao, Yogesh N. Joglekar, Kater Murch

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.25836v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.25836v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-29


### 摘要

量子动力学的么正模型和消散模型是状态空间或密度矩阵上的线性映射。这种线性编码了叠加原理，这是量子理论的关键特征。然而，这一原理可能会在后选择量子进化产生的有效非埃尔米特动力学中崩溃。我们从理论上描述和实验上研究了消耗性超导子电路中的这种击穿。在电路的三能级流形中，无跳跃后选择产生一个有效的非厄米特哈密顿量，控制激发的二能级子空间和反厄米特非线性。我们准备不同的初始状态，并使用量子态层析成像跟踪他们的演变下，这个有效的，非线性的哈密顿量。通过比较一个叠加态的演化叠加的单独发展的基础状态，我们测试线性度，并观察到明确的违规行为，我们量化的异常点（EP）简并的非厄米哈密顿量。我们扩展到密度矩阵的分析，揭示了两级子空间的线性故障，同时表明，线性保存在完整的三能级系统。这些结果提供了非埃尔米特量子进化中非线性的直接证据，凸显了经典非埃尔米特系统中所缺乏的独特特征。


### Abstract

Unitary and dissipative models of quantum dynamics are linear maps on the
space of states or density matrices. This linearity encodes the superposition
principle, a key feature of quantum theory. However, this principle can break
down in effective non-Hermitian dynamics arising from postselected quantum
evolution. We theoretically characterize and experimentally investigate this
breakdown in a dissipative superconducting transmon circuit. Within the
circuit's three-level manifold, no-jump postselection generates an effective
non-Hermitian Hamiltonian governing the excited two-level subspace and an
anti-Hermitian nonlinearity. We prepare different initial states and use
quantum state tomography to track their evolution under this effective,
nonlinear Hamiltonian. By comparing the evolution of a superposition-state to a
superposition of individually-evolved basis states, we test linearity and
observe clear violations which we quantify across the exceptional-point (EP)
degeneracy of the non-Hermitian Hamiltonian. We extend the analysis to density
matrices, revealing a breakdown in linearity for the two-level subspace while
demonstrating that linearity is preserved in the full three-level system. These
results provide direct evidence of nonlinearity in non-Hermitian quantum
evolution, highlighting unique features that are absent in classical
non-Hermitian systems.

## 建立光量子机器学习的基线：来自开放、协作计划的见解

**Original Title:** Establishing Baselines for Photonic Quantum Machine Learning: Insights from an Open, Collaborative Initiative

**Authors:** Cassandre Notton, Vassilis Apostolou, Agathe Senellart, Anthony Walsh, Daphne Wang, Yichen Xie, Songqinghao Yang, Ilyass Mejdoub, Oussama Zouhry, Kuan-Cheng Chen, Chen-Yu Liu, Ankit Sharma, Edara Yaswanth Balaji, Soham Prithviraj Pawar, Ludovic Le Frioux, Valentin Macheret, Antoine Radet, Valentin Deumier, Ashesh Kumar Gupta, Gabriele Intoccia, Dimitri Jordan Kenne, Chiara Marullo, Giovanni Massafra, Nicolas Reinaldet, Vincenzo Schiano Di Cola, Danylo Kolesnyk, Yelyzaveta Vodovozova, Rawad Mezher, Pierre-Emmanuel Emeriau, Alexia Salavrakos, Jean Senellart

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.25839v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.25839v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-29


### 摘要

Perceval挑战是一个开放、可重复的基准，旨在评估光量子计算在机器学习中的潜力。它专注于MNIST数字分类任务或近期光处理器的简化和硬件可行版本，提供了一个具体的框架来评估光量子电路如何从有限的数据中学习和概括。该挑战赛进行了三个多月，第一阶段吸引了全球64支球队。经过初步筛选，11个决赛团队获得了通过云服务访问图形处理器资源的权限，用于大规模模拟和光硬件执行。这些结果建立了光学机器学习性能的第一个统一基线，揭示了变式、硬件原生和混合方法之间的互补优势。这一挑战还强调了开放、可重复的实验和跨学科合作的重要性，凸显了共享基准如何加速量子增强学习的进展。所有实现均在单个共享存储库（https：//github.com/Quandela/HybridAIQuantum-Challenge）中公开提供，支持透明的基准测试和累积研究。除了这项具体任务之外，Perceval挑战赛还展示了系统性的协作实验如何绘制当前的光量子机器学习格局，并为混合的量子增强人工智能工作流程铺平道路。


### Abstract

The Perceval Challenge is an open, reproducible benchmark designed to assess
the potential of photonic quantum computing for machine learning. Focusing on a
reduced and hardware-feasible version of the MNIST digit classification task or
near-term photonic processors, it offers a concrete framework to evaluate how
photonic quantum circuits learn and generalize from limited data. Conducted
over more than three months, the challenge attracted 64 teams worldwide in its
first phase. After an initial selection, 11 finalist teams were granted access
to GPU resources for large-scale simulation and photonic hardware execution
through cloud service. The results establish the first unified baseline of
photonic machine-learning performance, revealing complementary strengths
between variational, hardware-native, and hybrid approaches. This challenge
also underscores the importance of open, reproducible experimentation and
interdisciplinary collaboration, highlighting how shared benchmarks can
accelerate progress in quantum-enhanced learning. All implementations are
publicly available in a single shared repository
(https://github.com/Quandela/HybridAIQuantum-Challenge), supporting transparent
benchmarking and cumulative research. Beyond this specific task, the Perceval
Challenge illustrates how systematic, collaborative experimentation can map the
current landscape of photonic quantum machine learning and pave the way toward
hybrid, quantum-augmented AI workflows.

## 具有峰值电路的启发式量子优势

**Original Title:** Heuristic Quantum Advantage with Peaked Circuits

**Authors:** Hrant Gharibyan, Mohammed Zuhair Mullath, Nicholas E. Sherman, Vincent P. Su, Hayk Tepanyan, Yuxuan Zhang

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.25838v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.25838v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-29


### 摘要

我们在Quantinuum的系统型号H2量子处理器上设计并展示了具有峰值电路（HQAP电路）的启发式量子优势。通过对最先进的经典模拟策略进行广泛实验，我们发现了经典运行时和量子运行时之间的明显差距。我们最大的实例涉及与2000个两量子位门的全对全连接，H2可以在2小时内直接产生目标峰值位串。我们从领先的经典模拟技术（例如具有信念传播的张量网络和泡利路径模拟器）中进行的推断表明，同样的实例在亿级系统（Frontier、Summit）上需要数年时间，这表明可能存在指数级分离。这项工作标志着可验证量子优势的重要里程碑，并为当前实用规模的量子硬件提供了有用的基准测试协议。我们勾勒出我们的协议设计这些电路，并提供了广泛的数值结果，导致我们的外推估计。从我们构建的HQAP电路分开，我们证明了硬度的决策问题，涉及通用峰值电路。当峰值电路的输入和输出位串都是未知的时，确定电路是否是峰值构成了QCMA完全问题，这意味着即使对于量子多项式时间机器，在普遍接受的复杂性假设下，这个问题仍然很难。受这一观察结果的启发，我们提出了将峰值电路作为潜在的量子安全加密方案的应用~\cycle {chen 2016 report，guardar 2020 Post，joseph 2022 transitioning，dam 2023 survey}。我们将我们的峰值电路公开，并邀请社区尝试其他方法来解决这些电路，看看即使使用新颖的经典技术，这种差距是否仍然存在。


### Abstract

We design and demonstrate heuristic quantum advantage with peaked circuits
(HQAP circuits) on Quantinuum's System Model H2 quantum processor. Through
extensive experimentation with state-of-the-art classical simulation
strategies, we identify a clear gap between classical and quantum runtimes. Our
largest instance involves all-to-all connectivity with 2000 two-qubit gates,
which H2 can produce the target peaked bitstring directly in under 2 hours. Our
extrapolations from leading classical simulation techniques such as tensor
networks with belief propagation and Pauli path simulators indicate the same
instance would take years on exascale systems (Frontier, Summit), suggesting a
potentially exponential separation. This work marks an important milestone
toward verifiable quantum advantage, as well as providing a useful benchmarking
protocol for current utility-scale quantum hardware. We sketch our protocol for
designing these circuits and provide extensive numerical results leading to our
extrapolation estimates. Separate from our constructed HQAP circuits, we prove
hardness on a decision problem involving generic peaked circuits. When both the
input and output bitstrings of a peaked circuit are unknown, determining
whether the circuit is peaked constitutes a QCMA-complete problem, meaning the
problem remains hard even for a quantum polynomial-time machine under commonly
accepted complexity assumptions. Inspired by this observation, we propose an
application of the peaked circuits as a potentially quantum-safe encryption
scheme~\cite{chen2016report,kumar2020post,joseph2022transitioning,dam2023survey}.
We make our peaked circuits publicly available and invite the community to try
additional methods to solve these circuits to see if this gap persists even
with novel classical techniques.

## GZZ保护闸门和优化的蒸馏电路

**Original Title:** GHZ-Preserving Gates and Optimized Distillation Circuits

**Authors:** Mingyuan Wang, Guus Avis, Stefan Krastanov

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.25854v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.25854v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-29


### 摘要

Greenberger-Horne-Zeilinger（GZZ）状态作为典型的多方纠缠资源，在量子计算和通信协议中发挥着核心作用。这项工作为电路引入了一种有效的列举和模拟方法，该方法保留和提取有噪的GZZ状态，显着降低了$n$量子位上门的模拟复杂性，从标准状态载体方法的指数$O（2 ' n）$或Clifford电路的$O（n）$，到这里介绍的方法的常数$O（1）$。这种方法对量子网络的设计具有深远的影响，其中以最少的资源负担保存和净化纠缠至关重要。特别是，我们展示了在快速模拟实现的优化过程中使用新方法，发现GZZ蒸馏回路的性能远远优于现有技术水平。对任意噪音模型进行微调也是可能的。我们还表明，该方法自然扩展到与GZZ状态局部Clifford等效的图状态。


### Abstract

Greenberger-Horne-Zeilinger (GHZ) states play a central role in quantum
computing and communication protocols, as a typical multipartite entanglement
resource. This work introduces an efficient enumeration and simulation method
for circuits that preserve and distill noisy GHZ states, significantly reducing
the simulation complexity of a gate on $n$ qubits, from exponential $O(2^n)$
for standard state-vector methods or $O(n)$ for Clifford circuits, to a
constant $O(1)$ for the method presented here. This method has profound
implications for the design of quantum networks, where preservation and
purification of entanglement with minimal resource overhead is critical. In
particular, we demonstrate the use of the new method in an optimization
procedure enabled by the fast simulation, that discovers GHZ distillation
circuits far outperforming the state of the art. Fine-tuning to arbitrary noise
models is possible as well. We also show that the method naturally extends to
graph states that are local Clifford equivalent to GHZ states.

## 超导二极管效应理论

**Original Title:** Theories of Superconducting Diode Effects

**Authors:** Daniel Shaffer, Alex Levchenko

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.25864v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.25864v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-29


### 摘要

由于在经典和量子计算以及超导体传感器中的潜在应用，体导体和约瑟夫森结中的超导体二极管效应（RST）引起了广泛关注。在这里，我们回顾了理论上为实现这些目标而提出的各种机制。我们首先提供一个简短的历史概述，并讨论基本但微妙的现象学金茨堡-兰道轮回理论，强调同时打破时间逆转和倒置对称的必要性。然后我们继续进行更微观的处理，特别关注拉什巴-泽曼模型描述的非中心对称材料中的实现。最后，我们回顾了基于其他凝聚体系统的提案，例如互磁铁、谷极化和拓扑材料以及失衡系统。


### Abstract

Superconducting diode effects (SDE), both in bulk superconductors and in
Josephson junctions, have garnered a lot of attention due to potential
applications in classical and quantum computing, as well as superconducting
sensors. Here we review various mechanisms that have been theoretically
proposed for their realization. We first provide a brief historical overview
and discuss the basic but subtle phenomenological Ginzburg-Landau theory of
SDE, emphasizing the need to the simultaneous breaking of time-reversal and
inversion symmetries. We then proceed to more microscopic treatments, focusing
especially on implementations in noncentrosymmetric materials described by the
Rashba-Zeeman model. Finally, we review proposals based on other condensed
matter systems such as altermagnets, valley polarized and topological
materials, and systems out of equilibrium.

## 非平衡热纯态的空间结构纠缠

**Original Title:** Spatially Structured Entanglement from Nonequilibrium Thermal Pure States

**Authors:** Chen Bai, Mao Tian Tan, Bastien Lapierre, Shinsei Ryu

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.25868v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.25868v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-29


### 摘要

我们研究了（1+1）维临界系统中的量子猝灭动力学，从称为crosscap态的热纯态开始，并在空间非均匀哈密顿量下发展它们。空间不均匀性是通过变形的哈密顿量，表示为线性组合的生成元的$SL^{（q）}（2，\mathbb {R}）$子代数的Virasoro代数。我们分析了自由无质量狄拉克费米子理论和全息共形场论作为可积和不可积动力学的典型例子。与一般的预期一致，“M\“obius型”变形导致热化的不可积的情况下，和周期性的复苏的可积的。相比之下，“正方形型”和“位移型”变形既防止热化和扰乱，反而产生后期图形状纠缠图案。这些图案是由变形汉密尔顿量和交叉点初始状态之间的相互作用产生的，并且似乎是普遍的：它们仅由变形轮廓决定的，同时在很大程度上对微观细节不敏感。最后，我们使用AdS$_3$/CFT$_2$进行三维重力全息计算，这重现了我们（1+1）维研究的主要特征。


### Abstract

We study quantum quench dynamics in (1+1)-dimensional critical systems,
starting from thermal pure states called crosscap states, and evolving them
under spatially inhomogeneous Hamiltonians. The spatial inhomogeneity is
introduced through a deformation of the Hamiltonian, expressed as linear
combinations of the generators of the $SL^{(q)}(2,\mathbb{R})$ subalgebra of
the Virasoro algebra. We analyze the free massless Dirac fermion theory and
holographic conformal field theory as prototypical examples of integrable and
non-integrable dynamics. Consistent with general expectations, "M\"obius-type"
deformations lead to thermalization in the non-integrable case, and to periodic
revivals in the integrable one. In contrast, "sine-square-type" and
"displacement-type" deformations prevent both thermalization and scrambling,
instead producing late-time, graph-like entanglement patterns. These patterns
emerge from the interplay between the deformed Hamiltonian and the crosscap
initial state and appear to be universal: they are determined solely by the
deformation profile while remaining largely insensitive to microscopic details.
Finally, we perform a holographic calculation in three-dimensional gravity
using AdS$_3$/CFT$_2$, which reproduces the main features of our
(1+1)-dimensional study.

## 基于自旋相关压缩态的最优位移传感

**Original Title:** Optimal Displacement Sensing with Spin-Dependent Squeezed States

**Authors:** Liam J. Bond, Christophe H. Valahu, Athreya Shankar, Ting Rei Tan, Arghavan Safavi-Naini

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.25870v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.25870v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-29


### 摘要

位移传感是计量学中的一项基本任务。然而，开发充分利用多体量子系统中可用自由度的量子增强传感器仍然是一个突出的挑战。我们提出了新型的多体位移传感方案，该方案使用与旋转相关的压缩（SDs）状态--混合的旋转玻色子状态，其玻色子压缩积分取决于辅助旋转。我们证明了SDs状态是最优的，即它们的量子Cram\' r-Rao束缚饱和了海森堡极限。我们提出了明确的测量序列，可以很容易地实现在系统中，如被困离子。我们还介绍了一个可扩展的状态准备协议和数值演示的准备$8.7$~dB的自旋相关压缩$15$倍的速度比标准的方法使用二阶边带在被困离子。我们的传感协议的潜在应用范围从测量单光子散射搜索暗物质。


### Abstract

Displacement sensing is a fundamental task in metrology. However, the
development of quantum-enhanced sensors that fully utilize the available
degrees of freedom in many-body quantum systems remains an outstanding
challenge. We propose novel many-body displacement sensing schemes that use
spin-dependent squeezed (SDS) states -- hybrid spin-boson states whose bosonic
squeezed quadrature is conditioned on an auxiliary spin. We prove that SDS
states are \emph{optimal}, i.e. their quantum Cram\'{e}r-Rao bound saturates
the Heisenberg limit. We propose explicit measurement sequences that can be
readily implemented in systems such as trapped ions. We also introduce a
scalable state-preparation protocol and numerically demonstrate the preparation
of $8.7$~dB of spin-dependent squeezing $15$ times faster than the standard
approach using second-order sidebands in trapped ions. The potential
applications of our sensing protocols range from measuring single-photon
scattering to searches for dark matter.

## 通过周期驱动控制旋转玻色子系统中的记忆效应

**Original Title:** Control of memory effects in a spin-boson system by periodic driving

**Authors:** Pietro Follia, Bassano Vacchini, Heinz-Peter Breuer

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.25875v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.25875v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-29


### 摘要

我们研究了有限温度下由外部时间周期力驱动的旋转玻色子系统中量子记忆效应的出现。通过基于轨迹距离的非马尔科夫性测量来量化记忆效应，并采用分层运动方程方法进行数值模拟，当将非马尔科夫性测量绘制为驱动幅度的函数时，我们发现了明显的峰值结构。使用Floquet理论和Floquet-Lindblad主方程来解释这一独特特征，将峰值与准能谱的简并性联系起来，这导致系统的驰豫时间的强烈增强。这些结果提出了通过周期性驱动有效控制开放量子系统中非马尔科夫性的策略。


### Abstract

We study the emergence of quantum memory effects in a spin-boson system at
finite temperature driven by an external time-periodic force. Quantifying
memory effects by the trace-distance based measure for non-Markovianity and
performing numerical simulations employing the hierarchical equations of motion
approach, we find a pronounced peak structure when plotting the
non-Markovianity measure as a function of the driving amplitude. This
distinctive feature is interpreted using Floquet theory and the
Floquet-Lindblad master equation, associating the peaks with the degeneracies
of the quasienergy spectrum which lead to a strong enhancement of the
relaxation times of the system. These results suggest strategies for the
efficient control of non-Markovianity in open quantum systems by periodic
driving.

## 从分歧系列到几何：量子度规的复兴

**Original Title:** From Divergent Series to Geometry: Resurgence of the Quantum Metric

**Authors:** Marcos J. Hernández, Bogar Díaz, J. David Vergara

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.25907v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.25907v1

**Submitted:** 2025-10-29


### 摘要

在这项工作中，我们分析了非谐振子中量子度量张量（QMT）的微扰展开，重点关注四次、六次和$d $维模型。利用高级微扰理论，我们证明了分歧QMT系列表现出因子增长。我们的分析确定了普遍的非微扰尺度，系数显示与复兴理论一致的高位行为。然后，我们将复兴和Borel--Pad\' e复兴应用到QMT。与精确对角化的比较证实，Borel-Pad插值会产生准确的结果，尤其是对于底价。为了完整性，我们还在示例中进行了能量本征值的分析。我们的发现将复兴技术从能量扩展到QMT，强调了量子几何和非微扰物理之间的相互作用。


### Abstract

In this work, we analyze perturbative expansions of the quantum metric tensor
(QMT) in anharmonic oscillators, focusing on quartic, sextic, and
$d$-dimensional models. Using high-order perturbation theory, we show that the
divergent QMT series exhibit factorial growth. Our analysis identifies
universal non-perturbative scales, with coefficients displaying large-order
behavior consistent with resurgence theory. Then, we apply resurgence and
Borel--Pad\'e resummation to the QMT. Comparisons with exact diagonalization
confirm that Borel--Pad\'e resummations yield accurate results, especially for
the ground state. For completeness, we also present the analysis of the energy
eigenvalues in the examples. Our findings extend resurgent techniques from
energies to the QMT, highlighting the interplay between quantum geometry and
non-perturbative physics.

## 自适应量子计算的双重类别

**Original Title:** Double categories for adaptive quantum computation

**Authors:** Cihan Okay, Walker Stern, Redi Haderi, Selman Ipek

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2510.25915v1

**PDF:** http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.25915v1
