## QBalance：用于量子编译、噪音抑制和误差缓解策略选择的可重复多目标工作流程

**Original Title:** QBalance: A Reproducible Multi-Objective Workflow for Quantum Compilation, Noise Suppression, and Error-Mitigation Strategy Selection

**Authors:** Soumyadip Sarkar

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.02966v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.02966v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-03


### 摘要

近期量子工作负载由耦合的编译和执行选择决定：量子位布局、路由、基转换、门抑制、测量缓解、射击预算和伪影再现性。本文分析了QBalance，这是一个Python工作流库，用于在QisKit生态系统上构建的量子编译、噪音抑制和错误缓解策略中进行设备级选择。该贡献被表述为针对电路、后台和转型政策的有限多目标策略选择问题。该手稿推导出了实现的加权目标、非支配选择规则、生存产品错误代理、Bayesian线性候选排序代理和分布诊断。它还相对于QisKit通行管理器编译、SABER风格路由、随机编译、动态脱钩、零噪音外推、无矩阵测量缓解、电路切割和Thompson采样方面的既定工作来定位系统。分析表明，QBalance为量子工作流程研究提供了可重复的编排和工件模型。它还建立了精确的限制：当前的强盗机制对候选人进行排序，但不会减少候选人评估的数量，自定义布局启发式是贪婪的，并且仅部分地感知布局，实现的ZNE助手是以平价为中心的，切割集成是一个挂钩而不是完整的重建管道。


### Abstract

Near-term quantum workloads are shaped by coupled compilation and execution choices: qubit layout, routing, basis translation, gate suppression, measurement mitigation, shot budget, and artifact reproducibility. This paper analyzes QBalance, a Python workflow library for dataset-level selection among quantum compilation, noise-suppression, and error-mitigation strategies built on the Qiskit ecosystem. The contribution is formulated as a finite multi-objective strategy-selection problem over circuits, backends, and transformation policies. The manuscript derives the implemented weighted objective, non-dominated selection rule, survival-product error proxy, Bayesian linear candidate-ordering surrogate, and distributional diagnostics. It also positions the system relative to established work on Qiskit pass-manager compilation, SABRE-style routing, randomized compiling, dynamical decoupling, zero-noise extrapolation, matrix-free measurement mitigation, circuit cutting, and Thompson sampling. The analysis shows that QBalance provides a reproducible orchestration and artifact model for quantum workflow studies. It also establishes precise limitations: the current bandit mechanism orders candidates but does not reduce the number of candidate evaluations, the custom layout heuristic is greedy and only partially topology-aware, the implemented ZNE helper is parity-centered, and the cutting integration is a hook rather than a full reconstruction pipeline.

## 无相辅助场量子蒙特卡罗的量子试验波函数基准

**Original Title:** Benchmarking quantum trial wavefunctions for phaseless auxiliary-field quantum Monte Carlo

**Authors:** Rod Rofougaran, Neil Mehta, Katherine Klymko, Pooja Rao, J. Wayne Mullinax, Samuel Stein, Norm M. Tubman, Ermal Rrapaj

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.02056v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.02056v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-03


### 摘要

无相辅助场量子蒙特卡罗（ph-AFQMC）方法是一种随机辅助时间投影技术，用于计算强相关量子系统的接地状态性质，其准确性主要取决于试验波函数的选择。在这里，我们研究了使用参数化量子电路准备的试验状态的ph-AFQMC。在这项工作中，我们对跨越正耦合团簇、汉密尔顿启发、贾斯特罗启发和自适应构造的量子试验波函数进行了全面的基准研究。基准测试在QC-AFQMC框架内评估这些分析的准确性、可表达性和可扩展性。我们在键伸展下在线性氢链上测试了这些反离子，发现几个反离子家族在整个分解曲线上产生了化学准确的ph-AFQMC能量。我们使用CUDA-Q量子开发平台在PerlMutter超级计算机的图形处理分区上进行了模拟。当比较相似数量的变分参数的分析时，我们发现不同的模拟家族产生了相当的ph-AFQMC结果，尽管表现出显着不同的变分能量、优化成本和电路深度。我们的结果表明，对于ph-AFQMC来说，animals的变化能量并不总是其质量的可靠指标，并揭示了过度参数化的情况。在强相关区域中，从自适应分析获得的试验波函数（这里以具有UCCSD操作员池的ADAPT-VQE为例）可以在投影能量方面优于固定分析器对应的试验波函数（UCCSD），同时使用更加紧凑的电路，提供了一种灵活的路径来优化ph-AFQMC框架内的量子资源。


### Abstract

The phaseless auxiliary-field quantum Monte Carlo (ph-AFQMC) method is a stochastic imaginary-time projection technique for computing ground-state properties of strongly correlated quantum systems, with accuracy that depends critically on the choice of trial wavefunction. Here, we investigate ph-AFQMC with trial states prepared using parameterized quantum circuits. In this work, we present a comprehensive benchmarking study of quantum trial wavefunctions spanning unitary coupled-cluster, Hamiltonian-informed, Jastrow-inspired, and adaptively constructed ansatze. The benchmarking evaluates accuracy, expressibility, and scalability of these ansatze within the QC-AFQMC framework. We test these ansatze on linear hydrogen chains under bond stretching and find that several ansatz families produce chemically accurate ph-AFQMC energies across the dissociation curve. We have performed simulations using the CUDA-Q quantum development platform on the GPU partition of the Perlmutter supercomputer. When comparing ansatze at similar numbers of variational parameters, we find that different ansatz families yield comparable ph-AFQMC results despite exhibiting substantially different variational energies, optimization costs, and circuit depths. Our results indicate that the variational energy of an ansatz is not always a reliable indicator of its quality for ph-AFQMC and reveal instances of over-parameterization. In the strongly correlated regime, trial wavefunctions obtained from adaptive ansatze, exemplified here by ADAPT-VQE with the UCCSD operator pool, can outperform their fixed-ansatz counterparts (UCCSD) in terms of projected energies while using substantially more compact circuits, providing a flexible route to optimize quantum resources within the ph-AFQMC framework.

## 后量子SSL准备性的可观察性：多表面证据框架

**Original Title:** Observability for Post-Quantum TLS Readiness: A Multi-Surface Evidence Framework

**Authors:** José Luis Delgado

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.02978v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.02978v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-04


### 摘要

传输层安全（CTS）中的后量子迁移需要证据感知测量，以区分会话协商、端点能力、证书链证据和缺失观察结果的来源。这种区别在SSL 1.3加密、恢复、相互SSL、跟踪截断、碎片化、合并、主动证书检索和时间漂移下至关重要。   我们提出了一个后量子SSL可观测性的多表面框架。该框架将被动会话证据、主动探测、证书链证据和注册表知识分开，并将它们映射到会话行为、密钥建立、端点能力、身份验证、生命周期、可观察性和策略的测量平面上。我们将其实例化为一个可重复的工件，具有模式强制的观察和结果、版本化注册表、可审计的推理规则、压力契约和基线适配器。   我们评估了29种受控场景的框架，涵盖了SSL 1.2和SSL 1.3、经典和混合密钥建立、相互SSL、恢复、HelloRetry请求、截断、碎片化和合并、时间漂移、IPv6和链深度变化。被动证据关闭会话级平面，主动探测建立能力下限，多表面证据关闭完整的测量对象，同时在需要时保留不确定性和矛盾性。针对继承的SSL量子漏洞分析器，基线检测到29次运行中的2次和23次SSL 1.3运行中的0次。在针对1000个目标和2000个新探测的分层公开活动中，该框架完成了1971次握手，收集了1368个链制品，确认了310个目标的混合能力，并识别了310个端点能力超过任何单个经典会话视图所揭示的情况。


### Abstract

Post-quantum migration in Transport Layer Security (TLS) requires evidence-aware measurements that distinguish session negotiation, endpoint capability, certificate-chain evidence, and the provenance of missing observations. This distinction is essential under TLS 1.3 encryption, resumption, mutual TLS, trace truncation, fragmentation, coalescing, active certificate retrieval, and temporal drift.
  We present a multi-surface framework for post-quantum TLS observability. The framework separates passive session evidence, active probing, certificate-chain evidence, and registry knowledge, and maps them onto measurement planes for session behavior, key establishment, endpoint capability, authentication, lifecycle, observability, and policy. We instantiate it as a reproducible artifact with schema-enforced observations and results, versioned registries, auditable inference rules, stress contracts, and baseline adapters.
  We evaluate the framework on 29 controlled scenarios spanning TLS 1.2 and TLS 1.3, classical and hybrid key establishment, mutual TLS, resumption, HelloRetryRequest, truncation, fragmentation and coalescing, temporal drift, IPv6, and chain-depth variation. Passive evidence closes session-level planes, active probing establishes capability lower bounds, and multi-surface evidence closes the full measurement object while preserving uncertainty and contradiction when required. Against an inherited TLS quantum-vulnerability analyzer, the baseline detects 2 of 29 runs and 0 of 23 TLS 1.3 runs. In a stratified public campaign over 1000 targets and 2000 fresh probes, the framework completes 1971 handshakes, collects 1368 chain artifacts, confirms hybrid capability for 310 targets, and identifies 310 cases where endpoint capability exceeds what any single classical session view reveals.

## 通过多参数调制调节冷$^{87}$RB原子的左手性

**Original Title:** Adjusting the left-handedness in a cold $^{87}$Rb atom via multiple parameter modulation

**Authors:** ShunCai Zhao. Qi-Xuan Wu, Kun Ma

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.02980v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.02980v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-04


### 摘要

我们通过数密度、强耦合场和两个非相干抽运场证明了冷\（^{87}\）镭原子的左手调节性。结果表明，越密集的镭原子和越强的耦合场对左手性的影响越大，而增加的两个非相关抽运场构成负磁响应，但抑制了负电响应。冷\（^{87}\）镭原子系统中多参数调节的左手性为接下来的实验提供了灵活性和可行性。


### Abstract

We demonstrate the adjusting left-handedness in the cold \(^{87}\)Rb atom by its number density, the strong coupling field and two incoherent pumping fields. The results show that more dense \(^{87}\)Rb atoms and more stronger coupling field can influence the left-handedness more greatly, while the increasing two incoherent pumping fields construct the negative magnetic response but depress the negative electric response. The left-handedness adjusted by multiple parameter in the cold \(^{87}\)Rb atomic system provides the flexibility and feasibility for the coming experiment.

## 色散相互作用中的自洽辐射反作用：最小mQED模型

**Original Title:** Self-consistent radiative backaction in dispersion interactions: a minimal mQED model

**Authors:** Johannes Fiedler

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.02981v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.02981v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-04


### 摘要

通常假设相互作用量子系统的内部光谱固定，即可推导出分散相互作用。在这里，我们放宽了这一假设，并研究当允许激发能和过渡DMZ对相互作用本身做出反应时，自相容电磁反作用如何修改范德华相互作用。   在宏观量子电动力学框架内，我们制定了一种自相容的处理方法，其中包括自能修正和相互反作用。使用最小三层模型，我们表明，虽然单边自能效应是短期的，但完全自相容的反作用可以导致有效范德华相互作用的实质性、长期修改。   我们的分析表明，这些效应源于重复的光介导散射过程的一致积累。结果强调了固定光谱的微扰分散理论的局限性，并将少级系统确定为研究分散力反作用的干净平台。


### Abstract

Dispersion interactions are usually derived assuming fixed internal spectra of the interacting quantum systems. Here, we relax this assumption and study how self-consistent electromagnetic backaction modifies van der Waals interactions when excitation energies and transition dipole moments are allowed to respond to the interaction itself.
  Within a macroscopic quantum electrodynamics framework, we formulate a self-consistent treatment that includes both self-energy corrections and mutual backaction. Using a minimal three-level model, we show that, while one-sided self-energy effects are short-ranged, fully self-consistent backaction can lead to substantial, long-ranged modifications of the effective van der Waals interaction.
  Our analysis demonstrates that these effects originate from the coherent accumulation of repeated photon-mediated scattering processes. The results highlight limitations of perturbative dispersion theories with fixed spectra and identify few-level systems as a clean platform for studying backaction in dispersion forces.

## 弱非调和光对暗态动力学的微扰分析

**Original Title:** Perturbative Analysis of Dark State Dynamics in Weakly Anharmonic Photon-Emitter Pairs

**Authors:** Christopher Campbell, Matti Silveri

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.02412v2

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.02412v2

**Submitted:** 2026-05-04


### 摘要

暗态是激发量子态，它们与环境脱钩，从而不会发射或吸收外部量子。这些状态存在于各种不同的开放量子系统中，并且可以从各个量子发射体相互作用的集体相互作用中得到。存在这些状态的最简单模型之一是在博斯-哈伯德模型下描述的一对消散耦合的谐振子中。当包括现场相互作用时，这些状态就不能再被归类为真正的暗状态，因为其中会引发消散。本文通过使用弱非调和性作为扰动因素来研究暗态中这种消散的起源。在我们的分析中，我们找到波函数的一阶和二阶修正，并将这些修正应用于主方程，以跟踪动力学。


### Abstract

Dark states are excited quantum states that decouple from their environment in such a way that they do not emit or absorb external photons. These states are found in a variety of different open quantum systems and can be derived from the collective interactions of individual quantum emitters interacting with one another. One of the simplest model where these states exist is in a pair of dissipatively coupled harmonic oscillators described under the Bose-Hubbard model. When on-site interactions are included, these states can no longer be classified as genuine dark states since dissipation is induced in them. In this paper we study the origin of this dissipation in dark states by using weak anharmonicity as a perturbing factor. In our analysis, we find the first and second order corrections to the wavefunction and apply these corrections to the master equation in order to track the dynamics.

## 利用酉线性组合中的所有辅助结果：低秩恢复和量子陷门函数

**Original Title:** Exploiting all ancilla outcomes in linear combinations of unitaries: low-rank recovery and quantum trapdoor functions

**Authors:** Ammar Daskin

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.02986v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.02986v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-04


### 摘要

线性正元组合（LCU）是一种基本的量子算法基本原理，它通过辅助寄存器上的后选择嵌入非正元运算符。在标准LCU中，只有$|0\dots0\rangle$ ancilla结果将保留;剩余的“垃圾”结果将被丢弃。我们通过引入替代LCU电路来研究这些废弃部分，该电路简化了使用阿达玛门和单个旋转量子位进行的系数准备。辅助单元的每个计算基础测量都将系统投影到目标单位的不同线性组合上。收集这些结果状态并将其重塑为$2K\乘N$矩阵，揭示了因式分解$® = C X$，其中$C$编码系数，$X$包含每个正元对输入的作用;这立即显示$\operatorName{rank}（Pi）\le K$。该结构实现了两个互补的应用：（i）经典的低阶矩阵完成可以从其条目的一小部分重建完整输出（包括目标），将每个镜头转化为有用的信息;（ii）将$C$视为秘密密钥隐藏了输入状态，从而导致候选量子陷门函数和对称加密。因此，该计划将“垃圾”辅助结果转化为结构化资源，可能为进一步的应用开辟道路。


### Abstract

The linear combination of unitaries (LCU) is a fundamental quantum algorithm primitive that embeds non-unitary operators via post-selection on an ancilla register. In standard LCU, only the $|0\dots0\rangle$ ancilla outcome is retained; the remaining "junk" outcomes are discarded. We study these discarded parts by introducing an alternative LCU circuit which simplifies the coefficient preparation unitary with Hadamard gates and a single rotation qubit. Every computational basis measurement of the ancilla projects the system onto a different linear combination of the target unitaries. Collecting these outcome states and reshaping them into a $2K\times N$ matrix reveals a factorization $Φ= C X$, where $C$ encodes the coefficients and $X$ contains the action of each unitary on the input; this immediately shows $\operatorname{rank}(Φ)\le K$. This structure enables two complementary applications: (i) classical low-rank matrix completion can reconstruct the full output (including the target) from a fraction of its entries, turning every shot into useful information; (ii) treating $C$ as a secret key hides the input state, leading to a candidate quantum trapdoor function and symmetric encryption. The scheme thus turns the "junk" ancilla outcomes into a structured resource, possibly opening paths for further applications.

## 克尔和立方媒体中非经典动态的基于断层图的量化器

**Original Title:** Tomogram-based quantifiers of nonclassicality dynamics in Kerr and cubic media

**Authors:** K. M. Athira, M. J. Neethu, M. Rohith

**Link:** http://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03746v1

**PDF:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.03746v1

**Submitted:** 2026-05-04


### 摘要

在现实退相关下对量子状态中的非经典性进行可靠量化仍然是推进量子技术的核心挑战。常规量化器，例如Wigner负性、Mandel的$Q$-参数、非经典深度等，通常在实验上很难处理、非唯一或对关键量子签名不敏感。我们证明，基于断层摄影的测量，即零差非经典面积和总和断层摄影量，为量化非经典动力学提供了一种稳健的、实验上可行的替代方案，因为它们可以通过平衡零差检测直接从光学断层摄影图中获得，避免密度矩阵重建并确保可行性。我们研究了克尔和立方非线性系统中演变的相干、加光相干甚至相干状态，并在幅度和相衰减下使用Lindblad主方程建模环境效应。零差非经典区域量化了超出相干状态的超额象限方差，跟踪非经典性的开始和衰变，清楚地识别分数复兴、波包分裂和宏观叠加。我们发现，幅度衰减会导致向真空方向快速单调衰减，而相衰减则允许部分复兴特征存活更长时间。与此相辅相成的是，从共轴断层图像中推导出的和断层图像信息通过弱衰减下的持续性信息最小值捕获了更高次的分数复兴和相空间干扰。我们的结果将基于同频元的量化器确立为跟踪非线性光学媒体中非经典动力学的强大、实时且实验上可行的工具，为传统的、具有实验挑战性的措施提供了令人信服的替代方案。


### Abstract

### 摘要

容错量子计算需要了解错误纠正码如何在不同物理硬件上执行。这通常是通过对高性能计算规模的逻辑电路进行噪音稳定器模拟来评估的，并结合产生相关代码距离和深度的逻辑错误率的噪音模型。均匀去极化模型是标准基线，但其均匀假设未能捕捉到真实设备的均匀性、不对称性和相关性，而泡利、测量和时空误差并不是弱耦合的。然而，这些相同的结构化特征为联合代码-硬件协同设计创造了机会，激励噪音模型更忠实地反映目标硬件，同时保持易于模拟。   我们引入FTPrimitiveBench，这是一种系统性基准测试方法，用于研究逻辑基元如何与硬件驱动的噪音交互。它支持自定义规范和代表性的结构化噪音家族：泡利偏差、测量偏差以及空间或时空非均匀性--以及核心表面代码Clifford基元的生成器：逻辑记忆、格子手术、横向逻辑Hadamard和通过格子手术的逻辑相门。我们发现结构化噪音以定性不同的方式影响这些基元，其结果由噪音模型、基元和解码器选择之间的相互作用决定。这些结果将内存基准扩展到主动逻辑计算，其中噪音结构和原始实现之间的交互很重要。通过标准化噪音模型规范和原始结构之间的联系，FTPrimitiveBench实现了QEC协议和解码器的可重复比较研究，支持硬件感知的硬件协同设计。代码：https://github.com/ShuwenKan/FTPrimitiveBench。


### Abstract

Fault-tolerant quantum computing requires understanding how error-correcting codes perform on diverse physical hardware. This is typically assessed via noisy stabilizer simulation of logical circuits at HPC scale, combined with a noise model that yields a logical error rate for the relevant code distances and depths. The uniform depolarizing model is the standard baseline, but its homogeneous assumptions fail to capture the heterogeneity, asymmetries, and correlations of real devices, where Pauli, measurement, and spatio-temporal errors are not weakly coupled. Yet these same structured features create opportunities for joint code-hardware co-design, motivating noise models that more faithfully reflect target hardware while remaining tractable to simulate.
  We introduce FTPrimitiveBench, a systematic benchmarking approach for studying how logical primitives interact with hardware-motivated noise. It supports both custom specifications and representative structured noise families: Pauli bias, measurement bias, and spatial or spatio-temporal non-uniformity -- together with generators for core surface-code Clifford primitives: logical memory, lattice surgery, transversal logical Hadamard, and the logical phase gate via lattice surgery. We find that structured noise affects these primitives in qualitatively distinct ways, with outcomes shaped by the interplay between noise model, primitive, and decoder choice. These results extend memory benchmarks to active logical computation, where the interaction between noise structure and primitive implementation matters. By standardizing the link between noise-model specification and primitive construction, FTPrimitiveBench enables reproducible comparative studies of QEC protocols and decoders, supporting hardware-aware co-design of fault-tolerant architectures. Code: https://github.com/ShuwenKan/FTPrimitiveBench.
